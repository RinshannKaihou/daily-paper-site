Causal Alignment & Structural Enforcement for Chain-of-Thought Faithfulness

Ziming Wang · Yinghua Yao · Changwu Huang · Ke Tang · Xin Yao  —  SUSTech · A*STAR CFAR · UIC · Lingnan Univ.

Key Finding

CASE aligns chain-of-thought reasoning to a faithful Z → X → Y causal chain — training-time causal alignment strengthens the rational path while inference-time attention masking blocks the instruction→answer shortcut.

01

Methodology

A two-stage attack on the Z→Y shortcut: train the model to depend on the CoT, then forbid it from reading the instruction when answering.

Faithfulness = Y ⊥ Z | X . TCA trains the model to honor it; ISE enforces it structurally at decode time.

TCATraining-time Causal Alignment

Three auxiliary datasets on top of SFT set D_A: counterfactual-CoT (L_X-int: change the CoT, change the answer), biased-instruction (L_Z-int: mislead the instruction, answer stays), empty-instruction (L_Z-drop: answer from CoT alone). Auxiliary sets supervise answer tokens only.

ISEInference-time Structural Enforcement

At answer generation, every answer token's attention logit to every instruction token is set to −∞ across all layers & heads. The explicit Z→Y path is removed from the computation graph, while CoT-side states H_X are preserved.

Theory — Mutual-Information Grounding

Four propositions tie losses to MI criteria: L_Z-drop ↑ lower bound on I(X;Y); L_X-int ↑ I(X;Y|Z); L_Z-int ↓ upper bound on I(Y;Z|X); and masking gives p_AM(y|z,h_X) = p_AM(y|h_X).

03

Strengths & Limitations

Strengths PROS

  • Theory & practice closed-loop. Every training loss maps to an MI proposition; the attention mask carries an explicit computation-graph proof.
  • Complementary by design. Ablation cleanly separates TCA (CIE/CDE) from ISE (FT/EA) — motivation matches the observed behavior.
  • Selective loss supervises only answer tokens on auxiliary data, so the model is never forced to mimic synthetic CoT.
  • Single-model & cheap. 3× faster than FRODO; engineering-friendly for deployment.
  • Transfer is the standout. Baselines' faithfulness gains don't transfer; CASE does — directly relevant to real rollout.
  • Fair evaluation. Unified LoRA, few-shot prompts, and decoding across all methods.

Limitations CONS

  • Idealized faithfulness. Y⊥Z|X is hard in natural-language CoT; masking removes only the explicit Z→Y path — instruction info already encoded into H_X survives.
  • Eval–optimize overlap. CIE/CDE are both training targets and metrics, risking optimistic bias; independent FT/EA gains are smaller.
  • Narrow scope. Only multiple-choice / boolean tasks on 7B–8B models — no open-ended generation, math (GSM8K/MATH), or code; no 70B+ scaling.
  • Unstable on generative boolean. On Qwen3-8B + StrategyQA, CASE's CDE rises to 0.366 vs DU_c's 0.146.
  • External data dependency. Counterfactual/biased data rely on DeepSeek-V3.2; human-audit agreement rates are not reported.
  • Mask ratio β is task-specific (full masking hides the proposition in T/F tasks); KV-cache / long-context cost and adversarial-prompt robustness are unaddressed.
04

Questions for Further Exploration

KV-cache & long context. How does attention masking scale to 128k-token multi-turn decoding — does implementation cost grow linearly with prompt length?
Implicit leakage probe. If we measure how much instruction is decodable from H_X, how large is the gap between CASE's "explicit" faithfulness and true causal independence?
Open-ended & math reasoning. Can CASE extend to GSM8K / MATH / code? How are FT, EA, CIE, CDE redefined over free-text answers?
Breaking the eval loop. If CIE/CDE are dropped from training and only FT/EA losses are used, does the G-meanFaith gain survive — directly testing the optimization–evaluation circularity?
Composability. Does masking synergize or conflict with contrastive decoding, activation steering, self-consistency, or FRODO-style pipelines?
Scaling law of faithfulness. At 70B+ or full DeepSeek-R1 / o1-class models, do marginal returns decay — requiring layer-selective masking instead of global?
Adversarial robustness. How does masking hold under prompt injection or unseen misleading cues — does D_C generalize beyond its construction distribution?
Loss weighting & RL. Could token-importance-weighted selective loss, or GRPO-style token-level RL, replace SFT and raise sample efficiency?
