> **一句话总结：** 这篇论文把"听起来在做推理+搜索"的 Lattice Deduction Transformer 解剖开来，发现在线索充足的数独里它其实是一个"一次性预测器"——一次前向传播就把整盘填满，所有失败都源于第一pass就自信地删掉了正确答案（first-pass poisoning），而额外加入的 CDCL 式神经搜索层（CoLT）只能把浪费的验证调用砍掉 1497 倍，对准确率毫无影响；真正能修复准确率的是对称性校准（训练时数字置换增强把 9×9 从 <1% 提到 96.5%，测试时对称帧并集把 hard slice 从 76.1% 提到 100%）。

> **TL;DR:** By building CoLT — a deliberately strong CDCL-inspired neural search layer on top of LDT's sound verify-or-abstain envelope — the author shows that in clue-rich Sudoku these systems are not learned search procedures but one-shot amortized predictors wrapped in an exact verifier: one forward pass commits essentially the whole grid, every hard-slice failure (43/43) is caused by "first-pass poisoning" (a confidently deleted true value), the added branching/MRV/backtracking/nogoods cut wasted wrong-completion derivations 1,497× but leave accuracy unchanged, while two symmetry-based cures both work — train-time digit-permutation augmentation lifts 9×9/25-clue from below 1% to 96.5% ± 0.3% over 3 seeds on a symmetry-disjoint split, and test-time union over symmetry frames lifts the hard-slice checkpoints from 72.8–78.9% to 100% without retraining.

## 核心贡献

1. **一个机制性的解剖（mechanistic anatomy）**：证明在标准 6×6 数独上，单次前向传播就把所有空格坍缩成单一候选（singleton rate 1.000），所谓"迭代推理循环"在测试时纯粹是装饰；系统本质上是一个包裹在精确验证器里的"一次性预测器"。所有失败都可归因于"首遍投毒"（first-pass poisoning）——第一pass就自信地把正确解的某个值删掉了。完美的列联表（43/43 失败 vs 0/137 干净通过，在不同硬件上重复为 42/42 vs 0/138）证明这是必要且充分条件；单调性引理保证被删的真值无法被后续步骤恢复。

2. **一个有界的负面结果（bounded null）**：作者刻意把 CoLT 的搜索层做得足够强（学习式 branch-policy head、chronological DFS、value exclusion、跨链 nogood ban set），构成一个 2×3 的消融网格 {restart, dfs}×{random, MRV, learned}，在所有线索充足的数独设置下对准确率无可检测的影响（n=180 时 95% 区间半宽达 ±7 pp），但把重复无效推导从 74,864 砍到 50（1,497 倍）。这把"搜索层能买到准确率"的假证伪了。

3. **两个被预测、且都被证实的"治愈方案"**：(a) 训练时数字置换增强，在 9×9/25-clue 上把准确率从低于 1% 提到 96.5% ± 0.3%（3 个种子，symmetry-disjoint split）；(b) 测试时在 K 个对称变换帧上做并集聚合，把 hard slice 的三个 checkpoint 从 72.8–78.9% 全部拉到 100%，无需重训。两者通过同一个"值对称去相关"机制发挥作用。

4. **一套冻结协议 + 仪器（instruments）**：在训练前把数据集（pinned MD5）、预算、基线、成功准则全部冻结在 Git tag `r14-2026-07-15`；CoLT 的约束图注意力偏置（Graphormer 模式）让一个 checkpoint 同时训练 4×4 和 6×6 并零样本迁移到 9×9（仅局部传播器）；附 Claim Audit 把每条因果主张映射到对应的测量工件。

## Key Contributions

1. **Mechanistic anatomy of sound lattice reasoning.** A single forward pass commits essentially the entire grid (singleton rate 1.0 on standard 6×6, 0.956 on the hard slice), so the "iterative deduction" loop is cosmetic at test time and LDT-like systems are one-shot amortized solvers inside a verifier shell. A perfect threshold-insensitive contingency (43/43 failures vs. 0/137 clean, replicated at 42/42 vs. 0/138 on different hardware) attributes every failure to first-pass poisoning, and a symmetry argument (Proposition 1) shows the exact depth-0 target contains no elimination signal under value symmetry — explaining why the loop has nothing to iterate.

2. **A bounded negative result for learned search.** CoLT deliberately instantiates a strong CDCL-inspired subset (learned branch-policy head with dense free supervision from LDT's α-operator, decision-stack DFS with value exclusion, a cross-chain ban set of verified leaf-level nogoods), each independently switchable into a 2×3 ablation grid from one checkpoint. In every clue-rich Sudoku setting it has no detectable effect on accuracy (the 95% interval half-width reaches ±7 pp near 50% at n=180) while cutting wasted wrong-completion derivations by three orders of magnitude (74,864 → 50 on the hard slice). The anatomy explains the null mechanistically: search cannot help when nothing is left to branch on.

3. **Two predicted, both-confirmed cures via symmetry calibration.** Train-time digit-permutation augmentation lifts 9×9/25-clue accuracy from below 1% to 96.5% ± 0.3% over three training seeds on a provably symmetry-disjoint split; test-time union over symmetry-transformed frames lifts all three hard-slice checkpoints from 72.8–78.9% to 100% without retraining (they compose to 180/180 at 9×9). The two cures act on the same value-symmetry mechanism from complementary sides.

4. **Frozen-protocol instruments.** Datasets, budgets, baselines, and success criteria were committed before any training (immutable tag `r14-2026-07-15`); CoLT's constraint-graph attention bias, normalized coordinates, and value-slot padding remove all size-specific parameters so one checkpoint trains 4×4+6×6 and transfers zero-shot to 9×9 (local propagator only). A claim-audit appendix maps each causal claim to its committed raw JSON.

## Methodology

**Lattice state.** A CSP with C cells and V values is a multi-hot state x ∈ {0,1}^{C×V}; deduction moves monotonically down the candidate lattice (cells only ever lose candidates), ⊥ = any state with an empty cell. The training target via the abstraction operator is ŷ = x ⊓ α({y ∈ Y : y consistent with x}), which collapses to ⊥ exactly when no known solution survives — the conflict-head supervision.

**CoLT = LDT envelope + three CDCL-inspired additions.** CoLT keeps LDT's lattice, step operator, asymmetric-BCE losses (w+=4.0, w−=0.5 so false eliminations cost 8× more — the soundness-preserving bias), pool training, and verify-or-abstain emission, and changes only: (1) who chooses the branch cell — a learned per-cell policy head πi supervised for free by the branch-survival probability p* derived from the α-operator machinery; (2) what happens on conflict — chronological DFS with value exclusion plus a per-puzzle, cross-chain ban set of verifier-rejected complete grids; (3) how structure enters the network — a relational constraint-graph attention bias (the Graphormer pattern over the CSP constraint graph), normalized-coordinate MLP features, and value-slot padding to Vmax=9, removing all size-specific parameters so one model serves any board size/box shape.

**Frozen protocol.** Datasets are deterministic generated unique-solution Sudoku (sudoku4: 4×4 minimal-clue 1700/300; sudoku6: 6×6 box-2×3 20-clue 1020/180; plus a held-out 9×9/25-clue Phase-4 split), byte-identical to the author's public LDT reimplementation. A symmetry-exact leakage audit enumerates the full symmetry group per grid (3,359,232 cell transforms at 9×9): 9×9 splits are clean at every level, while 6×6 admits no symmetry-disjoint split (≈50 essentially different grids exist), so class-level generalization rests entirely on 9×9. Budgets are matched to LDT baselines (32 chains × 60 rounds at 4×4/6×6; 64×200 at 9×9). Comparisons are internal (learned-vs-learned at matched neural budget), since a classical backtracking solver closes all test sets at median 0.017–0.21 ms/puzzle.

## Results & Highlights

- **6×6 standard slice (CoLT vs LDT baseline, frozen 32×60 budget):** CoLT solves 180/180 [97.9, 100.0] Wilson 95% CI across all six search×policy arms vs. the LDT reproduction's 89/180 = 49.4% [42.2, 56.7], a +50.6 pp gap resolved at this n; median rounds-to-solve is 1 (vs. 3 for LDT) with zero suppressed wrong completions. Soundness is 100% in every cell. Over seeds 42/43/44 full-CoLT scores 99.4 ± 0.6%.

- **Hard slice (6×6, 14 clues, zero-shot, identical accuracy/budget):** DFS + nogood ban set cut wasted wrong-completion derivations 1,497× (74,864 → 50) at identical 76.1% accuracy across all six arms — a pure efficiency result. A budget sweep (5/15/60 rounds) leaves all accuracies unchanged at 76.1%, showing the puzzle population bifurcates: trivially solvable by propagation, or unsolvable by any arm.

- **First-pass poisoning contingency (the central diagnostic):** On the hard slice, all 43 failures and only the failures are first-pass poisoned — a perfect 43/43 vs. 0/137 dichotomy, replicated on seeds 43/44 at 49/49 vs. 0/131 and 38/38 vs. 0/142, and on GPU hardware at 42/42 vs. 0/138. Lowering θ_elim ∈ {0.10, 0.05, 0.02} has zero effect (the propagator confidently zeroes true values, σ(b) < 0.02), proving calibration must come from training or ensembling, not thresholds.

- **9×9/25-clue head-to-head (64×200, 3 seeds, symmetry-disjoint split):** Without augmentation CoLT solves at most 1/180 (0.2%, 0–0.6%) and first-pass poisons 179–180/180; the LDT reimplementation without augmentation also scores 0% (suppressing 1.84M wrong completions). Adding a single exact symmetry (random digit permutation per sampled trajectory) lifts CoLT to **96.5% ± 0.3% (96.1–96.7%)** at 100% soundness, median 1 forward pass per solve, zero suppressed completions — a regime-wall removal driven by commit precision rising from 0.584–0.589 to 0.998–0.999.

- **Test-time union cure (no retraining):** K=8 union over random digit-permutation frames on the unaugmented checkpoint drops first-pass poisoning from 23.9% to 1.7% and lifts hard-slice accuracy from 76.1% to 100%; across seeds 42/43/44 it closes all three hard-slice checkpoints from 72.8–78.9% to 100%. Mean aggregation reaches only 97.8% (poisoning 16.1%), confirming union is the active ingredient. A frame-type factorial confirms value-specific bias dominates (digit frames → 180/180; identity frames leave all 43 poisonings; geometry-only → 163/180).

- **From-scratch graph coloring (capability boundary, §8):** This is the only regime where one-shot amortization vanishes (singleton rate 1.4% at c=3.0 vs. 100% on Sudoku) and search changes accuracy: DFS beats restart by +11.4 pp at c=3.0 (single seed) and +2.9 pp pooled over three policy-grid seeds (positive in every seed). The solvable region ends between c=3.25 and c=3.5 while the unsatisfiable fraction is still only 7% — i.e., the neural amortizer's boundary is far tighter than the classical solver's (a backtracking solver closes all sweep instances at median 0.66–1.31 ms).

- **One checkpoint, multiple boards (§5.5):** A single multi-task checkpoint matches single-size checkpoints (6×6: 99.4% vs. 98.9% = one puzzle in 180); local elimination transfers zero-shot to 9×9 (precision 0.977 at recall 0.597, above the frozen bar) but global heads (conflict, policy) do not (conflict AUC 0.50 = chance), and a base-rate control shows most of the transfer is just relearned constraint propagation.

- **Crossing attempts at c=4.0 (one-axis-at-a-time):** Search-side resources buy almost nothing (12× inference budget → +1.9%, no-learning stub → 0%), while training-side resources dominate — 4× denser solution sampling (K=48) lifts accuracy 0 → 15.2%, 10× compute → 9.5%.

## Critical Assessment

The paper's intellectual honesty is its most distinctive feature and what makes the negative result credible. The author **deliberately built CoLT's search layer strong** (learned policy head, DFS, value exclusion, nogood ban set, with a 2×3 ablation grid from one checkpoint) so that a null on accuracy would be decisive rather than a strawman, and then reports that null plainly: in clue-rich Sudoku the search layer is accuracy-inert because the propagator amortizes the entire solution in one pass. The mechanism is nailed down by a near-perfect first-pass-poisoning contingency (43/43 vs. 0/137, replicated on multiple seeds and hardware) plus a monotonicity lemma (poisoned values can never be restored) and a symmetry proposition (the exact depth-0 target carries no elimination signal under value symmetry). Two interventions *predicted by this diagnosis* both confirm — train-time digit-permutation augmentation (<1% → 96.5% ± 0.3% on a symmetry-disjoint 9×9 split) and test-time union over symmetry frames (76.1% → 100% on the hard slice, no retraining) — with a frame-type factorial isolating digit relabeling as the dominant de-poisoning factor and a 2×2 isolating the structure×augmentation interaction. The boundary study (§8) is admirably self-aware: it sides with critics of physics-inspired GNN solvers for from-scratch random CSPs, shows DFS buys accuracy only there (+2.9 pp pooled), shows the learned policy never separates from hand-coded MRV (criterion 2 fails in strict form), and proves via Proposition 1 that the deduction target itself supplies no signal at depth zero. Throughout, every causal claim is mapped to a committed JSON artifact (Appendix A).

The most important caveat — and the one the author is most upfront about — is that **the LDT baseline row omits LDT's own digit-permutation augmentation** (flagged as the reproduction's top deviation), so the headline +49.5–50.6 pp gap measures CoLT against LDT-minus-its-calibration-mechanism rather than against the full published LDT recipe; the author explicitly states "the decomposition above, not the gap, is the finding." Second, the 6×6 tier admits no symmetry-disjoint split at all (≈50 essentially different grids), so 6×6 generalization is within-symmetry-class by construction and class-level claims rest entirely on the clean 9×9 splits. Third, the AI-assisted execution concentrates residual risk in the analysis scripts, which is why the revision protocol re-runs the central probes in a single environment.

## Strengths

- **Mechanistic explanation that ties together every result:** one-shot amortization → first-pass poisoning → symmetry calibration as the cure is a single causal chain confirmed by complementary train-time and test-time interventions on two boards, with a perfect contingency and a proved proposition as bookends.
- **Deliberately strong negative result with a real instrument:** CoLT is built to falsify, not strawman, the "learned search buys accuracy" hypothesis, and the 2×3 ablation plus budget sweeps make the null (1497× efficiency, 0 pp accuracy) hard to dismiss.
- **Exceptional reproducibility posture:** frozen pre-training protocol, immutable Git tag, pinned-MD5 data, symmetry-exact leakage audit, clean-room independent verifier (25,231 cases, zero disagreements), three-seed replications, cross-host replication, claim audit.
- **Intellectual honesty about failures:** falsified frozen criteria (e.g., criterion 2: learned policy never beats MRV; criterion 5: global heads fail to transfer) are reported as failures, not buried.
- **Genuinely useful boundary study (§8):** the from-scratch graph-coloring sweep is the one place search matters, and it directly motivates the proposed division of labor (exact propagation/verification classical; learned components only where classical solvers delegate to heuristics).

## Limitations

- **The headline accuracy gap is against an augmentation-stripped LDT baseline,** not the full published recipe; the honest framing is that the decomposition (graph-bias sufficiency, augmentation-as-calibration) is the finding, not the gap itself.
- **6×6 admits no symmetry-disjoint split** (only ≈50 essentially different grids exist), so all 6×6 numbers are within-symmetry-class by construction; class-level generalization rests entirely on 9×9 splits.
- **Several cells are single-seed** (the 2×2 factorial, transfer probes, boundary densities other than c=3.0), with repetition deferred to a revision protocol (E3); training-seed variance in the boundary regime is large (5–32% at fixed data).
- **CDCL-style conflict analysis is structurally unavailable** because the neural propagator is a black box offering no explanation of which inference caused a conflict; nogoods are limited to whole verified-wrong grids. Leaf-level is the strongest sound nogood such a system admits.
- **GRAM baseline is budget-starved by 10–100×** relative to its published recipe, so the GRAM row carries no information about GRAM's capability ceiling — only about what verify-or-abstain buys.
- **Global heads (conflict, policy) do not transfer across board sizes** (conflict AUC 0.50); only the relational local propagator transfers, and most of that transfer is just relearned constraint propagation (a base-rate control cuts precision from 0.977 to 0.33).
- **Generated unique-solution Sudoku is not the Sudoku-Extreme corpus** used in LDT's headline 100%/100% claim, so cross-paper number comparisons are out of scope.
- **Boundary study (§8) uses modest instance size** (n=40); Maze-style multi-solution tasks with K in the hundreds remain untested.

## Relevance (中文)

这篇论文对"神经推理系统到底在做什么"这一核心问题给出了一个非常硬核的诊断，对多个方向都有直接价值：

- **对神经符号推理 / SAT-CSP 神经求解器社区**：它澄清了一个普遍误解——带 verify-or-abstain 的"迭代推理"Transformer 在线索充足时其实是一次性摊销预测器，搜索层只省算力、不增精度。这对设计 Searchformer、Stream of Search、GRAM、LDT 这类系统时如何分配"学习式搜索"预算有直接指导意义。
- **对 test-time compute / 搜索与推理 scaling 的讨论**：它给出了"搜索何时有用"的明确边界——在线索充足（clue-rich）的补全任务里搜索是惰性的，只有 from-scratch 生成（如相变点附近的图染色）里 DFS 才真正改变准确率（+2.9 pp pooled）。这为"何时该投入 test-time 搜索"提供了实证依据。
- **对数据增强与对称性校准**：它把"数字置换增强"从 LDT 论文里一个未被重视的实现细节，提升为一个有理论解释（去相关值特异性的错误消除）和测试时对偶（union over symmetry frames）的校准机制，并证明了二者通过同一机制可叠加。
- **对 AI 辅助科研的可信度讨论**：论文坦率披露全部由 Claude 系列自主 agent 完成，并以冻结协议、独立 clean-room 验证器、claim audit 等机制回应可靠性担忧，本身就是 AI-for-science 治理的一个范例。
- **方法学借鉴价值**：冻结-在-训练-前的协议、symmetry-exact leakage audit、把每个因果主张映射到 JSON 工件的 claim audit，是任何想"证伪自己假设"的实证 ML 论文的范本。

## Questions for Further Exploration

1. **Exact value-permutation equivariance（结构化的对称性不变性）是否能在不靠数据增强的前提下，把 9×9 的 wall 一次性消除？** 论文将其冻结为修订协议中的开放问题：exact equivariance 在 clue-rich 任务里是否"免费"（clue 数字仍需通过 context 打破对称性）尚未经验证。
2. **Global heads（conflict / policy）能否通过约束图池化（而非 CLS token）实现跨棋盘尺寸的 size-invariant 迁移？** 当前只有局部传播器迁移，全局读出失败（conflict AUC 0.50）。
3. **CDCL 式 conflict analysis 在什么条件下变得可用？** 作者指出这需要传播器变得 exact 且可解释（如 lazy clause generation），从而把 nogood 从"整张验证失败的网格"提升为"通用子句"——这是一个设计论证，值得具体实现。
4. **From-scratch CSP（相变附近）的边界能被哪根轴实质推动？** 单轴实验显示监督密度（K=48 → 15.2%）收益最大、推理预算几乎无效；exact-supervision 设计能否移除采样瓶颈是明确的后续工作。
5. **Learned policy 在长搜索链 regime 下能否真正超过手写 MRV？** 当前严格准则 2 失败（learned ≈ MRV），需要构造一个既有长 sound 搜索链又非 from-scratch 的中间 regime 来公平测试 policy head。
6. **多解（K≫1）任务上的扩展性如何？** 当前 K>1 的 α-operator 路径只在固定图的小规模图染色上被验证，Maze 式 K 达数百的任务尚未触及。
