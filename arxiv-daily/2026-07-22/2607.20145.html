SLAI T-Rex: Full-Parameter Post-training of the DeepSeek-V4 Family on Ascend SuperPOD

Dongfang Li, Xiaodong Luo, Ruoyu Sun, Xuhui Chen, Linyuan Qiu, Jian Meng, Zhengxuan Lu, Yiting Wang, Yucheng Xie, Tao Guo, Tianxiang Fang, Jing Li, Sihang Chen … et al. — AI Training Platform Team, Shenzhen Loop Area Institute (SLAI)
PART I 2026-07-22 · first-stage practice technical report
Key Finding

Full-parameter post-training of the 1.6T DeepSeek-V4 MoE on Ascend SuperPOD reaches 34.22% MFU, a 2.93× gain over baseline — while an OR-specialized Flash model hits 71.81% average Pass@1.

Why This Matters

A rare, detailed engineering study of full-parameter post-training for a trillion-parameter MoE on non-GPU (Ascend) hardware — with concrete kernel, parallelism, and communication recipes.

Critical Edge

Pro-scale 34.22% MFU training is not paired with downstream OR evaluation — the systems and domain halves are validated on different models, and no error bars are reported.

01Methodology

Two tightly coupled halves — one hardware, one data.

A · Ascend SuperPOD Training

target » DeepSeek-V4-Pro, 1.6T · CloudMatrix384 (910C)

Step time decomposed as T_step = T_comp + T_comm + T_bubble + T_idle after profiling 206,503 kernels (39.84s device compute). Three-layer optimization: Expert–Tensor Parallelism (TP=2 attn + full-width MoE via ETP→EP), Virtual Pipeline Parallelism chosen over DualPipeV to fit the 64GB HBM budget, asynchronous AllToAll overlap, a double-buffered swap optimizer, and AuraKernel — an OR-guided AscendC tiling agent with a branchable K-Search tree.

B · OR-Oriented Post-Training

platform » DeepSeek-V4-Flash

Capability-boundary analysis motivates a two-stage design. OR-CPT: a solver-verified synthetic engine renders optimization documents; a domain-dominant mixture (~75% OR) avoids catastrophic forgetting. SFT: a solver-oriented objective targeting feasible + structurally-equivalent Gurobi programs, with a self-distilled modeling flywheel, contract-aware cleaning + CoT enhancement, and progressive SFT anchored to general capabilities. A full CPT–SFT–Deploy–Eval provenance chain tracks confidence.

Strengths

  • End-to-end story measured on a real 1.6T-parameter workload, not microbenchmarks.
  • Exceptionally detailed kernel-level profiling with a clean diagnosis → optimization mapping.
  • AuraKernel's OR-guided tiling is a generalizable idea beyond hand-tuned heuristics.
  • Honest negatives: 50K SFT regresses; SFT hurts 5-shot; Pure-OR CPT drops general caps 10–15pp.

Limitations

  • OR results are Flash-only; Pro-scale post-training lacks downstream OR eval.
  • No error bars / multiple seeds for the main benchmark numbers.
  • Strong dependency on proprietary Ascend HW and 2026-era weights limits verification.
  • Kernel gains reported as ratios, not absolute end-to-end wall-clock training times.
  • VPP-vs-DualPipeV left as future work — potential MFU left on the table.

05Open Questions for Further Exploration

  1. How much of the 2.93× MFU gain comes from each layer (parallelism vs swap optimizer vs AuraKernel vs fusion) — is there a marginal-contribution ablation?
  2. Would adopting DualPipeV now that the swap optimizer reclaims HBM change the VPP-vs-DualPipeV trade-off?
  3. Can the OR-guided AuraKernel tiling formulation generalize to CUDA/Triton kernel tuning beyond Ascend?
  4. Why does CPT+SFT regress 5-shot B4O-Feasible below base — a format/prompt mismatch, and can ICL-aware SFT fix it?
  5. Does CPT→SFT transfer generalize beyond OR to other verifiable-reasoning domains (code, theorem proving)?
  6. What are absolute tokens/sec per NPU, for cross-platform comparison with GPU-based trillion-parameter MoE reports?
