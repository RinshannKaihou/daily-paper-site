Key Finding

W1.58A4 becomes practical, not theoretical

TWLA is the first PTQ pipeline that jointly fixes weight→ternary codebook misalignment and activation heavy tails, making ternary weights + 4-bit activations deployable on real hardware.

Key Metric

3.64× speedup, 1.3× over W2A4

On LLaMA-2-13B with llama.cpp ternary kernels, TWLA hits 86.35 tok/s while cutting parameter memory from 23.7 GB to 3.34 GB.

Why This Matters

End-to-end low-bit inference without retraining

W1.58A4 is the bit-budget sweet spot for fast kernels. TWLA needs only 128×2048 WikiText2 tokens and no fine-tuning, lowering the barrier for edge/local deployment.

Critical Edge

Indirect activation smoothing; missing BitNet compare

KOTMS only indirectly suppresses outliers; no head-to-head with BitNet b1.58/v2; throughput claims are llama.cpp-specific and 70B reasoning benchmarks are absent.

1 E2M-ATQ

Euclidean → Manifold Asymmetric Ternary Quantizer absorbs per-row mean bias via W̄ = µ1ᵀ + diag(α)T.

  • Warm-start in Euclidean space for stable ternary pattern.
  • Relocate µ, α on activation second-moment manifold.
  • Closed-form 2×2 linear system per row.

2 KOTMS

Kronecker Orthogonal Tri-Modal Shaping rotates weights toward a ternary-friendly distribution.

  • R = R₁ ⊗ R₂ keeps overhead near zero.
  • Cayley transform preserves exact orthogonality.
  • Same rotation indirectly smooths activation outliers.

3 ILA-AMP

Inter-Layer Aware Activation Mixed Precision solves bit allocation exactly.

  • First-order layer costs + adjacent second-order interactions.
  • Chain-structured quadratic objective.
  • Dynamic programming under global bit budget.

🚨 Critical View

  • No BitNet b1.58 or BitNet v2 comparison, the most direct W1.58A4 competitors.
  • KOTMS activation benefit is indirect and uneven across layers.
  • Throughput measured only on llama.cpp; vLLM / TensorRT-LLM unverified.
  • Missing 70B reasoning benchmarks (MMLU, GSM8K, HumanEval) at A4.

Pipeline Snapshot

Calibration (128×2048 tokens) → E2M-ATQ (15 iters) → KOTMS (100 iters) → ILA-AMP DP over {2,4,6,8} bits → packed ternary weights.

At runtime the Kronecker rotation is absorbed into adjacent projections; only two compact matmuls are applied once per forward pass.

Relevance

Directly relevant to LLM inference acceleration and extreme low-bit PTQ. TWLA shows that W1.58A4 — the bit combination that actually unlocks fast ternary kernels — is reachable without retraining, making local/edge/privacy-sensitive deployment far more realistic.
