🧭Core Idea

Zeta intervenes in the Muon-family update pipeline. Instead of running Newton–Schulz directly on a raw momentum matrix, it first rescales rows and columns so each coordinate has comparable variance. The result is a more isotropic input, which Newton–Schulz can orthogonalize faster and more accurately.

The paper's central claim is that the order is not negotiable: Newton–Schulz convergence depends on near-isotropy, so coordinate whitening is a prerequisite, not an optional preprocessing step.

"Zeta inserts a coordinate-wise whitening step before Muon's Newton–Schulz spectral whitening." — Paper TL;DR

🔬Methodology

  • Coordinate whitening — per-coordinate standardization that restores statistical isotropy.
  • Spectral whitening — Newton–Schulz iteration to approximately orthogonalize the preconditioned momentum matrix.
  • Strict ordering — coordinate whitening runs first; spectral whitening operates only on the better-conditioned output.
  • Parameter split — matrix-shaped weights use Zeta; embeddings, layer norms, and 1-D parameters likely remain on AdamW.

🏆Results & Highlights

  • Language-model scale sweep: 0.6B – 8B parameters.
  • Evaluated on Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) architectures.
  • Tested on vision tasks.
  • Matches or surpasses strong matrix-optimizer baselines on convergence speed and generalization.

🎲Critical Assessment

  • Novelty: Incremental over Muon; the strongest contribution is the ordering-as-necessity argument.
  • Rigor: Non-trivial proof of strict error reduction; empirical case focused on Newton–Schulz-based methods.
  • Reproducibility: Open code; chi-square diagnostic is well-specified.
  • Significance: Potentially a drop-in optimizer upgrade if gains hold at frontier scale (≥70B).
  • Open questions: Cost overhead, low-precision stability (bf16/fp8), and behavior under distributed sharding need inspection.

🔗Relevance

Directly continues the Muon/PSGD/SOAP line of work. For anyone building or tuning large-scale training optimizers, Zeta offers a concrete, theoretically grounded refinement.
