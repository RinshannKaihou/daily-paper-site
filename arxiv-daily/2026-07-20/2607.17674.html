Uncovering Latent Reasoning Strategies in Language Models

Awni Altabaa · John Lafferty — Yale University
latent-variable-models variational-inference interpretability posterior-collapse reasoning-strategies
Key metric

0.95+

Strategy Alignment (Analogical Consistency) on Qwen2.5 0.5B / 1.5B — at Distributional Fidelity ≈ 0.95–1.0.

ELBO baseline: ~0.3 (chance)  ·  504-run diagnostic grid

Why this matters

Makes entangled LLM reasoning strategies explicit, sampleable, and intervenable — directly enabling RLVR strategy coverage, reasoning audits, and strategy-level intervention.

Critical edge

All evidence is on six synthetic algorithmic tasks; the leap to open-ended reasoning (GSM8K, MATH) is acknowledged as open — and baselines are near-strawman ELBO / β-ELBO only.

01

Methodology

· 方法

One autoregressive Transformer (initialized from base θ) plays three roles sharing LoRA-adapted parameters φ: a router r(z|x), a generator g(y|x,z) that injects z as a pseudo-token, and a training-time posterior q(z|x,y) discarded at inference. Responses are sampled from the frozen base model itself — no strategy labels enter the optimization.

Rθ = (1/cθ) · Ez~q[ Σt aθ,t · (−log g(yt|x,z,y<t)) ]
cθ · global scale
Base-model reference; E[Rθ]=1 ⇒ reconstruction reads as fractional information gain.
aθ,t · token weight
Surprisal-directed (γ concentration, α mixture). Focuses gradient on branch points where pθ is uncertain.
Key insight
Branch points = where strategy choices are made. Uniform ELBO wastes capacity on already-determined tokens.
Sweep
β ∈ {1, 0.1, 0.01} × {constant, linear-warmup}; latent dim 64; inverse-surprisal negative control.
02

Results & Evidence

0.75–1.0linear probe acc on UNFACTORIZED base
~0.3ELBO strategy alignment (chance)
0.85–0.95+model-directed alignment
~1.0distributional fidelity held
  • Strategy is implicit in pθ — linear probes on the unmodified base reach ~0.75–1.0 across six tasks.
  • ELBO/β-ELBO fail — fidelity ≈ 1.0 but alignment stuck near chance; router KL collapses. The latent is provably available but never exposed.
  • Inverse-surprisal ablation drops alignment — the direction of pressure matters, not just non-uniformity.
  • Latent geometry separates into task/strategy regions; in multi-task settings, strategies are linearly separable given task identity.
  • Robust across random-init (504 runs) and pretrained Qwen2.5 0.5B / 1.5B.

Authors & Credibility

04

Strengths

  • Crisp problem framing — distinguishes “factorize a realized distribution” from “fit a latent model,” with a precise collapse diagnosis.
  • Principled objective — base-normalized Rθ has genuine fractional-information-gain semantics (E[Rθ]=1); surprisal weighting has an information-theoretic rationale.
  • Well-matched benchmark — parseable traces, held-out labels, cross-input analogical-consistency metric (not just probe accuracy).
  • Mechanism-level evidence — phase-space dynamics and token-weighted-loss tracking support the causal story.
  • Theoretical appendix — population recovery theorem with explicit margin conditions.
05

Limitations

  • Synthetic only — no GSM8K / MATH / HumanEval / MBPP; generalization to open-ended reasoning unaddressed.
  • Weak baselines — no cyclical annealing, Free-bits, InfoVAE, lagging-inference, SAE feature steering, or concept bottlenecks.
  • Partly by construction — deterministic trace rendering may inflate Analogical Consistency vs. natural-language traces.
  • Theory decoupled — complexity proxy C(r,g) never instantiated; oracle criterion not tied to SGD dynamics.
  • Scale — only 0.5B/1.5B; continuous Gaussian latent only; no downstream application demonstrated.
06

Open Questions for Follow-up

  • Does model-directed reconstruction recover meaningful latents on open-ended benchmarks (GSM8K, MATH, HumanEval) where traces aren’t parseable?
  • How does it compare to established posterior-collapse remedies (Free-bits, InfoVAE, lagging inference) and SAE / concept-bottleneck alternatives on the same benchmark?
  • Can the recovered z drive strategy-directed RLVR exploration — sampling under-represented strategies to broaden the reachable reasoning distribution?
  • Is a continuous Gaussian latent right, or would discrete/categorical codes better match “strategy identity”?
  • Does it scale beyond 1.5B, and does the working β transfer to frontier-sized models?
  • Can the complexity proxy C(r,g) be instantiated (e.g. LoRA-norm / router-entropy) so the theorem yields a computable certification?
