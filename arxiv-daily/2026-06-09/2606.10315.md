> **一句话总结：** 本文在生产级多轮点单agent（Lumivate）上以详尽人工审核为真值，发现内置LLM-as-judge的运营放行门仅捕获不到1/4的人确认系统性问题（一批22%，一批0/23），且盲点是结构化的——judge能抓到单轮表层缺陷，但系统性漏掉跨轮状态类缺陷（确认门锁死、购物车幻觉、升级锁死、过期指代），原因是评分rubric缺少state/guardrail/recovery轴且放行门未与rubric相连。

> **TL;DR:** Studying a deployed multi-turn F&B ordering agent (Lumivate) with exhaustive human transcript review as ground truth, the authors find the built-in LLM judge's operational gate surfaces well under a quarter of human-confirmed systematic problems — 2/9 (22%) on batch B1 and 0/23 defects (0%) on batch B2's 100 rounds. The miss is structured: the raw judge emits a suspected_bug note on 125/220 rounds (57%) and notices confirm-gate/cart-state defects, but 113/114 of those notes are filed under "brand voice" (the only loose axis), and none become gate failures because the shipping gate is wired only to hangs/hard assertions. Recall degrades monotonically with how "cross-turn" a defect is.

## 核心贡献

- **真值化而非一致性的recall度量**：首次以详尽人工转录审核为真值，在生产多轮交易agent上度量LLM judge的运营门recall（而非与人工评分的一致性），发现远低于1/4。
- **结构化盲点分类法**：盲点不是随机的——judge可靠捕获单轮表层问题（捏造统计、错误语言），但系统性漏掉需要会话弧推理的跨轮状态问题；recall随所需上下文宽度单调下降。
- **可定位的失败机制**：raw judge其实经常注意到缺陷（220轮中125轮发出suspected_bug），失败在于路由——rubric只有intent/brand_voice/personalization三轴，无state/guardrail/recovery/safety类别，故确认门违规被记为"brand voice"（113/114），且放行门架构上不读rubric（仅由hang/hard assertion触发失败）。
- **Rogan–Gladen退化的尖锐警告**：当门的表观prevalence为零时，Rogan–Gladen估计器退化——无法恢复真prevalence；而非静默情况下则隐含3–6×低估。

## Key Contributions

- **Recall, not just agreement**: Measures the operational gate's recall of human-confirmed systematic problems on a deployed multi-turn transaction agent (a per-tenant Singapore coffee-brand ordering agent), rather than judge–human agreement — finding well-under-a-quarter recall across three review batches.
- **Structured blind-spot taxonomy**: The blind spot is structured rather than random. Turn-local, surface-checkable defects (fabricated statistic, wrong-language reply) are caught; cross-turn-state defects (confirm-gate lockout, cart hallucination, escalation lockout, stale referents) are systematically missed; recall degrades monotonically with required context width.
- **Pinned-down mechanism (routing, not perception)**: The raw judge notices the defect on a majority of defective rounds (125/220 suspected_bug notes, 114 describing confirm-gate/cart-state), but the rubric has no state/guardrail/recovery/safety axis so 113/114 are filed "brand voice", and the shipping gate (`live_passed`) is architecturally disconnected from the rubric (fails only on hangs/hard assertions) — so 0/220 became gate failures.
- **Prevalence-correction contract with a degenerate case**: Applies the Rogan–Gladen estimator; its headline finding is the degenerate point — when the gate's apparent prevalence is zero (papp=0), the correction recovers nothing, a stronger statement than any finite 3–6× multiplier; in the non-silent regime a 10% gate rate implies 29–56% true prevalence.

## Methodology

**System under study.** A per-tenant brand agent that takes food-and-drink orders in natural language over multi-turn conversations: searches a menu, builds/mutates a cart, answers product questions, applies dietary constraints, and places orders behind an explicit confirmation gate. The evaluation tenant is a Singapore coffee brand with a bilingual (English/Mandarin, code-switching) customer base.

**Corpus.** A randomized multi-turn corpus generated by a loop driver sampling scenario family (e.g. the_usual, deep_dietary, large_group, cancel, slot_conflict), locale (en-SG/zh-SG), identity mode (anonymous/verified-with-favourites), and depth of 5–9 user turns. Spans 34 scenario families, balanced 50/50 across locales, conversation depths T5–T9 (median latency 73 s, p95 192 s per round).

**Built-in judge.** Per loop round, an LLM judge scores the transcript against a rubric emitting three capability axes — intent, brand_voice, personalization — each a pass/fail bit with free-text reason, plus an optional free-text suspected_bug note. Axis scores feed an auto-export regression ledger (a case recorded when an axis scores 0; auto-promoted to a fixture on recurrence). Separately, the loop emits the single operational gate verdict `live_passed` — the output that actually ships and blocks. The two are architecturally disconnected: `live_passed` flips to fail only on an operational failure (exception/no response) or a hard per-turn assertion, never on a low quality score.

**Ground truth.** Exhaustive human transcript review: an analyst reads full turns plus tool calls and records a pattern only when a behaviour recurs across multiple rounds, families, or locales. In the largest batch (B3), an adversarial multi-reviewer workflow — one reviewer per family, then a skeptic verifier prompted to refute each finding before synthesis — raised 147 suspected issues, of which 85 were adversarially confirmed (22 explicitly refuted).

**Method.** Per batch, compares the set of human-confirmed problems against the set the judge flagged: (i) pattern recall; (ii) distribution of confirmed problems over behavioural dimensions vs. the judge's rubric axes; (iii) blind-spot taxonomy keyed on single-turn vs. conversation-arc decidability. A parallel blind double-annotation on an 80-round sample (judge scores stripped) measured inter-annotator agreement.

**Prevalence correction.** Applies the Rogan–Gladen estimator (Rogan & Gladen 1978) π̂ = (papp + sp − 1)/(se + sp − 1) to de-bias gate-reported prevalence.

## Results & Highlights

- **B1 (R166–R196, 9 patterns)**: Operational gate auto-captured 2/9 human-confirmed patterns = 22% pattern recall (Wilson 95% CI [6.3%, 54.7%]).
- **B2 (R166–R265, 100 deep rounds)**: Operational gate failure rate (`live_passed`) = 0%; human review confirmed 23 distinct defects and promoted 7 new patterns. Gate recall 0/23 = 0% (rule-of-three 95% upper bound 13%; Wilson [0%, 14.3%]); round-level 0/100 gives upper bound 3.7%.
- **B3 (R1–R160, post-fix)**: Adversarial review raised 147 suspected issues, 85 adversarially confirmed (22 refuted), establishing 14 further patterns (P17–P30), several regressions of B1/B2 fixes the gate did not surface.
- **Raw-vs-gate split (current 220-round corpus)**: Raw judge emitted a suspected_bug on 125/220 rounds (57%); 114 describe confirm-gate/cart-state defects; 113/114 (99%) scored under brand_voice; 0/220 became operational gate failures. Pooling both corpora (220 + 381), 269 responded-with-a-quality-note rounds produced zero gate failures.
- **Defect distribution**: Of 30 confirmed patterns, 15/30 (50%) — every state-tracking (8), guardrail (3), recovery (2), and safety (2) pattern — fall into dimensions the judge's rubric has no category for. The judge's own 74-entry ledger is distributed brand_voice 43, intent 28, personalization 3.
- **Inter-annotator agreement (80-round blind sample)**: Cohen's κ=0.61 on defect presence (raw agreement 90%, 72/80, n=80, bootstrap 95% CI [0.33, 0.83]); κ=1.0 on turn-local-vs-cross-turn class for the 8 co-identified defects. The two annotators flagged overlapping but non-identical sets (15 vs. 9); 7 of 8 disagreements were a genuine defect one annotator caught and the other missed.
- **Rogan–Gladen degenerate case**: On B2 (papp=0) the estimator recovers π̂≤0 regardless of se/sp — a silent gate licenses no estimate of true prevalence. In the non-silent regime, a 10% gate rate with se≈0.18–0.22 implies 29–56% true prevalence (3–6× undercount).

## Critical Assessment

- **The headline B2 result (0/23) rests on a single analyst plus the multi-occurrence promotion rule.** The authors are unusually honest about this: the parallel blind double-annotation (κ=0.61, two annotators flagging 15 vs. 9 defects) shows two careful humans each under-detect, so the single-analyst ground truth is a conservative floor — but a re-annotation of the exact B2 rounds with full disagreement reconciliation is explicitly listed as a residual camera-ready step. The companion methodology paper means this hardening propagates to two results.
- **Per-batch sample sizes are small.** B1 has n=9 patterns (Wilson CI [6.3%, 54.7%]); the authors deliberately avoid collapsing to a single clean number and report Wilson intervals rather than bare points, but the wide intervals mean the precise recall is not pinned.
- **The ≈18% pooled running estimate mixes grains.** It pools auto-captured vs. human-confirmed patterns across loop batches rather than being computed on one batch; the authors flag it only as an order-of-magnitude anchor, never a precise rate. The Rogan–Gladen 3–6× correction similarly substitutes a pattern-level se into a formula requiring turn-level sensitivity — an admitted granularity mismatch making it directional, not calibrated.
- **External validity is bounded to one tenant.** All data come from one F&B ordering agent on one bilingual tenant, and the corpus is adversarially loop-sampled (over-sampling cross-turn-heavy families like deep_dietary/slot_conflict/cancel) rather than organic traffic — which biases toward the gate's worst-case defect classes. The authors claim the shape generalizes, not the precise percentages; post-pilot replay on live traffic is future work.
- **sp (specificity) is unmeasured.** A calibrated Rogan–Gladen point estimate needs a dedicated human-labeling pass (N≈200 turn-level triples) that is left to camera-ready, so the only robust quantitative takeaway is the degenerate-case warning, not any finite multiplier.

## Strengths

- Measures recall (not agreement) against exhaustive human review on a real deployed system — answering the question that matters for shipping software.
- Pins the failure to a concrete, fixable mechanism (rubric-axis mismatch + gate disconnected from rubric) rather than vague "judge is biased".
- Exceptionally honest about threats to validity: explicitly reports that the single-analyst ground truth is a floor, that sp is unmeasured, that sampling biases toward cross-turn defects, and that small-n intervals are wide.
- The Rogan–Gladen degenerate-case argument is a clean, strong theoretical contribution: a silent gate licenses no estimate of true prevalence.
- Concrete, testable fix proposals (arc-level judging; rubric axes for state/guardrail/recovery/safety wired to the gate) stated as falsifiable hypotheses.

## Limitations

- Single-tenant F&B domain; exact recall values will differ elsewhere and on organic traffic.
- B2 (the marquee 0/23 result) rests on a single analyst; full multi-annotator reconciliation is a residual camera-ready step.
- Per-batch n is small (B1 n=9); precise recall is bounded by wide Wilson intervals, not pinned.
- sp unmeasured, so the 3–6× Rogan–Gladen multiplier is order-of-magnitude, not calibrated; turn-level se also not measured.
- The corpus over-samples cross-turn-heavy families, so turn-local share (and gate recall) would be higher on organic traffic — the prevalence undercount of §5.6 would shrink.

## Relevance

本文直接映射到「LLM Inference Evaluation」研究兴趣——量化生产agent中LLM-as-judge作为质量门的recall与盲点结构，并给出Rogan–Gladen去偏的退化警告。同时与「LLM Training Stability & Observability」相关：揭示了评估管道的结构性失败（rubric轴缺失、放行门与质量信号断连），属于部署期可观测性的可靠性问题。

## Questions for Further Exploration

- The paper predicts (§7) that adding rubric axes alone recovers the labeling of guardrail failures but little of the recall of cross-turn ones (which need the arc-level unit) — has the ablation experiment (extend rubric, re-judge a human-labeled batch) been run to falsify or confirm this?
- Given the raw judge already notices the defect on 125/220 rounds, what is the latency/cost trade-off of the cheapest proposed fix — a tool-augmented judge calling the same `review_cart`/state accessors the agent does and asserting consistency?
- The κ=0.61 (n=80) inter-annotator agreement was pre-reconciliation; how much does a fully reconciled double-annotation change the B2 denominator, given that 7 of 8 disagreements were genuine misses by one annotator?
- Does the degenerate-case Rogan–Gladen warning (silent gate ⇒ no prevalence estimate) generalize to other production gates that are wired only to operational failures, as the authors argue it is immune to sampling-bias direction?
- The corpus over-samples cross-turn families — would the turn-local-vs-cross-turn blind-spot ratio invert on organic traffic, and would that change the practical "regression floor, not substitute" conclusion?
