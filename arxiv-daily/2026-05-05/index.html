DGPO: Distribution-Guided Policy Optimization for Fine-Grained Credit Assignment

Hongbo Jin, Rongpeng Zhu, Zhongjing Du & 4 others  ·  Peking University · SJTU · Tsinghua

DGPO 用有界 Hellinger 距离替代无界 KL 散度，将 GRPO 的序列级信用分配升级为 token 级，在 AIME 2024 上达到 60.0%（超越 DAPO 10pp），仅增加 3.6% 训练开销，无需额外价值网络。 DGPO upgrades GRPO's coarse sequence-level credit assignment to fine-grained token-level via bounded Hellinger distance, achieving 60.0% on AIME 2024 (+10 pp over DAPO) with only 3.6% training overhead and no value network.

Anna Sokol, Marianna B. Ganapini, Nitesh V. Chawla  ·  Notre Dame

对抗性对话树（ADT）揭示：2026 旗舰模型能抵抗社会压力，但在元论证攻击下崩溃；"友好"人设消融证明抵抗力是安全对齐产物而非真正认知基础。 Adversarial Dialogue Trees reveal that 2026 flagship LLMs resist social pressure but collapse under meta-argumentative attacks; persona ablation proves epistemic stability is a guardrail artifact, not genuine cognition.
