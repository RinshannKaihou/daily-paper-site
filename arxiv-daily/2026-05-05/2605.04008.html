Methodology

The authors employ the SegResNet architecture, a widely-used 3D segmentation network, and train it using automatic mixed-precision training on brain MRI data. The Dice loss function serves as the training objective, and performance is evaluated via the Dice similarity coefficient across three tumor sub-regions defined by the BraTS challenge: tumor core (TC), whole tumor (WT), and enhancing tumor (ET).
