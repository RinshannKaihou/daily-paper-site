> **一句话总结：** 本文将Walsh-Hadamard变换旋转与基于激活能量的列重缩放应用于LLM权重矩阵的W2A16量化，通过将量化误差预算重新分配给高谱能量通道，在135M至1.5B参数的四个模型上实现15-58%的wikitext-2困惑度降低，并针对Qwen3注意力、RoPE位置编码和MoE架构分别设计了兼容方案。

> **TL;DR:** Applies a fixed Walsh-Hadamard rotation Q plus a closed-form per-coordinate spectral-energy diagonal scale D (computed from a single calibration forward pass) to LLM weight matrices before Intel auto-round quantization. The transformation is exactly math-invariant at full precision (verified to < 5×10⁻⁷ relative error). At W2A16 (group size 64), reduces wikitext-2 perplexity by 15–58% across SmolLM-135M/360M and Qwen2.5-0.5B/1.5B. Includes three architecture-specific extensions: (1) per-head spectral-PCA with asymmetric MatrixGammaRMSNorm for Qwen3's q_norm/k_norm (PPL 136.76→88.99), (2) SO(2) per-RoPE-pair PCA for non-norm architectures (PPL 36.93→21.84 on Qwen2.5-1.5B), (3) MoE-aware input-side absorption fix discovered via architectural fuzzing on Laguna-XS.2. A W4 ablation shows the benefit vanishes within noise floor, confirming the method is specific to extreme low-bit regimes. All checkpoints deploy via OpenVINO IR on Intel NPU/Arc/CPU with device-invariant PPL.

## 核心贡献

- 提出一种基于Walsh-Hadamard变换（WHT）旋转 + 逐通道谱能量缩放的预处理流程（BBT-spectral），用于W2A16权重量化，全精度下数学恒等（相对误差 < 5×10⁻⁷）
- 在SmolLM-135M/360M和Qwen2.5-0.5B/1.5B四个模型上实现15-58%的wikitext-2困惑度降低，收益与MLP宽度比（d_ff/d_h）正相关而非参数量
- 设计per-head spectral-PCA（非对称MatrixGammaRMSNorm）解决Qwen3注意力中q_norm/k_norm的兼容问题（PPL 136.76→88.99）
- 设计SO(2) per-RoPE-pair PCA解决非norm架构中旋转与RoPE的冲突（PPL 36.93→21.84 on Qwen2.5-1.5B）
- 通过Laguna-XS.2架构模糊测试发现并修复MoE中g_proj门控的输入侧吸收bug，验证了方法的泛化性
- W4消融实验作为负面对照，确认方法收益仅存在于极低位宽场景（W4偏差仅+0.06 PPL，在噪声范围内）

## Key Contributions

- A drop-in spectral-basis pre-quantization pipeline: WHT-rotate weight matrices, rescale columns by per-coordinate Walsh-basis activation energy s_ℓ = (1 + d_pad · ρ_ℓ)^α (α = 0.5), then hand off to Intel auto-round. Exactly math-invariant at full precision (verified to relative error < 5×10⁻⁷).
- Empirical validation across four decoder-only LLMs (SmolLM-135M/360M, Qwen2.5-0.5B/1.5B) at W2A16 g=64: 15–58% wikitext-2 PPL improvement over vanilla auto-round, with largest wins on models with wider MLP ratios (d_ff/d_h = 5.4–5.8 for Qwen vs. 2.67 for SmolLM).
- Per-head spectral-PCA (Route A) for Qwen3-style attention: replaces q_norm/k_norm with asymmetric MatrixGammaRMSNorm (Γ_h = diag(γ)U_h), preserving RoPE compatibility — PPL 136.76 → 88.99 on Qwen3-0.6B.
- SO(2) per-RoPE-pair PCA for non-norm architectures: constrains rotation to commute with RoPE's 2D rotation structure, improving three of four models by 17–22% over input-WHT BBT-spectral.
- MoE-aware input-side absorption fix: architectural fuzzing on Laguna-XS.2 (33B MoE) uncovered a g_proj gate pattern that leaked per-channel scaling through softplus nonlinearity; fix is one line (extend absorption allowlist from {q,k,v}_proj to {q,k,v,g}_proj).
- W4 deliberate negative control: redistribution payoff falls within ±0.5 PPL noise floor at W4, consistent with Schur-convexity intuition that per-channel reallocation value vanishes with shrinking noise budget.

## Methodology

The method applies a single math-invariant transformation to each linear layer's weight matrix before quantization, factored as an orthogonal rotation Q and a diagonal scale D:

**x → x_pad · Q · D⁻¹**, **W^T → D · Q^T · W^T_pad**

where Q = H_{d_pad} / √d_pad (Walsh-Hadamard, orthogonal) and D = diag(s) with s_ℓ = (1 + d_pad · ρ_ℓ)^α, α = 0.5.

The spectral-energy proxy ρ_ℓ is computed from calibration activations:
- Run a single forward pass to collect input activations x
- Compute h = H_{d_pad} · x_pad / √d_pad (WHT of zero-padded input)
- ρ_ℓ = E[h_ℓ²] / Σ_k E[h_k²] (per-coordinate Walsh-basis activation energy)

High-ρ_ℓ columns are boosted before quantization, giving them proportionally more grid resolution from the per-group INT2 quantizer (auto-round SignRound, group size 64). At dequantization, Q and D are unfolded, preserving the mathematical identity.

**Architecture-specific extensions:**

1. **Spectral-PCA (Qwen3 attention):** For models with post-projection q_norm/k_norm, standard input-side WHT breaks RMSNorm. Fix: eigendecompose per-head output covariance C_h = U_h Λ_h U_h^T, rotate per-head row blocks of W_q/W_k by U_h^T, replace q_norm with asymmetric MatrixGammaRMSNorm (Γ_h = diag(γ)U_h) that restores vanilla outputs before RoPE. The symmetric alternative Γ_h = U_h^T·diag(γ)·U_h breaks RoPE (PPL ≈ 18,886).

2. **Pair-PCA (RoPE-compatible):** For architectures without q_norm, the rotation must commute with RoPE's 2D rotation planes (i, i+d_h/2). Constrained PCA computes 2×2 covariance per (head, RoPE-pair), eigendecomposes to U_{h,p} ∈ SO(2) (forcing det = +1), and applies rotation to corresponding weight row-pairs.

3. **MoE input-side absorption:** For Laguna-style fused-expert layouts, identified via synthetic architectural fuzzing that g_proj (input-side attention gate) must be included in the absorption allowlist, since softplus((W_g · x)/s) ≠ softplus(W_g · x)/s.

**Scale-exponent ablation (α):** Sweeping α ∈ {0, 0.25, 0.5, 1.0} on Qwen2.5-0.5B shows the fixed rotation (α=0) captures ~10.3% of the gain; per-channel scaling adds the rest. α = 0.5 is near the empirical optimum (α ≈ 0.25–0.5); aggressive scaling (α=1) is clearly worse.

## Results & Highlights

- **W2A16 headline results** (wikitext-2, auto-round SignRound, g=64, enable_alg_ext=False):

|-------|--------|-------------|-------------------|---|
| SmolLM-135M | 135M | 81.03 | 45.11 | −44.3% |
| SmolLM-360M | 362M | 43.21 | 36.55 | −15.4% |
| Qwen2.5-0.5B | 494M | 119.31 | 50.22 | −57.9% |
| Qwen2.5-1.5B | 1.54B | 36.93 | 28.16 | −23.7% |

- **Pair-PCA additional gains** over BBT-spectral: SmolLM-360M 36.55→30.34 (−17%), Qwen2.5-0.5B 50.22→40.15 (−20%), Qwen2.5-1.5B 28.16→21.84 (−22%)
- **Qwen3-0.6B spectral-PCA:** 136.76 → 88.99 (−35%)
- **TinyLlama-1.1B pair-PCA:** PPL 16.0 (vanilla baseline unavailable due to VRAM constraints)
- **W4 negative control:** Qwen2.5-1.5B W4 vanilla PPL 9.65 vs. BBT pair-PCA 9.71 (Δ = +0.06, within noise floor)
- **Hardware deployment:** All checkpoints exported via OpenVINO IR; PPL invariant across Intel NPU, Arc B580 dGPU, and CPU within ±0.1; Arc B580 dominates throughput at 131–222 tok/s for 1.5B models

## Critical Assessment

- **Novelty**: Moderate-to-high. The core idea — separating the orthogonal factor Q (fixed WHT) from the adaptive diagonal factor D (spectral-energy scaling) — is a clean design-space contribution. The insight that per-channel adaptation can be recovered in closed form via D rather than learned via Q is non-obvious and well-motivated. The architecture-specific fixes (asymmetric MatrixGamma for q_norm, SO(2)-constrained PCA for RoPE) show genuine engineering depth.

- **Rigor**: Strong within its self-imposed scope. The W4 negative control is excellent methodology. Full-precision invariance checks with explicit numerical floors, bit-equivalence regression tests for the g_proj fix, and the separable Q/D factorization discussion show rigorous thinking. However: single-seed results, 8,192-token evaluation subset (not full wikitext-2 test set), and no FP16 reference column are notable gaps.

- **Reproducibility**: Good. The transformation is described as a closed-form recipe with no per-model tuning (α = 0.5 fixed). Pinned evaluation stack (transformers 4.57.6, torch fp16, auto-round 0.12.2). Reference implementation at boolean_fourier/bbt_quant/. Missing: public GitHub link (only a path is given).

- **Significance**: Potentially significant for W2 deployment scenarios, but limited by (1) model scale (max 1.5B — no evidence of transfer to 7B+), (2) no comparison against the strongest W2 baselines (QuIP#, AQLM, OmniQuant), and (3) evaluation only on wikitext-2 perplexity without downstream task benchmarks. The design-space analysis (Q vs. D factorization) is a valuable conceptual contribution regardless.

- **Red flags**: (1) No head-to-head benchmarks against any competing method at W2 — the comparator is only vanilla auto-round. (2) Scale limited to 1.5B; the author acknowledges this. (3) The theoretical link to Boolean influence is explicitly disclaimed as qualitative, which somewhat weakens the framing but is honestly handled. (4) ButterflyQuant code was unavailable at submission time, preventing the most direct comparison. (5) Perplexity evaluated on only 8,192 tokens of wikitext-2 (a hardware convenience), not the standard full evaluation.

## Strengths

- Clean, math-invariant recipe with no per-model hyperparameter tuning
- Exceptional intellectual honesty: explicit disclaimers, deliberate negative control, honest framing of limitations
- W4 ablation as negative control strengthens the scientific narrative — the method does not "magically improve everything"
- Architecture-specific fixes demonstrate practical engineering depth and generalize beyond the tested models
- Thorough math-invariance verification with explicit numerical error floors for each variant
- Separable Q/D factorization framework provides a clean design-space vocabulary for comparing rotation-based methods
- Architectural fuzzing methodology (Laguna-XS.2) is a good practice for catching generalization bugs cheaply
- Hardware deployment portability concretely demonstrated across three Intel platforms with throughput numbers

## Limitations

- No comparison against strong W2 baselines (QuIP#, AQLM, OmniQuant, ButterflyQuant) — acknowledged as future work
- Model scale capped at 1.5B parameters; scalability to 7B+ is unknown
- Evaluation on wikitext-2 only (8,192-token subset), no downstream task benchmarks (MMLU, ARC, HellaSwag, GSM8K)
- Single-seed results with ±0.5 PPL noise floor; no multi-seed statistical significance testing
- No FP16 reference perplexities reported; gap-to-FP16-closure metric absent
- The theoretical grounding (Boolean influence → real-valued spectral energy) is explicitly qualitative, not formal
- TinyLlama-1.1B vanilla baseline unavailable (hardware limitation), creating a gap in the evidence
- Some throughput degradation on certain BBT variants (Qwen2.5-0.5B pair-PCA: 24% GPU slowdown)

## Relevance

该方法将Walsh-Hadamard谱旋转与逐通道能量缩放分离为正交因子Q和对角因子D的设计空间，为极低位宽（W2）LLM量化提供了新范式。对在资源受限设备（Intel NPU/Arc）上部署大模型具有直接工程价值，其Q/D分离框架也为理解现有旋转量化方法提供了清晰的概念工具。
