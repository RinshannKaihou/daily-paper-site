Linear & Spline Steering Are Degenerate Special Cases of Riemannian Geodesics

A GAGA encoder trained on output-Hellinger distances (no per-prompt labels) is the only method that reliably drives Llama-3.1-8B to target classes across all four arithmetic tasks — waypoint legibility ≥ 99%, endpoint target probability 1.4–5.7× the best baseline.

Why This Matters
Geometry-aware steering without per-prompt labels makes LLM behavior control more principled and practical.
Critical Edge
Fidelity collapses on large output spaces (letters, ages). Single model, single seed, small corpora only.

Methodology

Activations h₀, h₁
Llama-3.1-8B, L28, d=4096 → Riemannian Metric g
flat / spline / GAGA → Geodesic Solver
L-BFGS, K=50 waypoints → Steered Path
evaluated via EBC, legibility
  • Steering reframed as geodesic computation on activation space ℝ4096 under metric g.
  • Flat g=I recovers linear steering; JsTJs recovers spline steering — both degenerate cases.
  • GAGA encoder: MLP 4096→1024→512→256→128→k (BatchNorm + ReLU). Three losses: distance-matching Ldist, reconstruction Lrecon, cycle Lcycle.
  • Two supervision modes: GAGA-PHATE (diffusion distances) and GAGA-Out (Hellinger on output distributions). GAGA-Out wins.
  • Metric ↔ solver decoupled: any metric pairs with any compatible solver (closed-form, L-BFGS, amortized bridge).
ⓘ Four tasks: weekdays (|Z|=7), months (|Z|=12), letters (|Z|=22), ages (|Z|≈91) — all 1-D conceptual domains on Llama-3.1-8B.

  • Surrogate validity: GAGA-Out ≈ analytical GF pullback on weekdays (identical EBC 0.0085, legibility 1.000).
  • PCA(64) encoders are completely inert — 15 ablation variants all match the linear baseline. Only raw-4096 representation works.

Strengths

  • Elegant unification: prior baselines become special cases, not competitors.
  • Metric↔solver decoupling = clean, extensible design space.
  • Label-free supervision removes a major practical barrier.
  • Honest reporting: the output-space-size split is presented with sharp paired-t statistics.
  • 15-variant PCA ablation definitively isolates the raw-4096 requirement.

Limitations

  • Output-space-size puzzle: fidelity degrades on letters & ages — intrinsic or fixable is unresolved.
  • 1-D tasks only: extension to tori, graphs, hierarchical structures is claimed but not shown.
  • Small corpora: weekdays = 49 facts × 21 paraphrases; effective sample complexity unclear.
  • Single model + layer: Llama-3.1-8B at layer 28 only.
  • Schema ≠ fully unsupervised: concept-token vocabulary is a fixed input requiring domain knowledge.

Questions for Further Exploration

  1. Can the output-space-size fidelity gap close with multi-seed training or full-vocabulary supervision instead of concept-token schemas?
  2. How does the framework extend to multi-dimensional concept manifolds (grids, hierarchical ontologies)?
  3. Does the raw-4096 requirement scale tractably to 70B+ models?
  4. Can GeodesicBridge produce on-manifold curves matching L-BFGS quality?
  5. Could UMAP or spectral methods recover steering in lower dimensions, unlike PCA(64)?
