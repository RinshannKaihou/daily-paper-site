Strengths
  • Elegant geometric formulation provides clear intuition for PoT failures
  • O(N) analytical solver eliminates gradient-based optimization entirely
  • Cross-modal validation: ViT + LLM + 28nm RTL synthesis
  • Dual-mode (GEO/REF) for data-free vs. calibration scenarios
  • Strictly multiplier-free with silicon-level timing proof
Open Questions
  • How does ORP scale to 70B+ models?
  • Can Strided Dual-Exchange generalize to mixed-precision codebooks?
  • Sensitivity of smoothing parameter α across architectures?
  • Interaction with speculative decoding?
  • Is GEO-mode OOD robustness consistent for multilingual / code-gen?
Critical Edge
  • Paper marked "In preparation" — results may change before peer review
  • 4-bit PPL (5.09) slightly trails AWQ (5.00) on 13B — advantage concentrates at 3-bit
  • No evaluation on models larger than LLaMA-2-13B or newer architectures (Mixtral, LLaMA-3)
  • LLM activation quantization limited to ≥8-bit; W3/A3 shown for ViTs only
