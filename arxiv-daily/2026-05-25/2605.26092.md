> **一句话总结：** 本文提出ORP框架，通过正交残差投影将量化重新建模为双基几何投影问题，在纯移位加法（无乘法器）的硬件约束下实现了3-4 bit量化Transformer的高保真部署，LLaMA-2-7B校准仅需约15分钟。

> **TL;DR:** OrpQuant formulates quantization as a dual-basis geometric projection to fix the Low Angular Resolution Regime of Power-of-Two lattices at sub-4-bit. It achieves multiplier-free shift-and-add inference with competitive accuracy (PPL 6.10 on LLaMA-2-7B at W3/A16), ~15 min calibration, and 2.85 GHz clock at 28nm.

## 核心贡献

- **揭示PoT量化的几何缺陷：** 首次形式化了Power-of-Two量化格点在超低比特下存在的"低角分辨率区间"（Low Angular Resolution Regime），指出性能退化根源在于向量方向的角偏差，而非仅是幅度失配。
- **提出正交残差投影（ORP）框架：** 通过Gram-Schmidt式扩展构造与主基正交的次基 $b_2$，将量化误差从1D标量修正提升为2D几何修复，且严格限定在移位加法操作域内。
- **O(N)解析求解器：** 利用Strided Dual-Exchange定理保证离散基的正交性，避免了梯度优化或穷举搜索，LLaMA-2-7B全模型校准仅需约15分钟。
- **算法-硬件协同设计验证：** 28nm RTL综合显示，并行移位加法数据通路的临界路径延迟为0.35 ns（理论频率2.85 GHz），能效达3.33 TOPS/W。

## Key Contributions

- Formalizes the **Low Angular Resolution Regime** inherent to Power-of-Two lattices, identifying angular deviation—not scalar mismatch—as the primary degradation source at sub-4-bit.
- Proposes the **Orthogonal Residual Projection (ORP)** framework: a dual-basis geometric projection (primary PoT basis + orthogonal secondary basis) that corrects angular distortion while staying within shift-and-add hardware constraints.
- Introduces the **Strided Dual-Exchange Theorem** guaranteeing orthogonality of discrete bases via index permutation and sign flips, enabling an O(N) closed-form basis search with no gradient optimization.
- Achieves **~15 min full-model calibration** for LLaMA-2-7B using only vanilla PyTorch operators, compared to 32+ min (AWQ) or 358 min (SpinQuant).
- Demonstrates **28nm RTL synthesis** results: 0.35 ns critical path delay, ~2.85 GHz theoretical clock, 3.33 TOPS/W energy efficiency under W3/A8.

## Methodology

ORP decouples quantization into three stages:

1. **Manifold-Aligned Preconditioning:** Channel-wise smoothing scales weights by $\mathbf{s}_{\text{vec}} = \mathbf{a}_{\max}^{\alpha} \odot \mathbf{w}_{\max}^{1-\alpha}$ to align the Euclidean weight space with the empirical feature manifold, prioritizing high-activation channels.

2. **Geometric Discrete Basis Search:**
   - **Primary projection** maps preconditioned weights to an asymmetric 3-bit PoT lattice $L_{3\text{bit}} = \{-1, -0.5, -0.25, -0.125, 0, +0.25, +0.5, +1\}$ (explicit zero-state for hardware zero-skipping).
   - **Orthogonal residual extraction** via Gram-Schmidt: $\mathbf{r}_\perp = \mathbf{w}_{\text{proc}} - \frac{\langle \mathbf{w}_{\text{proc}}, \mathbf{b}_1 \rangle}{\|\mathbf{b}_1\|^2}\mathbf{b}_1$.
   - **Strided Dual-Exchange** constructs $\mathbf{b}_2$ by permuting indices and flipping signs within micro-blocks (G=32), with analytical sign inference $\eta_i^* = \text{sign}(x_i r_{\perp,j} - x_j r_{\perp,i})$ and constant-time stride enumeration over 16 candidates. The orthogonal property $\langle \mathbf{b}_1, \mathbf{b}_2 \rangle = 0$ is guaranteed by Theorem 1.

3. **Joint Scale Optimization:**
   - **GEO mode** (data-free): Solves a diagonalized 2×2 least-squares system (cross-terms vanish due to orthogonality), yielding $c_1, c_2$ via simple scalar projections.
   - **REF mode** (activation-aware): Incorporates calibration data via Ridge regression $\mathbf{c}^* = (\mathbf{A}^T\mathbf{A} + \lambda\mathbf{I})^{-1}\mathbf{A}^T\mathbf{Y}$ with Tikhonov regularization ($\lambda = 10^{-4}$).

Hardware deployment uses hierarchical block architecture: continuous scales $(c_1, c_2)$ are shared across macro-blocks (N=128) to bound memory, while $\mathbf{b}_2$ is constructed at micro-block granularity (G=32).

## Results & Highlights

| Configuration | Model | Metric | ORP | Best Baseline |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| W3/A16 | LLaMA-2-7B | PPL | **6.10** | 6.49 (AWQ) |
| W4/A16 | LLaMA-2-13B | PPL | **5.09** | 5.00 (AWQ) |
| W4/A4 | ViT-Base | Top-1 Acc | **79.54%** | 68.48 (RepQ-ViT) |
| W3/A8 (28nm) | Hardware | TOPS/W | **3.33** | 3.31 (AWQ) |
| W3/A8 (28nm) | Hardware | Delay | **0.35 ns** | 0.50 ns (AWQ) |

- **Calibration speed:** ORP completes LLaMA-2-7B calibration in 15.1 min (vanilla PyTorch, no CUDA), vs. AWQ's 32.1 min and SpinQuant's 358 min.
- **Ablation (K=1→K=2):** Dual-basis Log2 at 3-bit (PPL 6.10) outperforms single-basis Linear quantization (PPL 6.68), proving geometric correction beats brute-force compute.
- **Zero-shot reasoning:** At W3/A16 on LLaMA-2-7B, GEO mode maintains 67.72% on WinoGrande; REF mode reaches 68.35%, trading slight OOD generalization for in-domain precision.

## Critical Assessment

- **Novelty: High.** The geometric reframing of PoT quantization as a Low Angular Resolution problem and the Strided Dual-Exchange Theorem providing guaranteed discrete orthogonality are genuinely novel contributions. The O(N) analytical solver is a meaningful advance over gradient-based and search-based calibration.
- **Rigor: Moderate-High.** Theoretical proofs (Theorem 1) are sound. Experiments cover multiple model families (LLaMA-2, ViT, DeiT, Swin) and include RTL synthesis. However, the paper lacks comparisons with recent methods like QuIP# at 2-bit and does not evaluate on larger models (e.g., LLaMA-3, 70B-scale).
- **Reproducibility: Moderate.** Uses only vanilla PyTorch (no custom CUDA), which is a plus. However, the 28nm RTL synthesis results require specialized EDA tools not widely available. The asymmetric lattice $L_{3\text{bit}}$ encoding details could be clearer.
- **Significance: High for edge deployment.** If the hardware claims hold, a 2.85 GHz multiplier-free accelerator at 28nm is compelling. The 15-min calibration time is practically transformative for on-site edge adaptation.
- **Red flags:** (1) The paper is marked "In preparation," so results may change. (2) The 4-bit results show ORP slightly behind AWQ on PPL (5.09 vs 5.00 for 13B), suggesting the advantage concentrates at 3-bit. (3) No activation quantization below 4-bit for LLMs (W3/A3 for LLMs is absent), limiting claims about "ultra-low bit" to weights only.

## Strengths

- Elegant geometric formulation that provides clear intuition for PoT quantization failures.
- O(N) analytical solver eliminates the need for gradient-based optimization entirely.
- Cross-modal validation (ViT + LLM) with hardware synthesis confirmation.
- Dual-mode (GEO/REF) deployment flexibility for data-free vs. calibration scenarios.
- Strictly multiplier-free datapath with demonstrated silicon-level timing benefits.

## Limitations

- 4-bit performance is competitive but not superior to MAC-intensive baselines like AWQ.
- No evaluation on models larger than LLaMA-2-13B or on newer architectures (Mixtral, LLaMA-3).
- LLM activation quantization is limited to ≥8-bit; W3/A3 results are only shown for ViTs.
- The macro-block group size (N=128) introduces quantization granularity that may not suit all layer types.
- "In preparation" status means the work has not undergone peer review.

## Relevance

本文与边缘端LLM部署和高效推理高度相关。ORP框架通过纯移位加法实现3-bit量化，显著降低硬件时序瓶颈，为资源受限设备上运行大模型提供了一条可行路径。其对量化误差的几何解释也具有方法论上的启发意义。
