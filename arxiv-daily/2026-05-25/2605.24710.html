The Four Theorems

Theorem A

Entropy-regularized mean-field Langevin equation under μP has a unique global solution. Finite-particle approximation converges at O(N⁻¹) via synchronous coupling — no independent quantization estimates needed. Maximal admissible weight w* defined as reciprocal moment-growth boundary.
Theorem B
Identifiability
