Quantization Impact on the Accuracy and Communication Efficiency Trade-off in Federated Learning for Aerospace Predictive Maintenance

arXiv: 2604.08474v1

Date: 2026-04-09

Tags: [federated-learning, quantization, predictive-maintenance, aerospace, FPGA, C-MAPSS, RUL-prediction, edge-computing, communication-efficiency]

Summary / 摘要

English

This paper investigates how symmetric uniform gradient quantization (at bit-widths $b \in \{32, 8, 4, 2\}$) affects the accuracy-communication trade-off in federated learning (FL) for aerospace turbofan engine Remaining Useful Life (RUL) prediction. The author proposes AeroConv1D, a lightweight 1-D CNN with only 9,697 parameters designed for FPGA inference, and evaluates it on the NASA C-MAPSS benchmark (FD001 and FD002 subsets) under realistic Non-IID client partitioning with a rigorous 10-seed evaluation protocol.

Key findings:

  1. INT4 achieves accuracy parity with FP32. On both FD001 ($p=0.341$ for MAE, $p=0.802$ for NASA score) and FD002 ($p=0.264$ for MAE, $p=0.534$ for NASA score), INT4 quantization produces results statistically indistinguishable from full-precision training while delivering an $8\times$ reduction in gradient communication cost (37.88 KiB to 4.73 KiB per round).
  1. IID partitioning inflates apparent quantization benefits. A critical methodological contribution: naive IID client data partitioning artificially suppresses variance, making quantization appear beneficial. Under correct Non-IID evaluation, the relationship resolves to statistical parity, not dominance. This is a warning for practitioners who evaluate under IID assumptions but deploy under heterogeneous conditions.
  1. INT2 is unsuitable for safety-critical regression. INT2 exhibits fundamentally different behavior across subsets -- MAE degrades on FD001 (+8.6%, $p=0.018$) but paradoxically improves on FD002 (-20.2%, $p=0.001$). The latter is identified as an over-regularization artifact from the 3-level quantization grid $\{-\alpha, 0, +\alpha\}$, which forces conservative predictions rather than genuine learning. Critically, INT2 shows catastrophic NASA score instability (CV = 45.8% on FD002 vs. 22.3% for FP32), rendering it operationally unusable.
  1. FPGA feasibility. Analytical projections on the Xilinx ZCU102 show INT4 fits within hardware constraints at 85.5% DSP utilization, leaving 366 spare DSPs that could potentially host an NTT-based homomorphic encryption co-processor for a complete FL pipeline on a single SoC.

Strengths: The paper's strongest contribution is the rigorous methodological critique of IID vs. Non-IID evaluation in FL quantization studies, supported by a well-designed 10-seed experimental protocol with proper statistical testing (paired t-tests). The direct IID vs. Non-IID comparison is compelling evidence. The FPGA projection, while analytical-only, provides a concrete path toward deployment. The open-source codebase enhances reproducibility.

Weaknesses: The study is limited to two of four C-MAPSS subsets. FPGA projections remain unvalidated on physical silicon. The gradient-distortion privacy proxy ($\mathcal{L}_{\text{priv}}$) is acknowledged as heuristic rather than a formal differential privacy guarantee. The paper evaluates only symmetric uniform quantization, leaving more sophisticated schemes (QSGD, SignSGD, FedHQ) for future work. The model (AeroConv1D) is custom-designed for this study, making it difficult to assess how findings transfer to other architectures. With only 9,697 parameters, the model is very small -- whether INT4 parity holds for larger models in aerospace settings remains an open question.

中文

本文研究了对称均匀梯度量化（位宽 $b \in \{32, 8, 4, 2\}$）对航空涡扇发动机剩余寿命（RUL）预测联邦学习系统中精度-通信权衡的影响。作者提出了 AeroConv1D，一个仅有 9,697 个参数的轻量级一维 CNN，专为 FPGA 推理设计，并在 NASA C-MAPSS 基准（FD001 和 FD002 子集）上，采用现实的 Non-IID 客户端数据划分和严格的 10-seed 评估协议进行了实验。

主要发现：

  1. INT4 与 FP32 精度相当。 在 FD001（MAE $p=0.341$，NASA 分数 $p=0.802$）和 FD002（MAE $p=0.264$，NASA 分数 $p=0.534$）上，INT4 量化产生的结果与全精度训练在统计上不可区分，同时将梯度通信成本降低了 $8\times$（从 37.88 KiB 降至 4.73 KiB/轮）。
  1. IID 数据划分会夸大量化的表观收益。 这是一个重要的方法论贡献：朴素的 IID 客户端划分会人为地抑制方差，使量化看起来更有利。在正确的 Non-IID 评估下，量化与全精度之间是统计对等关系，而非优势关系。这对在 IID 假设下评估但在异构条件下部署的实践者是一个警示。
  1. INT2 不适用于安全关键型回归任务。 INT2 在两个子集上表现出截然不同的行为——FD001 上 MAE 显著恶化（+8.6%，$p=0.018$），而在 FD002 上反而改善了（-20.2%，$p=0.001$）。后者被识别为 3 级量化网格 $\{-\alpha, 0, +\alpha\}$ 导致的过度正则化伪影，迫使模型做出保守预测而非真正学习退化模式。关键问题在于 INT2 的 NASA 分数极度不稳定（FD002 上 CV=45.8% vs. FP32 的 22.3%），使其在操作层面不可用。
  1. FPGA 可行性。 在 Xilinx ZCU102 上的分析性预测表明 INT4 在 85.5% DSP 利用率下满足硬件约束，剩余 366 个 DSP 可用于潜在的 NTT 同态加密协处理器，实现在单个 SoC 上完成完整 FL 流水线。

优点： 本文最强的贡献是对 FL 量化研究中 IID 与 Non-IID 评估的严格方法论批判，辅以精心设计的 10-seed 实验协议和适当的统计检验（配对 t 检验）。IID 与 Non-IID 的直接对比提供了有力的证据。FPGA 投影虽为分析性，但提供了具体的部署路径。开源代码增强了可复现性。

不足： 研究仅限于 C-MAPSS 四个子集中的两个。FPGA 预测未在实际硬件上验证。梯度失真隐私代理仅为启发式指标而非正式的差分隐私保证。仅评估了对称均匀量化，未涉及更复杂的方案（QSGD、SignSGD、FedHQ）。AeroConv1D 模型为本研究专门设计，发现向其他架构的可迁移性有待验证。仅 9,697 参数的模型规模很小，INT4 对等性在更大规模模型上是否成立仍是开放问题。
