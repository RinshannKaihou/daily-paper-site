Date: 2026-04-09

Sinkhorn Doubly Stochastic Attention Rank Decay Analysis

Authors

Michela Lapenna (University of Bologna), Rita Fioresi (University of Bologna), Bahman Gharesifard (Queen's University)

Summary (English)

This paper studies the rank collapse phenomenon in Transformer self-attention when attention matrices are normalized to be doubly stochastic via the Sinkhorn algorithm, comparing this with the standard Softmax row-stochastic normalization.

Theoretical contributions:

  • The authors derive norm bounds on the residual matrix (distance from rank-one) for pure self-attention networks with Sinkhorn-normalized attention. The key result (Theorem 2) shows that for a multi-head, multi-layer self-attention network SAN(X), the residual decays as:

$\|\text{res}(\text{SAN}(X))\|_2 \leq \left(\frac{\lambda \beta H}{\sqrt{n^3 d_{qk}}}\right)^{\frac{3^L - 1}{2}} \|\text{res}(X)\|_2^{3^L}$

This confirms that, like the Softmax case (Dong et al., 2021), pure Sinkhorn self-attention converges to rank one doubly exponentially with depth. The proof relies on a first-order Taylor approximation of the Sinkhorn operator via an orthogonal projection Q onto the tangent space of doubly stochastic matrices, and uses the proper spectral norm (rather than the composite l_{1,infty} norm used in prior work).

Empirical contributions:

  • Experiments on AG's News (text classification), MNIST, and Cats & Dogs (image classification) show that Sinkhorn doubly stochastic normalization mitigates rank collapse compared to Softmax row-stochastic normalization. This advantage is particularly pronounced when skip connections are present.
  • An ablation with randomly generated (uncorrelated) stochastic matrices reveals that the rank-preservation advantage disappears, suggesting the phenomenon is linked to correlations between attention matrices across layers in trained Transformers.

Critical assessment:

  • The theoretical bound itself is not surprising: it mirrors the doubly exponential decay already known for Softmax. The main novelty is confirming this structural property extends to doubly stochastic attention.
  • The empirical gap between Sinkhorn and Softmax is clear in attention matrix products (Section 4.1), but becomes ambiguous in the full self-attention output SAN(X) (Section 4.2), where Sinkhorn's advantage is only visible on the AG's News dataset. The authors are transparent about this limitation.
  • The random-matrix ablation (Appendix G) is a thoughtful addition that highlights the role of inter-layer correlations, though understanding these correlations is left entirely to future work.
  • The paper does not address computational cost comparisons or whether Sinkhorn normalization improves downstream task accuracy; the focus is strictly on structural rank properties.

摘要 (Chinese)

本文研究了使用 Sinkhorn 算法将注意力矩阵归一化为双随机矩阵时，Transformer 自注意力中的秩坍缩（rank collapse）现象，并与标准 Softmax 行随机归一化进行了对比。

理论贡献：

  • 作者推导了纯自注意力网络中残差矩阵（到秩一的距离）的范数界。核心结果（定理 2）表明，对于多头多层自注意力网络 SAN(X)，残差以双指数速率衰减。这与 Dong 等人 (2021) 对 Softmax 的已知结论一致。证明依赖于 Sinkhorn 算子的一阶泰勒近似，通过正交投影 Q 到双随机矩阵空间的切空间来实现，并使用了真正的谱范数（而非先前工作中使用的组合 l_{1,infty} 范数）。

实验贡献：

  • 在 AG's News（文本分类）、MNIST 和 Cats & Dogs（图像分类）上的实验表明，Sinkhorn 双随机归一化相比 Softmax 行随机归一化能有效缓解秩坍缩，尤其在有跳跃连接时优势更明显。
  • 随机（无相关）随机矩阵的消融实验表明秩保持优势消失，说明该现象与训练后 Transformer 中层间注意力矩阵的相关性有关。

评价：

  • 理论界本身并不令人意外，因为它与已知 Softmax 的双指数衰减形式一致。主要贡献在于确认这一结构性质可推广到双随机注意力。
  • Sinkhorn 在注意力矩阵乘积上的优势清晰，但在完整自注意力输出 SAN(X) 中优势不明确，作者对此做了诚实的讨论。
  • 随机矩阵消融实验是有价值的补充，但层间相关性分析留给了未来工作。
  • 论文未涉及计算成本对比或下游任务准确率提升，严格聚焦于结构性的秩性质分析。
