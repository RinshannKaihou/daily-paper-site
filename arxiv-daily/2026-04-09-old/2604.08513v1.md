## 一句话总结

本文提出"语义漂移"(semantic drift)的概念，揭示在胸部X光多分类任务中，微调(fine-tuning)虽不损害分类精度，却会以架构依赖的方式系统性改变模型预测所依赖的视觉证据结构，且不同归因方法(LayerCAM vs. Grad-CAM++)下的稳定性排名甚至会发生逆转。

## 核心贡献

1. **语义漂移的形式化定义**: 将迁移学习与微调之间归因图(attribution map)的系统性变化定义为语义漂移，并提出一套无参考指标的量化框架（空间位移、重叠IoU、模式相关性、熵变化），不需要像素级标注即可评估解释的跨阶段稳定性。
2. **严格实验控制**: 仅保留在两个训练阶段均被正确分类的真阳性样本（2430/3354），从而隔离解释演化与预测正确性变化的混淆因素。
3. **架构与方法交互效应的实证发现**: 在 DenseNet201、ResNet50V2 和 InceptionV3 上证明，即使分类性能完全收敛（AUC > 99%），不同归因方法下架构稳定性排名可以逆转——LayerCAM 下 InceptionV3 最稳定(IoU=0.777)，Grad-CAM++ 下 DenseNet201 最稳定(IoU=0.690)，而 ResNet50V2 在两种方法下均最不稳定。

## Methodology

The study operates on a five-class chest X-ray classification task (Normal, Pneumonia, Tuberculosis, COVID-19, Lung Opacity) with 16,762 total images. Three ImageNet-pretrained architectures -- DenseNet201, ResNet50V2, and InceptionV3 -- are trained under a two-phase protocol: transfer learning with frozen backbones (epochs 1-10, lr=1e-4), followed by full fine-tuning (epochs 11-20, lr=1e-5). The authors compare epoch 8 (transfer-learning plateau) against epoch 19 (fine-tuning convergence).

Attribution maps are generated via LayerCAM and Grad-CAM++ on penultimate convolutional layers, normalized to [0,1] and thresholded at 0.2. Four reference-free drift metrics are proposed: spatial displacement (center-of-mass movement), overlap IoU (binary mask intersection-over-union), pattern correlation (Pearson between continuous maps), and concentration change (Shannon entropy difference). All metrics are aggregated with inverse-frequency class weighting to handle imbalance.

A key methodological choice is restricting analysis to 2,430 true-positive samples that are correctly classified at both epochs across all three architectures, thereby isolating explanation evolution from changes in predictive correctness.

## Results

- **Predictive performance converges**: All three architectures achieve >99% AUC at epoch 19, with comparable accuracy and F1, demonstrating equivalent classification capability.
- **Coarse localization is preserved, but evidential structure reorganizes**: Spatial displacement remains low (<=0.14) across architectures, yet overlap IoU varies dramatically from 0.383 (ResNet50V2 under Grad-CAM++) to 0.777 (InceptionV3 under LayerCAM).
- **Ranking reversal across attribution methods**: Under LayerCAM, stability ranks InceptionV3 > DenseNet201 > ResNet50V2. Under Grad-CAM++, this reverses to DenseNet201 > InceptionV3 >> ResNet50V2.
- **DenseNet201 exhibits method-robust stability**: DenseNet201 shows minimal cross-method IoU variation (0.699 to 0.690), while InceptionV3 drops sharply from 0.777 to 0.643. The authors hypothesize that dense connectivity regularizes feature reuse during fine-tuning.
- **ResNet50V2 shows pronounced attention redistribution**: Negative concentration change under Grad-CAM++ (-0.516) indicates heterogeneous internal reorganization across cases.

## Critical Assessment

**Strengths:**
- The concept of semantic drift addresses a genuine and underexplored gap: the assumption that improved accuracy implies stable explanations. The finding that stability rankings reverse across attribution methods is both novel and practically important.
- Methodological rigor in controlling for true-positive-only samples and using inverse-frequency class weighting is commendable.
- The paper provides a clear formalization (Eq. 3) that frames explanation stability as a property of the triplet (architecture, attribution method, training phase), not just the model.

**Weaknesses:**
- The study is narrowly scoped to three CNN architectures and two gradient-based attribution methods. No transformer-based models (ViT, Swin, etc.) or perturbation-based explainers (SHAP, LIME, RISE) are evaluated, which limits generalizability.
- The drift metrics quantify consistency, not correctness -- high stability does not mean the model is attending to clinically relevant regions. The authors acknowledge this but it remains a significant limitation for clinical applicability.
- Analyzing only two discrete checkpoints (epochs 8 and 19) misses potentially informative temporal dynamics of evidence evolution across training.
- The dataset (chest X-ray with five classes) is relatively standard; it remains unclear whether these findings extend to other imaging modalities (CT, MRI, pathology) or more fine-grained classification tasks.
- The paper does not propose any mitigation strategy for semantic drift -- it remains a diagnostic observation without a remedy.
- The true-positive filtering (72.5% of test samples) excludes all boundary cases where drift may be most consequential for clinical safety.

## 中文 relevance

本文对医学影像AI的可解释性研究具有重要意义。在实际临床部署中，模型经常需要针对新数据集进行微调，而临床医生依赖模型的解释(如热力图)来判断预测的可信度。本文揭示了一个关键问题：微调可以在不降低分类精度的前提下，悄然改变模型关注的解剖区域，且这种变化取决于架构选择和归因方法，这对于临床AI系统的安全审计和监管合规具有重要参考价值。建议在模型微调后增加解释一致性检查流程，并将语义漂移指标作为模型部署的附加质量维度。然而，鉴于本文仅限于CNN和胸部X光，结论向其他模态和架构的泛化性仍需进一步验证。
