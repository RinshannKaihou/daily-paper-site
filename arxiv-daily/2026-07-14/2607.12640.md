> **一句话总结：** 本文在 4B/8B 的 Qwen3-VL 网页智能体（MiniWoB）上严格证明：在智能体已经掌握的任务上，GRPO 后训练不能可信地提升、甚至在中高学习率下会损害能力；该"零结果"源于任务缺乏 headroom（采样成功率不高于贪心），并给出基于有效秩与权重嫁接的因果机制解释。
>
> **TL;DR:** Across an 18-run grid on a 4B Qwen3-VL MiniWoB web agent, GRPO adds no credible skill over a supervised baseline on mastered tasks (best arm only +3.6 points, paired interval [+0.0, +10.9] touching zero), and mid/high learning rates credibly degrade it (up to −49.1 points); the null is shown to be a headroom property, not a broken pipeline, since the identical recipe gains +22.0 points (interval [+8.0, +40.0]) on tasks where sampling already beats greedy.

## 核心贡献

1. **受控的零结果（Controlled Null）：** 在 4B 文本观测轨道上构建了 18 组运行的网格（扫学习率、KL 权重、种子、初始化、clip 上界），并辅以完整的鲁棒性检验（25 个评测种子、6 个训练种子、warmup+cosine 调度、group size 8/16/32、Set-of-Marks 视觉轨道、扩到 8B）。没有任何配置能在智能体已掌握的任务上可信地超越 SFT 基线，而一旦学习率离开一个很窄的低带，能力单调下降。

2. **正控制（Positive Control）定位因果：** 用完全相同的 harness、reward 与 recipe，只在"采样成功率已高于贪心"的 10 个任务上重训，成功率从 20.0% 提升到 42.0%（+22 点，配对区间排除零）。证明零结果来自任务本身缺乏 headroom，而非训练管线损坏；并给出一个可先用 SFT 策略廉价测量的 headroom gap（采样−贪心）作为是否值得投入 RL 的筛查指标。

3. **学习率门控的双分离机制（Double Dissociation）：** 中等学习率"退化"——摧毁晚期层的有效秩（effective rank，L35 从约 9 跌到接近 1），而早期层完好；高学习率"崩溃"——保持甚至抬高晚期秩，却摧毁了输出 readout（argmax 一致性归零）。两种损伤是不同的病灶。

4. **因果定位（权重嫁接）：** 把 attention、MLP 或 embedding 单独从初始化还原回去后重新评测。退化智能体的损伤可定位到 attention 或 MLP（任一还原即可把 frontier 成功率从 11% 恢复到 37%–40%），而漂移最大的 embedding 反而是因果惰性的——纠正了"按权重移动幅度归因"的常见误区。崩溃则没有任何单一组能修复，是分布式的 readout 损坏。

5. **尺度依赖性：** 在 4B 上，晚期有效秩与能力双向耦合（能力升则秩健康，能力降则秩塌）；但 8B 上秩与 argmax 一致性从不同时下降，故该秩-能力耦合只对小模型成立，作者明确将其限定为 4B 的性质而非跨尺度的定律。

6. **完整统计电池开源：** 配对 McNemar 检验、任务聚类 bootstrap、TOST 等价检验、可解释性探针全部随文发布，供他人复现零结果。

## Key Contributions

- A **controlled null**: GRPO does not credibly add skill to a competent 4B–8B language-and-vision web agent on mastered MiniWoB tasks; higher learning rates credibly remove skill.
- A **positive control** isolating the cause as a **headroom condition**, plus a cheap pre-RL screen (sampled minus greedy success) that predicts both the null and the climb.
- A **mechanistic, learning-rate-gated double dissociation** causally localized by weight grafting, with the dominant embedding drift shown to be a correlational red herring.
- **Scale-dependent evidence**: late-layer effective rank tracks capability at 4B (both directions) but dissociates at 8B.
- A full battery of paired statistics, equivalence tests, and interpretability measurements released for independent verification.

## Methodology

- **Agent & environment:** Qwen3-VL at 4B (and 8B) acting on MiniWoB with a compact action vocabulary (click/type/select/navigate/finish). Two observation tracks share all logic: a serialized text track (used for the grid and the mechanism) and a Set-of-Marks screenshot track (a modality check).
- **Reward:** sparse binary terminal signal `r(τ) = 1[success(τ)] ∈ {0,1}`, broadcast to every step.
- **GRPO recipe:** group mean-centered advantage (no std division, following Liu et al. 2025), asymmetric clip-higher `ε_lo=0.20 < ε_hi=0.28` (DAPO-style), optional KL anchor using the non-negative k3 estimator against the frozen init, linear warmup + cosine decay. The 18-run grid varies learning rate, KL weight β, seed, initialization (SFT or base), and clip bound; ablations add schedule and group size G ∈ {8,16,32}.
- **Evaluation & statistics:** clean greedy decoding on 11 tasks × 5 seeds = 55 matched episodes; Wilson score intervals; **exact paired McNemar** on discordant pairs; **task-clustered bootstrap** 95% interval on ΔSR; **TOST equivalence** within ±δ. "Credibly better/worse" only when the interval excludes zero.
- **Interpretability probes (on a fixed cache of N=1999 hidden states):** effective rank (entropy of normalized covariance eigenvalues), participation ratio, stable rank, argmax agreement with init, mean entropy, degeneracy fraction, and weight-movement magnitude.
- **Causal grafting:** restore one component group (attention / MLP / embedding / random-matched) from initialization, keep the rest, re-evaluate recovery; a 24-draw random-component null calibrates specificity.

## Results & Highlights

- **No credible gain on mastered tasks (4B, text):** SFT baseline = 49.1% (27/55). The best of 18 arms is nominally only +3.6 points with paired interval [+0.0, +10.9] and McNemar p = 0.50–0.69 — indistinguishable from noise. TOST places 4 of the top 5 configs within ±5 points of SFT (one episode-identical).
- **Learning rate is the only dial that moves success, and only downward:** clean success falls monotonically from 52.7% at lr 3e-6, through ~49.1% near 5e-6, to 33.3% at 1e-5, and to 0% at 2e-5. Pooled degrade = −15.0 pp [−20.9, −9.5]; pooled collapse = −49.1 pp [−62.7, −36.4], both excluding zero.
- **Positive control delivers a credible +22.0 points:** on 10 headroom tasks (where sampling already beats greedy), SFT = 20.0% and the best config = 42.0%, interval [+8.0, +40.0], McNemar p = 0.007. Five of six configs climb 12–22 points; the low rate that was a pure no-op on the mastered grid gains 12–18 points here — so the task, not the step size, decides the outcome.
- **Robustness:** 25 eval seeds lower the baseline to 44.7% with no credible winner; the one borderline arm (p=0.001 unclustered) loses credibility once clustered and a 6-seed replication averages 49.7% (back on baseline) — the apparent edge was a lucky training seed. Set-of-Marks raises baseline to 63.6% but yields no credible gain; 8B repeats the pattern.
- **Mechanism (4B, layer 35):** degrade collapses effective rank from ~9 to ~1 while collapse preserves/raises it but drives argmax agreement to zero. Grafting: restoring attention or MLP recovers frontier success from 11% to 37%/40% (above the random-null 95th percentile of 22.9%); restoring the (largest-drift) embedding leaves it at ~14% — causally inert.
- **Scale boundary:** 8B degrade collapses L35 rank to 1.9 yet keeps argmax agreement at 0.954 with intact success; rank and agreement never fall together across the 8B sweep, so the rank-to-capability coupling does not transfer.
- **Not reward hacking:** per-episode taxonomy puts premature-finish near 0% in every regime; degrade is 63% invalid-output and collapse is 98% invalid-output. Best-checkpoint selection does not surface a hidden win.

## Critical Assessment

The study's central methodological insight is the **headroom gap** `Δ_head = SR_sample − SR_greedy` (Eq. 15/16). GRPO optimizes sampled rollouts, so it can only climb toward a policy that sampling already reaches; on the mastered grid greedy sits at 0.49 against a sampled 0.32–0.38, so there is nothing above greedy to move toward, while on the headroom set the gap is positive by construction and the agent climbs. This reframes the contested "does RL add capability or just sharpen" debate (Chen et al. 2026, argued via pass@k on reasoning) into a specific, testable, practitioner-actionable claim for an interactive agent.

The grafting result is the most pointed contribution: the embedding has the **largest raw weight drift** in the grid, so a naive movement-based attribution would blame it, yet grafting proves it is causally inert while attention/MLP carry the damage — and the KL-rescue arm moves the most of all while staying behaviorally at baseline, demonstrating that weight-movement magnitude does **not** predict failure mode. The double-dissociation framing (degrade = localizable late-rank lesion; collapse = distributed readout corruption no single group repairs) is clean and survives at N=1999 states and across three degrade checkpoints.

Two honest boundaries stand out: (1) the credible-harm finding is established only on the 4B text track and is merely nominal under Set-of-Marks (lr 2e-5 gives −10.9 pp with p=0.070, interval touching zero); (2) the rank-to-capability coupling is explicitly a 4B property that breaks at 8B, so the mechanism is scale-scoped rather than universal.

## Strengths

- Tight experimental control: one agent, one environment, one reward, one task set, with every RL knob swept — changes in SR attribute cleanly to the intervention.
- Paired statistics done properly (McNemar on matched episodes, task-clustered bootstrap, TOST equivalence) instead of loose confidence intervals; "credible" is defined conservatively as interval excluding zero.
- The positive control is the decisive design choice: it converts "GRPO doesn't help here" from a possibly-broken-pipeline worry into "GRPO fails exactly where a working GRPO would also fail."
- Causal, not correlational, mechanism via grafting plus a random-component null — directly overturns the movement-based attribution to the embedding.
- Honest scoping throughout: text vs. SoM, 4B vs. 8B, mastered vs. headroom, single-checkpoint noise explicitly flagged.

## Limitations

- **Single benchmark (MiniWoB) and single model family (Qwen3-VL 4B/8B).** MiniWoB is chosen for determinism and exact success checks to enable clean attribution, at the cost of generality; WebArena is explicitly avoided due to run-to-run variation that would confound the mechanism. The null and mechanism may look different on a noisier, more deployment-realistic environment or a different backbone.
- **Credible harm is a text-track result.** On Set-of-Marks the high-rate degradation is only nominal (p=0.070, interval touching zero), so the harm side is not a universal claim.
- **Scale boundary on the mechanism.** The link between late-layer rank and capability holds at 4B but breaks at 8B, where a rank collapse and a behavior change never coincide. The rank account is therefore a property of the smaller model, not a law across scale.
- **Limited checkpoints per regime.** Causal grafting and rank-health readings rest on a small number of checkpoints; point estimates are noisy, so these are reported qualitatively rather than as tight numbers.
- **Scope of the null.** It concerns only the regime where the agent has already mastered its tasks. The authors explicitly make no claim that RL is useless for web agents in general — only that it adds no skill in the competent-agent regime they isolate.

## Relevance

对网页智能体与 RL 后训练实践者高度相关。它给出了一个朴素但可操作的工程判据：在投入 GRPO 算力之前，先在留出集上测量 `Δ_head = SR_sample − SR_greedy`；若 ≤ 0，则应把预算花在监督/蒸馏而非 RL 上（文中蒸馏试点在 RL 无效的任务集上把基线提升了 30 点）。对 4B–8B 小模型 agent 的部署尤其重要：能力天花板更可能由监督质量决定，而非更多 RL。对可解释性研究者，权重嫁接 + 随机组件零分布的因果检验范式，以及"权重移动幅度不预测失效模式"的发现，提供了比纯相关分析更强的归因工具。该工作也把"RL 是否真正扩展能力"的争论从推理任务的 pass@k 延伸到了交互式 agent，并用受控实验给出了明确边界：RL 只在采样策略已优于贪心策略时扩展 agent。

## Questions for Further Exploration

- 该 headroom gap 判据在更接近部署的环境（如 WebArena、真实浏览器任务，存在 run-to-run 噪声）上是否仍能预测 GRPO 的增益？需要怎样的统计修正来抵消环境方差？
- 对于中等规模（如 7B–14B）的模型，rank-to-capability 耦合的断裂是平滑过渡还是阈值跳变？是参数量、层数还是其他几何因素决定该耦合？
- 若退化病灶确实在 attention/MLP，能否设计只冻结/约束这些组件的 RL 方案（例如仅对 embedding 或 readout head 做 RL，或对 compute block 加更强的正则）来避免退化同时保留任何可能的增益？
- KL anchor 在文中是"保模式稳定器"而非"教师"（保持 argmax 一致性=1.0 但熵膨胀到 4.9–7.7）。能否构造一个既抑制晚期秩塌缩、又允许真正能力提升的 KL 变体或辅助损失？
- 当任务部分可掌握、部分有 headroom（混合分布，而非全 mastered 或全 headroom）时，GRPO 的净效应如何？是否存在一个自适应的课程策略能自动按 headroom 分配算力？
- 因果嫁接表明 embedding 漂移是惰性的——这是否意味着 GRPO 训练中可安全地冻结 embedding 以省算力，或在 distillation 中把 embedding 作为跨任务共享的稳定锚点？
