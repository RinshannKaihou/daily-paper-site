01

The instrument, not the algorithm

— PoPE: Popperian Placebo-controlled Evaluation

Popperian decomposition

The retry loop is split into three separable components: (a) error content learned from refutations, (b) the form of the retry prompt/adapter, (c) generator scale. The paper names four confounds in prior learned-controller work — form, matched-budget, channel, and scale confounding.

Two-gate promotion rule

Content is credited only when BOTH gates pass: G_s (content arm beats frozen base) AND G_c (content arm beats every placebo twin). Pass-signal-but-fail-content is labeled "form, not content." Same 40 resistant units, matched R=4 budget, both channels.

Novel weight-space placebo

A SHA-deranged QLoRA (Sf) matches the content adapter (Ct) on example count, surface form, and family marginals — while removing the task→error assignment. The first form control for PEFT evaluation.

Prescription & budget

QLoRA: NF4, rank r=16, α=32, 2 epochs, 491 steps → train loss 0.002. Matched R=4 output budget only (input length, FLOPs, wall-clock NOT matched). Hardware: a single RTX 3060 6 GB laptop GPU.

03

What holds up — and what doesn't

✓ Strengths

  • First to combine all four controls — placebo (content vs form), matched R=4 budget, both prompt+weight channels on the same units, and a self-audit that withdraws its own positive (Table 17).
  • SHA-deranged placebo LoRA (Sf) — a genuinely new weight-space form control matching Ct on every surface property except the task→error assignment.
  • Reflexive falsification — the apparatus turns on the researcher's own claims, not just model outputs. The supersession record is a model for reporting withdrawn positives.
  • Adoptable two-gate rule (signal → content) practitioners can run before crediting a "learned controller."
  • Unusual rigor: full preregistration, byte-level SHA placebo audits, leakage=0, open-defect retention.

! Limitations

  • 6–8 discordant pairs power the primary contrasts — nulls are "failed to confirm," not refutations; families were powered for +3 to +5 unit effects never observed.
  • No equivalence testing — ties don't prove sameness; no TOST margin is defined.
  • Hidden tier never accessed — negative public screen terminated spend by design; all verdicts are U_p screens only.
  • Tightly bounded transport: single QLoRA config, one 236-unit corpus, one scaffold, Python only, ≤1.5B. Task diversity not separated from adapter capacity (named prime suspect).
  • Data/code not publicly deposited; some headline numbers (27.5%→60%) live only in the non-frozen "program cycle log."
