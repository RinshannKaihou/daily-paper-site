> **一句话总结：** 本文提出 PoPE（Popperian 安慰剂对照评估）方法，在冻结的 0.5–1.5B 代码模型上对"学习式错误条件自修复"进行预注册、安慰剂对照的双通道（提示/权重）测试，结果在两个通道中错误内容都未超过其形状匹配的安慰剂孪生（提示通道 Sh 12 ≥ On 10，权重通道 Ct 8 = Fr 8），得出 content-null 的负面边界结论，并撤回了自身最强的正向分配收益。

> **TL;DR:** On the 40-unit 1.5B-marginal resistant band (HumanEval+/MBPP+/BigCodeBench), under a matched R=4 output-generation budget, learned error content did NOT beat its form-matched placebo in either the prompt channel (On 10 vs Sh 12, mechanism-null) or the QLoRA weight channel (Ct 8 = Fr 8, train-null), and the paper's own strongest allocation positive (+6.0pp) was withdrawn by a built-in matched-budget self-audit.

## 核心贡献

本文不提出新的修复算法，而提出一套**可证伪、可复测的评估度量仪器**——PoPE（Popperian Placebo-controlled Evaluation）。其将"失败的程序视为猜想、执行反例视为 oracle-relative 的反驳"，并用安慰剂对照在冻结小代码模型（0.5–1.5B）上检验"从执行证据中学到的错误内容，能否被同一模型操作性地使用"。具体贡献五项（C1–C5）：

1. **C1（双通道内容归因失败边界）**：在公开层 U_p 筛查上，提示通道与权重通道都未确认"可归因于错误内容"的优越性。该结论仅限于 1.5B 带，不声称形式优越、等价性或一般不可迁移性。
2. **C2（通道无关的安慰剂层级）**：把 Sh（内容消融安慰剂）扩展为 {Sh, De}，并新增 SHA-扰乱安慰剂 LoRA（Sf），把"反射式度量工具"从提示空间推广到 PEFT 权重空间，两通道在同一终点、同一 R=4 预算、同一 discovery-vs-confirmation 规则下裁定。
3. **C3（预注册 kill gate 的机制归因）**：通过 stratum-null、p2-null-offline、g1-kill†、以及可学习性/可实现性 gate（doa、unrealizable、underpowered）区分每个 null 归因于哪个组件；控制器必须先过可学习性 gate 才能开启 live 消耗。
4. **C4（匹配预算分配器的自审计与溯源覆盖）**：AEG-BANDIT 在朴素分析下看似正向的分配收益（+6.0pp）被 pool-cap 混淆识别后撤回，通过持久 supersession 指针记录；匹配预算回放判为 replay-null。
5. **C5（溯源可审计的诊断记录）**：分类饱和度、Hedge 界、outcome-calibrated severity、C-matrix、public–hidden 校准等度量与迁移 null 分离报告，显式缺陷（如 HEF decoy-synthetic test 自 Torch 2.12 起确定性失败）也保留在同一记录中。

## Key Contributions

- **PoPE methodology**: A preregistered, placebo-controlled, two-gate (signal gate G_s vs Fr; content gate G_c vs form-matched placebo) evaluation framework that operationalizes severe-testing logic for learned error-conditioned self-repair in frozen small code LLMs.
- **Channel-agnostic placebo hierarchy**: Extends placebo control to weight space via a SHA-deranged QLoRA (Sf) that matches the content adapter (Ct) on example count, surface form, and family marginals while removing the task→error assignment.
- **Mechanism attribution via preregistered kill gates** (C3) and a **matched-budget self-audit with provenance-tracked supersession** (C4) — the apparatus can withdraw its own positive result within the experiment.
- **Negative-result framing as reusable boundary evidence**: The paper is explicitly a "program of negative-result and measurement-methodology character," not a claim of no-go theorems.

## Methodology

**Epistemological decomposition (Popperian)**: The retry loop is split into three components — (a) error content learned from refutations, (b) the form of the retry prompt/adapter, (c) generator scale. The paper identifies four confounds in prior learned-controller literature: form confounding, matched-budget/order-statistic confounding, channel confounding, and scale confounding.

**Population & endpoints**: Unit of analysis = task cell u = ⟨benchmark, model, task⟩. Two test tiers — public T_p (mechanism screen U_p) and prompt-hidden T_h (true unlock U = public ∧ hidden). The two headline instruments (ELF prompt controller, ELW QLoRA adapter) use a two-stage design: Stage-1 U_p screen; Stage-2 hidden confirmation is opened ONLY if the screen passes (gate-as-result stopping rule). Because screens were negative, **no hidden-tier spend was incurred** — all ELF/ELW counts are "U_p screen," not "true unlock."

**Arms (mirror chains)**:
- Prompt channel A_p = {On (live error-lattice controller), Fr (frozen base), Sh (content-ablated structure-matched placebo), De (donor mismatched decoy)}.
- Weight channel A_w = {Ct (QLoRA on 1,964 genuine task–fail–error pairs), Sf (SHA-deranged placebo LoRA on shuffled pairs), Fr (base, byte-for-byte restoration verified)}.

**Two-gate promotion rule**: Content is credited only when BOTH G_s (content arm > Fr/blind) AND G_c (content arm > every placebo) pass. Pass-signal-but-fail-content = labeled "form." On–Sh is the prompt content contrast; Ct–Sf is the weight content contrast.

**The On controller** = 5 components: (i) two-level family/atom error ontology (K=93 categories); (ii) outcome-calibrated severity v via corrected-sign projected SGD gradient (y−p)e; (iii) Hedge reservoir; (iv) disjoint-linear LinUCB policy over 6 repair actions (Eq. 2.7–2.8); (v) interaction matrix C admitted only if ≥1% relative log-loss improvement.

**QLoRA prescription (Eq. 2.9–2.10)**: NF4 quantized frozen base, rank r=16, scale α=32, dropout 0.05, LR 2e-4, 2 epochs, 491 optimizer steps, target layers = attention+MLP projections; causal LM loss computed only on target tokens (input masked with −100).

**Budget**: Matched output-generation budget R=4 (4 outputs per arm per unit). Only output count is matched — input length, FLOPs, wall-clock NOT matched. Realized accounting: prompt 640 generations (4 arms × 40 units × 4), weight 480 generations (3 arms × 40 × 4 = 280 blind + 200 augmented).

**Hardware**: Single NVIDIA RTX 3060 Laptop 6 GB GPU (WSL2).

## Results & Highlights

**Prompt channel (ELF, G7 family, Table 7) — verdict: mechanism-null.**
- On−Sh: net **−2** (b_01=2, b_10=4), McNemar p=1.0; the content-ablated placebo Sh unlocked MORE units than the learned controller. Pooled: **Sh 12 ≥ On 10**.
- On−De: net +2 (b_01=4, b_10=2), p=0.34375; net-threshold not met.
- On−Fr: net +3 (b_01=5, b_10=2), p=0.2266; frozen net threshold met but exact test n.s.

**Weight channel (ELW, G8 family, Table 8) — verdict: train-null.**
- Ct−Fr: **net 0 (8 = 8)**, b_01=4, b_10=4, McNemar p=1.0; the QLoRA adapter trained to loss 0.002 tied the intervention-free base.
- Ct−Sf: net −2, p=1.0; the **SHA-deranged placebo LoRA (Sf=10) was numerically highest**.
- Forgetting guard passed (content-blind 4 ≥ frozen-blind 3, allowance 2).

**Scale two-point direction (Regularity 2)**: any-portfolio public-tier unlock rate rose descriptively from **27.5% (0.5B) to 60% (1.5B)** — read from cycle logs, explicitly NOT a scaling law or causally isolated lever.

**Self-audit withdrawal (AEG-BANDIT, Regularity 3)**: superseded B=12 record showed +6.0pp (Δ=0.06025, p=0.01375); under pool-cap confound the matched B=8 replay gave adaptive−best-fixed Δ=+0.02209 (p=0.2022) and adaptive−random Δ=**−0.00227** (p=0.6176) → **replay-null**. The +6.0pp delta was retained with its +0.001 control as a supersession record.

**Only confirmed positive (LG P0)**: unit-by-portfolio interaction in public-tier dense progress, permutation **p=0.0001**, power ≥0.97 (n=80/g=8); NOT interpreted as controller superiority.

**Standing diagnostics**: JEPA embedding ema_cos=0.9929, erank=24.97; allocator ρ̂=0.7771, κ_eff=1.175; C-matrix log loss 0.254→0.185 (27.3% admitted); Ct and Fr per-arm mean best-of-4 q identical at floating-point precision (0.40329).

## Critical Assessment

The paper's intellectual honesty is its distinguishing feature. It systematically refuses to over-claim: every tie is explicitly stated as "absence of discrimination," not equivalence (no TOST margin defined); nulls rest on only **6–8 discordant pairs**, acknowledged as low-powered "failed-to-confirm" rather than "refuted"; hidden-tier confirmation is deferred-by-design (negative public screen terminated spend); and the +6.0pp allocation positive was self-corrected via supersession. The epistemological framing (Popperian conjecture/refutation, severity) is load-bearing — it justifies the two-gate rule where G_s∧¬G_c is read as "form, not content."

However, several boundaries weaken the strength of the central negative:
1. **Construct**: U_p (public-only) screen is the decisive endpoint; a content effect invisible at public tier is not ruled out.
2. **Internal**: placebo twins preserve scaffold components but NOT exact token length or lexical distribution — residual content/form aliasing remains.
3. **External**: restricted to 0.5–1.5B, zero-pass-in-pool regime, Python only, single scaffold, single QLoRA config (NF4/r16/α32), 236-unit corpus. The "memorize-don't-generalize" reading (near-zero train loss + no eval superiority) is plausible but not isolated from task-diversity confound.
4. The descriptive 27.5%→60% scale claim is sourced from non-frozen cycle logs, slightly undercutting the "frozen record" discipline championed elsewhere.

The claim "form, not content" is therefore best read as a **local failure boundary on a resistant band**, exactly as the author scopes it — not a universal statement that compiled criticism is useless.

## Strengths

- First study to jointly combine: (a) placebo separating content from form, (b) matched R=4 output budget, (c) BOTH prompt and weight channels on the SAME units with channel-specific placebo twins, and (d) a self-audit that withdraws its own in-experiment positive. The controls-coverage map (Table 17) shows no prior work has all four.
- Novel **SHA-deranged placebo LoRA (Sf)** — a weight-space form control that matches Ct on every surface property except task→error assignment. This is a genuinely new instrument for PEFT evaluation.
- Reflexive falsification: the apparatus applies to the researcher's own claims, not just model outputs. The AEG-BANDIT supersession record is a model of how to report withdrawn positives.
- Clear, prescriptive two-gate rule (signal gate → content gate) that other practitioners can adopt before crediting a "learned controller."

## Limitations

- **Low statistical power**: primary paired contrasts rest on 6–8 discordant pairs; families were powered for +5/+3 (ELF) and +4 (ELW) unit effects that were not observed. Nulls are "failed to confirm," not refutations.
- **No equivalence/non-inferiority testing**: ties do not demonstrate sameness; no TOST margin.
- **Hidden tier never accessed**: all headline verdicts are public-tier U_p screens only.
- **Single QLoRA configuration, single rank, single 236-unit corpus, single scaffold, Python only, ≤1.5B**: external transport is tightly bounded. Task diversity not separated from adapter capacity (the named prime suspect for the weight null).
- **Data/code not publicly deposited** — reproducibility depends on author response to reasonable requests.
- Several headline-adjacent numbers (scale 27.5%→60%) are in the non-frozen "program cycle log."
- Residual surface aliasing: exact token length/lexical distribution not matched between content and placebo arms.

## Relevance

对**冻结小代码模型的本地部署**场景有直接指导意义。当从业者面对"失败后是否把执行错误信息再喂给模型"这一retry决策时，本文证据表明：在 ≤1.5B、zero-pass-in-pool、R=4 的预算下，相比同样预算的盲采（best-of-N mixing）或换更大模型（0.5B→1.5B），"学习式错误条件控制器"并无可测量的额外贡献，且其表观收益可能只是提示/适配器的"形式"效应。更广泛地，它为自修复、self-refine、执行反馈 RL、PEFT/自训练等正向文献提供了一个未被它们覆盖的"亚 2B + 小数据 + 安慰剂对照"边界区域，并提供了可复用的 Sf 安慰剂 LoRA 与两-gate 裁定规则。对研究方法论本身，它是"如何把负面结果做成可证伪、可复测的边界证据"的范本。

## Questions for Further Exploration

1. 在 7B 生成器上、独立构建的新 resistant band 上重复同一 PoPE 协议，content 归因边界是否会随尺度/体制改变？（作者明确列为 follow-up #2）
2. 保持 236 units 不变、换成 task-diverse 语料重训同一 QLoRA 配置 —— 任务多样性是否是权重通道 null 的真正驱动因素？（follow-up #1，被点名为 prime suspect）
3. 把 AEG-BANDIT 分配器从"draws 之间"移到"第一次 draw 之前"作为先验，能否在形式匹配安慰剂下胜出？（follow-up #4；FDP 的 g1-kill 已为该方向建立 gate 纪律）
4. ERA（基于 JEPA/VICReg 的 error-set 架构）的奖励有效性测试被推迟 —— 其 d_z=128 嵌入（ema_cos 0.9929, erank 24.97）非坍缩但下游无用（ridge R²_oof≈0.0096），是否说明非坍缩保证与下游效用本就解耦？
5. 如何在保持 scaffold 不变的同时，进一步匹配 token 长度/词法分布，以消除残存的 content/form 表面混叠？
6. 在有 hidden-tier 真终点（而非仅 U_p 筛查）的设计下，content 效应是否会在公开层不可见但在隐层显现？
7. "memorize-don't-generalize"解读（train loss→0.002 但 Ct=Fr）能否通过 probing/representation 分析从"adapter 容量"与"训练目标结构"中分离出来？
