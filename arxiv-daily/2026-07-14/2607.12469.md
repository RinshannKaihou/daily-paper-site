> **一句话总结：** 本文提出一种厂商中立、可运行的"重构性(reconstructability)"评估有效性指标，按八类决策属性打分，衡量安全评估所捕获的轨迹证据是否足以重构其声明依赖的决策；在公开与内置轨迹上的演示显示，相同表层读数下十二字段充分性跨越 0.458–0.833，所有六张卡的逆向重放前置条件均未满足，合成发布门控对原始变体(0.542)阻断、对已插桩变体(0.667)放行。

> **TL;DR:** This single-author paper introduces a vendor-neutral, runnable, schema-based reconstructability metric that scores (per decision, in [0,1] over eight property classes) whether the evidence an agent-safety evaluation harness captured suffices to reconstruct the claim-dependent decision; a cross-harness adapter emits per-decision Evidence Sufficiency Cards and a per-run monitor-coverage release gate. On four existing public/bundled traces (no new model runs), twelve-field sufficiency spans 0.458–0.833, replay preconditions are unmet in all 6 reported cards (100%), and a synthetic release-gate pair blocks the raw variant (0.542) while passing the eval-instrumented variant (0.667) — a delta of 0.125.

## 核心贡献

论文明确声明四项贡献，均以工程工件形式发布（Apache-2.0 可复现包，scorer 版本 v0.3.0，2026-07-01 归档于 Zenodo）：

1. **属性级重构性指标 (Property-level reconstructability metric)**：针对 agent 决策评估声明，在单位区间 [0,1] 上按八类决策属性逐属性打分——动作可恢复性、权限可恢复性、监控可见性、策略状态可见性、责任归因、反事实可重放性、声明充分性、评估感知混淆。这是从作者先前针对厂商 SDK 的描述性试点 (Solozobov, 2026b) 扩展为"评估声明"为分析单元的评估有效性构造。
2. **厂商中立归一化适配器 (Vendor-neutral normalization adapter)**：将来自至少两种评估 harness（UK AISI 的 Inspect 与 ControlArena）的轨迹归一化为共享的 Evidence Sufficiency Card——每决策一份记录，支撑 per-run 的监控覆盖发布检查。Inspect 字段映射在第 5 节被实际执行，ControlArena reader 已实现但仅在合成 fixture 上测试覆盖。
3. **反事实重放干预协议 + 可重放性前置条件探针**：协议固定要修改的条件（监控可见性、权限结果、策略/rollout 状态、评估感知线索）；本版本只实现并打分前置条件探针（六条），执行式重放被显式标记为 v2 协议。
4. **声明-证据(过度声明)间隙指标 (Claim-evidence overclaim gap G)**：对报告相同名义结果的两证据体制定义为瓶颈重构性得分的绝对差，使"相同标题数字背后证据质量差异"变得可度量。

## Key Contributions

1. A **property-level reconstructability metric** scoring per-property recoverability in [0,1] over eight decision-property classes (action recoverability, permission recoverability, monitor visibility, policy-state visibility, responsibility attribution, counterfactual replayability, claim sufficiency, evaluation-awareness confounding), generalizing the prior pilot's three-valued discrete scale to a unit-interval-valued proportion.
2. A **vendor-neutral cross-harness adapter** that ingests traces from ≥2 evaluation harnesses (Inspect + ControlArena targets; generic JSON/JSONL also supported) into a shared Evidence Sufficiency Card, a per-decision JSON record backing a per-run monitor-coverage release gate.
3. A **counterfactual-replay intervention protocol** with an implemented **replayability-precondition probe** that scores (without executing) six preconditions of well-defined replay, emitting a possible/degraded/impossible verdict per decision.
4. A **claim-evidence (overclaim) gap G = |R(A) − R(B)|** quantifying how much less evidence supports the same nominal outcome in the weaker of two matched regimes, plus its property-resolved vector g_p.

## Methodology

The metric is **definitional and deterministic, with no model in the loop and no learned parameters** — scores are computed by counting present-and-bound evidence elements against author-declared required-evidence sets (Definition 6), using a triple binding (decision_id, agent_step_id, tool_call_id).

- **Per-property score (Definition 2)**: r_p = |present(p)| / |R_p|, recovering the pilot's {0.0, 0.5, 1.0} labels as special cases of a finite fractional grid.
- **Aggregation (Definition 3)**: primary output is the 8-tuple reconstructability vector r = (r_1..r_8); the headline **bottleneck scalar R_min = min over claim-required properties** is non-compensatory (one zero drives headline to zero); coverage mean is reported only alongside the vector.
- **Overclaim gap (Definition 4)**: defined only between regimes sharing the same nominal outcome; semantics explicitly comparative, not absolute adequacy.
- **Released v0 scorer** operationalizes the 8 design classes as **12 three-valued field checks F01–F12** (present=1.0/partial=0.5/missing=0.0); Table 1 documents that the release is deliberately coarser than the design (per-trace not per-decision; discrete not continuous; F01/F12 carry scoring-time/card-self provenance rather than trace evidence).
- **Monitor-coverage gate (§4.5)**: atomic invariant — every risky tool-execution span must be scored by an identified monitor; CLI exit codes encode block (exit 1 on insufficient/partial-coverage, exit 2 on tool errors).

## Results & Highlights

Empirical component is a **demonstration on existing traces, not a controlled experiment** — no new model runs, descriptive and non-pre-registered.

1. **Four scored Evidence Sufficiency Cards** span sufficiency 0.458–0.833: Trace Commons coding-agent (0.458, insufficient), Assayo web-research (0.458, insufficient), Inspect mockllm log (0.667, conditional), synthetic Inspect-style fixture (0.833, conditional); **zero cards reached sufficient-for-named-decision**.
2. **RQ1 (per-check gaps)**: gaps differ by source/format — e.g., scorer/monitor identity F07 is missing only on the raw coding trace; model binding F03 is partial on all three real inputs (no resolved provider snapshot), present only where a digest was hand-authored into the fixture; retry/error evidence F08 is missing on both public traces yet present on the real Inspect log.
3. **RQ2 (sufficiency spread)**: the two real public traces report identical 0.458 with identical status counts but **different blocking-gap composition (F07 vs F06)** — the scalar alone cannot separate them; the cross-substrate spread between extremes is |0.833 − 0.458| = 0.375.
4. **Release-gate matched pair (RQ2 analogue)**: raw variant sufficiency 0.542 (present 4/partial 5/missing 3) vs instrumented 0.667 (present 6/partial 4/missing 2) — **field-level gap G_analogue = 0.125, resolved to F03 and F07**; under `--gate --require-present F07`, raw exits 1 (blocked), instrumented exits 0 (passes).
5. **RQ3 (replay preconditions)**: **counterfactual-replay preconditions unmet in all 6 reported Evidence Sufficiency Cards (100%)** — F10 in the blocking list of all six; F09 rises past partial on no real input; executed replay is "not merely unperformed but undefined" on this substrate, which is itself the finding.
6. **Monitor-coverage gate (§6.4)**: covered fixture coverage_ratio 1.0 (gate exit 0); uncovered fixture coverage_ratio 0.333 with uncovered_spans naming a transfer and delete_record turn (gate exit 1); narrowing the risky set to mutating tools drives coverage to 0.0/uncovered.

## Critical Assessment

The paper is unusually honest about its own scope — a virtue that also signals its limits. Several paper-specific details warrant scrutiny:

- **The headline metric G (Definition 4) is not actually measured in this paper.** §3.4 and §6.2 explicitly state that the released v0 scorer supports only a "field-level analogue" of G — a mean-like twelve-field sufficiency_score difference, **not the bottleneck R_min over the claim-required subset**, and "no equivalence between the two aggregations is established." The abstract's framing ("sufficiency spans 0.458–0.833 across four inputs sharing a surface reading") is a cross-substrate spread, not a Definition-4 overclaim gap, since no matched nominal outcome exists across the four real inputs.
- **The eight property classes and their required-evidence sets R_p are author-declared, not empirically validated** (Threat T1) — the paper calls this "the central calibration target" and defers ground-truth labelling to future work (FW2). Every downstream score inherits this asserted schema.
- **The v0 release is substantially coarser than the §3 design** (Table 1): per-trace not per-decision; discrete not unit-interval; responsibility attribution and evaluation-awareness confounding carried only indirectly; 3 of the 6 §3.5 replay preconditions are "not operationalised in v0." The design remains aspirational; the artifact measures a strict subset.
- **No executed counterfactual replay (T4)** — the entire RQ3 causal form (H3) is untested; the paper converts this into a "measured finding" (preconditions unmet → replay undefined), which is defensible but means the most distinctive methodological claim (replay protocol) is specified, not demonstrated.
- **T2 is important and well-stated**: the metric scores evidence sufficiency, not correctness — "a claim can be reconstructable and false, or unreconstructable and true." Readers tempted to read a high score as a safety endorsement are explicitly warned off.

## Strengths

- Conceptually sharp framing: distinguishes **outcome vs. evidence** and names the failure mode (identical nominal outcomes atop materially different evidence regimes) that no prior vendor-neutral runnable instrument scores.
- Clean positioning against closest prior art: AgenTracer (failure attribution, which "presumes the failure is reconstructable"), EviBound (audits the agent's own task-completion claim, not the monitor's observation), Auditable Agents' Auditability Card (per-system disclosure, not per-claim cross-harness score).
- Engineering honesty: deterministic, schema-based, no model-in-the-loop; byte-identical reproducibility; explicit design-to-release correspondence table (Table 1) admitting where the release is coarser than the design.
- Genuinely novel wedge: the **third verdict "insufficient evidence"** (⊥, undecidable from the record) — a verdict attribution systems are built to avoid — is the paper's distinctive contribution, derived from the auditability literature's three-valued policy-check outcome.
- Actionable reporting recommendation: report the reconstructability vector + bottleneck scalar alongside any safety-evaluation claim; CI-friendly exit-code gate.

## Limitations

- **Construct validity (T1, T5)**: required-evidence sets R_p and the eight-class taxonomy itself are author-declared cross-walks from the pilot + Nian et al. (2026) auditability dimensions, not empirically calibrated; unconditional membership (Definition 6) cannot distinguish structurally-absent from not-applicable or evidenced-negative, conservatively depressing r_p.
- **External validity (T3)**: substrate is 4 scored traces + 2 fixture pairs; 3 of 6 inputs are hand-built synthetic anchors; ControlArena reader implemented but not demonstrated on a real export; no real matched-outcome pair (only the synthetic release-gate pair); nothing establishes generalization to frontier/closed models or other trace formats.
- **No statistical testing**: the paper repeatedly states analyses are descriptive, non-pre-registered, with no hypothesis tests — appropriate for a demonstration but limits the strength of H1/H2/H3 support.
- **Headline scores modestly overstate trace-earned sufficiency**: up to 2 of 12 fields (F01, F12) are credited from scoring-time/card-self provenance rather than the trace; separating capture scores from documentation scores is deferred to v2.
- **Heavy reliance on author's own prior pilot** (Solozobov, 2026b) for the underlying schema and anchor methodology.

## Relevance

本文对 LLM agent 安全评估、可审计性与 AI 治理社区高度相关。它直接回应了 Rabanser et al. (2026) 指出的"单一指标不足以理解 agent 行为"问题，以及 Nian et al. (2026) 关于部署系统"日志部分缺失或不存在"的观察。对于构建评估 harness（如 Inspect、ControlArena）的团队、撰写结构化安全案例 (safety case) 的研究者，以及任何在 CI 流水线中对 agent 发布做门控的工程团队，其"报告重构性向量"的建议是低成本、可立即采纳的实践。对评估有效性 (evaluation validity)、监控覆盖、反事实重放与证据溯源感兴趣的读者会从中发现一个可运行、可复现的诊断工具与一个清晰的未来校准议程 (FW1/FW2)。

## Questions for Further Exploration

1. 当 R_p 的成员资格由两位标注者 ground-truth 校准后（FW2），八类属性的重构性分布与瓶颈得分会如何变化？作者声明的 required-evidence 集合在多大程度上能经受独立审计员的修订？
2. v2 协议执行反事实重放后，是否有任何"结果稳定但证据贫乏"的声明被翻转——即重放是否真的能改变重构性裁定（H3 因果形式）？本文未能测试这一最核心的因果主张。
3. 在真实的 ControlArena 导出（而非合成 fixture）上，跨 harness 的字段映射是否仍保持厂商中立性？Inspect 之外的第二种真实 harness 验证缺失，这是 v0 的主要外部效度缺口。
4. 当把 F01/F12 这类"评分时/卡片自述"来源字段从 sufficiency_score 中剔除后（capture-only recount），四张卡的得分排序与阻断结论是否会发生实质变化？
5. monitor-coverage 门控的"覆盖 ≠ 准确性"边界如何在实践中被执行——一个对每个风险 span 都打分但判断全部错误的监控器会通过门控，这是否削弱了门控作为发布证据的承载能力 (load-bearing)？
6. 定义 4 的瓶颈 R_min 与 v0 发布的十二字段均值 sufficiency_score 之间是否存在等价关系？若不等价，论文摘要中以"spans 0.458–0.833"作为证据差异的论据是否需要更严格的限定？
