Knowledge Distillation On-Policy Structured Output Phase Transition LLM Post-Training

The Extrapolation Cliff in On-Policy Distillation of Near-Deterministic Structured Outputs

Xin Li, Hao Jiang, Annan Wang, Yichi Zhang, Chau Yuen
Nanyang Technological University, Singapore · 2026
Key Finding
Reward extrapolation beyond a closed-form threshold λ*(p,b,c) causes a sharp phase transition from format-preserving to format-collapsing training in on-policy distillation — not gradual degradation, but a cliff driven by cumulative IS-clip drift.
Key Metric
0.0005 error
Cross-clip prediction matches closed-form formula: observed midpoint 1.069 vs predicted 1.070 — below grid resolution.
Why This Matters
A 1.7B student matches an 8B-SFT baseline at one-fifth the parameters with ~5x lower seed variance — and practitioners get a calculable safety threshold to avoid the cliff.
Critical Edge
Theory applies only to near-deterministic structured outputs with a single dominant binding equivalence class. It does not generalize to free-form generation, math CoT, or already-saturated formats. Llama cross-architecture test fails to reproduce the cliff.

The λ* Threshold

// Clip-safety threshold (Theorem 4.1)
λ*(p, b, c) =
  [ ln((1-p)/(c-1+p)) − ln((1-b)/b) ]
  / [ ln((1-p)/p) − ln((1-b)/b) ]

// p = teacher modal prob
// b = warm-start mass
// c = IS clip strength

// Calibrated on Fashion:
ptyp=0.9993, b=0.81, c=5
→ bracket [1.18, 1.28]
→ observed onset [1.15, 1.25]

Above λ*, the extrapolated fixed point exits the clip-safe region. The transition is driven by cumulative drift through IS-clip asymmetry, not by discrete clip events.

Pre-registered Validation

  • 5-seed fine grid: Parse ≥ 0.80 onset at 95% bootstrap CI [1.204, 1.228], containing closed-form prediction λ*=1.22.
  • Budget extension (N=200): Locked bracket [1.00, 1.10]; observed cliff midpoint at 1.061 (single-seed) / 1.068 (3-seed mean) — both inside.
  • Cross-clip (c=1.5, N=200): Predicted λtyp*=1.070. Observed midpoint between λ=1.05 (parse 0.939) and λ=1.075 (parse 0.632) is 1.0695. Error: 0.0005.
  • ASPO head-to-head: Collapses at λ=1.5 under same protocol, confirming the cliff is mechanism-driven, not GRPO-specific.

Six Pre-registered Ablations

Extra SFT Steps
Rules out that cliff is an artifact of undertrained warm-start.
Forward-KL
Confirms cliff is specific to reverse-KL extrapolation, not universal.
Scaling Alone
Rules out that parameter count alone explains the parity result.
Constrained Decoding
Five strict-K systems reach parse 0.783; unconstrained ListOPD reaches 0.943.
Seed Cherry-Picking
Multi-seed CI confirms the cliff is not a seed artifact.
No Extrapolation
λ=1 (vanilla reverse-KL) shows no cliff — extrapolation is necessary.
