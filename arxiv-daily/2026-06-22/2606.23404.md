# ReasoningLens: Hierarchical Visualization and Diagnostic Auditing for Large Reasoning Models

## TL;DR
ReasoningLens is an open-source framework that transforms the monolithic, hard-to-inspect chain-of-thought (CoT) traces of Large Reasoning Models (LRMs) into structured, interactive hierarchical graphs. It provides three integrated capabilities: (1) hierarchical visualization that separates exploration-level strategy from exploitation-level execution, (2) a multi-agent agentic auditor that automatically detects and localizes reasoning errors across five categories, and (3) systemic profiling that aggregates cross-trajectory patterns to reveal model-level blind spots. The authors also introduce LensBench, a 130-instance benchmark for evaluating both structural visualization fidelity and error-annotation accuracy.

## TL;DR (中文)
ReasoningLens 是一个开源框架，将大型推理模型 (LRM) 中单块式、难以审查的思维链 (CoT) 轨迹转化为结构化的交互式层级图。它提供三项集成能力：(1) 层级可视化，将探索级策略与执行级操作分离；(2) 基于多智能体的自动错误检测与定位系统，覆盖五类推理错误；(3) 系统性分析，聚合跨轨迹模式以揭示模型级别的盲区。作者还构建了 LensBench，一个包含 130 个实例的基准，用于评估结构可视化保真度和错误标注准确性。

---

## Problem & Motivation / 问题与动机

Large Reasoning Models like DeepSeek-R1, GPT-5, and Qwen3 produce increasingly long CoT traces (tens of thousands of tokens). While this verbosity enables more complex reasoning, it creates a fundamental transparency burden: critical logical dependencies become buried in an unstructured "wall of text," making manual inspection, error diagnosis, and safety assurance extremely challenging. Prior visualization tools remain largely heuristic and descriptive, limited to superficial text rendering without capturing underlying reasoning structures. No existing work provides a holistic taxonomy that unifies visualization, automated diagnosis, and systemic profiling into a single framework.

大型推理模型（如 DeepSeek-R1、GPT-5、Qwen3）产生的思维链轨迹越来越长（数万 token）。虽然冗长的推理过程使复杂问题求解成为可能，但也带来了根本性的透明度挑战：关键逻辑依赖被埋藏在非结构化的"文字墙"中，使人工审查、错误诊断和安全评估变得极其困难。现有可视化工具大多基于启发式方法，停留在表面文本渲染层面，未能捕捉底层推理结构。目前没有工作将可视化、自动诊断和系统性分析统一在一个框架内。

## Method / 方法

### 1. Hierarchical Visualization / 层级可视化

The framework introduces a two-level taxonomy of reasoning behaviors:

**Exploration-Level (high-level strategy):**
- **Decomposition**: Divide-and-conquer partitioning of complex problems into atomic sub-goals, expanding linear paths into tree structures.
- **Backtracking**: Failure-triggered strategy that prunes failed branches and resumes search from prior decision points.
- **Validation**: Self-correction mechanism that introduces verification loops before committing to a path.

**Exploitation-Level (low-level execution):**
- **Knowledge Retrieval**: Extracting task-relevant priors from parametric memory.
- **Procedural Execution**: Rule-governed transformations over instantiated inputs.
- **State Assertion**: Committing intermediate findings into the working state.

The pipeline works as follows:
1. **Planning Unit Extraction**: The CoT trace is segmented into atomic planning units using decision-oriented lexical cues ("but," "wait," "alternatively").
2. **Exploration-Level Modeling**: Planning units are partitioned into M disjoint macro-nodes, each typed with a functional role (problem decomposition, validation, intermediate answers), connected via structural edges (forward reasoning, backtracking, detached verification) to form a tree-structured hierarchy.
3. **Exploitation-Level Modeling**: Each macro-node is further refined into micro-nodes labeled with specific execution behaviors, encoding procedural dependencies for precise error attribution.

该框架引入了两级推理行为分类体系：

**探索级（高层策略）**：分解（分而治之）、回溯（失败触发的分支剪枝）、验证（提交前的自校正循环）。

**执行级（底层操作）**：知识检索、过程执行、状态断言。

流程：(1) 利用决策导向词汇线索将 CoT 分割为原子规划单元；(2) 将规划单元聚合为宏节点，形成树状层级结构；(3) 将每个宏节点细化为微节点，编码过程依赖关系以实现精确的错误归因。

### 2. Agentic Diagnosis / 智能体诊断

The error taxonomy comprises five primary categories:
1. **Overthinking**: Redundant reasoning cycles (repeated verification, circular loops) that waste deliberation time without improving results.
2. **Safety**: Generating harmful content, toxicity, bias, or leaking sensitive information.
3. **Knowledge Error**: Incorrect recall or misuse of established knowledge (factual hallucinations, outdated information).
4. **Logical Error**: Flawed reasoning strategies, non-sequiturs, or internal contradictions leading to invalid conclusions.
5. **Formal Error**: Non-compliance with strict symbolic rules (syntax, LaTeX, arithmetic) resulting in invalid output.

The multi-agent diagnostic system consists of three modules:
- **Memory Module**: Incrementally inspects the CoT through memory compression, ensuring granular localization of local errors while preserving trace-level consistency.
- **Verification Module**: Leverages external tool invocations to resolve internal ambiguity, enabling precise and verifiable diagnostic outcomes.
- **Suggestion Module**: Generates actionable mitigation strategies mapped to specific error types, covering both training-free approaches and post-training techniques from a curated repository.

错误分类体系包含五大类：过度思考、安全问题、知识错误、逻辑错误、形式错误。多智能体诊断系统由三个模块组成：记忆模块（增量式检查与压缩）、验证模块（调用外部工具解决内部歧义）、建议模块（生成可操作的修复策略）。

### 3. Systemic Profiling / 系统性分析

This module moves beyond instance-level inspection to distribution-level trajectory analysis. Tree-structured artifacts and failure signals are mapped into a shared representation to extract global invariants (backtracking distributions, error types). LLM-driven semantic deduplication compresses similar reasoning paths while preserving distinct heuristic features. The distilled evidence is synthesized into a model-level profile along three axes:
- **Exploration habits** (depth-breadth trade-offs)
- **Verification reliability** (self-correction consistency)
- **Stability bottlenecks** (high-variance logical structures)

该模块从实例级检查扩展到分布级轨迹分析，沿三个维度生成模型级画像：探索习惯（深度-广度权衡）、验证可靠性（自校正一致性）、稳定性瓶颈（高方差逻辑结构）。

## Experimental Setup / 实验设置

**Benchmark -- LensBench**: 130 instances spanning 5 representative categories of reasoning failures, sourced from the Mixture-of-Thoughts benchmark. Traces with at least 10 planning units were retained; mixed-language traces removed; GPT-5.4 used to filter pre-existing errors and inject controlled, taxonomy-guided errors. All instances were manually verified.

**Backbone Evaluators**: DeepSeek-V4-Pro, MiniMax-M2.7, Qwen3.5-27B, Gemma-4-26B-A4B, Qwen3-32B.

**Metrics**:
- Hierarchical visualization: Node Type Accuracy (NTA) and Graph Edit Similarity (GES).
- Error diagnosis: Per-type and micro-averaged Precision, Recall, and F1.

**基准 -- LensBench**：130 个实例，覆盖 5 类推理失败，来源于 Mixture-of-Thoughts。使用 GPT-5.4 进行控制性错误注入，所有实例经人工验证。
**骨干评估器**：DeepSeek-V4-Pro、MiniMax-M2.7、Qwen3.5-27B、Gemma-4-26B-A4B、Qwen3-32B。

## Key Results / 关键结果

### Error Diagnosis Performance
| Model | Overthinking F1 | Safety F1 | Knowledge Error F1 | Logical Error F1 | Formal Error F1 | Overall F1 |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| DeepSeek-V4-Pro | 87.0 | 98.5 | 65.1 | 60.3 | 86.0 | 82.3 |
| MiniMax-M2.7 | 87.2 | 97.0 | 47.6 | 55.1 | 80.8 | 79.4 |
| Qwen3.5-27B | 80.6 | 96.9 | 50.0 | 45.0 | 78.0 | 75.0 |
| Gemma-4-26B-A4B | 84.9 | 91.8 | 46.7 | 54.0 | 60.3 | 74.0 |
| Qwen3-32B | 86.1 | 89.9 | 39.0 | 34.6 | 46.2 | 66.3 |

### Hierarchical Visualization Performance
| Model | NTA | GES |
|---|---|---|
| DeepSeek-V4-Pro | 79.5 | 72.3 |
| MiniMax-M2.7 | 73.0 | 69.0 |
| Qwen3.5-27B | 77.4 | 70.8 |
| Gemma-4-26B-A4B | 70.9 | 68.2 |
| Qwen3-32B | 74.0 | 68.0 |

**Key findings:**
- The Agentic Diagnosis module achieves reliable error detection with overall F1 ranging from 66.3 (Qwen3-32B) to 82.3 (DeepSeek-V4-Pro), scaling with backbone model strength.
- Safety detection is consistently high across all models (F1 > 89), suggesting current safety alignment is effective for this category.
- Knowledge Error and Logical Error detection are strongly model-dependent, with F1 as low as 39.0 and 34.6 on Qwen3-32B, recovering notably on stronger models -- diagnosing deeper reasoning failures relies heavily on the backbone's own reasoning capacity.
- Hierarchical Visualization proves consistently robust across model scales, with stable NTA (~75) and GES (~70) averages, indicating this component works well independent of backbone choice.

**关键发现**：(1) 智能体诊断模块的整体 F1 从 66.3 到 82.3，随骨干模型能力提升；(2) 安全检测在所有模型上一致性高（F1 > 89）；(3) 知识错误和逻辑错误检测强烈依赖模型能力；(4) 层级可视化在不同模型规模下表现稳健（NTA ~75, GES ~70），是与骨干选择无关的鲁棒组件。

## Case Analysis / 案例分析

In a case study with Qwen3-32B, ReasoningLens decomposes the model's reasoning trajectory for a propaganda-related query into semantically coherent functional blocks (Strategy Shift, Verification), with high alignment to human-annotated ground truth. The Agentic Diagnosis module surfaces critical reasoning vulnerabilities otherwise obscured by verbosity -- including unsafe manipulation tactics and redundant overthinking -- and associates them with targeted remediation strategies (e.g., Early Stopping) in a "How to Fix" panel.

在 Qwen3-32B 的案例中，ReasoningLens 将模型的推理轨迹分解为语义一致的功能块（策略转换、验证），与人工标注高度一致。智能体诊断模块揭示了被冗长文本掩盖的关键推理漏洞（不安全操纵策略、冗余过度思考），并关联了针对性修复策略。

## Limitations / 局限性

1. Currently focuses on static CoT traces rather than dynamic, multi-step agentic interactions (e.g., Plan-Act-Observe cycles).
2. Deployment remains relatively monolithic; the authors plan to evolve toward a modular plugin ecosystem.
3. Error injection for benchmark construction relies on GPT-5.4, which may introduce biases in error distribution.

1. 目前仅关注静态 CoT 轨迹，尚未支持动态多步智能体交互（如 Plan-Act-Observe 循环）。
2. 部署相对单块化，作者计划向模块化插件生态演进。
3. 基准构建中的错误注入依赖 GPT-5.4，可能引入错误分布偏差。

## Critical Assessment / 批判性评价

**Strengths:**
- The two-level taxonomy (exploration vs. exploitation) is well-motivated and provides a principled decomposition that goes beyond prior surface-level visualization.
- The unified framework connecting visualization, diagnosis, and profiling fills a genuine gap -- most prior work addresses only one of these in isolation.
- The error taxonomy is comprehensive and practically useful, covering both reasoning quality (logical, knowledge, formal errors) and deployment concerns (safety, overthinking).
- Open-source availability with code, dataset, and demo video enhances reproducibility.

**Weaknesses:**
- The framework's reliance on an LLM backbone for both segmentation and diagnosis creates a circular dependency -- the quality of the analysis is bounded by the very type of model it aims to audit. The gap between DeepSeek-V4-Pro (F1=82.3) and Qwen3-32B (F1=66.3) in error detection underscores this limitation.
- LensBench is relatively small (130 instances) and uses synthetic error injection rather than naturally occurring errors, which may not fully represent the distribution of real-world reasoning failures.
- The paper does not evaluate how well the hierarchical visualization scales to very long traces (e.g., 50k+ tokens), where the structural decomposition would face the greatest challenge.
- Systemic Profiling is described at a high level but receives no quantitative evaluation in the experiments, making it difficult to assess its practical value.
- The framework is evaluated only on mathematical/logical reasoning domains; generalization to code generation, scientific reasoning, or open-ended tasks remains unverified.

**优势**：两级分类体系设计合理；统一框架填补了可视化-诊断-分析之间的空白；错误分类全面实用；开源可复现。
**不足**：依赖 LLM 骨干进行分析构成循环依赖；LensBench 规模较小且使用合成错误注入；未评估超长轨迹的可扩展性；系统性分析模块缺乏定量评估；仅在数学/逻辑推理领域验证。

## Relevance / 相关性

This work is relevant to anyone working on LRM transparency, reasoning chain debugging, or safety auditing. The structured taxonomy of reasoning behaviors and errors could serve as a useful reference for building evaluation frameworks. The framework's modular design (visualization + diagnosis + profiling) provides a template for reasoning analysis tooling. For practitioners deploying LRMs in production, the agentic diagnosis pipeline offers a path toward automated quality assurance of reasoning traces.

本工作与 LRM 透明度、推理链调试和安全审计相关。推理行为和错误的结构化分类可作为构建评估框架的参考。模块化设计为推理分析工具提供了模板。对于在生产中部署 LRM 的实践者，智能体诊断流水线提供了推理轨迹自动化质量保证的路径。
