> **一句话总结：** 提出 Interchange-Group Sobol Decomposition (IGSD)，通过配对比较同一 Transformer 组件上的"激活替换"与"零消融"两种干预的 Sobol 方差指数，将组件角色分解为"内容传输通道"与"删除敏感基底"，在事实回忆任务中发现被标准重要性方法低估的早期 MLP 关系帧内容通道。

> **TL;DR:** Standard interpretability methods conflate two reasons a transformer component matters: it carries task-relevant content vs. it is structurally needed for forward computation. IGSD separates these by pairing matched-donor activation swaps with zero ablations on the same component and comparing their Sobol-style variance indices. On factual recall in GPT-2 small and Qwen2.5-1.5B, IGSD identifies an early-layer MLP content channel (carrying relation-frame information) that DLA, zero-ablation, and AtP* all underestimate, and confirms that late attention carries subject-retrieval content -- with downstream clamping validation showing the early signal is functionally transformed by later layers, not merely passed through residually.

## 核心贡献

- **提出 IGSD 框架**：一种配对干预方法，在同一 Transformer 组件上同时执行匹配供体激活替换（swap）和零消融（zero），计算两个 Sobol 方差指数并用其有符号差 delta(g) 分解组件的"内容传输"与"基底删除敏感"角色。
- **揭示被低估的早期内容通道**：在事实回忆任务中，MLP_L0 被 IGSD 排名第一，而 DLA（#11）、零消融（#9）、AtP*（#13）均严重低估其重要性。该通道主要传输关系帧（relation-frame）内容。
- **任务特异性的角色分配**：delta 的符号不是组件的固有属性，而取决于任务和干预分布 -- 同一个 MLP_L0 在事实回忆中为正（内容通道），在 IOI 中变负（基底）。
- **理论保证**：提供误指定界（Proposition 1）、配对 bootstrap 的渐近正态性（Proposition 2）、因子桶对比的内容因子识别（Proposition 3）、以及基于潜在内容-基底质量的角色标签接地（Proposition 4）。
- **下游验证**：通过晚层钳位实验证明早期关系帧信号被后续层功能性变换，而非简单残差直通，恢复了 82% 的替换引起的输出方差。

## Key Contributions

- **IGSD framework**: A paired-intervention method that estimates two Sobol-style variance indices (swap vs. zero) on the same transformer component, using their signed difference to decompose component roles into "content-transport channel" vs. "deletion-sensitive substrate." Includes a symmetric off-manifold diagnostic (ST > 1) to flag invalid interventions.
- **Discovery of underestimated early content channels**: On factual recall, IGSD ranks MLP_L0 as #1 among 24 layer-local groups, whereas DLA (#11), zero-ablation (#9), and AtP* (#13) all underestimate it. A factorial donor design shows MLP_L0 carries relation-frame content while late Attn_L9 carries subject-retrieval content.
- **Task-specificity of role labels**: The sign of delta is not a fixed anatomical property -- MLP_L0 is content-positive on factual recall but content-negative on IOI, showing roles depend on task, donor distribution, and output functional.
- **Theoretical guarantees**: Mis-specification bound (Prop. 1), asymptotic normality of the paired bootstrap (Prop. 2), content-factor identification via factorial buckets (Prop. 3), and grounding of role labels in latent content-substrate mass (Prop. 4).
- **Downstream validation via late-layer clamping**: Clamping late attention + late MLP recovers 82% of swap-induced output variance, with late attention contributing 0.17 and late MLP contributing 0.57, ruling out a pure residual-carry explanation.

## Methodology

IGSD operates on each layer-local group g (one attention block or one MLP per layer) of a transformer. For a matched pair of prompts (x_A, x_B):

1. **Swap intervention**: Replace the written activation h_g(x_A, t_A) at the answer-aligned target token with h_g(x_B, t_B) from a matched donor prompt, then continue the forward pass. This produces Y_swap.
2. **Zero intervention**: Replace the same activation with the zero vector, producing Y_zero.
3. **Sobol indices**: Compute ST_swap(g) = E[(Y_A - Y_swap)^2] / (2 Var(Y_A)) and ST_zero(g) = E[(Y_A - Y_zero)^2] / (2 Var(Y_A)). The shared denominator makes the two indices directly comparable.
4. **Role decomposition**: The signed contrast delta(g) = ST_swap(g) - ST_zero(g) separates content sensitivity (delta > 0, meaning wrong content is more disruptive than absence) from substrate sensitivity (delta < 0, meaning absence is more disruptive than alternative content).

**Donor matching**: For each source prompt x_A, a donor x_B is selected from k=10 nearest neighbors in a standardized feature space combining the clean logit margin Y(x) and per-layer total DLA magnitudes. This keeps donors close to the empirical activation manifold while preserving variation in task-relevant content.

**Off-manifold diagnostic**: ST > 1 flags interventions that fall outside the formal [0,1] range, indicating off-manifold artifacts. These groups are excluded from interpretation.

**Factorial bucket design**: For factual recall, prompts are partitioned into three buckets (diff_both, same_subject, same_relation) to identify whether a component transports subject content, relation content, or both.

**Inference**: Paired-bootstrap (n_b = 1000) on matched triples (Y_A, Y_swap, Y_zero) yields 95% confidence intervals and pairwise ranking probabilities.

The output functional is the logit margin: Y(x) = logit(correct | x) - logit(foil | x).

## Results & Highlights

**Factual recall (CounterFact)**:
- MLP_L0 is identified as the top content-transport channel by IGSD (rank #1) with ST_swap/ST_zero = 2.31 and delta = 0.103, while standard methods rank it between #9 and #13.
- The factorial bucket design reveals MLP_L0 is relation-dominant: holding relation fixed reduces ST_swap by 2.5x, while holding subject fixed has little effect.
- Attn_L9 shows the reverse pattern (subject-dominant), consistent with prior work on the factual retrieval head Attn_L9H8.
- At head-level granularity, IGSD recovers Attn_L9H8 as the top-ranked head.

**Indirect Object Identification (IOI)**:
- The roles reverse: Attn_L9 becomes the dominant positive-delta component; MLP_L0 becomes negative, behaving as substrate rather than content channel. This confirms task-specificity.

**Synthetic induction**:
- Yet another profile emerges, with Attn_L10 dominant and most components showing negative delta.

**Late-layer clamping**:
- Clamping late attention and late MLP jointly recovers 82% of swap-induced variance (95% CI [0.77, 0.86]), with unique contributions of 0.17 (late attention) and 0.57 (late MLP), confirming that the early relation-frame signal is functionally processed downstream.

**Cross-model consistency**:
- In Qwen2.5-1.5B the dominant factual component shifts to Attn_L0, preserving the early-layer location while changing the module type.

**Pruning application**:
- Keeping only the top-2 IGSD-ranked groups and zero-masking the remaining 22 retains 70.0% top-1 accuracy and 78.5% of clean logit difference, versus 57.4% and 26.4% under zero-ablation ranking (+12.6 accuracy points, 95% CI [+6.6, +18.6]).

## Critical Assessment

**Novelty**: Moderate-to-high. The core insight -- that swap and zero interventions measure fundamentally different things and should be compared rather than conflated -- is genuinely useful and clearly articulated. The Sobol framing provides a principled statistical lens that goes beyond ad hoc ablation comparisons. However, the qualitative observation that "resample > zero" has been noted before (Heimersheim and Nanda 2024, Zhang and Nanda 2024); IGSD's contribution is making it quantitative and component-level.

**Rigor**: High. The paper provides four formal propositions with proofs (in appendix), paired-bootstrap inference with confidence intervals, multiple robustness checks (donor-fidelity sweeps, IPW reweighting, per-relation stratification, multi-seed stability), and an off-manifold diagnostic. The theoretical framework is unusually careful for a mechanistic interpretability paper.

**Reproducibility**: Good. The method is clearly specified algorithmically (Algorithm 1 in appendix). Models used (GPT-2 small, Qwen2.5-1.5B) are publicly available. Tasks (CounterFact, IOI, induction) are standard benchmarks. TransformerLens is used for both models. However, no code release is mentioned in the paper.

**Significance**: Moderate. The finding that replacement and deletion are not interchangeable controls is important for the interpretability community's methodology. The early MLP relation-frame channel is a concrete new finding. However, the practical impact (pruning application) is modest, and the paper is limited to single-token prediction tasks at the answer-aligned position.

**Red flags**:
- The matched-donor design relies on k-NN in a DLA-based feature space, introducing potential confounds from the DLA itself. The authors acknowledge this and provide robustness checks, but the donor distribution is not fully propensity-balanced.
- The off-manifold flag (ST > 1) fires on 3 of 72 layer profiles across three tasks, which is manageable but suggests the framework is not entirely clean.
- The paper evaluates only on small models (GPT-2 small, Qwen2.5-1.5B) and simple retrieval tasks. Generalization to larger models and more complex behaviors is unknown.
- No code release mentioned.

## Strengths

- Clearly frames a fundamental methodological issue (conflation of content and substrate roles) and provides a principled solution with statistical grounding.
- The factorial bucket design (same-subject vs. same-relation donors) is an elegant way to identify what content factor a channel transports.
- Strong validation pipeline: the late-layer clamping experiment goes beyond correlation to show functional downstream transformation.
- Unusually thorough appendix with formal proofs, robustness analyses, and diagnostics.
- Task-specificity of delta is presented honestly as a feature of the framework rather than hidden as an inconvenient result.

## Limitations

- Limited to single-token prediction at the answer-aligned position; does not address multi-token generation or non-retrieval tasks.
- Only tested on GPT-2 small (12 layers) and Qwen2.5-1.5B -- unclear how the method scales to larger models where activation manifolds are higher-dimensional.
- Zero ablation is only one deletion operator; the signed contrast delta is relative to this specific baseline, not universal.
- Block-level granularity (attention/MLP per layer) does not resolve full head-level causal paths, though head-level refinement is demonstrated for specific components.
- Matched-donor selection depends on DLA-based features, creating a circular dependency with the attribution being studied.
- No code or data release documented.

## Relevance

本文与机制可解释性（mechanistic interpretability）领域高度相关，提出了一种统计上更严格的干预方法来分解 Transformer 组件的功能角色。对于关注 LLM 内部机制、事实回忆电路、以及模型剪枝的研究者，IGSD 提供了替换与删除不可互换的量化证据，以及一个可直接用于内容通道发现的实用工具。

## Questions for Further Exploration

1. How does IGSD scale to larger models (e.g., Llama-2 7B, GPT-J 6B) where the number of layer-local groups is much larger and activation manifolds are higher-dimensional?
2. Can the donor-matching procedure be improved with propensity scoring or other causal-inference techniques to reduce residual confounding?
3. Does the early MLP relation-frame channel identified here correspond to specific sparse autoencoder features, and can IGSD be combined with dictionary learning for finer-grained content decomposition?
4. How does IGSD behave on tasks beyond single-token retrieval, such as multi-hop reasoning, arithmetic, or open-ended generation?
5. Can the content-substrate decomposition inform targeted knowledge editing (e.g., ROME/MEMIT) by distinguishing which components to target for content vs. which to leave as substrate?
