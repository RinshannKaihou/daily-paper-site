Quantifying the Agreement Between Data-Influence and Data-Similarity to Understand LLM Behavior

arXiv: 2606.23591

Date: 2026-06-22

https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.23591

Summary / 摘要

This paper systematically quantifies the agreement and differences between two major approaches for tracing LLM outputs back to training data: data-similarity (e.g., BM25) and data-influence (e.g., influence functions using Hessians or gradients). The key finding is that the two measures agree significantly in their top-ranked documents, but there is a consistent asymmetry: data-influence is more predictive of data-similarity rankings than the reverse. This asymmetry suggests a practical hybrid strategy -- use cheap data-similarity for initial retrieval, then refine with expensive data-influence -- achieving a favorable cost-accuracy trade-off.

本文系统地量化了两种将LLM输出追溯到训练数据的主要方法之间的一致性和差异：数据相似度（如BM25）和数据影响力（如基于Hessian或梯度的影响函数）。核心发现是两种度量在排名靠前的文档上具有显著一致性，但存在一种持续的不对称性：数据影响力对数据相似度排名的预测能力强于反向预测。这种不对称性提示了一种实用的混合策略——先用低成本的数据相似度进行初步检索，再用高成本的数据影响力进行精化——从而实现有利的成本-精度权衡。

Key Contributions / 主要贡献

  1. First systematic comparison for output tracing: Prior work compared data-similarity and data-influence on ground-truth tasks (e.g., fact tracing), but no study had directly compared them for the output-tracing setting where absolute ground truth is unavailable. This paper fills that gap using ranking-overlap metrics (agreement at depth d).

首个面向输出追溯的系统性对比：此前的工作在有标准答案的任务（如事实追溯）上比较过这两种方法，但尚无研究在缺乏绝对标准答案的输出追溯场景下直接对比。本文使用排名重叠度量（深度d处的一致性）填补了这一空白。

  1. Discovery of asymmetric agreement: The top documents ranked by data-similarity receive more consistent ranks from data-influence than the reverse. For OLMo2-1B, 34% of the top-100 data-similarity documents fall within the top-1000 of data-influence, but only 28% in the opposite direction. This asymmetry is statistically significant (Wilcoxon signed rank test, p < 0.05) across most rank depths.

发现不对称一致性：数据相似度排名靠前的文档在数据影响力中获得的排名更为一致，反之则不然。以OLMo2-1B为例，数据相似度top-100文档中有34%落入数据影响力的top-1000，而反向仅为28%。该不对称性在大多数排名深度上通过Wilcoxon符号秩检验具有统计显著性（p < 0.05）。

  1. Consistency across models and estimators: Results hold across six LLMs (OLMo2-1B, Qwen3-1.7B, LlaMa3.2-1B, Gemma3-1B, GPT2-medium, GPT2-small) and three influence estimators (EK-FAC/KFAC, diagonal approximation DIAG, gradient-based GRAD). Better influence estimators yield higher agreement and stronger asymmetry.

跨模型和估计器的一致性：结果在六个LLM和三种影响力估计器上均成立。更好的影响力估计器产生更高的一致性和更强的不对称性。

  1. Practical hybrid strategy (pre-filter and refine): A proof-of-concept shows that using data-similarity for pre-filtering and data-influence for re-ranking achieves an ROC AUC of 0.83, compared to only 0.63 for the reverse direction, validating the hybrid approach.

实用的混合策略（预过滤+精化）：概念验证表明，用数据相似度预过滤、数据影响力重排可达到0.83的ROC AUC，而反向操作仅为0.63，验证了混合方案的可行性。

Methodology / 方法论

Experimental Setup: The authors generate 100 diverse prompts (via Gemini), have each LLM complete them, and form prompt-response queries. They rank 100,000 training documents (from DCLM, truncated to 300 tokens) using both BM25 (data-similarity) and EK-FAC influence functions (data-influence).

实验设置：作者生成100个多样化的prompt（通过Gemini），让每个LLM完成续写，形成prompt-response查询对。对100,000个训练文档（来自DCLM，截断至300 token）分别用BM25（数据相似度）和EK-FAC影响函数（数据影响力）进行排名。

Analysis Pipeline (9 steps A-I):

  • A: Create queries (100 prompts, diverse topics)
  • B: Rank training documents by both Sim and Inf
  • C: Visualize top documents with per-token score attribution
  • D: Compare document ranks between the two measures
  • E: Compute agreement at depth d (fraction of overlap in top-d sets)
  • F: Quantify asymmetry via directional rank prediction (Inf->Sim vs Sim->Inf)
  • G: Conduct Wilcoxon signed rank tests for statistical significance
  • H: Pre-filter with one measure and refine with the other (ROC analysis)
  • I: Compare across influence estimators (KFAC, DIAG, GRAD)

Computational Scale: Computing EK-FAC for one prompt on 100,000 documents for OLMo2-1B requires ~1 hour on 16 NVIDIA V100 GPUs. The full budget was ~18 days of GPU time.

计算规模：对OLMo2-1B计算一个prompt在100,000个文档上的EK-FAC影响力需要16块NVIDIA V100 GPU约1小时。总计算预算约为18天GPU时间。

Key Results / 关键结果

  • Agreement: For OLMo2-1B, 11% of the top-100 documents are identical between Sim and Inf. Agreement rises sharply in the top ranks and gradually approaches 1.0 at full depth (100,000).

一致性：OLMo2-1B中，两种方法的top-100文档有11%完全相同。一致性在靠前排名处急剧上升，在完整深度处趋近1.0。

  • Asymmetry: Inf->Sim consistently outperforms Sim->Inf. For OLMo2-1B, ~80% of Sim's top-100 are in Inf's top-20,000, but only ~65% vice versa. This pattern is statistically significant and holds across all tested models except Gemma3-1B at very high rank depths.

不对称性：Inf->Sim始终优于Sim->Inf。OLMo2-1B中，Sim的top-100有约80%在Inf的top-20,000内，而反向仅约65%。

  • Influence estimator quality matters: KFAC > DIAG > GRAD in terms of agreement with BM25 and strength of asymmetry. Better estimators systematically improve both.

影响力估计器质量有影响：在与BM25的一致性和不对称性强度方面，KFAC > DIAG > GRAD。

  • Qualitative examples: The third-ranked document by data-similarity for "opening locked doors" was irrelevant (about Scrum methodology), ranked 9,772nd by data-influence. Data-influence's top documents were all topically relevant.

定性示例：对于"开锁"查询，数据相似度排名第3的文档是无关的（关于Scrum方法论），而数据影响力将其排在第9,772位。

Critical Assessment / 批判性评估

Strengths:

  • Addresses a genuine gap: output tracing lacks ground truth, so comparing the two measures against each other is the right approach.
  • Thorough experimental design with 9 analysis steps, 6 LLMs, 3 influence estimators, and proper statistical testing (Wilcoxon tests with p-value analysis across all rank depths).
  • The practical hybrid strategy is well-motivated and directly actionable for systems like OLMo-Trace.

优势：

  • 针对真实空白：输出追溯缺乏标准答案，因此将两种度量相互比较是正确的方法。
  • 实验设计完善：9个分析步骤、6个LLM、3种影响力估计器，配合适当的统计检验。
  • 混合策略具有实际可操作性。

Weaknesses:

  • Scale limitation is significant: only 100,000 training documents (vs. trillions of tokens in real training sets) and 100 prompts. The authors acknowledge this requires ~18 days on 16 V100s, but it is unclear whether the asymmetry pattern holds at full scale.
  • All tested models are 1.7B parameters or smaller. Whether the findings generalize to production-scale models (30B+) is an open question the authors explicitly flag.
  • Prompts are not categorized by topic or difficulty, so it remains unknown whether the agreement pattern varies systematically across domains.
  • The proof-of-concept pre-filter experiment (Step H) uses an unrealistic inverse setup for comparison, weakening the practical claims.
  • BM25 is the only data-similarity measure tested; embedding-based methods (e.g., InfiniGram, semantic embeddings) may behave differently.

不足：

  • 规模限制显著：仅100,000个训练文档和100个prompt，与实际训练集（万亿token）差距巨大。
  • 所有测试模型均不超过1.7B参数，是否适用于生产级模型（30B+）未知。
  • prompt未按主题或难度分类，无法确定一致性模式是否在不同领域间系统性变化。
  • 预过滤实验的比较设置不够现实。
  • 仅测试了BM25一种数据相似度方法。

Relevance / 相关性

This work is relevant to anyone building or improving LLM output-tracing systems (like OLMo-Trace), studying training data attribution, or working on LLM interpretability. The practical implication is clear: a two-stage pipeline (cheap similarity retrieval + expensive influence re-ranking) can improve tracing quality without full influence computation. The finding that better influence estimators strengthen the agreement pattern provides additional motivation for investing in improved influence-function approximations.

本文对构建或改进LLM输出追溯系统（如OLMo-Trace）、研究训练数据归因或LLM可解释性的研究者具有参考价值。实际启示明确：两阶段流水线（低成本相似度检索 + 高成本影响力重排）可在不进行全量影响力计算的情况下提升追溯质量。
