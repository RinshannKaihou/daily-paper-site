# MINCE: Shrinking LLM Evaluation Datasets via Few-Model Monte Carlo Calibration

## Summary / 摘要

**English:** MINCE (Monte Carlo Informed N-sizing for Compact Evaluation) is a method for reducing the size of LLM evaluation benchmarks by asking *how many* items are needed rather than *which* items to keep. Given per-item evaluation logs from a small calibration pool of just 7 models, MINCE uses Monte Carlo simulation (10,000 random draws per candidate size) to identify the minimum subset size n* at which accuracy drift between subset and full benchmark is bounded. It then freezes a randomly sampled subset at that size. The method reduces IFEVAL by 54%, MMLU by 89%, and GSM8K by 70%, with maximum drift at most 2.62 percentage points on BF16 models and mean drift of 0.77-3.59 pp on held-out INT4 NPU models. Median GPU evaluation speedups range from 2.7x to 8.1x; NPU speedups reach 1.7-2.0x.

**Chinese:** MINCE（蒙特卡罗引导的紧凑评估N值确定）是一种通过回答"需要多少道题"而非"保留哪些题"来缩减LLM评估基准规模的方法。给定仅7个校准模型的逐题评估日志，MINCE在每个候选子集大小下进行10,000次蒙特卡罗随机抽样，度量子集与完整基准之间的最坏情况精度漂移（accuracy drift），找到漂移边际收益低于阈值的最小子集大小n*，然后在该大小下冻结一个随机采样子集。该方法将IFEVAL缩减54%、MMLU缩减89%、GSM8K缩减70%，BF16模型上最大漂移不超过2.62个百分点，在未参与校准的INT4 NPU模型上平均漂移仅0.77-3.59 pp。GPU评估中位加速比为2.7x-8.1x，NPU加速比达1.7-2.0x。

## Motivation / 研究动机

**English:** Full benchmark evaluation is increasingly costly as practitioners test many model variants (quantized, fine-tuned, deployment-specific) across benchmarks with hundreds to tens of thousands of items. This cost is especially acute on edge accelerators (NPUs) where inference throughput is orders of magnitude lower than datacenter GPUs. Existing subset selection methods (tinyBenchmarks, MetaBench, EssenceBench, SparseEval, TailoredBench) either require large calibration pools (395-5000+ models), learned item selectors, prediction layers, or optimization procedures -- all impractical for newer benchmarks where such infrastructure does not yet exist. MINCE addresses the practical question: can benchmark subsets be determined reliably from a small number of calibration models, without any learned components?

**Chinese:** 随着从业者需要在包含数百到数万题目的基准上测试大量模型变体（量化、微调、部署特定版本），完整基准评估的时间成本不断攀升。这在边缘加速器（NPU）上尤为严重，推理吞吐量比数据中心GPU低数个数量级。现有子集选择方法要么需要大规模校准池（395-5000+模型），要么依赖学习式项目选择器、预测层或优化程序，这些对于尚无此类基础设施的新基准而言都不切实际。MINCE回答的核心问题是：能否仅用少量校准模型、无需任何学习组件就可靠地确定基准子集？

## Method / 方法

**English:** MINCE operates in two stages:

1. **Monte Carlo N-Sizing:** For each candidate subset size n_t (swept at fixed increments: 50 for IFEVAL, 100 for GSM8K, 500 for MMLU), draw R=10,000 random subsets uniformly without replacement. For each subset, compute accuracy drift |acc(S, m_k) - acc(B, m_k)| for every (model, metric) pair and retain the worst case. Take P95 of these worst-case drifts across all R draws. Compute marginal gain Delta(n_t) = P95(n_{t-1}) - P95(n_t) and select n* as the first size where Delta < tau (set to 1 pp). This identifies where adding more items yields diminishing returns in drift reduction.

2. **Subset Construction:** Sample n* items via uniform random sampling and freeze the subset for all future evaluations. The paper shows that stratified sampling (by natural categories) and k-means clustering on sentence embeddings produce comparable drift at n*, validating that random sampling suffices once the right size is determined.

Key design choices: (a) the method requires only per-item pass/fail logs from K calibration models, not model weights or embeddings; (b) drift is measured as worst-case across all models and metrics, providing conservative bounds; (c) the 95th percentile ensures 95% of random subsets at n* meet the drift bound.

**Chinese:** MINCE分两个阶段运作：

1. **蒙特卡罗N值确定：** 对每个候选子集大小n_t（以固定步长扫描：IFEVAL为50，GSM8K为100，MMLU为500），均匀无放回地抽取R=10,000个随机子集。对每个子集计算所有（模型，指标）对的精度漂移，取最坏情况。再取这些最坏情况漂移在所有R次抽样中的第95百分位（P95）。计算边际增益Delta(n_t) = P95(n_{t-1}) - P95(n_t)，选择Delta首次低于阈值tau（设为1 pp）的n_t作为n*。

2. **子集构建：** 在n*大小下进行均匀随机采样并冻结子集用于后续所有评估。论文证明分层采样和k-means聚类采样产生的漂移与随机采样相当，验证了一旦确定正确大小，随机采样即可胜任。

核心设计：(a) 仅需K个校准模型的逐题通过/失败日志，无需模型权重或嵌入；(b) 漂移取所有模型和指标的最坏情况，提供保守上界；(c) 使用第95百分位确保95%的随机子集在n*处满足漂移约束。

## Key Results / 关键结果

**English:**

| Benchmark | Full Size | n* (Subset) | Reduction | Mean Drift | Max Drift |
|-----------|-----------|-------------|-----------|------------|-----------|
| IFEVAL    | 541       | 250         | 54%       | 0.99 pp    | 1.83 pp   |
| MMLU      | 14,042    | 1,500       | 89%       | 0.44 pp    | 1.20 pp   |
| GSM8K     | 1,319     | 400         | 70%       | 1.40 pp    | 2.62 pp   |

- **NPU generalization:** On 3 held-out INT4 NPU models not used for calibration, mean drift is 3.59/0.77/0.77 pp on IFEVAL/MMLU/GSM8K. MMLU and GSM8K remain within 1.48 pp max drift; IFEVAL shows higher sensitivity for Phi-4-mini-Inst (8.17 pp).
- **Model rankings preserved:** Spearman rho = 1.0 on IFEVAL and GSM8K, 0.96 on MMLU.
- **Speedups:** Median GPU speedups of 2.7x (IFEVAL), 8.1x (MMLU), 3.3x (GSM8K); NPU speedups of 1.7-2.0x on IFEVAL/MMLU, up to 3.4x for individual models.
- **vs. tinyBenchmarks:** MINCE achieves 12x lower mean drift on MMLU (0.44 vs. 5.26 pp) and 3.3x lower on GSM8K (1.40 vs. 4.68 pp) while using 57x fewer calibration models (7 vs. 395).
- **Calibration robustness:** n* for MMLU and GSM8K is perfectly stable across all 98 leave-K-out combinations (K=1,2,3,4). IFEVAL n* is stable in 87/98 cases; the 11 unstable cases all involve removing both Qwen2.5-3B and Mistral-7B.
- **Threshold sensitivity:** n* is stable across tau in [0.75, 1.25] pp for all three benchmarks.

**Chinese:**

- IFEVAL从541题缩减至250题（54%），MMLU从14,042题缩减至1,500题（89%），GSM8K从1,319题缩减至400题（70%），最大漂移均不超过2.62 pp。
- 在3个未参与校准的INT4 NPU模型上验证泛化性，MMLU和GSM8K最大漂移仅1.48 pp。
- 模型排名完美保持：Spearman相关系数在IFEVAL和GSM8K上为1.0，MMLU上为0.96。
- 与tinyBenchmarks相比，MINCE在MMLU上平均漂移降低12倍，GSM8K上降低3.3倍，且仅使用1/57的校准模型。
- 校准池鲁棒性高：将7个模型减至3个时，MMLU和GSM8K的n*完全不变。

## Technical Details / 技术细节

**English:**

- **Calibration models (7 BF16):** Llama-3.1-8B (base), Qwen2.5-3B-Instruct, Mistral-7B-Instruct, Phi-4-mini-Instruct, Llama-3.1-8B-Instruct, Qwen3-8B, Qwen3.5-35B-A3B (MoE). Spanning 3B-35B parameters across 4 architecture families, with accuracy ranging 25%-89% to cover both weak and strong performers.
- **Held-out NPU models (3 INT4):** Llama-3.1-8B-Inst, Phi-4-mini, Qwen3-8B -- testing generalization to unseen quantization and hardware.
- **Evaluation harness:** lm-eval-harness for all BF16 accuracies. Hardware: AMD Instinct MI210 and MI300X GPUs; AMD RyzenAI NPUs.
- **Sampling strategies compared:** Uniform random, stratified random (by natural categories: 25 instruction types for IFEVAL, 57 subjects for MMLU), k-means clustering on Qwen3-Embedding-8B embeddings. All three produce comparable drift (mean differences < 0.2 pp across 100 seeds), validating that at n*, random sampling variance is already sufficiently low.
- **Monte Carlo parameters:** R=10,000 draws per candidate size; tau=1 pp marginal gain threshold; seed 42 for final subset construction.

**Chinese:**

- **校准模型：** 7个BF16模型，跨4个架构系列，参数量3B-35B，精度覆盖25%-89%。
- **验证模型：** 3个INT4 NPU模型，测试对未见量化方案和硬件的泛化性。
- **评估框架：** 使用lm-eval-harness收集所有BF16精度。硬件为AMD Instinct MI210/MI300X GPU和AMD RyzenAI NPU。
- **采样策略对比：** 均匀随机、分层随机、k-means聚类三种策略在n*处产生的漂移差异不超过0.2 pp，证明随机采样即可。
- **蒙特卡罗参数：** 每个候选大小10,000次抽样；边际增益阈值tau=1 pp；最终子集构建使用种子42。

## Critical Assessment / 批判性评估

**English:**

- **Strengths:** (1) Remarkable simplicity -- the method requires no learned components, no optimization, no embeddings, just pass/fail logs from 7 models and a standard Monte Carlo procedure. This makes it immediately applicable to any new benchmark. (2) Strong empirical results with 12x lower drift than tinyBenchmarks using 57x fewer calibration models. (3) Thorough ablations on sampling strategies, calibration pool sensitivity, and threshold sensitivity that build confidence in robustness. (4) Practical NPU validation demonstrates real-world applicability for edge deployment scenarios.

- **Weaknesses:** (1) The calibration pool spans only 3B-35B models from 4 families and excludes frontier-scale (100B+) and non-transformer architectures. Whether the chosen n* remains sufficient for models with fundamentally different error patterns is unvalidated. (2) The frozen subset is model-agnostic by design, meaning it cannot exploit model-specific signal to further reduce items -- this is simultaneously a feature (simplicity) and a limitation (efficiency ceiling). (3) IFEVAL drift on NPU is notably higher (8.17 pp for Phi-4-mini-Inst), suggesting the method may be less reliable for generative tasks under quantization. (4) The 1 pp marginal-gain threshold is a practical heuristic but may not suit safety-critical applications where tighter bounds are needed. (5) Each new benchmark still requires a full calibration run on all items with the calibration models, which partially offsets the cost savings for one-time evaluation.

- **Missing comparisons:** No comparison with MetaBench (Kipnis et al., 2025) which also targets few-model calibration, or with EssenceBench and SparseEval on the same benchmarks. The tinyBenchmarks comparison is only on MMLU and GSM8K (IFEVAL not available in tinyBenchmarks).

**Chinese:**

- **优势：** (1) 方法极其简洁，无需学习组件，仅需7个模型的逐题日志和标准蒙特卡罗过程，可立即应用于任何新基准。(2) 比tinyBenchmarks漂移低12倍且仅用1/57校准模型。(3) 对采样策略、校准池敏感性、阈值敏感性做了充分消融实验。(4) NPU验证展示了边缘部署场景的实用性。

- **不足：** (1) 校准池仅覆盖3B-35B、4个架构系列，未验证前沿规模（100B+）和非Transformer架构。(2) 冻结子集与模型无关，无法利用模型特定信号进一步缩减。(3) IFEVAL在NPU上的漂移明显偏高（Phi-4-mini-Inst达8.17 pp），表明量化条件下生成式任务的可靠性有待提升。(4) 1 pp阈值是实用启发式，安全关键应用可能需要更严格的约束。(5) 每个新基准仍需完整的校准运行，部分抵消了成本节省。

## Relevance to Broader Research / 与更广泛研究的关联

**English:** MINCE addresses a growing practical bottleneck in LLM development: the cost of evaluation across many model variants. As the field moves toward edge deployment with quantized models on NPUs, efficient evaluation becomes critical for rapid iteration. The paper's key insight -- that *how many* items matter more than *which* items at sufficient scale -- challenges the premise of learned item selection methods and connects to classical sampling theory. The approach is complementary to methods like tinyBenchmarks: MINCE offers a lightweight, immediately applicable solution for any benchmark, while IRT-based methods may achieve smaller subsets when large calibration pools are available. A natural extension would be hybrid approaches combining MINCE's size determination with adaptive item selection at evaluation time.

**Chinese:** MINCE解决了LLM开发中日益突出的实际瓶颈：跨多模型变体的评估成本。随着领域向NPU上的量化模型边缘部署发展，高效评估对快速迭代至关重要。论文的核心洞察——在足够规模下"需要多少题"比"保留哪些题"更重要——挑战了学习式项目选择方法的前提，并与经典采样理论相呼应。该方法与tinyBenchmarks互补：MINCE提供了轻量级、可立即应用于任何基准的方案，而当大规模校准池可用时，基于IRT的方法可能实现更小的子集。自然的扩展方向是将MINCE的大小确定与评估时的自适应项目选择相结合的混合方法。
