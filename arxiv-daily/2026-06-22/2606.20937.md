# Learning through Internalization

## TL;DR

This paper formally studies **internalization** -- the process by which neural networks absorb an explicit multi-step computational procedure (such as chain-of-thought reasoning) into their weights, enabling the same computation in fewer steps. The authors investigate which tasks are easy or hard to internalize using semiautomata simulation as a testbed, reveal that internalization favors width over depth, show that prime-order semiautomata are harder to internalize, expose a fundamental OOD degradation tradeoff, and provide the first provable analysis of learning through internalization for the sparse parity task.

本文正式研究了**内化(internalization)**现象——即神经网络将显式的多步计算过程（如链式思维推理）吸收进自身权重，从而用更少步骤完成相同计算的过程。作者以半自动机模拟为测试平台，研究了哪些任务容易或难以内化，揭示了内化更偏好宽度而非深度的规律，证明了素数阶半自动机更难内化，暴露了内化带来的根本性分布外(OOD)性能退化问题，并首次对稀疏奇偶校验任务给出了通过内化进行学习的可证明分析。

---

## Problem Setting / 问题背景

The paper addresses a fundamental question in machine learning: how can neural networks learn tasks that appear computationally intractable when approached directly (end-to-end), but become learnable when decomposed into intermediate steps?

The authors formalize **internalization** (Definition 1): given a neural network h_theta and a procedure P that uses h_theta multiple times (e.g., autoregressive CoT generation), internalization occurs when a new parameterization h_{theta'} from the same hypothesis class can reproduce P(h_theta) on all inputs while being strictly faster -- computing the answer in fewer steps (ideally a single forward pass).

The key learning paradigm is: (1) first train a model with explicit chain-of-thought supervision to learn the "slow" multi-step system, then (2) progressively remove CoT tokens via a curriculum, forcing the model to internalize the computation into its weights. This two-phase approach can sidestep the computational hardness of direct end-to-end learning.

本文解决了一个机器学习中的基本问题：神经网络如何学习那些直接端到端方法在计算上看似不可行、但分解为中间步骤后变得可学习的任务？

作者将**内化**形式化定义为：给定神经网络h_theta和一个多次使用h_theta的过程P（如自回归CoT生成），当同一假设类中存在新的参数化h_{theta'}能在所有输入上复现P(h_theta)的输出且严格更快时（理想情况下仅需一次前向传播），即发生了内化。

核心学习范式为：(1) 首先用显式链式思维监督训练模型，学习"慢"的多步系统；(2) 然后通过课程学习逐步移除CoT标记，迫使模型将计算内化到权重中。这种两阶段方法可以绕过直接端到端学习的计算困难性。

## Method / 方法

### Experimental Framework: Semiautomata Simulation

The authors study internalization through the lens of **semiautomata** -- state machines with a state space, input space, and transition function. The task is to predict the final state after T transition steps given input symbols and an initial state. They primarily use modular counter semiautomata C_n (states from Z_n, transition q_{t+1} = (q_t + sigma_t) mod n) and the non-commutative permutation group S_3.

Three training regimes are compared:
- **End-to-end (E2E)**: only the final state q_T is supervised
- **Chain-of-thought (CoT)**: all intermediate states q_1, ..., q_{T-1} are included as supervision
- **Internalization via curriculum**: starts with CoT training, then progressively removes intermediate tokens (left curriculum, right curriculum, or a novel inductive curriculum)

### Theoretical Framework: Parity Learning with Linear Attention

For the provable result, the authors analyze a simplified one-layer transformer with linear attention and a fixed nonlinear MLP (piecewise-linear parity activation). The model learns hidden sparse parity over d-dimensional binary inputs via a curriculum that progressively removes CoT tokens from the left. The training algorithm (Algorithm 2) operates in k phases, drawing fresh batches and rounding weights after each phase.

### 实验框架：半自动机模拟

作者通过**半自动机**视角研究内化——半自动机是具有状态空间、输入空间和转移函数的状态机。任务是给定输入符号和初始状态，预测经过T步转移后的最终状态。主要使用模循环计数器半自动机C_n（状态取自Z_n，转移规则q_{t+1} = (q_t + sigma_t) mod n）和非交换置换群S_3。

比较三种训练方式：
- **端到端(E2E)**：仅监督最终状态q_T
- **链式思维(CoT)**：包含所有中间状态q_1, ..., q_{T-1}作为监督
- **通过课程学习内化**：先进行CoT训练，再逐步移除中间标记（左课程、右课程或新提出的归纳课程）

### 理论框架：线性注意力下的奇偶校验学习

在可证明结果部分，作者分析了一个简化的单层线性注意力Transformer，配合固定的非线性MLP（分段线性奇偶激活函数）。模型通过从左逐步移除CoT标记的课程学习，学习d维二进制输入上的隐藏稀疏奇偶校验。训练算法在k个阶段中运行，每个阶段抽取新批次并对权重进行取整。

## Key Results / 关键结果

### 1. E2E Fails, CoT Succeeds, Internalization Works with Shallow Models
- E2E training completely fails to learn semiautomata simulation -- even "simple" local operations like mod-n addition create long-range dependencies that are hard to learn directly.
- CoT training succeeds easily with good in-distribution and OOD generalization, but at high inference cost.
- Internalization succeeds even with depth-1 transformers, demonstrating that the model does not need to explicitly represent all intermediate reasoning steps.

### 2. Width Matters More Than Depth
A striking finding: at a fixed parameter budget, **wider and shallower** architectures consistently outperform deeper ones for internalization (Figure 3, right). This contradicts the intuition that internalized reasoning tokens would be "unfolded vertically" across layers. Instead, the computation appears to be internalized "horizontally" across the model's width.

### 3. Prime-Order Semiautomata Are Harder to Internalize
Among modular counter semiautomata C_n, those with prime n (e.g., C_13, C_17) show noticeably lower internalization success rates than those with composite n (e.g., C_10, C_12, C_16). **Theorem 1** provides the explanation: C_n can be represented by a transformer with MLP width O(sum of prime-power factors of n). Smooth numbers (small prime factors) yield compact representations; primes require width proportional to n itself.

### 4. The Flip Side: OOD Degradation
Internalization comes with a fundamental tradeoff: OOD performance degrades progressively as more CoT tokens are removed (Figure 1, right; Figure 4). The internalized models tend to learn shortcut solutions that generalize worse than the representations learned during CoT training. This parallels how humans who automate skills can become more error-prone under novel conditions.

### 5. Provable Internalization for Sparse Parity (Theorem 2)
The paper's main theoretical contribution: a one-layer linear-attention transformer provably learns hidden sparse parity through internalization. After Phase 1, the model correctly generates all explicit CoT tokens. After the full k-phase curriculum, it directly computes the parity in a single forward pass. The runtime is poly(d, log(1/delta)), and the resulting representation is computationally hard to find via direct learning (hard in the statistical query model).

### 6. Connection to Positive Distribution Shift
The authors connect internalization to the Positive Distribution Shift (PDS) framework (Medvedev et al., 2026). The curriculum of training distributions used during internalization constitutes a "positive distribution shift" relative to the hard end-to-end distribution. Notably, the curriculum can often be replaced by a **single mixture distribution** over all CoT lengths, which is simpler, more natural, and allows the same model to both answer directly and generate full CoT.

### 1. E2E失败、CoT成功、浅层模型的内化有效
- E2E训练完全无法学习半自动机模拟——即使"简单"的局部运算（如mod-n加法）也会产生难以直接学习的长程依赖。
- CoT训练轻松成功，分布内和OOD泛化良好，但推理代价高。
- 内化甚至在深度为1的Transformer上也能成功，表明模型无需显式表示所有中间推理步骤。

### 2. 宽度比深度更重要
一个显著发现：在固定参数预算下，**更宽更浅**的架构在内化方面始终优于更深的架构（图3右）。这与"内化的推理标记会沿层'垂直展开'"的直觉相矛盾。相反，计算似乎在模型宽度上"水平"内化。

### 3. 素数阶半自动机更难内化
在模循环计数器半自动机C_n中，素数n（如C_13、C_17）的内化成功率明显低于合数n（如C_10、C_12、C_16）。**定理1**给出了解释：C_n可用MLP宽度为O(n的素数幂因子之和)的Transformer表示。光滑数（小素因子）产生紧凑表示；素数则需要与n成正比的宽度。

### 4. 内化的另一面：OOD性能退化
内化伴随着根本性权衡：随着更多CoT标记被移除，OOD性能逐步退化（图1右；图4）。内化后的模型倾向于学习捷径解，其泛化能力不如CoT训练获得的表示。这与人类将技能自动化后在新情境下更容易出错的现象类似。

### 5. 稀疏奇偶校验的可证明内化（定理2）
本文的主要理论贡献：单层线性注意力Transformer可证明地通过内化学习隐藏稀疏奇偶校验。第1阶段后，模型正确生成所有显式CoT标记。完成全部k阶段课程后，它在单次前向传播中直接计算奇偶校验。运行时间为poly(d, log(1/delta))，所得表示通过直接学习在计算上是困难的（在统计查询模型中困难）。

### 6. 与正向分布偏移的联系
作者将内化与正向分布偏移(PDS)框架（Medvedev et al., 2026）建立联系。内化过程中使用的训练分布课程构成了相对于困难端到端分布的"正向分布偏移"。值得注意的是，课程通常可以被所有CoT长度上的**单一混合分布**替代，这更简单、更自然，并允许同一模型既能直接回答又能生成完整CoT。

## Technical Contributions / 技术贡献

1. **Formal definition of internalization** (Definition 1) that cleanly separates it from distillation: both the slow and fast systems must belong to the same hypothesis class, and the procedure P can only query (not modify) the base model.

2. **Theorem 1** (Compact representation of C_n): A transformer with O(log T) layers, O(k) embedding dimension, and O(sum of prime-power factors) MLP width can simulate any counter semiautomaton C_n over T steps. This yields exponential improvement over generic O(|Q|^2) width bounds for smooth numbers.

3. **Theorem 2** (Provable internalization for parity): The first formal proof that learning through internalization can enable a model to find representations that are computationally hard to discover via direct learning. The algorithm runs in polynomial time and achieves exact internalization on all inputs.

4. **Novel inductive curriculum**: A new curriculum strategy that trains on sequences with progressively larger leaps between states, complementing existing left/right curriculum approaches.

5. **Mixture distribution alternative**: Demonstrating that curriculum-based internalization can be replaced by training on a single mixture distribution, connecting to PDS and offering practical benefits (no curriculum scheduling, single model supporting multiple inference budgets).

## Critical Assessment / 批判性评估

### Strengths
- **Strong theoretical grounding**: The paper does not just present empirical observations but provides formal definitions, representation theorems, and provable learning guarantees. Theorem 2 is particularly significant as the first provable example of learning through internalization.
- **Well-chosen experimental setting**: Semiautomata provide a clean, controllable testbed where the structure of the task can be systematically varied (order n, commutativity, simulation horizon T).
- **Honest about limitations**: The paper explicitly identifies and characterizes the OOD degradation problem rather than only highlighting positive results. The parallel to human automatization is apt and well-drawn.
- **Surprising and actionable finding**: The "width over depth" result has practical implications for how models should be configured when internalization is the goal.

### Weaknesses
- **Simplified theoretical model**: The provable result (Theorem 2) uses linear attention, a fixed piecewise-linear activation, and weight rounding -- all significant simplifications relative to practical transformers with softmax attention and learned MLP layers. The gap between the theoretical model and practice is acknowledged but not bridged.
- **Limited task scope**: The empirical study is restricted to semiautomata simulation and parity tasks. It remains unclear how findings transfer to the more complex reasoning tasks (arithmetic, multi-step logic) where CoT internalization has been previously studied (Deng et al., 2024).
- **OOD degradation not resolved**: The paper identifies but does not solve the fundamental OOD tradeoff. The mixture distribution approach (Section 5.2) partially addresses this for in-distribution learning but the degradation under distribution shift remains an open issue.
- **Curriculum sensitivity**: The success of internalization depends on curriculum design choices (left vs. right vs. inductive, scheduling) that are themselves task-specific, somewhat undermining the generality of the approach.
- **Missing author-level comparison with concurrent work**: Lin et al. (2025b) showed that internalized reasoning can overfit to specific patterns. The paper acknowledges this but does not deeply engage with what conditions distinguish successful from unsuccessful internalization beyond their semiautomata setting.

### 优势
- **理论基础扎实**：论文不仅呈现实证观察，还提供了形式化定义、表示定理和可证明的学习保证。定理2作为首个通过内化学习的可证明示例尤为重要。
- **实验设置精心选择**：半自动机提供了干净、可控的测试平台，可以系统地改变任务结构。
- **对局限性坦诚**：论文明确识别和刻画了OOD退化问题，而非只展示正面结果。
- **发现出人意料且有实用价值**："宽度优于深度"的结果对内化场景下的模型配置具有实际指导意义。

### 不足
- **理论模型过于简化**：可证明结果使用线性注意力、固定分段线性激活和权重取整，与实际Transformer差距显著。
- **任务范围有限**：实证研究仅限于半自动机模拟和奇偶校验任务，向更复杂推理任务的迁移性不明。
- **OOD退化未解决**：论文识别但未解决根本性的OOD权衡问题。
- **课程学习敏感性**：内化成功依赖于任务特定的课程设计选择，一定程度上削弱了方法的通用性。

## Relevance to Broader Research / 与更广泛研究的关联

This paper is highly relevant to several active research directions:

- **Implicit vs. explicit reasoning in LLMs**: As the field debates whether LLMs can reason without explicit CoT, this work provides principled understanding of when and how explicit reasoning can be compressed into weights.
- **Inference-time compute optimization**: Internalization offers a path to reduce inference costs for reasoning tasks, directly relevant to deployment of reasoning models (e.g., o1-style systems).
- **Curriculum learning theory**: The connection to Positive Distribution Shift provides a theoretical framework for understanding why curricula and mixture distributions facilitate learning.
- **Expressivity vs. learnability gap**: The paper cleanly demonstrates settings where a model has sufficient expressivity to solve a task but cannot learn the solution directly -- internalization bridges this gap.

本文与当前多个活跃研究方向高度相关：隐式推理与显式推理的权衡、推理时计算优化、课程学习理论，以及表达能力与可学习性之间的鸿沟。
