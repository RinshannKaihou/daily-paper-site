# Orthogonal Representation Editing: Decoupling Semantic Entanglement in Batch Knowledge Editing of LLMs

## TL;DR

Batch knowledge editing methods (e.g., MEMIT) degrade when edits share semantic structures because their edit vectors overlap in representation space. ORE fixes this by (1) building a "general semantic subspace" via PCA on structurally-similar-but-factually-unrelated samples, (2) projecting each edit vector to be orthogonal to that subspace, and (3) injecting the cleaned edits through a gated non-linear representation head built on ReFT. On ZsRE, CounterFact, and cross-lingual Bi-ZsRE, ORE consistently achieves the highest average scores across LLaMA-3-8B and Qwen-2.5-7B, with particularly strong gains in cross-lingual settings (+4.4 pp over MEMIT on Bi-ZsRE).

批量知识编辑方法（如MEMIT）在编辑请求共享语义结构时性能下降，因为编辑向量在表示空间中发生重叠。ORE通过以下方式解决：(1) 对结构相似但事实无关的样本进行PCA构建"通用语义子空间"；(2) 将每个编辑向量正交投影到该子空间之外；(3) 通过基于ReFT的门控非线性表示头注入清洗后的编辑。在ZsRE、CounterFact和跨语言Bi-ZsRE上，ORE在LLaMA-3-8B和Qwen-2.5-7B上均取得了最高平均分，在跨语言场景中提升尤为显著（Bi-ZsRE上比MEMIT高4.4个百分点）。

## Abstract

Knowledge editing aims to efficiently update factual information in Large Language Models (LLMs) without full retraining. However, existing methods still suffer from performance degradation in batch knowledge editing. The authors identify that semantic representation entanglement -- where overlapping concepts and shared syntactic patterns accumulate interference in the representation space -- reduces editing precision. To bridge this gap, they propose Orthogonal Representation Editing (ORE), which performs edits in the hidden representation space of LLMs by constructing a general semantic subspace and enforcing orthogonal constraints on edit vectors, effectively decoupling semantic entanglement. Furthermore, a gated non-linear representation head is introduced to enable adaptive learning of editing locations and precise control over knowledge injection. Extensive experiments show that ORE outperforms existing methods and achieves superior performance in cross-lingual knowledge editing scenarios.

知识编辑旨在无需完全重训练即可高效更新大语言模型（LLM）中的事实信息。然而，现有方法在批量知识编辑中仍然存在性能退化问题。作者发现语义表示纠缠——重叠概念和共享句法模式在表示空间中累积干扰——降低了编辑精度。为此，他们提出正交表示编辑（ORE），在LLM的隐藏表示空间中执行编辑，通过构建通用语义子空间并对编辑向量施加正交约束来有效解耦语义纠缠。此外，引入门控非线性表示头以实现编辑位置的自适应学习和知识注入的精确控制。大量实验表明ORE优于现有方法，并在跨语言知识编辑场景中表现出色。

## Problem Statement / 问题提出

Batch knowledge editing methods like MEMIT suffer performance degradation when editing many facts simultaneously. The core issue: when edits share semantic structure (e.g., "The capital of [Country] is [City]"), their edit vectors activate overlapping regions in the model's representation space, causing mutual interference. This is what the authors call **semantic representation entanglement**. The problem is especially severe in cross-lingual settings, where shared multilingual semantic spaces allow edits to propagate unintentionally across languages.

批量知识编辑方法（如MEMIT）在同时编辑大量事实时性能下降。核心问题在于：当编辑请求共享语义结构时（例如"[国家]的首都是[城市]"），它们的编辑向量在模型表示空间中激活重叠区域，导致相互干扰。作者将此称为**语义表示纠缠**。该问题在跨语言场景中尤为严重，因为共享的多语言语义空间使编辑会意外跨语言传播。

**Empirical evidence**: On LLaMA-3-8B with entangled "capital" samples, MEMIT's editing success drops from 86% (random samples) to 71% (French), 64% (Chinese), and 56% (cross-lingual French-Chinese). AlphaEdit shows similar degradation.

**实证证据**：在LLaMA-3-8B上使用纠缠的"首都"样本，MEMIT的编辑成功率从86%（随机样本）下降到71%（法语）、64%（中文）和56%（跨语言法中混合）。AlphaEdit也表现出类似的退化。

## Proposed Method / 方法

ORE consists of two main components:

### 1. Representation Subspace Orthogonalization / 表示子空间正交化

- **General Semantic Subspace Construction**: Collect N structurally-similar-but-factually-unrelated samples (drawn from ZsRE/CounterFact, sharing the same prompt template but different subject-object pairs). Extract hidden states at the target layer and apply PCA to obtain the top-k principal components, forming an orthogonal basis U_gs that spans the General Semantic Subspace S_gs.
- **Orthogonal Constraint on Edit Vectors**: Compute the edit vector as Delta = h_alt - h_pred (target minus current prediction representations). Remove its projection onto S_gs to get the orthogonalized edit direction: Delta_orth = Delta - U_gs * U_gs^T * Delta. The target representation becomes h_tgt = h_src,orig + Delta_orth.

- **通用语义子空间构建**：收集N个结构相似但事实无关的样本（从ZsRE/CounterFact中抽取，共享相同提示模板但不同主宾对）。在目标层提取隐藏状态并进行PCA，取前k个主成分组成正交基U_gs，张成通用语义子空间S_gs。
- **编辑向量的正交约束**：计算编辑向量Delta = h_alt - h_pred（目标减去当前预测的表示）。去除其在S_gs上的投影得到正交化编辑方向：Delta_orth = Delta - U_gs * U_gs^T * Delta。目标表示变为h_tgt = h_src,orig + Delta_orth。

### 2. Gated Non-linear Representation Head / 门控非线性表示头

Built on top of ReFT (Representation Fine-Tuning), ORE adds two innovations:

- **Non-linear Bottleneck Structure**: A low-rank projection (W_down: d->r) followed by SiLU activation and expansion (W_up: r->d), producing the edit vector v_edit. This captures finer-grained semantic features than linear interventions.
- **Dynamic Gating Mechanism**: A parallel gating network produces a binary gate g in {0, 1} per token position, deciding where to intervene. It uses MLP + normalization + SiLU + sigmoid, then applies a threshold with straight-through estimator (STE) for gradient flow. The final intervention: h' = h + g * v_edit.

基于ReFT（表示微调），ORE增加了两项创新：

- **非线性瓶颈结构**：低秩投影（W_down: d->r）经SiLU激活后扩展（W_up: r->d），生成编辑向量v_edit。比线性干预能捕获更细粒度的语义特征。
- **动态门控机制**：并行门控网络为每个token位置生成二值门控g in {0, 1}，决定是否干预。使用MLP+归一化+SiLU+sigmoid，然后用直通估计器（STE）进行阈值截断以保持梯度流通。最终干预：h' = h + g * v_edit。

### Loss Functions / 损失函数

Total loss: L_total = lambda_1 * L_ce + lambda_2 * L_kl + lambda_3 * L_gate + lambda_4 * L_orth

- L_orth: Cosine similarity loss enforcing orthogonality to S_gs
- L_gate: BCE loss for gating supervision (activate on subject tokens, silent elsewhere)
- L_ce: Cross-entropy for correct output
- L_kl: KL-divergence for preserving locality on unrelated prompts

## Experiments / 实验

### Setup
- **Models**: LLaMA-3-8B, Qwen-2.5-7B
- **Datasets**: ZsRE, CounterFact (2000 and 5000 edits), Bi-ZsRE (1600 edits, cross-lingual)
- **Metrics**: Efficacy (correct on original prompt), Generality (correct on paraphrased prompts), Specificity (unrelated knowledge preserved), Average
- **Baselines**: FT, ROME, MEMIT, PRUNE, RECT, NSE, AlphaEdit, ReFT

### Key Results

**ZsRE + CounterFact (2000 edits, LLaMA-3-8B)**:
| Method | ZsRE Avg. | CounterFact Avg. |
|---|---|---|
| MEMIT | 67.81 | 84.14 |
| AlphaEdit | 67.75 | 86.34 |
| **ORE** | **71.03** | **91.34** |

ORE achieves the best average across both datasets. On CounterFact, ORE leads by ~5 pp over the next best method. The advantage holds at 5000 edits and on Qwen-2.5-7B.

**Cross-lingual Bi-ZsRE (1600 edits, LLaMA-3-8B)**:
| Method | Avg. |
|---|---|
| MEMIT | 60.96 |
| AlphaEdit | 60.22 |
| **ORE** | **65.36** |

ORE improves by +4.4 pp over MEMIT, demonstrating strong cross-lingual robustness.

**Anti-Interference Analysis**: ORE reduces cosine similarity between edit vectors and the general semantic subspace to about 1/6 of MEMIT's level, confirming effective decoupling.

**Ablation Study (1000 edits on ZsRE)**:
| Variant | Avg. |
|---|---|
| Full ORE | 72.25 |
| -L_orth | 70.43 |
| -SiLU | 70.33 |
| -Gate | 69.85 |

All components contribute; removing the gating mechanism has the largest negative impact.

## Critical Assessment / 批判性评估

**Strengths**:
1. The identification of semantic representation entanglement is well-motivated with clear empirical evidence (the entangled vs. random sample experiment is convincing).
2. The geometric intuition is clean: shared semantics form a subspace; project edits orthogonal to it. The PCA-based construction is straightforward and reproducible.
3. Strong cross-lingual results address a genuine gap -- most knowledge editing methods are evaluated only monolingually.
4. The cosine similarity analysis (Figure 4) provides direct geometric evidence that ORE actually decouples edits from the shared subspace, not just correlational performance gains.

**Weaknesses**:
1. **Specificity scores are consistently low** across all settings (29-35 range on ZsRE). ORE's representation-space soft constraints are inherently weaker than parameter-level isolation for preserving unrelated knowledge. The authors acknowledge this but it remains a practical limitation.
2. **Scale limitations**: Experiments are limited to 7B-8B models. The authors note that larger models have higher-dimensional representation spaces with more complex entanglement patterns, and it is unclear whether the PCA-based subspace construction will scale.
3. **Subspace construction depends on task structure**: The general semantic subspace requires structurally-similar auxiliary samples. In practice, knowing the template structure of incoming edits may not always be feasible.
4. **Limited evaluation scope**: Only factual QA benchmarks are tested. The impact on downstream reasoning, multi-hop questions, or real-world deployment scenarios is unexplored.
5. **Comparison fairness**: ORE builds on ReFT, which itself is a strong representation editing baseline. Part of ORE's gain may come from the non-linear bottleneck and gating improvements to ReFT, independent of the orthogonalization contribution. The ablation partially addresses this but does not fully disentangle these contributions.

## Relevance / 相关性

This work is relevant to anyone working on scalable knowledge editing for LLMs, especially in multilingual or batch settings. The semantic entanglement diagnosis and the orthogonal projection remedy offer a geometric lens that could generalize beyond the specific ORE framework -- e.g., any representation-editing method could adopt subspace orthogonalization as a pre-processing step. The cross-lingual results are particularly noteworthy given the growing demand for multilingual LLM maintenance.

本文与LLM的可扩展知识编辑研究密切相关，尤其适用于多语言或批量设置。语义纠缠的诊断和正交投影的解决方案提供了一种几何视角，可推广到ORE框架之外——例如，任何表示编辑方法都可以将子空间正交化作为预处理步骤。鉴于对多语言LLM维护的需求日益增长，跨语言实验结果尤为值得关注。
