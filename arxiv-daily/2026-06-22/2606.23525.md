# Self-Compacting Language Model Agents

## TL;DR
SelfCompact is a training-free scaffold that lets LM agents decide *when* and *how* to compress their own context during long-horizon tasks. It pairs a compaction tool (the model summarizes its trajectory) with a lightweight rubric (a checklist that gates whether compression should fire). Across 6 benchmarks and 7 models, SelfCompact matches or exceeds fixed-interval summarization at 30-70% lower per-question cost, improving over no-compaction baselines by up to 18.1 points on math and 5-9 points on agentic search.

SelfCompact是一个无需训练的推理时脚手架，让语言模型智能体自主决定*何时*以及*如何*压缩自身上下文。它将压缩工具（模型自行总结轨迹）与轻量级评判标准（判断是否应该触发压缩的检查清单）配对使用。在6个基准和7个模型上，SelfCompact在每题成本降低30-70%的情况下，匹配或超越固定间隔摘要方法，相比无压缩基线在数学任务上最高提升18.1分，在智能体搜索任务上提升5-9分。

## Problem & Motivation / 问题与动机

Long agent traces accumulate stale content -- abandoned reasoning branches, dead-end search results, flawed case splits -- that anchors subsequent generations and eventually outgrows the context window. This is known as **context rot**: feeding a model its own stale reasoning degrades performance. Current solutions use **fixed-interval compaction** (summarize every k tokens or turns), which is content-agnostic. A fixed threshold cannot distinguish a model that just resolved a sub-problem from one mid-derivation; poorly timed compression wipes verified facts mid-reasoning and forces the model to guess.

长链推理中，智能体的上下文轨迹会积累大量过时内容——被放弃的推理分支、无效的搜索结果、错误的分类讨论——这些"垃圾"不仅占用上下文窗口，还会锚定后续生成，导致所谓的**上下文腐烂（context rot）**。现有方案采用**固定间隔压缩**（每隔k个token或k轮进行摘要），但这种方式完全不感知内容状态。固定阈值无法区分模型刚解决完一个子问题还是正在推导中间步骤；时机不当的压缩会抹去已验证的事实，迫使模型猜测。

## Method / 方法

SelfCompact pairs two inference-time elements, both executed by the same model (no external verifier, no fine-tuning):

SelfCompact配对了两个推理时组件，均由同一模型执行（无需外部验证器，无需微调）：

### 1. Compaction Tool (压缩工具)
An inline summarization tool $\mathcal{S}: (x, y_{1:t}) \to \bar{y}$ that takes the original prompt and accumulated trajectory, producing a condensed version. The summarizer prompt is appended as a user-role message so the KV cache of the existing trajectory is preserved (avoiding $O(L^2)$ re-encoding). Generation then resumes from $(x, \bar{y})$.

一个内联摘要工具，接收原始提示和累积轨迹，生成压缩版本。摘要提示以用户消息形式追加，从而保留已有轨迹的KV缓存（避免$O(L^2)$重编码开销）。生成随后从压缩后的上下文$(x, \bar{y})$继续。

### 2. Rubric (评判标准)
A lightweight, task-specific rubric $P_R$ that is periodically appended to the context at probe intervals. The model produces a binary verdict $r_t \in \{\text{COMPRESS}, \text{CONTINUE}\}$. The rubric translates "a sub-task has resolved" and "the trajectory is not stuck mid-derivation" into concrete, cite-able conditions the model can verify against its own trajectory. Rubrics differ for math (checking if sub-problems are resolved) and search (checking if search queries have returned useful results).

一个轻量级的任务特定评判标准$P_R$，在周期性探测间隔追加到上下文中。模型产生二元判定$r_t \in \{\text{COMPRESS}, \text{CONTINUE}\}$。评判标准将"子任务已完成"和"轨迹未卡在推导中间"转化为模型可以依据自身轨迹验证的具体、可引用的条件。数学和搜索任务使用不同的评判标准。

### Algorithm Flow (算法流程)
At each probe interval $t \equiv 0 \pmod{N}$: (1) append rubric, (2) sample verdict, (3) if COMPRESS, append summarizer prompt, generate summary $\bar{y}$, hard-reset context to $(x, \bar{y})$; if CONTINUE, pop the rubric probe and continue unchanged. Both rubric and summarizer reuse the KV prefix, making the probe essentially free.

在每个探测间隔：(1) 追加评判标准，(2) 采样判定结果，(3) 若为COMPRESS则追加摘要提示、生成摘要$\bar{y}$、将上下文重置为$(x, \bar{y})$；若为CONTINUE则移除探测并继续。评判标准和摘要器均复用KV前缀，使探测几乎零开销。

## Key Results / 关键结果

### Competition Math (竞赛数学)
Evaluated on 4 Qwen models across IMO-Answerbench, HMMT Nov 2025, HMMT Feb 2026:
- SelfCompact **wins 11 out of 12** benchmark/model settings under matched token budgets
- On Qwen3.5-9B: +16.4 on IMO-Answerbench, +10.0 on HMMT Nov, +18.1 on HMMT Feb vs. no-compaction baseline
- Consistently outperforms both no-compaction and fixed-interval summarization

在4个Qwen模型、3个竞赛数学基准上评估：SelfCompact在12个设置中的11个取胜，Qwen3.5-9B上分别在三个基准提升16.4、10.0和18.1分。

### Agentic Search (智能体搜索)
Evaluated on 3 deployed search agents (GLM-4.7-Flash, MiniMax-M2.5, MiMo-V2-Flash) across BrowseComp, BrowseComp-Plus, DeepSearchQA:
- SelfCompact is the strongest method across all three models
- Accuracy improvements: +8.5 (GLM), +9.2 (MiniMax), +5.3 (MiMo) on BrowseComp-Plus
- **30-70% lower per-question cost** compared to no-compaction baseline (e.g., BrowseComp-Plus cost drops from \$0.12 to \$0.04 for GLM-4.7-Flash)

在3个搜索智能体、3个搜索基准上：SelfCompact全面领先，准确率提升5-9分，每题成本降低30-70%。

### Key Ablation: Rubric is Essential (关键消融：评判标准不可或缺)
Removing the rubric (letting the model freely decide when to summarize) collapses performance by 5.4 points on average, falling to the level of naive fixed-interval scheduling. The tool alone yields uneven behavior -- some models call it at unhelpful moments, others not at all. The rubric is what makes self-compaction reliable.

去除评判标准后性能平均下降5.4分，退化到朴素固定间隔水平。工具单独使用时行为不稳定。评判标准是使自压缩可靠的关键。

### Meta-cognitive Gap (元认知差距)
40.4% of fixed-interval summarization transitions cause degradation (correct to wrong). An oracle that skips summarization when the answer is already correct achieves 52.9% (vs. 45.5% for SelfCompact, 41.4% for fixed-interval), indicating substantial remaining headroom for adaptive policies.

固定间隔摘要中40.4%的状态转换导致退化。跳过正确答案时摘要的Oracle策略达到52.9%，表明自适应策略仍有大量提升空间。

## Architecture & Design Insights / 架构与设计洞察

1. **KV-cache reuse is critical for cost**: Both rubric and summarizer are appended (not substituted) as user messages, preserving the KV cache prefix. The rubric probe generates only a short verdict token on top of the cached prefix, making it essentially free. The bulk cost savings come from post-summarization: subsequent tokens attend to $\bar{y}$ (short) rather than $y_{1:t}$ (long).

2. **SelfCompact fires earlier than fixed-interval**: The rubric triggers compression before the 30% context threshold, at natural reasoning boundaries when sub-tasks resolve. This left-skew in firing distribution means the fixed threshold typically fires too late, retaining stale tokens past useful breakpoints.

3. **Harder problems benefit most**: On the two hardest difficulty bins of BrowseComp-Plus, SelfCompact improves over fixed-interval by 5-20 percentage points across all 3 models, while easy bins show no significant difference.

1. **KV缓存复用对成本至关重要**：评判标准和摘要器均以用户消息形式追加，保留KV缓存前缀。主要成本节省来自摘要后的生成阶段。
2. **SelfCompact比固定间隔更早触发**：评判标准在自然推理边界触发压缩，早于30%上下文阈值。
3. **越难的问题获益越大**：在最困难的题目上，SelfCompact比固定间隔提升5-20个百分点。

## Critical Assessment / 批判性评估

### Strengths (优势)
- **Practically useful**: Addresses a real pain point in deployed agent systems (context rot) with a training-free, model-agnostic solution
- **Thorough evaluation**: 7 models, 6 benchmarks spanning two distinct task families (math reasoning and agentic search), with cost analysis
- **Clean ablation design**: The tool-only vs. tool+rubric ablation isolates exactly why the approach works (it is the rubric, not just the tool)
- **Honest oracle analysis**: The paper quantifies remaining headroom (52.9% oracle vs. 45.5% SelfCompact), showing the approach is far from optimal

### Weaknesses (不足)
- **Only open-weight models evaluated**: The authors acknowledge that frontier models (GPT-5.5, Claude Opus 4.7, Gemini 3 Pro) may have stronger meta-cognition that reduces the gap. The approach's value on the strongest models remains unknown
- **Rubric is hand-crafted and task-specific**: Different rubrics for math vs. search; unclear how much rubric engineering is needed for new task families, limiting out-of-the-box generality
- **Fixed probe interval N is still a hyperparameter**: The method replaces one rigid schedule (when to compress) with another (when to probe), though the probing cost is much lower
- **No reinforcement learning integration**: The authors frame RL as future work, but the rubric could serve as a reward signal -- this connection is noted but not explored
- **Cost model is approximate**: The single-rate approximation billing every token at cached rate is acknowledged as conservative but may not reflect actual API pricing accurately

## Relevance & Connections / 相关性与联系

This work is directly relevant to anyone building long-horizon LM agents (coding agents, research agents, multi-step reasoning systems). The key insight -- that *when* to compact matters more than *how* to compact, and that a lightweight rubric can supply this timing signal without training -- is applicable beyond the specific benchmarks tested. The approach is complementary to KV-cache eviction methods (which operate at the representation level) and to RL-based compaction training (which could use the rubric as a reward signal).

本文与构建长链推理智能体（编程智能体、研究智能体、多步推理系统）的工作直接相关。核心洞察——*何时*压缩比*如何*压缩更重要，且轻量评判标准可以在无需训练的情况下提供时机信号——具有广泛的适用性。
