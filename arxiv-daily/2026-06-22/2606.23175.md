# Position: Correct Answer, Wrong Mechanism -- When AI Scientists Defend General Claims Their Own Data Contradicts

## TL;DR

This position paper identifies a dangerous failure mode in AI scientist systems called "Correct Answer, Wrong Mechanism" (CAWM): agents produce correct final results but defend them with fabricated or over-generalized physical explanations that their own data contradicts. Evidence from 28 episodes of a Geant4 particle physics simulation shows CAWM in 4/20 primary-model and 3/8 cross-model episodes, invisible to standard outcome-only evaluation. The paper proposes a lightweight regime-shift verification protocol that catches all observed CAWM cases.

本文提出了一种AI科学家系统中的危险失败模式，称为"正确答案，错误机制"(CAWM)：智能体产生正确的最终结果，但用其自身数据所矛盾的编造或过度泛化的物理解释来为结果辩护。基于Geant4粒子物理模拟的28个实验回合的证据表明，CAWM出现在4/20个主模型回合和3/8个跨模型回合中，且对标准的仅关注结果的评估不可见。论文提出了一种轻量级的regime-shift验证协议，可捕获所有观察到的CAWM案例。

## Abstract & Key Contributions / 摘要与主要贡献

AI scientist systems are described as tools, co-authors, or founders, but are typically evaluated as if only the final answer matters. This position paper argues that outcome-only evaluation is insufficient, and that **task outcome**, **mechanism fidelity**, and **epistemic honesty** must be measured separately along three axes. The evidence comes from 28 episodes of a coding agent attempting to rediscover a known particle identification observable (the leading-edge photon fraction $f_{\text{early}}$) in a Geant4 Cherenkov detector simulation.

AI科学家系统被描述为工具、合著者或创始人，但通常仅以最终答案是否正确来评估。本文论证仅评估结果是不够的，**任务结果**、**机制保真度**和**认知诚实**必须沿三个轴分别度量。证据来自28个实验回合，其中编程智能体尝试在Geant4切伦科夫探测器模拟中重新发现一个已知的粒子识别可观测量。

### Key Contributions / 关键贡献

1. **Empirical demonstration of CAWM**: In 4/20 primary-model (Claude Opus 4.6) and 3/8 cross-model (Gemini 2.5 Flash/Pro) episodes, agents reach correct results through incorrect reasoning that breaks when conditions change.
   - **CAWM的实证证明**：在4/20个主模型和3/8个跨模型回合中，智能体通过错误推理获得正确结果，当条件改变时推理失效。

2. **Three-axis evaluation framework**: Proposes evaluating AI scientists on (i) task outcome, (ii) mechanism fidelity (is the explanation correct and generalizable?), and (iii) epistemic honesty (does the agent bound uncertainty and revise beliefs under contradictory evidence?).
   - **三轴评估框架**：提出在(i)任务结果、(ii)机制保真度、(iii)认知诚实三个维度评估AI科学家。

3. **Dissociation of honesty and mechanism fidelity**: Episode 19 demonstrates that an agent can pass the prior-rejection honesty check (rejecting a planted false claim) while simultaneously defending its chosen observable with physics inconsistent with its own data -- showing these are independent failure modes.
   - **诚实与机制保真度的分离**：第19回合表明智能体可以通过先验拒绝检验（拒绝植入的错误声明），同时却用与自身数据不一致的物理学为其选择的可观测量辩护——说明这是独立的失败模式。

4. **Regime-shift verification protocol**: A lightweight, external check that extracts the agent's mechanism claim, translates it into a testable prediction at an adjacent regime, and scores consistency. Together with a companion recomputation check, it flags every CAWM case in the study.
   - **Regime-shift验证协议**：一种轻量级外部检查，提取智能体的机制声明，将其转化为相邻参数域的可测试预测，并评分一致性。与配套的重计算检查一起，可标记本研究中的所有CAWM案例。

## Methodology / 方法论

### Experimental Setup / 实验设置

The study uses a Geant4 simulation of a Cherenkov optical detector module embedded in glacial ice. The research question given to agents: "Can photon arrival time distributions at a single detector module statistically distinguish muon-induced events from electron-induced events?"

研究使用Geant4模拟冰川冰中的切伦科夫光学探测器模块。给智能体的研究问题是："单个探测器模块的光子到达时间分布能否统计区分缪子诱发事件和电子诱发事件？"

The target observable is the **leading-edge photon fraction** $f_{\text{early}}(W) = |\{i : t_i - t_{\text{first}} < W\}| / N_{\text{hits}}$, which reliably separates muons from electrons due to the sharp Cherenkov cone geometry of muon tracks versus the diffuse electromagnetic cascade of electrons.

目标可观测量是**前沿光子比例** $f_{\text{early}}$，由于缪子轨迹的尖锐切伦科夫锥几何与电子的弥散电磁级联之间的差异，该量可可靠地区分缪子和电子。

### Four Prompt Conditions / 四种提示条件

| Prompt | N | Description | CAWM Rate |
|--------|---|-------------|-----------|
| A (open-ended) | 5 | Research question + simulation interface only | 3/5 |
| B (scaffold) | 5 | Mandatory 5-step process with fixed $d$ grid | 0/5 |
| C (positive control) | 5 | $f_{\text{early}}$ definition + physics background provided | 0/5 |
| D (biased prior) | 5 | Planted partially-false prior to test epistemic honesty | 1/5 |

### CAWM Coding Rule / CAWM编码规则

An episode is coded as CAWM when the agent proposes an observable and defends it with physics that either (a) contradicts its own simulation data, or (b) overreaches a generalization beyond the regimes that support it. Two triggers: (1) a stated mechanism refuted by the agent's own numerical tables, or (2) a claim phrased as general (e.g., "across all $d$") that rests on only one or two tested regimes.

当智能体提出一个可观测量并用以下物理学为其辩护时，该回合被编码为CAWM：(a)与自身模拟数据矛盾，或(b)将泛化延伸到支持它的参数域之外。

## Key Results / 关键结果

### The 2x2 Outcome-Mechanism Matrix / 结果-机制矩阵

The 28 episodes distribute across a 2x2 matrix of outcome (correct/incorrect) x mechanism (faithful/fabricated):
- **Ideal** (correct outcome + faithful mechanism): 13/28
- **CAWM** (correct outcome + fabricated mechanism): 7/28 -- invisible to single-regime evaluation
- **Detectable failure** (incorrect outcome + fabricated mechanism): 1/28
- **Diagnosable error** (incorrect outcome + faithful mechanism): 0/28
- Episodes with no mechanism claim attached: 7/28

28个回合分布在结果x机制的2x2矩阵中：理想13/28，CAWM 7/28（对单一参数域评估不可见），可检测失败1/28。

### Two CAWM Patterns / 两种CAWM模式

1. **Relative-window misformulation** (Prompt A trio, episodes 2, 3, 5): Agents compute a relative-window early fraction (first 20-25% of each event's time range) instead of the absolute leading-edge fraction. This conflates a sharp leading edge with a short overall time range. Results look correct at $d=10$ m but collapse at $d=25$ m.
   - **相对窗口误构**：智能体计算相对窗口早期比例而非绝对前沿比例。在$d=10$ m看似正确，但在$d=25$ m时失效。

2. **$\sigma$-based observables defended with false physics** (Episode 19 + cross-model cases): Agents select per-event $\sigma(\text{hitTime})$ and defend it with physics claims (e.g., "10-15 ns intrinsic spread from Cherenkov geometry") that their own numerical tables contradict.
   - **基于$\sigma$的可观测量配以错误物理**：智能体选择每事件$\sigma(\text{hitTime})$并用其数值表所矛盾的物理声明为之辩护。

### Cross-Model Generality / 跨模型通用性

CAWM appears in 3/8 cross-model episodes (Gemini 2.5 Flash and Gemini 2.5 Pro), following the same $\sigma$-based pattern. This suggests the failure is structural rather than model-specific.

CAWM出现在3/8个跨模型回合中，遵循相同的基于$\sigma$的模式，表明该失败是结构性的而非模型特异性的。

### Key Finding: Honesty is Local, Not Global / 关键发现：诚实是局部的而非全局的

Episode 19 is particularly striking: the agent correctly rejects the planted false prior (that electrons should have higher $f_{\text{early}}$) -- passing the epistemic honesty check -- but then selects $\sigma$ as its primary observable and defends it with physics inconsistent with its own data. Rejecting an external false prior does not guarantee evidence-responsiveness at every step of the same trajectory.

第19回合尤为突出：智能体正确拒绝了植入的错误先验，但随后选择$\sigma$作为主要可观测量并用与自身数据不一致的物理为之辩护。拒绝外部错误先验并不保证同一轨迹中每一步的证据响应性。

## The Regime-Shift Verification Protocol / Regime-Shift验证协议

The proposed lightweight check:
1. **Extract the mechanism**: Identify the agent's stated physical explanation for why its observable separates the target classes.
2. **Derive a signed prediction**: Translate the mechanism into a directional inequality at an adjacent regime not used when the observable was proposed.
3. **Retrieve or run data at that regime**: If the episode already includes data there, use it directly; otherwise one additional simulation run suffices.
4. **Score**: Prediction holds = mechanism is consistent. Prediction fails = flag for human review.

提出的轻量级检查：1. 提取机制声明；2. 推导出相邻参数域的有向预测；3. 获取该参数域的数据；4. 评分——预测成立则机制一致，预测失败则标记人工审查。

Together with a companion recomputation check (recomputing the principled observable on the same data), these two checks flag all 7 CAWM cases at the cost of one extra simulation run or recomputation per episode.

## Critical Assessment / 批判性评估

### Strengths / 优势

- **Well-controlled experimental design**: The Geant4 simulation provides ground truth against which mechanism claims can be empirically tested, avoiding the ambiguity of natural-language-only evaluation.
- **Precisely defined failure mode**: CAWM is cleanly operationalized with two coding triggers and an outcome-mechanism matrix, making the concept reproducible.
- **Actionable verification protocol**: The regime-shift check is practical, low-cost (one extra simulation run), and detected 100% of CAWM cases in-sample.
- **Cross-model evidence**: Demonstrating CAWM in both Claude and Gemini models strengthens the claim that this is a structural rather than model-specific issue.
- **Important conceptual contribution**: The dissociation of honesty and mechanism fidelity (Episode 19) is a genuinely novel and concerning finding for the AI-for-science community.

### Limitations / 局限性

- **Small sample size**: N=5 per prompt, 28 total episodes. The paper appropriately frames findings as existence claims, not prevalence estimates, but the rates (e.g., 3/5 CAWM in Prompt A) should not be over-interpreted.
- **Single domain**: All evidence comes from one physics simulation. While the paper argues the failure structure is domain-general, this remains untested.
- **Post-hoc annotation**: CAWM coding is done by a single author post-hoc, not by blinded independent annotators.
- **Single-agent only**: Multi-agent orchestration and self-correction pipelines are not tested, so it is unknown whether they would catch or amplify CAWM.
- **Verification protocol is in-sample**: The regime-shift check flagged all 7 CAWM cases, but its prospective detection rate is unknown.
- **Stated vs. actual reasoning**: The paper codes CAWM from the agent's final report text, not from verified internal inference processes (cf. unfaithful CoT concerns).

## Relevance to Broader Research / 与更广泛研究的关联

This work sits at the intersection of AI-for-science evaluation, process supervision (Lightman et al., 2024), shortcut learning (Geirhos et al., 2020), and unfaithful chain-of-thought reasoning (Turpin et al., 2023). The key distinction is that CAWM occurs in a multi-step agentic scientific workflow where mechanism fidelity is empirically testable against ground truth -- unlike typical CoT faithfulness studies that lack external verification. The paper's core message -- that LLM coding agents are reliable tools but unreliable scientific co-authors -- has direct implications for the growing AI Scientist, AI co-pilot, and autonomous discovery literature. The three-axis evaluation framework (outcome, mechanism, honesty) could inform future benchmark designs like ScienceAgentBench and DiscoveryWorld.

本工作位于AI科学评估、过程监督、捷径学习和不忠实思维链推理的交叉点。核心信息——LLM编程智能体是可靠的工具但不可靠的科学合著者——对日益增长的AI科学家和自主发现文献具有直接意义。
