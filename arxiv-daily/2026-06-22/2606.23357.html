SOAP-Bubbles: Structured Weight Uncertainty for Neural Networks

Summary / 概述

This paper introduces SOAP-Bubbles, a novel family of expressive, non-diagonal posterior distributions for Bayesian deep learning, and EVON (Eigenspace Variational Online Newton), a practical optimizer that estimates these posteriors during training. The key insight is to run IVON (a diagonal-covariance variational method) in the eigenspace of SOAP's Kronecker-factored preconditioner, then use that preconditioner to transform the diagonal estimate back into a non-diagonal covariance in the original parameter space. This yields structured weight uncertainty at costs similar to SOAP, with only minor modifications to existing SOAP implementations.

本文提出了 SOAP-Bubbles，一种用于贝叶斯深度学习的新型富表达力非对角后验分布族，以及 EVON（特征空间变分在线牛顿法），一种可在训练过程中估计该后验的实用优化器。核心思想是在 SOAP 的 Kronecker 分解预条件器的特征空间中运行 IVON（一种对角协方差变分方法），然后利用该预条件器将对角估计变换回原始参数空间中的非对角协方差。这样做能以与 SOAP 相近的计算代价获得结构化的权重不确定性，且只需对现有 SOAP 实现做少量修改。

Key Contributions / 主要贡献

  1. SOAP-Bubble variational family: Defines a new family of Gaussian posteriors with non-diagonal covariances obtained by rotating diagonal covariances through SOAP's eigenbasis. This captures block-diagonal covariance structure without the prohibitive costs of full structured covariance methods.

SOAP-Bubble 变分族：定义了一种新的高斯后验族，通过 SOAP 特征基对对角协方差进行旋转来获得非对角协方差，从而在不承担全结构化协方差方法高昂开销的前提下捕获块对角协方差结构。

  1. EVON optimizer: A simple modification of the SOAP optimizer that replaces Adam in SOAP's eigenspace with IVON, enabling variational inference with structured posteriors. The algorithm requires only ~5 lines of changes to an existing SOAP implementation (highlighted in Algorithm 2 vs Algorithm 1).

EVON 优化器：对 SOAP 优化器的简单修改，将 SOAP 特征空间中的 Adam 替换为 IVON，从而实现结构化后验的变分推断。该算法仅需在现有 SOAP 实现基础上修改约 5 行代码。

  1. Theoretical exactness guarantees (Theorem 2): For binary logistic regression with orthogonal features and an isotropic prior, the exact full Gaussian posterior among all multivariate Gaussians is a SOAP-Bubble -- i.e., it lies within EVON's variational family. Experiments on USPS confirm EVON recovers the exact full 256x256 posterior covariance.

理论精确性保证（定理 2）：对于具有正交特征和各向同性先验的二元逻辑回归，所有多元高斯中的精确全高斯后验恰好是一个 SOAP-Bubble——即它属于 EVON 的变分族。在 USPS 数据集上的实验证实 EVON 恢复了精确的 256x256 后验协方差矩阵。

  1. Scalable language model training: EVON scales to LLM pretraining (NanoGPT-125M on FineWeb-1B, LLaMA-134M on C4-2B), consistently improving validation loss over IVON and even surpassing SOAP's final loss while additionally providing a variational posterior for free.

可扩展的语言模型训练：EVON 可扩展至大语言模型预训练（NanoGPT-125M 在 FineWeb-1B 上、LLaMA-134M 在 C4-2B 上），一致性地优于 IVON 的验证损失，甚至超过 SOAP 的最终损失，同时免费提供变分后验。

Method Details / 方法细节

Core formulation: The SOAP-Bubble variational family is defined as:

$$\mathcal{Q}_{\text{bubble}} = \left\{ \mathcal{N}(\theta \mid \mathbf{P}\mathbf{m}, \mathbf{P}\text{diag}(\sigma^2)\mathbf{P}^\top) \mid \mathbf{m} \in \mathbb{R}^P, \sigma \in \mathbb{R}^P_+ \right\}$$

where $\mathbf{P} = \mathbf{Q}_R \otimes \mathbf{Q}_L$ is the Kronecker product of SOAP's left and right eigenbasis matrices. By Theorem 1, minimizing the variational objective over $\mathcal{Q}_{\text{bubble}}$ is equivalent to minimizing a transformed diagonal variational problem over $\mathcal{Q}_{\text{diag}}$ in the rotated coordinate space, via the invariance of KL divergence under invertible transformations.

核心公式：SOAP-Bubble 变分族定义为上述公式，其中 $\mathbf{P}$ 是 SOAP 左右特征基矩阵的 Kronecker 积。根据定理 1，在 $\mathcal{Q}_{\text{bubble}}$ 上最小化变分目标等价于在旋转坐标空间中对 $\mathcal{Q}_{\text{diag}}$ 最小化一个变换后的对角变分问题，这利用了 KL 散度在可逆变换下的不变性。

Key algorithmic differences from SOAP:

  • (i) Weights are sampled from the posterior (noise injection) to get an unbiased variational objective estimate
  • (ii) Squared-gradient estimator is replaced with a Hessian estimator (with optional clipping) to avoid bias in variational settings
  • (iii) A quadratic correction term ensures posterior precision remains positive
  • (iv) Preconditioned gradient includes the prior via weight decay
  • (v) Element-wise or spectral clipping options for the parameter update

与 SOAP 的关键算法差异：(i) 从后验中采样权重（噪声注入）以获得无偏变分目标估计；(ii) 用 Hessian 估计器（带可选裁剪）替代平方梯度估计器以避免变分设置中的偏差；(iii) 二次校正项确保后验精度保持正定；(iv) 预条件梯度通过权重衰减包含先验；(v) 参数更新可选逐元素裁剪或谱裁剪。

Implementation considerations: Uses QR decomposition as an efficient approximation to eigendecomposition; float32 for eigenbasis computation with bfloat16 for the rest; careful distributed data-parallel reduction for consistent Hessian estimation across GPUs.

Experiments / 实验

Bayesian Logistic Regression

On binary USPS digit classification (16x16 grayscale, 256-dim), EVON recovers the exact full 256x256 Gaussian posterior covariance matrix, matching the variational bound of the full Gaussian solution. IVON (diagonal) converges slower and to a worse objective value. On multiclass Iris logistic regression, EVON's marginals closely match HMC ground truth, while IVON's mean-field marginals are overly concentrated.

在 USPS 二分类上，EVON 恢复了精确的 256x256 全高斯后验协方差；在 Iris 多分类上，EVON 的边际分布与 HMC 真值高度吻合，而 IVON 的平均场边际过于集中。

Language Model Pre-Training

  • NanoGPT-125M on FineWeb-1B: EVON reaches lower validation loss faster than IVON and maintains a large gap throughout training. Test-time posterior sampling (Bayesian model averaging) with EVON's SOAP-Bubbles shows PPL improvements that scale with the number of MC samples (up to 64), while IVON's improvements saturate quickly.
  • LLaMA-134M on C4-2B: EVON improves over IVON training similarly to how SOAP improves over AdamW. EVON's final validation loss is lower than SOAP's, while also providing a variational posterior. Posterior ensembling again shows larger gains for EVON than IVON.

NanoGPT-125M / FineWeb-1B：EVON 比 IVON 更快达到更低验证损失；测试时后验采样在 MC 样本数增加时持续改善。LLaMA-134M / C4-2B：EVON 改进幅度类似 SOAP 相对 AdamW 的提升，且最终损失低于 SOAP，同时额外提供变分后验。

CLIP ViT-B/16 Fine-Tuning (8 classification datasets)

EVON performs on par with or better than AdamW, IVON, and SOAP across accuracy, NLL, ECE, and Brier score on 8 visual classification benchmarks (CARS, DTD, EuroSAT, GTSRB, MNIST, RESISC45, SUN397, SVHN). With Bayesian Model Averaging (@32 MC samples), EVON often achieves the best results, with larger gains from ensembling compared to IVON, suggesting more informative posteriors.

在 8 个视觉分类基准上，EVON 在准确率、NLL、ECE 和 Brier 分数上与最佳优化器持平或更优。使用 32 个 MC 样本的贝叶斯模型平均后，EVON 往往取得最佳结果。

Critical Assessment / 批判性评估

Strengths:

  • Extremely elegant idea: reusing SOAP's already-computed eigenbasis as the rotation for structured variational inference is both theoretically clean and practically efficient. The modification to SOAP is truly minimal (~5 lines).
  • Strong theoretical result showing exactness for logistic regression, validated empirically.
  • Comprehensive experiments spanning small-scale Bayesian inference, LLM pretraining, and vision fine-tuning -- demonstrating both theoretical soundness and practical scalability.
  • The method provides structured uncertainty "for free" on top of a competitive optimizer, making it immediately useful for downstream applications like model merging, continual learning, and test-time scaling.

优势：思想优雅，复用 SOAP 已有特征基作为结构化变分推断的旋转矩阵，理论清晰且实践高效；对逻辑回归的精确性有理论保证；实验覆盖面广（小规模贝叶斯推断、LLM 预训练、视觉微调）；在竞争力强的优化器之上"免费"提供结构化不确定性。

Weaknesses / Limitations:

  • Model scale is still modest (125M-134M parameters). Whether EVON's advantages hold at billion-parameter scale remains undemonstrated. The extra hyperparameter zeta and its sensitivity may complicate scaling.
  • The structured covariance is limited to block-diagonal (per-layer Kronecker structure inherited from SOAP). Cross-layer correlations are not captured. Whether this level of structure is sufficient for practical uncertainty tasks at scale is unclear.
  • The paper does not report wall-clock overhead relative to SOAP in detail. While costs are claimed to be similar, the noise injection and Hessian estimation add some overhead that is not precisely quantified.
  • ECE metrics after BMA show inconsistencies for both IVON and EVON, which the authors acknowledge but attribute to known issues with ECE estimation rather than investigating further.
  • Hyperparameter zeta requires careful tuning: too large causes posterior collapse, too small leads to instability. No principled guidance or scaling law is provided.

不足：模型规模仍然有限（125M-134M 参数），在十亿级模型上的表现未经验证；结构化协方差限于块对角（继承自 SOAP 的逐层 Kronecker 结构），未捕获跨层相关性；未详细报告相对于 SOAP 的墙钟开销；超参数 zeta 需要仔细调节且缺乏原则性指导。

Relevance to My Research / 与我研究的相关性

This work bridges second-order optimization and Bayesian deep learning in a practical way. For anyone already using SOAP for LLM training, EVON offers a near-free upgrade path to structured weight uncertainty. The resulting posteriors could be valuable for uncertainty-aware decoding, model merging, and test-time compute scaling -- all active research areas in foundation model development.

本工作以实用方式连接了二阶优化和贝叶斯深度学习。对于已使用 SOAP 进行 LLM 训练的研究者，EVON 提供了几乎零成本的结构化权重不确定性升级路径，所得后验可用于不确定性感知解码、模型合并和测试时计算扩展等活跃研究方向。
