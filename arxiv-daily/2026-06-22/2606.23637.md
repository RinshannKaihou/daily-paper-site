## Summary / 摘要

This paper reveals that Muown, a matrix-aware optimizer that decomposes weight matrices into per-row magnitudes and directions, implicitly performs Riemannian optimization on the row-oblique manifold. The key insight is that the directional update in Muown is equivalent to a Riemannian steepest-descent step on the manifold of row-normalized matrices, and the growing row norms silently anneal the per-row angular step size over training. Building on this geometric understanding, the authors propose AngularMuown, which makes the angular step size an explicit, schedulable quantity decoupled from the radial magnitude update. AngularMuown consistently outperforms AdamW, Muon, NorMuon, and Muown across dense and mixture-of-experts transformer architectures, achieving roughly 2x speedup over AdamW and 1.5x over NorMuon/Muown on 124M PlainLM models.

本文揭示了矩阵感知优化器Muown（将权重矩阵分解为逐行幅度和方向）隐式地在行斜流形(row-oblique manifold)上执行黎曼优化。核心发现是：Muown的方向更新等价于在行归一化矩阵流形上的黎曼最速下降步，而不断增长的行范数会在训练过程中悄然退火逐行角步长。基于这一几何理解，作者提出AngularMuown，将角步长显式化为可调度的量，与径向幅度更新解耦。AngularMuown在密集和混合专家Transformer架构上一致超越AdamW、Muon、NorMuon和Muown，在124M PlainLM模型上相对AdamW实现约2倍加速，相对NorMuon/Muown实现约1.5倍加速。

## Key Contributions / 主要贡献

1. **Riemannian interpretation of Muown**: The paper shows that Muown's directional update is equivalent to a retracted, spectrally-conditioned Riemannian (quasi-)gradient step on the oblique manifold M_ob = {U : ||U||_row = 1}. The row-norm growth during training acts as an implicit angular step-size annealing mechanism, explaining Muown's robustness to large step sizes.
   **Muown的黎曼解释**：论文证明Muown的方向更新等价于斜流形M_ob上的回缩谱条件黎曼（拟）梯度步。训练中行范数的增长充当隐式角步长退火机制，解释了Muown对大步长的鲁棒性。

2. **AngularMuown algorithm**: By keeping directions at unit norm and introducing an explicit angular learning-rate multiplier kappa_t, AngularMuown decouples angular and radial dynamics. The angular multiplier uses an inverse-polynomial schedule kappa_t = (1 + c(t - t_w)+)^{-p}, and a spectral-condition shape scale s_{m,n} = sqrt(max(1, m/n)) ensures dimension-independent angular updates.
   **AngularMuown算法**：通过将方向保持在单位范数并引入显式角学习率乘子kappa_t，AngularMuown解耦了角动力学和径向动力学。角乘子采用逆多项式调度kappa_t = (1 + c(t - t_w)+)^{-p}，谱条件形状缩放s_{m,n} = sqrt(max(1, m/n))确保角更新与维度无关。

3. **Convergence guarantee**: For an idealized version, they prove an epsilon-stationary point is reached in O(min{m,n} * Delta_1 * L * sigma^2 * epsilon^{-4}) gradient evaluations under non-convex settings with reasonable assumptions.
   **收敛保证**：对于理想化版本，在非凸设定和合理假设下，证明了在O(min{m,n} * Delta_1 * L * sigma^2 * epsilon^{-4})次梯度评估内达到epsilon-稳定点。

4. **Strong empirical results**: AngularMuown leads the per-optimizer category of the modded nanoGPT speedrunning competition, and experiments on PlainLM (124M, 500M), Qwen2-0.5B, and 1.1B MoE models consistently show improvements. Learning rate transfer across model widths (67M, 124M, 300M) is also demonstrated.
   **强实证结果**：AngularMuown领先modded nanoGPT速度赛的每优化器类别，在PlainLM（124M、500M）、Qwen2-0.5B和1.1B MoE模型上实验一致展现改进。还展示了学习率在不同模型宽度（67M、124M、300M）间的迁移性。

## Methodology / 方法论

**Geometric framework**: The paper starts from Muown's weight parameterization W = Diag(g/||R||_row)R, which separates a per-row magnitude g from a direction matrix R. Only the row-normalized part U = Diag(||R||_row)^{-1}R enters the weight, so the directional degrees of freedom live on the oblique manifold M_ob (product of m unit spheres S^{n-1}).

**几何框架**：论文从Muown的权重参数化W = Diag(g/||R||_row)R出发，将逐行幅度g与方向矩阵R分离。只有行归一化部分U = Diag(||R||_row)^{-1}R参与权重，因此方向自由度存在于斜流形M_ob（m个单位球S^{n-1}的乘积）上。

**Three angular regimes (Table 1)**:
- Weight decay (Muon, lambda > 0): norm reaches equilibrium, angular step plateaus at theta_inf > 0
- Implicit (Muown): unregularized norm grows as sqrt(t), angular step anneals as t^{-1/2}
- Explicit (AngularMuown): norm fixed at 1, angular step scheduled directly via kappa_t

**三种角步长机制（表1）**：
- 权重衰减（Muon，lambda > 0）：范数达到平衡，角步长稳定在theta_inf > 0
- 隐式（Muown）：未正则化的范数增长为sqrt(t)，角步长退火为t^{-1/2}
- 显式（AngularMuown）：范数固定为1，角步长通过kappa_t直接调度

**Algorithm design**: AngularMuown (Algorithm 1) internally reparameterizes W = Diag(g)U, computes the Riemannian gradient on the manifold, updates U via Muon (momentum + orthogonalization + row-normalization with angular multiplier kappa_t), and updates g via Adam independently. The shape scale s_{m,n} calibrates each directional step so that the per-row angle tan(theta_i) = eta * kappa_t * s_{m,n} * ||O_i^perp||_2 is approximately eta * kappa_t, giving the angular step a physical meaning in degrees (~57 * eta * kappa_t degrees).

**算法设计**：AngularMuown（算法1）内部将W重参数化为Diag(g)U，计算流形上的黎曼梯度，通过Muon更新U（动量+正交化+带角乘子kappa_t的行归一化），并独立通过Adam更新g。形状缩放s_{m,n}校准每个方向步长，使得逐行角度tan(theta_i) = eta * kappa_t * s_{m,n} * ||O_i^perp||_2约等于eta * kappa_t，赋予角步长物理意义（约57 * eta * kappa_t度）。

## Experimental Results / 实验结果

**PlainLM 124M (FineWeb-Edu, up to 10B tokens)**:
- AngularMuown substantially outperforms all baselines at every checkpoint-branching horizon (2.5B, 5B, 7.5B, 10B tokens)
- Achieves ~2x speedup over AdamW and ~1.5x over NorMuon and Muown
- With angular decay (kappa_t proportional to 1/t), angular displacement smoothly anneals toward zero, while without decay it stays flat

**PlainLM 124M（FineWeb-Edu，最多10B tokens）**：
- AngularMuown在所有检查点分支时刻（2.5B、5B、7.5B、10B tokens）上均大幅超越所有基线
- 相对AdamW实现约2倍加速，相对NorMuon和Muown实现约1.5倍加速
- 带角衰减（kappa_t正比于1/t）时，角位移平滑退火至零；无衰减时保持平坦

**Qwen2-0.5B**: AngularMuown with polynomial angular decay attains the best loss overall at its well-tuned learning rate (eta=4e-2, loss=2.648), improving over NorMuon consistently. The unscheduled variant (kappa_t = 1) degrades sharply at large learning rates, confirming the importance of angular annealing.

**Qwen2-0.5B**：AngularMuown在其最优学习率（eta=4e-2，损失2.648）下取得整体最佳损失，一致超越NorMuon。无调度变体（kappa_t = 1）在大学习率下急剧退化，证实了角退火的重要性。

**1.1B MoE (DeepSeek-V3 style, 370M active)**: AngularMuown with spectral-condition scaling s_{m,n} = sqrt(max(1, m/n)) achieves 2.4807 at eta=2e-3, outperforming Muown's best of 2.4914 at eta=8e-3. The improvement persists in the sparse model setup.

**1.1B MoE（DeepSeek-V3风格，370M活跃参数）**：AngularMuown使用谱条件缩放s_{m,n} = sqrt(max(1, m/n))在eta=2e-3时达到2.4807，超越Muown最佳的2.4914（eta=8e-3）。改进在稀疏模型设置中持续存在。

**Width transfer**: Optimal learning rate transfers perfectly across model widths (67M, 124M, 300M) for AngularMuown, validating the spectral-condition shape scale's effectiveness at removing implicit shape dependence.

**宽度迁移**：最优学习率在不同模型宽度（67M、124M、300M）间完美迁移，验证了谱条件形状缩放在消除隐式形状依赖方面的有效性。

## Critical Assessment / 批判性评估

**Strengths**:
- The Riemannian geometric interpretation is clean and illuminating -- it transforms a somewhat ad-hoc optimizer design choice (Muown's magnitude-direction split) into a principled geometric framework, revealing why it works.
- The three-regime analysis (Table 1) clearly unifies weight decay, Muown's implicit annealing, and AngularMuown's explicit scheduling under a single angular step-size lens.
- The practical payoff is concrete: leading a competitive nanoGPT speedrunning leaderboard and showing consistent improvements across architectures (dense, MoE) and scales.
- The shape scaling for dimension independence is well-motivated and empirically validated through width transfer experiments.

**优势**：
- 黎曼几何解释清晰且富有启发性——将Muown略显临时的优化器设计（幅度-方向分离）转化为有原则的几何框架，揭示了其有效性原因。
- 三机制分析（表1）在统一的角步长视角下清晰统一了权重衰减、Muown的隐式退火和AngularMuown的显式调度。
- 实际收益明确：领先竞争性nanoGPT速度赛排行榜，在不同架构（密集、MoE）和规模上展现一致改进。
- 维度无关的形状缩放有良好的理论动机，并通过宽度迁移实验得到验证。

**Weaknesses / Limitations**:
- All experiments are limited to language-model pre-training at medium scale (up to 1.1B parameters). Whether the benefits hold at 10B+ scale or for other modalities (vision, multimodal) is unknown.
- The convergence guarantee applies to an "idealized" version of AngularMuown (Algorithm 2 in appendix) that differs from the practical one (momentum in ambient space, retraction without re-projection). The gap between theory and practice is acknowledged but not fully bridged.
- The inverse-polynomial schedule introduces hyperparameters (c, p, t_w), though the authors report c=0.001, p=1 works uniformly. The sensitivity of these defaults to significantly different training regimes (e.g., very long runs, different architectures) is not thoroughly explored.
- The comparison with concurrent work (Hagele et al., 2026) is acknowledged but not experimentally evaluated.

**不足/局限**：
- 所有实验限于中等规模（最多1.1B参数）的语言模型预训练。在10B+规模或其他模态（视觉、多模态）上的优势尚未验证。
- 收敛保证适用于AngularMuown的"理想化"版本（附录算法2），与实际版本（环境空间中的动量、无重投影的回缩）有差异。理论与实践的差距被承认但未完全弥合。
- 逆多项式调度引入了超参数（c, p, t_w），尽管作者报告c=0.001, p=1普遍有效。这些默认值在显著不同的训练方案下的敏感性未充分探索。
- 与并发工作（Hagele et al., 2026）的比较被提及但未进行实验评估。

## Relevance to Broader Research / 对更广泛研究的意义

This work fits into a growing line of research on geometry-aware optimization for transformers, connecting weight normalization, spectral steepest descent (Muon), and Riemannian optimization into a unified framework. The angular step-size perspective offers a principled lens for understanding and designing optimizers for scale-invariant or normalized networks. The practical implication is immediate: AngularMuown is a drop-in replacement for Muown that eliminates weight decay for the directional component and provides better control over training dynamics. The width-transfer property is particularly promising for large-scale practitioners who need to tune hyperparameters on smaller models.

本工作属于Transformer几何感知优化的研究脉络，将权重归一化、谱最速下降（Muon）和黎曼优化统一在一个框架中。角步长视角为理解和设计尺度不变或归一化网络的优化器提供了有原则的透镜。实际意义直接：AngularMuown是Muown的即插即用替代，消除了方向分量的权重衰减，并提供更好的训练动态控制。宽度迁移特性对需要在小模型上调参的大规模实践者尤其有前景。
