The Topology of Ill-Posed Questions: Persistent Homology for Detection and Steering in LLMs

[Guangyu Jiang, Sizhe Tang, Mahdi Imani, Tian Lan]

arXiv: 2606.23590

Date: 2026-06-22

Tags: [persistent-homology, ill-posed-questions, activation-steering, topological-data-analysis, LLM-interpretability]

https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.23590

Summary / 摘要

This paper proposes a topological framework for detecting and steering ill-posed questions (ambiguous, underspecified, or contradictory queries) in large language models. Instead of analyzing model outputs or using single pooled hidden vectors, the authors treat the contextual hidden states of prompt tokens at each transformer layer as a point cloud and characterize its geometry using finite zero-dimensional persistent homology (H_0). Each layer's topology is compressed into three scalar descriptors -- mean finite lifetime, normalized lifetime entropy, and largest-lifetime concentration ratio -- which are concatenated across all layers to form a question-level topology vector. This vector supports both binary and fine-grained (9-way) ill-posedness classification. The authors further introduce topology-conditioned local activation steering, which retrieves topologically similar examples and constructs query-specific steering directions to encourage source-aware clarification or abstention rather than generic refusal.

本文提出了一种基于拓扑的框架，用于检测和引导大语言模型中的病态问题（歧义、欠规范或矛盾的查询）。作者没有分析模型输出或使用单一的池化隐藏向量，而是将每个Transformer层中提示词的上下文隐藏状态视为点云，并使用有限零维持久同调（H_0）来刻画其几何结构。每一层的拓扑结构被压缩为三个标量描述符——平均有限生存期、归一化生存期熵和最大生存期集中比——跨所有层拼接后形成问题级拓扑向量。该向量支持二分类和细粒度（9类）病态性分类。作者还引入了拓扑条件局部激活引导方法，通过检索拓扑相似的样本构造查询特定的引导方向，以鼓励面向来源的澄清或弃权，而非泛化的拒绝。

Key Contributions / 主要贡献

  1. Layer-wise token-cloud representation / 逐层词元点云表示: Models prompt-token hidden states at each transformer layer as a point cloud and applies finite H_0 persistent homology via Vietoris-Rips filtration to capture the multiscale connectivity structure of question representations. This is the first work to cast ill-posedness as a topological property of internal token geometry.

将每一层Transformer中提示词的隐藏状态建模为点云，并通过Vietoris-Rips滤波应用有限H_0持久同调，捕获问题表示的多尺度连通结构。这是首项将病态性视为内部词元几何拓扑属性的工作。

  1. Compact topological descriptors / 紧凑拓扑描述符: Three fixed-dimensional scalar features per layer -- mean finite lifetime (average fragmentation scale), normalized lifetime entropy (spread of lifetime mass), and largest-lifetime concentration ratio (dominance by largest merges) -- that together form a 3L-dimensional topology vector. These outperform both sparse-neuron baselines (AEN) and multiple prompt-based baselines.

每层三个固定维度的标量特征——平均有限生存期、归一化生存期熵和最大生存期集中比——共同构成3L维拓扑向量，在性能上超越稀疏神经元基线（AEN）和多种基于提示的基线。

  1. Topology-conditioned local activation steering / 拓扑条件局部激活引导: Instead of applying a single global steering direction, the method retrieves k nearest neighbors in standardized topology space and constructs a local contrast vector from their mean-pooled hidden states. This produces question-specific, grounded responses rather than generic refusals.

该方法不使用单一全局引导方向，而是在标准化拓扑空间中检索k个最近邻，从其均值池化的隐藏状态构造局部对比向量，从而产生针对具体问题的、有依据的回应，而非泛化拒绝。

Method / 方法

Token Cloud Construction / 词元点云构建

For each question q and transformer layer l, the hidden states of all T prompt tokens are collected into a matrix H^(l)(q). These are centered (mean-subtracted) and projected via layer-specific PCA (fitted on training data) to r dimensions, yielding a point cloud X^(l)(q) in R^r.

对于每个问题q和Transformer层l，收集所有T个提示词的隐藏状态构成矩阵H^(l)(q)，经中心化后通过逐层PCA（在训练数据上拟合）投影到r维空间，得到R^r中的点云X^(l)(q)。

Persistent Homology Computation / 持久同调计算

A Vietoris-Rips filtration is applied to each layer's point cloud. As the scale parameter epsilon grows from 0, initially disconnected token components merge. The finite H_0 bars record these merge events -- their lifetimes (death minus birth) capture the scales at which token groups connect. The computation is equivalent to Kruskal's minimum spanning tree algorithm, making it efficient and interpretable.

对每层点云应用Vietoris-Rips滤波。随着尺度参数epsilon从0增大，最初断开的词元分量逐渐合并。有限H_0条记录这些合并事件——其生存期（死亡减去诞生）捕获词元组连接的尺度。计算等价于Kruskal最小生成树算法，高效且可解释。

Three Descriptors / 三个描述符

  • Mean finite lifetime mu^(l): Average merge scale, measuring fragmentation / 平均合并尺度，衡量碎片化程度
  • Normalized lifetime entropy E^(l): Whether lifetime mass is spread uniformly or concentrated / 生存期质量是否均匀分布
  • Largest-lifetime concentration R^(l): Fraction of total lifetime held by the top-5 largest bars / 前5个最大条占总生存期的比例

These three descriptors are computed per layer, concatenated across all L layers, standardized, and fed to a logistic regression classifier.

三个描述符逐层计算，跨所有L层拼接、标准化后送入逻辑回归分类器。

Topology-Conditioned Steering / 拓扑条件引导

For a test question, its topology vector is used to retrieve k nearest positive (ill-posedness-aware response) and negative (direct-answer response) training examples. Their mean-pooled hidden states at an intervention layer l* form a local contrast vector, which is added to the test question's hidden states during both prefill and decoding with a scalar strength alpha.

对于测试问题，利用其拓扑向量检索k个最近的正样本（病态感知响应）和负样本（直接回答响应）训练样本。它们在干预层l*的均值池化隐藏状态构成局部对比向量，以标量强度alpha添加到测试问题的预填充和解码阶段的隐藏状态中。

Results / 实验结果

Classification / 分类

Evaluated on three benchmarks across three open-weight models (Gemma-7B-it, Llama-3.1-8B-Instruct, Mistral-7B-Instruct-v0.3):

| Benchmark | Task | Best Prompt Baseline (avg acc) | AEN (avg acc) | H_0 Topology (avg acc) |

|---|---|---|---|---|

| AmbigQA | Binary | 55.1% | 67.4% | 78.9% |

| SituatedQA | Binary | 58.2% | 79.9% | 88.5% |

| CLAMBER | 9-way | 33.0% | 57.6% | 69.6% |

The topology classifier uses only 3 scalar features per layer with a logistic regression model, yet consistently outperforms both the 5-neuron AEN baseline and all prompt-based methods (zero-shot, few-shot, few-shot CoT, InfoGain).

拓扑分类器仅使用每层3个标量特征和逻辑回归模型，却在所有指标上一致超越5神经元AEN基线和所有基于提示的方法。

Steering / 引导

Topology-local steering vs. global full-vector steering (averaged across 9 dataset-model settings):

  • Grounded acceptable rate: 11.9% -> 16.4% (topology-local improves)
  • Total acceptable rate: 61.4% -> 70.6%

Key qualitative finding: topology-local steering increases grounded responses while reducing generic abstentions. For example, on AmbigQA with Llama, grounded responses rose from 5.0% to 11.1% while generic responses dropped from 81.5% to 72.6%.

拓扑局部引导相比全局全向量引导，有根据的可接受率从11.9%提升至16.4%，总可接受率从61.4%提升至70.6%。关键定性发现：拓扑局部引导增加了有根据的回应，同时减少了泛化弃权。

Ablation Highlights / 消融实验

  • All three H_0 descriptors contribute complementary information; removing any one degrades performance.
  • All-layer topology consistently outperforms single-layer topology, confirming that ill-posedness is a distributed, evolving property across layers, not localized to one.

三个H_0描述符提供互补信息；全层拓扑一致优于单层拓扑，确认病态性是跨层分布的演化属性。

Critical Assessment / 批判性评估

Strengths / 优势:

  • The use of persistent homology to characterize token-cloud geometry is a genuinely novel perspective on the ill-posedness problem. It provides geometric interpretability that pooled-vector or neuron-selection methods lack.
  • The three-descriptor compression is elegant: fixed-dimensional regardless of sequence length, efficient to compute (MST equivalence), and demonstrably informative.
  • The topology-conditioned steering approach is well-motivated -- using topology for retrieval while keeping the intervention in native activation space avoids the problem of inverting topological features back into activations.
  • Comprehensive evaluation across 3 datasets, 3 models, multiple baselines, and ablations.

持久同调的使用为病态性问题提供了真正新颖的几何可解释性视角。三描述符压缩优雅高效，拓扑条件引导方法设计合理，评估全面。

Weaknesses / 不足:

  • Experiments are limited to three 7B-scale open-weight models. It remains unclear whether the topological signatures generalize to larger models (70B+) or closed-weight systems where hidden states are inaccessible.
  • Only H_0 (connected components) is used. H_1 (cycles/loops) could capture richer structural information about how token groups relate, but is left to future work.
  • The steering evaluation uses GPT-5 nano as the LLM judge, which introduces its own biases. No human evaluation is provided for the steering quality.
  • The PCA dimensionality r and the intervention layer l* are fixed across experiments without detailed sensitivity analysis on these choices.
  • The grounded acceptable rates, while improved, remain relatively low in absolute terms (peaking around 42% on SituatedQA), suggesting the steering mechanism has substantial room for improvement.

实验局限于三个7B规模模型，未验证更大模型的泛化性；仅使用H_0而未使用H_1；引导评估仅用LLM作为评判，缺乏人工评估；有根据的可接受率绝对值仍较低。

Relevance / 相关性

This work bridges topological data analysis and LLM interpretability/controllability. It is relevant to researchers working on: (1) question answering reliability and uncertainty, (2) activation steering and representation engineering, (3) topological methods in NLP, and (4) practical systems that need to detect when a user's query is ill-defined and respond appropriately rather than hallucinating an answer or giving a generic refusal. The topology-as-retrieval-key idea for conditioning activation steering is a transferable technique that could apply to other behavioral steering tasks beyond ill-posedness.

本工作连接了拓扑数据分析与LLM可解释性/可控性，与QA可靠性、激活引导、NLP中的拓扑方法以及实际系统的病态查询检测相关。拓扑作为检索键用于条件化激活引导的思路可迁移到其他行为引导任务。
