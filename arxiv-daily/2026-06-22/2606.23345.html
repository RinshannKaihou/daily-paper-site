Abstract representational geometry supports inference in large language models

arXiv: 2606.23345

Summary / 摘要

This paper investigates whether large language models (LLMs) form abstract representational geometry similar to the hippocampus when performing inference, using a text-based contextual reversal-learning task adapted from neuroscience. The authors find that when LLMs successfully infer latent task structure, their internal hidden states organize into orthogonal, disentangled manifolds resembling hippocampal population geometry. This geometry is not uniform across layers but is hierarchically organized: lower layers encode stimulus identity while higher layers form a "hippocampal-like functional band" enriched for abstract context geometry. Two complementary interventions -- task-sequence language modelling and direct geometric regularization (GeoStruct) -- causally link this geometry to inference, establishing abstract representational geometry as a mechanistic principle supporting reasoning in LLMs.

本文研究了大语言模型(LLM)在进行推理时，是否会形成类似于海马体的抽象表征几何结构。作者使用了一种从神经科学改编的文本化情境反转学习任务，发现当LLM成功推断潜在任务结构时，其内部隐藏状态会组织成正交、解耦的流形，类似于海马体中的群体几何结构。这种几何结构并非均匀分布于各层，而是呈层级组织：低层编码刺激身份，高层形成"类海马功能带"，富集用于抽象情境几何。两种互补的干预实验——任务序列语言建模和直接几何正则化(GeoStruct)——因果性地将该几何结构与推理关联，确立了抽象表征几何作为支撑LLM推理的机制性原理。

Key Contributions / 主要贡献

  1. Behavioural convergence between humans and LLMs / 人类与LLM的行为趋同: Using a contextual reversal-learning task with pseudoword stimuli, the authors show that both humans and LLMs exhibit two distinct behavioral regimes -- rapid "Inference Present" sessions (single-shot context updating after one error signal) and gradual "Inference Absent" sessions (stimulus-by-stimulus relearning). The behavioral dynamics are strikingly isomorphic, though LLMs exhibit inference less frequently than humans (scaling with model size: Pearson r = 0.827, Spearman rho = 0.888).

利用伪词刺激的情境反转学习任务，作者展示了人类和LLM都表现出两种不同的行为模式——快速的"推理存在"会话(单次错误信号后即完成情境更新)和渐进的"推理缺失"会话(逐刺激重新学习)。两者的行为动态高度同构，但LLM表现推理的频率低于人类(随模型规模增长：Pearson r = 0.827, Spearman rho = 0.888)。

  1. Hippocampal-like abstract geometry in LLM hidden states / LLM隐藏状态中的类海马抽象几何: In inference-present sessions, LLM hidden states exhibit significantly higher Cross-Condition Generalization Performance (CCGP) and Parallelism Score (PS) for the latent context variable, indicating that context is encoded along orthogonal, stimulus-invariant axes -- the hallmark of factorized representation. Context CCGP increased by +0.090 and context PS by +0.081 in inference-present vs. inference-absent sessions.

在推理存在的会话中，LLM隐藏状态对潜在情境变量表现出显著更高的跨条件泛化性能(CCGP)和平行性分数(PS)，表明情境沿正交的、刺激不变的轴编码——这是因子化表征的标志。情境CCGP提升+0.090，情境PS提升+0.081。

  1. Hierarchical depth organization / 层级深度组织: The abstract context geometry is not uniformly distributed but is preferentially enriched in higher transformer layers. Lower layers stably encode stimulus identity; middle-to-higher layers progressively organize a shared context axis. This parallels the functional specialization seen in biological neural circuits.

抽象情境几何并非均匀分布，而是优先富集于高层Transformer层。低层稳定编码刺激身份；中高层逐步组织共享的情境轴。这与生物神经回路中的功能特化相对应。

  1. Causal interventional evidence / 因果干预证据:
  • Task-sequence fine-tuning: Language-modelling fine-tuning on reversal-learning sequences raised inference-present sessions from 16.2% to 76.1%, with PS increasing ~33% and CCGP ~13%, without explicit geometric supervision.
  • GeoStruct regularization: Direct geometric constraints (cosine-distance alignment of coding directions) increased inference prevalence by 2.7x (16.2% to 43.2%) without any language-modelling objective, with strongest effects in middle-to-higher layers.
  • 任务序列微调：在反转学习序列上进行语言建模微调，将推理存在会话从16.2%提升至76.1%，PS提升约33%，CCGP提升约13%，无需显式几何监督。
  • GeoStruct正则化：直接几何约束(编码方向的余弦距离对齐)在无语言建模目标的情况下将推理频率提升2.7倍(16.2%至43.2%)，效果最强的是中高层。

Methodology / 方法论

Task design: A contextual reversal-learning paradigm with 4 pseudoword stimuli, 2 latent contexts with reversed stimulus-response-outcome mappings, and unannounced context switches. Humans were tested via Amazon Mechanical Turk (n=41); LLMs received the same task serialized as autoregressive text sequences. Models tested include LLaMA-3.1 (1B-70B), Qwen-2.5 (0.5B-14B), GPT-5.1, Claude-4.5-Sonnet, Gemini-2.5-Flash, and Grok-4-Fast.

Representation analysis: Hidden states extracted at response token positions across intermediate layers. Representations aggregated into 8 conditions (2x2x2: context x response x outcome). Three neuroscience-derived metrics used: (1) decoding accuracy via linear classifiers, (2) Cross-Condition Generalization Performance (CCGP), (3) Parallelism Score (PS). Statistical testing via session-level permutation tests with Benjamini-Hochberg FDR correction across 35 balanced dichotomies.

Interventions: (1) Task-sequence language modelling fine-tuning on independent reversal-learning sequences; (2) GeoStruct -- a geometric regularization loss minimizing cosine distance between difference vectors of each latent factor across conditions, applied selectively to different depth blocks.

任务设计：情境反转学习范式，包含4个伪词刺激、2个潜在情境(刺激-反应-结果映射反转)和未通知的情境切换。人类通过Amazon Mechanical Turk测试(n=41)；LLM接收相同任务的自回归文本序列化版本。测试模型包括LLaMA-3.1(1B-70B)、Qwen-2.5(0.5B-14B)、GPT-5.1、Claude-4.5-Sonnet、Gemini-2.5-Flash和Grok-4-Fast。

表征分析：在中间层的响应token位置提取隐藏状态。表征聚合为8个条件(2x2x2：情境x响应x结果)。使用三个源自神经科学的指标：(1)线性分类器的解码准确率，(2)跨条件泛化性能(CCGP)，(3)平行性分数(PS)。统计检验采用会话级置换检验，并在35个平衡二分法上进行Benjamini-Hochberg FDR校正。

干预实验：(1)在独立反转学习序列上进行任务序列语言建模微调；(2)GeoStruct——一种几何正则化损失，最小化每个潜在因子在不同条件下差异向量间的余弦距离，选择性地应用于不同深度块。

Critical Assessment / 批判性评估

Strengths:

  • The paper bridges neuroscience and AI interpretability with a carefully controlled experimental paradigm. Using pseudoword stimuli to eliminate semantic confounds is a strong methodological choice.
  • The combination of observational analysis with two complementary interventions (fine-tuning and geometric regularization) provides relatively strong causal evidence, not just correlation.
  • The breadth of models tested (spanning two open-weight families and several proprietary models across orders of magnitude in scale) strengthens the generality claim.
  • The neuroscience-derived metrics (CCGP, PS) provide principled, quantitative measures of geometric structure rather than relying on visualization alone.

Weaknesses:

  • The task is deliberately simple (4 stimuli, 2 contexts, binary responses). It remains unclear whether these geometric findings scale to the complex, multi-dimensional reasoning that LLMs perform in practice. The gap between this controlled setting and real-world inference is substantial.
  • The "hippocampal-like" analogy, while suggestive, is fairly loose. The authors note that the LLM stage profile actually differs from the hippocampal benchmark (baseline-stage context geometry is already strong in LLMs due to autoregressive history). The analogy risks being more evocative than mechanistically grounded.
  • The GeoStruct intervention, while showing a causal link, only raises inference from 16.2% to 43.2% -- still well below humans (49%) and far below fine-tuned models (76.1%). This suggests geometry alone is insufficient and other mechanisms are at play.
  • The paper's discussion of implications for "Mechanistic Alignment" and AGI safety feels speculative relative to the narrow task studied. Monitoring coding direction orthogonality as a safety tool is an interesting idea but far from validated.
  • Only open-weight models could be analyzed at the representational level; proprietary models were limited to behavioral analysis, leaving the strongest-performing models' internal geometry unverified.

评价：

  • 优点：论文在神经科学与AI可解释性之间建立了有控制的实验范式桥梁；使用伪词刺激消除语义混淆是很好的方法论选择；观察分析与两种互补干预结合提供了较强的因果证据；测试模型的广度增强了普遍性主张。
  • 不足：任务刻意简单(4个刺激、2个情境)，能否推广到复杂推理尚不清楚；"类海马"类比较为宽泛，作者自己也承认LLM的阶段特征与海马基准不同；GeoStruct干预效果有限(43.2%仍远低于人类)，表明几何结构不是唯一机制；关于"机制对齐"和AGI安全的讨论相对于所研究的狭窄任务显得过于推测；最强模型(专有模型)无法进行表征分析。

Relevance / 相关性

This work contributes to the growing intersection of neuroscience-inspired interpretability and LLM mechanistic understanding. It complements existing approaches (sparse autoencoders, linear probes) by operating at the population-geometry level rather than individual-feature level. The finding that representational geometry is functionally linked to reasoning -- not just correlated -- is a meaningful step beyond prior correlational studies. The GeoStruct regularization technique could potentially serve as a geometry-aware training objective for improving reasoning capabilities. For researchers working on LLM interpretability, this paper offers concrete metrics (CCGP, PS) that could be applied to study reasoning in more complex settings.

本工作为神经科学启发的可解释性与LLM机制理解的交叉领域做出了贡献。它在群体几何层面而非个体特征层面运作，补充了现有方法(稀疏自编码器、线性探针)。表征几何与推理之间的功能性(而非仅相关性)联系是超越先前相关性研究的有意义进步。GeoStruct正则化技术可能作为几何感知的训练目标用于提升推理能力。对于从事LLM可解释性研究的研究者，本文提供了可应用于更复杂设置的具体指标(CCGP, PS)。
