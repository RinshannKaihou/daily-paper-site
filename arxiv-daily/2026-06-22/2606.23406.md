## Summary / 概述

HyperQuant is a unified, data-free, post-training quantization (PTQ) pipeline for compressing both the weights and KV cache of large language and diffusion transformers. The name stands for **H**adamard, **optimallY P**acking, **E**ntropy **R**ice-coding, **Quant**ization. It combines four established ideas into one coherent system: (i) per-tile Randomized Hadamard Transform (RHT) to Gaussianize coordinates, (ii) quantization to optimal low-dimensional lattices (E8, D4, A2, or Z), (iii) lossless bit-stripping plus near-entropy-optimal variable-length Rice coding of lattice indices, and (iv) bias-correction methods (QJL-style rotation + Schuchman subtractive dither) for the KV cache. The pipeline integrates with Hopper (FP8/INT8) and Blackwell (NVFP4/MXFP4) Tensor-Core MMA paths, and is benchmarked end-to-end on Llama-3.1-8B-Instruct and the 19B-parameter LTX-2 video DiT.

HyperQuant是一个统一的、无需校准数据的训练后量化(PTQ)流水线，用于压缩大型语言模型和扩散Transformer的权重与KV缓存。其名称代表Hadamard、optimallY Packing、Entropy Rice-coding、Quantization。该方法将四个经典思想整合为一个完整系统：(i) 逐块随机Hadamard变换(RHT)使坐标分布近似高斯化；(ii) 量化到最优低维格(E8、D4、A2或Z)；(iii) 无损位剥离加近熵最优的Rice变长编码；(iv) 用于KV缓存的偏差校正机制(QJL风格旋转+Schuchman减法抖动)。该流水线集成了Hopper (FP8/INT8) 和 Blackwell (NVFP4/MXFP4) Tensor-Core MMA路径，并在Llama-3.1-8B-Instruct和19B参数的LTX-2视频DiT上进行了端到端基准测试。

## Key Contributions / 主要贡献

1. **Per-tile RHT with lattice quantization and Rice coding**: Each linear layer's input/weight tiles are independently randomly rotated via the RHT (which folds into LayerNorm/RMSNorm at zero runtime cost), then quantized to one of four lattice families. Deterministic membership bits are stripped losslessly, and the remaining indices are Rice-coded, achieving rates within ~0.01 bps of any target. This replaces HIGGS's fixed-rate finite Lloyd codebook with an unbounded lattice + variable-length code, recovering 0.6-5.9% entropy slack that HIGGS wastes.
   - 逐块RHT与格量化和Rice编码：每个线性层的输入/权重块通过RHT独立随机旋转（可零开销融入LayerNorm/RMSNorm），然后量化到四种格族之一。确定性的成员位被无损剥离，剩余索引用Rice编码，实现与目标码率~0.01 bps的偏差精度。这用无界格+变长码替代了HIGGS的固定码率有限Lloyd码本，回收了HIGGS浪费的0.6-5.9%的熵冗余。

2. **Rate-distortion decomposition of the HIGGS gap**: At matched 2-D dimension (A2 vs HIGGS-p2), the advantage decomposes into (a) an index-entropy piece (0.18-0.61 dB) recoverable by any entropy-coded retrofit, and (b) a structural unbounded-codebook piece (0.13-1.79 dB) that grows with rate and is only possible with variable-length coding. Switching to E8 adds further ~0.49 dB granular gain.
   - HIGGS差距的率失真分解：在匹配2D维度(A2 vs HIGGS-p2)时，优势分解为(a)索引熵部分(0.18-0.61 dB)，任何熵编码改造均可恢复；(b)结构性无界码本部分(0.13-1.79 dB)，随码率增长，仅变长编码才能实现。切换到E8进一步增加~0.49 dB的粒度增益。

3. **Two-regime KV-cache characterization**: Above 2.5 bps (high-quality regime), all bias-correction variants lie within 0.04 PPL of BF16 baseline; in the high-compression regime (1.7-2.5 bps), QJL rotation pulls ahead by up to ~0.5 PPL. Subtractive dither achieves provably strict per-vector inner-product unbiasedness, unlike QJL's distribution-average guarantee.
   - KV缓存两区域特征：高于2.5 bps（高质量区域），所有偏差校正变体与BF16基准相差不超过0.04 PPL；在高压缩区域(1.7-2.5 bps)，QJL旋转领先约0.5 PPL。减法抖动实现了可证明的严格逐向量内积无偏性，优于QJL的分布平均无偏保证。

4. **INT8 beats FP8 on post-RHT data**: After RHT Gaussianization, post-lattice tensors have light tails, making INT8's 256 uniform levels more useful than FP8's logarithmic spacing. INT8 beats FP8-E4M3 by ~0.10 PPL (LLM) and ~0.7 dB PSNR (LTX-2 video).
   - INT8在RHT后数据上优于FP8：经RHT高斯化后，格后张量具有轻尾分布，使INT8的256个均匀量化级比FP8的对数间隔更有效。INT8在LLM上优于FP8-E4M3约0.10 PPL，在LTX-2视频上优于约0.7 dB PSNR。

5. **End-to-end stress test on 19B LTX-2 video DiT**: Demonstrates the pipeline transfers cleanly from an 8B LLM to a 19B video diffusion transformer with 3.7x weight compression, no observable per-frame artifacts, and PSNR/LPIPS stable across the 49-frame generation window.
   - 19B LTX-2视频DiT端到端压力测试：验证该流水线从8B LLM干净地迁移到19B视频扩散Transformer，实现3.7倍权重压缩，无可观察的逐帧伪影，PSNR/LPIPS在49帧生成窗口内保持稳定。

## Methodology / 方法论

**Pipeline architecture**: The encode path (BF16 -> compact code) consists of: RHT -> Rotate (KV only) -> Normalize -> Add dither (KV only) -> Quantize -> Strip -> Rice encode. The decode path reverses each block: Rice decode -> Unstrip -> Dequantize -> Undither -> Denormalize -> Derotate -> (RHT inverse absorbed into MMA operand) -> Cast. The RHT has no explicit inverse -- it cancels algebraically via W = (W H^T) H on the MMA contraction axis.

**Lattice choices**: Four lattice families are supported, each with an O(1) nearest-neighbor decoder:
- E8 (8-D, Gosset): best granular gain (0.88 dB to Shannon), strips 1.0 bit/scalar
- D4 (4-D, Schlafli): strips 0.25 bit/scalar
- A2 (2-D, hexagonal): strips 0.5 bit/scalar
- Z (1-D, scalar): simplest, no stripping, 1.53 dB to Shannon

All use integer embeddings that fit in signed bytes (8-bit code budget). The lattice choice matters below 3.5 bps; above 4.25 bps all four are essentially equivalent.

**Bit-stripping**: Lattice membership constraints (parity, coset, sum-mod-2) deterministically fix certain bits that are stripped before Rice coding and reconstructed at decode time. This is lossless and provably rate-optimal (not heuristic), leaving near-independent symbols whose marginal entropy matches the entropy-coded lattice ideal.

**Rice coding on GPU**: Uses an offset-indexed one-pass decoder: the encoder groups lattice symbols into sub-streams of S=512 symbols, storing a 32-bit start offset and Rice parameter k per sub-stream. The decoder launches one thread per sub-stream. Metadata overhead is ~1% at 4 bps.

**KV cache bias correction menu**: Three rotation options (none, signs S=diag(+/-1), full QJL) crossed with dither on/off yields six variants. At >= 2.5 bps, none is sufficient; at 1.7-2.5 bps, qjl without dither is best for PPL; dither buys strict per-vector unbiasedness when needed for long-context workloads.

**流水线架构**：编码路径(BF16 -> 紧凑编码)由以下步骤组成：RHT -> 旋转(仅KV) -> 归一化 -> 加抖动(仅KV) -> 量化 -> 剥离 -> Rice编码。解码路径逆序执行。RHT无需显式求逆——通过W = (W H^T) H在MMA收缩轴上代数消去。

**格选择**：支持四种格族(E8/D4/A2/Z)，均具有O(1)最近邻解码器。格选择在3.5 bps以下有意义，4.25 bps以上四者基本等价。

**位剥离**：利用格成员约束(奇偶校验、陪集、模2求和)确定性固定某些比特，在Rice编码前无损剥离。这是可证明的率最优操作。

**GPU上的Rice解码**：使用偏移索引单遍解码器，编码器将符号分成S=512的子流，每子流存储32位起始偏移和Rice参数k。

## Experimental Results / 实验结果

**Weight quantization (Llama-3.1-8B, WikiText-2, BF16 baseline PPL 7.161)**:
- E8+Rice dominates HIGGS at every rate from 3 to 5 bps
- At 4 bps: HyperQuant PPL 7.434 (E8) vs HIGGS-p2 PPL 7.618 (+0.18 PPL advantage)
- At 3 bps: HyperQuant PPL 8.744 (E8) vs HIGGS-p2 PPL 9.527 (+0.78 PPL advantage)
- The gap grows at lower rates where granular gain and unbounded-codebook advantage dominate

**Full-model 8-bit MMA (Llama-3.1-8B at 4 bps)**:
- BF16 baseline: 7.161 PPL
- INT8 weights+KV: 7.503 PPL (+0.34) -- best overall
- FP8 weights+KV: 7.644 PPL (+0.48)
- KV bias-correction choice moves PPL by < 0.01

**KV cache vs. TurboQuant/OCTOPUS (Qwen2.5-7B, context 4096)**:
- At matched bias correction (qjl), HyperQuant beats OCTOPUS-QJL at every rate (+0.2% vs +2.7% W2 delta at 4 bits)
- With 32-token residual window, HyperQuant wins on both quality and compression at every operating point (+7.4% vs OCTOPUS's +34.7% at 2 bits, KV 6.4x vs 2.9x)
- At 1.7 bps, HyperQuant reaches KV 7.1x compression

**LTX-2-19B video DiT (4 bps, 32-prompt evaluation)**:
- INT8 MMA: PSNR 22.74 dB, SSIM 0.8172, LPIPS 0.2008
- Weight memory: 35.16 -> 9.5 GiB (3.7x reduction)
- No visible per-frame artifacts; PSNR flat across 49-frame window

**End-to-end deployment (Llama-3.1-8B on H100)**:
- Weight compression: 3.9x (14.96 -> 5.29 GiB resident)
- KV compression: 3.79x (0.516 -> 0.136 bytes/scalar effective)
- Prefill throughput: 4,655 tok/s (W+KV) vs 16,505 tok/s (BF16)
- Decode throughput: 5.4 tok/s (W+KV) vs 51.8 tok/s (BF16)

**权重量化**：E8+Rice在3-5 bps全范围内优于HIGGS。4 bps时PPL 7.434 vs HIGGS-p2的7.618；3 bps时PPL 8.744 vs 9.527。

**全模型8位MMA**：INT8权重+KV达到7.503 PPL (+0.34)，优于FP8的7.644 PPL (+0.48)。

**KV缓存对比**：在匹配偏差校正下，HyperQuant在每个码率上都优于OCTOPUS。2 bit时质量差异+7.4% vs +34.7%，压缩比6.4x vs 2.9x。

**LTX-2视频**：权重从35.16 GiB压缩到9.5 GiB (3.7x)，无可见伪影。

**部署**：权重压缩3.9x，KV压缩3.79x，但解码吞吐量显著下降(5.4 vs 51.8 tok/s)，因为变长解码增加延迟。

## Ablation Studies / 消融实验

- **Lattice choice**: Above 4.25 bps, all four lattices are equivalent; below 3.5 bps, E8's granular gain becomes meaningful (up to 0.57 PPL improvement over Z scalar at 3 bps)
- **RHT tile size**: Tile size (128-2048) is not a quality lever for weights or W+KV; per-tile differences fall within seed noise. Default of 128 chosen for kernel efficiency (matches MMA K-dim)
- **HIGGS codebook efficiency**: Empirical index entropy is 0.6-5.9% below fixed-rate budget; Rice coding closes this entropy slack

- **格选择**：4.25 bps以上四种格等价；3.5 bps以下E8粒度增益变得有意义（3 bps时比Z标量改善0.57 PPL）
- **RHT块大小**：128-2048范围内对质量无可复现影响，默认128匹配MMA K维度
- **HIGGS码本效率**：经验索引熵比固定码率预算低0.6-5.9%，Rice编码关闭此熵冗余

## Critical Assessment / 批判性评估

**Strengths**:
- Theoretically principled: each design choice (lattice, stripping, Rice, dither) is grounded in information theory with formal rate-optimality guarantees. The bit-stripping is proved rate-optimal (not just heuristic), and the subtractive dither provides strict per-vector unbiasedness.
- Unified pipeline: a single encode/decode path serves both weights and KV cache, differing only in optional bias-correction blocks. This architectural simplicity is attractive for deployment.
- Strong empirical results: beats HIGGS at every operating point on weights, and beats OCTOPUS/TurboQuant on KV cache at matched settings. The decomposition of the HIGGS gap into entropy-coding and unbounded-codebook pieces is intellectually clean.
- Generalization demonstrated beyond LLMs to a 19B video DiT with no re-tuning.

**Weaknesses/Limitations**:
- **Memory win, not a speedup**: The variable-length Rice decode adds latency. Decode throughput drops from 51.8 to 5.4 tok/s on Llama-3.1-8B (H100). The paper is honest about this, but it is a significant practical limitation. The per-forward weight decode and O(T) KV re-decode are fundamental bottlenecks. The proposed kernel optimizations (intra-stream parallelism, fused decode+MMA, rANS) are future work.
- **Single-bps allocation**: All layers get the same target bps. The dynamic-programming per-layer allocator of HIGGS, which concentrates bits on sensitive layers, is acknowledged as composable but not yet integrated.
- **Evaluation scope**: Benchmarks are on 7-8B models (Llama-3.1-8B, Qwen2.5-7B) and one 19B DiT. No experiments on truly large models (70B+) where the memory savings would matter most and where per-layer sensitivity may differ significantly.
- **No comparison with calibration-based methods**: GPTQ, AWQ, OmniQuant etc. are discussed as orthogonal but not benchmarked against. The paper's claim of being "data-free" is valid, but practitioners often have calibration data available and may achieve better quality with those methods.
- **The throughput cost may limit adoption**: For latency-sensitive serving, the current 10x decode slowdown would be prohibitive. The memory savings are most valuable for batch inference and long-context workloads where memory is the bottleneck, not latency.

**优势**：理论上有严格信息论保证（率最优位剥离、严格逐向量无偏抖动）；统一流水线设计简洁；在权重和KV缓存上均优于现有最优方法；从LLM到视频DiT的泛化无需重新调优。

**不足**：变长解码导致解码吞吐量下降约10倍（51.8->5.4 tok/s），是重要的实际限制；未集成逐层比特分配；仅在7-19B模型上验证，缺少70B+大模型实验；未与校准方法(GPTQ/AWQ)直接比较；当前吞吐量代价可能限制延迟敏感场景的采用。

## Relevance / 相关性

This paper advances the data-free PTQ Pareto frontier by demonstrating that lattice quantization with variable-length entropy coding is strictly superior to the finite fixed-rate codebooks used by HIGGS, the previous state of the art. The theoretical framework -- connecting lattice granular gain, bit-stripping rate-optimality, and subtractive-dither unbiasedness -- is clean and provides a solid foundation. The practical gap is the decode throughput, which the authors acknowledge and outline clear kernel-engineering directions to address. The extension to video diffusion transformers (LTX-2-19B) is a notable contribution, being the first end-to-end PTQ result on a billion-parameter video DiT. For memory-bound workloads (long-context serving, batch inference, edge deployment), HyperQuant's 3.9x weight and 3.79x KV compression at near-lossless quality represents a meaningful advance.

本文通过证明格量化+变长熵编码严格优于HIGGS的有限固定码率码本，推进了无数据PTQ的帕累托前沿。理论框架清晰（格粒度增益、位剥离率最优性、减法抖动无偏性）。实际瓶颈在于解码吞吐量，作者对此坦诚并给出了明确的内核工程方向。对视频扩散Transformer (LTX-2-19B) 的扩展是首个十亿参数级视频DiT的端到端PTQ结果。对于内存受限的工作负载，HyperQuant的3.9x权重和3.79x KV压缩在近无损质量下代表了有意义的进展。
