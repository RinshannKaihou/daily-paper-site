# Right Knowledge, Wrong Answer: Test-Time Steering for Temporal Fact Conflicts in Open-Weight Language Models

## Summary / 摘要

This paper formalizes the phenomenon of **Parametric Temporal Conflict (PTC)** -- where a language model has both an outdated fact and its newer replacement stored in its parameters, but standard prompting preferentially elicits the outdated answer. The authors introduce **Temporal Attractor Steering (TAS)**, a three-stage test-time intervention pipeline (detect, locate, steer) that identifies PTC cases, localizes the conflict-critical layer via causal activation patching, and steers hidden states toward the newer fact's representation -- all without retraining or external retrieval. Evaluated on an 8,746-record Wikidata-verified benchmark across four open-weight LMs (Qwen-2.5-1.5B/7B, Mistral-7B-v0.3, Llama-3.1-8B), TAS recovers 29--57% of PTC cases while preserving 85--99% accuracy on non-conflict queries, outperforming a matched ITI baseline on three of four models.

本文形式化定义了**参数化时序冲突 (Parametric Temporal Conflict, PTC)** 现象：语言模型的参数中同时存储了过时事实和更新的替代事实，但标准提示倾向于给出过时答案。作者提出了**时序吸引子引导 (Temporal Attractor Steering, TAS)**，这是一个三阶段的推理时干预流水线（检测、定位、引导），能够识别 PTC 案例、通过因果激活补丁定位冲突关键层、并将隐藏状态引导至新事实的表示方向——全程无需重训练或外部检索。在基于 Wikidata 验证的 8,746 条记录基准上，对四个开源权重模型进行评估，TAS 恢复了 29--57% 的 PTC 案例，同时在非冲突查询上保持 85--99% 的准确率，在四个模型中的三个上优于匹配的 ITI 基线。

## Key Contributions / 主要贡献

1. **Formalization of PTC**: Defines Parametric Temporal Conflict as a distinct failure mode separate from knowledge absence or generic temporal degradation. A fact quadruple (q, a_old, a_new, t_update) exhibits PTC when the model assigns higher probability to a_old under standard prompting, yet a temporal cue reverses this preference. A knowledge-recovery filter (log P threshold >= -3) ensures only genuinely recoverable conflicts are studied.
   **PTC 的形式化定义**：将参数化时序冲突定义为一种独立于知识缺失或泛化时序退化的故障模式。当模型在标准提示下给旧答案更高概率、但时序提示可以逆转偏好时，即存在 PTC。知识恢复过滤器确保仅研究真正可恢复的冲突。

2. **TAS Pipeline**: A three-stage inference-time framework: (1) a lightweight linear probe detects likely PTC cases; (2) causal activation patching localizes a conflict-critical layer l*; (3) a precomputed steering vector Delta shifts the hidden state at l* toward the newer fact. Three granularity variants (global, per-relation, per-domain) are tested, with per-relation (V2) performing best.
   **TAS 流水线**：三阶段推理时框架——(1) 轻量线性探针检测可能的 PTC 案例；(2) 因果激活补丁定位冲突关键层 l*；(3) 预计算的引导向量 Delta 在 l* 处将隐藏状态移向新事实。按关系粒度 (V2) 的效果最佳。

3. **Verified Benchmark**: An 8,746-record benchmark from Wikidata spanning 5 relations (head of state, CEO, head of government, head coach, chairperson) across 3 domains (politics, corporate, sports), with verified superseding temporal updates.
   **经验证的基准数据集**：基于 Wikidata 构建的 8,746 条记录基准，覆盖 5 种关系、3 个领域，包含经验证的时序替代更新。

4. **Theoretical Motivation**: Two sufficient conditions (Parametric Co-encoding + Local Separability + Bounded Steering) under which TAS provably flips the preference inequality.
   **理论动机**：提出了 TAS 可证明地翻转偏好不等式的充分条件。

## Methodology / 方法论

### Problem Formulation / 问题建模
A temporal fact quadruple Q = (q, a_old, a_new, t_update) exhibits PTC when:
- P(a_old | q) > P(a_new | q) -- standard prompting prefers the outdated answer
- P(a_new | q, c_temp) >> P(a_old | q, c_temp) -- a temporal cue c_temp (e.g., "As of 2024, who is...") recovers the newer fact

The knowledge-recovery filter retains records only if log P(a_new | q, c_temp) >= -3, ensuring PTC reflects genuine conflict rather than knowledge absence.

### TAS Three Stages / TAS 三阶段

**Stage 1 -- Detection**: A linear probe s(h_l*(q)) in [0,1] trained on verified PTC positives and matched non-conflict negatives. If the score exceeds threshold tau, the query proceeds to steering; otherwise the forward pass is left unchanged. Calibrated via isotonic regression.

**Stage 2 -- Layer Localization**: For each layer l, the answer-flip rate AFR(l) is computed by patching h_l from the temporal-prompt forward pass into the standard-prompt forward pass at the last prompt token. The conflict-critical layer is l* = argmax_l AFR(l). Key finding: l* lives in the upper half of every architecture (l* in {23, 23, 30, 31} for the four models) with plateau widths of 6--14 layers.

**Stage 3 -- Steering**: At l*, a steering direction Delta = mean_q[h_l*(q, c_temp) - h_l*(q)] is computed and averaged over verified PTC instances. The intervention: h'_l*(q) = h_l*(q) + alpha * c * Delta, where alpha controls strength and c is the detector score.

## Key Results / 关键结果

### PTC Screening
- **Outdated-preference rate (OPR)** is family-invariant across all models (0.530--0.544)
- **Filtered PTC rate scales monotonically with model capacity**: 0.041 (Qwen-1.5B) -> 0.071 (Qwen-7B) -> 0.085 (Mistral-7B) -> 0.103 (Llama-8B), refuting the hypothesis that PTC is a small-model artifact
- The elicitation gap (EG) is positive for every model (+0.090 to +0.180 nats)

### Activation Patching
- Single-layer activation patching achieves answer-flip rates of **0.72--0.85** across all models
- The conflict-critical layer l* is consistently in the upper half of the network
- Qwen models share a tight 6-layer plateau; Mistral has an unusually wide 14-layer plateau

### End-to-End TAS Performance
| Model | Recovery | PA (clean) | Best config |
|---|---|---|---|
| Qwen-2.5-1.5B | 0.392 | 0.987 | tau=0.15 |
| **Qwen-2.5-7B** | **0.569** | **0.948** | **tau=0.15** |
| Mistral-7B-v0.3 | 0.288 | 0.936 | tau=0.30 |
| Llama-3.1-8B | 0.374 | 0.849 | tau=0.30 |

### TAS vs. ITI Baseline
TAS's verified-conflict Delta outperforms ITI's behavioural-contrast Delta on three of four models, with Recovery gains of +0.05 to +0.15. On Mistral-7B-v0.3, the ordering reverses (-0.056), suggesting the behavioural-contrast direction happens to align better with Mistral's conflict-critical layer.

### Cross-Model Agreement
Only 6 records are flagged by all four models simultaneously (union 1,287). The Mistral-Llama overlap (193) is 5x the two-Qwen overlap (38), suggesting training-data recency is more predictive of PTC-instance alignment than architecture.

## Critical Assessment / 批判性评估

**Strengths / 优势:**
- The PTC formalization cleanly separates a specific, actionable failure mode (recoverable but suppressed knowledge) from generic knowledge absence, which is a meaningful conceptual contribution.
- The three-stage pipeline is principled: the detector gate avoids unnecessary intervention on non-conflict queries, preserving baseline behavior. The 85--99% preservation accuracy is reassuring.
- The knowledge-recovery filter is an important methodological safeguard -- without it, raw PTC rates are confounded by coverage differences across models.
- The layer localization findings (upper-half, family-specific but stable) are mechanistically informative and practically useful -- l* only needs to be identified once per model family.
- The benchmark is publicly released with verified Wikidata provenance, enabling reproducibility.

**Weaknesses / 不足:**
- The evaluation is limited to **single-hop superseding updates** with exactly two mutually exclusive answers. Real-world temporal knowledge evolution often involves more nuanced changes (additive facts, partial updates, multi-hop chains).
- TAS uses **relation-specific** (V2) rather than per-instance steering vectors. The oracle-vs-full gap (Table 3) reveals that the detector is the primary bottleneck on Qwen-1.5B (0.123 gap), suggesting per-instance or finer-grained steering could substantially improve results.
- The method requires **open-weight model access** (residual-stream intervention), making it inapplicable to API-only models -- a significant practical limitation.
- The evaluation covers only **four models from three families**; generalization to very large models (70B+), different architectures (e.g., Mamba, mixture-of-experts), or instruction-tuned models is unknown. Instruction-tuned models are explicitly excluded because chat alignment confounds the parametric-memory signal.
- The **Mistral underperformance** (TAS loses to ITI by 0.056) is acknowledged but not fully explained. The paper frames it as an ablation rather than a failure, but this weakens the universality claim.
- The theoretical sufficient conditions (Section 4) are stated but proofs are deferred to the appendix; the practical gap between the conditions and empirical verification remains somewhat hand-wavy.

**Missing comparisons / 缺失的对比:**
- No comparison with retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) approaches, which are the dominant practical solution for temporal knowledge updating. The paper argues for a retrieval-free setting, but a RAG baseline would contextualize the practical value of TAS.
- No comparison with weight-editing methods (ROME/MEMIT) on the same benchmark, though these require different infrastructure.

## Relevance / 相关性

This work is relevant to anyone working on **knowledge management in LLMs**, particularly the challenge of keeping deployed models factually current without retraining. The PTC formalization and the finding that temporal conflicts scale with model capacity (not shrink) has implications for larger models. The activation-steering approach provides a lightweight alternative to retrieval augmentation or continual learning for a specific class of factual errors. The benchmark and codebase (released on GitHub and HuggingFace) are useful resources for the temporal knowledge community.

本文与 LLM 中的**知识管理**高度相关，特别是在不重训练的情况下保持已部署模型事实准确性的挑战。PTC 随模型容量单调增长的发现对更大模型具有启示意义。激活引导方法为特定类别的事实错误提供了一种轻量级替代方案。
