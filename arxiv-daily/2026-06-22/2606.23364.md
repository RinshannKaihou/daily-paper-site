# Convergence of Gradient Descent for General Neural Network Architectures Beyond the NTK Regime

## Summary / 摘要

This paper develops a convergence framework for gradient descent (GD) on a broad family of neural network architectures that goes beyond the neural tangent kernel (NTK) regime. The key idea is to combine three ingredients -- polynomial generalized smoothness (a block-level regularity condition weaker than global Lipschitz smoothness), a local relaxed dissipative condition (controlling trajectory boundedness near stationary points), and analytic nondegeneracy of the network Jacobian (enabling an iterate-dependent PL-type inequality via measure-zero arguments). Under mild assumptions, the paper proves that for almost all initializations and almost all datasets, GD with regular (non-infinitesimal) learning rates converges to the neighbourhood of a stationary point. The framework is formulated at the level of network blocks and covers architectures including pre-normalized multi-layer transformers, feedforward networks, convolutional networks, and residual networks. Additionally, the paper provides structural insights into why residual connections and function composition preserve nondegeneracy, and characterizes global minimizers outside a measure-zero bad set.

本文为一大类神经网络架构建立了超越神经切线核 (NTK) 范式的梯度下降 (GD) 收敛理论框架。核心思路是将三个要素相结合：多项式广义光滑性（一种比全局 Lipschitz 光滑性更弱的块级正则条件）、局部松弛耗散条件（控制轨迹在驻点附近的有界性）、以及网络 Jacobian 的解析非退化性（通过测度零集论证实现依赖于迭代的 PL 型不等式）。在温和假设下，论文证明了对于几乎所有初始化和几乎所有数据集，使用常规（非无穷小）学习率的 GD 收敛到驻点的邻域。该框架在网络模块层面建立，覆盖了包括预归一化多层 Transformer、前馈网络、卷积网络和残差网络在内的架构。此外，论文还从结构角度解释了残差连接和函数复合为何保持非退化性，并刻画了测度零坏集之外的全局极小点。

## Problem & Motivation / 问题与动机

The NTK framework has been the dominant theoretical tool for understanding GD convergence in neural networks, but it requires very large widths (polynomial in the sample size), infinitesimal learning rates, and training dynamics that stay close to initialization -- effectively reducing networks to linear models. This lazy regime fails to capture feature learning, which is central to the practical success of deep networks. Meanwhile, modern architectures (transformers, U-Nets, state-space models) are built from heterogeneous compositions of attention, normalization, residual connections, and feedforward blocks, making the NTK assumptions especially unrealistic.

The classical nonconvex optimization toolkit relies on two conditions that neural networks generally violate: (1) a global Polyak-Lojasiewicz (PL) condition (the loss landscape has singular regions where PL fails), and (2) global Lipschitz smoothness of the gradient (neural network losses have parameter-dependent smoothness that grows polynomially). The paper is motivated by the need for convergence guarantees that apply to practical architectures with regular learning rates and that track the actual nonlinear GD trajectory rather than a linearization.

NTK 框架一直是理解神经网络 GD 收敛性的主流理论工具，但它需要非常大的宽度（样本量的多项式量级）、无穷小的学习率，以及停留在初始化附近的训练动态——实质上将网络退化为线性模型。这种"惰性"范式无法捕捉特征学习，而特征学习是深度网络实际成功的核心。同时，现代架构（Transformer、U-Net、状态空间模型）由注意力、归一化、残差连接和前馈模块等异构组件组合而成，使得 NTK 假设尤为不切实际。

经典非凸优化工具依赖于神经网络通常违反的两个条件：(1) 全局 PL 条件（损失曲面存在 PL 条件失效的奇异区域），(2) 梯度的全局 Lipschitz 光滑性（神经网络损失的光滑性随参数多项式增长）。本文的动机在于：需要能适用于实际架构、使用常规学习率、并且追踪实际非线性 GD 轨迹（而非线性化近似）的收敛保证。

## Method / 方法

The framework is built on three technical pillars:

**1. Polynomial Generalized Smoothness (Definitions 1-3, Proposition 1).** The paper introduces polynomial boundedness, polynomial generalized continuity, and polynomial generalized smoothness -- conditions where the Lipschitz constant of the gradient is bounded by a polynomial in the parameter norms rather than a global constant. Crucially, these properties are *closed under addition, multiplication, and composition*, meaning they can be verified block-by-block and automatically hold for networks built from standard components (Lemma 1). This replaces the usual global Lipschitz smoothness assumption.

**2. Analytic Nondegeneracy and Iterate-Dependent PL Inequality (Assumption 4, Lemma 7).** The paper leverages the fact that common activation functions (tanh, GELU, SiLU, sigmoid, softmax) and normalizations (LayerNorm, BatchNorm) are real-analytic (Lemma 3). Combined with a mild architectural nonlinearity condition -- requiring the stacked Jacobians at some layer to have full row rank for generic parameters -- this yields an iterate-dependent PL-type inequality: the squared gradient norm is lower-bounded by a data-dependent constant times the loss. This constant, denoted mu_{low,s,X}, is shown to be strictly positive along the GD trajectory for almost all initializations and datasets, preventing stagnation at non-stationary points.

**3. Local Relaxed Dissipative Condition (Definition 5, Proposition 2).** Instead of assuming global trajectory boundedness, the paper introduces a local condition near a reference stationary point that controls the alignment between the gradient direction and the direction toward the stationary point. Every C^1 function satisfies this condition for some constant (Proposition 2), making it very mild. This condition ensures the GD trajectory remains in a bounded ball of radius R around the reference stationary point.

**Main Convergence Theorem (Theorem 1).** Under these three conditions, the paper proves that GD converges to the epsilon-neighbourhood of a stationary point (where the gradient norm drops below epsilon_L). Specifically, for all iterations where the gradient is large, the loss decays multiplicatively:

L(theta^k) <= prod_{s=0}^{k-1} (1 - eta(1 - eta*L/2) * 2*mu_{low,s,X}/N) * L(theta^0)

The learning rate eta can be chosen in the interval (0, min{(2-delta)/L, 1}], excluding at most finitely many exceptional values. The local smoothness constant L is proved to be bounded along the trajectory (not assumed a priori).

**Practical Scaling (Corollaries 1-2).** Under Xavier initialization, the learning rate scale depends on the depth L and the effective bottleneck dimension r = max{d, some bottleneck dimension}, rather than the largest hidden width m. Specifically: R = O(sqrt(rL)), rho = O(rL), L = O((1+sqrt(rL))^{q_L}), and the learning rate scales as Omega(1/(1+sqrt(rL))^{q_L}).

**Architectural Implications (Section 6).** The paper shows that adding a small identity component (i.e., a residual connection) generically restores nondegeneracy of analytic maps (Lemma 8), providing theoretical justification for residual architectures. It also proves that stationary points outside the measure-zero bad set are global minimizers (Theorem 2).

该框架建立在三个技术支柱之上：

**1. 多项式广义光滑性。** 引入多项式有界性、多项式广义连续性和多项式广义光滑性——梯度的 Lipschitz 常数由参数范数的多项式上界约束，而非全局常数。关键性质是这些条件在加法、乘法和复合运算下封闭，因此可逐块验证，并自动适用于由标准组件构成的网络。

**2. 解析非退化性与依赖迭代的 PL 不等式。** 利用常见激活函数和归一化层的实解析性，结合温和的架构非线性条件（要求某一层的堆叠 Jacobian 对一般参数具有满行秩），得到依赖于迭代的 PL 型不等式。关键量 mu_{low,s,X} 沿 GD 轨迹严格为正，防止在非驻点处停滞。

**3. 局部松弛耗散条件。** 引入关于参考驻点附近的局部条件，控制梯度方向与指向驻点方向的对齐关系。任何 C^1 函数都满足此条件，确保 GD 轨迹保持在有界球内。

**主收敛定理：** 在上述条件下，GD 收敛到驻点的 epsilon 邻域，损失以乘法速率衰减。学习率可在 (0, min{(2-delta)/L, 1}] 范围内选取，仅需排除有限个例外值。**实际缩放：** 在 Xavier 初始化下，学习率尺度取决于深度和有效瓶颈维度，而非最大隐藏宽度。**架构含义：** 残差连接泛型地恢复解析映射的非退化性；测度零坏集之外的驻点均为全局极小点。

## Experiment / 实验

This is a purely theoretical paper and does not contain empirical experiments or numerical simulations. The results are demonstrated through rigorous mathematical proofs and verified examples of architectures that satisfy the assumptions.

本文是纯理论工作，不包含实验或数值模拟。结果通过严格的数学证明和满足假设的架构示例来展示。

## Key Results / 关键结果

1. **Convergence for general architectures (Theorem 1):** GD with regular learning rates converges to the neighbourhood of a stationary point for almost all initializations and almost all datasets, covering pre-normalized multi-layer transformers, feedforward, convolutional, and residual networks.

2. **Width requirement is mild (Assumption 4, Lemma 4):** Only one feedforward block near the output needs width larger than Nd (number of samples times output dimension). Other layers can be narrow. This contrasts sharply with NTK analyses requiring all layers to be polynomially wide.

3. **Learning rate scales with depth and bottleneck, not width (Corollaries 1-2):** Under Xavier initialization, the admissible learning rate depends on depth L and effective bottleneck dimension r, decoupled from the largest hidden width m.

4. **Residual connections restore nondegeneracy (Lemma 8):** Adding a small identity component to an analytic function generically makes its Jacobian full-rank, providing a theoretical explanation for why residual architectures are so effective.

5. **Global minimizer characterization (Theorem 2):** Outside a measure-zero bad parameter set, every stationary point of the squared loss is a global minimizer -- all training samples are fitted exactly.

1. **一般架构的收敛性（定理1）：** 使用常规学习率的 GD 对几乎所有初始化和数据集收敛到驻点邻域，覆盖预归一化多层 Transformer、前馈、卷积和残差网络。
2. **宽度要求温和：** 仅需输出端附近的一个前馈块宽度大于 Nd，其他层可以较窄，与 NTK 要求所有层多项式宽度形成鲜明对比。
3. **学习率由深度和瓶颈维度决定，而非宽度：** Xavier 初始化下，可用学习率取决于深度 L 和有效瓶颈维度 r，与最大隐藏宽度 m 解耦。
4. **残差连接恢复非退化性（引理8）：** 为解析函数添加小的恒等分量泛型地使其 Jacobian 满秩，为残差架构的有效性提供理论解释。
5. **全局极小点刻画（定理2）：** 在测度零坏参数集之外，平方损失的每个驻点都是全局极小点。

## Critical Assessment / 批判性评估

**Strengths:**
- The framework is genuinely compositional: regularity conditions are verified block-by-block and preserved under the operations used to build neural networks. This is a meaningful advance over analyses that treat the network as a monolithic function.
- The width requirement (only one near-output block exceeds Nd) is dramatically weaker than NTK-type polynomial overparameterization, bringing theory closer to practice.
- The learning rate scaling depending on bottleneck dimensions rather than the largest width is a practically relevant insight that aligns with empirical practice where transformers use learning rates that do not shrink with model width.
- The structural insight about residual connections and nondegeneracy (Lemma 8) is elegant and provides a clean theoretical explanation for a widely observed empirical phenomenon.

**Weaknesses and Limitations:**
- The result proves convergence to a *neighbourhood* of a stationary point (where the gradient norm is below epsilon_L), not convergence to a stationary point itself, and not global convergence. The gap to global convergence remains substantial.
- The framework only covers deterministic full-batch GD with a fixed learning rate. SGD, Adam, learning rate schedules, and weight decay -- the actual ingredients of modern training -- are not addressed.
- The local dissipative condition, while mild in principle (every C^1 function satisfies it), requires knowledge of a reference stationary point theta* and its relationship to the initialization. In practice, one does not know theta* a priori, making it hard to verify this condition constructively.
- The "almost all initializations" qualifier hides a measure-zero exceptional set whose structure is not characterized. It is unclear whether practically relevant initializations might fall into this set.
- The convergence rate depends on mu_{low,s,X}, which can decay along the trajectory, potentially leading to arbitrarily slow (sublinear) convergence. The theorem does not provide a concrete rate for practical architectures.
- No experiments are provided to validate the tightness of the bounds or the practical relevance of the scaling predictions. For a paper claiming applicability to transformers, even simple verification experiments would strengthen the contribution.

**优势：** 框架真正具有组合性，正则条件逐块验证并在构建网络的运算下保持；宽度要求远弱于 NTK；学习率缩放依赖瓶颈维度而非最大宽度，与实践一致；残差连接与非退化性的结构洞见优雅且实用。

**不足与局限：** 仅证明收敛到驻点邻域而非驻点本身或全局收敛；仅覆盖确定性全批 GD，不涉及 SGD、Adam 等实际优化器；局部耗散条件需要先验已知参考驻点；"几乎所有初始化"的例外集结构未刻画；收敛速率依赖可能沿轨迹衰减的量，实际速率不明确；缺乏实验验证。

## Relevance / 相关性

This paper addresses a fundamental gap in optimization theory for deep learning: extending convergence guarantees beyond the NTK/lazy training regime to cover architecturally realistic networks with regular learning rates. It is relevant to researchers working on optimization theory, training dynamics, and the theoretical foundations of deep learning architectures. The compositional nature of the framework and the insights about residual connections and bottleneck-based scaling could inform architectural design choices. However, the purely theoretical nature and the gap between the covered setting (full-batch GD) and practical training (SGD with Adam) limit its immediate practical impact.

本文解决了深度学习优化理论中的一个根本性缺口：将收敛保证从 NTK/惰性训练范式扩展到具有常规学习率的现实架构。对优化理论、训练动力学和深度学习架构理论基础的研究者具有相关性。框架的组合性质以及关于残差连接和瓶颈缩放的洞见可为架构设计提供参考。但纯理论性质以及所覆盖设定（全批 GD）与实际训练（SGD+Adam）之间的差距限制了其直接实践影响。
