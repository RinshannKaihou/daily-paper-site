# SCOPE: Sequential Conformal Probing for Reliable OOD Rejection in LLM Services

## TL;DR

SCOPE reframes OOD rejection for LLM services as a calibrated service-boundary gating problem rather than an offline scoring problem. It selects the intermediate hidden layer of a frozen LLM where the IND/OOD boundary is most readable, fits a lightweight linear probe, calibrates the rejection threshold via conformal prediction (guaranteeing finite-sample IND false rejection control), and then accumulates sequential evidence via a supermartingale e-process to certify that the gate persistently rejects OOD traffic. The main instantiation, the Conformal Linear Gate (CLG), outperforms standard final-layer detectors (Mahalanobis, cosine, MSP, energy) on gate-level metrics across seven LLM backbones and six boundary conditions, while adding only 1.5% latency overhead.

SCOPE 将 LLM 服务的 OOD 拒绝问题重新定义为校准化的服务边界门控问题，而非离线评分问题。它从冻结 LLM 的中间隐藏层中选择 IND/OOD 边界最可读的层，拟合轻量级线性探针，通过保形预测（conformal prediction）校准拒绝阈值（保证有限样本下 IND 误拒率的控制），然后通过超鞅 e-过程（supermartingale e-process）累积序贯证据以认证门控持续拒绝 OOD 流量。其主要实例化——保形线性门控（CLG）——在七个 LLM 骨干和六种边界条件下，门控级指标优于标准最终层检测器（Mahalanobis、cosine、MSP、energy），仅增加 1.5% 的延迟开销。

## Problem Statement / 问题背景

LLM services increasingly need to decide *when not to answer*. An e-commerce assistant handling order tracking should reject medical or insurance queries before wasting compute on full generation. Existing OOD detectors have three gaps: (1) they assume the OOD signal is best exposed at the final output or final layer, but domain/intent shifts may appear at different depths and become entangled near the output; (2) consistency-based uncertainty methods require multiple generations, making them too expensive for a lightweight pre-generation gate; (3) calibration methods fix a threshold but do not test whether calibrated rejections persist as valid evidence on a held-out stream.

LLM 服务越来越需要决定"何时不作答"。例如，一个处理订单追踪的电商助手应该在完整生成之前拒绝医疗或保险咨询。现有 OOD 检测器存在三个不足：(1) 假设 OOD 信号在最终输出或最终层最为显著，但领域/意图偏移可能出现在不同深度，并在输出层附近纠缠；(2) 基于一致性的不确定性方法需要多次生成，对轻量级预生成门控过于昂贵；(3) 校准方法固定阈值但不测试校准后的拒绝在留出流上是否构成持续有效的证据。

## Method / 方法

SCOPE operates in three steps on a frozen LLM backbone:

**Step 1: Select a Readable Representation.** For each transformer layer l, a layerwise score S_{l,m} is fitted using development IND data (I_d) and a representative OOD source (O_r). Two score families are considered: (a) a *linear readout* (logistic regression on the last-token hidden state), and (b) a *directional readout* (projection onto the IND-to-OOD mean direction). The layer with the highest development AUROC is selected as l*.

**Step 2: Build an IND-Calibrated Rejection Gate (Conformal Linear Gate, CLG).** The selected-layer score is calibrated using a held-out IND-only calibration set C_IND. For a target IND false rejection budget epsilon, the threshold tau is set as the (1-epsilon)-quantile of calibration scores. Under exchangeability, IND false rejection probability is bounded by epsilon + 1/(M+1). This is the *Conformal Linear Gate* (linear readout) or *Conformal Directional Gate* (directional readout).

**Step 3: Certify Service-Boundary Evidence via E-process.** The calibrated gate maps a held-out stream T = {x_t} into binary rejections R_t = 1{S(x_t) > tau}. A nonnegative supermartingale e-process E_t is constructed such that under the null hypothesis (the gate rejects no more often than the IND-calibrated baseline), E[e_t | F_{t-1}] <= 1. When E_t crosses 1/alpha, the framework certifies with probability at most alpha of false certification that the gate persistently rejects above the IND baseline -- i.e., the stream carries genuine OOD signal.

SCOPE 在冻结 LLM 骨干上分三步运行：

**步骤一：选择可读表示。** 对每个 Transformer 层 l，使用开发集 IND 数据和代表性 OOD 源拟合逐层评分。考虑两种评分族：(a) 线性读出（对最后一个 token 隐藏状态的逻辑回归）；(b) 方向读出（投影到 IND-OOD 均值方向）。选择开发集 AUROC 最高的层 l*。

**步骤二：构建 IND 校准拒绝门控（保形线性门控 CLG）。** 使用仅含 IND 的校准集校准所选层评分。对于目标 IND 误拒预算 epsilon，阈值 tau 设为校准分数的 (1-epsilon)-分位数。在可交换性假设下，IND 误拒概率不超过 epsilon + 1/(M+1)。

**步骤三：通过 E-过程认证服务边界证据。** 校准后的门控将留出流映射为二元拒绝序列。构造非负超鞅 e-过程 E_t，当 E_t 超过 1/alpha 时，以至多 alpha 的虚假认证概率认证该门控持续拒绝超出 IND 基线——即流中包含真正的 OOD 信号。

## Experimental Setup / 实验设置

- **Models:** LLaMA-2-7B (primary), Qwen2.5-1.5B/7B/14B, Mistral-7B, OLMo-2-7B, Falcon-7B. All frozen, inference-only.
- **IND domains:** SST-2 (sentiment), 20 Newsgroups (topic), CLINC150 (intent), Banking77 (intent).
- **Six boundary conditions (P1-P6):** P1 = far-OOD transfer (SST-2 vs. WMT14); P2 = cross-task transfer (20NG vs. MNLI); P3 = near-domain (CLINC150 Travel vs. Banking); P4 = fine-grained intent split (Banking77, 38 IND vs. 39 OOD intents); P5 = paraphrase stress (Banking77 paraphrases); P6 = same-distribution null stream (disjoint SST-2).
- **Baselines:** Mahalanobis, Cosine, MSP, Energy, Conformal Directional Gate (CDG).
- **Metrics:** AUROC, FPR@95 (offline); IND false rejection rate, OOD-TPR@tau (calibrated); e-process certificate rate, stopping time T_alpha (sequential).

## Key Results / 关键结果

1. **CLG is the strongest gate-level detector (Table 1).** On LLaMA-2-7B with fixed splits, CLG achieves near-perfect AUROC on SST-2, CLINC-Banking, and CLINC-Travel (1.0000), and gives the lowest FPR@95 across all five settings (0.0000 on four of five). CDG performs well on easy settings but drops on tighter intent boundaries where a one-dimensional direction is insufficient.

2. **Cross-model certification reveals a boundary hierarchy (Figure 4).** All seven backbones achieve reliable certification (rate >= 0.8) on P1 (far-OOD) and P3 (near-domain). P2 (cross-task transfer) produces partial certification (0.2-0.8) for some models. P4 (fine-grained intent) and P5 (paraphrase) are consistently harder, with AUROC around 0.42-0.47 and weak certification. P6 (null stream) correctly stays near chance with near-zero certification.

3. **Sequential evidence works as designed (Figure 3).** Far-OOD streams cross the certification threshold rapidly, near-OOD streams accumulate evidence more gradually, and IND streams stay below the threshold. The certificate is driven by repeated IND-calibrated rejections, not raw confidence scores.

4. **Different boundaries correspond to different geometries (Table 2).** For representation-strong boundaries (P1, P3), multiple readout families (LogReg, Mahalanobis, PCA, KNN) all produce high AUROC and certificate rates. For P4 (intent split), distance-based readouts recover some signal (Maha: 0.70 AUROC, 0.76 cert) but calibrated OOD-TPR remains low. For P5 (paraphrases), distance-based and manifold-based readouts (Maha, KNN) achieve near-perfect AUROC and certification, while the linear readout is weak -- the shift is geometric (rewrite-induced) rather than directional.

5. **Scaling behavior (Figure 6).** Across the Qwen2.5 family (1.5B, 7B, 14B), AUROC generally improves and certification stopping time decreases with model size. The relative depth of the selected layer remains qualitatively stable within the model family.

6. **Deployment overhead (Table 5).** CLG adds only 1.5% latency (9.8 ms vs 9.6 ms base) and requires only a one-time layer search offline. This is comparable to cosine (1.4%) and lower than Mahalanobis (3.6%).

2. **跨模型认证揭示了边界层级（图4）。** 所有七个骨干在 P1（远 OOD）和 P3（近领域）上均达到可靠认证。P4（细粒度意图）和 P5（释义）一致性地更难。P6（零流）正确保持在随机水平。

4. **扩展行为。** 模型越大，AUROC 越高，认证停止时间越短，所选层的相对深度在模型家族内保持稳定。

5. **部署开销极低。** CLG 仅增加 1.5% 延迟，仅需一次性离线层搜索。

## Novelty and Significance / 创新性与意义

- **Reframing:** Treats OOD rejection not as an offline scoring problem but as a calibrated service-boundary certification problem with three composable stages (layer selection, conformal calibration, sequential testing).
- **Layer selection over final-layer assumption:** Demonstrates empirically that intermediate layers can be more readable for service-boundary signals than the final layer, and that the optimal layer varies by boundary type.
- **Sequential evidence accumulation:** The e-process provides anytime-valid certificates that distinguish persistent OOD signal from noise, going beyond single-threshold decisions.
- **Multi-boundary geometry analysis:** Reveals that different OOD boundary definitions (far-OOD, near-domain, intent-level, rewrite-induced) correspond to qualitatively different geometric structures in hidden space, requiring different readout families.

**重新定义问题框架**：将 OOD 拒绝视为校准化的服务边界认证问题。**层选择**：证明中间层可能比最终层更适合检测服务边界信号。**序贯证据累积**：e-过程提供随时有效的认证。**多边界几何分析**：揭示不同 OOD 边界定义对应隐空间中不同的几何结构。

## Critical Assessment / 批判性评价

**Strengths:**
- Principled three-stage framework with clear statistical guarantees (conformal calibration for IND false rejection, Ville's inequality for certification).
- Thorough experimental design with six carefully constructed boundary conditions that stress-test different aspects of OOD rejection, plus a null-stream control (P6) that validates the certificates are not spurious.
- Practical: works on frozen LLMs with minimal overhead, making it deployment-friendly.
- The geometry analysis (Table 2) is genuinely informative -- showing that detection difficulty is not a scalar property but depends on the interaction between boundary type and readout geometry.

**Weaknesses and open questions:**
- The method is *representation-relative*: it certifies that the frozen model's selected layer exposes a readable boundary, not that the boundary is intrinsically separable. If the LLM does not encode the relevant boundary at any layer, SCOPE will (correctly) fail to certify, but offers no recourse.
- Exchangeability assumption between calibration and future IND traffic may not hold under non-stationary real-world conditions (acknowledged by the authors).
- The representative development OOD source must be chosen manually and may not match the actual held-out OOD distribution. The P2 results (cross-task transfer from TREC to MNLI) show this transfer is imperfect.
- P4 and P5 results reveal that SCOPE struggles with fine-grained intent boundaries and paraphrase-induced shifts when using the default linear readout, achieving AUROC around 0.42-0.47 -- these are arguably the most practically relevant scenarios for deployed services.
- No evaluation on instruction-tuned or chat models, which are the actual deployment targets for most LLM services.
- The six boundary conditions, while well-designed, use relatively standard NLP classification datasets rather than realistic service-scope definitions.

**优势**：有原则的三阶段框架，统计保证清晰；实验设计周到（六种边界条件加零流控制）；部署友好。**不足**：方法依赖于冻结模型是否在某层编码了相关边界；可交换性假设在非平稳流量下可能不成立；代表性 OOD 开发源需手动选择；在细粒度意图和释义场景（最具实际意义的场景）下默认线性读出效果有限；未评估指令微调或对话模型。

## Relevance to My Research / 与我的研究的相关性

This paper contributes to the growing literature on representation probing and deployment-time safety for LLMs. The conformal prediction + e-process combination is a clean statistical framework that could generalize to other deployment-time certification problems (e.g., hallucination detection, capability boundary monitoring). The finding that different OOD boundaries take different geometric forms in hidden space connects to the linear representation hypothesis literature (Marks & Tegmark, Park et al., Bao et al.) and suggests that "readability" is feature-dependent rather than universal. The layer-selection strategy could inform work on steering vectors and representation engineering by identifying which layers are most informative for specific semantic distinctions.
