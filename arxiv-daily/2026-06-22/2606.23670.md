## Summary / 摘要

This paper introduces **Tapered Language Models (TLMs)**, an architectural principle where the MLP intermediate dimension monotonically decreases across depth under a fixed total parameter budget. The key insight is that layers contribute non-uniformly to the final output -- earlier layers produce more novel, orthogonal representations while later layers increasingly reinforce content already present in the residual stream. By front-loading MLP capacity (wider early layers, narrower late layers) via a smooth cosine decay schedule, TLMs consistently improve perplexity and downstream benchmark accuracy across four architecture families (Transformer, Gated Attention, Hope-attention, Titans) and three model scales (440M, 760M, 1.3B), at zero additional parameter or compute cost.

本文提出了**Tapered Language Models (TLMs，渐缩语言模型)**，一种在固定总参数预算下令 MLP 中间维度沿深度单调递减的架构设计原则。核心发现是：各层对最终输出的贡献并不均匀——浅层产生更多新颖的、与残差流正交的表征，而深层则越来越多地强化残差流中已有的内容。通过平滑余弦衰减调度将 MLP 容量前置（浅层更宽、深层更窄），TLMs 在四种架构族（Transformer、Gated Attention、Hope-attention、Titans）和三个模型规模（440M、760M、1.3B）上一致性地提升了困惑度和下游基准准确率，且不增加任何参数量或计算开销。

## Key Contributions / 主要贡献

1. **Architectural principle -- tapering**: Formal definition of tapered models where a parameter-bearing component's dimension monotonically decreases across depth while the average dimension (and thus total parameter count and FLOPs) is preserved. Although the principle is general (applicable to attention heads, KV dimensions, memory slots, expert count), this work instantiates it on MLP width.
   **架构原则——渐缩**：形式化定义了渐缩模型，即某个承载参数的组件的维度沿深度单调递减，同时保持平均维度（从而保持总参数量和 FLOPs）不变。虽然该原则具有通用性（可应用于注意力头数、KV 维度、记忆槽、专家数），本文以 MLP 宽度为实例进行研究。

2. **Three decay schedules**: Linear, cosine, and sigmoid schedules for smoothly transitioning MLP width from d_start to d_end. Cosine consistently outperforms the others, achieving the best perplexity at a 1.5/0.5 width ratio (relative to baseline d_ff).
   **三种衰减调度**：线性、余弦和 sigmoid 调度用于从 d_start 到 d_end 平滑过渡 MLP 宽度。余弦调度一致性地优于其它方案，在宽度比为 1.5/0.5（相对于基线 d_ff）时达到最优困惑度。

3. **Architecture-agnostic gains**: Tapering improves all four architectures at both 760M and 1.3B scales -- average commonsense reasoning accuracy improves in every case, and LAMBADA perplexity improves in all 8 comparisons.
   **架构无关的增益**：渐缩在所有四种架构的 760M 和 1.3B 两个规模上均带来提升——每种情况下平均常识推理准确率均有提高，LAMBADA 困惑度在全部 8 组对比中均有改善。

4. **Mechanistic analysis via layer-wise novelty**: Cosine similarity between MLP outputs and the residual stream (rho_MLP) rises monotonically with depth across the GPT-2 family (124M to 1.5B), showing later MLPs increasingly reinforce existing content rather than adding new features. This directly motivates reducing capacity where it contributes least.
   **基于逐层新颖性的机理分析**：MLP 输出与残差流之间的余弦相似度 (rho_MLP) 在 GPT-2 系列（124M 至 1.5B）中随深度单调上升，表明深层 MLP 越来越多地强化已有内容而非添加新特征。这直接支持了在贡献最小处减少容量的设计。

## Methodology / 方法论

**Motivating experiment**: On a 440M Transformer, layers are split into three equal groups (early, middle, late) with different MLP widths but the same total parameter count. The "wider-early" allocation (1.5x / 1.0x / 0.5x) achieves the lowest perplexity (15.96), beating the uniform baseline (16.28) by 0.32 points. The reverse "wider-late" allocation hurts substantially (17.29), a >1 point asymmetry.

**激励实验**：在 440M Transformer 上，将各层分为三个等分组（浅层、中层、深层），分配不同 MLP 宽度但保持总参数量不变。"浅层更宽"分配（1.5x / 1.0x / 0.5x）获得最低困惑度（15.96），超过均匀基线（16.28）0.32 点。反向的"深层更宽"分配则严重恶化（17.29），不对称性超过 1 点。

**Tapering formulation**: Replace the constant d_ff with per-layer d_ff(l) that decreases monotonically, subject to the constraint that the average equals the baseline d_ff. Three schedules:
- **Linear**: d_ff(l) = d_start - (d_start - d_end) * l/(L-1)
- **Cosine**: d_ff(l) = d_end + (d_start - d_end)/2 * (1 + cos(pi*l/(L-1)))
- **Sigmoid**: d_ff(l) = d_end + (d_start - d_end) / (1 + exp(k*(l/(L-1) - 0.5)))

Per-layer widths are rounded to multiples of 16 for hardware efficiency. All other hyperparameters (d, attention heads, KV dimension) remain unchanged.

**渐缩公式化**：将常量 d_ff 替换为逐层递减的 d_ff(l)，约束条件为平均值等于基线 d_ff。三种调度如上。每层宽度四舍五入到 16 的倍数以保证硬件效率。所有其他超参数保持不变。

**Schedule and width sweep**: A 5x3 grid search over width ratios {1.25/0.75, 1.375/0.625, 1.5/0.5, 1.625/0.375, 1.75/0.25} and three schedules on the 440M Transformer. Cosine dominates at every ratio; the optimal ratio is 1.5/0.5 with perplexity 14.44 (vs. 16.28 baseline, a 1.84 point improvement). The U-shaped response to width ratio shows the optimum is neither too mild nor too aggressive.

**调度与宽度扫描**：在 440M Transformer 上对宽度比和三种调度进行 5x3 网格搜索。余弦调度在每个比率上均占优；最优比率为 1.5/0.5，困惑度 14.44（基线 16.28，改善 1.84 点）。困惑度对宽度比呈 U 形响应，表明最优点既非过于温和也非过于激进。

## Results / 实验结果

**Main results (Table 2, 760M and 1.3B)**:

| Architecture | Scale | Avg. Accuracy (Uniform -> Tapered) |
|---|---|---|
| Transformer++ | 760M | 52.25 -> 52.84 |
| Gated Attention | 760M | 52.61 -> 52.88 |
| Hope-attention | 760M | 53.69 -> 54.05 |
| Titans | 760M | 52.30 -> 53.29 |
| Transformer++ | 1.3B | 56.05 -> 56.38 |
| Gated Attention | 1.3B | 56.51 -> 56.80 |
| Hope-attention | 1.3B | 56.95 -> 57.05 |
| Titans | 1.3B | 56.73 -> 57.08 |

All eight architecture-scale combinations improve. LAMBDA perplexity improves in all 8 cases. WikiText perplexity improves in 7 of 8 cases.

所有八个架构-规模组合均有提升。LAMBADA 困惑度在全部 8 种情况下均改善。WikiText 困惑度在 8 种中的 7 种有改善。

**Mechanistic finding**: MLP-residual cosine similarity (rho_MLP) rises monotonically with depth across all GPT-2 scales (124M to 1.5B), with Pearson correlations ranging from r=0.49 to r=0.71. This confirms that later MLPs produce less novel information, justifying capacity reduction at deeper layers.

**机理发现**：MLP-残差余弦相似度 (rho_MLP) 在所有 GPT-2 规模（124M 至 1.5B）上随深度单调上升，Pearson 相关系数从 r=0.49 到 r=0.71。这确认了深层 MLP 产生的新颖信息更少，为深层减少容量提供了理论依据。

## Critical Assessment / 批判性评估

**Strengths**:
- Extremely clean experimental design: single axis of variation (MLP width), matched parameters and FLOPs, same training recipe. This isolation is rare and makes the results credible.
- The breadth across four genuinely different architectures (softmax attention, gated attention, self-modifying memory, neural long-term memory) is impressive and suggests the finding is about depth itself, not a quirk of attention.
- The mechanistic analysis (layer-wise novelty measurement) provides a principled reason beyond empirical observation.
- Zero-cost improvement -- no additional parameters, FLOPs, or training changes.

**优势**：
- 极其干净的实验设计：单一变量（MLP 宽度），匹配的参数量和 FLOPs，相同的训练方案。这种隔离控制在该领域中罕见，使结果可信。
- 横跨四种本质不同的架构令人印象深刻，表明发现与深度本身有关，而非注意力机制的特性。
- 机理分析提供了超越经验观察的原则性解释。
- 零成本改进——不增加参数、FLOPs 或训练变化。

**Weaknesses**:
- The schedule/ratio sweep is only done on the 440M Transformer and transferred unchanged to other architectures and scales. The authors acknowledge the optimal configuration may differ, meaning the reported gains are a lower bound but also that the method requires per-setting tuning for maximum benefit.
- The absolute magnitude of improvements (0.3-1.0 points in average accuracy) is modest, though consistent. At larger scales (7B+, 70B+), it is unclear whether the gains persist or diminish.
- Only MLP width is tapered; the paper repeatedly notes that attention heads, KV dimension, etc. are natural candidates but provides no experiments on these axes.
- No comparison with recent concurrent work that also varies per-layer capacity (e.g., Ikeda et al. 2025 which deactivates FFNs entirely in some layers, or Baroian and Notebomer 2025 which ablates layer-wise scaling shapes). The related work section discusses these but does not provide head-to-head results.
- Training data is not specified beyond token counts; reproducibility relies on architectural details only.

**不足**：
- 调度/比率搜索仅在 440M Transformer 上进行并直接迁移到其他架构和规模。作者承认最优配置可能不同，这意味着报告的增益是下界，但也说明该方法需要针对具体设置调优才能获得最大收益。
- 改进的绝对幅度（平均准确率 0.3-1.0 点）较为有限，尽管一致。在更大规模（7B+、70B+）下增益是否持续尚不清楚。
- 仅对 MLP 宽度进行渐缩；论文反复提到注意力头数、KV 维度等是自然候选但未提供这些轴上的实验。
- 未与同期变化逐层容量的工作进行直接对比（如 Ikeda et al. 2025 完全停用某些层的 FFN，Baroian & Notebomer 2025 消融逐层缩放形状）。
- 训练数据未详细说明，可复现性依赖于架构细节。

## Relevance / 相关性

This work addresses a fundamental and under-examined design choice in language model architecture: the uniform allocation of parameters across depth. The finding that a simple, smooth reallocation (wider early, narrower late) consistently helps across architectures and scales is practically useful for anyone training language models. The principle is easy to implement (a few lines of code to generate per-layer widths) and costs nothing. The mechanistic insight that later layers are increasingly redundant connects to the broader literature on layer importance, early exit, and model compression, potentially informing pruning strategies and efficient inference.

本工作解决了语言模型架构中一个基础但未被充分研究的设计选择：参数在深度上的均匀分配。简单平滑的重新分配（浅层更宽、深层更窄）在各种架构和规模上一致有效，对训练语言模型的从业者有实际价值。该原则易于实现（几行代码生成逐层宽度）且零成本。深层冗余性递增的机理洞见与层重要性、早退出和模型压缩的文献相关联，可能为剪枝策略和高效推理提供指导。
