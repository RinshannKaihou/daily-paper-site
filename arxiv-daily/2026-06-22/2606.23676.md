# Open Problem: Is AdamW Effective Under Heavy-Tailed Noise?

## TL;DR
This paper formally poses an open problem in optimization theory: can AdamW match the convergence rates of sign-based optimizers (Lion, Muon) under heavy-tailed stochastic gradient noise? The authors provide partial results -- a positive weighted-metric benchmark showing AdamW makes progress, and a corridor lower-bound mechanism showing its second-moment denominator can hide large gradients -- but the core question remains unresolved.

本文正式提出一个优化理论中的开放问题：在重尾随机梯度噪声下，AdamW 能否匹配基于符号的优化器（如 Lion、Muon）的收敛速率？作者提供了部分结果——一个正向的加权度量基准表明 AdamW 确实在进步，以及一个"通道"下界机制表明其二阶矩分母可能隐藏大梯度——但核心问题仍未解决。

## Problem Statement / 问题陈述

AdamW is the de facto optimizer for training large language models, yet its convergence theory has been developed almost entirely under finite-variance noise assumptions. Empirical evidence increasingly shows that stochastic gradient noise in LLM pretraining is heavy-tailed: only finite p-th moments exist for p in (1,2], meaning the second moment (and thus variance) can be infinite. Sign-based optimizers like Lion and Muon have been proven to achieve sharp heavy-tailed convergence rates of O(T^{-(p-1)/(3p-2)}), which is tight. No analogous result exists for AdamW.

AdamW 是训练大语言模型的事实标准优化器，但其收敛理论几乎完全建立在有限方差噪声假设之上。越来越多的实证表明，LLM 预训练中的随机梯度噪声是重尾的：仅存在 p 阶矩（p 在 (1,2] 之间），意味着二阶矩（从而方差）可能是无穷大。基于符号的优化器（如 Lion 和 Muon）已被证明在重尾条件下可达到尖锐的收敛速率 O(T^{-(p-1)/(3p-2)})，且该速率是紧的。AdamW 尚无类似结果。

The open problem asks: under the gradient-curvature (GC) and heavy-tailed (HT) noise model, can AdamW achieve the same ell_1-stationarity rate as sign-based methods, or does its second-moment accumulator create a genuine obstruction?

开放问题是：在梯度-曲率（GC）和重尾（HT）噪声模型下，AdamW 能否达到与基于符号方法相同的 ell_1 平稳性速率，还是其二阶矩累加器构成了真正的障碍？

## Technical Setup / 技术设定

The paper adopts the coordinate-wise gradient-curvature condition (GC) from Yu et al. (2026b), where local smoothness can depend on the current gradient magnitude, and the heavy-tailed noise model (HT) with finite p-th moments (p in (1,2]) per coordinate. The core difficulty is that for p < 2, the conditional second moment E[g_{t,i}^2 | F_{t-1}] may be infinite, so the standard strategy of replacing Adam's v_t by a conditional second-moment proxy is unavailable.

本文采用 Yu et al. (2026b) 提出的逐坐标梯度-曲率条件（GC），其中局部光滑性可依赖于当前梯度幅度，以及逐坐标的重尾噪声模型（HT），仅要求有限的 p 阶矩（p 在 (1,2]）。核心困难在于：当 p < 2 时，条件二阶矩 E[g_{t,i}^2 | F_{t-1}] 可能是无穷大，因此无法使用标准的"用条件二阶矩代理替换 v_t"的分析策略。

## Key Results / 核心结果

### Proposition 2: Positive Weighted-Metric Benchmark / 正向加权度量基准

For AdamW with lambda=0, constant learning rate, and beta_2 = beta_1 (equal-memory regime), the authors prove that a weighted stationarity measure S_t = <a_t, |grad f(x_t)|> achieves the target heavy-tailed rate O((BT)^{-(p-1)/(3p-2)}), where a_{t,i} = |m_{t,i}| / (sqrt(v_{t,i}) + eps_t) are the Adam self-normalized weights. This shows AdamW does make progress in a weighted sense, but the weights themselves are data-dependent and may collapse after heavy-tailed outliers.

对于 lambda=0、恒定学习率、beta_2 = beta_1（等记忆机制）的 AdamW，作者证明加权平稳性度量 S_t = <a_t, |grad f(x_t)|> 达到目标重尾速率 O((BT)^{-(p-1)/(3p-2)})，其中 a_{t,i} = |m_{t,i}| / (sqrt(v_{t,i}) + eps_t) 是 Adam 的自归一化权重。这表明 AdamW 在加权意义下确实在进步，但权重本身是数据依赖的，可能在重尾异常值后崩塌。

### Proposition 3: Averaging Is Necessary / 平均化的必要性

Without momentum or mini-batching (beta_1 = beta_2 = 0), there exist unbiased oracles with finite p-th moments where AdamW (reducing to SignSGD) has negative expected alignment E[mu*g/|g|] < 0. This means no AdamW convergence theorem can rely on unbiasedness alone -- it must explain how mini-batching, momentum, or robustness restores alignment.

在没有动量或小批量（beta_1 = beta_2 = 0）的情况下，存在具有有限 p 阶矩的无偏预言机，使得 AdamW（退化为 SignSGD）的期望对齐 E[mu*g/|g|] < 0。这意味着任何 AdamW 收敛定理都不能仅依赖无偏性——必须解释小批量、动量或鲁棒性如何恢复对齐。

### Proposition 5: Corridor Lower-Bound Mechanism / 通道下界机制

For 1D AdamW with long memory (beta_1^2 < beta_2), the authors construct a smooth function and an unbiased oracle where AdamW's average stationarity is bounded below by Omega(mu), even though the sign-method target is o(mu). The mechanism: rare negative outliers with probability q < 1/2 leave SignSGD with positive drift, but Adam's v_t remembers their squares and can make |m_t|/sqrt(v_t) polynomially small. This identifies a concrete long-memory regime where weighted stationarity can coexist with failure of plain ell_1 stationarity.

对于一维长记忆 AdamW（beta_1^2 < beta_2），作者构造了一个光滑函数和无偏预言机，使得 AdamW 的平均平稳性被 Omega(mu) 从下方界定，而符号方法的目标是 o(mu)。机制是：概率 q < 1/2 的罕见负异常值使 SignSGD 保持正漂移，但 Adam 的 v_t 会记住它们的平方，使 |m_t|/sqrt(v_t) 多项式地变小。这确定了一个具体的长记忆机制，其中加权平稳性与普通 ell_1 平稳性失败可以共存。

## Why This Matters / 为什么重要

The practical stakes are significant. If AdamW can match sign-based rates under heavy tails, then the empirical advantage of Lion and Muon over AdamW in LLM training must come from aspects beyond classical convergence theory (e.g., implicit regularization, better constants). If AdamW provably fails, it would provide theoretical justification for the ongoing shift toward sign-based and matrix optimizers (Muon) in large-scale LLM systems like Kimi-K2, GLM-5, and DeepSeek-V4.

实际意义重大。如果 AdamW 能在重尾条件下匹配基于符号的速率，那么 Lion 和 Muon 在 LLM 训练中对 AdamW 的经验优势必须来自经典收敛理论之外的因素（如隐式正则化、更好的常数）。如果 AdamW 被证明失败，将为大规模 LLM 系统（如 Kimi-K2、GLM-5 和 DeepSeek-V4）从 AdamW 转向基于符号和矩阵优化器（Muon）提供理论依据。

## Critical Assessment / 批判性评估

**Strengths:**
- The open problem is sharply formulated with a concrete comparison target (the sign-based rate), making it falsifiable in both directions.
- The partial results are genuinely informative: the weighted-metric benchmark (Proposition 2) isolates the exact remaining step (proving self-normalized weights cannot hide large gradients), while the corridor lower bound (Proposition 5) shows this step is non-trivial.
- The paper correctly identifies the key technical barrier: the mismatch between exponential numerator (momentum) and exponential squared-gradient denominator in AdamW, which does not arise in AdaGrad's monotone accumulator.

**Weaknesses:**
- The positive result (Proposition 2) holds only in the equal-memory regime beta_2 = beta_1, whereas practical AdamW uses beta_2 >> beta_1 (typically 0.999 vs 0.9). The gap between the analyzed and practical regimes is acknowledged but not bridged.
- The corridor lower bound (Proposition 5) is not a universal lower bound over all hyperparameters -- it identifies a regime where failure can occur, but does not rule out that a careful tuning of beta_1, beta_2 could avoid it.
- The paper does not discuss whether weight decay (lambda > 0, the "W" in AdamW) might fundamentally change the picture, noting only that lambda = 0 is the "core case."

**优势：**
- 开放问题被精确地表述，具有具体的比较目标（基于符号的速率），使得在两个方向上都可以证伪。
- 部分结果确实有启发性：加权度量基准（命题2）精确隔离了剩余步骤（证明自归一化权重不能隐藏大梯度），而通道下界（命题5）表明这一步骤并非平凡。
- 正确识别了关键技术障碍：AdamW 中指数动量分子与指数平方梯度分母之间的不匹配，这在 AdaGrad 的单调累加器中不会出现。

**不足：**
- 正向结果（命题2）仅在等记忆机制 beta_2 = beta_1 下成立，而实际 AdamW 使用 beta_2 >> beta_1（通常 0.999 对 0.9）。分析与实际机制之间的差距被承认但未弥合。
- 通道下界（命题5）不是对所有超参数的普遍下界——它识别了可能失败的机制，但未排除仔细调整 beta_1、beta_2 可以避免的可能性。
- 论文未讨论权重衰减（lambda > 0，AdamW 中的"W"）是否可能从根本上改变局面，仅指出 lambda = 0 是"核心情况"。

## Relevance to LLM Training / 与 LLM 训练的相关性

Directly relevant. This paper addresses the theoretical foundation of the most widely used LLM optimizer. The heavy-tailed noise model is empirically validated on nanoGPT pretraining (Yu et al., 2026b). The competing optimizers (Lion, Muon) with proven heavy-tailed guarantees are already deployed in production LLM systems. Resolving this open problem would have immediate implications for optimizer selection in LLM pretraining.

直接相关。本文探讨了最广泛使用的 LLM 优化器的理论基础。重尾噪声模型已在 nanoGPT 预训练上得到实证验证（Yu et al., 2026b）。具有重尾保证的竞争优化器（Lion、Muon）已在生产级 LLM 系统中部署。解决此开放问题将对 LLM 预训练中的优化器选择产生直接影响。
