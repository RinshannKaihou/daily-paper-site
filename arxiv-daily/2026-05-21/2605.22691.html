Posterior Collapse as Automatic Spectral Pruning
Johannes Hirn 2026-05-21
VAE posterior-collapse spectral-analysis representation-learning interpretability
Key Metric
Signal fraction M² = μ²/(μ² + σ²) as scale-invariant order parameter; mode-level duality Tₖ = ΔD̃ₖ = λₖ/V confirmed for ranks 1–16 on WorldClim with no level crossings across 32 latent dimensions.
Why This Matters
Transforms posterior collapse from a perceived failure mode into a principled analysis tool — enabling systematic ranking of latent dimensions by reconstruction utility and providing a dataset-independent criterion for choosing β.
Critical Edge
Landau stability analysis applied to β-VAE, yielding the normalized information price T = βσ²_dec/V as a dataset-independent control parameter, and proving the collapse–utility–PCA triple duality analytically for linear Gaussian VAEs.
Methodology

For a scalar linear Gaussian VAE, the decoder is analytically eliminated by optimizing w at fixed posterior statistics, reducing the loss to a function of signal fraction M² and absolute latent scale A². Minimization yields a branch structure — active (M² > 0) for T < Tₖ = 1, collapsed (M² = 0) for T ≥ 1 — with a Landau expansion near the critical point. In the d-dimensional case, PCA diagonalizes the data covariance and the global loss factorizes into independent one-mode problems per eigenvalue λₖ, establishing Tₖ = λₖ/V and the central identity Tₖ = ΔD̃ₖ. The collapse spectroscopy protocol scans T over many decades, trains a 32-latent linear VAE at each point to equilibrium, monitors four observables per latent (μ², σ², log σ², KL), ranks latents by M², and fits collapse thresholds. Validated on WorldClim (19 bioclimatic variables, 808K samples, geography-aware splits) over T ∈ [10⁻⁷, 1].

Strengths & Limitations
Strengths
  • Elegant reframing: turns a perceived failure mode into a principled analysis tool.
  • Clean math with explicit normalization resolving ambiguities in β-VAE literature.
  • Signal fraction M² is robust and scale-invariant — practical for VAE interpretability.
  • Honest scope; linear case presented as calibrated null model, not oversold.
  • Normalized information price T is actionable and dataset-independent.
Limitations
  • Results proven only for linear Gaussian VAEs; nonlinear case is qualitative only.
  • Single dataset (WorldClim) validation; image/text modalities untested.
  • 32 latents with 19 input features leaves many redundant by construction.
  • No comparison with existing pruning or latent selection methods.
  • Duality degrades at high ranks (17+) due to fitting noise.
Questions for Further Exploration
  1. Does the utility–threshold duality hold approximately for nonlinear decoders, or do mode interactions break it?
  2. Can collapse spectroscopy adapt to discrete latent spaces (e.g., VQ-VAE with autoregressive priors)?
  3. Does the normalized T outperform raw β for hyperparameter selection in standard nonlinear β-VAEs?
  4. How does the collapse cascade interact with disentanglement — are the most useful modes also the most disentangled?
  5. What does the spectral tail reveal about model capacity waste when latents are deliberately over-specified?
