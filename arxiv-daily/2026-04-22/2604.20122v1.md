> **一句话总结：** 作者提出 W₁-ACAS：把预训练时间序列基础模型（TTM、Chronos、TiRex）的预测误差作为 nonconformity score，通过最小化加权 p-value 经验 CDF 与均匀分布的 1-Wasserstein 距离，在线学习历史权重以适应分布漂移，保留加权 conformal prediction 的形式化误警率保证。

> **TL;DR:** W₁-ACAS is a post-hoc, model-agnostic adaptive conformal anomaly detection framework for time series. Nonconformity scores come from pretrained time-series foundation models (TTM, Chronos-Bolt-Small, TiRex); conformal p-values are aggregated across forecast horizons via median. Weights on past scores are learned online by minimizing the 1-Wasserstein distance between the p-value CDF and uniform, subject to effective-sample-size ≥ 1/αc. ICLR 2026 paper with code released.

## 核心贡献
- 把加权 conformal quantile 的权重学习形式化为 1-Wasserstein 校准目标。
- 把 TSFM 预测误差包装成 conformal p-value，实现零 fine-tune 的即插即用异常分数。
- 多时刻预测（horizon aggregation via median of p-values）给出鲁棒单一分数。

## Key Contributions
- Model-agnostic adaptive conformal framework with formal false-alarm rate bounds under non-exchangeability (Proposition 4.1, via Barber et al. 2023 weighted-CP theorem).
- 1-Wasserstein calibration objective that keeps scores uniform across time under distribution shift.
- Practical online optimization via projected gradient descent on past-sample window (Algorithm 1).
- Integration with three TSFMs across 7 univariate and 4 multivariate benchmarks (YAHOO, NEK, NAB, MSL, IOPS, STOCK, WSD, TAO, GECCO, LTDB, Genesis).

## Methodology
Pretrained TSFM h predicts Y_{t+1} over D=15 steps with context length 52. Nonconformity S = |Y − Ŷ|. Weighted conformal quantile uses weights w ∈ [0,1]^n, effective sample size ≥ 1/αc = 99 for αc = 0.01. Online W₁ minimization approximates empirical CDF, yields projected gradient update on w (Eqs. 12–14). Aggregates horizon p-values by median. Compared against Gaussian baseline, offline Conformal, KShape, POLY, Sub-PCA, Sub-KNN, SAND, CNN, USAD, OmniAnomaly, MOMENT (zero-shot).

## Results & Highlights
- Strongest PA-F1 and Affiliation-F1 on threshold-dependent metrics against semi-supervised deep baselines.
- Lowest variance in FPR vs threshold curves in the low-FPR regime.
- Shows adaptation to seasonal / periodic error patterns (Figure 1(c)).
- Multivariate extension via horizon-specific detectors aggregated.

## Critical Assessment
- Novelty: Strong. Extends Barber 2023 weighted CP with a principled calibration objective (W₁ to uniform) and couples it to TSFMs for zero-shot monitoring.
- Rigor: Formal guarantees inherited from weighted CP; Proposition 4.1 explicitly states residual TV-distance correction term. Online optimization derivation is explicit.
- Reproducibility: Code released, datasets are standard, hyperparameters reported (αc=0.01, batch size 10, γ=0.001).
- Significance: High — industrial monitoring with zero training data and formal guarantees is a real pain point.
- Red flags: The TV-distance slack in the bound can be vacuous in heavily non-stationary regimes; the paper needs this to be small for the formal guarantee to bite. 1-Wasserstein minimization alone does not guarantee optimal detection power — only calibration. αc lower bounds the minimum detectable p-value.

## Strengths
- Combines TSFM strengths (zero-shot forecasting) with CP's distribution-free coverage in one clean pipeline.
- Median-over-horizons aggregation is robust and interpretable.
- Open source with a broad empirical evaluation.

## Limitations
- Guarantees are approximate under heavy non-exchangeability.
- Relies on TSFM quality; for domains where foundation forecasters are weak, baseline Gaussian fit outperforms.
- Does not detect structured anomalies that preserve forecasting error statistics.
- Horizon aggregation via median is a heuristic; principled combination not derived.

## Relevance
直接契合你关心的可观测性 / ML 可靠性方向——给出了具形式保证且可在线适应分布漂移的异常分数，适用于 LLM 推理监控、silent data corruption 检测等场景。

## Questions for Further Exploration
- How does W₁-ACAS compare to adaptive quantile regression-based CP (Romano et al.) on heavy-tailed industrial signals?
- Can the learned weights reveal interpretable change-points (e.g., concept drift)?
- Does replacing TSFM with an LLM-based forecaster preserve the guarantees?
- How sensitive is the detector to αc choice in the presence of adversarial signal injection?
