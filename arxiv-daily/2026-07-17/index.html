xHC Pushes the Residual Stream Past Its Ceiling

First Hyper-Connections variant to drive the residual-stream expansion rate N meaningfully beyond 4 — all the way to 16. Answer-Conditioned CoT Silently Poisons Distillation

A pristine one-bit experiment: when a generator writes chain-of-thought after seeing the gold answer, the student's verifiable reasoning degrades — even when every answer is correct. Costs 16 MATH-500 points; widens to 27 on AIME.
