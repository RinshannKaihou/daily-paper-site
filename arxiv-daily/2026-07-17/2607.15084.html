01

Methodology — measuring leakage in pure geometry

black-box / grey-box · identity-level MIA

An auditor queries the target model f with ≥2 probe images per identity, reads ℓ2-normalised 512-d embeddings, computes per-identity cluster statistics, then sweeps a threshold to report membership-AUC. No logits, loss, or weights exposed.

Four per-identity statistics

  • PairCos — mean pairwise cosine within the identity (tighter cluster ⇒ higher).
  • vMF κ — von Mises-Fisher concentration, closed-form MLE on the unit hypersphere.
  • PenLogit — margin-penalised logit via leave-one-out prototype (needs s, m → grey-box).
  • ProtoCE — surrogate cross-entropy vs a per-identity prototype.

Factorial design & analysis

  • Grid: 3 backbones × 3 loss heads × 5 nids × 4 epoch counts = 180 models; ViT-T/CosFace control.
  • Reference: same-domain held-out WebFace4M split (≤2500 members + 2500 non-members per model) to disentangle domain shift.
  • Stats: Type-II ANOVA partial η̂p², Cohen's d′, Kruskal–Wallis ϵ², probit-scale AUC translation, 2-layer MLP fusion (64→32, 5-fold CV).

03

Strengths

measurement, not attack
  • Scale. 180 fully-crossed configs + ViT-T control — an order of magnitude beyond prior work (max 9).
  • Honest framing. Measurement study with explicit lower-bound caveats; no overclaiming.
  • Clean device. Same-domain held-out reference quantifies how much prior AUCs were inflated by domain shift.
  • Statistically backed. ANOVA, η̂p², Cohen's d′, Kruskal–Wallis ϵ², Wilcoxon — including the negative result that backbone/loss matter little.
  • Deployable guidance. Train on more identities; use same-domain references; expect inflated AUC on cross-pose/cross-quality benchmarks.
04

Limitations

what it cannot say
  • Single corpus. WebFace4M only; absolute AUCs may not generalise (no comparable public set remains).
  • No defences. DP-SGD, distillation, unlearning all out of scope — mitigation story incomplete.
  • Geometry only. A richer model on raw embeddings could extract more; all numbers are lower bounds.
  • Uncontrolled probe count k across benchmarks biases per-statistic estimates non-uniformly.
  • No transfer test. Fusion validated in-distribution; shadow-model / cross-model utility not established.
  • Unreleased splits. "Fixed seed" held-out partition and per-identity partitions not published.
