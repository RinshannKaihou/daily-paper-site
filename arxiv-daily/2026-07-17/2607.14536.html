Why this matters
A new conceptual axis for LLM optimizer design — explains why Muon beats normalized momentum and guides principled non-native matrixizations for awkward non-square layers.
Critical edge
No comparison to competitive Muon variants (Muoneq, Soap, Adafactor) at 600M, and AdamW is omitted from the 600M row — so it stays unclear whether any non-native representation is ever strictly better than Native.

01 Methodology

Parameterize Muon-style updates by a fixed Frobenius-isometric representation T: R^d → R^{p×q}, p≤q, holding the momentum rule and Newton–Schulz polar backend fixed. Only the layout varies.

  • Update. M_t = μM_{t-1} + (1-μ)G_t; X_{t+1} = X_t - η T^{-1}Orth(T(M_t)).
  • Spectral-ball descent (Thm 3.1): ⟨G,D_T(G)⟩ = ‖T(G)‖_* — polar step is steepest descent on the spectral ball.
  • Stationarity (Thm 3.2): unified O(K^{-1/4}) nonconvex rate; constants enter only via short side p_T.
  • Curvature collapse + MP (Thm 3.3/3.5): teacher–student Hessian collapses to a scalar Frobenius quadratic; early dissipation ordered by β(γ)·γ^{1/4}.
  • Setup. LLaMA2 130M (2.60B tok, 8×910B) & 600M (12.58B tok, 8×910C); 3 seeds; module-level fixed-momentum diagnostics.

  • MP shape factor (Fig 1b). Empirical g_T = d^{-1/4}‖T(M)‖_* / ‖T(M)‖_F tracks theory g(γ)=β(γ)γ^{1/4} and rises monotonically with represented aspect ratio.

04 Strengths

  • Clean isolation. One degree of freedom (representation) varied; momentum rule, polar backend, and scaling held shared.
  • Unified geometry. Muon, nSGDM, and the Muse family placed on a single spectral-ball descent surface (Thms 3.1–3.2).
  • Falsifiable prediction. β(γ)γ^{1/4} dissipation ordering is concrete and empirically supported at two scales.
  • Honest scope. Section 6 explicitly enumerates locality, single-cell, and β_{1,C,N}-can-go-negative caveats.
  • Empirical rigor. Three seeds at both 130M and 600M; module-level fixed-momentum diagnostics across transformer block types.

05 Limitations

  • Local theory. Dissipation result holds only for fixed ReLU masks, single empirical cell, large-C/N asymptotics; β_{1,C,N} can turn negative under real activation-pattern drift.
  • MP is a model. Validated against g_T computed on Muon-produced momentum blocks — partially circular; non-MP sensitivity untested.
  • Missing baselines. No Muoneq / balanced-Muon / Soap / Adafactor at 600M; AdamW omitted from the 600M row.
  • Modest ceiling. 600M / 12.58B tok is below where Muon shines; depth-amplification hints conclusions may shift at scale.
  • No ablations. Weight decay, momentum μ, and Newton–Schulz iteration count all held fixed.
