01Methodology

One checkpoint, two roles, refreshed every iteration.

A two-stage recipe layered on outcome-based RL for multi-turn agents, where trajectory-level reward mismatches token-level updates.

Stage 1 · Hindsight-skill SFT

Base policy rolls out K0 trajectories per task; an external analyzer (GLM-5.2) writes a natural-language hindsight skill from each. Base model is SFT-ed to predict the skill from the trajectory — bootstrapping analysis capability.

Stage 2 · Self-evolving OPD

Frozen snapshot collects N=8 trajectories + writes one skill each. Same sampled tokens are re-scored under ordinary (student) vs skill-augmented (teacher) context. Detached shift Δ = sg[ℓ_skill − ℓ_θ] passes a sigmoid gate g = σ(β·Δ), β=5.0. Loss combines with GRPO: L_SEED = L_rl + λ·L_opd, λ=0.01. Gradients flow only through the student — the skill is internalized.

  • Zero inference overhead: analyzer removed at deploy; no retrieved skills, no extra prompts.
  • Builds on SDAR (Lu et al., 2026a) for the gating formulation; delta is self-evolving + synchronized analyzer.
  • Compute: 8× A800 80GB · 150 steps · lr 1e-6 · max 30/15/4 interaction steps.

03Strengths

A clean, deployable recipe with theory that does work.

  • One model, two roles, refreshed each iteration — no inference-time skill prompts, no external memory, clean deployment story.
  • Largest gains on the weakest backbone (Qwen3-1.7B: +45.9), exactly where dense supervision should matter most.
  • Sample efficiency: 60% data SEED > 100% data GRPO; meaningful cross-domain generalization (+15.3 unseen).
  • Theory earns its keep: tied-reward variance identity (Var_π[w]) explains why SEED still learns when GRPO's gradient is exactly zero.
  • Honest framing — theory proves informativeness, not correctness; no monotonic-return guarantee claimed.
  • Public code, checkpoints, prompts, full hyperparameter table.

04Limitations

The vision claim, the analyzer dependency, and the modest horizon.

  • Vision experiments claimed in the abstract but not delivered in the body — only text-based benchmarks (ALFWorld, WebShop, Search-QA) are reported.
  • Depends on GLM-5.2 as the SFT-time external analyzer; no sensitivity study to analyzer choice or size. “Self-evolving” only holds after external bootstrap.
  • No quantitative skill-quality evaluation — the exact failure mode (behaviorally-misleading hindsight) the theory flags is never measured.
  • Concedes specific cells: WebShop-Succ on 7B (78.1 < SDAR 82.8); ALFWorld Pick2 on 3B (70.6), underexplained.
  • Qwen-only backbones — no Llama/Mistral/proprietary; cross-family generalization unverified.
  • Modest horizons (≤30 steps); behavior on cited long-horizon suites (OdysseyArena, LH-Terminal-Bench) untested.
  • Compute: double forward passes per update; no wall-clock or token-throughput comparison reported.
