Accuracy is not consistency.

GPT-5.4-mini scores 98.9% accuracy but only 60.3% family consistency across logically equivalent reformulations — a 38.6pp gap on a benchmark where the answers should not move.

Methodology

  • Question family = 1 base + 4 controlled reformulations; 350 families, 1,750 questions.
  • Seven balanced categories: propositional, syllogistic, De Morgan, quantifier, multi-hop, nested negation, conditional fallacies.
  • Equivalence-preserving transforms: contrapositive, double negation, De Morgan, quantifier rewrite, passive voice.
  • Answer-reversing: negation flip. Surface control: passive voice (isolates logic vs. phrasing).
  • Two metrics: base accuracy and family consistency (all 5 correct).
  • Zero-shot, T=0 (o4-mini除外), Yes/No/Cannot-be-determined, regex extraction. API calls April 2026.

Results & highlights

  • o4-mini leads: 96.9% consistency vs 98.6% accuracy.
  • Reasoning effort on GPT-5.4-mini: 60.3%→85.4% (+25.1pp), accuracy slips 98.9→96.0%.
  • GPT-5.4 with reasoning stays flat at 86.6% — quantifier reasoning 92%→20%.
  • Root cause: 119 spurious "Cannot be determined" (all wrong) on 250 quantifier items under high effort.
  • CoT adds only +1.4pp for mini, +0.0pp for GPT-5.4 — not significant at α=0.05.
  • Hardest category (GPT-5.4): nested negation 50.0%; De Morgan & multi-hop hit 100%.

Strengths

  • Logically grounded taxonomy enabling operator-level attribution — real advance over paraphrase tests.
  • Honest reporting of an unflattering finding: reasoning hurts GPT-5.4 on quantifiers, with a mechanistic story.
  • Per-category and per-transformation tables make breakage loci visible.
  • Strong April-2026 frontier coverage (GPT-5.4, o4-mini, Claude Sonnet 4, Gemini 2.5 Flash).
  • Confidence intervals at n=350 reported — a practice many eval papers skip.

Limitations

  • Template-generated, Yes/No/Cannot only — open-ended and multi-step chains untested.
  • Single prompt template, zero-shot (+ one CoT ablation); no template-sensitivity analysis.
  • No inter-annotator agreement or human naturalness study — manual audit was internal QC.
  • Uneven coverage: De Morgan n=50, quantifier rewrite n=60 — wide CIs on those rows.
  • No human baseline, no intervention (self-consistency, constrained decoding, consistency-aware training).
  • Proprietary models may silently drift, threatening long-term reproducibility.
