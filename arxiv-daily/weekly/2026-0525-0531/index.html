Eight papers that recurred across days or were flagged a daily must-read — the "if you read nothing else" list. The lead recurred across two days.

Must ReadScaling Laws · Recurred 2 Days

Why Larger Models Learn More

OLMo 4M→4B controlled experiments give a mechanistic account of scaling via gradient interference — larger models retain rare tasks that smaller ones crowd out. The week's only paper flagged a daily must-read twice.

本周唯一跨两天复现并两次入选单日必读；用 OLMo 4M→4B 受控实验从梯度干扰视角机理化解释缩放优势，证明大模型能保留被高频任务挤出的稀有任务。

Must ReadQuantization · Recurred 2 Days

LFQ

Swap the final transformer block's MSE objective for cross-entropy and low-bit quantized generation jumps +13pp on AIME. Accepted at ICML 2026 — minimal change, clear payoff. The other paper to recur across two days.

Scaling Monosemanticity

Must ReadLong-Context Architecture

Language Models Need Sleep

A sleep-like consolidation mechanism compresses KV-cache into SSM fast weights via offline iterative refinement when the context window fills — decoupling extra compute from inference latency.

提出类“睡眠”整合机制，在上下文窗口满时通过多轮离线循环把 KV 缓存压缩进 SSM 快速权重，巧妙地将额外计算与推理延迟解耦。

Must ReadRL Post-Training

Credit Assignment with Resets in Language Model Reasoning

SRPO self-localizes the first erroneous reasoning step and resamples continuations, beating GRPO with no step-level supervision and 2–3× faster convergence across 10 benchmarks and LiveCodeBench.

SRPO 自定位首个错误推理步并重采样后续，在无需外部步骤监督下持续超越 GRPO，收敛快 2–3 倍，有望成为 RL 后训练新标准。

Must ReadOptimizers

MONA: Muon + Nesterov for Scalable LM Training

Augments Muon with Nesterov acceleration via gradient-difference EMA, beating both Muon and AdamW across 1B–68B MoE pretraining and reaching SOTA on BigCode — the most compelling large-scale evidence yet for Muon-family optimizers.

用梯度差分 EMA 为 Muon 引入 Nesterov 加速，在 1B–68B MoE 预训练中超越 Muon 与 AdamW，SFT 后 BigCode 达 SOTA。

Must ReadOn-Policy Distillation

ESR: Early Stopping Rollout for Distillation

Identifies the Off-policy Teacher Decay problem and proposes the elegantly simple ESR (distill on only the first few rollout tokens), outperforming full-rollout distillation across sizes, families, and tasks while saving compute.

识别在线策略蒸馏的“Off-policy Teacher Decay”现象，提出极简的 ESR——只用前几个 token 的 rollout 做蒸馏，全面超越完整 rollout 且更省算力。

Must ReadSpeculative Decoding

Draft-OPD

Identifies the training-inference mismatch bottleneck in speculative-decoding draft models; the error-position replay on-policy distillation framework achieves 5x+ lossless speedup on Qwen3 thinking models.

识别投机解码中草稿模型 SFT 的“训练–推理不匹配”瓶颈，提出错误位置回放的在线蒸馏框架，在 Qwen3 思维模型上实现 5x+ 无损加速。
