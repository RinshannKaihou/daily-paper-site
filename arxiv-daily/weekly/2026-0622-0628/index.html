CAT-Q: Ternary Quantization for LLMs

仅用 512 校准样本（~1M tokens）即可把 1.7B–235B 预训练 LLM 量化为 1.58-bit 三值模型，性能超越用 100B tokens 训练的 BitNet，训练 token 减少约 10 万倍、235B 模型 60 GPU-小时完成——把极低比特 PTQ 从"奇技"变为可落地现实。 Turns ternary (1.58-bit) post-training quantization into a deployable reality, beating QAT-trained BitNet with ~100,000x fewer tokens and quantizing a 235B model in 60 GPU-hours.

Transient Semantic Geometry in Next-Token Prediction

严格 one-hot 监督下 Transformer 仍在训练早期自发形成语义聚类几何，但该结构是短暂的，随容量/时间塌缩为神经坍塌预测的对称 ETF。 Transformers spontaneously form semantic geometry early in training, then collapse to the neural-collapse ETF — reconciling "neural collapse erases semantics" with "LLMs plainly learn semantics."

Capability Frontier: Benchmarks Miss 82%

单模型、单次基准系统性低估能力；去偏纠错率 54%，叠加单次采样后达 82%，并以 85% 成本匹配 SOTA。 Across 21 models × 16 benchmarks, debiasing cuts error 54% (82% with single-sample correction) while matching SOTA at 85% cost.

A-Evolve-Training

首个前沿规模（30B）全自主后训练系统，自主检测到内部代理指标失效并反转搜索策略。 First public autonomous post-training at frontier scale (30B); the loop detected its dev metric had become a misleading proxy and reversed its search policy.

Self-Evolving & Autonomous Agents

AutoLab

36 个超长时程任务（2544 壁钟小时）评 17 个前沿模型：决定胜负的不是一次性代码质量，而是持续迭代与时间感知。 17 frontier models on 36 ultra-long-horizon tasks; the dominant success predictor is persistence, not one-shot quality — a harness ablation swings scores by up to 0.43.

Self-Evolving & Autonomous Agents

INFUSER

把数据生成奖励从"难度"换成"对求解器有用"的影响分数 + DuGRPO；Qwen3-8B 相对提升 >20%，8B 共演化生成器超越冻结 32B。 Replaces difficulty heuristics with an optimizer-aware influence score; Qwen3-8B gains >20% on Olympiad/SuperGPQA, and an 8B co-evolving generator beats a frozen 32B.

Quantization & Inferencenote

Quantization Inflates Reasoning

低比特量化让推理模型即使保持正确率也生成明显更长的思维链（CTIR），侵蚀量化本应带来的每 token 加速。 Low-bit quantization makes reasoning models emit substantially longer CoT even when accuracy holds, eroding the very per-token speedup quantization is meant to deliver.

Reasoning & Evaluation

Co-Failure Ceiling on Routing, Voting & Mixture-of-Agents

证明任何 LLM 编排的准确率上限是 1−β（所有模型同时答错率），而非成对相关 ρ；ρ 系统性低估共失败尾部约 2.5 倍。 Proves the orchestration accuracy ceiling is 1−β (the all-models-wrong rate), not pairwise ρ — which underestimates the co-failure tail ~2.5x across 67 frontier models.
