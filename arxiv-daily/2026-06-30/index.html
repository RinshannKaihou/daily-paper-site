Emergent Misalignment

Evil Spectra: How Optimisers Amplify or Suppress Emergent Misalignment

By Jason R. Brown, Patrick Leask & Lev McKinney · Cambridge · Durham · Toronto

A ~330-run sweep shows the optimiser is the single dominant factor in Emergent Misalignment — a 7× spread, Muon 5.3% vs Lion 37.0% — while scale (270M–235B) barely matters. A near-zero-cost spectral regulariser recovers alignment for Adam & Lion. 大规模扫描证明优化器选择是涌现性失准的主导因素（7 倍差异），基于 LoRA 谱平坦度给出几乎零损失的谱正则化缓解手段。

By John Sweeney · Sideplane AI

LoRA Init Theory

Geometry-Preserving Orthonormal Initialization for Low-Rank Adaptation in RLVR

By Ruijia Zhang, Jiacheng Zhu, Hanqing Zhu & Laixi Shi · JHU · Meta · UT Austin

Proves orthonormal init (B₀=0) minimises the LoRA-to-full-FT gap — yielding LoRA-RLPO that explains and fixes the KL-breach collapse of PiSSA/MiLoRA under RLVR. 正交初始化理论证明能最小化 LoRA 与全参微调的逼近误差，统一解释并修复 PiSSA/MiLoRA 在 RLVR 中的崩溃。
