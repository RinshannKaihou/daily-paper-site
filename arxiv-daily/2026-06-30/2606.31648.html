01 / Methodology

A three-stage adaptation of Command A

  1. SFT

    Hybrid Supervised Fine-Tuning

    ~80% reasoning / 20% non-reasoning. Preamble conditioning teaches two inference modes — concise replies vs. step-by-step tool reasoning — from one weight set. Rejection-sampled traces retained only when the final answer matches ground truth.

  2. RLVR

    Reinforcement Learning, Verifiable Rewards

    RLOO over >50K NL2SQL + 4K math prompts. Prompt band 12.5–87.5% pass-rate; language-consistency penalty (−0.5 on Korean→English drift). Gains reasoning & tools, but adds verbosity.

  3. DPO

    Offline Preference Alignment

    Low learning-rate DPO with targeted conciseness pairs (direct = chosen, verbose post-RLVR = rejected) cures verbosity without eroding the gains.

DeployFP8 → 4-bit quant (~50% memory) fits the 111B model on a single 80GB H100; near-parity with FP8 on academic benchmarks.

03 / Strengths vs Limitations
  • ＋Real Cohere × LG CNS production collaboration targeting actual enterprise constraints.
  • ＋End-to-end recipe: training (SFT+RLVR+DPO) and deployment (4-bit, single-H100) with inference-time mode switching.
  • ＋Honest failure-mode analysis (cold-start, drift, verbosity) with concrete mitigations.
  • ＋Quantitatively justified choices (English-reasoning advantage, penalty working).
  • −No ablations isolating the four techniques or three stages.
  • −Proprietary data & internal eval sets limit reproducibility.
  • −Still relies on English reasoning for many Korean prompts.
  • −Minor English regressions vs Command A; Korean-enterprise focus may not transfer.
04 / Open Questions
  • What does a per-stage ablation (SFT vs +RLVR vs +DPO) show — does DPO's conciseness fix erase RLVR gains?
  • Does forcing Korean answers ever hurt accuracy, not just mismatch rate, on culturally Korean content?
  • Is the 111B scale justified for reasoning when a smaller open Qwen3 235B beats it on every math column?
  • What are the real serving numbers (latency, throughput, energy) for the 4-bit single-H100 config?
  • Does the cold-start SFT recipe generalize to other near-zero-accuracy tool domains (retrieval, API orchestration)?
