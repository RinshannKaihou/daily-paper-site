> **一句话总结：** 作者将 Transformer 的 FFN 子层替换为显式模糊集合运算（交集 A·B 与差集 A·(1−B)，后者以补码提供门控/双线性单元缺失的有界"否定"），并引入带可学习遗忘率的序列模糊量词（软存在/软比例）模块；在 125M 规模上困惑度与 GELU 持平，量词块恢复了语法缺陷，且无需字典学习即可在语义层直接读出语法授权检测器——每个单元在授权词上激活、以约 1.5 token 的半衰期把信号前传到被授权的功能词（than/by/into/nor）。

> **TL;DR:** The paper replaces a transformer FFN with explicit fuzzy-set operations (intersection A·B and bounded set-difference A·(1−B), giving a negation primitive gated/bilinear units lack), plus a small block of self-forgetting sequence quantifiers (soft existential/proportion with a per-unit learned forgetting rate). It ties GELU perplexity at 125M, recovers a grammatical deficit, and makes the FFN legible-by-construction: units read, without dictionary learning, as grammatical licensing detectors.

## 核心贡献

- **参数中性的否定能力 FFN（NC-FFN）：** 将每个隐藏单元定义为基于 sigmoid 界定 [0,1] 隶属度的显式模糊集合运算——交集 A·B 与差集 A·(1−B)，后者用补码 1−B 提供门控/双线性单元结构上缺失的有界正向"否定"（"A but not B"）。整套预算与 GELU FFN 精确匹配，不改注意力或参数量。
- **能力解耦的受控探针：** 在纯前馈堆叠的 N-bit 奇偶性任务上量化推理能力。结论是清晰的解耦——*有界的*乘法单元（NC-FFN、sigmoid-bilinear）是浅层最参数高效的推理基底（NC-FFN 单层 reach N=8 vs GELU 6 vs 原始双线性 2）；*无界*原始乘积则在浅层无用但随深度急剧爬升（2→4→7→8，对应 2^L 次数翻倍）。乘法性与有界性分别控制效率的两个轴。
- **忠实参数中性替换的代价刻画：** 在 125M/OpenWebText 上 NC-FFN 困惑度与 GELU 持平（差距从 epoch1 的 +0.11 收窄到 epoch2 的 +0.04），但存在小而持久的语法（BLiMP）缺陷（−0.019→−0.033），集中在长距离提取、NPI 授权与量词。作者将其归因于"容量分配"而非能力缺失——有界布尔单元每个参数能提供的独立单目特征更少，而语言建模正需要单目特征。
- **逻辑内容是任务塑形的动态读数：** 同一布尔块在奇偶性任务上于 grokking 时刻*结晶*（两操作逻辑单元占比从 ~0.006 跳到 ~0.475），而在语言建模中*侵蚀*为软门控——"有多少逻辑"并非架构固有属性，而是任务奖励的读数。
- **自遗忘序列量词模块（头牌结果）：** 引入一小块参数中性的因果模糊量词（软存在 = 泄漏累积最大；软比例 = 运行均值），每个单元带从粘性极限 γ₀≈0.99 初始化的可学习遗忘率。它在 epoch1 恢复 BLiMP 缺陷（0.810 vs 基线 0.807，vs NC-FFN 0.788），并在 LAMBADA 上略优（0.271 vs 0.254）；逻辑结构从只驻留 layer0 转为*保持并迁移到深层*。
- **可构造性可解释的语法授权机制：** 训练后每个量词都从粘性初始化"反学习"出短的局部记忆（存在量词中位半衰期 1.52 token，比例量词 1.32；零个近永久单元，88.5% 在两 token 内遗忘）。在语义层（L9–L11）单元无需字典学习即读作语法授权检测器——在*授权词*（more/rather→than，made/caused→by，turning/put→into，neither/either→or/nor）上激活，且半衰期恰好匹配授权到被授权词 1–2 token 的语法窗口。
- **可训练性边界的诚实刻画：** 当布尔占比上升超过 ~50% 时模型训练更久但突然发散（75% ~136k 步，100% ~16k 步）；残差写归一化与平行线性高速路只能延缓不能阻止，指向饱和乘积梯度病理而非写幅度。可解释性因此"只在一个分区上免费"，端到端可解释模型是开放问题。

## Key Contributions

- **Parameter-neutral negation-capable FFN (NC-FFN):** Each hidden unit is an explicit fuzzy-set operation on sigmoid-bounded [0,1] memberships — intersection A·B and set-difference A·(1−B), the latter using the complement 1−B to give a bounded positive negation ("A but not B") that gated/bilinear units structurally lack. Parameter budget exactly matches a GELU FFN, with no change to heads or attention.
- **Clean capability dissociation via a controlled probe:** On pure feed-forward stacks of N-bit parity, the result is a dissociation, not a single ranking — *bounded* multiplicative units (NC-FFN, sigmoid-bilinear) are the most parameter-efficient reasoning basis at shallow depth (NC-FFN reach N=8 at one layer vs GELU 6 vs raw-bilinear 2); *unbounded* raw products are useless shallow but climb steeply with depth (2→4→7→8, tracking degree-doubling 2^L). Multiplicativity and bounding govern two different axes of efficiency.
- **Honest accounting of a faithful drop-in's cost:** At 125M/OpenWebText, NC-FFN ties GELU perplexity (gap tightens from +0.11 epoch-1 to +0.04 epoch-2) but shows a small, persistent, *widening* BLiMP grammatical deficit (−0.019→−0.033), concentrated in long-distance extraction, NPI licensing, and quantifiers. The author attributes this to capacity allocation, not missing capability — a bounded Boolean unit yields fewer independent standalone features per parameter than GELU, and next-token prediction does not reward the products.
- **Logical content is task-shaped, dynamic:** The same Boolean block *crystallizes* exactly at the grokking transition on parity (fraction of two-operand units jumping from ~0.006 to ~0.475) but *erodes* toward soft gating under language-model training. "How much logic" is a legible readout of what the objective rewards, not a fixed property of the architecture.
- **Self-forgetting sequence quantifier block (headline result):** A small, parameter-neutral block of causal fuzzy quantifiers (soft existential = leaky cumulative max; soft proportion = running mean), each with a per-unit learned forgetting rate initialized at the sticky limit γ₀≈0.99. It recovers the BLiMP deficit at epoch 1 (0.810 vs baseline 0.807, vs NC-FFN's 0.788), leads on LAMBADA (0.271 vs 0.254), and — crucially — the logical structure stops eroding and *migrates into depth* instead of collapsing to layer 0.
- **Interpretable-by-construction grammatical licensing mechanism:** The decay "un-learns" its sticky initialization into a short, local memory (median existential half-life 1.52 tokens, proportion 1.32; zero near-permanent units; 88.5% forget within two tokens). At semantic layers (L9–L11), units read — without dictionary learning — as grammatical licensing detectors that fire on the *licensor* (comparatives→than, passive participles→by, motion/transform verbs→into, NPI/alternatives→or/nor) and carry membership forward on a ~1.5-token memory that matches the grammatical-licensing window.
- **Honest characterization of a trainability boundary:** As the Boolean fraction rises above ~50%, the model trains normally then diverges abruptly (75% ~136k steps, 100% pure ~16k steps); residual-write normalization and a parallel linear highway only delay divergence, implicating a saturated-product gradient pathology rather than write magnitude. Legibility is therefore "free only up to a partition," and an end-to-end-legible model is an open problem.

## Methodology

- **NC-FFN layer (Section 3):** From input x, two membership vectors A=σ(Wₐx), B=σ(W_b x) are formed and combined as h_∩ = A⊙B and h_\ = A⊙(1−B), read out through one shared down-projection W_o. For k operators per (A,B) pair the budget is (2+k)md, parameter-matched to a 2d_ff·d GELU FFN when (2+k)m = 2d_ff.
- **Hybrid partition:** Because a pure Boolean layer diverges in training, the working config uses a GELU-dominant split ρ=0.75 (3:1): h = [GELU(W_g x); h_∩; h_\], with g=2304 GELU units and m=384 operator pairs (768 Boolean hidden units). The Boolean output columns of W_o are zero-initialized so the layer *starts* as a pure GELU FFN and wires the operators in only if useful (function-preserving init). Per-block RMS norm balances the bounded Boolean block against unbounded GELU on weight-decay-fair footing.
- **Graceful degeneration property:** Sigmoid bounding lets an unused unit collapse (B→0.5) into a single-operand sigmoid gate, which a raw bilinear unit (with no non-zero constant state) cannot do — central to interpreting the depth-resolved behavior.
- **Capability probe (Section 5):** N-bit parity in pure FFN stacks (no attention) — embedding → L residual FFN blocks → linear head — sweeping FFN type, depth L∈{1..4}, width, N∈{1..12}, 5 seeds, summarized by "reach" (largest N solved, acc ≥0.75). Four FFN types isolate multiplicativity and bounding: GELU (additive), raw-bilinear (mult, unbounded), sigmoid-bilinear (mult, bounded, no complement), NC-FFN (+complement).
- **Language-modeling setup (Section 4):** 12-layer GPT-2-small-scale decoder (d=768, 12 heads, d_ff=3072, seq 2048, 125.1M params), OpenWebText, batch 16, AdamW (β=(0.9,0.95), wd 0.1), peak LR 3e-4 with 2000 warmup + cosine decay to 10% over one epoch (272,687 steps), checkpoints every 50k. Eval via lm-eval-harness: LAMBADA, BLiMP (67 subtasks), ARC-Easy, HellaSwag, WinoGrande (the last three carry little signal at this scale).
- **Inference-only probes (Section 4.4):** (i) engagement/realization — bool share of residual write, and operand statistics (Ā, B̄, A·B) measuring genuine two-operand logic; (ii) causal ablation — zeroing Boolean output columns vs an equal-sized random GELU-column control; (iii) logic-unit scout — ranking units by a selective sparse A-gate and informative B, then reading tokens included vs excluded to render the and/and-not statement.
- **Bilinear control ladder (Section 4.5):** NC-FFN, sigmoid-bilinear, raw-bilinear, and GELU anchor compared at ρ=0.75 to attribute differences to what NC-FFN changes (bounding + complement), treating Pearce et al. (2025)'s bilinear FFN as the nearest fair prior-art baseline.
- **Quantifier block (Section 9):** m_q units form M_t = σ(W_q x_t); two causal fuzzy quantifiers with per-unit learned decays γ=σ(θ_γ), λ=σ(θ_λ)∈(0,1): existential E_t = max(M_t, γ⊙E_{t−1}) and proportion P_t = (1−λ)⊙M_t + λ⊙P_{t−1}. Initialized at the non-forgetting limit (γ₀,λ₀≈0.99) so the block starts as a sticky cumulative quantifier and must learn any forgetting. Three ladder members studied: +quant (fixed γ=λ=1), +decay (learned), +decay+gate (adds a per-layer learned scalar gate β on the quantifier write).
- **Trainability sweep (Section 8):** 12L, one epoch, sweeping Boolean fraction at all layers (parameter-matched), declaring divergence when instantaneous perplexity >100 after a 15k-step grace window.

## Results & Highlights

- **Capability (Table 1–2):** At depth L=1, NC-FFN reaches parity N=8, sigmoid-bilinear 7, GELU 6, raw-bilinear <5 — and this shallow ordering is *width-robust* (flat ceiling from d_ff=16 to 256). Raw-bilinear instead climbs with depth (2→4→7→8) tracking 2^L; bounded units stay near ceiling. The complement adds a small shallow margin (NC-FFN 8 vs sigmoid-bilinear 7 at L=1).
- **LM quality (Table 3):** Perplexity tie that *tightens* (NC-FFN 19.11 vs gelu 19.00 ep1; 18.36 vs 18.32 ep2). LAMBADA read as a tie (ep1 edge +0.009 washes out by ep2). A single robust signal: BLiMP deficit that *widens* (0.788 vs 0.807 → 0.790 vs 0.823), concentrated in island/long-distance extraction (left-branch-island −0.31, coordinate-structure −0.25), NPI licensing (npi-present-1 −0.18), and quantifiers (−0.10 to −0.13). HellaSwag/WinoGrande at chance for both.
- **Legibility (Table 4–7):** Boolean block is heavily used at every layer (bool share 0.30–0.47) but genuine two-operand realization is *localized to layer 0* (B̄=0.48, A·B=0.13) and collapses (B̄→0.50) at every deeper layer — a recruitment-vs-realization gap. Redundant operand pairs are <5%; the rest retire one operand (~45%) or stay independent (~50%). Causal ablation: zeroing layer-0 Boolean costs +2.23 nats (+187 ppl) — the most load-bearing FFN component; deep blocks individually removable but collectively essential (+5.16 nats all-block, above summed single-layer penalties, i.e. distributed redundancy). Fewer than ~10 strict readable units (e.g., a function/content discriminator: A∩B keeps "the"/","/"\n", A\B isolates "Antonio"/"Nintendo").
- **Dynamic logic (Figure 2):** Layer-0 two-operand signal decays monotonically across two epochs (A·B 0.195→0.087); conversely, on parity it snaps from 0.006 before grok to 0.475 *at* the grok step (15 runs, 5 seeds). Same architecture, same probe, opposite trajectories.
- **Trainability ceiling (Table 8):** 25%/50% complete the epoch (50% reaches dev ppl 21.6); ≥75% diverge — 75% ~136k, 90% ~24k, 95% ~32k, 100% ~16k steps. Latent failure: a 75% run looked healthy (ppl ≈38) to step 123k then blew up at 136k. Write-magnitude fixes (output RMS-norm, parallel linear bypass) only delay divergence (~4×), pointing to a saturated-product gradient pathology.
- **Quantifier results (Table 9–12):** +decay+gate recovers BLiMP to 0.810 at epoch 1 (level with 0.807 baseline, well above NC-FFN's 0.788) and posts the best LAMBADA (0.271 vs 0.254); recovery is monotone in the ladder (+quant 0.795 < +decay 0.800 < +decay+gate 0.810). At epoch 2, baseline grammar pulls ahead (0.823) but +decay halves NC-FFN's now-wider deficit (0.810 vs 0.790) and posts the best LAMBADA (0.279).
- **Structure migrates into depth (Table 10, Figure 3):** Unlike NC-FFN's L0 erosion, the intersection signal A·B falls at L0 (0.059→0.037) while deep layers roughly double (L8 0.008→0.014, L9 0.011→0.018).
- **Decay un-learns the latch (Table 11, Figure 4):** From sticky γ₀≈0.99, every unit learns a short half-life — existential median 1.52 tokens, proportion 1.32; 88.5%/96.4% forget within two tokens; zero units have γ>0.97; max γ=0.963. A mid-stack lifetime hump peaks at L4–7 (~1.7 tokens).
- **Licensing detectors (Table 12, Figure 5):** Four recurring units at layer 9, each firing on the licensor and writing the licensed function word — comparison (more/rather/-er → than, "rather"→than 0.95), passive/agentive (made/caused → by, 0.72), directional (turning/put → into, 0.84), disjunction (neither/either → or/nor, 0.57). The membership fires *one token earlier* than the word it predicts; the ~1.5-token decay bridges the 1–2-token licensing gap.
- **Honest scope (Section 9.6):** Whole-network nameability is only ~2% of parameters (~4% excluding the deliberate GELU gradient highway), FFN nameability back-loaded to L11 (25.6%; membership alone 62.5%); attention nameability the mirror image (shallow positional heads only).

## Authors & Credibility

- **Mark Oskin**, Professor, School of Computer Science and Engineering, University of Washington (mhoskin@uw.edu) — sole author. June 2026 preprint.
- The work is methodologically careful: parameter-matched comparisons, a four-rung control ladder isolating the contribution of bounding vs complement, inference-only probes, causal ablation with a matched random-column control, and grok-aligned averaging across 15 runs/5 seeds for the dynamics claim. The author is unusually explicit about what the architecture does *not* buy (no LM-quality gain; a real grammar cost; legibility localized not network-wide; single-seed caveats stated plainly). The single-author, wide-claim-surface nature warrants independent replication of the quantitative headline numbers, but the qualitative dissociations (bounded-shallow vs unbounded-deep; crystallize-on-parity vs erode-on-LM) are robustly demonstrated.

## Critical Assessment

- **Novelty — High.** The defensible fingerprint — an explicit (1−B) complement inside a transformer FFN, plus a self-forgetting causal fuzzy quantifier as a sublayer — is genuinely fresh. The paper carefully locates itself against the nearest prior art (Pearce et al. 2025 bilinear FFN, Shazeer GLU variants, Ren & Leskovec KG-query embeddings, Petersen differentiable logic gates, Huang & Raza 2025 fuzzy-membership LM) and states precisely what each lacks. The "logic is task-shaped" result (crystallize vs erode) is a genuinely insightful mechanistic observation.
- **Rigor — Strong.** Parameter-matched throughout; a controlled capability probe that removes attention entirely; causal ablation with a random-column control to separate Boolean-necessity from generic co-adaptation; direction-level (not unit-level) ablation to handle redundant carriers; grok-aligned multi-run averaging. Honest about redundant-vs-causal distinction and about the gap between "independent operands" and "informative two-operand signal."
- **Reproducibility — Moderate-to-Good.** The NC-FFN layer, hybrid partition, zero-init function-preserving trick, and the quantifier recursions (E_t, P_t with per-unit γ,λ) are fully specified and implementable from the text; 125M GPT-2-scale on OpenWebText is a standard, reproducible recipe. Limitations: the quantifier results and finer LM numbers are single-seed; the parity probe absolute reach depends on optimization budget and seeds.
- **Significance — High for interpretability, modest for raw LM quality.** It does not beat GELU on perplexity and only ties/marginally-leads on downstream; the value is interpretability-first — a readable grammatical-licensing mechanism inside an otherwise ordinary transformer at no quality cost. The "how much logic is a readout of the objective" framing is conceptually important for the field.
- **Red flags / caveats:**
  - **Quantifier + LM numbers are single-seed** (acknowledged); the +decay+gate BLiMP recovery to 0.810 exactly matching the 0.807 baseline is within noise and needs a seed sweep and per-subtask attribution.
  - **The capability win is on one synthetic family (parity)**; other reasoning families may apportion the width/depth axes differently.
  - **Legibility is localized (~2–4% of parameters)**, concentrated in late layers; the directly-readable licensing detectors are a small minority (~4 recurring), and the bulk of operands remain distributed/polysemantic. This is not an "understandable network."
  - **A fully Boolean, end-to-end-legible FFN is untrainable** by the present recipe; legibility is "free only up to a partition," so the strongest interpretability goal remains open.
  - **Scale is 125M only**; whether the licensing detectors and the no-erosion property survive at 1B+ is unaddressed.
  - **Headline licensing readings are qualitative** (logit-lens over W_o columns + token firing statistics), not validated by full causal-intervention proof of necessity/sufficiency; the honest claim is "firing is faithful evidence a causally-used feature is active," not "this unit caused token X."
  - **Author benefit framing**: the architecture's negation primitive does *not* help grammatical negation (NPI licensing is among its weakest areas) — the paper states this irony plainly, which is a credibility plus but a caveat on the motivating narrative.

## Strengths

- Clean, principled FFN reformulation with explicit set-operator semantics and a defensible distinction from GLU/bilinear/logic-gate neighbors.
- Parameter-neutral swap that preserves LM quality rather than trading it away — interpretability without a perplexity tax.
- The self-forgetting quantifier is an elegant design: sticky init + learned decay yields a local, predictive operator whose half-life (~1.5 tokens) self-tunes to the grammatical-licensing window.
- Rigorous, honest probing: causal ablation with matched controls, direction-level (not unit-level) intervention, grok-aligned multi-run dynamics.
- Striking, easily-appreciated qualitative result: licensing detectors readable without dictionary learning, firing on the licensor and predicting the licensed word.
- Intellectual honesty throughout — explicit about the widening BLiMP deficit, the trainability ceiling, the ~2% network-wide legibility, and the single-seed caveats.

## Limitations

- 125M scale only; legibility, no-erosion, and quality parity unverified at larger scale.
- Quantifier + finer LM numbers are single-seed; needs seed sweeps and per-subtask BLiMP attribution.
- Capability claim rests on a single synthetic family (parity) under a fixed optimization budget.
- A fully Boolean FFN diverges in training, so the design space is partition-bounded; end-to-end-legible LM remains open.
- Perplexity only ties (does not beat) GELU; downstream gains are modest and read as ties.
- Headline licensing detectors are qualitative (~4 recurring units); the bulk of operands stay distributed/polysemantic, so the architecture supplies the combination rule but not monosemantic operands.
- The legible logic is monadic predicate logic (unary predicates + sequence quantification), explicitly not n-ary relational binding.

## Relevance

对关注机制可解释性与语言模型内部结构的研究者，本文提供了一条把 FFN 重写为显式模糊逻辑、并以自遗忘序列量词直接读出语法授权机制的清晰路径，直接回应"FFN 在计算什么、如何组合、如何授权"的核心问题——其"逻辑内容是任务塑形的动态读数"与"授权词→被授权词的局部记忆检测器"两类结果对可解释性研究有较高参考价值。

## Questions for Further Exploration

- Does NC-FFN legibility and the quantifier's no-erosion property survive at 1B+ scale, or does the licensing structure diffuse/polysemanticize like the within-token logic at layer 0?
- A seed sweep + per-subtask BLiMP attribution: does the quantifier recovery concentrate exactly in the licensing/quantifier subtasks NC-FFN lost, or is it diffuse?
- Can causal direction-level patching/ablation prove the four licensing detectors are *necessary* (not merely faithful evidence) for predicting than/by/into/nor?
- What remedy aimed at the saturated-product gradient (rather than the residual write) would push the trainable Boolean fraction past 50% toward a fully end-to-end-legible FFN?
- Where should the capability "pay off" — do bounded NC-FFN blocks measurably help symbolic/arithmetic/algorithmic reasoning benchmarks where next-token prediction does not?
- How does NC-FFN compose with sparse-autoencoder / dictionary-learning tooling — complementary (operators + monosemantic operands) or redundant?
- Can the monadic-with-sequence-quantification fragment be extended to n-ary relational binding (e.g., via tensor-product representations) without sacrificing the legibility?
