01 Methodology pipeline

STEP 01
Image
hiPSC cortical neurons, 4 genotypes, 4 organelle markers (Hoechst · TMRM · LysoTracker · Sytox). 3-channel training input.
STEP 02
Backbone
Custom CNN (chosen over ViT for spatial locality) trained MoCo-style supervised-contrastive, 4-class. GAP from f_stage5_out.
STEP 03
Theory
Generalized Welch bound → strictly-positive metric-distortion floor. Inner product = pure semantic Sij + non-removable contamination Gij.
STEP 04
Gated SAE
Overcomplete, ultra-sparse expansion drives distortion → 0. Sweep d∈{1k–8k}, λ∈{200–3200}; report FVU, L0, eRank.
STEP 05
Image-as-cell
Purified GAP vectors run through PHATE / PAGA / DPT as if single-cell state vectors — bypassing scRNA-seq dropouts.
STEP 06
GW-map
Gromov–Wasserstein OT structurally aligns PCA-reduced image vectors ↔ scVI-corrected scRNA-seq latent. No spatial-transcriptomics reference.

03 Strengths & Limitations

▣  Strengths

  • Novel bridge between mech-interpretability (SAEs / superposition) and spatial/cellular biology.
  • Reframes superposition as a geometry problem with a usable bound (Eq. 1–2), not just intuition.
  • Clever orthogonal probe — an unseen continuous cell-death rate — validates geometry recovery.
  • Removes dependence on expensive or unavailable reference spatial transcriptomics.
  • Theory + experiments + open code + deposited Zenodo dataset.
  • Scales to >100k images across multiple PD genotypes; translational for Parkinson's.

▣  Limitations

  • Demonstrated only on PD cortical neurons — generalization to other cells / diseases / tissue is unproven.
  • “Geometric recovery” is asymptotic in theory, partial in practice (finite d, FVU 0.30–0.73).
  • GW-map measured vs permutation nulls, not gold-standard spatial transcriptomics.
  • GW sensitivity to entropic regularization & cost matrix is not reported.
  • SAE sometimes lowers classification accuracy — interpretability isn't strictly free.
  • Gij assumptions & compute cost at 100k-image scale unverified / undiscussed.

核心贡献 · Key Contributions (full, bilingual)

First systematic demonstration that superposition corrupts representational metric geometry in bio-imaging AI — not just interpretability, but the inner-product / distance structure itself, especially severe in biology's high-dimensional, zero-inflated regimes.

Rigorous theory: generalized Welch bound (Eq. 1) gives a strictly-positive distortion floor; Eq. 2 decomposes every latent inner product into pure semantic Sij + non-removable contamination Gij. SAEs project to an overcomplete, ultra-sparse space driving distortion → 0.

Porting scRNA-seq methodology to images: SAE-purified GAP vectors run directly through PHATE / PAGA / DPT; SAE L0/L1 sparsity structurally mirrors scRNA-seq's zero-inflated expression.

GW-map: Gromov–Wasserstein OT aligns PCA-reduced SAE image vectors with scVI-corrected scRNA-seq latent vectors — enabling label transfer, gene-expression prediction, and functional-module reconstruction without reference spatial transcriptomics.

中文：首次系统揭示 superposition 对生物影像表征度量几何的污染；给出基于广义 Welch 界的严格几何污染理论；将 scRNA-seq 分析方法（PHATE/PAGA/DPT）迁移到图像域；提出 GW-map 在无空间转录组参考下完成跨模态对齐，无监督恢复 Presynaptic Architecture、Calcium-AIS Scaffold 等功能模块。

Critical assessment · full

Novelty — high. Transplanting gated-SAE / superposition tooling from LLMs into spatial biology, framing it as a metric-geometry problem, and adding a GW optimal-transport bridge is a genuinely creative synthesis uncommon in either literature alone.

Rigor — strong with caveats. The Welch-bound argument is clean, but “drives distortion to zero” is asymptotic; real SAEs have finite d and FVU ≈ 0.30–0.73, so recovery is partial. The cell-death probe is clever but a single continuous label.

Significance — high, contingent on validation. If GW-map faithfully reconstructs biology without spatial transcriptomics it could lower the cost barrier for spatial biology — but conclusions are correlational and ideally need orthogonal ground truth.

Red flags: sweeping claims vs single-system evidence; benchmarked vs permutation nulls while claiming to obviate the gold standard (circular); GW hyper-robustness unreported; SAE sometimes slightly reduces accuracy.
