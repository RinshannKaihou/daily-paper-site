> **一句话总结：** 本文指出当前自主科研智能体普遍把"实验失败恢复"压缩为一次自由形式的反思，导致局部试错或丢弃上下文的硬转折；为此提出 SAGE，其核心机制"多假设失败归因（MHFA）"将失败恢复重构为结构化因果诊断——先生成 3-5 个证据支撑的竞争性解释、由独立批判者评分排序，再用确定性规则把已验证的根因路由到正确干预层级（假设/设计/实现），并辅以"数据充分性钳制"防止误弃有效但微弱的结果、以两阶段落地报告机制抹除数值表中的幻觉数字；在 ARC-Bench 的 12 主题、5 领域冻结子集上，SAGE 将含指标输出从 5/12 提升到 11/12，盲评整体得分 52.0 超过 AI-Scientist-v2 的 48.2，增益集中在代码开发与执行。

> **TL;DR:** SAGE argues that the dominant "single free-form reflection" recovery loop makes autonomous research agents brittle, and replaces it with Multi-Hypothesis Failure Attribution (MHFA) — a structured causal-diagnosis pipeline that generates 3–5 evidence-grounded competing explanations, has an independent skeptical critic rank their severity, then deterministically routes the verified root cause to the correct intervention level (hypothesis / design / implementation), guarded by a data-sufficiency clamp that prevents discarding valid-but-weak results and a two-stage grounded-reporting layer that redacts hallucinated table numbers; on a frozen 12-topic/5-domain ARC-Bench subset it lifts metrics-bearing outputs from 5/12 to 11/12 and blindly outscores AI-Scientist-v2 (52.0 vs. 48.2), with gains concentrated in code development and execution.

## 核心贡献
- **重新定义失败恢复的结构性问题**：明确指出"单次自由形式反思"会把丰富的轨迹（指标、日志、设计选择）压缩成一条口头批评，使智能体陷入两类失败——反复调参的局部试错，或重置流水线、丢弃累积证据的硬转折；其本质是"跨抽象层级的结构化信用分配"难题。
- **提出 MHFA（多假设失败归因）**：将恢复操作化为"发散式生成 + 收敛式评分 + 确定性路由"三阶段。生成阶段以"资深研究员"人格提出 3–5 个类别为 Method/Design 的竞争性归因；评分阶段用独立的"怀疑论"批判者打分（避免自我背书偏差）；最后用符号规则路由，把诊断与执行解耦。
- **层级感知路由 + 两道安全护栏**：用确定性规则 π(c*, s*) 输出 Proceed / Local-Refine / Method-Refine / Design-Refine / Pivot 等动作（MaxPivots=2）；其中"数据充分性钳制（verdict clamp）"在 LLM 判定结果为 Valid 且高置信时，把会丢弃假设的 Pivot 降级为原地 Method-Refine，保护"有效但微弱"的真实阴性结果；"失败感知再生成"将失败档案作为负知识先验，避免重生近似假设。
- **两阶段落地报告机制**：用"前置白名单清单 + 后置数值消毒器（1% 容差，未验证值替换为 `---`）"约束数值表只能引用真实测量值，明确说明该方法只覆盖数字表，不验证正文中的方法/数据集/库名等"方法溯源"声明。
- **扎实的实证与诚实定位**：在 ARC-Bench 冻结的 12 主题/5 领域子集上，含指标输出从 5/12 升至 11/12；盲评整体 52.0 vs AI-Scientist-v2 48.2 vs AutoResearchClaw 24.8；3 位 ML 博士人工盲评 SAGE 5.67 vs AI-Scientist-v2 4.72（配对 Wilcoxon p=0.031）；并坦诚所有论文在严格会议评审下仅 2–3/10，明确将"方法溯源落地"列为下一个瓶颈。

## Key Contributions
- **Reframes agent reflection as structured causal attribution.** Identifies that single free-form reflection collapses a rich trajectory (metrics, logs, design choices) into one verbal critique, causing either repeated hyperparameter tuning of a fundamentally flawed design or hard pipeline resets that discard accumulated evidence — fundamentally a structural credit-assignment problem across abstraction levels.
- **Introduces Multi-Hypothesis Failure Attribution (MHFA).** Operationalizes Chamberlin's "method of multiple working hypotheses" and Platt's "strong inference" for agents: a divergent generator proposes 3–5 candidate attributions (categorized as Method or Design), an independent skeptical critic ranks their severity (criticality ∈ [0,1]) to avoid self-endorsement bias, and a deterministic symbolic rule routes the verified cause to an action — decoupling diagnosis from execution.
- **Hierarchy-aware routing with two safety guards.** The deterministic router π(c*, s*) emits Proceed / Local-Refine / Method-Refine / Design-Refine / Pivot (MaxPivots = 2). A "data-sufficiency verdict clamp" uses an independent LLM judge to detect valid-but-weak results and downgrade hypothesis-discarding Pivots to in-place refinement (confidence floor κ₀ = 0.6), preserving honest negative findings. A "failure-aware regeneration" archives a failure profile as a negative-knowledge prior to avoid near-identical hypotheses after a pivot.
- **A scoped two-stage grounded-reporting mechanism.** A proactive manifest derives a whitelist of permissible tabular data from the best experiment summary; a reactive sanitizer checks every table cell against a measured-value registry (1% tolerance) and redacts unverified numbers to `---`. The authors are explicit that this covers only numeric tables, not prose-level method-provenance claims (library/dataset/test names).
- **Honest, multi-standard empirical study.** On a frozen 12-topic/5-domain ARC-Bench subset, metrics-bearing recovery rises from 5/12 to 11/12; blind uniform artifact scores are SAGE 52.0 vs. AI-Scientist-v2 48.2 vs. AutoResearchClaw 24.8; blind human scoring by three ML PhDs gives SAGE 5.67 vs. 4.72 (paired Wilcoxon p=0.031). The paper transparently admits all papers score only 2–3/10 under a strict conference bar and names method-provenance grounding as the next bottleneck.

## Methodology
SAGE (Self-correcting, Autonomous, Grounded Experimenter) runs on the **AutoResearchClaw** harness for stages orthogonal to its contribution (scoping, literature search, experiment scaffolding, writing) and inserts a structured recovery loop at the Analysis phase. Recovery is modeled over an abstraction hierarchy **L = {l_H (hypothesis), l_D (design), l_I (implementation)}** given a terminal observation O_end (e.g., a plateau or no usable metric).

**Structured failure context C_fail = ⟨T, K⟩.** The knowledge component K holds the hypothesis, design config, result summary, and a structured code/diagnostic summary. The trajectory component T is produced by **TrajPivot**, an advisory stagnation detector: all primary metrics are direction-normalized (higher = better); from per-iteration gains it computes a marginal decay D = 1 − g_last/max(ε, g_first) and a regression slope S, plus inter-run volatility and gap-to-target. Decay ≈ 1.0 signals stalled improvement, near-zero slope with low volatility signals saturation, high volatility signals instability rather than a flawed hypothesis.

**MHFA — divergent generation.** A "senior-researcher" persona samples K ∈ [3,5] candidate attributions h_i = ⟨d_i, c_i, e_i, p_i⟩ (description, category ∈ {Method, Design}, cited evidence ⊂ C_fail, proposed fix). Generation is retried if the set lacks categorical diversity or any hypothesis fails to cite concrete evidence. Low-level zero-metric implementation faults are handled by a separate execution-repair path before structural attribution.

**MHFA — convergent scoring.** A separate "skeptical" critic (not the generator) audits logical soundness against cited evidence, rectifies the category if needed, and assigns a criticality score s_i ∈ [0,1] from a fixed rubric; the top h* = argmax s_i becomes the intervention target.

**Deterministic routing + guards.** The fixed rule π(c*, s*) follows "severity gates depth, category selects the level": a Design cause routes to the protocol level; a Method cause routes to method refinement, escalating to hypothesis-level Pivot only for verified fatal flaws. Two guards: (1) the **data-sufficiency verdict clamp** — an independent judge returns v ∈ {Valid, ExecFail} and confidence κ; if v = Valid ∧ κ ≥ 0.6 ∧ the action discards the hypothesis, it is downgraded (e.g., Pivot → Method-Refine), so real-but-weak results are refined toward an honest technical report rather than discarded; (2) **failure-aware regeneration** — on a pivot, a failure profile A_fail is archived and a distinctness gate rejects near-identical regenerated candidates up to a retry budget.

**Grounded reporting (scoped).** (1) A proactive manifest builds a whitelist of permissible metrics/conditions from the best experiment summary, injected into the drafting prompt. (2) A reactive sanitizer checks every numeric table cell against a measured-value registry R (1% relative tolerance) and replaces unverified values with a redaction sentinel `---`; a verifier also refuses drafts with no measured metrics or fabricated condition names. The authors stress this does not verify prose-level method claims.

## Results & Highlights
- **Outcome-level recovery:** metrics-bearing results on **11/12 topics vs. 5/12** for the reflection baseline. The dominant baseline pathology is non-runnable/non-measuring code (Config-only main.py, leaked prose preamble raising SyntaxError, omitted models, no experiment loop). SAGE recovers 4 method-attributable baseline failures outright (ML20, ML12, Q01, Q03) and honestly abstains on S02 rather than fabricating, whereas the baseline ships a degraded paper with unsupported claims.
- **Blind uniform artifact evaluation (Table 1, Claude Opus 4.8, 2:2:3 weighted):** SAGE **52.0** (Code Dev 67.5 / Code Exec 62.5 / Result Analysis 34.6), AI-Scientist-v2 48.2 (58.3 / 51.7 / 39.2), AutoResearchClaw 24.8. Head-to-head wins: SAGE 7 vs. AI-Scientist-v2 2. Gains concentrate in the two code dimensions; Result Analysis stays low for both (within single-judge noise).
- **Dual-standard evaluation:** under the strict main-conference bar all papers score only 2–3/10 (8 grounded SAGE drafts average 2.25/10); under the calibrated AR-Eval bar, the 8 canonical SAGE deliverables average **6.75/10** vs. **5.00/10** for the 6 baseline deliverables.
- **Blind human evaluation (3 ML PhDs, 6 ML topics):** SAGE 5.67 > AI-Scientist-v2 4.72 > AutoResearchClaw 3.00; paired Wilcoxon p = 0.031 vs. AI-Scientist-v2. Human–AR-Eval Spearman ρ = 0.61, comparable to the ρ = 0.625 agreement between two LLM judges (Opus gap +1.75, Sonnet gap +0.92). Reviewers credit SAGE with controlled, falsifiable designs and confound isolation; reservations center on limited experimental scale per single autonomous run.
- **Ablation (Table 5, AR-Eval ranges):** full SAGE 5–8; w/o independent critic 5–7; w/o divergent generation (K=1) 3–7; w/o MHFA 0–6 — confirming the multi-hypothesis structure, not the repair path alone, drives recovery.
- **Cost:** ~$10–$20 per end-to-end SAGE run in LLM API calls.
- **Notable deep-recovery cases (Table 3):** after entry-point repair unblocks ML20, MHFA re-diagnoses a seasonal-naive MASE-denominator error; on S01 it identifies bit-identical condition degeneracy — both structural causes a single Refine loop cannot reach. Pivots fire on only 2/12 topics (ML01, ML02); elsewhere the verdict clamp downgrades Pivot → Method-Refine.

## Critical Assessment
- **Novelty:** High within the autonomous-science subfield. Reframing recovery as "structural credit assignment" via multi-hypothesis attribution + deterministic hierarchy-aware routing is distinct from prior search methods (ToT, multi-agent debate, hypothesis search), which diversify answers/actions/trajectories rather than *causal explanations of an observed failure*. The verdict clamp and scoped grounded-reporting are concrete, well-motivated additions.
- **Rigor:** Solid by subfield standards: controlled comparison (same harness, backbone, budget), three evaluation lenses (uniform rubric, AR-Eval, strict conference bar), two LLM judges plus blind human review, and a clean component ablation. However, **statistical power is weak** — 12 topics, single-judge primary AR-Eval with a second judge only as a reliability check, and rank agreement is explicitly "low" on methodological soundness. Headline deltas on Result Analysis are within judge noise.
- **Reproducibility:** Promising but not guaranteed. Code is referenced (github.com/JieMaMagic/SAGE); cost and config are reported. Reproducibility hinges on the ARC-Bench subset, trajectory features, and prompt/persona definitions being released. LLM stochasticity means trajectory-level reproduction will be approximate.
- **Significance:** Plausibly significant for the autonomous-research-agent community if structured recovery generalizes beyond ARC-Bench. The paper's most valuable conceptual contribution may be the **"bottleneck shift" diagnosis**: once experiments run and measure reliably, the binding limit becomes method-provenance grounding (faithful implementation, prose-table consistency).
- **Red flags (mitigated but worth scrutiny):**
  - The benchmark is a self-curated subset of ARC-Bench; although the authors pre-registered the topic list and report all attempts (including baseline-favored reversals B07 and S02), selection/anchor bias remains a concern.
  - Absolute quality is modest (52/100 uniform; 2–3/10 conference bar) — the work improves *artifact reliability*, not publication quality.
  - A **method-provenance gap** persists in SAGE itself (e.g., naming Friedman-1 when California Housing was used), so "grounded reporting" is narrower than the title might imply.
  - LLM-as-judge for LLM-built artifacts is an acknowledged circularity; the human study (6 ML topics only) partially addresses this but is small and domain-restricted.
  - The verdict clamp and routing thresholds (κ₀ = 0.6, MaxPivots = 2) are hand-set; sensitivity analysis is limited.

## Strengths
- Targets a real, sharply articulated failure mode (lossy single-reflection) with a principled, multi-hypothesis solution grounded in classic philosophy of science (Chamberlin, Platt).
- Cleanly decouples diagnosis from action via deterministic routing, making recovery auditable rather than a free LLM call.
- The verdict clamp is a thoughtful safeguard against false hypothesis rejection on valid-but-weak results — a subtle but important integrity point.
- Exceptionally honest scoping: explicitly states what the reporting mechanism does *not* verify and names the next open problem, rather than overclaiming.

## Limitations
- Small benchmark (12 topics); statistical significance is strong only on the ML subset human study (p=0.031), and many deltas rest on LLM judges with acknowledged rank-disagreement.
- Method-provenance gap is unsolved: generated prose can still name libraries/models/datasets/tests not in the executed code, and prose–table inconsistency (writer not forced to revise prose after sanitizer redaction) caps scores under both bars.
- Gains concentrate in code development/execution; Result Analysis quality is low for both arms and within judge noise.
- All generated papers remain far below main-conference quality (2–3/10) — the contribution is reliable, measurable artifacts, not polished publications.
- Hand-tuned thresholds (κ₀, MaxPivots, severity rubric) with limited sensitivity analysis; generalization beyond ARC-Bench is untested.
- Reporting layer caught a thousands-separator parsing bug during evaluation, indicating implementation fragility in the sanitizer.

## Relevance
SAGE 直击当前 LLM 自主科研智能体"实验失败后如何正确恢复"这一关键瓶颈，其结构化多假设归因 + 层级路由 + 数据充分性钳制的组合，对构建可信、可自我纠错的科研 Agent 具有直接方法论参考价值，尤其适合关注自主科研自动化、智能体自我改进与科研产物可信度验证方向的研究者；其坦诚披露的"方法溯源落地"瓶颈也为后续工作指明了明确切入点。

## Questions for Further Exploration
- How robust is the deterministic router when multiple root causes interact or co-occur (e.g., a flawed design that also masks an implementation bug)?
- How sensitive are recovery outcomes to the hand-set thresholds (κ₀ = 0.6, MaxPivots = 2, severity rubric), and can these be learned/calibrated rather than fixed?
- Does the structured-recovery advantage transfer to non-ARC-Bench benchmarks and to wet-lab / theory-heavy research subtasks, or is it specific to code-centric ML-style experiments?
- Can the grounded-reporting mechanism be extended to verify prose-level method provenance (library/dataset/test names against executed code) without prohibitive cost, closing the named open problem?
- What is the false-redaction rate of the sanitizer on genuine multi-seed/variance-bearing results, and how should prose be co-revised when table cells are redacted to avoid internal inconsistency?
- How does MHFA's compute overhead scale with trajectory length, and does maintaining the negative-knowledge failure archive degrade over long multi-experiment campaigns?
