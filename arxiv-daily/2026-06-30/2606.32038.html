Why This Matters

Fixed counterfactual datasets can be reused across similar models and double as always-on behavioral-drift probes inside post-training pipelines.

Directly connects to RLHF oversight & mechanistic interpretability — lowering the cost of auditable LMs.

Critical Edge

The effect needs behavioral variance.

A fully-aligned M0 collapses to a constant label, making training degenerate; r=0.89 is correlational, not a causal circuit; no downstream safety benefit is yet demonstrated.

01 Methodology

Faithfulness = counterfactual simulability

An explanation is faithful if it names the input cue whose removal would change the model's answer. Four-step pipeline on base model M₀:

  1. Construct ground-truth explanations E(M₀): "the response [would/would not] change to <M(x\C)>."
  2. Explanation-train M₀ with cross-entropy on E(M₀) plus a KL behavioral regularizer (λ).
  3. Re-construct explanations E(M_reg) post hoc.
  4. Compare M_reg's predictions vs. E(M₀) (Orig) and E(M_reg) (Self). Self > Orig = coupling.
L(M) = 𝔼[−log p_M(e|q)] + λ · 𝔼[ KL( p_M0(y|x) ‖ p_M(y|x) ) ]  → λ=1: M_reg · λ=0: M_unreg

Model: Qwen3-8B (full FT) primary; replicated on Qwen3-32B & Llama-3.1-8B-Instruct, full + LoRA.

3 tasks: Hint-MMLU (sycophancy) · AITA (person framing) · Refusal (roleplay, LLM-judged).

  • Regularization is the switch: coupling appears only at λ ≳ 5×10⁻³; without it the signature reverses (Self < Orig).
  • Sharp ν ≈ 0.7 threshold: ~70% online label–self agreement needed; below ν=0.6 both Self & Orig EM collapse.
  • Cross-family works: pure Llama labels (only 53% agreement) still couple in Qwen — Self EM degrades 88%→69% as α→1.
  • Generalizes to new behaviors: mixed auxiliary training → 90.5% EM on held-in Jabberwocky, 79.8% held-out, with no explanation supervision.
03 Strengths  ⇄  Limitations
▲ Strengths
  • Memorable, crisp phenomenon with an operational test (Self > Orig).
  • Exceptional confound isolation: regularization vs. drift, provenance vs. online compatibility, memorization vs. generalization.
  • Broad external validity: 3 tasks × 2 families × 8B/32B × full/LoRA + mechanistic analysis.
  • Intellectually honest Limitations section.
▼ Limitations
  • Needs behavioral variance; fully-aligned M0 → constant label → degenerate training.
  • Emergence theory incomplete (LR-sweep counter-evidence, §D.2).
  • r=0.89 is correlational; unexplained patching asymmetry (§C.2).
  • Exact-Match is brittle; gap shrinks on freeform refusal (63%).
  • Tops out at 32B; frontier-scale untested. No downstream safety benefit shown.
04 Open Questions
  • Is the ν ≈ 0.7 threshold universal, or does it shift with scale, cue complexity, or metric strictness?
  • Does coupling survive frontier-scale multi-dimensional drift under RLHF/RLAIF?
  • Can the mechanism be made causal/steerable — intervene on explanations to shape introspected behavior?
  • Does faithful coupling yield measurable downstream gains — better refusal calibration, earlier misalignment detection?
  • How robust is it under adversarial deception / unfaithful-rationale training?
