Why this matters

"How much logic" lives in an FFN is a dynamic, task-shaped readout of what the objective rewards — not a fixed property. A legible licensing mechanism emerges with no quality tax.

→ reframes FFN interpretability for the field

Critical edge

Legibility is localized to ~2–4% of parameters; quantifier + LM numbers are single-seed, and a fully-Boolean end-to-end-legible FFN diverges in training. Legibility is "free only up to a partition."

01 / METHODThe NC-FFN, in four operators
Intersection h∩ = A ⊙ B "A and B." Sigmoid-bounded [0,1] membership.
Set-difference ← complement h\ = A ⊙ (1−B) "A but not B." The bounded negation gated/bilinear units structurally lack.
Hybrid partition (ρ=0.75) [ GELU ; h∩ ; h\ ] 3:1 GELU : Boolean. Zero-init Boolean columns → starts as pure GELU.
Self-forgetting quantifier Et = max(Mt, γ⊙Et−1) Causal soft-existential with per-unit learned decay γ; sticky init γ₀≈0.99, then un-learns to ~1.5-token memory.

Budget exactly matches a 2·dff·d GELU FFN — a parameter-neutral swap with no change to heads or attention.  Proportion quantifier Pt = (1−λ)⊙Mt + λ⊙Pt−1 runs in parallel.

03 / DETAILSetup, strengths & honest limits

Experimental scaffold

  • LM: 12-layer GPT-2-small scale, d=768, d_ff=3072, seq 2048, 125.1M params; OpenWebText; AdamW, cosine LR 3e-4, 1 epoch (272,687 steps).
  • Eval: lm-eval-harness — LAMBADA, BLiMP (67 subtasks), ARC-Easy, HellaSwag, WinoGrande.
  • Capability probe: pure feed-forward N-bit parity stacks (no attention), depth L∈{1..4}, 5 seeds, "reach" = largest N at acc≥0.75.
  • Control ladder (ρ=0.75): NC-FFN ↔ sigmoid-bilinear ↔ raw-bilinear ↔ GELU — isolates the contribution of bounding and the complement.
  • Inference probes: operand statistics (Ā, B̄, A·B), causal ablation vs matched random-column control, grok-aligned dynamics averaging.
  • Trainability sweep: Boolean fraction across all layers; divergence = instant PPL >100 past a 15k grace window.

Strengths  vs  limits

Strengths
  • Principled FFN with explicit set-operator semantics; clean distinction from GLU / bilinear / logic-gate neighbors.
  • Parameter-neutral swap that preserves LM quality — interpretability with no perplexity tax.
  • Sticky-init + learned decay self-tunes the ~1.5-token memory to the licensing window. Elegant.
  • Rigorous probing: causal ablation with matched controls, direction-level intervention, grok-aligned dynamics.
  • Striking qualitative payoff: licensing detectors readable without dictionary learning.
Limitations
  • 125M only — legibility, no-erosion, quality parity unverified at 1B+.
  • Quantifier + finer LM numbers are single-seed; the 0.810 vs 0.807 tie is within noise.
  • Capability claim rests on one synthetic family (parity) under a fixed budget.
  • A fully-Boolean FFN diverges ≥75%; design is partition-bounded. End-to-end-legible LM open.
  • Perplexity only ties GELU; downstream gains read as ties.
  • Licensing detectors are ~4 recurring units — the bulk of operands stay polysemantic. No n-ary binding.
04 / HEADLINELicensing detectors, read off in plain language

At semantic layers L9–L11, recurring units fire on the licensor and write the licensed function word — with membership igniting one token earlier than the prediction, the decay bridging the gap.

05 / CREDIBILITYRigor, scope & red flags
NoveltyHigh RigorStrong ReproducibilityModerate–Good SignificanceHigh (interp.) · modest (LM)
  • Parameter-matched throughout; attention removed entirely in the capability probe; causal ablation carries a random-column control to separate Boolean-necessity from generic co-adaptation.
  • Single-author, wide claim surface — quantitative headlines (esp. quantifier + LM) warrant independent replication; the qualitative dissociations are robustly demonstrated.
  • Headline readings are qualitative (logit-lens + firing statistics), not validated by full causal necessity/sufficiency — the honest claim is "faithful evidence," not "this unit caused token X."
  • Trainability is partition-bounded: ≥75% Boolean diverges abruptly; write-magnitude fixes only delay (~4×), implicating a saturated-product gradient pathology.
06 / OPENQuestions worth chasing
Q1

Does legibility and the no-erosion property survive at 1B+ scale, or does licensing diffuse / polysemanticize like within-token logic at layer 0?

Q2

A seed sweep + per-subtask BLiMP attribution — does recovery land exactly in the licensing/quantifier subtasks NC-FFN lost, or spread diffusely?

Q3

Can causal direction-level patching prove the four detectors are necessary, not merely faithful, for predicting than / by / into / nor?

Q4

What remedy aimed at the saturated-product gradient (not the residual write) pushes the trainable Boolean fraction past 50% toward a fully legible FFN?

Q5

Where should the capability pay off — do bounded NC-FFN blocks help symbolic / arithmetic / algorithmic benchmarks that next-token prediction does not reward?

Q6

Can the monadic + sequence-quantification fragment extend to n-ary relational binding (e.g. tensor-product representations) without sacrificing legibility?
