Transformer Geometry Observatory TGO-II: Representational Similarity Observatory

arXiv: 2607.02386

Date: 2026-07-02

https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.02386

一句话总结

本文是 Transformer Geometry Observatory 系列的第二篇(TGO-II),用 CKA、SVCCA、TwoNN 内在维度和 token 协方差四种探针,追踪 ViT-Small/16 在 ImageNet-100 上训练 100 个 epoch 的表征几何演化,发现层级专业化持续增强、内在维度先升后稳、而 token 强交互结构贯穿训练始终——从而否定"复杂度增长来自 token 解耦",并提出"流形扩张(Manifold Expansion)"与"过渡区(Transition Zone)"两个新假说。

TL;DR

TGO-II is the second paper in a multi-part "Transformer Geometry Observatory" series (following TGO-I: Spectral Geometry Observatory, arXiv:2606.19249). It probes ViT-Small/16 trained on ImageNet-100 for 100 epochs using four complementary measures — linear CKA, SVCCA, TwoNN intrinsic dimensionality, and token covariance/coupling. Three empirical regularities emerge: (1) cross-layer similarity (CKA/SVCCA) drops then stabilizes → increasing layer specialization; (2) intrinsic dimensionality rises then plateaus → representation manifold expands into more accessible degrees of freedom; (3) strong token coupling (substantial off-diagonal covariance) persists across all epochs → complexity does not arise from token decoupling. The authors reject TGO-I's "Token Diversification" hypothesis, leave the "Semantic Expansion" hypothesis open, and introduce two new hypotheses: Manifold Expansion (the strong one, backed by qualitative agreement between TwoNN-ID and TGO-I's Effective Rank) and Transition Zone (speculative — layers 4–5 mark a regime shift). Future work plans TGO-III through TGO-VI.

核心贡献

  1. 构建了系统化的表征几何观测框架 TGO-II:在 TGO-I(谱几何)基础上,首次将 CKA、SVCCA、TwoNN-ID 与 token 协方差/耦合分析组合,用于刻画 ViT 训练全程的表征演化轨迹,并形式化给出每种度量的定义(含 token 耦合比 Coupling = Σ_{i≠j}|C_ij| / Σ_{i,j}|C_ij|)。
  2. 揭示四项可重复的经验规律:
  • CKA/SVCCA 训练初期快速下降后趋于稳定,层级专业化加深;
  • 相邻层相似度在 Layer 4–5 之间出现明显低谷,Layer 6–12 保持高相似;
  • 内在维度先升后稳,且呈深度依赖:从浅层递增至 Layer 8 达峰,Layer 7–10 最高,Layer 10 后回落,CLS 略回升但低于中段峰值;
  • token 协方差始终保有大量非对角元素,从弥散结构演化为条带状结构,深层(尤其 Layer 12)演化最显著。
  1. 对 TGO-I 假说的检验与新假说:
  • 否定 TGO-I 的 "Token Diversification"(token 解耦)——证据显示 token 并未独立;
  • "Semantic Expansion" 既未证实也未证伪(未直接测量语义信息);
  • 新提出 Manifold Expansion 假说(证据最强):谱扩张由表征流形向更高内在维度扩张所驱动,无需 token 独立;
  • 新提出 Transition Zone 假说(推测性):Layer 4–5 是两阶段处理的过渡区。

Key Contributions

  • A geometry observatory framework (TGO-II): extends TGO-I's spectral analysis with four representation-level probes — linear CKA [Kornblith 2019], SVCCA [Raghu 2017], TwoNN intrinsic dimensionality, and token covariance/coupling — applied across all ViT-Small/16 layers at every training checkpoint, on a fixed 1000-image validation subset.
  • Four robust empirical findings (with depth-resolved detail): monotonic-then-plateauing drop in CKA/SVCCA; a Layer 4–5 adjacent-layer similarity dip; rising-then-stable intrinsic dimensionality peaking at Layer 8 (Layers 7–10 highest, falling off after Layer 10); and persistent strong token coupling that organizes from diffuse into stripe-like covariance patterns.
  • Hypothesis testing and two new hypotheses: formally rejects "Token Diversification," leaves "Semantic Expansion" open, and proposes Manifold Expansion (strongest, supported by qualitative agreement between TwoNN-ID and TGO-I's Effective Rank) and Transition Zone (speculative regime-shift around layers 4–5).
  • Open research program: outlines TGO-III (Semantic Geometry), TGO-IV (Token Dynamics), TGO-V (Attention Geometry), TGO-VI (Optimization Geometry), toward a unified geometric framework. Code, checkpoints, and pipelines are stated to be public.

Methodology

  • Backbone & training: ViT-Small/16 [Dosovitskiy 2021] trained on ImageNet-100 for 100 epochs on a single NVIDIA Quadro RTX 6000.
  • Probe setup: activations extracted from all Transformer blocks + final CLS via forward hooks; all measurements taken on a fixed analysis subset of 1000 validation images for consistent comparability across epochs.
  • Representation matrices: per-layer H_l ∈ R^{N×D} (CLS reps, N samples, D embedding dim); feature covariance C_feat ∝ A^T A and token covariance C_token ∝ AA^T share identical non-zero singular values (complementary views of the same geometry via SVD).
  • Four observables:
  • CKA: linear centered kernel alignment, scale-/rotation-invariant similarity of sample arrangements.
  • SVCCA: project each layer onto dominant singular subspace, then canonical correlation — measures principal-subspace alignment.
  • TwoNN-ID: from nearest-neighbor distance ratios μ_i = r_2/r_1, estimates locally accessible degrees of freedom of the manifold.
  • Token coupling ratio: Coupling = Σ_{i≠j}|C_ij| / Σ_{i,j}|C_ij|; →0 means independent tokens, larger means stronger interaction.

Results & Highlights

  • SVCCA: sharp early drop then gradual stabilization across all layers. Adjacent-layer SVCCA shows a clear reduction between Layers 4–5, after which similarity rises and stays high for the remaining blocks.
  • CKA: mirrors SVCCA — rapid early decrease then convergence; minimum adjacent-layer similarity at Layers 4–5; Layers 6–12 consistently highly similar.
  • TwoNN intrinsic dimensionality: rises rapidly then plateaus. Depth profile increases from early blocks, peaks at Layer 8, with Layers 7–10 showing the largest dimensions (highest local manifold complexity); drops substantially after Layer 10 (Layer 12 among the lowest); CLS shows a modest rise over Layer 12 but stays below mid-network peak; positional embedding has negligible intrinsic dimensionality.
  • Token covariance: highly structured patterns persist throughout training; substantial off-diagonal covariance never disappears; structure evolves from comparatively diffuse into organized stripe-like patterns; Patch Embedding shows modest change, while Layer 12 undergoes the most substantial structural evolution.
  • Synthesis: specialization (↓CKA/SVCCA) and manifold expansion (↑TwoNN-ID) co-occur without token decoupling — complexity emerges from richer transformations of a still-strongly-coupled token system.

Critical Assessment

  • Generalization is the biggest caveat: a single architecture (ViT-Small/16), a single dataset (ImageNet-100), a single training run, and only 100 epochs. No variance/error bars across seeds, no comparison to other ViT sizes, CNNs, or self-supervised regimes (MAE/DINOv2/CLIP). The three "regularities" may not be regularities at all in a broader setting.
  • "Token coupling persists" needs careful reading: self-attention structurally mixes tokens every block, so non-zero off-diagonal token covariance is partly forced by architecture, not learned. The interesting claim — that semantic/statistical coupling persists — is not separated from structural coupling; a causal intervention (e.g., ablating attention mixing) is missing.
  • All hypotheses are correlational: there are no perturbation/intervention experiments to establish causality. "Manifold Expansion" rests on a qualitative match between TwoNN-ID and TGO-I's Effective Rank, which is suggestive but not a formal relationship.
  • A probable citation error worth noting: the TwoNN estimator is attributed to Guan & Loew 2023 (arXiv:2010.13380), whereas the canonical TwoNN method is Facco et al. 2017 (Scientific Reports). Readers should verify the actual estimator implementation.
  • No link to behavior: the paper deliberately avoids connecting geometry to downstream accuracy, generalization, robustness, or calibration — so practical implications for practitioners are limited; this is pure mechanistic/empirical interpretability.
  • Thin quantitative reporting: the paper is descriptive ("increases," "decreases," "plateaus") with figures but few hard numbers or statistical tests in the text; readers must extract values from plots.

Strengths

  • 多探针组合且形式化定义清晰(CKA + SVCCA + TwoNN-ID + token 协方差/耦合比),方法学稳健、可复现。
  • 聚焦"训练动态"而非仅终态表征,弥补既有 probing 研究的空白。
  • 对 TGO-I 假说做了显式检验而非回避:用数据否定 Token Diversification,并给出新的 Manifold Expansion 假说。
  • 把 feature-space 与 token-space 协方差通过 SVD 关联起来(C_feat ∝ A^T A、C_token ∝ AA^T),为互补解读提供了理论桥梁。
  • 诚实标注推测性内容,并把语义测量留给后续 TGO-III,分寸得当。

Limitations

  • 单一架构(ViT-Small/16)、单一数据集(ImageNet-100)、单次训练、仅 100 epoch,且无多种子方差;结论普适性未经验证。
  • token 耦合的"结构性"与"语义性"未分离,缺少注意力混合消融等因果干预。
  • Manifold Expansion 仅基于 TwoNN-ID 与 Effective Rank 的定性吻合,未给出形式化关系或量化预测。
  • TwoNN 估计器的引用疑似有误(应为 Facco et al. 2017),需核对实现。
  • 未连接到下游性能/泛化/鲁棒性,工程意义有限。
  • 定量数值偏少,主要靠图示描述趋势,缺少统计检验。

Relevance

  • 对可解释性与机理研究社区有价值:为 ViT 训练中表征几何的演化提供多探针证据,可作为后续跨架构对比的基线。
  • 对高效注意力/稀疏模型设计有潜在启发:若 token 强交互需被保持,则稀疏/线性注意力在设计取舍时需兼顾耦合保持能力。
