> **一句话总结：** 本文针对时空相关数据（航拍、精准农业、医学影像）揭示随机划分的两类系统性失败——数据泄漏（相关样本跨训练/验证，虚高性能）与隐藏分层（少数子群错误被聚合指标掩盖），并提出 SASP（用冻结 DINOv2 嵌入做语义不相交+类平衡的划分）+ CDRO（课程式 DRO 稳健训练）；在 VisDrone/GWHD/BCCD 上 SASP 把近重复泄漏降低 98.5%，SASP+CDRO 比 Random+ERM 在 Test mAP 上提升（如 BCCD 85.6→89.2、GWHD-v5x 61.5→68.0、VisDrone-v8n 29.1→30.0）。

> **TL;DR:** The paper targets the i.i.d.-assumption breakdown in spatiotemporally correlated CV domains (aerial surveillance VisDrone, precision agriculture Global Wheat Head Detection GWHD, medical imaging BCCD), identifying two failure modes of random splitting: **data leakage** (correlated near-duplicate samples spanning train/val, inflating performance) and **hidden stratification** (errors on minority subpopulations obscured by aggregate metrics). It proposes **SASP** (Structure-Aware Stratified Partitioning — uses frozen DINOv2 embeddings to enforce semantic disjointness while preserving class balance) and **CDRO** (Curriculum Distributionally Robust Optimization — a curriculum relaxation of DRO that stabilizes training under SASP's stricter splits). On VisDrone, SASP **reduces near-duplicate leakage by 98.5%** (cosine-similarity > 0.95 near-duplicates, Fig. 3). Test-mAP gains (SASP+CDRO vs Random+ERM): **BCCD-v8n 85.6 → 89.2**, **GWHD-v5x 61.5 → 68.0**, **VisDrone-v8n 29.1 → 30.0**, **VisDrone-v10x 50.0 → 51.9**; on VisDrone-v10x, Random+ERM Val mAP 56.0 overestimates Test mAP 50.0 (a 6-point performance illusion) while SASP+CDRO Val 53.0 / Test 51.9 closes the val-test gap.

## 核心贡献

1. **识别并刻画两类失败**：在时空相关 CV 数据上实证 spatiotemporal leakage（近重复样本跨划分）与 hidden stratification（少数子群错误被聚合指标掩盖）作为随机划分的系统性失效模式。
2. **SASP 结构感知分层划分**：用冻结 DINOv2 嵌入把数据集划分建模为受约束优化——同时强制语义不相交与类平衡分层，无需显式子群标签。
3. **CDRO 课程式分布鲁棒优化**：课程化渐进强调困难/欠表现子群，稳定 SASP 严格划分下的训练（标准 ERM 或全对抗 DRO 在此条件下不稳定）。
4. **三领域一致验证**：航拍（VisDrone）、精准农业（GWHD）、医学影像（BCCD）上 SASP 把泄漏降 98.5%、SASP+CDRO 在 Test mAP 上持续优于 Random+ERM。

## Key Contributions

- **Identifies and empirically characterizes spatiotemporal leakage and hidden stratification** as pervasive failure modes of standard random-split evaluation in spatially correlated CV datasets.
- **SASP (Structure-Aware Stratified Partitioning)**: formulates dataset splitting as a constrained optimization using frozen DINOv2 embeddings — enforces semantic disjointness (no near-duplicate leakage) while preserving class balance (stratification), without requiring explicit subgroup labels.
- **CDRO (Curriculum Distributionally Robust Optimization)**: a curriculum-based relaxation of DRO that progressively emphasizes difficult/underperforming subgroups, stabilizing optimization under SASP's stricter splits where standard ERM and full-adversarial DRO are unstable.
- **Cross-domain validation** on aerial surveillance, precision agriculture, and medical imaging showing consistent generalization and calibration improvements.

## Methodology

**Failure modes (§1.1–1.2).** (1) *Spatiotemporal leakage*: in video/sequential/spatial data (VisDrone drone flights, GWHD nearby plots, BCCD per-patient/scanner clusters), temporally adjacent or spatially near-duplicate frames split across train/val → model memorizes background structure rather than learning robust object-centric representations, inflating val performance. (2) *Hidden stratification* (Oakden-Rayner): long-tailed subpopulations (pedestrians in adverse lighting, uncommon crop patterns, rare pathologies) are under-represented or absent in splits; aggregate metrics mask systematic minority-subgroup failures.

**SASP (§3, constrained partitioning).** Two complementary principles: (1) **Semantic disjointness** — validation folds should not contain samples near-duplicate (in semantic-embedding space) to training samples; (2) **Stratification** — each fold approximately preserves the global class distribution. SASP computes semantic structure from **frozen DINOv2 embeddings** (task-agnostic, no subgroup labels needed), clusters to recover latent domains, and formulates splitting as constrained optimization jointly enforcing disjointness + stratification. Leakage metric: max cosine similarity between each val image and the training set in DINOv2 space; > 0.95 = near-duplicate.

**CDRO (§3, curriculum DRO).** Standard DRO (IRM, Group DRO) is optimization-unstable, sensitive to group definitions, and hard to scale to large vision models / object detection. CDRO applies a *curriculum* — progressively emphasizes difficult/underperforming subgroups rather than enforcing worst-case constraints from the outset — stabilizing training under SASP-induced distributional stress.

**Evaluation progression (§4.6).** Three conditions isolate evaluation-fidelity from optimization: (i) Random split + ERM; (ii) SASP + ERM; (iii) SASP + CDRO. Reported on VisDrone (v8n, v10x), GWHD (v8n, v5x), BCCD (v8n). Setup: SGD momentum 0.937, weight decay 5e-4. GWHD reference = public winning solution [13].

## Results & Highlights

- **Leakage reduction (Q1, Fig. 3):** SASP **reduces near-duplicate leakage by 98.5%** vs random splits (DINOv2 cosine-similarity > 0.95 mass), creating genuinely out-of-distribution evaluation.
- **Latent-domain recovery (Q2, Fig. 2 t-SNE on GWHD):** SASP induces distinct semantic islands matching true geographic structure; random splitting produces confetti-like mixing that obscures domain structure.
- **Class balance preserved (Fig. 4, VisDrone):** despite strict structural constraints, SASP maintains low variance in class proportions across folds — improvements are not due to favorable class reallocation.
- **Test-mAP gains (Table 1, SASP+CDRO vs Random+ERM):**
  - **BCCD-v8n:** Val 92.4 → 88.8, **Test 85.6 → 89.2** (+3.6).
  - **GWHD-v5x:** Val 80.0 → 75.0, **Test 61.5 → 68.0** (+6.5).
  - **GWHD-v8n:** Val 82.5 → 77.0, Test 70.0 → 72.0 (+2.0).
  - **VisDrone-v8n:** Val 38.0 → 31.2, **Test 29.1 → 30.0** (+0.9).
  - **VisDrone-v10x:** Val 56.0 → 53.0, **Test 50.0 → 51.9** (+1.9); the Random+ERM Val 56.0 vs Test 50.0 gap (6-point performance illusion) is closed by SASP+CDRO (Val 53.0 / Test 51.9).
- **Component necessity (§4.x):** neither SASP-structure alone nor DRO alone is effective in isolation — latent structure alone yields pessimistic-but-underperforming models; DRO alone is unstable; the coupling (SASP+CDRO) is required.

## Critical Assessment

- **The 98.5% leakage reduction is the strongest and most reproducible claim**, and the val-vs-test gap closure (VisDrone-v10x 56.0/50.0 → 53.0/51.9) is a clean demonstration of the "performance illusion" thesis. However, the absolute Test-mAP gains from CDRO are modest on VisDrone (+0.9 to +1.9) and larger on GWHD/BCCD (+2 to +6.5) — the gain magnitude is domain-dependent and the paper does not characterize what domain properties predict larger CDRO gains.
- **The "SASP+CDRO is necessary, neither alone works" claim** is the central methodological argument, but the parsed text states it qualitatively ("neither component is effective in isolation: latent structure alone yields pessimistic but underperforming models, while DRO [alone unstable]") without a full ablation table isolating {Random+ERM, Random+DRO, SASP+ERM, SASP+CDRO} on all benchmarks — the table reports Random+ERM / SASP+ERM / SASP+CDRO (DRO-without-curriculum and Random+DRO cells are missing on several benchmarks).
- **DINOv2 embeddings as the semantic-disjointness oracle** is a strong modeling choice: if DINOv2's embedding space has its own biases (e.g., over-clusters by texture/illumination), SASP's "latent domains" inherit them. The paper validates clusters against true geographic structure on GWHD but does not stress-test the embedding choice on domains where DINOv2's geometry may mislead.
- **Scale is modest**: object detection on three mid-scale benchmarks; whether SASP+CDRO changes rankings on large-scale detection (COCO, Open Images) or segmentation is unverified — and on genuinely i.i.d. benchmarks the method may add cost without benefit.
- **CDRO's curriculum schedule** (how difficulty is measured, how the emphasis ramps) is not fully captured in the parsed sections, making the "curriculum" component harder to reproduce than the SASP partitioning.

## Strengths

- Clean framing of evaluation fidelity as a first-class concern, with a reproducible leakage metric (DINOv2 cosine-sim > 0.95).
- SASP as constrained semantic-disjointness + stratification optimization is principled and label-free (DINOv2 embeddings).
- The val-test gap closure on VisDrone-v10x is a compelling, concrete demonstration of the performance illusion.
- Cross-domain consistency (aerial/agriculture/medical) supports generality of the failure-mode diagnosis.

## Limitations

- Test-mAP gains modest on VisDrone; domain-dependent magnitude uncharacterized.
- "Neither alone works" ablation incomplete (Random+DRO, DRO-without-curriculum cells missing on several benchmarks).
- DINOv2 as semantic oracle inherits its embedding biases; not stress-tested on domains where its geometry may mislead.
- Mid-scale benchmarks only; behavior on large-scale i.i.d. detection (COCO) unverified.
- CDRO curriculum schedule (difficulty measure, ramp) under-specified in parsed text.

## Questions for Further Exploration

1. SASP+CDRO 的 Test-mAP 增益幅度（VisDrone +0.9 vs GWHD +6.5）由哪些领域属性预测？能否给出"泄漏量→预期增益"的量化关系？
2. 完整消融（Random+ERM / Random+DRO / SASP+ERM / SASP+CDRO）在所有基准上的表现如何？DRO-without-curriculum 与 CDRO 的差距是多少？
3. DINOv2 嵌入作为语义不相交的 oracle——若换用其他基础模型（SigLIP、CLIP、DINOv3）的嵌入，SASP 的 latent-domain 划分稳定性如何？
4. SASP 在大规模、接近 i.i.d. 的检测基准（COCO、Open Images）上是否会增加评估成本而无显著收益？是否存在"何时不该用 SASP"的判据？
5. CDRO 的课程调度（难度度量、强调爬坡）能否形式化为自适应规则，使其在不同基准上免手动调参？
6. 隐藏分层（少数子群）在 SASP 下被"代表性保留"——但若某子群样本极少，分层也无法保证其统计显著性，如何给出小子群的置信评估？
7. 该框架迁移到时序/LLM 数据相关（如对话会话划分、用户聚类划分）时，语义不相交的度量应如何定义？
