TL;DR: Introduces gradient-guided reward optimization (GGRO) for inference-time alignment — monitors token-level entropy to detect drift/misalignment, then injects gradient-guided nudging tokens to steer generation, outperforming Best-of-N on safety/helpfulness/reasoning benchmarks.

核心贡献

  • 提出 GGRO：一种轻量级推理时对齐方法，通过梯度引导在解码期间进行有针对性的最小干预
  • 基于 token 级熵监控检测高不确定性区域（漂移/不对齐指示器）
  • 注入由 reward model 梯度信号生成的"微调 token"来引导生成轨迹，而非重新排序样本
  • 在安全性、有用性和推理基准上持续改善推理时对齐

Key Contributions

  • GGRO: lightweight inference-time alignment via gradient-guided targeted intervention during decoding
  • Entropy-based detection of high-uncertainty tokens indicating drift or misalignment
  • Nudging tokens from reward model gradients steer generation rather than re-ranking
  • Consistent improvements across safety, helpfulness, and reasoning benchmarks
  • Increases coverage of high-quality responses and robustness to reward hacking

Methodology

GGRO monitors token-level entropy during LLM decoding to identify high-uncertainty regions. When detected, it injects "nudging tokens" generated using gradient signals from an off-the-shelf reward model. Unlike Best-of-N or rejection sampling which re-rank samples, GGRO actively steers the generation trajectory with minimal computational overhead.
