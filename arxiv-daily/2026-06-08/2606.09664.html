一句话总结： 通过在分子 VAE 潜空间上合成优化任务继续预训练表格基础模型，解决潜空间贝叶斯优化与 in-context 回归之间的分布不匹配问题。
TL;DR: Addresses the distribution mismatch between latent-space BO objectives and in-context model pretraining by continuing pretraining on synthetic molecular optimization tasks with an anchoring regularizer, achieving strong performance on held-out benchmarks.

核心贡献

  • 识别了潜空间贝叶斯优化（LSBO）与表格基础模型预训练分布之间的关键不匹配
  • 提出在分子 VAE 潜空间上定义的合成优化任务上进行继续预训练
  • 引入锚定正则化项保持原始模型的广泛回归先验，避免过度特化

Key Contributions

  • Identifies the mismatch between LSBO's latent-to-objective mapping and in-context model pretraining distributions
  • Proposes LSBO-specific continued pretraining with synthetic optimization tasks on molecular VAE latent spaces
  • Introduces an anchoring regularizer to preserve broad regression prior while adapting

Methodology

The authors extend the pretraining of tabular foundation models (like TabPFN/TabICL) with synthetic optimization tasks defined on the latent space of a molecular VAE. A regularizer anchors the model to the original checkpoint, preserving broad regression capability while adapting to LSBO-specific distributions. Evaluated on held-out molecular optimization benchmarks.

Results & Highlights

  • Strong performance on held-out molecular optimization benchmarks
  • Continued pretraining with anchoring avoids overspecialization
