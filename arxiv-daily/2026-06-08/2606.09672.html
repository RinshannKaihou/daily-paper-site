⚠️ Abstract-only summary

一句话总结： 揭示了生物医学嵌入模型在跨域语义相似度上的系统性偏差（不相关概念余弦相似度高达 0.83），提出 BODHI 对比学习方法和 OpenVINO 量化优化来修复。
TL;DR: Shows that off-the-shelf biomedical encoders assign 0.76-0.92 cosine similarity to unrelated cross-domain pairs (0% discrimination accuracy), proposes BODHI contrastive training (2.30x separation) and OpenVINO deployment (133x latency reduction).

核心贡献

  • 揭示关键问题：生物医学编码器（BioBERT, PubMedBERT, BioM-ELECTRA）对完全不相关的跨域概念给出 0.76-0.92 的余弦相似度
  • 提出两阶段对比学习方案：第一阶段提升 BIOSSES 相关性从 0.633 到 0.828，第二阶段 BODHI 通过硬负样本挖掘将域内外分离度提升至 2.30x
  • OpenVINO + AMX 优化实现 133x 延迟降低（1367ms → 10ms），达到 555 sentences/sec
  • 发现 FP16 在 AMX 硬件上优于 INT8，与常规建议相反

Key Contributions

  • Identifies systematic cross-domain similarity inflation in biomedical encoders (0.76-0.92 for unrelated pairs)
  • Two-pass contrastive training: first pass improves BIOSSES to 0.828, second pass (BODHI) achieves 2.30x separation using hard negatives from biomedical KG
  • OpenVINO deployment: 133x latency reduction, 555 sentences/sec on Intel Xeon with AMX
  • Counter-intuitive finding: FP16 beats INT8 on AMX silicon at every batch size

Methodology

The authors first demonstrate that biomedical language models systematically assign high cosine similarity to semantically unrelated cross-domain pairs. They propose a fix through contrastive learning over 72,034 pairs, followed by BODHI (hard negative mining from absent edges in a biomedical knowledge graph). For deployment, they use OpenVINO with Intel AMX instructions, comparing FP16 and INT8 quantization strategies.

Results & Highlights

  • Cross-domain discrimination: 0% accuracy with off-the-shelf encoders → fixed with BODHI
  • BIOSSES correlation: 0.633 → 0.828 (first pass) → maintained with 2.30x separation (BODHI)
  • Latency: 1367ms → 10ms (133x speedup) on Intel Xeon 6737P with AMX
  • Throughput: 555 sentences/sec
  • Quantization: FP16 outperforms INT8 on AMX hardware
