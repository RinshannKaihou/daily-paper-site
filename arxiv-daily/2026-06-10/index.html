Mechanistic Interpretability 机制可解释性
5 Papers  ·  ICA · SAE · Phase transitions · Brain alignment
Section §2

The interpretability stack is being stress-tested at its foundations. ICA is shown to recover directions without SAE training. SAEs are shown to be stable only in subspace, not in basis. And the headline finding is statistical-physical: induction-head emergence in single-layer softmax attention is a first-order phase transition with no precursor — capability monitoring is therefore provably unworkable in this regime.

Section §6

Two foundational pieces. One overturns an implicit industry assumption: dense retrieval under quantization is not corpus-size-independent. The other clarifies that the aleatoric / epistemic dichotomy is task-relative, not intrinsic — a position paper with practical reach.

Retrieval Theory★4

Quantization Limits on Dense Top-k Retrieval

First-moment proof: B-bit per-coordinate quantization needs Bd = Ω(k ln N) to realize every top-k set. For ℓ₂-normalized uniform scalar quantization, a hard precision floor B* = O(ln ln N) exists below which no dimension suffices. Overturns infinite-precision O(k) folklore.
