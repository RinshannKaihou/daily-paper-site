RL · Reasoning · Efficiency

Rethinking RL for LLM Reasoning: It’s Sparse Policy Selection, Not Capability Learning

Ömer Faruk Akgül, Rajgopal Kannan, Willie Neiswanger, Viktor Prasanna — ReasonMaxxer

本文用 oracle 干预和熵分析因果地证明 RLVR 仅在 1–3% 的高熵"决策点"token 上重排策略，而非学习新推理能力。据此提出的 ReasonMaxxer 用 LoRA 对比损失以 $4–$25 的训练成本，跨 3 个模型族、6 项数学基准匹配乃至超越耗资数千美元的全量 RL checkpoint。

RLVR doesn’t teach new reasoning — it reranks plausibilities at sparse “decision tokens” (1–3% of positions, 5–12× higher entropy). ReasonMaxxer exploits this with an RL-free entropy-gated contrastive LoRA loss, achieving RL-checkpoint performance at three orders of magnitude lower training cost across 6 math benchmarks. A finding that, if it holds up, reframes a substantial portion of current RL-for-reasoning work.

Normalized Architectures are Natively 4-Bit
