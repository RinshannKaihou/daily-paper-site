> **一句话总结：** 本文将数值线性代数中的伪谱理论（Kreiss 常数）首次系统地引入耦合梯度下降分析，为双层优化和两时间尺度随机近似中的**瞬时放大**现象提供了可计算的、实例相关的上下界，并推导出样本复杂度 $O(K(J)^2 \log(1/\delta) / (1-\gamma)^2)$ 的精确扩展律。

> **TL;DR:** Standard spectral-radius analysis misses the large transient growth that precedes asymptotic convergence in coupled gradient descent. This paper imports pseudospectral theory (Kreiss constant) into optimization, derives sharp closed-form bounds on the Kreiss constant for block-triangular Jacobians arising in bilevel/two-time-scale settings, and shows the resulting sample-complexity scaling law is tight and 2–5x tighter than IQC-based bounds.

## 核心贡献

1. **Kreiss 常数闭合界（Theorem 4）**：对块三角 Jacobian $J = \begin{bmatrix} A & 0 \\ C & D \end{bmatrix}$（$A, D$ 对称，谱半径 $\leq \gamma < 1$），证明
   $$K(J) \leq \frac{2}{1-\gamma} + \frac{\|C\|}{4(1-\gamma)} \quad (\text{弱耦合})$$
   并给出一般耦合的精确闭合形式（Eq. 10）。

2. **极小极大下界（Theorem 7）**：类 $\mathcal{C}(\gamma, c)$ 上任意估计量 $\hat{K}$ 的误差至少为 $\Omega(c/(1-\gamma)^2)$，证明上界的渐近最优性。

3. **临界耦合阈值（Theorem 10）**：当 $\|B\|\|C\| < (1-\rho(A))(1-\rho(D))$ 时，全耦合 Jacobian 谱半径严格小于 1——给出导致不稳定的精确耦合强度临界值。

4. **近自参照系统的扰动扩展（Theorem 9）**：通过 Neumann 级数框架，将块三角结果推广到 $B \neq 0$ 的情形，只要 $\varepsilon\|B_0\|K_0 < 1-\gamma$。

5. **样本复杂度扩展律（Theorem 11）**：随机耦合下降达到 $\delta$-精度需要
   $$T = O\!\left(\frac{K(J)^2}{(1-\gamma)^2}\log\frac{1}{\delta}\right)$$
   次迭代，将经典两时间尺度分析的渐近结果精化为非渐近、实例相关的量。

6. **时变 Jacobian 扩展（Appendix L，Proposition 22）**：将理论推广至非平稳训练动态。

## Key Contributions

1. **Closed-form Kreiss-constant bound (Theorem 4):** For block-triangular $J$ with symmetric $A, D$ and $\rho(A), \rho(D) \leq \gamma < 1$:
   - Weak coupling ($\|C\| \leq (1-\gamma)^2$): $K(J) \leq 2/(1-\gamma) + \|C\|/(4(1-\gamma))$
   - General coupling: explicit closed form via optimal resolvent radius $r^* = \gamma + \sqrt{(1-\gamma)^2 + \|C\|}$
   - Decoupled ($C=0$): $K(J) \leq 1$ (recovers the trivial normal case)

2. **Matching minimax lower bound (Theorem 7):** No estimator based only on $(\rho(A), \rho(D), \|C\|)$ can achieve error smaller than $\Omega(c/(1-\gamma)^2)$, confirming the bounds are essentially tight.

3. **Critical coupling threshold (Theorem 10):** Sharp necessary and sufficient conditions on coupling strength $\|B\|\|C\|$ for spectral stability; 2×2 characterization is exact.

4. **Perturbative extension (Theorem 9):** Neumann-series analysis handles nearly self-referential Jacobians ($B \neq 0$ small), degrading the Kreiss bound by a controlled factor.

5. **Sample-complexity scaling law (Theorem 11):** Non-asymptotic $O(K(J)^2 \log(1/\delta) / (1-\gamma)^2)$ bound for stochastic coupled descent — quadratic in Kreiss constant, interpretable as a scaling law for high-dimensional two-time-scale optimization.

6. **Neural Tangent Kernel connection (Theorem 12, Appendix H):** In the NTK/lazy-training regime, the effective Jacobian matches the theory directly, grounding the analysis in modern overparameterized networks.

## Methodology

### 问题建模 / Problem Formulation

联合梯度下降的线性化动力学：
$$\begin{bmatrix} x_{t+1} \\ y_{t+1} \end{bmatrix} = J \begin{bmatrix} x_t \\ y_t \end{bmatrix}, \quad J = \begin{bmatrix} A & B \\ C & D \end{bmatrix}$$
其中 $A = I - \alpha\nabla^2_{xx}F$，$D = I - \beta\nabla^2_{yy}G$，$B, C$ 编码跨参数依赖。

The linearized dynamics of simultaneous gradient descent around a fixed point $(x^*, y^*)$ yield a Jacobian $J$ whose spectral structure governs convergence. When $B=0$ (block-triangular), stability is determined by $\max(\rho(A), \rho(D))$, but **non-normality** due to coupling $C$ can cause $\|J^t\|$ to be far larger than $\rho(J)^t$ for intermediate $t$.

### 核心工具：伪谱与 Kreiss 定理 / Core Tool: Pseudospectra and Kreiss Theorem

- **$\varepsilon$-伪谱**：$\Lambda_\varepsilon(M) = \{z \in \mathbb{C} : \|(zI-M)^{-1}\| > 1/\varepsilon\}$
- **Kreiss 常数**：$K(M) = \sup_{|z|>1} (|z|-1)\|(zI-M)^{-1}\|$
- **Kreiss 矩阵定理**：$K(M) \leq \sup_t \|M^t\| \leq en \cdot K(M)$，精确控制瞬时放大的量级

The block resolvent formula for $J = \begin{bmatrix} A & 0 \\ C & D \end{bmatrix}$ is:
$$(zI-J)^{-1} = \begin{bmatrix} (zI-A)^{-1} & 0 \\ (zI-D)^{-1}C(zI-A)^{-1} & (zI-D)^{-1} \end{bmatrix}$$

Symmetry of $A, D$ implies normality, bounding diagonal-block resolvent norms at $1/(r-\gamma)$ for $|z|=r > \gamma$. The off-diagonal block adds $\|C\|/(r-\gamma)^2$. Optimizing over $r > 1$ yields the closed-form bound.

### 扰动框架 / Perturbative Framework

For $J_\varepsilon = J_0 + \varepsilon B_0$ with $J_0$ block-triangular, the resolvent identity $(zI - J_\varepsilon)^{-1} = (zI-J_0)^{-1}(I - \varepsilon B_0(zI-J_0)^{-1})^{-1}$ admits a Neumann series when $\varepsilon\|B_0\|K_0 < (1-\gamma)$, degrading $K$ by factor $(1 - \varepsilon\|B_0\|K_0/(1-\gamma))^{-1}$.

### 随机设置 / Stochastic Setting

With gradient noise $\xi_t, \zeta_t$ satisfying $\mathbb{E}\|\xi_t\|^2, \mathbb{E}\|\zeta_t\|^2 \leq \sigma^2$, the error propagation $e_t = J^t e_0 + \sum_{k=0}^{t-1} J^{t-1-k}\eta_k$ is bounded using the Kreiss theorem and Cauchy integral bound on $\|J^t\|$.

## Results & Highlights

### 定量结果 / Quantitative Results

**线性-二次实验（$p=q=50$，20 seeds）：**

| $\gamma$ | $c=\|C\|$ | $K_{\text{theory}}$ | $K_{\text{num}}$ | Ratio |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 0.90 | 0.01 | 20.03 | 12.41 ± 0.18 | 1.61 |
| 0.90 | 1.00 | 22.50 | 19.82 ± 0.41 | 1.14 |
| 0.95 | 0.10 | 40.50 | 29.36 ± 0.52 | 1.38 |
| 0.99 | 1.00 | 225.00 | 197.54 ± 4.30 | 1.14 |

理论界约 1.1–1.6 倍于实际值，与 Remark 6 中 2 倍因子的预测一致（因子来自使用 sum vs. max of diagonal-block resolvent norms）。

**伪谱 vs. IQC 界的比较：**

| $\gamma$ | $c$ | $K_{\text{PS}}$ | $K_{\text{IQC}}$ | $\sup_t\|J^t\|$ |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 0.90 | 0.10 | 22.50 | 48.72 | 14.37 |
| 0.95 | 1.00 | 90.00 | 225.2 | 38.91 |
| 0.99 | 1.00 | 450.00 | 2304 | 197.5 |

伪谱界比 IQC 界紧 **2–5 倍**，反映 $K(J)$ 的实例相关性。

**神经网络训练（2-layer MLP，GAN 设置）：**
- 峰值时间 $T_{\text{peak}} \approx \log K(J) / (-\log\gamma)$ 被定量验证（误差 < 10%）
- 瞬时放大确实先于收敛发生

### 扩展律解读 / Scaling Law Interpretation

当模型和数据维度增长时，$\gamma \to 1^-$，使得 $\|C\|/(1-\gamma) \to \infty$，从而 $K(J) = O(\|C\|/(1-\gamma)^2)$ 爆炸增长。Theorem 11 揭示这直接导致样本复杂度随维度的超线性增长——这是纯谱分析所**不可见**的现象。

## Critical Assessment

### 优势 / Strengths

1. **新颖的跨领域桥接**：将数值线性代数中成熟的伪谱工具（在 PDE 数值方法中被广泛使用）系统引入优化理论，视角新颖，潜在价值高。

2. **理论完整性**：上界、下界、临界阈值、扰动扩展、随机复杂度——六个贡献构成完整的理论体系，相互印证。

3. **可计算性**：Kreiss 常数可被高效数值计算（通过离散化 resolvent），而 IQC 方法需要求解 LMI，计算代价更高。

4. **实例依赖性**：$K(J)$ 是对具体问题实例的函数，比均匀 Lyapunov / IQC 界更精细，在实验中体现为 2–5 倍的收紧。

5. **扩展律视角**：将抽象的 Kreiss 常数解读为高维学习动态的扩展律，与现代大模型训练的关切直接挂钩。

6. **充分的实验验证**：线性-二次、IQC 对比、神经网络三类实验覆盖不同复杂度，且理论预测与实验吻合良好。

### 局限性 / Limitations

1. **对称性假设**：$A, D$ 对称（即 $F, G$ 二阶连续可微且 Hessian 对称）是核心假设，非对称情形（如动量方法）无法直接适用。

2. **上下界的 2 倍差距**：Theorem 4 上界与 Theorem 5 下界之间存在约 2 倍的未弥合差距（Remark 6 承认但未解决）。

3. **扰动条件的强限制**：Theorem 9 要求 $\varepsilon\|B_0\|K_0 < (1-\gamma)$，当 $K_0$ 大时极为限制，实际上排除了强耦合的 $B \neq 0$ 情形。

4. **局部线性化**：所有分析基于固定点附近的线性化，非线性效应（鞍点、逃逸轨迹）未被捕捉。

5. **Kreiss 定理的 $en$ 因子**：Kreiss 矩阵定理给出的上界包含维度 $n$ 的线性因子，这对高维系统可能很松（Theorem 18 指出典型实例接近 $K(J)$ 而非 $en \cdot K(J)$，但最坏情形仍是维度相关的）。

6. **作者背景**：单一作者、非标准机构，增加了证明审查的必要性。

---

## Strengths

- **Bridge between numerical linear algebra and optimization:** Pseudospectra (well-developed for PDEs and numerical methods) have been almost entirely absent from the ML optimization literature. The import is natural and technically sound.
- **Sharp, closed-form bounds:** Unlike IQC bounds (which require semidefinite programming), the Kreiss constant bound is a simple algebraic expression in $\gamma$ and $\|C\|$, immediately usable by practitioners.
- **Tight minimax lower bound:** The factor-of-2 gap aside, the bounds are essentially unimprovable from $(\rho(A), \rho(D), \|C\|)$ alone.
- **Non-asymptotic sample complexity:** Theorem 11 is non-asymptotic and instance-dependent — a genuine improvement over classical two-time-scale analyses that give asymptotic rates.
- **Good experimental alignment:** The theory-to-numerical ratio hovers between 1.1 and 1.6, suggesting the bounds are informative rather than vacuously large.

---

## Limitations

- **Symmetry requirement** on $A, D$ is essential to the resolvent norm bound; asymmetric Hessians or non-gradient-descent optimizers (e.g., Adam, momentum) are excluded.
- **Factor-of-2 gap** between upper and lower bounds is open; whether it is fundamental or an artifact of using sum vs. max of resolvent norms is unresolved.
- **Perturbative regime only** for $B \neq 0$: full coupling is handled only when $B$ is small relative to $1/K_0$, which can be very restrictive near the stability boundary.
- **Local analysis only:** Fixed-point linearization ignores large-deviation behavior, saddle points, and the nonlinear regime most relevant to GAN training in practice.
- **Worst-case $en$ factor:** The Kreiss theorem gap between $K(M)$ and $\sup_t \|M^t\|$ scales with dimension $n$ in the worst case, though experiments suggest typical behavior is much better.
- **Unverified authorship:** Solo work from an author with no verifiable prior publications; community verification of the proofs is needed.

## Questions for Further Exploration

1. **对称性假设能否放松？** 对非对称 Hessian（如 Adam 的预条件梯度）的伪谱分析是否可行？$A, D$ 非正规时 resolvent 界如何变化？

2. **因子 2 的差距：** 上界（$2/(1-\gamma)$）和下界（$1/(1-\gamma)$）的差距是否可以通过更精细的 resolvent 分析消除？使用 max 而非 sum 是否足够？

3. **非线性推广：** 局部线性化分析能否推广到非线性轨迹？特别是在 GAN 训练中，参数路径远离固定点时 Kreiss 常数如何随时间演化？

4. **与加速方法的联系：** 动量、Adam 等方法的 Jacobian 是否也具有类似的块结构？其 Kreiss 常数是否可被类似地界定？

5. **时变 Jacobian 的联合分析：** Proposition 22 使用乘积界（可能很松）。是否可以通过对轨迹的整体伪谱分析（如 input/output gain analysis）得到更紧的结果？

6. **独立证明验证：** 核心定理的证明是否已被社区独立验证？特别是 Theorem 7（minimax 下界）中 Le Cam 两点法的应用是否严格？
