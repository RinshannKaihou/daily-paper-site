Evan Duan  ·  University of Michigan

At INT6, 37–49 % of interpretable SAE features are silently destroyed — yet perplexity improves. A fragility predictor built from four full-precision statistics achieves AUC 0.92–0.97, making survival pre-screenable before any deployment.
