Recover-LoRA · AMD · 2026-06-05

10k synthetic samples recover 80–95% of 2-bit quantization accuracy loss — no labeled data required

Selectively push MLP gate and up projections to INT2, then attach LoRA adapters trained via logit distillation on synthetic data alone — achieving 7.5–23.3% throughput gains while nearly closing the accuracy gap on Qwen3-4B.

Methodology

5-Step Deployment Pipeline

1
Selective quantization: Gate + up projection layers → INT2 (group size 32), rest remains BF16
2
Synthetic data generation: Teacher generates first 3–5 tokens deterministically, then random sampling for diversity — 10k sequences total
3
Recover-LoRA: LoRA adapters (rank=32, α=64) on quantized gate/up layers, trained via KL divergence against teacher logits
4
INT4 remaining layers: Apply AWQ to non-2-bit layers → complete W4/W2-GateUp configuration
5
Deploy: No labeled data, no full-model retraining, single-GPU execution

Strengths & Limitations

What Works

  • Label-free: synthetic data matches curated OpenHermes on 5/9 benchmarks
  • Single GPU, 10k samples — far below the 90–120k required for random corruption recovery
  • OOD generalization (LAMBADA, TruthfulQA) matches in-distribution — distillation aligns general capability
  • 3-bit variant shows >100% recovery on some tasks (regularization bonus)

Failure Modes

Critical edge

Validated on Qwen3-4B only — no evidence of generalization to other architectures or sizes. Math reasoning (TinyGSM8k) recovers only 48–55%. No comparison against AQLM or QuIP# at 2-bit. AMD's internal LIFE throughput tool is not independently reproducible.

Open Questions

Further Exploration

  1. How does W4/W2-GateUp + Recover-LoRA compare to AQLM or QuIP# on the accuracy-throughput Pareto frontier?
  2. Can combining KV-cache quantization with W4/W2-GateUp close the long-context (32k) throughput gap?
  3. Is TinyGSM8k's weak recovery a data problem (need math-specific synthetic data) or a structural one?
  4. Can adapters trained on Qwen3-4B transfer partially to Qwen3-14B — same family, scaled up?
  5. Does iterating Recover-LoRA (distill→quantize→distill again) yield diminishing returns or compounding gains?
