> **一句话总结：** 通过将每个突触的巩固建模为 Doob h-变换的势垒条件扩散，论文证明模拟神经形态硬件（BrainScaleS-2）的内在器件噪声反而会以倒 U 型方式提升序列任务的记忆保持——在 Split-MNIST 上较零噪声提升 10.9 个百分点，并在真实芯片上比未条件化对照保留先验任务高 15.6 个百分点。

> **TL;DR:** By casting per-synapse consolidation as a Doob h-transform barrier-conditioned diffusion, the author shows that intrinsic analog device noise non-monotonically (inverted-U) improves sequential-task retention: on Split-MNIST (8 seeds) retention lifts 10.9 pts (57.4% → 68.3%) at an interior optimum σ*=0.02 (Wilcoxon p=0.004), beating the matched OU/EWC/naive anchors and tying MESU, while matched anchored-drift controls are monotone in noise; on real BrainScaleS-2 silicon the conditioned rule retains a prior task 15.6 pts better than the matched control (single seed).

## 核心贡献

- **Doob h-变换的突触规则（新框架）**：将每个突触的巩固过程形式化为以"权重永不越过围绕巩固值 µ 的记忆临界势垒"为条件的扩散。由 Doob h-变换得到一个额外的恢复漂移项 σ²∂w log h(w)，它指向安全区域、在势垒处发散，且**强度随噪声方差 σ² 放大**——即器件噪声本身为记忆"供能"。
- **可证伪的硬件预测（倒 U 型，承担核心论证）**：内在噪声对记忆保持的影响是非单调的倒 U 型，存在内部最优点 σ*。这是 OU Adaptation / MESU / EWC 等纯锚定漂移方法无法产生的特征，作者在实验前作为 go/no-go 门槛预注册并通过。
- **机制隔离的消融**：将条件化强度 κ 从 1 降到 0（恰好退化为 OU 对照），倒 U 效应从 13.0 pts 提升降至 0.0 pts；最优点随势垒尺度 b0 移动，证明效应来自条件化而非一般噪声。
- **器件保真的鲁棒性**：倒 U 在 BrainScaleS-2 噪声模型（有色 AR(1)、乘性、固定模式、6-bit 量化）下存活，并在第二个任务流（持续 Yin-Yang）上重现。
- **真实硅片测量与片上演示**：在真实 BrainScaleS-2 芯片上测量了内在噪声（加性、试次间独立，CV 从 12.0% 降至 1.6%，num_sends 以 N^-0.47 平均化），并将规则运行在芯片上（训练在环），以芯片自身噪声保留先验任务比对照高 15.6 pts。
- **诚实的归属**：明确放弃对锚定漂移 −s(w−µ) 的新颖性声明，将其识别为 OUA / MESU / EWC 的小噪声极限重推导；只保留 (a) Doob 势垒条件化框架 + (b) 倒 U 预测的**合取**为新贡献。

## Key Contributions

- **A synaptic Doob h-transform (novel framing).** Per-synapse consolidation is derived as the ground-state h-transform of the iso-loss interval, with the barrier half-width read off the Fisher metric; the resulting σ²∂w log h steering is a noise-amplified, barrier-divergent restoring force. Every h-transform use the author located is generative modeling, Schrödinger bridges, or transition-path sampling — none synaptic.
- **A pre-registered falsifier that passes (load-bearing).** On Split-MNIST (8 seeds) the rule yields a retention inverted-U: 57.4% → 68.3% at σ*=0.02 (+10.9 pts, paired Wilcoxon p=0.004 vs. zero-noise and vs. high-noise ends), while matched OU/EWC/MESU/naive anchors are monotone-decreasing in noise (GATE F: GO).
- **Mechanism isolation.** Sweeping steering strength κ:1→0 flattens the curve (lift 13.0 pts → 0.0 pts); the optimum tracks the barrier scale b0. The effect is the conditioning, not generic noise.
- **Device-faithful robustness and a second modality.** The inverted-U survives a BSS-2 noise emulation (colored + multiplicative + fixed-pattern + 6-bit, lift 11.0 pts at σ*=0.005) and reproduces on continual Yin-Yang (lift 7.4 pts at σ*=0.035).
- **Real-silicon measurement and on-chip demonstration.** Intrinsic noise is measured on real BrainScaleS-2 (additive, 94.6% additive; CV 12.0%→1.6%; num_sends averages as N^-0.47), and the rule is run on the chip with the analog MAC in the training loop, retaining a prior task 69.6% vs. 54.0% for the unconditioned control (+15.6 pts, single seed, one operating point).
- **Intellectual honesty on novelty.** The anchored drift −s(w−µ) is surrendered as a re-derivation of OUA/MESU/EWC; novelty is claimed only for the conjunction of the Doob barrier-conditioning and the inverted-U prediction.

## Methodology

The setup is a continual-learning problem: a network learns a sequence of tasks, and after task t an anchor µ=θ_t and a per-synapse importance s_i (online-EWC running sum of the model-sampled diagonal Fisher) are stored. During a later task each weight follows an Euler–Maruyama SDE (Eq. 1):

dw_i = [ −∂L_task (learning)  −  s_i(w_i−µ_i) (anchored drift, surrendered)  +  σ_i² ∂w log h_i(w) (Doob steering, the paper's contribution) ] dt + σ_i dW_i (intrinsic noise).

The barrier-conditioned Doob steering conditions each weight to remain in the memory-critical interval (µ_i−b_i, µ_i+b_i). The ground-state h-transform of Brownian motion killed at the interval ends gives h_i(w) = cos(π(w−µ_i)/(2b_i)), so ∂w log h_i = −π/(2b_i) tan(π(w_i−µ_i)/(2b_i)) — a restoring force that diverges at the barrier and is multiplied by σ_i² in the dynamics: the intrinsic noise powers the confinement. The barrier half-width is the Fisher-metric iso-loss radius b_i = b0/√(1 + s_i/median(s)), so important (high-Fisher) synapses get a tight barrier; the same global noise is thereby steered per synapse by memory geometry. A finite-bandwidth cap limits the per-step Doob move to a fraction of b_i (a physical analog restoring force that also stabilizes the singular drift discretization).

Matched controls: OU is Eq. 1 with the Doob term deleted (the κ=0 ablation); EWC is a static Fisher anchor; MESU is the variance-scaled Bayesian anchor of Bonnet et al. 2025; naive is plain SGD. All methods receive the identical injected noise at matched σ. Testbeds: Split-MNIST (domain-incremental, five binary tasks on one shared 2-way head, MLP 784–100–100–2) and continual Yin-Yang (five rotations on a shared 3-way head). The hardware-faithful BSS-2 noise model adds temporal color (AR(1)), a multiplicative signal-dependent component, a static fixed-pattern offset, and 6-bit quantization. Two noise-injection realizations are tested: noise on the weights (§4.1–4.3) and noise in the forward pass / analog MAC (§4.7), the latter being the hardware-relevant case. On-chip training (§4.8) runs the analog-MAC forward pass on real BrainScaleS-2 silicon via hxtorch, so the chip's intrinsic noise perturbs the gradient.

## Results & Highlights

- **GATE F inverted-U (Split-MNIST, 8 seeds, primary falsifier).** Retention rises from 57.4% (zero noise) to 68.3% at an interior optimum σ*=0.02, a +10.9-point lift, then falls; both paired Wilcoxon tests give p=0.004 (all 8 seeds agree in direction). Matched OU, EWC, MESU and naive anchors are monotone-decreasing in noise (fraction of decreasing steps ≥0.78), peaking at zero noise.
- **Matched-budget rehearsal-free baselines (Split-MNIST, 8 seeds).** At its optimum the Doob rule (68.3%) is the strongest rehearsal-free method: it matches MESU (65.2%; Doob−MESU = +3.1 pts, Wilcoxon p=0.25, n.s.) and significantly beats OU (57.4%), EWC (59.1%), and naive (57.4%), all p≤0.008. Plain reservoir replay, which stores 250 raw exemplars, reaches 83.0% (+14.6 pts over Doob) but is a memory-based method in a different budget class and exhibits none of the noise-consolidation mechanism. The Benna–Fusi cascade baseline did not learn in this configuration (50.6%, chance across all seeds) and is flagged as uninformative rather than counted as beaten.
- **Mechanism isolation (κ sweep).** Interpolating the steering strength κ from 0 (unconditioned OU) to 1 (full h-transform): the retention lift grows from 0.0 pts (κ=0) to 13.0 pts (κ=1); the inverted-U test fails at κ=0. Varying the barrier scale b0 moves the optimum σ*, confirming the optimum is set by the conditioning geometry.
- **Device-faithful BSS-2 noise emulation (E2).** Replacing the white diffusion with the colored+multiplicative+fixed-pattern+6-bit BSS-2 model, the inverted-U survives: lift 11.0 pts at σ*=0.005. After calibrating the emulator to the measured real-silicon noise (E5), the lift is 13.2 pts at σ*=0.02.
- **Forward-noise (hardware-faithful) realization (E6).** With noise in the forward pass, Doob is an inverted-U (lift 12.9 pts at σ*=0.6, Wilcoxon p=0.004, 8 seeds) while the matched OU control is flat (0.0 pts). The optimum's noise level is tunable via the Doob-steering coupling down to σ*=0.05, into the device-reachable band.
- **Second modality (continual Yin-Yang).** The inverted-U reproduces with lift 7.4 pts at σ*=0.035 (OU control flat); the optimum shifts with task similarity/interference.
- **Real-silicon on-chip demonstration (E7, single seed).** With the chip in the training loop on real BrainScaleS-2 silicon, the barrier-conditioned rule retains the prior continual-Yin-Yang task at 69.6% vs. 54.0% for the unconditioned control (+15.6 pts retention), at the cost of some task-2 plasticity (46.6% vs. 64.0%); two-task average accuracy is matched (58.1% vs. 59.0%) — a stability-plasticity shift, not a net-accuracy win.
- **On-silicon intrinsic-noise measurement (E5).** Real BSS-2 noise is additive (94.6% additive, 5.4% multiplicative; trial-to-trial std 1.24 units over an 8.2× signal range), trial-to-trial independent at the update timescale, with CV from 12.0% down to 1.6% as signal grows; num_sends averages the relative noise as ≈N^-0.47 (close to 1/√N), a genuine on-chip effective-noise knob.
- **Energy model.** Generating the diffusion noise costs a digital accelerator a modelled 23% of each consolidation step (a constant-sensitive figure ranging 4%–41% as per-op energies vary); on analog silicon that generation is the device's own physics and is free, though the barrier-steering atan still costs digital energy on either substrate.

## Authors & Credibility

Single-author paper (Gunner Levi Howe, unaffiliated / independent, July 2026). Credibility rests on a pre-registered falsification design: the operating point, noise grid, seed counts, the inverted-U statistical test, and kill conditions K1–K3 were committed in PLAN.md before E0–E4 were run (git-verifiable). Every reported number is a machine-generated macro regenerated byte-identically by verify_regen.py, and all code, PLAN.md, committed results JSON, and figure generators are released with a DOI at submission. The author is unusually explicit about what is and is not novel — surrendering the anchored drift as a re-derivation of OUA/MESU/EWC and repeatedly marking the on-silicon result as a single-seed proof of concept rather than a tuned study. The paper was posted on arXiv (cs.LG) on 8 July 2026.

## Critical Assessment

- **Novelty:** Genuinely novel and well-scoped. The author's own "moat" argument — that every Doob h-transform use they located (Doob's Lagrangian transition-path sampling [Du et al. 2025], h-edit diffusion editing [Nguyen et al. 2025], reflected Schrödinger bridges [Deng et al. 2024], diffusion-bridge simulation [Heng et al. 2021]) is generative modeling, none synaptic — is a credible literature delimitation, and the inverted-U signature is a sharp, falsifiable prediction that the closest neighbors structurally cannot produce: ANV [Xie et al. 2021] injects artificial (not intrinsic) variability with a monotone benefit, and Caston et al. 2022's stochastic-resonance optimum is for single-task storage, not cross-task retention. The honesty in surrendering the −s(w−µ) drift as a re-derivation of OUA/MESU/EWC is a real strength rather than a hedge.
- **Rigor:** The statistical design is above average for the venue. Pre-registration of GATE F with an explicit one-sided paired Wilcoxon criterion, 8-seed agreement, and a "no control may be inverted-U" conjunctive gate is exactly the right way to test a non-monotone claim. Mechanism isolation (κ:1→0 flattening the lift from 13.0 to 0.0 pts; optimum tracking b0) is the correct ablation and is decisive.
- **Weakness in baselines (paper-specific):** The Benna–Fusi cascade synapse — a principled incumbent the paper explicitly compares against — "did not learn in this configuration" (50.6%, chance across all seeds). The author flags rather than buries this, but it means a meaningful continual-learning baseline is missing from the comparison of record. The headline "strongest rehearsal-free method" claim is therefore really a comparison against OU/EWC/MESU/naive on a small MLP, not against the strongest known rehearsal-free consolidation families (e.g., more modern functional-regularization or parameter-space methods are not tried).
- **Scale and scope (data quirk):** All simulation results are on small single-head MLP testbeds — Split-MNIST with a 784–100–100–2 net and a 3-way continual Yin-Yang head. There is no convolutional or transformer-scale evaluation, and the diagonal-Fisher barrier is acknowledged to be heavy-tailed (the importance-clamp load-bearing fix in §4.7, without which both methods collapse to 0.494 chance, is a fragility signal the author honestly reports).
- **On-silicon claim is thin:** The +15.6 pts on-chip result is a single seed at one operating point (79 min of chip time), explicitly stated to trade task-2 plasticity for retention (so two-task average accuracy is essentially matched at 58.1% vs. 59.0% — it is a stability-plasticity shift, not a net-accuracy win). Energy is modelled, not measured; the 23% noise-generation figure swings 4%–41% with assumed per-op constants.
- **Reproducibility:** Strong for E0–E4 (pre-registered, code + PLAN.md + JSON + macro-verified numbers released). E5–E7 are post-access additions (BrainScaleS-2 access obtained mid-project), honestly dated in PLAN.md's deviations log rather than retroactively pre-registered; they require EBRAINS chip access to reproduce.
- **Significance:** Conceptually significant regardless of scale — reframing intrinsic analog noise as a consolidation resource that "gets cheaper as devices get noisier" is a genuinely contrarian hardware-aligned idea. Practical impact is currently limited by the small testbeds and single-seed silicon demonstration.
- **Red flags:** None beyond the scope limitations the author self-discloses. No cherry-picked benchmark selection (only two testbeds, both standard); the down-slope of the inverted-U is reported, not hidden; replay beating the rule by 14.6 pts is stated openly.

## Strengths

- Sharp, falsifiable, pre-registered prediction (inverted-U) tested with the correct paired statistical machinery and a conjunctive gate that no matched control may satisfy.
- Decisive mechanism isolation via the κ:1→0 ablation and the barrier-scale optimum-tracking experiment.
- Intellectual honesty is exemplary: novelty is precisely delimited, the drift is surrendered as a known limit, the Benna–Fusi failure is flagged, the single-seed silicon result is scoped, and the down-slope of the inverted-U is reported.
- Hardware-aligned: actually measures intrinsic noise on real BrainScaleS-2 silicon and runs the rule on-chip with the analog MAC in the training loop, not just in simulation.
- Device-reachability argument: the optimum is shown to be tunable via the Doob-steering coupling into the few-percent-CV band the measured chip occupies.

## Limitations

- Small single-head MLP testbeds (Split-MNIST 784–100–100–2; 3-way Yin-Yang); no conv/transformer-scale or multi-head evaluation.
- Diagonal-Fisher barrier is heavy-tailed; the importance clamp that prevents weight blow-up is load-bearing (without it both Doob and OU collapse to chance), a fragility not fully analyzed.
- Ground-state (infinite-horizon) h-transform rather than a finite-horizon survival function; the finite-force cap removes the σ²-amplification of the steering on 8.8% of Doob steps at the operating point (the tight-barrier tail).
- On-silicon demonstration is a single-seed, single-operating-point proof of concept that trades plasticity for retention (net two-task accuracy matched); energy is modelled, not measured.
- Device-noise distribution is tested only for additivity and a single trial-to-trial independence check — no full autocorrelation spectrum, no normality test.

## Relevance

与持续学习/灾难性遗忘、神经形态计算（BrainScaleS-2）以及"噪声作为资源"等研究方向高度相关：它给出了一种将模拟器件固有噪声从"精度税"转化为可被势垒条件化驾驭的"巩固红利"的新框架，对在能效受限的模拟硬件上部署终身学习模型具有直接启发。

## Questions for Further Exploration

- Does the inverted-U survive at convolutional/transformer scale and on harder continual-learning benchmarks (e.g., Split-CIFAR-100, ContinualWorld), or is it an artifact of the small single-head MLP regime?
- Can the heavy-tailed diagonal-Fisher fragility (the load-bearing importance clamp) be removed by a more robust barrier geometry, e.g., a Fisher-smoothed or functional-regularization barrier?
- A multi-seed on-silicon noise sweep tracing the full inverted-U on BrainScaleS-2 with measured joules — does the device-reachable band reproduce the simulation optimum, and is the modelled 23% energy saving realized?
- How does the rule interact with stronger modern rehearsal-free baselines (e.g., parameter-space functional regularization, DER-style logit replay without exemplars) that the paper does not test?
- Does the finite-horizon survival-function h-transform (vs. the ground-state approximation) change the optimum or the shape of the inverted-U, especially on long task streams?
- Can the additivity-only noise characterization be extended to a full autocorrelation/distribution test, and does colored (AR(1)) device noise beyond the scanned ρ band break the mechanism?
