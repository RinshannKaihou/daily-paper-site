Critical Edge
Smaller models (7B) retain only ~83% of FP16 accuracy; activation outlier suppression is an indirect by-product of weight-domain optimization.

Methodology — Three Coupled Stages

1
E2M-ATQ — Euclidean-to-Manifold Asymmetric Ternary Quantizer
Iterative Euclidean warm-start finds a stable ternary pattern T; then manifold relocation optimizes shift μ and scale α on the calibration activation metric via a closed-form 2×2 linear system per row.
2
KOTMS — Kronecker Orthogonal Tri-Modal Shaping
A Kronecker-structured rotation (R = R₁ ⊗ R₂, Cayley-parameterized for orthogonality) reshapes weight distributions into ternary-friendly tri-modal clusters. The same rotation statistically disperses activation outliers.
3
ILA-AMP — Inter-Layer Aware Activation Mixed Precision
Models adjacent-layer second-order interaction costs; allocates per-layer activation bit-widths via dynamic programming under a global budget, preventing cascading degradation from weak layers.

Strengths

  • Principled co-design: each module has clear mathematical motivation and composes naturally
  • State-of-the-art under the extremely challenging W1.58A4 regime across 7 model variants
  • Real efficiency gains: measured speedup & memory savings on llama.cpp, not theoretical bit-count claims
  • Thorough evaluation covering perplexity, zero-shot, instruction-tuned, and reasoning benchmarks

Limitations

  • Uneven activation gains: weight-domain shaping indirectly benefits activations; a joint objective could be more effective
  • Smaller models suffer: LLaMA-2-7B retains only ~83% of FP16 at W1.58A4
  • Calibration sensitivity: only 128 WikiText2 samples; robustness to data choice not deeply explored
  • No QAT comparison: accuracy gap vs BitNet v2 (QAT-based W1.58A4) remains unclear

Questions for Further Exploration

  1. Can ILA-AMP's interaction model extend beyond adjacent layers to capture longer-range cross-layer coupling?
  2. How does TWLA perform on non-Transformer architectures (Mamba, RWKV) with different activation distributions?
  3. What is the accuracy gap between TWLA (PTQ) and BitNet v2 (QAT) at the same W1.58A4 setting?
  4. Could KOTMS shaping loss be augmented with a direct activation-domain term for more uniform outlier suppression?
  5. How does TWLA scale beyond 70B parameters (Llama-3-405B, DeepSeek-R1-671B)?
