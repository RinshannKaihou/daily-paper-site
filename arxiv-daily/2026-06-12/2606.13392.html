Block-wise sparse attention matches full GQA quality at 109B scale — cutting 1M-context compute by 28.4×

MiniMax Sparse Attention (MSA) · X. Lai, W. Xu, Y. Yang & 8 others · sparse-attention long-context GPU-kernels LLM-architecture efficient-inference
Key Metric
28.4×
FLOP reduction at 1M context. 14.2× prefill & 7.6× decode wall-clock speedup on H800.
Why This Matters
Solves ultra-long-context LLM inference bottleneck with just two extra projection matrices and near-lossless checkpoint conversion from pretrained GQA.
Critical Edge
Only 0.2% of context attended at 1M tokens — multi-hop reasoning over scattered information may suffer. Wall-clock gain is ~50% of theoretical FLOP reduction.

⚙ Methodology

Index Branch

One index-query head per GQA group + one shared index-key head. Max-pools token scores to block level (Bk=128), selects Top-16 blocks per group independently. Input is gradient-detached from backbone.

Main Branch

Standard scaled dot-product attention restricted to the ~2048 causally-visible tokens in selected blocks. Fixed per-query cost O(k·Bk) regardless of sequence length.

Training Recipe

KL alignment loss confined to index projections only (gradient detach). ~40B-token warmup on full attention before switching to sparse. Local block always included for stability.

GPU Kernels

Exp-free TopK via register min-heaps (5.1× faster than torch.topk). KV-outer iteration with pre-scheduled tile chunking. Two-phase forward with log-sum-exp combine.

+ Strengths

  • Elegant minimalism — only two additional projection matrices, no value head at inference
  • Comprehensive ablations at both 10B pilot and 109B full scale
  • Co-designed kernels deliver real speedups, not just theoretical reductions
  • Per-GQA-group selection captures head diversity naturally
  • Two adoption paths: from-scratch (MSA-PT) and checkpoint conversion (MSA-CPT)
  • Open-source: inference kernel + MiniMax-M3 model on HuggingFace

– Limitations

  • Wall-clock 14.2× is only 50% of theoretical 28.4× FLOP reduction — ~50% sparse-path overhead
  • MSA-CPT shows benchmark-dependent degradation (code, image, video) vs full attention
  • Extremely sparse budget (2,048 / 1M = 0.2%) — long-context reasoning beyond retrieval unclear
  • Only validated on one model family (109B MoE); dense-model transfer unknown
  • No head-to-head comparison with NSA, DSA, or MoBA under controlled settings
  • KV cache compression interaction (quantization, pruning) not addressed
