Geometric Factual Recall in Transformers

Shauli Ravfogel, Gilad Yehudai, Joan Bruna, Alberto Bietti

Proves that a Transformer needs only O(R log N) embedding dimensions to memorize N subjects across R relations—subject embeddings encode facts as linear superpositions, while the MLP acts as a ReLU-gated relation selector, not a key-value memory. CoT collapses k-hop reasoning to single-hop complexity. Zero-shot transfer to unseen bijections confirms the MLP learns a generic mechanism.

证明 Transformer 仅需 O(R log N) 维嵌入即可实现事实记忆：主体嵌入线性叠加属性向量，MLP 为关系条件 ReLU 门控选择器——从根本上改写知识存储理论，CoT 将 k 跳推理降为单跳复杂度。

Entropy Polarity in Reinforcement Fine-Tuning (PAPO)

Jiazheng Zhang, Ziche Fu et al.

Defines entropy polarity P = A·T at the token level, revealing a structural asymmetry in RLVR training that drives entropy collapse. PAPO adaptively reweights polarity branches, surpassing DAPO by +3.3% AIME24 at half the training budget.

熵极性 P=A·T 揭示 RLVR 熵收缩的结构性不对称；PAPO 超越 DAPO +3.3% AIME24。

Optimizer Theory

Muon is Not That Special: Random or Inverted Spectra Work Just as Well

Zakhar Shumaylov, Nathaël Da Costa, Peter Zaika et al.

Kaon replaces Muon's gradient singular values with chaotic random noise yet matches performance—Muon's success stems from gradient alignment and descent potential, not spectral geometry. A paradigm shift for optimizer design.

随机奇异值的 Kaon 匹敌 Muon；Muon 的成功源于梯度对齐而非谱几何——颠覆优化器设计认知。
