Why this matters

First PAC-Bayes generalization certificate for quantum policies; entanglement supersedes parameter counting as a budgetable complexity axis.

Critical edge

01Methodology

theory · controlled design · invariants

A stochastic quantum policy πθ(a|s)=σ(α⟨Z₀⟩) is read off a parameterized circuit U(s,θ). Two theorems re-express its generalization cost in terms of Fisher geometry — and one proposition ties that geometry to entanglement.

Theory

Thm 1 ports the McAllester PAC-Bayes bound onto MDPs under a mixing-time assumption (meff=m/κ; κ=1 for one-step). Thm 2 picks a Gauss–Newton (Fisher-shaped) posterior Σ=τ²(I+γF̂)⁻¹, collapsing the KL into a distance term plus an effective dimension deff(γ)=Σᵢ log(1+γλᵢ) over Fisher eigenvalues — independent of parameter count.

Proposition 1 proves deff is non-decreasing under added entangling gates: the readout backward light-cone L(θ) grows, raising rank(F̂) while d stays fixed. The falsifiable prediction: entangled ⇒ larger deff ⇒ larger gap at identical d.

Experiments & controls

Ansatz of L RY/RZ layers on n qubits (d=2nL params), varying only entangling connectivity — none / linear (chain) / full (all-to-all) — all sharing identical d. Sweeps N∈{16…128}, 8–22 seeds, PennyLane (default/lightning), plus one IBM Heron ibm_aachen run (156 qubits, 4096 shots). Fixed hyperparams: τ²=1, γ=50, δ=0.05, Rmax=1.

Controls rule out the obvious confounders: matched training accuracy (early-stop), alt readout Z₁, alt optimizer (SGD vs Adam), and a local-vs-global readout swap. A partial-correlation analysis shows deff screens off the entangling label (ΔR²<0.01).

The load-bearing design move

Fix parameter count; vary only entangling connectivity. This is what isolates entanglement as a structural axis — parameter-counting bounds all tie at the same d and cannot order these circuits at all.

The ranking-certificate framing

The bound is numerically loose (~2.3–4.5×) yet valid everywhere and, crucially, correctly orders none < linear < full at each N — a use of PAC-Bayes that needs no numerical tightness, only τ²-invariant ordering.

03Strengths & Limitations

honest scope · readout-gated mechanism · boundary of the claim

Strengths

  • Right manipulation. Fixing parameter count and varying only connectivity is the textbook way to isolate a structural factor.
  • Ranking certificate. Turns PAC-Bayes looseness from weakness into strength — valid and informative ordering at fixed d.
  • Breadth. Supervised, bandit, value-function, REINFORCE, real datasets (Iris/Wine/Digits), IBM Heron hardware, and a barren-plateau boundary — one mechanism.
  • Intellectual honesty. Explicitly concedes fitted-Q washes out and multi-step ordering is partially unresolved.
  • Adversarial control. The local-vs-global readout swap confirms the light-cone account: "deff, not the label, tracks the gap."

Limitations

  • Thin end-to-end RL. Only linear-vs-none at N=8 (2.8σ); all-to-all and larger N unresolved under high return variance.
  • Readout-gated. A single global observable at n=4 collapses the contrast to 0.9σ; reach depends on the local Pauli readout convention.
  • Barren plateaus erode it. deff falls 13.2→3.1 from n=4→12 at fixed L=4 — claim restricted to shallow, trainable circuits.
  • Loose & τ²-dependent. Absolute values vary with prior scale τ²=1; only the ranking is invariant.
  • Gaps in evidence. No γ=50 sensitivity ablation; no code release URL; sim caps at n=16; held-out acc near chance for n≥10.

04Questions for Further Exploration

where the light-cone argument breaks · and what would tighten the claim

  1. Does the trade-off survive under richer readouts (multi-observable, non-local, adaptive) where the local light-cone argument is partly bypassed?
  2. Can the bound become non-vacuous in absolute terms via a data-dependent prior (e.g. a Fisher-shaped prior at a pre-trained init) — and would that change the ranking?
  3. How does the mechanism interact with data re-uploading circuits, where parameter count and light-cone grow in a more tangled way with depth?
  4. Can offline/batch RL on richer MDPs (image-state, continuous control) recover the clean bandit ordering, given bootstrapped fitted-Q iteration washes the effect out?
  5. Does entanglement also dominate other capacity measures (covering number, Rademacher complexity) at fixed parameter count, or is the deff advantage specific to the Fisher / PAC-Bayes framing?
