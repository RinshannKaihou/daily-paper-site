Mechanistic Interpretability / Knowledge Injection / arXiv 2607.08393

Memorized facts get stored in early/late layers but are never routed into mid-layer reasoning circuits — a routing failure, not a capacity limit.

一句话总结：本文形式化微调 LLM 时"记住但不会用"的 Knowing-Using Gap（准确率差距 + 时间滞后），提出 self-patching 干预，发现新知识编码在早/晚期存储层却未路由到中层推理电路（知识-电路错位），仅用两个固定层对就能恢复 58–75% 的 oracle 上限。

SO WHAT Why this matters

Reframes fine-tuning's generalization failure as a layer-routing problem, not a capacity limit — pointing toward alignment-aware training.

CRITICAL EDGE Sharpest caveat

The headline gap is really a chaining phenomenon — intersection is near-ceiling without intervention, narrowing the result's true breadth.

— Critical Assessment · Limitations

Methodology

From a 2-axis failure metric to self-patching

The Knowing-Using Gap is defined along two axes: an accuracy gap ΔA = Amem − Agen at convergence, and a temporal lag ΔT between the saturation epochs of memorization vs. downstream use.

01Dataset. Mem2Gen built on STaRK — STaRK-Prime (biomedical) & STaRK-MAG (academic). Single-hop recall vs. 2-hop chaining & noisy intersection. Novelty verified at ≤6% zero-shot.
02Self-patching. Copy the anchor-token residual stream from a source layer into a target layer, scan all (ls, lt) pairs → a "permeation map." Unlike causal tracing, it needs no clean correct trajectory, so it applies to failed cases.
03Dynamics. Per-epoch heatmaps show a red off-diagonal region appearing at memorization, then expanding toward the diagonal — reaching the diagonal ⇒ generalization emerges; halting before it ⇒ failure (loss vanishes, no gradient).
04Heuristic. Effective patches cluster into early→mid & late→mid. A fixed two-pair rule (⌊0.10L⌉→⌊0.45L⌉, ⌊0.82L⌉→⌊0.45L⌉) needs no per-instance search.

Strengths

  • Two-axis formalization with saturation-time definitions, robust to training fluctuation (validated via a τ/w grid).
  • Self-patching works on failed cases — no clean trajectory needed, exactly where the diagnosis matters.
  • Strong causal evidence: permeation dynamics, cross-context patching, token-position ablation, irrelevant-patch control rule out prompting artifacts.
  • Broad robustness: 2 domains, 6 models (1B–8B), LoRA + FFT, McNemar / Wilson / binomial stats.
  • Honest limitations appendix + a practical (if modest) heuristic moving from diagnosis to partial remedy.

Limitations

  • Single fixed anchor position (head-entity token) — headroom is a lower bound; two-cluster pattern is anchor-conditioned.
  • Oracle is per-instance best-pair; fixed heuristic recovers only 58–75% and still needs a source-prompt forward pass at inference (cost unquantified).
  • Purely post-hoc/diagnostic: no early-training signal predicts which facts will fail → cannot yet drive proactive training-time intervention.
  • Mechanistic granularity stops at token-layer resolution — no localization to heads or MLP sublayers.
  • Scale ceiling at 8B; early/late→mid pattern & recovery unverified at 70B+.
  • Gap is heavily a chaining phenomenon (intersection near-ceiling) — and the CoT baseline looks like a naive single template.

Open Questions for Further Exploration

  1. Does the early/late→mid two-cluster pattern and the 58–75% recovery hold at 70B / 405B scale, or does storage/computation layering shift with depth?
  2. Can the misalignment hypothesis become a training-time loss or regularizer (encouraging representations to permeate into mid-layer circuits) rather than a post-hoc intervention?
  3. How does self-patching interact with model editing (ROME/CAKE)? Can the fixed heuristic repair ripple-effect failures on MQuAKE / RippleEdits?
  4. Is the chaining-vs-intersection asymmetry an artifact of task design — would a harder intersection variant with more confounders reopen a large gap?
  5. The heuristic needs the source-prompt hidden state at inference; what is the real deployment cost, and can a cached/proxy representation remove this dependency?
  6. Could a per-instance adaptive layer selector close the remaining 25–42% gap to oracle — and is that gap explained by fact-specific storage variation?
  7. Does the misalignment story extend to in-context / RAG-provided knowledge, or is it specific to parametric knowledge injected by gradients?
