Methodology ┬Ę in brief

AutoPersonas is framed as a systems architecture + diagnostic method, not a model or training result. It targets an individualized life-environment layer that sits between the macro-world and persona State.

It names the failure risk current-state authority: because State S_t appears on both sides of the loop C_tŌåÆY_tŌåÆR_tŌåÆS_{t+1}, old state becomes the main evidence for its own continuation.

The fix is the OSO loop ŌĆö three objects with separated temporal authority that cannot be merged: Observation (evidence), State (continuity, a schema-bounded but semantically-open machine), and Occurrence (environment-side openings, not the agent's action). An event counts as growth only if it traverses Occurrence ŌåÆ lived material ŌåÆ Observation ŌåÆ State movement ŌåÆ revised reachability.

Five public mechanisms (production sampling/ranking/gating withheld): conditional variation, bounded context governance (~60% token-budget threshold), information orthogonality, progressive causal propagation, and trajectory reorientation ŌĆö revised on daily / weekly / monthly / long-horizon timescales.

Strengths

  • Sharp problem definition. ŌĆ£Self-lockingŌĆØ as functional collapse beneath varied surface text is a frame other long-horizon agent builders can adopt immediately.
  • Honest, falsifiable baseline. Fixed canon, strictness variants, and a temperature probe that explicitly attacks the rival ŌĆ£hotter sampling fixes itŌĆØ hypothesis.
  • Architectural clarity. OSO loop and the Occurrence-vs-action distinction are well-motivated, with comparison tables vs. perception-state-action controllers and Generative Agents / Agentopia.
  • Transparency on the disclosure boundary. Enumerates what is disclosed vs. withheld; releases the baseline evaluator + aggregate CSVs.
  • Practitioner heuristics. The ~60% token-budget threshold and the day-level-as-phase-boundary argument are directly usable design cues.

Limitations

  • Single canon, single run, no variance bands for the central A/B; positive validation = one primary persona + one fictional stress case.
  • No scalar metric for persona-life growth ŌĆö metrics quantify mode-lock symptoms, not evolution quality.
  • Subjective LLM-based macro-theme evaluator at the heart of the headline result; no inter-annotator agreement, no released grouping logic.
  • Event-density confound (144/160/85/170 events across A/B lanes) partially inflates theme-count comparisons.
  • Withheld production mechanisms (per-sample targeting, masking assets, gating, ranking) make the central positive claim non-reproducible from public materials.
  • No OSO component ablation, no human eval, no head-to-head with Generative Agents / Agentopia on a shared task.
