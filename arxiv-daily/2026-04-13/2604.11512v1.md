# EdgeCIM: A Hardware-Software Co-Design for CIM-Based Acceleration of Small Language Models

> **一句话总结：** 提出EdgeCIM软硬件协同设计框架，基于65nm CIM宏实现INT4精度的SLM边缘推理加速，在LLaMA3.2-1B上相比NVIDIA Orin Nano实现7.3x吞吐量提升和49.59x能效改善。

> **TL;DR:** EdgeCIM is a hardware-software co-design using 65nm compute-in-memory for INT4 SLM edge inference, achieving 7.3x throughput and 49.59x energy efficiency improvement over NVIDIA Orin Nano on LLaMA3.2-1B.

## 核心贡献

- 基于CIM宏的端到端decoder-only推理加速器，65nm实现
- 分块映射策略平衡流水线阶段，最大化并行度同时缓解DRAM带宽瓶颈
- 设计空间探索支持高达4B参数的SLM，在延迟和能耗上找到Pareto最优配置
- INT4精度下平均336.42 tokens/s和173.02 tokens/J

## Key Contributions

- CIM-based accelerator in 65nm for end-to-end decoder-only SLM inference
- Tile-based mapping strategy balancing pipeline stages and alleviating DRAM bottlenecks
- Design space exploration for SLMs up to 4B parameters
- 7.3x throughput, 49.59x energy efficiency vs Orin Nano on LLaMA3.2-1B; 9.95x vs Qualcomm SA8255P on LLaMA3.2-3B

## Methodology

65nm CIM macro with tile-based mapping strategy. Simulator enables design space exploration across multiple SLM architectures (TinyLLaMA, LLaMA3.2, Phi-3.5-mini, Qwen2.5, SmolLM, Qwen3) with INT4 precision.

## Results & Highlights

- 7.3x throughput over Orin Nano on LLaMA3.2-1B
- 49.59x energy efficiency improvement
- 9.95x throughput over Qualcomm SA8255P on LLaMA3.2-3B
- Average 336.42 tokens/s, 173.02 tokens/J under INT4
