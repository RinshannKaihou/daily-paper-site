# Not All Forgetting Is Equal: Architecture-Dependent Retention Dynamics in Fine-Tuned Image Classifiers

> **一句话总结：** 跟踪ResNet-18和DeiT-Small微调过程中逐样本的正确率变化，发现两种架构遗忘完全不同的样本（Jaccard重叠仅0.15-0.34），ViT遗忘更结构化（R²=0.74），且逐样本遗忘在不同随机种子间高度随机（Spearman ρ≈0.01）。

> **TL;DR:** Tracking per-sample forgetting during fine-tuning of ResNet-18 vs DeiT-Small reveals fundamentally different forgetting patterns (Jaccard overlap 0.15-0.34), with ViT showing more structured forgetting (R²=0.74) and per-sample forgetting being highly stochastic across seeds (Spearman ρ≈0.01).

## 核心贡献

- 发现不同架构（CNN vs ViT）在微调中遗忘完全不同的样本
- ViT遗忘模式更结构化，可拟合指数衰减曲线（R²=0.74 vs 0.52）
- 逐样本遗忘在随机种子间高度随机，挑战了"样本难度是内在属性"的假设
- 类级别遗忘一致且语义可解释

## Key Contributions

- Architecture-dependent forgetting: CNN and ViT forget fundamentally different samples (Jaccard 0.15-0.34)
- ViT forgetting is more structured (R²=0.74) than CNN (R²=0.52)
- Per-sample forgetting is stochastic across seeds (Spearman ρ≈0.01), challenging intrinsic difficulty assumption
- Loss after head warmup predicts long-term decay constant (ρ=0.30-0.50)

## Methodology

Track per-sample correctness at every epoch during fine-tuning of ResNet-18 and DeiT-Small on retinal OCT (7 classes, 56:1 imbalance) and CUB-200-2011 (200 bird species). Fit Ebbinghaus-style exponential decay curves.

## Results & Highlights

- Architecture-dependent forgetting: Jaccard overlap 0.15-0.34
- Stochastic per-sample forgetting across seeds (ρ≈0.01)
- Class-level forgetting is semantically interpretable
- Spaced repetition sampler based on decay constants does NOT outperform random sampling

## Critical Assessment

- **Significance:** Important implications for curriculum design and ensemble construction — architectural diversity provides complementary retention coverage.

## Strengths

- Careful per-sample analysis with exponential decay fitting
- Challenges assumption of intrinsic sample difficulty
- Practical implications for ensemble design

## Limitations

- Limited to image classification (not LLM fine-tuning)
- Small-scale models (ResNet-18, DeiT-Small)
- Spaced repetition sampler based on findings doesn't actually help
