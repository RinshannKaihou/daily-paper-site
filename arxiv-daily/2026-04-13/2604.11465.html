Three Roles, One Model: Role Orchestration at Inference Time to Close the Performance Gap

一句话总结： 在单张24GB GPU上，通过将冻结的Qwen3-8B模型分别扮演摘要器、主代理和校正器三个角色，无需额外训练即可将任务完成率从5.4%提升至8.9%（FP16），超越4倍大小的DeepSeek-Coder 33B。
TL;DR: By deploying a frozen Qwen3-8B in three roles (summarizer, agent, corrector) at inference time on a single 24GB GPU, task completion roughly doubles (5.4%→8.9% FP16), surpassing 4x larger DeepSeek-Coder 33B without any training.

核心贡献

  • 提出三层推理脚手架管道：摘要器（保留关键信息）、主代理（推理）、校正器（隔离审查代码输出）
  • 同一冻结模型扮演三个角色，无需额外训练计算
  • 在单张24GB GPU上超越4倍大小的模型

Key Contributions

  • Three-tier inference scaffolding: summarizer, agent, corrector from same frozen model
  • No training compute needed — pure inference-time intervention
  • 8B model surpasses DeepSeek-Coder 33B (7.1%) on AppWorld benchmark

Methodology

Same frozen Qwen3-8B model invoked three times with different conditioning: (1) summarization preserving critical artifacts, (2) main reasoning agent, (3) isolated correction model reviewing code without conversation history.

Results & Highlights

  • FP16: 5.4% → 8.9% task completion
  • AWQ 4-bit: 3.0% → 5.9%
  • Surpasses DeepSeek-Coder 33B (7.1%)
  • Strong gains on difficulty-1 tasks (15.8%→26.3%)

Authors & Credibility

Authors from Aimpoint Digital Labs and New York University. NYU has strong CS research. Practical engineering contribution with clear deployment realism focus.

Critical Assessment

  • Novelty: The three-role scaffolding is simple but effective. Not conceptually groundbreaking but practically useful.
  • Significance: Demonstrates that inference-time interventions can be competitive with much larger models.

Strengths

  • Practical and deployable on consumer hardware
  • Clear evaluation under realistic constraints
  • Competitive with 4x larger models

Limitations

  • Overall completion rates still low (<10%)
  • Only tested on AppWorld benchmark
