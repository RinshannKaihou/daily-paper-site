# Relax: An Asynchronous Reinforcement Learning Engine for Omni-Modal Post-Training at Scale

> **一句话总结：** 小红书团队推出Relax开源RL训练引擎，通过三层架构（全模态原生支持、独立故障隔离服务、TransferQueue异步训练总线）实现大规模全模态RL后训练，异步模式下Qwen3-4B提速1.76x、Qwen3-Omni-30B提速2.00x，所有模式收敛至相同奖励水平。

> **TL;DR:** Relax is an open-source RL training engine with omni-native architecture, fault-isolated services, and asynchronous training via TransferQueue, achieving up to 2.0x speedup over baselines while converging to the same reward level.

## 核心贡献

- 全模态原生架构：从数据预处理、模态感知并行到推理生成全栈支持多模态，而非在文本管道上追加多模态支持
- 每个RL角色作为独立的故障隔离服务运行，可独立扩展、恢复和升级，无需全局协调
- TransferQueue数据总线实现异步训练，单一staleness参数可在on-policy、近on-policy和完全异步之间平滑插值
- 在Qwen3-4B上实现1.20x端到端加速（on-policy），异步模式下1.76x加速；Qwen3-Omni-30B上2.00x加速

## Key Contributions

- Omni-native architecture with multimodal support throughout the full stack
- Fault-isolated independent services for each RL role (scale, recover, upgrade independently)
- TransferQueue data bus with configurable staleness for smooth on-policy to async interpolation
- 1.20x-2.00x speedups across model sizes while maintaining reward convergence

## Methodology

Three co-designed architectural layers: (1) omni-native data pipeline with modality-aware parallelism, (2) microservice-style RL roles with independent fault isolation, (3) TransferQueue asynchronous data bus. The single staleness parameter controls the trade-off between training freshness and throughput.

## Results & Highlights

- 1.20x speedup over veRL on Qwen3-4B on-policy training
- 1.76x async speedup on Qwen3-4B, 2.00x on Qwen3-Omni-30B
- All modes converge to same reward level
- R3 support for MoE models with only 1.9% overhead vs. 32% degradation in veRL
- Stable omni-modal RL convergence over 2,000+ video steps without degradation
