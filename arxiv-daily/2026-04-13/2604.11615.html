CUTEv2: Unified and Configurable Matrix Extension for Diverse CPU Architectures

一句话总结： 提出统一可配置的CPU矩阵扩展架构，通过解耦矩阵单元与CPU流水线实现低开销集成，支持混合精度运算，在四个开源CPU平台上矩阵单元利用率超90%，在ResNet/BERT/Llama3上实现1.57-2.31x加速。
TL;DR: A unified, configurable CPU matrix extension that decouples matrix units from the pipeline for low-overhead integration, supports mixed-precision, achieves >90% utilization across 4 CPU platforms and 1.57-2.31x speedup on AI workloads.

核心贡献

  • 将矩阵单元从CPU流水线解耦，实现跨CPU的低开销集成
  • 可配置矩阵单元支持混合精度运算，适应不同计算需求和内存带宽约束
  • 异步矩阵乘法抽象隐藏硬件细节，简化矩阵-向量重叠执行
  • 在四个开源CPU RTL平台上集成验证，矩阵单元利用率超过90%

Key Contributions

  • Decoupled matrix unit design for cross-CPU integration with minimal overhead
  • Configurable mixed-precision support adapting to diverse compute and bandwidth constraints
  • Asynchronous matrix multiplication abstraction for hardware detail concealment
  • >90% utilization on 4 open-source CPU platforms; 1.57-2.31x speedup on AI workloads

Methodology

Hardware-software co-design: decoupled matrix unit from CPU pipeline, configurable for mixed-precision, with asynchronous execution abstraction. Integrated into 4 open-source CPU RTL platforms. 4 TOPS@2GHz matrix unit occupies only 0.53 mm2 in 14nm CMOS.

Results & Highlights

  • Matrix unit utilization >90% across all platforms under GEMM
  • Speedups: 1.57x (ResNet), 1.57x (BERT), 2.31x (Llama3) vs Intel AMX-comparable config
  • 30%+ gains from overlapped matrix-vector execution
  • 0.53 mm2 area in 14nm for 4 TOPS@2GHz

Authors & Credibility

Authors from State Key Lab of Processors, Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, and UCAS. ICT CAS is China's leading processor research institution with strong hardware design track record.

  • Significance: Practically valuable for open-source CPU ecosystem's AI capabilities.
