A Mechanistic Analysis of Looped Reasoning Language Models

Hugh Blayney, Álvaro Arroyo, Johan Obando-Ceron, Pablo Samuel Castro, Aaron Courville, Michael M. Bronstein, Xiaowen Dong
循环推理LM的隐空间不动点分析——每层收敛到不同不动点，在潜空间形成一致循环轨迹；不动点稳定性与模型性能退化直接相关。来自 Courville、Bronstein 等顶级团队。
First mechanistic analysis of looped reasoning LMs: each layer converges to a distinct per-layer fixed point forming cyclic latent trajectories, with formal convergence proofs and empirical validation across Ouro, Huginn, and retrofitted Llama/OLMo families. Fixed-point instability predicts downstream performance degradation.
