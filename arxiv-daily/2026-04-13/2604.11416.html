Exact Certification of Neural Networks and Partition Aggregation Ensembles against Label Poisoning

一句话总结： 提出EnsembleCert框架，首次利用基分类器的白盒知识进行分区聚合集成的标签投毒认证，并开发ScaLabelCert利用神经正切核实现首个多项式时间可精确计算的神经网络抗标签投毒证书，在CIFAR-10上以100倍少的分区实现+26.5%的认证提升。
TL;DR: EnsembleCert provides the first white-box certification for partition-aggregation ensembles against label poisoning, while ScaLabelCert yields the first exact polynomial-time certificate for neural networks using the neural tangent kernel, achieving +26.5% more certified flips with 100x fewer partitions on CIFAR-10.

核心贡献

  • EnsembleCert：首个利用白盒知识的分区聚合集成认证框架
  • ScaLabelCert：利用神经正切核实现首个精确多项式时间可计算的标签投毒证书
  • 挑战了"大量分区是实现强认证鲁棒性必要条件"的现有观念

Key Contributions

  • First white-box certification framework for partition-aggregation ensembles against label poisoning
  • First exact polynomial-time certificate for neural networks (ScaLabelCert) via NTK equivalence
  • +26.5% more certified label flips with 100x fewer partitions on CIFAR-10

Methodology

Exploits equivalence between sufficiently wide neural networks and kernel methods via the neural tangent kernel (NTK). Aggregates per-partition white-box certificates to compute ensemble-level guarantees in polynomial time.

Results & Highlights

  • +26.5% more certified flips on CIFAR-10 (median over test set)
  • 100x fewer partitions needed vs. black-box approaches
  • Presented at ICLR 2026 Workshop on Principled Design for Trustworthy AI

Authors & Credibility

Authors from Technical University of Munich (TUM), Munich Data Science Institute, Munich Center for Machine Learning, and Australian Institute for Machine Learning. Stephan Gunnemann is a well-known ML security researcher at TUM with extensive publications. Strong team from top ML institutions.
