Architectural Trade-offs in the Energy-Efficient Era: A Comparative Study of Power-Capping NVIDIA H100 and H200

一句话总结： 对比分析NVIDIA H100和H200在不同功耗限制下的性能表现，发现H100在计算密集型负载中略优，而H200在内存密集型应用中能效更佳，为AI训练和推理的能效优化提供硬件级指导。
TL;DR: Comparative study of NVIDIA H100 and H200 under power-capping, finding H100 slightly better for compute-bound workloads while H200 shows superior efficiency for memory-bound applications.

核心贡献

  • 系统对比H100（HBM2e）和H200（HBM3e）在不同功耗上限下的性能和能效
  • 通过回归分析揭示内存功耗限制和异常高功耗节点
  • 使用计算密集型（DGEMM）和内存密集型（Triad）负载代表Roofline模型两个极端

Key Contributions

  • Systematic H100 vs H200 comparison under varying power caps
  • Regression analysis of memory power limits and outlier detection
  • Compute-bound (DGEMM) and memory-bound (Triad) workload evaluation
  • H100 better for compute-bound; H200 better for memory-bound applications

Methodology

Power-cap experiments on H100 and H200 using DGEMM (compute-bound) and TheBandwidthBenchmark (memory-bound) workloads. Regression analysis to characterize memory power behavior and identify outliers.

Results & Highlights

  • H100 slightly better for strictly compute-bound workloads
  • H200 superior for memory-bound applications
  • Memory bandwidth difference (HBM2e vs HBM3e) significantly impacts power distribution

Authors & Credibility
