A Triadic Suffix Tokenization Scheme for Numerical Reasoning

一句话总结： 提出三位后缀分词方案（TST），将数字按三位一组分割并附加量级标记，为LLM提供从10^-15到10^18共33个数量级的精确数值表示，但实验验证推迟到未来工作。
TL;DR: Triadic Suffix Tokenization (TST) partitions digits into three-digit triads with explicit magnitude markers, providing consistent gradient signal for numerical reasoning across 33 orders of magnitude (10^-15 to 10^18), though experimental validation is deferred.

核心贡献

  • 提出确定性三位后缀分词方案，保持精确数字并使量级关系在token级别透明
  • 定义整数部分和小数部分的固定一对一后缀-量级映射
  • 两种实现变体：词汇方法（最多添加10000个token）和后缀标记方法

Key Contributions

  • Deterministic triadic suffix tokenization with explicit magnitude markers
  • Fixed one-to-one suffix-magnitude mapping for integers and fractions
  • Two variants: vocabulary-based (≤10K new tokens) and suffix-marker approach

Methodology

Partitions numbers into three-digit triads with magnitude markers (thousands, millions, billions for integers; tenths, thousandths, millionths for fractions). Architecture-agnostic, drop-in preprocessing.

Results & Highlights

  • Covers 33 orders of magnitude (10^-15 to 10^18)
  • No experimental validation — deferred to future work
