# Beyond LLMs, Sparse Distributed Memory, and Neuromorphics: A Hyper-Dimensional SRAM-CAM System

> **一句话总结：** 提出一种基于超维计算的SRAM-CAM架构（VaCoAl），结合模拟-数字功能归纳方法，声称实现超高速、超低功耗和低成本的替代计算范式。

> **TL;DR:** Proposes VaCoAl, a hyper-dimensional SRAM-CAM architecture combining analog-digital functional induction for ultra-high speed, ultra-low power computing as an alternative to LLMs and neuromorphic systems.

## 核心贡献

- 提出超维SRAM-CAM架构作为LLM和神经形态计算的替代方案
- 结合模拟-数字功能归纳方法
- 声称在速度、功耗和成本上有显著优势

## Key Contributions

- Hyper-dimensional SRAM-CAM architecture (VaCoAl)
- Analog-Digital functional induction methodology
- Proposed alternative to probabilistic generative AI approaches

## Methodology

Builds on Kanerva's Sparse Distributed Memory theory with extensions using high-dimensional orthogonal spaces. The SRAM-CAM hardware implementation enables best-match operations in hyper-dimensional spaces.

## Results & Highlights

- 210K+ character paper suggesting comprehensive theoretical treatment
- Claims of ultra-high speed and ultra-low power operation
