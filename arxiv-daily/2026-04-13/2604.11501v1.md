# Quantization Dominates Rank Reduction for KV-Cache Compression

> **一句话总结：** 本文在5个模型（124M-14B参数）上系统比较了KV-cache压缩的两种策略——量化与秩缩减，发现在相同存储预算下量化一致优于秩缩减4-364 PPL，其根本原因在于softmax注意力路由下删除维度会翻转注意力分配（离散故障），而量化噪声有界且通常保持排序。

> **TL;DR:** At matched storage budgets across five models (124M-14B), INT4 quantization consistently outperforms rank reduction for KV-cache compression by 4-364 PPL, explained by a structural asymmetry: deleting dimensions flips softmax attention routing while quantization noise preserves score ordering.

## 核心贡献

- 首次在相同存储预算下系统比较KV-cache压缩的两种范式（量化 vs 秩缩减），涵盖5个模型、2个指标、2个数据集和混合基线
- 理论证明在softmax Fisher度量下，投影损伤超过量化损伤3×2^{2b}个方向（Proposition 2），因为维度删除会翻转注意力路由，而有界噪声通常保持排序
- 提出下游最优压缩框架（Theorem 1），统一了PCA、Fisher-SVD、GPTQ、AWQ和KQ-SVD等先前方法为特例
- 基底消融实验证明该优势与坐标系无关（差异<0.4 PPL），确认优势来自保留维度而非更好的坐标选择

## Key Contributions

- First matched-storage comparison of quantization vs. rank reduction for KV-cache across 5 models (124M-14B, MHA and GQA)
- Theoretical explanation via Proposition 2: projection damage exceeds quantization damage by 3×2^{2b} per direction under the softmax Fisher metric
- Downstream-optimal compression framework (Theorem 1) unifying PCA, Fisher-SVD, GPTQ, AWQ, and KQ-SVD as special cases
- Basis ablation showing the quantization advantage is coordinate-independent (spread <0.4 PPL)

## Methodology

The author compares two KV-cache compression strategies at matched bit budgets: rank reduction (projecting to fewer dimensions) and quantization (reducing precision while keeping all dimensions). Experiments span five models from 124M to 14B parameters with both MHA and GQA architectures. The theoretical analysis uses the softmax Fisher metric to formalize why deletion is worse than noise — projection can flip which token receives the highest attention weight (a discrete failure), while quantization noise is bounded and typically preserves the score ordering.

## Results & Highlights

- INT4 quantization achieves near-lossless compression on LAMBADA (+0.23 PPL on Mistral 7B, +0.58 on GPT-2), while rank-32 at identical storage collapses to 0.4% accuracy
- The quantization advantage grows with GQA aggressiveness
- Even hybrid baselines (rank reduction followed by quantization) cannot match full-rank quantization
- Joint K+V INT4 achieves 75% total KV reduction at only +0.18 PPL on Mistral 7B
- Basis ablation confirms finding is coordinate-independent
