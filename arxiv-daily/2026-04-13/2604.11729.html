Universality of First-Order Methods on Random and Deterministic Matrices

一句话总结： 通过图变换分布分析一般一阶方法（GFOM）的图式展开，首次计算了Walsh-Hadamard和DCT等确定性矩阵的极限动力学，并设计了统一的AMP迭代算法，在随机和确定性输入上均实现高斯极限动力学。
TL;DR: Analyzes diagrammatic expansions of general first-order methods via limiting traffic distributions, computing the first non-trivial deterministic matrix dynamics (Walsh-Hadamard, DCT) and designing a unified AMP algorithm with Gaussian limiting dynamics for both random and deterministic inputs.

核心贡献

  • 首次计算了非平凡确定性矩阵（Walsh-Hadamard、DCT/DST）的极限动力学，解决了Marinari-Parisi-Ritort (1994)的长期猜想
  • 设计了统一的AMP迭代算法，在随机和确定性输入上均产生条件高斯极限动力学
  • 给出Onsager校正的简单组合解释

Key Contributions

  • First calculation of traffic distribution for non-trivial deterministic matrices (Walsh-Hadamard, DCT)
  • Unified AMP algorithm with Gaussian limiting dynamics for diverse input types
  • Combinatorial interpretation of Onsager correction
  • Resolves parts of longstanding conjectures from 1994

Methodology

Diagrammatic expansions of GFOM via the limiting traffic distribution of input matrices. The traffic distribution captures all limiting values of permutation-invariant polynomials in matrix entries. New AMP iteration generalizes previous variants with Gaussian conditional dynamics.

Results & Highlights

  • Resolves parts of Marinari-Parisi-Ritort (1994) conjectures
  • Unified AMP algorithm works for both random and deterministic inputs
  • Simple combinatorial interpretation of Onsager correction
