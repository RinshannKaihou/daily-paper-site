Alignment Lead

Overtrained, Not Misaligned

Joel Schreiber & Ariel Goldstein
首次大规模系统性研究证明涌现性错位（EM）是过度训练的副产物而非微调必然代价，早停法可平均保留 93% 任务性能的同时完全消除 EM；仅 17% 的模型在多种子下稳定表现出 EM，且集中在 200B 参数以上。
First large-scale systematic study proving emergent misalignment is an overtraining artifact, not an inherent fine-tuning cost; early stopping eliminates EM while retaining ~93% task performance. Only 17% of models show consistent EM, exclusively above 200B parameters.
