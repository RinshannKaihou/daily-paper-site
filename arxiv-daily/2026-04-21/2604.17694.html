Improving reproducibility by controlling random seed stability in machine learning based estimation via bagging

arXiv: 2604.17694

Date: 2026-04-21

https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.17694

⚠️ Abstract-only summary

一句话总结： 本文形式化了随机种子稳定性概念，证明subbagging可保证任意有界回归算法的稳定性，并提出adaptive cross-bagging方法消除debiased ML中的种子依赖。
TL;DR: Formalizes random seed stability via a concentration condition and proves subbagging guarantees stability for any bounded-outcome regression algorithm. Introduces adaptive cross-bagging, a new cross-fitting procedure that eliminates seed dependence from both nuisance estimation and sample splitting in debiased ML, with small computational overhead compared to alternatives.

核心贡献

  • 通过浓度条件形式化了随机种子稳定性(random seed stability)概念
  • 证明subbagging可保证任意有界输出回归算法的稳定性
  • 提出adaptive cross-bagging交叉拟合新方法，同时消除nuisance估计和样本划分中的种子依赖
  • 相比替代方法，该方法计算开销小

Key Contributions

  • Formalization of random seed stability via concentration condition
  • Proof that subbagging guarantees stability for bounded-outcome regression
  • Novel adaptive cross-bagging procedure eliminating seed dependence in debiased ML
  • Numerical validation showing targeted stability with small computational penalty

Methodology

Theoretical analysis combining concentration inequalities with bagging theory. Introduces adaptive cross-bagging as a cross-fitting procedure for debiased machine learning estimators. Validates through numerical experiments comparing against alternative methods.

Results & Highlights

  • Subbagging provably guarantees random seed stability for bounded-outcome algorithms
  • Adaptive cross-bagging achieves targeted stability level where alternatives fail
  • Computational penalty is small relative to standard practice, unlike alternative methods
