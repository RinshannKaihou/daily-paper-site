Towards Disentangled Preference Optimization Dynamics Beyond Likelihood Displacement

arXiv: 2604.18239

Date: 2026-04-21

https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.18239

⚠️ Abstract-only summary

一句话总结： 本文通过统一的激励-分数分解揭示了偏好优化中likelihood displacement现象的动力学机制，提出"解耦带"条件和自适应奖励校准方法，在不修改基础目标函数的前提下缓解chosen响应被抑制的问题。
TL;DR: Presents a unified incentive-score decomposition of preference optimization objectives, revealing that diverse methods share identical local update directions differing only in scalar weights. Identifies the "disentanglement band" (DB) condition for when training avoids likelihood displacement, and proposes plug-and-play reward calibration (RC) that adaptively rebalances chosen vs. rejected updates to satisfy the DB.

核心贡献

  • 提出偏好优化的统一激励-分数分解，证明不同目标函数共享相同的局部更新方向，仅在标量权重系数上有差异
  • 发现"解耦带"(DB)条件，刻画了训练何时能避免likelihood displacement（抑制输家同时保持赢家）
  • 提出即插即用的奖励校准(RC)方法，自适应调整chosen与rejected更新的平衡
  • RC方法无需重新设计基础目标函数，可应用于多种偏好优化算法

Key Contributions

  • Unified incentive-score decomposition revealing that diverse preference optimization objectives share identical local update directions
  • Disentanglement band (DB) condition characterizing when training avoids likelihood displacement
  • Plug-and-play reward calibration (RC) that adaptively rebalances updates without modifying base objectives
  • Empirical validation showing RC steers training toward disentangled dynamics across multiple objectives

Methodology

Analyzes preference optimization dynamics through incentive-score decomposition. By examining chosen/rejected likelihood trajectories, identifies the disentanglement band condition. Proposes reward calibration as a modular correction layer that can be applied to any margin-based preference optimization objective.

Results & Highlights

  • RC improves downstream performance across a range of preference optimization objectives
  • Training dynamics become more disentangled (chosen likelihood maintained while rejected suppressed)
  • The DB condition provides a testable criterion for diagnosing likelihood displacement in training
