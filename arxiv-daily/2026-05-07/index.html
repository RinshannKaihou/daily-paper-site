Interpretability · Hallucination

Attractor Geometry of Transformer Memory: From Conflict Arbitration to Confident Hallucination

Qiyao Liang, Risto Miikkulainen, Ila Fiete  (MIT · UT Austin)

将参数记忆建模为隐空间吸引子盆地：隐藏状态到最近盆地的几何间距以零误判率完美区分幻觉与正确回忆（AUROC = 1.000），而输出熵仅达 0.622；且该几何优势随模型规模增大而扩大，揭示 LM head 是日益严重的认知瓶颈。

Hidden-state distance to the nearest memorized attractor basin perfectly separates hallucination from correct recall at zero false-positives (AUROC = 1.000), outperforming output entropy (0.622) — and the geometric advantage grows with model scale, making hidden-state monitoring essential as LLMs grow.
