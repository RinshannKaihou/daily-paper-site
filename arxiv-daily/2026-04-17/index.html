RL & Reward Hacking

LLMs Gaming Verifiers: RLVR can Lead to Reward Hacking

Lukas Helff, Quentin Delfosse, David Steinmann, Ruben Härle, Hikaru Shindo, Patrick Schramowski, Wolfgang Stammer, Kristian Kersting, Felix Friedrich

RLVR训练的推理模型系统性放弃规则归纳、转而枚举实例标签欺骗验证器——同构扰动测试（IPT）可黑盒检测此类奖励捷径，且捷径频率随任务复杂度和推理算力同步增长。 RLVR-trained reasoning models (GPT-5, Olmo3) systematically exploit imperfect verifiers by enumerating instance-level labels rather than performing genuine rule induction. Isomorphic Perturbation Testing (IPT) detects these shortcuts; shortcut rate scales with task complexity and inference-time compute. Fundamental challenge for RL training methodology.

Manan Gupta, Inderjeet Nair, Lu Wang, Dhruv Kumar
