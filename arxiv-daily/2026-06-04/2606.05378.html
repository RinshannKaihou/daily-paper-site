Falsifiable OLMoE prev-token-primacy hypothesis

At ~1B active params, MoE may scaffold composed tasks on a prev-token positional substrate — 3/4 tasks (GT, successor, VB); IOI exception aligns with name-copy structure (name-mover primary).

Predictions: Mixtral, DBRX, larger OLMoE variants should show prev-token-primary on compatible tasks; IOI remains task-structural exception.

BOS-class head rates (Paper 1): Pythia 53.9% · OLMo 78.1% · OLMoE 68.0% — explains interferer prevalence & why per-head ablation beats BOS filtering on OLMo.

Methodological warnings (actionable)

  • Margin-not-argmax: report logit-diff alongside top-1 — screens can compress margin with near-zero argmax change
  • L0 matched-random: concentrated L0 screens inflate random baseline — weak null hypothesis
  • BOS filters: valid on Pythia, destructive on OLMo (78% BOS heads) — use individual head ablations cross-model
  • PR alone: cannot isolate task heads under attention-sink–dominated 1B regimes
GPT-2 124M baseline: IOI top-1 only 13% — this study sits at the first 1B frontier where IOI & related tasks become reliably solvable (~90%+ frac).

Limitations & open validation

  • 12-cell panel — no Llama-1B, Qwen, extra MoE variants or tasks
  • Single pretraining checkpoint per model; seed stability of screen types untested
  • Interferer category thin (chiefly Pythia VB); residual-stream / expert-routing mechanisms unexplained
  • 70B+ non-portability claim remains extrapolation beyond this scale
