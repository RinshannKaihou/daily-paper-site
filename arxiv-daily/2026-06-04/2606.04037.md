> **一句话总结：** 作者提出企业 AI Agent 部署前验证框架：Agent Operational Envelope（权限/域/安全/治理/自治五级）、从行业本体自动生成监管/运营/对抗场景，以及带 Approved/Conditional/Rejected 分级裁决的机器可验证 Trust Certificate；在美越五行业×监管单元、1,800 场景（Claude 生成）+ 三模型 5,400 场景交叉验证中，本体驱动生成（G4）在监管覆盖率上显著优于 persona 基线（48.3% vs 33.1%，pc=.0006）、行业特异性最高（4.77/5.0），但对 G1/G3 的 RC 优势未过 Bonferroni 校正，故障检出率无显著差异，且 Claude 上存在探索性的「覆盖–精度」权衡。

> **TL;DR:** Tuan & Sanyal propose a pre-deployment assurance stack for regulated enterprise LLM agents: (1) **Agent Operational Envelope** EA=⟨Π,Σ,Φ,Γ,Λ⟩ derived from industry ontologies; (2) **ontology-to-scenario generation** (Algorithm 1: regulatory SR, KPI SK, adversarial SA suites); (3) **Trust Certificate** CA=⟨EA,S,R,V,t,sig⟩ with graduated verdicts (θhigh=0.95, θlow=0.80) and infrastructure **simulation gate**. A controlled pilot (4 conditions × 5 US/VN industry-regime cells × 3 reps × 30 scenarios = 1,800; 125 primary-source regulations; 25 injected faults) plus 3-generator replication (Claude Sonnet 4, Qwen 2.5 72B, Gemma 4 26B → 5,400 total) shows G4 beats persona G2 on regulatory coverage (48.3% vs 33.1%, pc=.0006) and industry specificity (4.77/5.0, p=2×10⁻⁶), while G4 vs G1/G3 RC gains are not Bonferroni-robust, FDR is null (p=.891), and adversarial coverage is equivalent (~88–91%). Complements runtime governance (OWASP/NIST/Microsoft) with an auditable deployment gate; limitations include ontology completeness, shared-author checklist/judge bias, and V1 simulation vs formal V4–V5 guarantees.

## 核心贡献

- **Agent Operational Envelope（AOE）**：形式化认证空间 EA=⟨Π,Σ,Φ,Γ,Λ⟩（权限边界、领域范围、安全不变量、治理约束、自治等级 L0–L3），从企业本体 O=⟨R,D,I⟩ 自动派生；执行轨迹需在 Π 内且满足 Φ、Γ。
- **本体→场景生成（Algorithm 1）**：遍历监管约束、KPI 指标与许可动作，生成监管（SR）、运营（SK）、对抗（SA）三类场景；信息层级 IG1⊂IG2⊂IG3⊂IG4，G4 使用结构化三层本体（30% 约束 holdout 防循环）。
- **Trust Certificate**：机器可验证元组 ⟨EA,S,R,V,t,sig⟩，绑定代码/模型/本体版本；裁决 Approved / Conditional / Rejected，FAOS 仿真门在 production 阻断未认证 Agent。
- **验证谱系（V1–V5）**：从统计仿真（V1）到概率 BMC（V2）、运行时监控（V3）、有界/完全形式验证（V4–V5），对标 DO-178C / IEC 62304 / ISO 26262 的「部署前验证」缺口。
- **跨法域实证**：金融科技、银行、保险、医疗 × 美越监管单元；1,800 场景 + 三生成器 5,400 场景；125 条一手法规清单、25 类注入故障；本体优势在 persona 基线与 ISS 上可复现，非单模型参数知识假象。

## Key Contributions

- **Agent Operational Envelope (AOE):** Formal certification tuple EA=⟨Π,Σ,Φ,Γ,Λ⟩ (permissions, domain scope, safety invariants, governance rules, autonomy L0–L3) auto-derived from enterprise ontology O=⟨R,D,I⟩; envelope-compliant traces require actions ∈ Π and ∀φ∈Φ, γ∈Γ.
- **Ontology-to-scenario generation (Algorithm 1):** Systematic traversal of regulations, metrics, and permitted actions yields regulatory (SR), operational (SK), and adversarial (SA) suites; strict information hierarchy IG1⊂IG2⊂IG3⊂IG4 with 30% regulatory holdout (E1 anti-circularity).
- **Trust Certificate & simulation gate:** Machine-verifiable attestation ⟨EA,S,R,V,t,sig⟩ with version binding; graduated verdicts (θhigh=0.95, θlow=0.80); infrastructure-level deploy(A) allow / require_L3_approval / block.
- **Verification spectrum (V1–V5):** Bridges statistical simulation to LTL+onto(d,c) specs, probabilistic BMC, and runtime monitors—positioned as the missing pre-deployment "how" behind OWASP/NIST/Vietnam AI Law mandates.
- **Cross-jurisdictional evidence:** US + Vietnam (2025 AI Law) pilot; ontology-grounded G4 significantly beats persona G2 on RC and all generators on ISS; three-model replication (5,400 scenarios) supports methodology-over-parametric-knowledge interpretation.

## Methodology

**Framework (Section 2).**
- **AOE derivation:** Π from role approval authority; Σ from domain verticals; Φ from governance invariants; Γ from regulatory frameworks; Λ from agent config (e.g., Fintech AML analyst: flag_suspicious, file_CTR/SAR, escalate; not approve_loan).
- **Scenario taxonomy:** SR from D.G (BSA/AML thresholds, HIPAA, NAIC, SBV circulars); SK from D.M (metrics); SA (prompt injection, exfiltration, regulatory bypass, role confusion).
- **Coverage metric:** RC(S,O) = |{γ tested}| / |O.D.G|; empirically validated against 125-item checklist (25/industry) from primary statutes—not the FAOS ontology alone.
- **Trust Certificate:** Results matrix R per scenario; judge(a,s)→⟨pass, score, rationale⟩ on regulatory compliance, domain accuracy, safety boundary, adversarial resilience; five judge variants (RC, ISS, AC, FDR-design, FDR-exec) at T=0.0.

**Implementation (proposed FAOS).** Rust simulation runner (tokio sandbox, 60s timeout, Temporal durability) + Python LLM-as-judge; Prometheus observability; bilingual US/VN ontologies.

**Empirical design (Section 3.2).**
| Factor | Levels |
|--------|--------|
| **IV (generation)** | G1 baseline (role+domain); G2 persona×6 categories (A-Sim style); G3 RAG (8 ontology chunks); G4 ontology-grounded (Algorithm 1, 30% holdout) |
| **Blocking** | 5 cells: US fintech, US insurance, US healthcare, VN banking, VN insurance |
| **Design** | 4×5×3 = 60 suites × 30 scenarios = **1,800** (Claude Sonnet 4 generator, T=0.3); judge fixed Claude T=0.0 |
| **Cross-model** | + Qwen 2.5 72B, Gemma 4 26B → **5,400** total |
| **DV** | RC (n=125), FDR-design/execution (n=25 faults), ISS 1–5 (n=150), AC (6 categories) |
| **Stats** | Friedman + Wilcoxon post-hoc, Bonferroni (m=3 per DV); Kendall's W; pre-registered H1–H4 |

**Formal extensions (Section 3.1).** LTL extended with onto(d,c); probabilistic BMC PBMC(A,φ,k,δ); runtime monitor over immutable event store (Algorithm 2).

## Results & Highlights

| Result | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **H1 RC (vs G2)** | G4 mean **48.3%** vs G2 **33.1%** (pc=.0006); healthcare G4 **59%** vs G1 **31%** (+28pp descriptive); VN insurance G4 **56%** vs G2 **28%** |
| **H1 RC (vs G1/G3)** | G4 vs G1 pc=.243; G4 vs G3 +3.7pp n.s.; VN banking G3 **57%** > G4 **49%** (different requirement subsets) |
| **H2 FDR** | No omnibus difference (p=.891); design ~55–64% all conditions; G4 exec lowest on Claude (**40%** vs design **55%**, gap **+15pp** exploratory) |
| **H3 ISS** | G4 **4.77/5.0** > all (p<10⁻⁵); significant G4–G3 gap despite similar RC |
| **H4 AC** | Equivalent ~88–91% (p=.995)—adversarial coverage needs explicit prompting, not ontology |
| **Cross-model H5–H6** | G4>G2 RC on Claude (+15.2pp), Gemma (+11.2pp), Qwen (+14.4pp); ISS replicates all three |
| **H7 FDR gap** | Model-dependent: Claude +14.7pp, Gemma +2.7pp, Qwen **−16.0pp** (exec > design) |
| **Production anecdote** | VN bank AML shadow mode: 2/27 false-negative sanctions matches; simulation gate caught diacritic-dropping romanization rule |

**Information hierarchy insight:** G3 (flattened RAG chunks) ≈ G4 on RC when content overlaps; G4 wins on ISS via **structure** (systematic regulatory traversal), not raw text alone.

**Governance alignment:** Maps OWASP agentic risks → scenario taxonomy; Trust Certificate as NIST monitoring pillar complement to Microsoft runtime toolkit; Vietnam Law 134/2025/QH15 motivates VN verticals.

## Critical Assessment

**Novelty (high for enterprise agent governance).** First claimed combination of operational envelope + ontology-derived scenarios + machine-verifiable trust certificate with regulated-industry pilot and tri-model replication. Distinct from A-Sim/Lyzr persona matrices (behavioral diversity) by optimizing **regulatory coverage** (RC metric).

**Rigor (moderate).** Strong experimental structure (blocked design, primary-source ground truth, fault injection, cross-model). Weaknesses: LLM-as-judge construct validity; author-designed checklist weakens E1; Bonferroni leaves G4>G1/G3 RC non-significant; FDR null undermines fault-oriented claims; small Kendall's W; illustrative verdict thresholds uncalibrated to incident rates.

**Reproducibility (fair-to-good).** Preprint v2 (4 Jun 2026); analysis code/ontologies on GitHub [52]; raw scenarios/judge logs "upon acceptance"; disaggregated seen-vs-unseen RC deferred to v0.2.

**Significance (high for regulated enterprise AI).** Directly addresses Vietnam 2025 AI Law high-risk financial AI compliance timeline; offers deployable vocabulary (envelope, certificate, simulation gate) bridging LLM benchmarks and safety-critical software V&V culture.

**Red flags:**
- "First to combine three components" is author-claimed; overlaps with sandbox/red-team literature in spirit
- G4 RAG parity on RC challenges how much **ontology structure** buys beyond retrieval
- Claude-only coverage-precision tradeoff does not generalize
- No end-to-end agent pass-rate deployment outcomes in pilot (scenario generation evaluation, not full agent certification runs)

## Strengths

- Coherent three-layer framework (specify → generate → attest) with clear governance mapping (OWASP, NIST, Vietnam AI Law)
- Rigorous comparison ladder G1–G4 isolating persona vs RAG vs structured ontology
- Cross-model replication undermines "Claude knows regulations" confound
- Honest hypothesis reporting (H2 fail, H1 partial, VN banking G3>RAG cautionary tale)
- Five-level verification spectrum gives adoption path from simulation to formal methods
- Production-motivated opening (AML sanctions liability) grounds the problem

## Limitations

- **Ontology completeness:** Absent regulations → systematic blind spots; ongoing curation burden
- **Judge/generator bias:** Claude Sonnet 4 fixed judge; same-author ontology/checklist; inter-rater triangulation future work
- **Statistical vs formal assurance:** Pilot is V1 simulation; no agent satisfied θhigh; PBMC/runtime V2–V3 proposed not demonstrated at scale
- **FDR null:** Ontology does not improve fault detection vs baselines; Claude execution FDR lowest for G4
- **RC vs RAG:** +3.7pp G4–G3 not significant—structured ontology value may concentrate in ISS/regime-complexity niches
- **Implementation gap:** Algorithm 1 full automation vs current "template-based with manual curation" per Table 9 footnote
- **Compositional multi-agent:** A2A team verification deferred (Section 4.4)

## Relevance

与「企业 LLM Agent 安全评估、部署前治理、监管合规测试、本体/RAG 场景生成」高度相关：若你关心 **上线前** 如何用可审计证书替代仅靠 prompt guardrail 与事后监控，该文给出 AOE + Trust Certificate 的形式化路径，并以美越四行业实证量化 persona/RAG/本体三种生成策略的差异；对纯模型 benchmark 或单轮 red-team 的直接价值有限，但对 **regulated workflow agents**（金融 AML、保险核保、医疗分诊）的 assurance engineering 是重要参考。
