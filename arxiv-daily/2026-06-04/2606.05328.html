Results & Highlights

  • **Pairwise** protocol (Garrido et al., 2025): diffusion models **always** beat V-JEPA and VideoMAE on both benchmarks.
  • Physical signal is **constructed by the DiT**, not inherited from structured input latents.
  • Best per-video accuracy **decreases** as DiT **hidden dimension** grows (inverse trend across three architectures).
  • **WAN** is smallest yet highest probe accuracy—suggestive that **narrower** models prioritize spatiotemporal semantics over texture, **concentrating** physical signal (authors note multi-factor confound).
  • **Heun vs. Euler** at 20 steps: more accurate integrator **partially recovers** physical decodability → signal tracks **discretized ODE path fidelity**, not endpoint alone.

Strengths

  • **Elegant access mechanism:** Reverse flow integration unlocks real-video internal trajectories without retraining.
  • **Clear negative control:** VAE-at-chance decisively separates input vs. computation locus of physical signal.
  • **Multi-faceted evidence:** Probing grid + interventions + continuous regression + integrator ablation tell a coherent story.
  • **Timely question:** Directly addresses whether scaling **generative** video models implicitly learns physical structure.

Limitations

  • Reverse trajectory is **approximate**; reconstruction fidelity ≠ trajectory fidelity for physics readout.
  • **No** demonstration that steering internal states improves **generated** physical correctness.
  • Intervention study emphasized on **WAN** (180 scenes); patterns differ by model—full causal map incomplete.
  • Benchmarks focus on **intuitive physics** violations and 2D ball trajectories—not full 3D rigid-body or long-horizon scenes.

TL;DR

By **reverse-integrating** the learned velocity field from clean video latents to noise, the authors probe internal DiT states of WAN, CogVideoX, and LTX on real videos with known physical plausibility. **Linear probes** on intermediate transformer blocks reach **~81.27%** average per-video accuracy on IntPhys + InfLevel—**outperforming** V-JEPA 2 ViT-L (71.36%) and VideoMAE-Large—while **VAE latents alone stay at chance** (48–53%). Physical signal is **sustained along the reverse trajectory**, peaks at **intermediate depth** for probing, encodes **quantitative** initial position/velocity (R² up to 1.00), and **depends on trajectory fidelity** (coarse Euler steps can reconstruct faithful video yet collapse probe accuracy). **Block noise interventions** show causal influence is **distributed** and does not coincide with maximal linear decodability layers.
