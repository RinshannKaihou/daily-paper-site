> **一句话总结：** 本文通过对真实视频做**反向流匹配采样**，在视频扩散模型的 DiT 中间态上探测物理合理性：在 IntPhys / InfLevel 上平均约 **81.27%** 准确率，优于 V-JEPA 2 与 VideoMAE；该信号**不出现在 VAE 潜空间**，而在去噪 Transformer 内部涌现，且可通过块级干预与连续物理量回归进一步验证——表明生成式去噪可能**副产品式**编码物理结构，即便生成结果仍违反物理定律。

> **TL;DR:** By **reverse-integrating** the learned velocity field from clean video latents to noise, the authors probe internal DiT states of WAN, CogVideoX, and LTX on real videos with known physical plausibility. **Linear probes** on intermediate transformer blocks reach **~81.27%** average per-video accuracy on IntPhys + InfLevel—**outperforming** V-JEPA 2 ViT-L (71.36%) and VideoMAE-Large—while **VAE latents alone stay at chance** (48–53%). Physical signal is **sustained along the reverse trajectory**, peaks at **intermediate depth** for probing, encodes **quantitative** initial position/velocity (R² up to 1.00), and **depends on trajectory fidelity** (coarse Euler steps can reconstruct faithful video yet collapse probe accuracy). **Block noise interventions** show causal influence is **distributed** and does not coincide with maximal linear decodability layers. Smaller / more compressed DiTs show **stronger** binary physical readout.

## 核心贡献

- **反向采样探测框架**：从干净视频潜变量出发，沿学习到的速度场**反向积分**至噪声（K=100 步），恢复近似潜轨迹并记录各 Transformer 块、各时间步的隐状态与注意力，使扩散模型可在**真实视频**上被探测（此前难以获得与真实样本对应的内部轨迹）。
- **物理合理性可线性解码且强于专用表征模型**：在 IntPhys 与 InfLevel 上，对 WAN-1.3B、CogVideoX-2B、LTX-2B 的中间态训练线性探针；在反向轨迹中点（t=0.5）跨块平均，**三模型均超越** V-JEPA 2 ViT-L 与 VideoMAE-Large；两基准各类别平均 **WAN 达 81.27%**，V-JEPA 为 71.36%；**成对**（plausible/implausible 同上下文）设定下扩散模型亦一致领先。
- **信号来源定位：非 VAE、在去噪 Transformer 内涌现**：直接探测 VAE 潜变量为**随机水平**；t-SNE 显示合理/不合理视频在 VAE 潜空间不可分。物理信号由扩散模型在**流运输**过程中构建，而非自监督预测目标塑造的输入表征——与 V-JEPA 类结论**性质不同**（表征非训练目标，而是生成的副产品）。
- **深度、时间与因果结构刻画**：全轨迹×全块网格显示除 t=0 外最佳块准确率常 **>76%**（WAN 峰值 **88.9%**），信号贯穿近噪声至近数据；**探针峰值在中层**（约块 15–25），与 V-JEPA 的“涌现区”一致。块级**加噪干预**（probe-surprise Δ_b）表明**因果敏感层≠最易解码层**——早期块扰动对 WAN/CogVideoX 影响大，LTX 呈双峰（早+晚）。
- **超越二分类：连续物理量与轨迹保真度**：在 Kang et al. 抛物运动模拟数据上，线性回归从单块预测初始位置 x₀、速度 v₀，三模型 x₀ **R²≥0.99**，v₀ **0.88–1.00**；**更窄的 WAN** 二分类最强但 v₀ 回归略弱。K 步数实验：20 步仍可视觉重建，探针准确率从 0.82 跌至 **0.57**——物理信息依附**连续 ODE 轨迹**而非端点；Heun 积分优于 Euler，支持该解释。

## Key Contributions

- **Reverse-sampling probing pipeline** for flow-based video diffusion: backward integration from clean latents through the learned velocity field recovers approximate internal trajectories on **real** videos, enabling block- and timestep-resolved analysis without generative rollouts alone.
- **Strong linear decodability of physical plausibility** on IntPhys and InfLevel across WAN-1.3B, CogVideoX-2B, and LTX-2B, with **~81.27%** pooled per-video accuracy (WAN) vs. **71.36%** for V-JEPA 2 ViT-L and lower VideoMAE-Large scores—despite diffusion models trained for **generation**, not predictive representation learning.
- **Emergence inside the DiT, not the VAE**: VAE latents probe at chance; physical structure appears during denoising transport, qualitatively distinct from V-JEPA-style predictive embeddings.
- **Spatiotemporal and causal characterization**: sustained decodability along reverse noise levels; intermediate-depth probing peaks; **block interventions** reveal distributed causal roles that **misalign** with maximal linear-readout layers.
- **Quantitative physics and trajectory-fidelity evidence**: regresses simulator ground-truth x₀, v₀; shows coarse discretization can preserve reconstruction while destroying physical decodability—physics as a property of the **flow path**, not merely the endpoint.

## Methodology

**Preliminaries (flow matching).** Models learn velocity field v_θ(z, t) transporting noise to data. Forward sampling integrates from z_0 ~ noise to z_1 (clean latent). Videos are encoded by standard reconstruction VAEs (not physics-aware).

**Reverse sampling (§3.1).** Given clean latent Z_1 from a real video, integrate v_θ **backward** in time to recover Z_0 and intermediate states {Z_t}. Cost matches one forward pass per step. Resampling Z_0 forward reproduces the video with minor artifacts. Approximation error bounded in Appendix A.

**Linear probing (§4).** At each inversion step and transformer block, extract hidden states; train **linear probes** (Alain & Bengio, 2018) for plausible vs. implausible labels. **60/40** train/test split, 5 seeds, per-video classification (harder than pairwise). Report held-out accuracy; map depth × noise grid.

**Block interventions (§3.2).** Inspired by causal tracing / activation patching: replace block output h with h̃ = h + α·σ(h)⊙ε, ε~N(0,I), applied at **every** denoising step. **Probe surprise** ψ_s(V) = logit_implausible − logit_plausible; aggregate ψ̄(V). **Δ_b = ψ̄(V_b) − ψ̄(V_base)** measures causal contribution of block b.

**Models & benchmarks.** Backbones: **WAN** [Wan et al., 2025], **LTX** [HaCohen et al., 2026], **CogVideoX** [Hong et al., 2022; Yang et al., 2024]—three latent video diffusion families. Benchmarks: **IntPhys** [Riochet et al., 2018], **InfLevel** [Weihs et al., 2022] (violation-of-expectation pairs); **Kang et al., 2025** 2D physics simulator for x₀, v₀ regression. Baselines: **V-JEPA 2 ViT-L**, **VideoMAE-Large**.

## Results & Highlights

### Main accuracy comparison (IntPhys + InfLevel, per-video, t=0.5)

| Model / baseline | Role | Highlight |
|------------------|------|-----------|
| **WAN-1.3B** | Diffusion | **81.27%** average across categories |
| **CogVideoX-2B, LTX-2B** | Diffusion | Exceed V-JEPA / VideoMAE per category |
| **V-JEPA 2 ViT-L** | Representation | **71.36%** average |
| **VideoMAE-Large** | Representation | Lower than diffusion family |
| **VAE latents (avg)** | Input | **48–53%** (chance) |

- **Pairwise** protocol (Garrido et al., 2025): diffusion models **always** beat V-JEPA and VideoMAE on both benchmarks.
- Physical signal is **constructed by the DiT**, not inherited from structured input latents.

### Where the signal lives (Figure 4)

| Dimension | Finding |
|-----------|---------|
| **Noise level** | Sustained >76% best-block accuracy for almost all t except t=0; not confined to near-clean endpoint |
| **Depth** | Best blocks cluster in **middle third** (~blocks 15–25 WAN/CogVideoX; slightly later LTX) |
| **vs. V-JEPA** | Similar intermediate-depth emergence under same probing protocol |

### Causal localization (Figure 5, WAN on IntPhys, 180 scenes)

| Model | Intervention pattern |
|-------|----------------------|
| **WAN-1.3B** | Broad sensitivity; **early blocks 0–8** peak (blocks 3–5) |
| **CogVideoX-2B** | Sharp **early-layer** dominance, partial late recovery |
| **LTX-2B** | **Bimodal**: early + late high sensitivity, weaker mid-depth |

**Key insight:** Layers easiest to **probe** (mid-depth) ≠ layers whose corruption most **changes** generated plausibility (often early)—physical structure may be **established early** and **read out** at intermediate depth.

### Capacity & compression (Figure 7, §4.3)

- Best per-video accuracy **decreases** as DiT **hidden dimension** grows (inverse trend across three architectures).
- **WAN** is smallest yet highest probe accuracy—suggestive that **narrower** models prioritize spatiotemporal semantics over texture, **concentrating** physical signal (authors note multi-factor confound).

### Quantitative physics (Figure 6, §4.4)

| Quantity | WAN | CogVideoX / LTX | Baselines |
|----------|-----|-----------------|-----------|
| **x₀** | R²≈1.00 | R²≥0.99 | Match V-JEPA / VideoMAE |
| **v₀** | R²=**0.88** | R²≥0.99 | WAN weaker on fine temporal detail |

### Trajectory fidelity (Figure 8–9, §4.5)

| Inversion steps (WAN) | Reconstruction | Mean probe accuracy |
|----------------------|----------------|---------------------|
| **K=20** | Visually faithful | **~0.57** (near chance) |
| **K=40+** | — | Most recovery toward asymptote |
| **K=100** | — | **~0.82** |

- **Heun vs. Euler** at 20 steps: more accurate integrator **partially recovers** physical decodability → signal tracks **discretized ODE path fidelity**, not endpoint alone.

## Critical Assessment

- **Novelty:** High for **world-model / video diffusion** discourse. Prior work improves **outputs** or studies **predictive encoders** (V-JEPA); this paper shows **hidden physical structure in standard generators** via invertible probing—reconciling “bad physics in generations” with “readable physics in internals.”
- **Rigor:** Solid multi-model, multi-benchmark design; per-video (hard) and pairwise (comparable) evaluations; intervention + regression + discretization ablations. Linear probes limit claims to **decodability**, not explicit law representation (acknowledged).
- **Reproducibility:** Code link in abstract; three public backbones and standard benchmarks; probing protocol specified (60/40 split, K=100 default).
- **Significance:** Relevant to **robotics/planning simulators** built on video diffusion (Sora/Veo/Cosmos narrative): internal states may support **physics-aware guidance** or latent world models even when pixels fail. `filtered_papers.json` relevance_score: **3** (representation-level analysis of generative models).
- **Caveats:**
  - **Approximate reverse sampling**—fine conclusions may depend on discretization (Appendix A bounds).
  - **Linear probes** ≠ mechanistic physics modules; correlational with partial causal evidence.
  - **Probe-surprise metric** may inherit classifier bias.
  - **Three architectures**—capacity–dimension trend is suggestive, not isolated.
  - Does not prove models **use** internal physics for faithful **generation**.

## Strengths

- **Elegant access mechanism:** Reverse flow integration unlocks real-video internal trajectories without retraining.
- **Clear negative control:** VAE-at-chance decisively separates input vs. computation locus of physical signal.
- **Multi-faceted evidence:** Probing grid + interventions + continuous regression + integrator ablation tell a coherent story.
- **Timely question:** Directly addresses whether scaling **generative** video models implicitly learns physical structure.
- **Cross-architecture generality:** WAN, CogVideoX, LTX cover distinct DiT families with consistent trends.

## Limitations

- Reverse trajectory is **approximate**; reconstruction fidelity ≠ trajectory fidelity for physics readout.
- **No** demonstration that steering internal states improves **generated** physical correctness.
- Intervention study emphasized on **WAN** (180 scenes); patterns differ by model—full causal map incomplete.
- Benchmarks focus on **intuitive physics** violations and 2D ball trajectories—not full 3D rigid-body or long-horizon scenes.
- **Internet-trained** models + synthetic test sets—ecological validity for deployment simulators remains open.

## Relevance

与 **世界模型 / 视频生成 / 具身智能** 相关：在“生成视频物理不真实”的批评之外，本文表明扩散 Transformer **内部**可能已编码物理合理性甚至连续动力学参数，为 **physics-aware guidance**、基于生成模型潜空间的规划，以及理解“生成目标 vs. 涌现表征”提供了实证基础；对关注 **多模态基础模型是否具备隐式物理推理** 的读者具有参考价值。

## Questions for Further Exploration

- Can **activation steering** on mid-depth blocks that encode physics improve **forward** generation of mechanically correct scenes?
- Does **fine-tuning** with physics losses **align** internal probes with output quality, or erase emergent structure?
- How do **text-conditioned** video diffusion models compare when probing **counterfactual** prompts with controlled physics violations?
- Can reverse sampling scale to **long-horizon** models (Sora-class) with memory-efficient integrators?
- Do **attention maps** (also recorded) localize object-level cues better than hidden states for solidity/contact violations?
- Is the **inverse hidden-dimension trend** causal—e.g., via width pruning/distillation experiments on a single architecture?
