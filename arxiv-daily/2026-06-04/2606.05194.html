Critical assessment

Strengths

  • Five methods × four dataset paradigms converge
  • Causal patching, not attribution alone
  • Turn-boundary collapse as mechanistic story
  • 30-model coherence contextualizes failures

Novelty

  • First causal subgraph for dimensional time horizon
  • Probing–steering dissociation reported honestly
  • Runtime control framing for alignment

Read before citing

  • Single model family (4B instruct) — no scale/family sweep in main text
  • Financial/investment prompt dominance; agent generalization untested
  • Subgraph not exclusive — meta-cognitive reliability co-decoded >95%
  • Linear CAA exceeds local linearity of curved manifold at high |α|
  • Frontier coherence may be heuristic — fails at longer horizons (authors note)
