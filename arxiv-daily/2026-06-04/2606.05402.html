ReasoningFlow — sub-clause discourse DAGs for LRM reasoning traces

KEY FINDING: 79.6% of erroneous reasoning steps never reach the final answer (unused branches); only 14.4% faithfully propagate errors. Different LRM families share near-identical trace structure (QwQ↔DeepSeek-R1 JS-Div 0.010) while mechanistic attention masks barely predict discourse edges (AUC 0.549).

Scale
247.7k annotated steps · 1,260 traces · Krippendorff α > 0.88 (manual)
Why this matters

First sub-sentence node+edge schema with IAA—infrastructure for PRMs, CoT monitoring, and faithfulness audits beyond surface answers.

Critical edge

Auto EDC F1 0.646 · no closed-source LRMs · DAG edges ≠ logical entailment · API-cost pipeline.

PRM implication

Step-level error labels alone mislead—reward models need discourse reachability, not binary step correctness.

Finding 3 — Errors weakly cause wrong answers (§7)

79.6% Unused errors
6.0% Neglected (wrong premise, right answer)
14.4% Faithful error propagation

Policy implications

  • PRM / step judges must use downstream DAG reachability
  • Deleting unused-error steps may not change answers
  • Best-of-N filtering on step errors over-penalizes
  • 6% neglected: models recover despite bad premises
A wrong step is not a wrong answer—most erroneous reasoning lives on dead branches the model never commits to. — Challenges step-level supervision orthodoxy (§7)

Finding 4 — Mechanistic ≠ discourse (§8)

0.549 Thought Anchors AUC
0.531 Recent-K baseline AUC
0.545 Best F1 (mechanistic)

Interpretation

  • Mask step sᵢ → measure KL on sⱼ tokens (Thought Anchors)
  • Predict ReasoningFlow edges barely above recency heuristic
  • Two notions of «causal» must not be conflated
  • Benchmark for aligning computation ↔ surface semantics

Critical assessment

Strengths

  • Sub-sentence + 14 edges + validated IAA (unique in Table 1)
  • 1,260 traces · three domains · five open LRMs
  • Error taxonomy with graph-theoretic grounding
  • Direct Thought Anchors comparison · open source
  • Local verification & reflection signals quantified

Engineering value

  • PRM training with discourse reachability labels
  • CoT monitoring dashboards (reflect sentiment)
  • Faithfulness audits beyond final-answer match
  • relevance_score: 4 · complements LLM-judge robustness work

Critical edge — read before citing

  • Auto EDC F1 0.646—edge errors may bias triplet & propagation stats
  • No full human audit of all 1.2k auto traces
  • No o1 / Gemini / Claude—structure similarity claims bounded
  • Gemini API pipeline costly; no released small annotator weights
  • DAG edges = discourse dependency, not strict logical entailment
  • No end-to-end PRM improvement experiment in paper
