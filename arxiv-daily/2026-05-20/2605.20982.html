Why This Matters

Mock benchmarks mislead interconnect design

Synthetic dispatch patterns fabricate a “bigger batch → worse imbalance” trend that completely vanishes with real text. Published AlltoAll proposals built on mock data are optimizing for a phantom workload.

  • MLA mock Gini rises 0.33 → 0.62 as GBS grows; wikitext Gini stays flat at 0.39–0.43
  • Nemotron shows a 47× gap in Dirichlet α between mock and real data
  • Architecture class, not EP degree or batch size, is the correct workload input for dispatch design
Critical Edge

Not yet validated at scale

The EP-invariance claim stops at EP = 32 (8 nodes). Production MoE training runs at EP = 256–4096. Whether the flat max/mean holds across multi-rail InfiniBand or Slingspot fabrics is an open question.

  • All five models use aux-loss balancing; aux-loss-free routing (DeepSeek-V3) is out of scope
  • Three 128-expert models lack EP = 4 data points (GPU memory)
  • Gini-vs-P99 correlation validated only at EP = 16
