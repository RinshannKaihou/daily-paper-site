> **一句话总结：** 提出一种在单一冻结检查点上、既无需优化器下降、也无需将死方向对齐到规范基的"脱规范"测量方法,通过方向 Fisher 速率(directional-Fisher rate)在优化器留下的任意基中以 log-log 斜率精确读出每方向 KL 阶数 k,导出每方向学习系数 λ_dir = 1/(2k),并把每个方向分类为"真实奇点(阶数由激活/深度固定)"或"平坦规范对称",再由 per-direction order 通过 typed intersection 组装回全局学习系数 (λ, m)。

> **TL;DR:** A single-checkpoint, descent-free, alignment-free read that recovers the KL order k of every dead direction of a trained network from the directional-Fisher rate alone, in whatever basis the optimizer left. The same read classifies each direction as a genuine singularity (an activation node-death with k = activation order, a depth/determinantal singularity with k = L) or a flat gauge (LayerNorm-kernel, attention rotations, cross-entropy shift, ReLU rescaling), with the magnitude floor breaking the curved-gauge-versus-finite-order tie the slope cannot. A pluggable detector (K-FAC A ⊗ G, convolutional spatial-patch covariance, algebraic LayerNorm kernel) supplies directions across architectures. Empirically: k̂ = {2.00, 3.00, 4.00} on planted activation node-deaths; k̂ = {3.00, 4.02, 5.07} on trained deep-linear networks (k = L); on a from-scratch ImageNet-100 ViT a pruned MLP reads as a node-death at the activation order whether the death is axis-aligned or rotated; on a DINOv2 fine-tuned ViT the dead structure is entirely a gauge (LayerNorm-kernel + QK / VO rotations). Per-direction orders assemble into the closed-form (λ, m) on twelve enumerable cells; the order also fixes the universal singular fluctuation ν(k), but a trained network's realized ν drops below ν(k) by a live-structure-overlap-dependent amount. The read runs in one forward+backward pass per block, no descent, no alignment.

## 核心贡献

- **冻结检查点、无下降、无规范对齐的读法**:把 Shirodkar (2026b) 的方向 Fisher 速率结果的两条前置——"下降"与"沿同一坐标"——一并移除。读法不进入训练轨迹,只需扫描一个综合位移 θ₀ + t u 并将速率的 log-log 斜率回归为 k,基就是优化器留下的那个。
- **每方向阶数 k 的精确恢复 → 学习系数 1/(2k)**:方向 Fisher 速率是"主不变量(master invariant)",沿死方向以 t^{2(k−1)} 衰减;log-log 斜率 2(k−1) 直接给出 k。每方向 λ_dir = 1/(2k) 由此精确推得。
- **真实奇点 vs 平坦规范对称的分类器**:同一读法既求 k 又给分类。有限 k 是"真奇点"(激活节点死亡 k=激活局部分析阶、深度塌陷 k=网络深度 L、单位重合分裂 k=2 由曲率定);Fisher 钉死在 floor 的是架构性规范对称(LayerNorm 核、QK 旋转、VO 旋转、cross-entropy 平移、ReLU 单边 rescaling),它给"附加到 multiplicity"但不携带有限阶。
- **量级判据解决斜率平局**:弯曲规范轨道读其切线斜率看起来像 2 —— 与 k=2 节点死亡不易分——只有 floor 深度把它们分开。这是量级判据(magnitude criterion)的关键。
- **可插拔检测器**:通用层由 K-FAC 因子对 A ⊗ G 通过"激活–梯度对偶"任一侧定位死方向;卷积层把输入因子替换为 spatial-patch 协方差;LayerNorm transformer 的死方向是 γ^{-1}/‖γ^{-1}‖ 的封闭形式核,无需扫描。读法下游不变。
- **跨架构实证**:节点死亡在 gelu / ReLU / squared-ReLU / cubed-ReLU、线性 MLP 与卷积通道上都恢复架构预测阶;deep linear network 的 k = L ∈ {3,4,5} 被逐个恢复(3.00, 4.02, 5.07);from-scratch ViT 的"被压缩 MLP"被读为 squared-ReLU 节点死亡 k=3、gelu 节点死亡 k≈2,在 wd ∈ {0.1, 0.2} 与三个种子上稳定;fine-tuned DINOv2 上的死结构全部识别为 LayerNorm-kernel 与 QK/VO 规范。
- **全局 (λ, m) 的 deterministic 装配**:在奇异结构可枚举的位置上,通过 transversal/separable/tangency/determinantal 四类交集的"类型化交集"装配,12 个 analytic cell 与 Aoyagi 的闭式解达到机器精度一致。
- **与 Watanabe 三元组的对接**:从每方向 k 直接得到局部 λ_dir;从 k 推出 ν(k) 的"通用"值(ν(2)≈0.173、ν(3)≈0.278);但训练网络的"实测 ν"低于 ν(k),因为活跃结构吸收了死方向的数据涨落;在"单一显著 locus"假设下从主导结构恢复 multiplicity m。
- **廉价 + 可在训练中追踪**:每个 block 一次 forward+backward,无采样、无校准,可在训练步上追踪 singular 结构(规范从初始化就在,节点死亡只在压缩期出现);既可作为训练诊断,也可作为对 sampling-based 学习系数估计(SGLD-LLC)的可解释分解。

## Key Contributions

- **Descent-free, alignment-free singular-structure read** at a single frozen checkpoint, removing the two preconditions of Shirodkar (2026b): canonical alignment (the dead direction sitting on the same coordinate at every layer) and descent under a theorem-compatible optimizer. The read synthesizes a displacement θ₀ + t u out from the checkpoint and takes the order from the log-log slope 2(k−1) of the directional Fisher; no optimization trajectory enters.
- **Exact per-direction order k and per-direction learning coefficient λ_dir = 1/(2k)**: the directional-Fisher rate is the *master invariant*; k follows directly from the slope, the per-direction coefficient in closed form. The squared-ReLU dead unit in Figure 2(a) returns slope 4 → k = 3 → λ_dir = 1/6; an axis-aligned gelu death returns k = 2; a depth-L linear collapse returns k = L.
- **Built-in classifier for genuine singularities vs flat gauges**: a finite recovered order means a *genuine degeneracy* the architecture fixes (a node-death at the activation's analytic order, a depth singularity at depth L, a unit-overlap split at k = 2 set by the curvature φ″); a directional Fisher pinned to the floor means a *flat gauge* (LayerNorm-kernel, attention QK rotation, attention VO rotation, cross-entropy shift, ReLU rescaling) that contributes to the architectural multiplicity without an order. The *magnitude* criterion breaks the curved-gauge-tangent / k=2-degeneracy tie that the slope alone cannot.
- **Pluggable, architecture-aware direction detector**: generic layers via K-FAC A ⊗ G under the activation–gradient duality; convolutional layers via spatial-patch covariance in place of A; LayerNorm transformer via the algebraic closed-form kernel u★ = γ^{-1} / ‖γ^{-1}‖ (no scan needed). The downstream read stays fixed.
- **Purity-matched window selector**: a self-tuning window (highest r² admissible at r² > 0.95) keeps a single clean power-law segment of the scan, isolating the t^{2(k−1)} regime above the contamination scale set by the cosine gap to the true direction. The selector fits the slope alone; the architecture-predicted k is then checked against the recovered value.
- **Construe-don't-search construction**: the order-carrying joint mode sits above the Fisher floor (which holds the (L−1)d² within-layer reparametrisation gauge orbit and, at n/d < 1, a sampling null). Searching the spectrum's bottom returns gauge or null directions; the paper constructs the joint mode from the factors and seats the scan on the order-carrying mode.
- **Cross-architecture empirical validation** (Table 2 summary): node-deaths (MLP, grokking, ViT, CNN), depth/determinantal singularities (deep linear), convolutional channel-deaths, unit overlaps, all detected at r² = 1.000 with the architecture-predicted k; LayerNorm-kernel, QK rotation, VO rotation, ReLU rescaling, and cross-entropy shift, all detected and classified as flat gauges. Two rows stay at "attempted" (attention-head death; MoE expert death) — no model in reach formed a clean instance.
- **Global (λ, m) assembly to machine precision** on 12 enumerable cells — normal crossings, separable sums, their composites, and a deep-linear scalar at depths 2–5 — matching Aoyagi's closed form. The blind assembly breaks only on wide matrix deep linear networks (determinantal locus); a structured resolution rule recovers the exact coefficient on all five determinantal cells at depth L ≤ 3 and a general exact resolver remains an open problem.
- **Map into the Watanabe triple (λ, ν, m)**: ν(k) is fixed by the order (universal, data-fluctuation-absorption-free), confirmed by direct functional-variance measurement on the canonical order-k cell y = a·s^k + ε to within a few percent of 0.173 (k=2) and 0.278 (k=3) across n ∈ [500, 4000]; a trained network's realized ν sits *below* ν(k) by an amount set by the dead–live basis overlap (ρ_eff ≈ 0.62–0.81 in the trained ViTs). m recovers from the dominant structure under a single-dominant-locus assumption.
- **Co-statement with sampling-based estimators**: the read is set alongside SGLD-LLC as a *deterministic, sampling-free, descent-free* counterpart; returns per-direction structure a single sampled scalar localizes at most to a parameter subset, with a Spearman ρ ≈ 0.82 cross-tracker (curvature-weighted effective K-FAC dimension d_eff(γ)) on a seven-optimizer cohort.

## Methodology

The reading procedure is a two-stage pipeline — a **detect** stage that names a candidate dead direction u from architecture-aware detectors, and a **read** stage that scans the directional Fisher along that u and takes k from the slope. Both halves are designed so neither assumes canonical alignment nor descent.

- **Direction detection.** For a generic y = W x layer the Fisher factorises as F_W ≈ A ⊗ G with A = E[x x⊤], G = E[g g⊤] (Martens & Grosse, 2015). The duality λ_min(A^ℓ)·λ_min(G^ℓ) = Θ(t^{2(L−1)}) (Shirodkar, 2026b, Thm. 3) keeps either factor informative wherever the other fails (A_1 stays full rank at the input; G_L goes flat at the output), so the detector reads the dead coordinate from whichever factor separates it more cleanly. Per-layer coordinates map through the layer Jacobian to fill any layers neither resolves, then assemble into the cross-layer joint mode u. Convolutional layers replace A with spatial-patch covariance (Grosse & Martens, 2016). LayerNorm transformer layers use the closed-form kernel u★ = γ^{-1}/‖γ^{-1}‖ of the per-channel gain. Attention rotations (QK, VO) and the cross-entropy shift live on their respective architectural objects; each requires its own detector and slots into the pipeline without touching the read.
- **Fisher estimation.** Both factors are accumulated as the *true* Fisher by label-resampled Monte-Carlo estimates (true-MC in the tables). The empirical Fisher (built from data labels) carries the model's fit error — g vanishes only at a perfect fit — so resampling labels from the model's own predictive distribution keeps λ_min and the effective rank geometric quantities.
- **Directional Fisher scan and order read.** The synthetic displacement θ₀ + t u is evaluated along u for a swept t, the directional Fisher u⊤F(θ(t))u plotted on log-log axes, and k = 1 + α/2 read from the slope α = 2(k−1). A purity-matched window — the highest-r² admissible window (r² > 0.95) — isolates the clean t^{2(k−1)} regime above the contamination scale set by the cosine gap to the true direction. Diagnoses: a finite slope with finite Fisher magnitude = genuine singularity; a flat scan at deep floor = gauge; a curved-gauge orbit read along its tangent rises with slope 2 (separated from k = 2 only by floor depth); slope shallower than predicted = contaminated scan; slope steeper than predicted = crossing of loci; unsettled slope = pre-asymptotic — the read returns the diagnosis, never a wrong order.
- **Global (λ, m) assembly.** Where the singular structure is enumerable, per-direction orders assemble via the typed intersection of the loci — transversal (sum contributions then take minimum), separable, tangency, determinantal — into (λ, m) reproducing Aoyagi's closed form.
- **Mapping to the singular fluctuation ν.** The order fixes ν(k) universally. On the isolated order-k cell y = a·s^k + ε the functional-variance estimator V_n = Σᵢ Var_post[log p(yᵢ|s)] (calibrated against the regular anchor where it returns ν = d/2 on a d-parameter linear-Gaussian model) recovers ν(k) within a few percent. In a trained network, a controlled cell y = b·g(x) + s^k·c(x) + ε shows the dead-direction contribution collapsing from ν(k) toward zero as the dead basis aligns with the live one — the live-structure-overlap absorption that suppresses a trained net's realized ν below ν(k).

## Results & Highlights

- **Constructed cells (Table 2)** — Recovery is at machine precision: a planted gelu / squared-ReLU / cubed-ReLU node-death in a small linear MLP reads k̂ = 2.00 / 3.00 / 4.00 at r² = 1.000; a constructed unit-overlap reads k = 2 on the split direction (curvature) while the transfer gauge reads flat; a constructed cross-entropy-shift gauge reads flat with a directional Fisher > 20 orders of magnitude below a live bias direction.
- **Trained deep linear networks** — At L ∈ {3, 4, 5}, k̂ = 3.00, 4.02, 5.07 at r² = 1.000. k = 5 is unreachable from any activation node-death, so the read is tracking the depth-induced order, not an artefact.
- **Trained convolutional networks** — Convolutional channel-death detection via spatial-patch-covariance factor: trained wide CNN reads k = 2 (ReLU, gelu) and k = 3 (squared-ReLU) across three seeds; the nominated channel-direction is usually a rotated combination of channels, so the trained convolutional read is itself off-canonical.
- **Grokking transformers** — A matched pair of gelu / squared-ReLU grokking transformers (Power et al., 2022; Nanda et al., 2023) trained on modular addition: squared-ReLU's dead subspace is axis-aligned → k = 3 along the axis; gelu's dead subspace is rotated off the axes → a per-coordinate scan returns the deviant 1.29, the off-canonical read recovers k = 2 at r² = 1.000.
- **From-scratch ViT on ImageNet-100 (Table 3)** — 6-block ViT (width 256, MLP hidden 1024, patch 16, 112×112) on 100 classes, two weight-decay strengths × two activations × three seeds: squared-ReLU k̂ = 3.00 (coord-conc. 0.07 / 0.34, dead-subspace dim. 4 / 2); gelu k̂ ≈ 2 (coord-conc. 0.59 / 0.76, dead-subspace dim. 14 / 100). The activation order survives a rotation in basis.
- **Fine-tuned DINOv2 (CIFAR-100)** — No node-death forms (smallest combined unit norm 0.44–0.73 of block median across all twelve blocks); every block's dead structure is the LayerNorm-kernel u★ aligned with the post-LayerNorm smallest-eigenvalue direction at |cos| = 1.0000 (Fisher floor 10⁴–5·10⁴ below live); QK rotation gauge placed 10⁶ below live; VO rotation gauge rises with slope 2 (curved orbit) but sits four orders of magnitude below a live direction. Projecting activations onto u★ gives std ≈ 8×10⁻¹⁶ about a LayerNorm-bias-set mean — the directional Fisher is that constant offset carried through the nonlinearity.
- **Optimizer axis (Table 4, Table 5)** — On a fixed deterministic squared-ReLU teacher–student cell the off-canonical read returns k ≈ 3 (asymptotic, r² = 1.0) under SGD, Adam, AdamW, RMSProp at three seeds; the optimizer sets the structure the order occupies (axis-aligned or rotated, single joint-zero or distributed near-kernel). A noisy teacher target separates the optimizers: SGD forms a clean k=3 death at every noise level, Adam holds the clean axis death across σ_train ∈ {0, 0.1, 0.25, 0.5, 1.0}, RMSProp keeps a near-kernel, AdamW breaks (deviant order read) once the target is noisy. The empirical-over-true-MC bottom-eigenvalue ratio grows to 94 (AdamW) and 100 (RMSProp) as noise rises — the inflation the true-MC geometry sees through.
- **Grokking-run optimizer contrast (Figure 4)** — Vanilla Muon (textbook NS5) leaves dead structure diffuse, axis-alignment falling 0.71 → 0.40 through depth; the scan returns deviant pre-asymptotic values, r² below the admissibility threshold — the read finds no order because the optimizer leaves no order to find. The DDCMuon scaled-polar orthogonaliser (with or without gauge projection) compresses the network into a clean dead subspace: alignment > 0.99 with k = 3 (squared-ReLU, on axis) or k = 2 (gelu, rotated), readable off-canonical.
- **Global assembly** — Twelve enumerable cells reproduce closed-form (λ, m) to machine precision under sum-versus-crossing; the blind assembly breaks only on wide-matrix deep linear networks (determinantal with crossings rotated off every coordinate).
- **ν(k) verification** — On the canonical order-k cell y = a·s^k + ε, the functional-variance ν̂ recovers ν(2) and ν(3) within a few percent across n ∈ [500, 4000] (data-averaged over 300 draws): 0.167–0.178 at k = 2, 0.275–0.289 at k = 3. Calibrated against the regular anchor where it returns ν = d/2 on a d-parameter linear-Gaussian model. In the controlled overlap cell, the dead-direction contribution to ν holds at ν(k) while bases stay distinct and collapses as ρ = corr(g, c) grows; trained over-parametrised networks sit at ρ_eff ∈ [0.62, 0.81] — inside the suppression band.
- **Wall-clock vs sampling** — Geometry read is one forward+backward pass per block, no calibration; SGLD-LLC needs a per-model calibration sweep to a stability plateau (full head-to-head in Appendix D.1).
- **Cross-optimizer separator (Figure 6)** — The cheap one-pass dead-unit census estimate λ̂ = N_total/2 − N_dead(1/2 − 1/(2k)) separates the gauge-fixed DDC family from vanilla-Muon on the d8 modular-addition cohort (pooled Spearman ρ = 0.71 over the seven converged models, ρ = 0.66 over eleven checkpoints); 17–106 dead directions in DDC vs 508–721 in vanilla-Muon — 0.1%–4.4% of N_total, so λ̂ barely moves off N_total/2, separating families but not within-run checkpoints.
- **Curvature-weighted effective dimension d_eff(γ) = 1/2 · Σᵢ λᵢ/(λᵢ + γ)** tracks SGLD-LLC at Spearman ρ = 0.82 on MLP, 0.79 on attention across a seven-optimizer width-128 cohort; bare rank / dead-unit count does not (ρ = 0.29 on the same cohort).
- **Developmental tracking (Appendix B.5)** — Read across training steps on the same ViT: dead-subspace dimension grows 188 → 562 while axis-alignment climbs 0.78 → 0.999, with k = 3 steady throughout; gauge directions are present from initialisation (fixed by the architecture); node-deaths emerge only as the network compresses — a regime boundary the read exposes but the loss curve does not.

## Critical Assessment

- **Novelty.** Strong and operationally meaningful. Turning Shirodkar (2026b)'s rate result into a single-checkpoint, descent-free, alignment-free measurement that *also* classifies genuine singularity vs gauge (with a documented magnitude criterion) closes a real gap. The result is, in effect, a free diagnostic on trained checkpoints; this is a measurement-methodology paper rather than an empirical-finding paper, which sets the novelty bar appropriately.
- **Rigor.** Exact-form claims are made in the abstract and held in the body: per-direction 1/(2k) is derived from the master-invariant rate (the rate itself is a theorem, Shirodkar 2026b Thm. 3); the global λ assembly reproduces Aoyagi's closed form to machine precision on 12 enumerable cells; ν(k) is confirmed by a calibration against the regular anchor (ν = d/2 on a d-parameter linear-Gaussian model). Two opacity points: (i) the typed-intersection assembly rule is stated but not formally pinned to the proof apparatus of resolution of singularities (Hironaka, 1964), and (ii) the r² > 0.95 admissibility gate is a convention, not a principled threshold tied to a noise model.
- **Reproducibility.** High for the constructed-cell and analytic-cell claims; documented in dedicated appendices (Appendix A.2 pipeline, A.1 setup, B.2 node-deaths, B.3 convolutional, B.4 ViT, C.1 optimizer, D.1 head-to-head). Trained-network experiments list architectures, optimizers, seeds, decay strengths; the ViT-imagenet-100 setup is supplied. The DINOv2 fine-tuning protocol and the modular-addition cohort are re-used from companion papers (Shirodkar 2026a; Shirodkar & Narayanan 2026b). The paper does not surface a public code repo from the abstract.
- **Significance.** Direct utility for the user's three intersecting interests:
  - *Hidden states & interpretability* — the read decomposes a single learning-coefficient scalar into per-direction typed structure: a per-block fingerprint of orders, gauge counts, and effective Fisher rank.
  - *LLM training stability* — a developmental tracker that separates accumulation vs compression by whether the read returns *no order* or a clean k, naming which optimizer / orthogonaliser leaves a clean dead subspace (DDCMuon) and which leaves it diffuse (vanilla Muon at deep widths).
  - *Inference evaluation* — the read sees whether a frozen checkpoint carries singular structure at all, and whether it lives at k = 2, 3, or depth-L — a per-checkpoint quality dimension not exposed by validation-loss curves.
  - *Computation reliability & precision errors* — the σ_min magnitude floor (8×10⁻¹⁶ on DINOv2 activations on u★) and the asymptoticity / floor rejection both connect directly to numerical-precision regimes; on a quantized or mixed-precision model the read's admissibility gate becomes a precision-aware diagnostic.
  - The singular-fluctuation ν(k) ↔ realized ν gap also gives a quantitative hook between Bayesian generalization theory and trained-network behaviour.
- **Red flags.** (i) Single-author, single-programme context — broad claims should continue to be cross-checked externally. (ii) The "magnitude criterion" for the curved-gauge vs k = 2 tie is empirical (a floor-depth cut), not a theorem. (iii) "Realized ν below ν(k) by live-structure absorption" is convincingly demonstrated but not inverted — the order alone does not recover the realized ν, and what additional information would close the gap is not pinned down. (iv) The read is conditional on a *detector* having nominated a direction; uniform-random direction selection gives vanishing overlap with the order-carrying mode, so the read cannot discover structure on its own. (v) The blind global assembly still fails on the wide-matrix determinantal locus (rotated crossings); a general exact resolver for arbitrary determinantal varieties is explicitly noted as open. (vi) On standard language-model pretraining "the read characterises its compressed checkpoints and reports the absence of singular structure on the rest" — i.e., most LLM-pretraining checkpoints are *outside* the read's applicability window, a scope fact that should constrain overclaim.

## Strengths

- One frozen-checkpoint, no descent, no alignment diagnostic — operational cost is small (one forward+backward per block, no sampling, no calibration) and so applicable at every training checkpoint, not only at converged ones.
- The same instrument *both* recovers the order *and* classifies the direction — a single directional-Fisher scan returns the verdict rather than routing two diagnostic streams.
- Pluggable detector generalizes the read across transformer, convolutional, and normalization layers without touching the downstream read.
- Empirical coverage is broad for a single paper: taxonomically (activation / depth / overlap / convolution); architecturally (MLP, ViT-scratch, ViT-fine-tune, DINOv2, deep linear, CNN); across optimizers (Muon, DDCMuon, SGD, Adam, AdamW, RMSProp); across training phases (accumulation vs compression).
- Outputs that interface with established theory — exact per-direction k, exact per-direction 1/(2k), ν(k) from order, m under single-locus, global (λ, m) by typed intersection to machine precision on 12 analytic cells.
- Built-in honesty about what it cannot do: the read is detector-conditional; the assembly rule has a known blind spot on rotated determinantal crossings; ν(k) is universal while realized ν is structure-suppressed.

## Limitations

- **Detector-conditional.** The read sees only the fraction of a real model's singular structure its detector family surfaces; an "unattributed remainder" (K-FAC factors see but no named detector types) bounds the deadness map. Attention-head death and MoE expert death are explicitly listed at "attempted" — no clean instance in the models tried.
- **Optimizer-dependent legibility.** Vanilla Muon on a deep grokking transformer leaves the dead structure too diffuse to carry a clean order; the read returns deviant pre-asymptotic values below the admissibility gate. The absence of singular structure on a checkpoint is a real answer (the read characterises that) but not a guaranteed outcome of "run the read on this checkpoint".
- **Single-dominant-locus assumption for m.** Multi-locus / interacting cases under-count; recovering the exact multiplicity where the dominant threshold splits across several equal-coefficient loci is left to enumeration.
- **σ_min magnitude in deeply singular checkpoints.** Bottom singular values approach the precision floor as the sample variance grows; the read rejects once it reaches the floor, so the absolute magnitude is lost at deep checkpoints (the rank-level reading and the per-block effective rank hold, but σ_min itself does not).
- **Realized ν requires more than order.** A trained network's realized ν < ν(k) by an amount set by the live-structure absorption (ρ_eff ≈ 0.62–0.81 on the trained ViTs); reconstructing the realized ν from k alone is an open problem in the paper.
- **Blind global assembly.** Fails on wide-matrix deep linear networks whose product-zero locus is determinantal and rotated off every coordinate; the structured resolution rule recovers exact coefficients for depth L ≤ 3 and a general exact resolver is on the open-problems register.
- **Sample-count regime.** The read needs n/d ≳ 64; at d = 24 this is met (n/d ≈ 100); at d = 8 it is at the edge (n/d ≈ 64) and the orders still hold at r² = 1.000, but the regime is not the high-n asymptotic.
- **Scope to language-model pretraining.** Explicit claim: standard LM pretraining sits in the accumulation phase and the read characterises its compressed checkpoints and the absence of singular structure on the rest — i.e., typical "epoch X" LM checkpoints carry no dead direction to read.
- **Walker parameters and parameter-tying across layers.** The behaviour under weight sharing (tied embeddings, grouped convs, LoRA, mixed-precision artefacts) is not addressed and is a real concern for the user's *computation reliability* interest.

## Relevance

与用户的几条兴趣高度相关:(1) Hidden States & Interpretability —— 给出一种按方向、按层、按训练阶段分解学习系数的 deterministic 解读,可替代"单标量 SGLD-LLC"作为细粒度的复杂度指纹;(2) LLM Training Stability —— developmental tracking 直接区分 accumulation(无 node-death)与 compression(节点死亡涌现)两阶段,并指出 vanilla Muon 在深度 transformer 上会让奇异结构过于弥散而不可读,是诊断优化器 / orthogonaliser 选择的有力工具;(3) Inference Evaluation —— 在冻结检查点上量化"该检查点是否压缩到携带 singular 结构"这一通常只由 loss 曲线间接反映的几何状态;(4) Computation Reliability & Precision Errors —— σ_min 的精度地板、r² > 0.95 准入门槛、以及 σ_min ≈ 8×10⁻¹⁶ 的实测,直接挂到量化 / 混合精度 / 浮点误差的研究上。论文在 Resource / Compute 上也明确标准 LM pretraining 的多数检查点都"无结构可读",这一 scope 边界对可靠性研究是重要提示。

## Questions for Further Exploration

- **Numerical-precision regime.** What does the read report on a LoRA-perturbed, INT8-quantized, or BF16 checkpointed model? Does the σ_min-precision floor rejection become a useful precision-error detector (e.g., flagging where quantization has eaten a node-death)?
- **Programmable detector registry.** Can the detector catalogue (K-FAC factors, spatial-patch covariance, LayerNorm kernel, QK/VO rotations, cross-entropy shift) be decomposed into a typed interface so that new architectures (state-space models, retrieval-augmented layers, MoE routing) drop in without re-validating the read?
- **Multi-locus global assembly.** General exact resolver for determinantal varieties of *arbitrary* depth — does the simplex-bound / generic-line-order bracket (Appendix D.3) extend, or does a more direct desingularisation replace it? Concretely: does a Type-II / Hironaka-style strategy work where the current blow-up stalls at the simplex bound?
- **Realized ν reconstruction.** Can the live-structure absorption factor (ρ_eff ≈ 0.62–0.81 in trained ViTs) be modelled so the realized ν is computable from (k, ρ_eff, λ_dir)? What additional read supplies ρ_eff as a first-class observable?
- **Validity under weight sharing.** How does the read behave on tied embeddings and grouped convolutions? Does the A-ℓ / G-ℓ duality still localize the dead coordinate, or does parameter tying require a different factorisation?
- **Deadness as a model-card axis.** Since the dead-subspace dimension and per-direction k are cheap to log per checkpoint, can a *reliability model card* digest be derived from them — separating "compressed + clean" from "compressed + diffuse" from "still accumulating"?
- **Attractor dynamics.** The DL claim that node-deaths emerge during compression rather than at initialization invites a longitudinal study: does the *time of death* (the training step at which k becomes readable at r² > 0.95) predict downstream generalization gap or learning-rate sensitivity, and is it a leading indicator of grokking / phase transitions?
- **Cross-optimizer legibility gap.** Why does vanilla Muon (textbook NS5) leave the deep transformer's dead structure diffuse, while the DDCMuon scaled-polar orthogonaliser compresses it into a clean readable subspace? Is the legibility gap a property of the solution the optimizer reaches (and therefore reachable in weight decay strength, learning-rate schedule, spectral constraints) or intrinsic to the optimizer's update rule?
