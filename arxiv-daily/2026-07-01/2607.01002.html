Mechanistic Interpretability · 2607.01002

Ablating just 50 heads collapses NoLiMa retrieval ROUGE-L from 0.401 → 0.000 — where attention-based detectors still retain 0.292.

Logit-Contribution Scoring Identifies Non-Literal Retrieval Heads · Aryo Pradipta Gema, Beatrice Alex, Pasquale Minervini et al. · 2026-07-01
mechanistic interpretability retrieval heads attention circuits OV-circuit analysis long-context LLMs non-literal retrieval
Key Metric
0.000NoLiMa ROUGE-L · Qwen3-8B · top-50 heads ablated
From baseline 0.401 → 0.000; best attention-based baseline retains 0.292.
Why This Matters

Reframes retrieval heads as an OV-write problem instead of an attention-read problem — unlocking write-aware KV-cache eviction for long-context LLMs.

Critical Edge

Ranking is model-dependent — on Gemma-3-27B the attention-only control actually beats LOCOS at large k; a practitioner has no a-priori guarantee the rank transfers to an unseen model.

Methodology

Per-head OV logit projection, contrasted spatially

Existing attention-based retrieval-head detectors reward heads whose attended token literally matches the answer — a QK-circuit (read) criterion that misses non-literal retrieval, where the answer must be synthesized through the head's OV circuit (write).

LOCOS scores each head by projecting its OV write onto the answer token's unembedding direction, then takes a needle-vs-off-needle spatial contrast within one answer step. Negative scores are kept: a strongly negative score is itself a dissociation signal.

STEP 1
Per-position φ
φ = uyT · (α · WO vj) — projection of head write onto answer direction.
STEP 2
Spatial contrast
Φ(+) − Φ(−) within one answer step; needle vs. off-needle, length-normalized.
STEP 3
Aggregate & ablate
Mean across passing trials → S(l,h); validate via mean-ablation (Nanda et al., 2023).
S^(l,h) = (1/|A_τ|) · Σ_τ Σ_t [Φ_t^(+) − Φ_t^(−)]

Results & Highlights

Ablation is far more damaging — and retrieval-specific

Setting (Qwen3-8B, top-50 ablation) NoLiMa ROUGE-L Δ vs. baseline
LOCOS (write-aware) 0.000 −0.401
Wu / NoLiMa (baseline) 0.337 −0.064
Wu / NIAH (baseline) 0.292 −0.109
Random heads (control) ≈0.40 ≈ 0.00

NoLiMa baseline = 0.401. Random-head ablation stays within 0.358–0.402 across all 6 models.

  • Cross-family sweep. 6 models / 3 families (Qwen3 8B/14B/32B, Gemma-3 12B/27B, OLMo-3.1 32B). LOCOS hits ≈0 ROUGE-L by k=50 in 5 of 6.
  • Bottom-k control (large φ but off-needle) tracks the random curve — rules out the "scoring function is circular" objection.
  • Retrieval specificity. Dissociation Score peaks higher for LOCOS than every baseline across all six configs; parametric recall + arithmetic stay at baseline.
  • Downstream transfer. Same top-50 on Qwen3-8B: MuSiQue 0.55 → 0.08; BABILong qa2+qa3 0.62 → 0.20.
  • Known outlier: Gemma-3-27B inverts at depth (LOCOS 0.369 vs. attention 0.118 at k=20) — flagged honestly by authors.

Strengths

What holds up

  • Isolates one precise failure mode (literal-copy detectors) and proposes a clean, per-head OV fix.
  • Five-plus independent controls in one paper — including the rare bottom-k control that falsifies circularity.
  • Six model configurations across three families; not a typical "two models, one benchmark" study.
  • Clean separation between observable (φ vs. α) and aggregation (spatial vs. temporal) enables a clean ablated comparison.
  • Direct line into KV-cache eviction and long-context inference engineering (DuoAttention, PyramidKV, SnapKV).

Limitations

Where to be careful

  • Head ranking is benchmark- and model-dependent — Gemma-3-27B inverts at depth; Wu/NIAH sometimes wins on Gemma-3 & OLMo-3.1.
  • NoLiMa is the sole probing benchmark; whether LOCOS scores transfer to non-QA probing distributions is untested.
  • Off-needle baseline is a length-normalized region — heads doing diffuse contextual integration may be systematically missed.
  • No MoE, encoder-decoder, or state-space coverage; effect-size magnitudes explicitly disclaimed for those architectures.
  • ROUGE-L masks qualitative failures (hallucination, partial answers, refusal) that score non-zero on degraded output.
  • "Single forward pass" really means one forward per probing trial, not one forward total — aggregation still needs the full probe distribution.

Questions for Further Exploration

Open threads worth chasing

Does LOCOS ranking transfer to non-QA probing distributions (reading comprehension, multi-doc summarization)?
For KV-cache eviction, would per-head φ-weighted retention beat top-k binary retention at long context?
Why does LOCOS lose to Wu/NIAH on Gemma-3-27B at large k — direct-path bias or genuine OV/FFN coupling?
Can LOCOS be combined with FFN attribution (RedeEP-style) for a unified head-and-expert write-aware score?
Relationship to copy-suppression heads (McDougall et al. 2023) — same circuit on different inputs, or distinct?
At what context length does the LOCOS ranking stabilize? 1k–5k tokens is tested; 100k+ is unverified.
