Must Read · Training Stability

Stale Rollouts Sink Async RLHF — and Now There's a Law for It

Jingwei Song, Haofeng Xu, Jie Xiao et al. · arXiv 2607.01083

In asynchronous GRPO, stale rollouts inject an O(S·η) per-step bias between the surrogate and true gradients. A sweeping (S, η) grid recovers twin stability constraints — η_max ∝ 1/S and M_collapse·S·η ≈ const — giving the first complete quantitative map from training collapse to stable production RLHF.
