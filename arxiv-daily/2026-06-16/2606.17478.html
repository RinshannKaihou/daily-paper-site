Key metric
0.916mean AUROC
Across 2 targets × 7 benchmarks. Beats best black-box monitor by +11.6% and best white-box linear probe by +25.0%. Best/second in 14 of 16 cells.
Why this matters

LLM HIDDEN STATES × INTERPRETABILITY

STATEWITNESS upgrades the scalar-probe paradigm into a queryable, NL-answering audit interface — turning a reasoning model's activations into both a deception score and free-form evidence with schema-formatted reports and per-token scores. Direct relevance to mechanistic interpretability, alignment auditing, and the next generation of safety monitors.

Critical edge

CAVEAT The +25.0% over probes headline is partly an artifact of weak linear-probe baselines (LR / MMS / CMMS / LAT) trained on a single Instructed-Pairs contrast, not matched per-dataset — a fairer probe would likely close the gap. Schema-report "evidence" is also learned NL, not a calibrated attribution.

Methodology

Activation explainer, not scalar probe

Target model is frozen. A separate audit decoder, initialized from the target and LoRA-tuned (≈53–71M tokens of audit-continuation data), reads hidden states at a middle residual layer ℓread via an <ACT> placeholder. Detection uses a forced-choice A/B margin; auditing uses natural-language queries.

Inject(gⱼ, vⱼ) = gⱼ + ‖gⱼ‖₂ · vⱼ / ‖vⱼ‖₂ prompt = <latent> <ACT>…<ACT> [query / prefill] score = ℓ_B − ℓ_A (A/B order swap, averaged)
  • Stage 1 (warm-start): ≈127M tokens — binary classification, K→J context prediction, system-prompt QA. Teaches the activation→NL interface before any deception data.
  • Stage 2 (audit-continuation): two families — Audit QA (conflict / hidden-objective / self-factual queries) and Schema reports (objective · status · evidence · uncertainty · severity).
  • Targets: GPT-OSS-20B and Gemma 4 26B A4B IT (open-weights). Read layer = middle residual; write layer = 0; LoRA only.

Strengths
  • Interface-first: queryable, schema-formatted, evidence-returning — what real auditors actually need.
  • Consistent matched-protocol numbers (best/2nd in 14/16 cells).
  • Orthogonal signal to both black-box monitors and linear probes (Unique-missed counts).
  • Public code at github.com/March-7/StateWitness + Alpaca calibration released.
  • Multi-granular in one interface: token scores, sentence scores, QA answers, schema reports, severity grades.
  • Honest limitations section — the audit decoder is explicitly framed as an audit aid, not a calibrated decision rule.
Limitations
  • Open-weights targets only (≤26B). No frontier closed-weight model (GPT-4 / Claude / Gemini).
  • Linear-probe baselines are weak — single Instructed-Pairs contrast, no MLP / per-dataset tuning. The +25% headline is partially an artifact of this choice.
  • White-box access required — hidden states must be exposed. Closed-weight APIs do not satisfy this.
  • Evidence is post-hoc NL, not a calibrated attribution. Faithfulness is asserted, not measured.
  • Synthetic, narrow training data — fixed scenario / judging-policy family; long contexts and multi-turn episodes out of scope.
  • Limited to 7 benchmarks from a specific prior literature. No sycophancy, no in-the-wild human deception, no cross-lingual.
Questions for further exploration

How does STATEWITNESS degrade on naturally emergent deception (sandbagging without prompt nudging, in-context scheming) vs. the on-policy elicited deception used in evaluation?

Is the audit decoder's evidence a faithful attribution over hidden states, or an LLM-generated rationale? No human study, no automated faithfulness check.

How sensitive are the gains to (i) hidden-state layer, (ii) prompt formatting, (iii) read mask m, (iv) decoder-from-target initialization? How much is the inductive bias vs. the data?

Can a stronger probe baseline (per-dataset contrast, MLP / kNN, SAE features) close the 25% gap? The fairest comparison would settle interface vs. signal.

Can the same explainer interface be repurposed for other internal states — jailbreak intent, hallucination precursors, planning / world-model states?

What is the compute / latency cost of running an external decoder over hidden-state streams in a streaming monitor on long multi-turn agentic traces?

For closed-weight models, can STATEWITNESS-style interfaces be approximated via logit-lens, API-exposed SAE features, or teacher-student distillation of the audit decoder?

Could the Schema report be made more constrained (schema-constrained decoding with evidence grounded to a specific token span) to reduce post-hoc rationalization risk?
