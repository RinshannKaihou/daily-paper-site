Safety · Deception Auditing

A frozen target, a trained auditor, and a courtroom for hidden states.

An "activation explainer" reads a target LLM's hidden states at the final-answer layer and returns a calibrated deception score and a schema-formatted audit report — 0.916 mean AUROC across 2 targets × 7 benchmarks, 11.6% above the best black-box monitor, 25.0% above the best linear probe.

By Kexin Chen et al. · 2606.17478
把"对目标 LLM 隐藏状态的标量探针"升级为"独立审计解码器 + 自然语言报告 + 结构化 Schema"；0.916 平均 AUROC，比最强黑箱监视器高 11.6%、比最强线性探针高 25.0%。

Reasoning · Theory of RL

Why SFT can't identify, but RL can enrich.

A hierarchical-latent-selection formalism proves a hidden-support obstruction in SFT and derives the disjoint SFT/RL curriculum that makes compositional generalization work.

Theory · Attention & PE

Why RoPE strictly reduces the symmetry group.

Sinusoidal PE preserves vanilla attention's full group; RoPE shrinks it to Sh × H(dh) × GL(dh) — a principled explanation for RoPE's dominance, plus a two-stage weight-matching recipe for LMC.

Functional Equivalence in Attention · 2606.17830
