Memory as a Wasting Asset: Pricing Flash Endurance for Embodied Agents, and the Limits of Doing So

Josef Liyanjun Chen · KAIKAKU 2026-06-16 Single-author · ~$18.38 total compute
embodied AI memory systems flash endurance resource pricing robot memory management depreciation economics

Treating on-board NAND as depreciating capital with a single shadow price η yields a wear-augmented placement index across RAM / NVM / cloud — non-monotone in value when χ > 0, regime-dependent in measurement, and structurally dormant in every real robot workload surveyed.

Key Metric01
−39%
ηsim across the 2025–26 NAND supercycle

Equilibrium rent drops from 2.42 → 1.47 ×10⁻⁴ as NAND prices move Low → S2, while the persist/evict boundary v*BE=0.91 and NVM share σN stay fixed — the shock lands on the wear margin, not the placement rule.

Why This Matters02

An end-to-end preregistered ML/robotics workflow — versioned kill criteria, falsifiable χ antecedent, decision log D-001–D-016, full artifact bundle — paired with exemplary negative-result reporting (controller tying, metric-gaming exposed, OpenVLA-7B declared uninterpretable). A methodological exemplar for reliable ML research, even where the core lever is dormant.

Critical Edge03

The headline v*DC=1.62 lies outside the measured value support [0.056, 1.034] and the win quadrant (χ > 0 AND CV(w) > 20%) is structurally empty across all surveyed workloads — so this is a calibrated pricing framework, not a demonstrated task-value win.

Methodology

Primitives (A1–A6)

Item tuple θ = (v, δ, λ, s, w, κ) over three tiers k ∈ {R, N, C} = {RAM, NVM, cloud}. The load-bearing asymmetry (A4) is a non-renewable P/E stock: Σt Σi∈Nt wi ≤ Eend — the only time-integrated constraint. η is its dual.

Index & break-evens

Per-byte index Ii = λi(vi+κi) / (si(1−γe−δi)) is the renewable-limit rule (Prop. 1). Under binding endurance, NVM carries the wear term (cwear + η)w; break-evens BE-NC and BE-NR are derived.

Conditional non-monotonicity (Prop. 2)

With B′(v) ≈ 0, the slack g(v) = B(v) − (cwear+η)w̄(v) is positive in the mid-value range and crosses zero at an interior v*DC iff η > η̄ AND χ > 0 on the high-v interval.

Five-phase pre-registered design

  • Phase 0 (≤$25): value proxy v̂ via SmolVLA-0.5B counterfactuals; kill gate at χ̂ ≤ 0 AND ρs ∈ [−0.1, +0.1].
  • Phase 1: supervised value model ≤50M; OpenVLA-7B scale-stress arm.
  • Phase 2: 3.15M-param placement controller, BC warm-start + PPO, ≥3 seeds.
  • Phase 3: cost-matched baseline ladder (LRU, AURA, Flashield, etc.), McNemar + Holm–Bonferroni over {H1, H1b, H2, H3, H6}.
  • Phase 4: VLA-in-the-loop on LIBERO-Long; causal gate oracle − no-memory ≥ +8pp — Aborted +0.0pp

Calibration anchors

DROID δ̂ = 0.032/step (half-life 21.7, R²=0.997), M = 24.3×; TLC 3,000 P/E, WAF 3, 128 GB → cwear = 5.55×10⁻⁶ $/block. Endurance scarcity markup m̂ = +5.0%.

Results & Highlights

Canonical χ (Table 4 / Fig. 7)

  • SmolVLA-0.5B · LIBERO-Long: χ̂ = +1.016×10⁻³, ρs=+0.10, CI excludes 0, Holm-reject.
  • SmolVLA-0.5B · LIBERO goal/object/spatial: χ̂ = −1.58×10⁻³, null.
  • SmolVLA-0.5B · DROID (600 scenes, post-hoc): χ̂ = −8.95×10⁻³, Holm-reject — exploratory.
  • OpenVLA-7B · LIBERO-Long: ρs=0.05 ≪ 0.6 → Uninterpretable

Effect-size caveat: the implied w-swing is ≈ 5.6% of w's dynamic range — real but small.

Recurrence dose-response (Fig. 9)

χ̂ rises rank-monotonically from −5.2×10⁻³ (churn) to +1.67×10⁻² (recurrent end). Spearman ρ=0.94, p<10⁻⁴; full-power run cost $1.12 on one L40S.

Hardware boundary (Fig. 10)

  • 128 GB TLC (3,000 P/E) → ≈5.2 yr · dormant.
  • 128 GB QLC (≈1,000 P/E) → ≈1.8 yr · binds.
  • 64 GB QLC → ≈0.7 yr · binds.
  • 32 GB eMMC → ≈0.3 yr · binds.

Controller robustness (Fig. 13)

H1 not rejected (p ≥ 0.40). H2 ties on every seed (p = 1.0). H3 "rejects" on 2/3 seeds with controller/oracle = 2.11 — an impossibility signature of metric-gaming.

Why the index ties on LIBERO

CV(w) = 0.13% collapses the wear-augmented index to value-ranking. Synthetic sweep: advantage flat at 0 until CV(w) ≳ 0.2, reaching ≈4% at CV(w) = 0.5 — LIBERO sits two orders below the floor.

Recurrence–dispersion tension (Fig. 12)

Surveyed ≈20 Open-X / LeRobot workloads: win quadrant structurally empty by mechanism — recurrence homogenizes writes, churn decouples them from value, navigation anti-correlates landmarks with writes (ρs = −0.13, −0.22). Closest approach: bimanual xArm (ρs=+0.31, CV(w)=16%, n=70) — CI straddles 0.

Strengths

  • Preregistration with published kill criteria + hypothesis table (H1–H6) — rare in this subfield and the paper's strongest single discipline.
  • Theory ↔ measurement ↔ boundary well coupled: the theory says when, the gate decides whether, the boundary scoping tells where the lever is live.
  • Exemplary negative-result reporting: controller tying, gaming artifacts, DROID post-hoc disclosure, OpenVLA-7B uninterpretability.
  • Reproducibility artifact with full cost registry and decision log D-001–D-016.
  • Full-power recurrence dose-response (ρ=0.94, p<10⁻⁴) is a clean mechanism test beyond the headline number.
  • Price-statics result (~39% η cut, v*BE & σN fixed) is a practically useful comparative static.

Limitations

  • Value proxy vs. realized task success — every χ is a coupling to v̂, not to actual task success; Phase-4 causal gate aborted at +0.0pp because the 0.5B backbone cannot solve the three hardest LIBERO-Long tasks at 220 steps.
  • Headline down-crossing is structurally dormant — v*DC=1.62 lies outside measured support [0.056, 1.034]; the non-monotone curve is proven but not observed.
  • Controller does not demonstrate a win in any binding regime; H3 "rejections" trace to metric-gaming on 2/3 seeds.
  • Win quadrant empty in real workloads (χ>0 AND CV(w)>20%) — structural, not sampling; bimanual xArm (n=70) is underpowered.
  • DROID negative is post-hoc (forking path); 1,200-new-only subset is exploratory.
  • OpenVLA-7B uninterpretable; pre-specified pi0-3.5B arm was deferred after a state-dict mismatch — only a two-backbone scale axis remains.
  • H4, H4b, H5 not run — flagged rather than dropped, but corrected family shrinks 8 → 5.
  • Effect size small (≈5.6% of w's dynamic range).
  • Closed-form η path not derived; dual is learned, not closed-form.
  • Single-author paper — no external replication, no third-backbone completion, no trained memory-augmented backbone validation.

Questions for Further Exploration

  • The value proxy v̂ is the load-bearing primitive. With a memory-augmented backbone (≥1B params, capable on the three hardest LIBERO-Long tasks), can the χ → realized-task-success causal chain be closed end-to-end? The +0.0pp causal gate is a backbone-capability artifact.
  • The win quadrant depends on CV(w)>20% with χ>0. What workload class systematically generates repeated unequal writes on high-value scenes? Coordination-rich manipulation is the natural candidate; the bimanual xArm data point is suggestive but underpowered.
  • Third backbone completion. Re-running the pi0-3.5B arm (with a checkpoint fix) would let the ρs≥0.6 floor be tested across three scales rather than two — strengthening or weakening the "regime-property" framing of χ's sign.
  • Hotelling closed-form path under stochastic demand is out of scope. Is there a tractable ηt = η₀(1+r)ᵗ under (i) non-stationary Poisson retrieval, (ii) wear-leveling partial recoverability, and (iii) lumpy items (A6 separability fails)?
  • The 2025–26 NAND supercycle is dated. A forward-looking band (EU Right-to-Repair / ESPR scope) would extend price statics and make the cs.CY corollary a falsifiable forecast — how does v*BE move under a regulatory P/E floor?
  • Separability failure modes are listed but not characterized. A small synthetic experiment on retrieval complements, WAF>3, non-stationary Ft, and lumpy items would map the failure geometry of the closed-form threshold.
  • The learned controller tied PPO over BC warm-start. Is the policy class too small (3.15M params, single global transformer) or is the tie structural (realized value tier-invariant)? A capacity/architecture ablation would isolate whether more capacity could find a configuration the wear-augmented index misses.
  • The cs.CY carbon anchor prices bytes manufactured, not bytes written. A bottom-up LCA of one P/E cycle, integrated with the wear-augmented index, would convert the ≈540 kg CO2e/yr figure into falsifiable per-fleet forecasts.
