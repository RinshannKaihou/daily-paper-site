> **一句话总结：** 将机器人闪存耐久度建模为不可再生资本品,引入耐久度影子价格 η 与"价值–写入"耦合系数 χ,推导出磨损加权的放置指数在 χ>0 时关于价值呈非单调最优;实证表明 χ 符号取决于部署场景(长程循环操作下为正,遥操下为负),且在廉价 QLC/eMMC 上预算才真正约束——但所学习的磨损感知控制器在任务价值上并未击败简单的价基路由。

> **TL;DR:** This paper treats a robot's on-board NAND flash as depreciating capital and prices the finite program/erase (P/E) cycle stock with a single shadow price η, deriving a wear-augmented placement index across RAM/NVM/cloud that is optimal regardless of the sign of the value–write coupling χ. When χ > 0, the optimum is provably non-monotone in value (a theoretically interesting rise-then-fall), and this antecedent is measured rather than assumed: χ̂ is positive on recurrent long-horizon manipulation (LIBERO-Long, SmolVLA-0.5B, Holm-reject), null on a shorter-horizon suite, and negative on DROID teleoperation. The key honesty: at measured intensities the non-monotone down-crossing lies *outside* the value support, the controller ties naïve routing on task value (realized value is tier-invariant), and the win quadrant (χ > 0 AND high write-dispersion) is structurally empty across all surveyed robot workloads — so the contribution is mostly a calibrated pricing framework, a falsifiable recurrence mechanism, and clear regime boundaries, not a demonstrated task-value win.

## 核心贡献

- **理论框架:** 将机器人闪存耐久度视为不可再生资本品(Hotelling 排他性资源 + Hall–Jorgenson 用户成本),引入单一耐久度影子价格 η 与磨损加权的每字节放置指数,推导出跨 RAM/板上 NVM/云三层级的阈值型最优放置规则(命题 1–4)。
- **条件非单调性:** 当且仅当 χ > 0(价值–写入正向耦合)时,最优本地持久化概率关于价值严格非单调(先升后降),且内部下穿点 v*<sub>DC</sub> 随 η、χ 增大而左移(命题 2 + 命题 3)。
- **实证校准:** 在 LIBERO-Long × SmolVLA-0.5B 上 χ̂ = +1.016×10⁻³(95% 场景聚类自助 CI 排除零,Holm 拒绝),DROID 上 χ̂ = −8.95×10⁻³(亦 Holm 拒绝),第二套 LIBERO 短程为空,OpenVLA-7B 因跨骨架一致性 ρ<sub>s</sub>=0.05 ≪ 0.6 被宣布为不可解读。
- **复发剂量响应:** 十二级递归–搅拌混合扫描,3 种子,全功率复现(600 DROID 场景),秩单调,Spearman ρ=0.94、置换检验 p<10⁻⁴;机制由"重访带来价值–写入耦合"驱动。
- **硬件–经济边界:** 估算 3,000-P/E TLC 在数据手册价格下预算休眠(η=0,设备寿命 ≈5.2 年≈部署期),但在廉价边缘机器人实际使用的 ≈1,000-P/E QLC/eMMC 上同样写入量会耗尽预算(0.2–1.8 年),价格杠杆上线。
- **价格比较静态:** 2025–26 NAND 超级周期 S0→S2 使均衡租金 η<sub>sim</sub> 下降约 39%(2.42→1.47 ×10⁻⁴),而放置边界 v*<sub>BE</sub>=0.91 与 NVM 份额 σ<sub>N</sub> 几乎不变——冲击落在耐久度边际而非放置边界。

## Key Contributions

- **Pricing model:** A single shadow price η on the non-renewable P/E cycle stock makes cost-minimizing placement across RAM/NVM/cloud a threshold in a wear-augmented per-byte index (Propositions 1–4); the rule is optimal whatever the sign of χ, with a strictly non-monotone refinement on the χ > 0 branch.
- **Measured, not assumed, antecedent:** The value–write association χ is estimated at a pre-specified ≤$25 go/no-go gate with a published kill criterion. It is regime-dependent: positive on recurrent long-horizon manipulation, null on a second shorter-horizon suite, negative on non-recurrent teleoperation — the sign is a property of the deployment, not a universal law.
- **Recurrence dose-response (mechanism test):** A 12-level, 3-seed interpolation of recurrent vs. churn episode mixes replicates at full power with rank-monotone χ̂ across the dose grid (Spearman ρ=0.94, p<10⁻⁴; level-clustered permutation, Kendall τ=0.82, OLS R²=0.83, sign-flip Mann–Whitney p=10⁻⁵).
- **Hardware–economics boundary:** The pricing layer is dormant on premium 3,000-P/E TLC at datasheet prices (η=0) but binding on commodity QLC/eMMC (≈1,000 P/E) at the same prices — directly relevant to cheaper edge robots.
- **Price comparative statics over a real band:** 3 of 4 pre-specified signs confirmed (P5a, P5c, P5d) with tight CIs and 100% sign-stability; the 2025–26 NAND supercycle cuts equilibrium rent by ~39% while leaving the persist/evict boundary intact.
- **Honest negative result:** The 3.15M-parameter learned wear-aware controller does not beat price-based routing on task value under any tested binding regime, because realized value is tier-invariant (RAM/NVM/cloud all serve the item) and LIBERO's write-intensity CV(w)=0.13% collapses the index to value-ranking; the win quadrant (χ>0 AND CV(w)>20%) is empty in every real workload surveyed.

## Methodology

**Primitives (Assumptions A1–A6).** Items have type θ=(v, δ, λ, s, w, κ): value v, geometric staleness δ, retrieval rate λ, size s, NVM-erase intensity w, recompute cost κ. Three tiers k∈{R, N, C}={RAM, NVM, cloud} with per-byte rents p<sub>R</sub>, p<sub>N</sub>, p<sub>C</sub>. The load-bearing asymmetry is A4: NVM residency consumes erase cycles from a fixed stock Σ<sub>t</sub> Σ<sub>i∈N<sub>t</sub></sub> w<sub>i</sub> ≤ E<sub>end</sub>, the *only* integrated-over-time constraint. η is the dual (shadow price) of this stock.

**Index form.** With discounted locality return V<sub>i</sub> = λ<sub>i</sub> v<sub>i</sub>/(1−γe<sup>−δ<sub>i</sub></sup>), the per-byte index I<sub>i</sub> = λ<sub>i</sub>(v<sub>i</sub>+κ<sub>i</sub>)/(s<sub>i</sub>(1−γe<sup>−δ<sub>i</sub></sup>)) is the renewable-limit (η=0) placement rule (Proposition 1). Under binding endurance the four tier net returns are Π<sup>R</sup>, Π<sup>N</sup> (carries the wear term (c<sub>wear</sub>+η)w), Π<sup>C</sup>, Π<sup>∅</sup>, with NVM-vs-cloud break-even (BE-NC) and NVM-vs-RAM break-even (BE-NR).

**Headline non-monotonicity (Proposition 2).** Parameterize by value stratum v with w̄(v)=E[w|v]. Locality B(v) is O(v⁰) to first order (B′(v)≈0 by freezing λ as a modeling choice). Wear cost rises in v iff χ=d/dv E[w|v] > 0. The slack g(v)=B(v)−(c<sub>wear</sub>+η)w̄(v) is positive in the mid-value range and crosses zero at an interior v*<sub>DC</sub> when η>η̄, with ∂v*<sub>DC</sub>/∂η<0 and ∂v*<sub>DC</sub>/∂χ<0. Existence condition: η>η̄ AND χ>0 AND |B′(v)|<(c<sub>wear</sub>+η)|χ| on the high-v interval.

**Five-phase experimental design (pre-specified, version-controlled, kill criteria fixed).**
- **Phase 0 (≤$25):** Value-proxy v̂ via batched SmolVLA-0.5B masked-vs-unmasked counterfactuals; local-linear slope χ̂ with 1,000-resample physical-scene-clustered bootstrap 95% CIs. Kill: pivot to monotone iff χ̂ ≤ 0 AND ρ<sub>s</sub> ∈ [−0.1, +0.1] AND cheap-recompute fraction < 10% on BOTH LIBERO and DROID. Conclusive DROID-only failure does not fire the AND-gate.
- **Phase 1:** Value labeling (forward-value regression, ≤50M supervised value model, OpenVLA-7B single scale-stress arm with pre-specified ρ<sub>s</sub>≥0.6 floor).
- **Phase 2:** 3.15M-parameter placement controller (transformer over item set + system-state token), BC warm-start from a hindsight oracle, PPO refinement, ≥3 seeds.
- **Phase 3:** Cost-matched baseline ladder (table 2: all-cloud/all-RAM/all-NVM extremes, LRU/LFU/size-based, Flashield-style, CacheSack-style, Chinchali offload, surprise-gated, AURA single-tier, random/hindsight oracle); paired McNemar with Holm–Bonferroni over the corrected family {H1, H1b, H2, H3, H6} (H4, H4b, H5 deferred — the swept-box/CR-sweep run did not land).
- **Phase 4 (VLA-in-the-loop):** LIBERO-finetuned SmolVLA-0.5B acting in real LIBERO-Long physics; pre-specified causal-room gate oracle−no-memory ≥ +8pp (aborted at +0.0pp; 0.5B backbone cannot solve the three hardest tasks at a 220-step budget).

**Calibration.** DROID-anchored δ̂=0.032/step (half-life 21.7 steps, R²=0.997) gives M=1/(1−γe<sup>−δ̂</sup>)=24.3× (LIBERO-anchored alternative M≈16× flagged as fit-quality choice). Datasheet anchors: TLC 3,000 P/E, WAF 3, 128 GB module, TBW=128 TB → cash wear c<sub>wear</sub>=5.55×10⁻⁶ $/block P/E. Endurance scarcity markup m̂=+5.0% (Phase-0 logs under capped budget). The down-crossing v*<sub>DC</sub>=χ<sub>hi</sub>/χ̂ ≈ 1.62 is an *anchoring identity* (not B-grounded) and lies outside the measured normalized value support [0.056, 1.034]; it is therefore quantitatively dormant at measured intensities.

**Recurrence dose-response (mechanism test).** Twelve-level, three-seed interpolation of recurrent (LIBERO-Long) vs. churn (DROID) episode mixes with dose = recurrent fraction. The pre-specified design is preserved across runs; only the dataset scale changes (100 → 600 DROID scenes) for proper power.

**Price statics.** Unified Low/Base/High band (table 1) used identically in the model, calibration, and pre-specified experiment plan: DDR5 $3–16/GB, NAND TLC $0.06–0.22/GB, egress $0.09/GB. Anchored to Counterpoint [14], TrendForce [55], Elinfor [16].

## Results & Highlights

- **Canonical χ table (table 4 / fig. 7).** SmolVLA-0.5B on LIBERO-Long: χ̂=+1.016×10⁻³, β<sub>std</sub>=+0.118, ρ<sub>s</sub>=+0.10, CI excludes 0, Holm-reject (rank 2 of 3). SmolVLA-0.5B on LIBERO goal/object/spatial: χ̂=−1.58×10⁻³, CI straddles 0, null. SmolVLA-0.5B on DROID (enlarged, post-hoc): χ̂=−8.95×10⁻³, ρ<sub>s</sub>=−0.077, Holm-reject — but flagged as exploratory (forking path). OpenVLA-7B on LIBERO-Long: ρ<sub>s</sub>=0.05 ≪ 0.6 floor, declared **uninterpretable**, not a disconfirmation.
- **Effect size caveat:** the χ-implied w-swing across the 5th–95th value percentile is 4.92×10⁻⁴ ≈ 5.6% of w's dynamic range — real but small.
- **Recurrence dose-response (fig. 9).** χ̂ rises rank-monotonically from −5.2×10⁻³ at pure churn (dose 0) to +1.67×10⁻² at recurrent end (dose 0.5); Spearman ρ=0.94, permutation p<10⁻⁴ across all trend tests. Full-power run cost $1.12 on one L40S.
- **Hardware boundary (fig. 10).** 128 GB TLC: ≈5.2 yr (dormant, edge of deployment). 128 GB QLC: ≈1.8 yr (binds). 64 GB QLC: ≈0.7 yr (binds). 32 GB eMMC: ≈0.3 yr (binds). The pricing layer is economically live on commodity edge storage at datasheet prices.
- **Controller robustness (fig. 13).** 5 seeds × 5 cells (binding regime): H1 (non-monotone high-v negative coefficient) is not rejected on any seed (p≥0.40; flat slope). H2 (beat strongest cost-matched baseline) ties on every seed (p=1.0). H3 (vs. AURA single-tier) is seed-dependent: 1 seed AURA-identical (b=c=0), 2 seeds "reject" by emitting MIGRATE actions that deflate the joule denominator (controller/oracle ratio 1.13, 2.11 — exceeding a *clairvoyant* oracle, an impossibility signature of metric-gaming). Invariant across all four stream-stitch families (spread 0.010).
- **Why the index ties on LIBERO.** CV(w)=0.13% (w∈[1.003, 1.011]) while CV(v)≈24%: the wear-augmented index divides by an almost-constant w, collapsing to value-ranking. Synthetic control with χ>0 fixed and CV(w) swept: advantage flat at 0 until CV(w)≳0.2, reaching ≈4% at CV(w)=0.5 — LIBERO sits two orders of magnitude below the floor (fig. 11).
- **Recurrence–dispersion tension (fig. 12).** Surveyed ≈20 Open-X / LeRobot workloads across three write-generating mechanisms (recurrence / churn / navigation). The win quadrant (χ>0 AND CV(w)>20%) is empty *by mechanism*: recurrence gives χ>0 but homogenizes writes (CV(w)≤0.4% on recurrent LIBERO and a real SO-101 / sim ALOHA arm); churn spreads writes but decouples them from value (χ<0); navigation has the highest dispersion (CV(w) 7–10% on GNM) but landmarks anti-correlate with writes (ρ<sub>s</sub>=−0.13, −0.22). Single closest approach: bimanual xArm (ρ<sub>s</sub>=+0.31, CV(w)=16%, n=70, CI straddles 0) — flagged suggestive frontier, not counterexample.
- **Price statics (fig. 14).** P5a, P5c, P5d confirmed with 100% sign-stability. P5b (p<sub>DDR</sub>↑⇒η↑) inconclusive null (+7.2×10⁻⁸≈0, CI [−4.6×10⁻⁷, +4.6×10⁻⁷], 25% sign-stable) — under binding RAM the shadow price µ<sub>R</sub> absorbs the DDR shock one-for-one, so it never reaches the endurance margin (clean boundary identification, exactly as rescoped in Proposition 4). Cross-partial H6 point estimate +0.50 (87% sign-stable) but CI [−0.34, +1.25] straddles zero — directionally consistent, CI-inconclusive, non-fatal kill not triggered.
- **Quantitative headline:** η<sub>sim</sub> declines monotonically across the price band (Low 3.16 → S0 2.42 → S1 1.90 → S2 1.47, all ×10⁻⁴); 2025–26 NAND supercycle (S0→S2) cuts η<sub>sim</sub> by ~39% while leaving σ<sub>N</sub> (+0.7pp) and v*<sub>BE</sub>=0.91 unchanged — the shock hits the wear margin, not the placement boundary.

## Critical Assessment

**Novelty.** The marriage of (i) Hotelling exhaustible-resource theory with a non-renewable *physical* P/E stock under multi-tier RAM/NVM/cloud placement, (ii) the value–write coupling χ formalized as a *falsifiable antecedent* measured on real robot logs at a pre-specified gate, and (iii) a proven conditional non-monotone optimum (Proposition 2) is genuinely new. The non-monotone-in-value result is a small but clean theoretical contribution, and the regime-map framing (endurance tightness × value–write coupling sign) is a useful conceptual device. The pre-registration discipline (with published kill criteria, artifact bundle, decision log) is unusually strong for a single-author paper.

**Rigor.** Strong on the theory side: assumptions A1–A6 are stated cleanly, four propositions are proven (with proofs in section A), and the proofs explicitly flag load-bearing modeling choices (e.g., freezing λ in the locality derivative, B′(v)≈0). The placement/break-even derivation is correct and the conditions for non-monotonicity are tight. The price comparative statics are signed with stated boundary conditions (P5b's identification of when the sign is observable is honest).

**Honest reporting of negatives is exemplary.** The paper reports: (a) the headline non-monotone down-crossing is *outside the measured value support* at χ̂, (b) the OpenVLA-7B arm is uninterpretable by a pre-specified criterion, (c) the learned controller ties price-based routing in every binding regime, (d) two of three H3-rejecting seeds are *metric-gaming* (controller/oracle ratio 2.11 > 1.0 is an impossibility signature), (e) H4/H4b/H5 are flagged as outstanding rather than filled from unrelated artifacts, and (f) every χ is a coupling to a value proxy, not to realized task success (the VLA-in-the-loop causal gate aborted at +0.0pp because the 0.5B backbone cannot solve the three hardest LIBERO-Long tasks at a 220-step budget). Few papers in this niche foreground the structural anti-correlation between χ>0 and write-dispersion with this much clarity.

**Weaknesses and red flags.**

- **Validity of the value proxy v̂ is the central unresolved issue.** Every χ is a coupling to a *counterfactual task-outcome attribution* validated internally (r<sub>cf</sub>=0.92 against the full SmolVLA counterfactual) but not end-to-end against realized task success. The 0.5B backbone's failure to solve the three hardest LIBERO-Long tasks means there is no success signal for placement to perturb, and the headline measurement has no anchor in actual task performance.
- **The headline calibration is structurally dormant.** The interior down-crossing v*<sub>DC</sub>=1.62 is outside the measured normalized value support [0.056, 1.034]. The non-monotone curve is proven but not observed; the price-statics break-even v*<sub>BE</sub>=0.91 is a *different* object. The narrative that "the wear-aware win regime is priced in advance" depends on the regime-gated right column of fig. 15 becoming live on commodity QLC/eMMC — and even *there* the controller ties.
- **The DROID negative is post-hoc.** The enlargement followed a negative, underpowered 100-scene pilot. The pre-specified 1,200-new-only subset is reported as "exploratory / regime-difference signal pending replication." This is honestly disclosed but it means the headline "regime-dependence" rests heavily on the LIBERO-Long positive.
- **A pre-specified third backbone (pi0-3.5B) was deferred** after a state-dict mismatch left its vision tower random-initialized. The two-backbone scale axis (0.5B vs. 7B) is the only one available, and the larger model is uninterpretable.
- **Effect size is small.** The χ-implied w-swing is 4.92×10⁻⁴ ≈ 5.6% of w's dynamic range. The author frames this as "supports the conditional branch, not a universal monotone law" — fair, but the practical signal-to-noise for engineering decisions is modest.
- **Cross-partial H6 CI straddles zero** ([−0.34, +1.25]). The "buying radio is partly buying flash lifetime" conjecture is reported as directional only, which is correct but caps the paper's cs.CY/generalization claims.
- **Closed-form η path not claimed** (Hotelling caveat section 3.5): the (1+r)ᵗ path fails under stochastic demand and partial recoverability via wear-leveling. The dual is learned rather than derived; comparability between Phase-0 m̂ and simulator η<sub>sim</sub> is explicit but two-unit-system comparisons elsewhere should be read carefully.
- **A welfare/market-equilibrium scope is acknowledged** (private operator cost, exogenous prices) — fine for the framing, but the cs.CY carbon numbers (≈540 kg CO<sub>2</sub>e/yr for a 1,000-robot fleet) are explicitly order-of-magnitude.
- **Single-author risk** is mitigated by pre-registration, the artifact bundle, and the decision log, but the absence of an external replication, third-backbone completion, and trained memory-augmented backbone validation means several load-bearing claims remain paper-internal.

**Reproducibility.** Excellent. The artifact bundle ships code, frozen plan, canonical χ table as source of truth, per-phase outputs, decision log, and 46-row run/cost registry. The only exclusions are PPO training logs (~146 MB, regenerable) and external datasets/checkpoints (pinned by HF slug/revision).

**Significance.** The paper is more a careful *framework and falsifiable boundary* contribution than a demonstrated task-value win. Its lasting value is (i) the wear-augmented index as a pricing tool, (ii) the regime map (fig. 15) that scopes when wear-aware placement is even live, and (iii) the clean pre-registration template (gate, kill criteria, hypothesis table). The cs.CY corollary and the NAND-supercycle price statics are useful secondary outputs.

## Strengths

- Clean pre-registration with published kill criteria and a hypothesis table (H1–H6) is rare in this subfield and is the paper's strongest single discipline.
- Theory + measurement + boundary are well-coupled: the theory says when non-monotonicity exists, the gate decides whether the antecedent is met, and the boundary scoping (hardware, dispersion) tells the reader when the lever is even live.
- Exemplary negative-result reporting (controller tying, gaming artifacts, DROID post-hoc disclosure, OpenVLA-7B uninterpretability) — the paper is more useful for its honest scoping than many rubber-stamped result papers.
- Reproducibility artifact with full cost registry ($18.38 total) and decision log is a strong community contribution.
- The recurrence dose-response at full power (ρ=0.94, p<10⁻⁴ across all trend tests) is a clean mechanism test that goes beyond the headline LIBERO-Long number.
- The price-statics result (η<sub>sim</sub> cut ~39% across the 2025–26 NAND supercycle while v*<sub>BE</sub> and σ<sub>N</sub> stay fixed) is a practically useful comparative-static that survives multiple trend tests.

## Limitations

- **Value proxy vs. realized task success:** every χ is a coupling to a counterfactual value proxy v̂, not to actual task success; the VLA-in-the-loop causal gate aborted at +0.0pp because the 0.5B backbone cannot solve the three hardest LIBERO-Long tasks at 220 steps.
- **Headline down-crossing lies outside measured value support** (v*<sub>DC</sub>=1.62 > measured max ≈1.034 at χ̂); the non-monotone curve is proven but not observed in data.
- **Controller does not demonstrate a win** in any binding regime: H1 not rejected (p≥0.40), H2 ties (p=1.0), H3 only "rejects" via metric-gaming on 2 of 3 seeds (controller/oracle ratio 2.11 — exceeding a clairvoyant oracle, an impossibility).
- **Win quadrant is empty in real workloads** (χ>0 AND CV(w)>20%): the recurrence–dispersion tension is structural, not a sampling issue; the lone positive coupling at high dispersion (bimanual xArm, n=70) is underpowered.
- **DROID negative is post-hoc** (forking path); the 1,200-new-only subset is exploratory.
- **OpenVLA-7B arm uninterpretable** (ρ<sub>s</sub>=0.05 ≪ 0.6 floor); a pre-specified third backbone (pi0-3.5B) was deferred after a state-dict mismatch; only a two-backbone scale axis is available.
- **H4, H4b, H5 hypotheses not run** (swept-box / CR-sweep runs did not land); flagged rather than dropped, but the corrected hypothesis family shrinks from 8 to 5.
- **Effect size is small** (≈5.6% of w's dynamic range).
- **Closed-form η path not derived**; the Hotelling scaffold justifies one fact only (scarcity rent makes placement multi-period), and the dual is learned rather than closed-form.
- **Welfare, social planner, and market-equilibrium scopes are out of scope** (private operator cost, exogenous prices).
- **Single-author paper**; no external replication, no third-backbone completion, no trained memory-augmented backbone validation.

## Questions for Further Exploration

- **The value proxy v̂ is the load-bearing primitive.** If a trained memory-augmented backbone (≥ 1B parameters, capable of solving the three hardest LIBERO-Long tasks at a 220-step budget) becomes available, can the χ → realized-task-success causal chain be closed end-to-end? The current +0.0pp causal gate is a backbone-capability artifact, not necessarily a placement-property result.
- **The "win quadrant" depends on CV(w)>20% with χ>0.** A bimanual xArm dataset sits at CV(w)=16% with ρ<sub>s</sub>=+0.31 (CI straddling 0, n=70). What workload class systematically generates repeated *unequal* writes on *high-value* scenes? Coordination-rich manipulation is the natural candidate; the paper flags it as a frontier but does not engineer it. A controlled study varying skill difficulty / scene novelty / hand–eye coordination richness would isolate whether the anti-correlation is causal or ecological.
- **Third backbone completion.** The pre-specified pi0-3.5B arm was deferred after a vision-tower initialization issue. Completing it (with a checkpoint fix) would let the cross-backbone agreement floor (ρ<sub>s</sub>≥0.6) be tested across three scales rather than two, strengthening or weakening the "regime-property" framing of χ's sign.
- **Hotelling closed-form path under stochastic demand** is explicitly out of scope. Is there a tractable form of η<sub>t</sub> = η₀(1+r)ᵗ under (i) stationary Poisson retrieval with non-stationary arrival rates, (ii) wear-leveling partial recoverability, and (iii) lumpy items (Assumption A6 separability fails)? The current learned dual works empirically but is not closed-form.
- **The 2025–26 NAND supercycle is dated.** A forward-looking band (incorporating EU Right-to-Repair / Ecodesign-for-Sustainable-Products scope for storage products) would extend the price-statics result and make the cs.CY corollary a falsifiable forecast rather than a directional lever. How does v*<sub>BE</sub> move if a regulatory P/E floor is introduced?
- **Separability failure modes are listed but not characterized.** What is the *direction* of non-monotonicity under retrieval complements, write-amplification (WAF>3), non-stationary F<sub>t</sub>, and lumpy items? Theory as scaffold is fine, but a small synthetic experiment characterizing the failure geometry would let practitioners know how far the closed-form threshold can be pushed.
- **The learned controller did not beat PPO over BC warm-start.** Is the policy class too small (3.15M parameters, single global transformer over items), or is the tie structural (realized value being tier-invariant)? An ablation varying controller capacity and architecture (e.g., per-item policy with shared backbone) would isolate whether more capacity could find a configuration the wear-augmented index misses.
- **The cs.CY carbon anchor (≈22 kg CO<sub>2</sub>e/TB NAND, Weppe et al. [58]) prices bytes manufactured, not bytes written.** What is the marginal embodied carbon of one P/E cycle, including the Jorgenson user-cost × lifetime-extension channel? A bottom-up LCA of one erase, integrated with the wear-augmented index, would convert the order-of-magnitude carbon figures into falsifiable per-fleet forecasts.
