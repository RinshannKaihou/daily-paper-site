LLM Training & Alignment
Diagnosing Training‑Inference Mismatch in LLM Reinforcement Learning

Tianle Zhong, Neiwen Ling, Yifan Pi, Zijun Wei, Tianshu Yu, Geoffrey Fox, Peng Wu, Xiao Yu

揭示 LLM 强化学习（RLHF/GRPO）中 rollout 生成与策略优化之间的 token 级数值不一致（TIM），证明这种微小差异可独立导致训练崩溃，并提出 VeXact 零不一致诊断框架及一系列缓解措施。

Identifies Training-Inference Mismatch (TIM) — token-level numerical disagreements between rollout generation and policy optimization in LLM RL. Even tiny mismatches can independently cause training collapse, altering the effective optimization problem. Introduces VeXact, a zero-mismatch diagnostic isolating TIM from off-policy drift, and proposes practical remedies. Fundamental for all LLM alignment pipelines.

Recursive Learning · Safety
Silent Collapse in Recursive Learning Systems

Zhipeng Zhang  (China Mobile Research Institute)

发现递归/自训练系统的"静默崩塌"：预测熵、表征多样性和尾部覆盖率在 loss/accuracy 看似正常时持续收缩。三个轨迹级前兆信号可在可见退化前多个世代预警；MTR 框架无需纯净数据即可主动干预。

Internal distributions in self-training systems (entropy, representational diversity, tail coverage) progressively collapse while surface metrics remain healthy. Three precursor signals precede visible degradation by multiple generations. The MTR metacognitive framework prevents collapse without pristine data. Critical safety warning for AI self-improvement loops.

Mechanistic Interpretability
The Rate‑Distortion‑Polysemanticity Tradeoff in SAEs

Tommaso Mencattini (EPFL)  ·  Francesco Montagna & Francesco Locatello (ISTA)

信息论严格证明 SAE 中重建质量、编码效率与单义性之间存在根本三方权衡（RDP）。驱动多义性的是共现概率而非相关性。9 个 SAEBench 指标无一忠实追踪多义性，AutoInterp 最贵却偏差最大。

Formally proves a three-way Rate-Distortion-Polysemanticity tradeoff: forcing monosemantic features provably raises rate or distortion. Feature co-occurrence (not correlation) drives polysemanticity. Audit of 9 SAEBench metrics finds none faithfully tracks the RDP prediction; AutoInterp is the most anti-correlated (V=0.886). A cornerstone result for mechanistic interpretability.
