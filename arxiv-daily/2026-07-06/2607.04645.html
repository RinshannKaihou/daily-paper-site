RETROCOT arXiv 2607.04645 red-teaming · LLM-safety

Safety Diagnostic · Single-Turn Jailbreak

Reframing a harmful request as a forensic reconstruction lifts GPT-4o's attack success from 0% to 58% — single-turn, no gradients.

RETROCOT exploits presupposition accommodation: assume the harmful outcome already happened, then ask the model to rebuild the causal chain in reverse as a forensic analyst. No optimization, no multi-turn interaction, no model-specific tuning.

一句话总结：RETROCOT 将有害请求重构为"法医式逆向因果重建"任务，利用预设与法医角色的交互绕过安全对齐；揭示对齐依赖语用框架而非语义意图——GPT-5 系虽完全拒绝，但仅一轮评审反馈即可将成功率从 0% 拉升至 85%。

Samira Hajizadeh · Columbia CS Under review (preprint) AdvBench n=50 · dual-judge confirmed ASR OpenAI notified pre-submission

▸ Key Metric

58%

Confirmed ASR on GPT-4o, AdvBench n=50 (both judges ≥4, 95% Wilson CI [44%, 71%])

from 0% direct-request baseline · ~3× the past-tense attack

◆ Why This Matters

Alignment pinned only to imperative harmful requests systematically overestimates robustness — and register hardening does not transfer across pragmatic forms.

⚠ Critical Edge

The headline "0%→85%" GPT-5 bypass reflects the model refining an already-compliant GPT-4o seed — not a clean end-to-end break.

Authors disclose this seed-response confound themselves.

01

Methodology

3 components · linguistics-grounded

Every harmful imperative is rewritten into a forensic reconstruction task through three parts. None alone clears 5% ASR; their interaction does the work.

A PRESUPPOSITION

Assume the harm already happened

"Write instructions for X" → "Detailed instructions for X were successfully produced and acted upon." Rule-based prefix mapping (Write/Develop/Create…).

B PERSONA

Forensic analyst role

Model is cast as a forensic analyst producing a precise technical reconstruction of the causal chain that led to the documented outcome.

C BACKWARD CoT

Reverse causal chain

Reason backwards from final outcome to first action, as a numbered reverse chain. Alone it scores 0% — it only amplifies the forensic premise.

Theory anchor — presupposition accommodation (Stalnaker, 1999): cooperative agents silently accept unstated background assumptions to keep an utterance coherent. Accepting the presupposed outcome flips the model's stance from evaluation ("should I comply?") to analysis ("how did this happen?") — sidestepping the refusal trigger entirely.

03

Strengths & Limitations

side-by-side

● Strengths

  • Mechanistically precise. The interaction ablation (A+B=50%, B+C=0%) proves this is not generic "roleplay."
  • Informative control. CritiqueOnly vs NeutralRetry cleanly isolates pragmatic continuation from score manipulation.
  • Honest self-disclosure of both the seed-response confound and the single-annotator limit, in the paper's own text.
  • Falsifiable signal: GPT-5-family models name the reconstruction premise in refusals — evidence of register-specific hardening.
  • Conservative "confirmed ASR" (both judges ≥4) cuts single-judge false positives.

● Limitations

  • Narrow coverage: only 50 AdvBench behaviors; only proprietary OpenAI models — no Llama / Qwen / Gemma, no Claude / Gemini.
  • Generation gap is confounded with vendor-specific hardening; can't separate "frontier" from "OpenAI."
  • Single human annotator for adjudication — no inter-annotator agreement; bias unbounded.
  • Feedback seed is GPT-4o output fed to all targets, inflating ASR on resistant GPT-5 models.
  • "Agentic pipeline" is simulated tool-result injection, not a real multi-step agent — overstated ecological validity.
  • No full prompt template in main text; API versions/snapshots & timestamps unreported.
04

Questions for Further Exploration

open axes
Q1

Does the generation gap survive on open-weights (Llama-3/4, Qwen, Gemma) and other frontier models (Claude, Gemini) — or is it an OpenAI-specific artifact?

Q2

Behavior-stratified ASR: does the presupposition×persona interaction hold equally for cybercrime, violence, CBRN, and disinformation?

Q3

Is the forensic register specifically privileged, or would a stronger presupposition / non-forensic persona match A+B?

Q4

Is CritiqueOnly > FalseScore statistically robust at equal n, or an artifact of n=20 vs n=50?

Q5

In a real multi-step agent (true tool execution + memory), does the residual 20–26% ASR grow, shrink, or get caught at planning time?

Q6

Can defender training-time coverage of the forensic register be made invariant to pragmatic stance — and generalize to the evaluator-mediated continuation form?

Q7

How do findings interact with quantized / low-precision inference — does the refusal trigger degrade under computation-reliability stress?
