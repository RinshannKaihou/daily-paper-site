Wrong Before Right: Late Rescue and Interface Failure in Aligned Language Models

By Jiaqi Deng · arXiv 2607.04640

对齐 LLM 在中层（25–90% 深度）会短暂"先选错答案"，仅靠后层纠正才输出正确结果——这一"wrong-dip"现象在 17 个模型上因果验证，能预测结构化压缩失效（3–7×）却对量化失明。

Aligned LLMs transiently commit to the wrong answer mid-layer and are rescued only by late layers — a "wrong-dip" verified across 17 models that predicts structural-compression failure (3–7×) but is blind to quantization.
