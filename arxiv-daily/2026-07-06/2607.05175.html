01 Methodology

Observation is modeled not as direct latent access but as geometry induced by a self-adjoint PSD operator Π on a Hilbert space H. The observable is the quadratic functional O(v)=⟨v,Πv⟩; the effective observable space is the quotient H/ker(Π) with semi-inner product ⟨v,w⟩_Π = ⟨v,Πw⟩.

A spectral split Π = Σλᵢ eᵢ eᵢ* partitions latent directions into strongly observable (λ≫0), weakly observable (λ≈0), and structurally unobservable (λ=0). Effective observable dimension = rank(Π).

  • Quantum measurement: ideal projectors satisfy Π²=Π; Born rule p(m)=⟨ψ|Pₘ|ψ⟩ matches the PPS functional.
  • Deep nets with linear readout: y=Wz ⇒ Π=W⊤W, a non-idempotent PSD operator.
  • Distillation: teacher/student aligned via ΦΠ_T ≈ Π_S Φ, regularized by L_PPS = ‖ΦΠ_T − Π_S Φ‖²_F added to KL.

02 Results & Highlights

Kernel-invariant
Rank-4 operator on 8-dim latent space: ker(Π) perturbations leave O(v) invariant to machine precision; range(Π) perturbations shift it ~quadratically. Operator-restricted SHAP assigns zero attribution to kernel dims by construction.
Rank-controlled
Accuracy climbs 0.61 (rank 1) → 0.94 (rank 8), then saturates at intrinsic rank r*=8. Kernel-attribution leakage drops monotonically with rank(Π).
Distillation
CIFAR-10, ResNet-18 → lightweight CNN: PPS-regularized KL (λ=0.5) shrinks the commutativity gap ‖ΦΠ_T−Π_SΦ‖²_F vs KL-only, at qualitatively comparable accuracy — no numeric delta reported.

03 Strengths

  • One geometric object — H/ker(Π) — cleanly unifies observability, distillation, and interpretability.
  • Honest self-scoping: repeatedly flags illustrative-not-benchmark intent, linear-only assumption, and structural-not-physical quantum analogy.
  • "Absence of attribution ≠ absence of latent relevance" — a sharp, demonstrated caution for the XAI community (Figure 5: a latent variable outside range(Π) gets ~zero SHAP yet shifts output).
  • L_PPS is concrete and implementable — drop-in for existing distillation pipelines.

04 Limitations

  • Linear self-adjoint PSD only. Softmax, attention outputs — the real readouts — are out of scope (sketched via Jacobian linearization).
  • Minimal empirics: 8-dim / 16-dim toys + one CIFAR-10 run, single seed (42), 10-epoch teacher. No LLMs, no ImageNet, no medical imaging.
  • No significance testing, no variance, no ablation over λ or temperature.
  • Elementary theorems (trivial kernel-orthogonality); no new operator machinery — authors concede this.
  • Code/data not yet released at submission.
