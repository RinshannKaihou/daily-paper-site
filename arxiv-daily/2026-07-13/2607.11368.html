Critical edge

Methodology — the bookkeeping device

Hardware

One host, 4× A5000 (24 GiB) in two NVLink pairs; cross-pair traffic crosses PCIe gen4. CUDA 12.4.

Two regimes

Operational single-run (SwiftServe HTTP, batch≤8, ancestral sampling) vs. controlled matched-greedy (in-process, batch 64, T=0, n=3).

Models

Llama-3.1-8B FP16 (~16 GiB) + GPTQ-INT4 (~5.7 GiB); 70B GPTQ-INT4 for the larger comparison. A fixed 10-prompt synthetic pool (~12 tok mean).

overall = runtime × kernel+quant A vLLM-FP16 intermediate cancels exactly (Eq. 5). Log-share (Eq. 6) composes to 1. Authors explicitly disclaim causal independence.

Strengths & limitations — side by side

Strengths

  • Honest about what decomposition can/can't attribute — explicitly states the identity is definitional, not evidence of factor independence (§3.9).
  • Dual reporting of operational + controlled figures answers two distinct deployment questions without overclaiming either.
  • Direct mechanism evidence — profiler traces, allreduce-call counts, NVLink-vs-PCIe control — backs qualitative conclusions.
  • Cross-model replication bounds the kernel+quant factor's model-axis variance to ≤1.46%.
  • Self-corrects an earlier overstated 70B advantage (~15% → ~7%) via a matched re-measurement.

Limitations

  • Single hardware config (4× A5000, pairwise NVLink) — no four-card switch fabric, no newer GPU generations.
  • Single quantization method (GPTQ-INT4 via Marlin); AWQ / MXFP / INT8 untested.
  • 10-prompt synthetic pool (~12 tok mean) is far from realistic serving distributions; prompt-conditional effects unbounded.
  • No factorial ablation separating batch-ceiling from sampling effects on the operational-vs-controlled gap.
  • Only two runtimes measured; two more were blocked by infrastructure incompatibilities (Appendix F).
  • Clocks unpinned, thermal state uncontrolled, idle time not enforced; no external reproduction.

Open questions for further exploration

Q1 · distribution

Does the runtime-vs-kernel split change on realistic prompts (ShareGPT, LMSys Chat 1M) with longer, more variable prefill?

Q2 · confound

Would 10.62× survive a factorial ablation isolating batch-ceiling (8→64) from sampling (greedy vs ancestral) — and which dominates?

Q3 · hardware

Does the negative TP=4 verdict reverse on a shared switch fabric, or on H100 SXM / B200 where launch overhead is a smaller fraction?

Q4 · generalization

Does the ≤1.46% kernel+quant stability hold beyond 7–8B GQA, and across AWQ / INT8 / FP8 / MXFP fused kernels?

Q5 · latency

For latency-sensitive single-request serving, at what concurrency does the shard's lower per-token latency lose to multi-instance aggregate throughput?

Q6 · method

Can the matched-intermediate method extend to SGLang / TensorRT-LLM / TGI to build a cross-runtime attribution table?
