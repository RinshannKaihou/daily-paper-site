> **一句话总结：** 本文从分析与实证两方面证明，以 token 对数概率为中心构造的鞅监控器对量化推理模型的"自信重复"失败模式本质上是盲目的，并提出一种免训练的校准 e-CUSUM 解码控制器——融合 token 不确定度与显式逐字重复信号——在 GSM8K 上将误报率从 93-95% 降至可用水平，并将 INT4 逐字退化指标下降，同时坦诚指出准确率提升（63%→69%）在 n=100 下并不显著。

> **TL;DR:** The authors prove analytically that the obvious decoding monitor for quantized reasoning models—a martingale built from centered token log-probabilities (log p(w_t)+H_t)—is mean-zero by construction under the model's own sampling law and is therefore structurally blind to confident repetition loops (silent when p≈1, log p≈0, H_t≈0). They replace it with a training-free, calibrated e-CUSUM controller that fuses token uncertainty with an explicit verbatim-repetition signal. On GSM8K with DeepSeek-R1-Distill-Qwen-1.5B, calibration at μ_0=0.41 (90th percentile of healthy FP16 traces) turns an alarm that fires on 93-95% of generations into a selective detector (INT4 precision 0.61 / recall 0.46, φ≈0.28) against a 0.38 base rate; INT4 consecutive-repetition drops 0.010→0.003 and severe loops 1%→0%, while accuracy moves 63%→69% but is statistically inconclusive (paired McNemar p=0.18, n=100) at a +28% token cost. A key honest finding: on GSM8K the dominant INT4 failure is non-termination (up to 49% of incorrect traces exhaust the budget), not verbatim looping.

## 核心贡献

- **精确的负面结果（方法学澄清）：** 形式化证明以 token 对数概率为中心、在模型自身采样律下构造的增量 D_t = log p(w_t) + H_t 是条件均值为零的鞅，因此它度量的是采样的自一致性而非轨迹健康度；在"自信重复循环"中（p≈1）该增量≈0，监控器恰好在模型自信地出错时失声，而 Azuma 带只对高熵区段敏感，即对"不确定"而非"错误"敏感。
- **退化感知告警分数：** 提出有界分数 a_t ∈ [0,1] = min(1, 0.7·r_t + 0.3·u_t)，其中 u_t 捕捉相对历史熵的尖峰、r_t 在滑动窗口内基于 n-gram 覆盖率捕捉逐字重复；r_t 在分布尖锐（H_t≈0）时仍可趋近 1，这是对数概率鞅在结构上无法提供的信号。
- **校准的 e-CUSUM 序贯检测器：** 以投注构造 E_n^raw = ∏(1+λ(a_t−μ_0)) 作为非负超鞅，在条件零假设 E[a_t|F_{t-1}]≤μ_0 下享有 Ville 不等式给出的时间一致误报保证；实际部署使用带下界的 CUSUM 对数统计量 S_n=max(0, S_{n-1}+log(1+λ(a_n−μ_0)))，因 a_t 强历史依赖，误报率以经验操作点报告而非直接由 Ville 保证给出。
- **离线校准是可用性的关键：** 将 μ_0 校准到健康 FP16 参考轨迹的 90 分位数（μ_0=0.41，取代初始未校准的 μ_0=0.15），把一个对 ~94% 生成 indiscriminately 触发的告警转化为有选择性的检测器。
- **诚实的实验登记：** 明确报告准确率提升不显著（McNemar p=0.18/0.48）、+28% token 成本，并指出在 GSM8K 上量化模型的主要失败是非终止（non-termination）而非逐字循环——与 Lotfi et al. (2026) 一致——这与方法最初的动机直觉相悖。

## Key Contributions

- **A precise negative result (methodological clarification):** Formalizes that the centered log-probability increment D_t = log p(w_t) + H_t is a mean-zero martingale under the model's own sampling law, so it measures sampling self-consistency, not trajectory health; during confident loops it is ≈0 by construction, and Azuma–Hoeffding bands react to high-entropy (uncertainty) stretches rather than to error.
- **A degeneration-aware alarm score:** A bounded score a_t ∈ [0,1] = min(1, 0.7·r_t + 0.3·u_t), where u_t flags entropy spikes relative to recent history and r_t flags verbatim repetition via sliding-window n-gram coverage; r_t approaches 1 during a loop even when the distribution is peaked and H_t≈0.
- **A calibrated e-process / e-CUSUM sequential detector:** A betting construction E_n^raw = ∏(1+λ(a_t−μ_0)) yields a Ville-valid time-uniform guarantee Pr[∃n: E_n^raw ≥ 1/δ] ≤ δ under the conditional null; the deployed CUSUM-floored statistic targets sustained rises of a_t, with false-alarm behaviour reported empirically.
- **Offline calibration as the decisive step:** Calibrating μ_0 to the 90th percentile of a_t over healthy FP16 traces (μ_0=0.41) converts an indiscriminate alarm (93–95% firing) into a selective detector.
- **An honest evaluation register:** Explicitly reports non-significant accuracy gains (McNemar p=0.18 INT4 / p=0.48 FP16), a +28% token cost, and the counter-intuitive finding that non-termination—not looping—dominates INT4 failures on GSM8K.

## Methodology

The controller is training-free and operates on the token stream of a single decoder. At each step, the decoder's logits are turned into a sampling distribution via temperature and min-p truncation (Nguyen et al., 2024); from this the controller reads the Shannon entropy H_t and, after sampling w_t, its log-probability. Everything is reduced to a bounded alarm score:

  a_t = min(1, w_rep·r_t + w_ent·u_t),  with w_rep = 0.7, w_ent = 0.3   (Eq. 1)

where u_t flags entropy spikes relative to the recent history of H_t, and r_t flags verbatim repetition via sliding-window n-gram coverage. The repetition channel r_t is what lets the monitor see confident loops that a log-probability martingale structurally cannot.

Detection uses a betting construction (Shafer, 2021). Given baseline μ_0 and betting fraction λ ∈ (0,1):

  E_n^raw = ∏_{t=1..n} (1 + λ(a_t − μ_0)),  log E_n^raw = Σ log(1 + λ(a_t − μ_0))   (Eq. 2)

Because each factor is positive and has conditional mean ≤ 1 under E[a_t|F_{t-1}] ≤ μ_0, E_n^raw is a nonnegative supermartingale and Ville's inequality gives Pr[∃n: E_n^raw ≥ 1/δ] ≤ δ (Eq. 3), valid at every token. The deployed detector uses a CUSUM-floored log statistic:

  S_0 = 0,  S_n = max(0, S_{n−1} + log(1 + λ(a_n − μ_0)))   (Eq. 4)

raising an alarm when S_n ≥ τ. The floor targets sustained rises above the healthy baseline rather than banking negative drift; because a_t is strongly history-dependent and autocorrelated, the authors treat the false-alarm rate as an empirical operating point rather than a direct consequence of Ville's inequality.

μ_0 is calibrated offline from healthy reference traces (correct, non-truncated FP16 generations): per-token a_t values are pooled and μ_0 set to the 90th percentile (μ_0 = 0.41). This is a pragmatic marginal calibration, not a certificate of the conditional bound in (3). Remediation, when S_n crosses τ, backtracks by truncating the last k tokens and rebuilding a clean KV cache, then resumes with a graded, training-free response: it widens/tightens min-p rather than inflating temperature (the earlier decoder version's temperature inflation increased randomness exactly when the model was off-track), and on repeated alarms injects a short correction marker ("Wait, let me re-check the previous step."). A budget caps interventions; normal steps use the incremental KV cache so per-token cost stays close to standard decoding. Three modules (logit→(u_t,r_t,a_t); raw product + CUSUM; intervention) are kept separate so a trace can be replayed for detector evaluation without rerunning the model.

## Results & Highlights

- **Sanity check on synthetic token streams (Figure 1, 100 streams/regime):** The log-probability martingale raises a false alarm on 63% of healthy streams (already disqualifying) and detects the confident loop only 42% of the time; the calibrated e-CUSUM detector abstains on healthy (0%) and pure-uncertain-drift (0%) streams and detects the confident loop and the realistic drift-then-collapse in 100% and 92% of constructed cases respectively. This is a mechanism check, not a competitive benchmark.
- **Main comparison (Table 1, GSM8K, DeepSeek-R1-Distill-Qwen-1.5B, n=100, 2048-token budget):** INT4 accuracy 63.0% (vanilla) → 69.0% (v3/ours); FP16 accuracy 68.0% → 72.0%. Both differences are NOT statistically significant (paired McNemar p=0.18 INT4, p=0.48 FP16); the controller fixes 10 and breaks 4 items on INT4. Token cost rises +28% and wall-clock +30%, so any accuracy claim needs a compute-matched baseline.
- **Degeneration metrics shift as intended (Table 1):** On INT4, consecutive-repetition rate falls 0.010 → 0.003 and severe loops (consec. rep. > 0.3) fall 1% → 0%; truncation 22% → 19%. The severe-loop figure rests on a single trace at n=100, so the authors report it descriptively.
- **Calibration is decisive (Figure 2):** Uncalibrated μ_0=0.15 fires on 93–95% of INT4 vanilla traces (precision at the 0.38 base rate—useless); re-scoring with calibrated μ_0=0.41 yields on INT4 precision 0.61 / recall 0.46 (φ=0.28) and on FP16 precision 0.57 / recall 0.56 (φ=0.32), against a 0.38 base rate of bad traces. The empirical false-alarm rate under calibration (≈0.20) still exceeds the nominal δ=0.05.
- **Counter-intuitive failure-mode finding (Section 6.2):** Splitting vanilla INT4 traces by outcome, consecutive repetition is 0.002 on correct answers vs 0.024 on incorrect, but truncation (non-termination) is 6% vs 49%, with incorrect answers consuming 1286 vs 828 tokens. The dominant INT4 failure on GSM8K is non-termination, not looping—verbatim loops are rare (≤4% of tokens even on failing traces)—corroborating Lotfi et al. (2026).

## Authors & Credibility

All four authors (El Hassane Ettifouri, Ayoub Belfatmi, Mahaman Sanoussi Yahaya Alassan, Walid Dahhane) are at Novelis Research, Paris, France, and provide ORCIDs. The paper is explicitly framed as "a preliminary study whose main contribution is methodological," and the authors are unusually disciplined about not overclaiming: they report non-significant p-values, an honest +28% token cost, a measured (not nominal) false-alarm rate, and a counter-intuitive empirical finding that partly contradicts their own motivating intuition. Code, calibration tools, and full traces are released with fixed seeds. The mathematical core (the martingale argument for why log p(w_t)+H_t is blind to confident loops) is elementary and verifiable.

## Critical Assessment

- **Novelty:** Genuine and specific. The negative result—that a centered token-log-probability martingale is mean-zero by construction under the model's own sampling law and therefore blind to confident loops—is a crisp, citable clarification rather than a vague "entropy doesn't work." The positive contribution (degeneration-aware a_t + calibrated e-CUSUM) combines individually standard pieces (Shannon entropy, n-gram repetition coverage, Ville-valid betting e-processes, CUSUM) in a way the authors could not find previously combined for this purpose. The closest prior work, Guan et al. (2026) reasoning-path deviation monitor, is entropy-only and only terminates the reasoning block; this work backtracks, adds the repetition channel, and calibrates an empirical operating point.
- **Rigor:** Mixed and self-aware. The martingale analysis is sound; the statistical testing is appropriate (paired McNemar for two decoders on the same items). However, the headline Ville guarantee (Eq. 3) applies only to the raw product process under the conditional null E[a_t|F_{t-1}]≤μ_0, while the actually-deployed CUSUM-floored statistic (Eq. 4) is explicitly NOT a literal e-process—the reset and the history-dependence of a_t break the supermartingale property, and the empirical false-alarm rate (≈0.20) exceeds the nominal δ=0.05. The authors flag this honestly rather than hiding it.
- **Reproducibility:** Strong on paper. Single NVIDIA RTX A4000, fixed seeds, exact configurations released, replayable traces. But it is a single-model (DeepSeek-R1-Distill-Qwen-1.5B only), single-dataset (GSM8K only), single-seed study at n=100—the accuracy claim could not survive a compute-matched comparison yet.
- **Significance:** Methodological more than empirical at this scale. The clarifying negative result about token-log-probability monitors is the durable contribution; the accuracy improvement is a suggestive trend the authors explicitly do not claim.
- **Red flags:** (i) The "Wait" correction marker injected during remediation is in direct tension with Lotfi et al. (2026), who show quantized models already over-produce exactly these markers and that penalizing them helps—whether injection aids recovery or compounds non-termination is unresolved by the current data, a tension the authors themselves flag as a priority ablation. (ii) No compute-matched baseline (best-of-n or self-consistency at the same +28% token budget) has been run, so the positive accuracy trend cannot be separated from "simply generating more tokens." (iii) The accuracy experiment is underpowered (n=100, non-significant), and the degeneration reductions rest on very small absolute counts (e.g., the 1%→0% severe-loop result is a single trace).

## Strengths

- A precise, citable, verifiable negative result about why a tempting token-level observable fails—rare in a literature full of folk remedies.
- Unusually honest reporting: non-significant p-values, the +28% token cost, the empirical (not nominal) false-alarm rate, and a finding that contradicts the paper's own motivating intuition.
- Clean ablation by construction: vanilla vs. controller share the same sampling code and differ only in whether intervention is enabled, isolating the controller's effect.
- The calibration story is itself a contribution—showing that μ_0 selection converts a 93–95% indiscriminate alarm into a selective detector is a practical lesson for anyone deploying e-process monitors.
- Released harness, calibration tools, and traces; reproducible on a single consumer GPU.

## Limitations

- Single-model, single-dataset (GSM8K), single-seed, n=100; the accuracy effect is not statistically significant and the authors do not claim it.
- The formal Ville guarantee applies only to the raw product process under the conditional null; μ_0 is marginally calibrated, not a certified conditional upper bound, and the CUSUM floor further separates the deployed statistic from a literal e-process. Restart-valid e-detector construction (Shin et al., 2024) or conditional calibration is needed for an exact statement at δ.
- No compute-matched baseline (best-of-n, self-consistency at the same token budget) has been run at scale, so the accuracy trend is confounded with extra generation.
- Degeneration reductions are small in absolute terms because verbatim loops are uncommon on GSM8K; bootstrap intervals or larger runs are needed for population-level claims.
- Generalization to harder benchmarks (MATH, AIME, GPQA) where non-termination and drift are more frequent is untested.
- The "Wait"-marker remediation may conflict with Lotfi et al. (2026)'s finding that such markers are over-produced by quantized models; a marker-free remediation is an open priority ablation.
- Two key ablations missing on real traces: entropy-only vs. repetition-only vs. combined alarms, and backtracking vs. plain termination/early-exit rules.

## Relevance

对关注推理模型量化部署与推理时（inference-time）干预的研究者高度相关：它澄清了一个常被"民间偏方"滥用的监控量（token 对数概率鞅）为何无效，并提供了一个可复现的校准 e-CUSUM 替代方案。对追踪 chain-of-thought 退化、anytime-valid 序贯检测、以及"过度思考/非终止"失败模式（Lotfi et al. 2026 同期工作）的读者也具有直接参考价值。

## Questions for Further Exploration

- Does the positive accuracy trend survive a compute-matched comparison against best-of-n or self-consistency at the same +28% token budget on GSM8K, MATH, AIME, and GPQA?
- On harder benchmarks where non-termination and drift are more frequent, does the degeneration-aware alarm a_t (and especially the repetition channel r_t) carry its weight, or does entropy alone suffice—i.e., what does the entropy-only vs. repetition-only vs. combined ablation show on real (not synthetic) traces?
- Does backtracking with KV-cache rebuild genuinely beat cheaper alternatives (plain termination of the reasoning block, early-exit rules) for the same intervention budget?
- Given Lotfi et al. (2026) show quantized models over-produce "Wait/but/alternatively" markers and benefit from penalizing them, does the controller's marker injection aid recovery or compound non-termination—and would a marker-free min-p-only or marker-penalty remediation do better?
- Can the deployed CUSUM statistic be turned into a genuinely restart-valid e-detector (Shin et al., 2024) so that the false-alarm guarantee holds at the nominal δ rather than at the empirical ≈0.20 operating point?
- Since the dominant INT4 failure is non-termination (49% of incorrect traces), would a detector and remediation explicitly targeting failure-to-commit (rather than verbatim repetition) yield larger gains than the current degeneration-focused design?
