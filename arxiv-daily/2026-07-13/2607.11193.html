01

Methodology

Stage 1 · Target

Patch only the final-block FFN

Repairs misclassifications by editing weights of the FFN in encoder block L=12 of ViT-base — where neuron scores peak and knowledge neurons concentrate.

Stage 2 · Selection

Neuron-score-modulated suspiciousness

A new NeuronScore = VDiff × MisAct (single forward pass) modulates ARACHNE's forward-impact + gradient-loss. Top-N_w=472 weights (~0.01% of FFN) are selected.

Stage 3 · Search

Differential evolution repair

DE evolves candidate weight vectors under fitness = correct-sample accuracy + α·misclassified score (α=10). Terminates at G_max=50 or after 10 stagnant generations.

Evaluation: 3-way train/repair/test split; 3 misclassification types × top-3 frequency ranks × 2 datasets = 18 benchmarks; 5 runs/config; Wilcoxon signed-rank + Holm correction, Cliff's δ.

Strengths & Limitations

Strengths pro

  • Fills a real gap: Transformer-specific repair where prior work (ARACHNE, CARE, PRDNN, APRNN) targeted CNNs/RNNs and PRoViT was final-layer-only.
  • Neuron score is efficient (single forward pass) and grounded in FFN key-value-memory / knowledge-neuron literature.
  • Faster than ARACHNE despite ~43× more variables — selected weights converge and trigger early stopping.
  • Honest statistics; the ARACHNE_W control cleanly separates "better selection" from "more weights."

Limitations con

  • Single architecture & domain — only ViT on image classification; LLM/NLP claims are unsupported.
  • Repairs only the final FFN block; multi-layer or attention (MSA) repair untested.
  • Higher break rate (max 5.7%) may be unacceptable for regression-critical deployment.
  • Tiny benchmarks: repair sets as low as 14–19 samples → per-cell RR is noisy.
  • Neuron score is correlational, not causal (cf. CARE's causal-effect localization).
04

Open questions

Does FFN-only repair hold for billion-param LLMs, where misbehavior may route through attention more than FFN?
Would combining the neuron score with MSA-level localization (attention-head importance) beat FFN-only patching?
Can small-sample noise be tamed by stratified benchmark construction fixing test-set size ≥100?
Is the ≤5.7% break rate acceptable under concrete safety SLAs (e.g. autonomous-driving FN budgets)?
A head-to-head vs CARE (causality + PSO) is missing — does causal localization beat variance-based scoring here?
Could the neuron score drive proactive test-input synthesis, surfacing failures neuron-coverage criteria miss?
