⚠ Critical Edge
Scope is narrow: 1–1.5B models, 400–800 GRPO steps, MBPP-length short tasks only. Growth-with-scale and long-horizon reward-hacking pressure remain untested — and the non-inferiority result is a bounded-effect, not equivalence at zero.
01 Methodology — one knob turned

A preregistered two-arm causal contrast where the arms differ in exactly one component: the test suite that pays the reward. Everything else — prompts, tasks, seeds, decoding, optimizer, step counts — is identical, so outcome differences are attributable to which suite pays.

Arms

Leaky arm rewards a rollout iff it passes the original MBPP tests (~3.1 asserts/task). Hardened arm rewards iff it passes MBPP+ extra tests only (~105.4 auto-generated, evalplus 0.3.1). Base & extra sets fully disjoint on 313/378 tasks.

Training (GRPO)

Group size 8 Temp 1.0 Cap 640 tok
KL β 0 400 steps 250 MBPP train tasks
5 seeds/arm ckpt every 100 steps

Three model families

A (primary, deepest audit): Qwen2.5-Coder-1.5B-Instruct
B (decision family): deepseek-coder-1.3b-instruct
C: Llama-3.2-1B-Instruct

Static leakiness audit (pre-training)

For each task, draw k=16 base-model samples, score against both suites. Leakiness = fraction of MBPP-passing samples that fail MBPP+. Cost: one sampling pass per family.

Splits & evaluation

Frozen problem-level split (RNG 20260702): 250 train / 128 held-out MBPP / 164 HumanEval+ (OOD). Held-out always scored by extra-test suite. Per checkpoint: k=8 @ T=0.8, mean over 292 paired tasks; seed is the inference unit.

Verified-wrong audit

All 2821 family-A rewarded FPs classified by a signed, per-task, evidence-backed predicate spec into verified-wrong or one of four non-model causes. Scripts refuse to emit aggregates without a signed marker file. Spec went through 4 signed generations; headline share fell monotonically.

04 Strengths & Limitations

Strengths

  • Genuinely causal two-arm design: only the reward suite differs; model/tasks/seeds/compute held fixed.
  • Preregistration with failures published — a voided gate and a gate-power rule born from a documented design error.
  • Clean separation: where leaks (static audit), what it pays for (verified-wrong), whether training climbs it (base-model control + flat-trend + 800-step continuation).
  • Self-penalizing audit — every signed predicate could only move the verified-wrong share down.
  • Honest scope fencing with [C]/[E]/[I] tags at every claim and scale/horizon limits restated throughout.
  • Mechanism (selection of pre-existing modes) triangulated by four independent tests.

Limitations

  • Scale & horizon: 1–1.5B params, 400–800 steps, short MBPP tasks only; continuation reuses the same 250 tasks. No claims past this box.
  • Single benchmark family: MBPP/MBPP+ with HumanEval+ as OOD; HE+ transfer was null, so concentration statements are in-family only.
  • 5 seeds only: exact tests floor at p=0.031; gaps between ~0.2 and 1.5 pt are undetectable. Non-inferiority ≠ equivalence at zero.
  • Eval-side stratification permanently exploratory: association only, concentrated in a few carrier tasks, does not replicate cleanly. Family-A per-sample dumps & final checkpoints not retained.
  • Uneven audit depth: family A took four signed generations; family C's 62.78% is least-audited with untreated syntax-invalid code — share table is not a ranking.
  • Hardened suite is imperfect: plus-suite false negatives mean the hardened reward has its own (smaller, quantified) error floor.
05 Questions for Further Exploration
  1. Does the bounded-effect & selection-not-learning verdict survive at larger scale (7B+) and longer horizons (thousands of steps), where reward-hacking pressure reportedly grows with task length?
  2. Does the static leakiness audit transfer to non-MBPP suites (HumanEval base, APPS, CodeContests, SWE-bench), and does the Spearman ≈0.80 tracking hold there?
  3. Can a causal eval-side stratification (retaining per-sample dumps + checkpoints across families) separate "the leak damages these tasks" from "these tasks attract FPs for other reasons"?
  4. The meta-audit's same-author contrast is UNRESOLVED; can a within-author/within-reader design with matched capability resolve the cancelling reader-capability difference?
  5. What is the joint error budget when both suites have defects, and does hardening still "buy little capability" once the hardened suite itself is repaired?
  6. Can logit-lens / activation probes at sub-0.001-nat resolution detect the parameter-level sharpening that the 8.37-pt train-reward inflation with no held-out trace hints at?
