Bangzhao Shu, Arinjay Singh, Mai ElSherief

Sparse autoencoders reveal a consistent three-phase representational flow — syntax → semantics → emotion — across Gemma-2 and Llama-3 families; Disgust lacks dedicated features; a data-efficient causal steering method needs only 5 labeled examples per emotion to improve Macro-F1 from 0.55 to 0.66.

稀疏自编码器揭示LLM情绪推理的三阶段前馈机制（语法→语义→情绪特征），发现Disgust缺乏专用表征，并提出仅需5个标注样本的数据高效因果引导方法。
