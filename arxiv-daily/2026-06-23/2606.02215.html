Better with Experience: Self-Evolving LLM Agents for Evidence-Grounded Health Community Notes

Zihang Fu, Fanxiao Li, Jianyang Gu, Haonan Wang, Preslav Nakov, Bryan Hooi, Min-Yen Kan, Jiaying Wu 2026-06-01
EvoNote self-evolving agent memory-augmented LLM health misinformation community notes MM-HEALTH CN
01 Key metric
89.6%
Pairwise win rate over human-written Community Notes on the 1.2K consensus-resolved MM-HEALTH CN set (89.4 / 89.3 / 90.1 across TEXT / IMAGE / VIDEO).
02 Why it matters
Health rumours spread in hours. By turning each correction trajectory into reusable, phase-tagged experience, EvoNote lets later similar posts inherit proven strategies — no retraining, no fresh scratchpad.
03 Sharpest critique
The automated judge is itself a GPT‑4.1 model, creating a self‑referential loop when an LLM grades another LLM on a domain it has been trained on — and the 98% annotator agreement rests on only 100 sampled comparisons.
↳ Critical Assessment · Red flags (1) & (3)
04 Methodology

Closed self-evolution, one episode at a time

Each flagged post (text / image / video) is captioned into a unified representation x̃k, then a Claim Analyzer, an evidence-acquisition loop, and a Note Writer run as one trajectory τk = (x̃k, ck, Hk, Ek, yk). A Social Utility Judge scores the trajectory on four health-communication dimensions and emits a mode uk ∈ {UNSATISFACTORY, SATISFACTORY, EXCELLENT}. A Memory Evolver distills τk into items m = (TRIGGER, ACTION STRATEGY, PHASE) and merges them into Mk = Mk−1 ∪ ΔMk. The backbone and tools never change.

τk → uk → ΔMk → τk+1
PHASE ∈ { CLAIM ANALYSIS · EVIDENCE ACQUISITION · NOTE WRITING }
backbones: MedGemma-27B · judge: GPT-4.1 · GPU: single H200

Inference-time retrieval: the Claim Analyzer and Note Writer each pull the top-n items whose TRIGGER overlaps the current query and whose PHASE matches the active stage — cheaper than retrieving full trajectories (ExpRAG) and more grounded than asking the actor to reflect on the fly (ReMem).

05 Results & highlights

Humans beat on every modality, every gate

On the 1.2K consensus-resolved set, EvoNote posts the highest H (81.17) and the highest pairwise win rate (89.59%) against the Human Baseline. Gains hold on TEXT, IMAGE, and VIDEO and on the three binary-helpfulness gates (evidence relevance R, evidence-representation correctness C, note-style helpfulness H). All memory-based agents beat one-pass CrowdNotes+; EvoNote beats both ExpRAG and ReMem.

06 Strengths
  • End-to-end self-evolution without retraining: τ → u → ΔM → τ' is interpretable, lightweight, reversible.
  • Phase-tagged memory beats whole-trajectory retrieval (ExpRAG) and on-the-fly reflection (ReMem) in the ablation.
  • 1.2K multimodal benchmark tied to real human Community Notes + crowd helpfulness — not synthetic.
  • Stronger evidence profile than baselines (multi-source rate, source quality, domain & semantic diversity).
  • Memory bank is inspectable: 4,277 items, each tied to a phase and a reusable strategy, classifiable by humans.
07 Limitations
  • Memory bank grows unbounded (4,277 items on 1.2K episodes) — decay / dedup are flagged as future work.
  • English-only, X-only — cross-platform and multilingual generalization are unproven.
  • MedGemma-27B fails on IMAGE; caption-then-LLM inherits any caption-induced information loss.
  • Self-referential judge: GPT-4.1 grades GPT-4.1-style output; 98% agreement rests on 100 samples.
  • No upstream detection: practical deployment still needs a system that decides when human review is required.
08 Questions for further exploration
  • How should the memory bank be bounded — embedding-based clustering, recency-weighted decay, or human-audited eviction — so retrieval stays tractable past tens of thousands of items?
  • Does EvoNote transfer to TikTok, Telegram, and multilingual posts without retraining, and which of the four utility dimensions need re-grounding for non-English evidence norms?
  • Could the (TRIGGER, ACTION STRATEGY, PHASE) schema be lifted to political fact-checking, financial rumour verification, or scientific retraction notices by swapping the four utility dimensions?
  • Would an independent open-weight judge — or a heterogeneous panel with disagreement-aware aggregation — materially shift the 89.6% win rate?
  • Is the IMAGE failure a backbone ceiling, or a structural choice driven by tool-use reliability rather than VLM capability?
