03 · Why this mattersRelevance

One of the rare works that fuses evaluator–solver co-evolution with the tool-integrated RL instability problem — giving a systematic fix for open-ended, ground-truth-free deep-research training.

04 · Sharpest critiqueLimits

The Meta-Harness and the final benchmark judge are both GPT-5.2 — a possible judge–harness–system co-adaptation, with no external or human cross-validation reported.

05 · Methodology 方法

Framework (Fig. 2)

A single LLM with shared parameters θ plays two roles: Evaluator samples a query-conditioned rubric (Hq, wq), performs constrained supplementary retrieval to build Dqe, then issues dimension-wise scores and citation checks; a reward verifier filters invalid rewards before they reach the solver. Solver sees q, Dqs, (Hq, wq) and rolls out K candidate reports.

Two-channel rewards

  • Solver (every step): GRPO on Rs(r) = a(r) · (1 + Σ wj sj(r)).
  • Evaluator (every step): REINFORCE on Ae(Γq) = C(Γq) − bt−1, with EMA baseline (β = 0.7) for variance reduction.

Meta-Harness (GPT-5.2, every 5 steps)

Not a reward channel — an environment shaper. It rewrites the dimension pool, the dimension scheduler (frequency / preferred / new-pool signals), and global constraints (exclusive groups, process constraints, format / claim / weight validation), preventing evaluator–solver collusion and rubric staleness.

Training knobs

VeRL · K = 8 solver rollouts · batch 64 · max response 4,096 / input 8,192 · EMA β = 0.7 · max 10 tool calls · KL regularization at the actor level · small Reddit-derived query set.

07 · Strengths

  • Diagnoses LLD collapse — a real, under-reported failure of GRPO/DPO in tool-integrated RL.
  • Shared-actor + Meta-Harness design is principled: theoretical (Prop. 1, 2) and empirical.
  • Stable gains across two agent paradigms × two base models.
  • Meta-Harness is a constraint, not a reward — clean architectural separation.
  • Data-efficient: works on a small Reddit query set.
  • Honest rollout-number ablation (K = 3 noisy, K = 8 stable, K = 10 collapses).

08 · Limitations

  • Judge–harness co-adaptation: both are GPT-5.2; no external / human cross-check.
  • Consistency reward will be hacked without the Meta-Harness — robustness hinges on the harness.
  • Readability regression admitted but not user-validated.
  • No public code/weights in the extracted portions; re-implementation is non-trivial.
  • Meta-Harness is itself an LLM (GPT-5.2), with its own biases — not a symbolic controller.
  • Consistency is a stability proxy, not a quality proxy.

09 · Open Questions

  1. How does SCORE behave when the Meta-Harness and judge are from different model families — and under human eval?
  2. Does the LLD-collapse diagnosis replicate on larger reasoning models (DeepSeek-R1, Qwen3-235B)?
  3. Clean per-component ablation: shared actor vs. consistency reward vs. Meta-Harness (with a symbolic harness)?
  4. Can cheap external verifiers (citation-presence, claim–source match) reduce superficial rubric learning?
