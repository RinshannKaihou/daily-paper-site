Why This Matters

If validated on silicon, AI-optimized silicon becomes the natural foundation for FugakuNEXT-scale HPC — reshaping hardware roadmaps and the entire double-precision software stack.

Critical Edge

All central numbers are TME projections, not silicon measurements. The paper is the analytical half of a two-part argument — β = 1 on real kernels remains empirically unproven.

Methodology · TME Model

The paper augments the classical Roofline with three emulation-specific parameters (α, β, γ) and reduces every Berkeley "dwarf" to sequences of FP8 matrix multiplies plus bounded INT32 Garner reconstruction.

Three parameters of emulation

  • α = FP8 MMAs per high-precision op. For Ozaki II on FP8: α = 3r + 1; at r = 12, α = 37.
  • β ≥ 1 = bandwidth multiplier. β = 1 for fully on-chip fused residue decomposition; β = r for unfused execution.
  • γ ≥ 0 = per-output Garner reconstruction latency.

Three derived regimes

  • Case A — native compute-bound, emulation memory-bound (the B300 regime): speedup = I · Bmem / PFP64.
  • Case B — both memory-bound: Temu / Tnat → β; register fusion becomes a correctness requirement.
  • Case C — both compute-bound (dense GEMM): speedup = ρ / α ≈ 135 TFLOPS on B300.

Strengths
  • Clean L0 → L4 framing collapses Berkeley dwarfs onto one FP8 primitive — verifiable, not rhetorical.
  • TME cleanly separates the bound question from the realizable-bound question; β is honestly flagged as the empirical open.
  • Honest v1 → v2 correction (~3× dense ceiling) credits the NVIDIA DevTech catch.
  • Explicit falsifiability in §9 — three clean failure modes rather than hedging.
  • Kernel-design discipline (register-resident residues) tied to occupancy and tile-size trade-offs.
Limitations
  • No silicon measurement — central claims are projections, not delivered numbers.
  • β = 1 idealization — residue planes compete with the register file that bounds occupancy.
  • Sparse kernel coverage leans on Blocked-Ellpack / hybrid CSR; adversarial sparsity patterns are open.
  • Exception semantics (NaN/Inf), bitwise reproducibility, emulated Krylov convergence — unresolved.
  • Composition overhead γ under nested solvers (Krylov, LOBPCG, sparse-direct panels) not modelled.
  • 2:4 structured sparsity + Ozaki II modular reduction — explicitly open.
