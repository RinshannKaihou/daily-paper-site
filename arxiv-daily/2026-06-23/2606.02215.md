> **一句话总结：** 提出 EVONOTE 自演化智能体框架，把每条社区注释的生成轨迹通过"Social Utility Judge + Memory Evolver"蒸馏成 (TRIGGER, ACTION STRATEGY, PHASE) 三元组形式的阶段化记忆，使后续同类健康谣言修正可在不更新模型参数的情况下复用前序经验；在 1.2K 多模态基准 MM-HEALTH CN 上以 89.6% 的胜率超过人工社区注释，并把单条候选注释的中位生成时间从 13 小时压缩到 2 分钟以内。

> **TL;DR:** EVONOTE turns each Community-Notes correction trajectory into reusable, phase-specific action-level memory so that later, similar health-misinformation posts inherit strategies from prior episodes instead of starting from scratch. A Social Utility Judge scores trajectories against four health-grounded note qualities (understandability, meaningfulness, usability, trustworthiness) and assigns a utility mode, then a Memory Evolver distills each episode into items of the form (TRIGGER, ACTION STRATEGY, PHASE) tied to claim analysis, evidence acquisition, or note writing. On the new MM-HEALTH CN benchmark (1.2K user-flagged posts, 400 each in TEXT / IMAGE / VIDEO) and built on MedGemma-27B, EVONOTE beats human-written Community Notes in 89.6% of pairwise comparisons, produces helpful notes on 82.0% of unresolved "Needs More Ratings" cases, and cuts median candidate-annotation latency from >13 hours to <2 minutes, with gains traced to stronger and more diverse evidence use.

## 核心贡献

- **自演化注释生成框架 EVONOTE**：首次把"健康类社区注释"任务建模为一个闭环的自演化过程——每条候选注释的完整生成轨迹（claim analysis → evidence acquisition → note writing）经评分后被蒸馏成可复用的动作级记忆，下一轮相同或相似的贴文可按阶段检索相关经验，模型本体与工作流保持不变。
- **细粒度信用分配机制**：Social Utility Judge 基于四个健康传播学维度（可读性 / 健康解读意义 / 可操作安全性 / 不确定性下的可信度）评估整条轨迹，并给出 UNSATISFACTORY / SATISFACTORY / EXCELLENT 三档效用模式，Memory Evolver 据此把课程分配到 claim analysis、evidence acquisition、note writing 三阶段之一。
- **MM-HEALTH CN 多模态基准**：1.2K 条 X 平台用户标记的健康类可疑贴文，每条配一条人工社区注释 + 众包 helpfulness 标签；按 TEXT / IMAGE / VIDEO 三种模态各 400 条均衡分布，是首个面向"LLM 增强健康类社区注释"的多模态评测集。
- **闭环实证**：在 1.2K 共识已达成样本上对人工注释 89.6% 胜率，在 300 条尚未达共识的 Needs More Ratings 帖上 82.0% 输出为 Helpful；中位生成时间从 13 小时降至 2 分钟以内，且赢得更高胜率的同时所用步骤数与总时间少于 DeepResearch 与 ReMem。

## Key Contributions

- **Self-evolving Community Notes framework (EvoNote)**: An agentic pipeline in which a note-generation trajectory τ_k = (x̃_k, c_k, H_k, E_k, y_k) — containing claim analysis, action history, collected evidence, and final note — is evaluated by a Social Utility Judge and distilled by a Memory Evolver into action-level memory ΔM_k = DISTILL(τ_k, u_k), giving M_k = M_{k−1} ∪ ΔM_k. The backbone, tools, and workflow stay fixed; adaptation is purely at the memory level.
- **Trajectory-level utility judgment grounded in health communication theory**: The Judge scores notes on (1) understandability (Sørensen et al., 2012; Warde et al., 2018), (2) meaningfulness for health interpretation and risk perception (Ferrer & Klein, 2015; Prike & Ecker, 2023), (3) usability for safe action (Heydari et al., 2021; Denniss et al., 2022), and (4) trustworthiness under evidence uncertainty (Schneider et al., 2021; Denniss et al., 2022), and emits a mode u_k ∈ {UNSATISFACTORY, SATISFACTORY, EXCELLENT} that routes memory construction toward failure-avoidance, improvement, or success-reuse.
- **Phase-specific action memory**: Each memory item is the triple m = (TRIGGER, ACTION STRATEGY, PHASE), where PHASE ∈ {CLAIM ANALYSIS, EVIDENCE ACQUISITION, NOTE WRITING}. At inference time, the Claim Analyzer and Note Writer retrieve top-n items whose TRIGGER overlaps the current retrieval query and whose PHASE matches the active stage, returning ACTION STRATEGY fields as guidance; this avoids the cost of retrieving full prior trajectories (cf. ExpRAG) or asking the actor to infer lessons on the fly (cf. ReMem).
- **MM-HEALTH CN benchmark**: 1.2K user-flagged health-related posts on X, each paired with a human-written Community Note and its crowd-derived helpfulness label (Helpful vs. Not Helpful, 400 each for TEXT / IMAGE / VIDEO). Image and video posts are converted into human-validated captions (0.95 / 0.96 majority-vote accuracy on 100 samples per modality), preserving correction-relevant content while letting medical-capable LLMs handle tool use and multi-step reasoning — a direct VLM backbone on IMAGE fails to complete valid trajectories in nearly all cases (63 cases remain invalid even after repair heuristics).
- **Hierarchical utility judgment protocol**: A length-compliance filter (≤280 chars) plus three progressive GPT-4.1 gates — evidence relevance (R), correctness of evidence representation (C), note-style helpfulness (H) — yields binary helpfulness; a separate GPT-4.1 pairwise judge scores win / tie / loss on the four health-utility qualities. Three independent annotators achieve 98% majority-vote agreement with the pairwise judge on 100 sampled comparisons, validating the automated protocol.

## Methodology

**Motivating analysis (§3).** From 4,874 helpful human-written Community Notes on X (May 2022 – Oct 2025), two expert annotators summarize 200 sampled note-post pairs into a correction-relevant taxonomy (medical-mechanism misrepresentation, unsupported causality, etc., full taxonomy in Table 4 / Appendix A.2) and GPT-4.1 labels the rest under that taxonomy. Two findings: (1) recurring claim patterns make correction needs transferable — Figure 2(a) clusters around mechanism overclaims and causal overgeneralization; (2) useful lessons can remain episode-local — Figure 2(b) shows a 22 May 2023 "Helpful" note that "clarify the claim by checking quote context" was available before a 17 Mar 2024 and a 15 Apr 2025 post that both missed the same lesson. This motivates the EVONOTE design: convert earlier episodes into structured experience for later ones.

**Episode formulation (§4.1).** Each flagged post x_k may contain text, image, or video. Multimodal posts are converted to a unified textual representation x̃_k via validated captions (Appendices C.2 / C.3). A note-generation episode produces τ_k = (x̃_k, c_k, H_k, E_k, y_k), where c_k is the instance-specific claim analysis, H_k is the action history (e.g., search / visit / write / abstain), E_k is collected evidence, and y_k is the final note.

**Trajectory-level judgment (§4.2).** Once τ_k is complete, the Social Utility Judge agent evaluates it against the four health-grounded qualities and outputs fine-grained feedback f_k plus a utility mode u_k. The mode routes memory construction: UNSATISFACTORY → failure-avoidance, SATISFACTORY → improvement-oriented, EXCELLENT → success-reuse.

**Actionable memory evolution (§4.3).** The Memory Evolver distills τ_k into memory items m = (TRIGGER, ACTION STRATEGY, PHASE), where TRIGGER specifies when the memory applies, ACTION STRATEGY states the reusable correction strategy, and PHASE indicates which stage (claim analysis / evidence acquisition / note writing) it should guide. A worked example: if a trajectory mishandles a statistic-based health claim, the Evolver creates an evidence-acquisition memory triggered on similar numerical claims, recommending checking the original source, denominator, and context before writing.

**Memory-guided note generation (§4.4).** At episode k+1, given x̃_{k+1}, EVONOTE runs (a) memory-guided claim analysis — the Claim Analyzer summarizes the post into a query q_C(x̃_{k+1}), retrieves the top-n items from M_k with PHASE = CLAIM ANALYSIS and overlapping TRIGGERs, and produces an instance-specific verification brief c_{k+1} = C(x̃_{k+1}, R_C^{k+1}); followed by (b) budgeted evidence acquisition and note writing — the Note Writer summarizes each decision state into q_t^A and selects a phase-matched memory subset by p_t ∈ {EVIDENCE ACQUISITION, NOTE WRITING}, retrieving top-n items by TRIGGER overlap. The action space A_t = {SEARCH, VISIT} initially, with WRITE / ABSTAIN added after at least one search and one visit. SEARCH is removed once its budget B_S is exhausted, VISIT once B_V is exhausted; ABSTAIN terminates generation when evidence cannot support a reliable correction.

**Closed self-evolution (§4.5).** τ_k → u_k → ΔM_k → τ_{k+1}. Each completed trajectory is judged, distilled, and merged into the memory bank; the loop runs without updating model parameters or relying on a fixed bank of human notes, allowing correction experience to accumulate even when reliable human notes are sparse, delayed, or unavailable.

**Backbone & baselines.** Backbone LLM is the medical-specific MedGemma-27B (Sellergren et al., 2025). Baselines include (i) web-search-enabled LLMs GPT-4.1, Grok-4.3, Gemini-2.5-Flash; (ii) Community-Notes generators CrowdNotes+ (Wu et al., 2025) and DeepResearch-30B-A3B (Tongyi DeepResearch Team et al., 2025); (iii) memory-augmented LLM agents from the Evo-Memory suite — ExpRAG and ReMem (Wei et al., 2025). All memory-based methods retrieve top-k memories; leakage-control constraints are documented in Appendices E.2 / E.3.

**Evaluation protocol.** Binary helpfulness: 280-character length filter → R gate → C gate → H gate. Pairwise utility: GPT-4.1 randomized win / tie / loss grounded in the four health-utility qualities; 100-sample human validation yields 98% agreement. Latency benchmarked on a single H200 GPU.

## Results & Highlights

- **Main effectiveness (Table 1)**: On the 1.2K consensus-resolved MM-HEALTH CN set, EVONOTE attains the highest H (81.17 overall) and pairwise win rate against the Human Baseline (89.59% overall; 89.38 / 89.25 / 90.13 on TEXT / IMAGE / VIDEO). EVONOTE outperforms every baseline on every modality and on every gate (R, C, H). All memory-based agents beat one-pass CrowdNotes+, confirming the value of reusable experience, but EVONOTE — which distills judge feedback into phase-specific memory — beats both ExpRAG (retrieves full trajectories) and ReMem (asks the actor to infer lessons from past trajectories).
- **Coverage before crowd consensus (§6.2)**: On 300 unresolved "Needs More Ratings" posts where crowd consensus has not yet been reached, EVONOTE produces helpful notes for 82.0% of cases, expanding coverage when no human majority has formed.
- **Rescuing vs. strengthening human notes**: For judge-rated Not Helpful human notes (N = 740), failures split across evidence relevance (40.8%), evidence representation correctness (33.2%), and note-style helpfulness (25.9%); EVONOTE produces a helpful alternative in 77.0% of these cases. For judge-rated Helpful human notes (N = 460), EVONOTE remains helpful in 87.6% of cases and is preferred in 74.1%. Figure 4 shows gains across all four utility dimensions (understandable / meaningful / usable / trustworthy).
- **Ablation confirms both components matter**: EVONOTE-FM freezes the warm-up memory bank during evaluation (H 73.17 / Win. 84.75); EVONOTE-NM removes memory retrieval and update (H 71.83 / Win. 84.25); EVONOTE-NM-NA further removes the Claim Analyzer (H 67.00 / Win. 79.42). Each removal degrades performance, isolating the contribution of both evolving memory and explicit claim analysis.
- **Evidence quality and diversity (Figure 5)**: Across five evidence-side metrics (average cited URLs, multi-URL rate, source quality, cited-domain diversity, semantic diversity), EVONOTE achieves the strongest overall evidence profile, with more multi-source support, higher source quality, and stronger domain and semantic diversity than CrowdNotes+, DeepResearch, ExpRAG, and ReMem.
- **Quality–efficiency trade-off (Table 2)**: On a single H200 GPU, EVONOTE reaches 89.59% win rate with mean 12.4 turns and 112.6 s total time per case — fewer turns and less total time than DeepResearch (153.0 s) and ReMem (118.3 s), and far below the >13-hour human-note latency. Phase-specific memory avoids the heavier information seeking of DeepResearch and the full-trajectory reasoning cost of ReMem by retrieving compact action-level strategies at the relevant stage.
- **Memorized strategies are interpretable**: Across MM-HEALTH CN, EVONOTE accumulates 4,277 memory items, each tied to an action phase and a reusable strategy. Dominant memories target primary-source verification, causal overgeneralization, safe next steps, and practical health implications (§F.4 / Figure 11).
- **Captioning beats direct VLM instantiation (Figure 6 / §F.6)**: On the IMAGE subset, EVONOTE with a multimodal MedGemma-27B backbone fails to complete valid note-generation trajectories in nearly all cases; even after complex repair heuristics, 63 cases remain invalid. Caption-based LLM instantiation remains both more reliable and higher-scoring, validating the caption-then-LLM design.
- **Median latency collapse**: From >13 hours in the human-note pipeline to <2 minutes per case, with EVONOTE simultaneously beating humans on pairwise utility — i.e., the speedup is not a quality compromise.

## Critical Assessment

- **Novelty**: The reframing of Community-Notes generation as a stream-level self-evolving memory problem — where the unit of learning is a (TRIGGER, ACTION STRATEGY, PHASE) triple keyed to claim analysis, evidence acquisition, or note writing, and where judgment is grounded in four health-communication desiderata rather than generic helpfulness — is a concrete contribution that goes beyond trajectory reflection (Shinn et al., 2023) and long-term memory agents (Chhikara et al., 2025; Rasmussen et al., 2025; Li et al., 2025). The pairing with MM-HEALTH CN — the first multimodal benchmark that ties each post to a real human-written Community Note and crowd-derived helpfulness label — is also a real contribution; prior LLM-augmented Community-Notes work (Wu et al., 2025; Singh et al., 2026) evaluates on smaller or text-only sets.
- **Methodological rigor**: Three strengths stand out. (1) The Judge's four-dimensional framework is anchored to established health-communication literature (Sørensen et al., 2012; Ferrer & Klein, 2015; Heydari et al., 2021; Schneider et al., 2021; Denniss et al., 2022) rather than ad-hoc criteria. (2) Human validation of the automated pairwise judge (98% majority-vote agreement among three annotators on 100 samples) is non-trivial — many LLM-as-judge papers skip this step. (3) Three ablations (freeze memory / remove memory / remove Claim Analyzer) cleanly attribute gains to both evolving memory and explicit claim analysis.
- **Statistical and reproducibility considerations**: The <50% binary-helpfulness rate of human-written Community Notes under the hierarchical judge is consistent with prior findings that crowd-rated "Helpful" status can mask subtle evidence failures, so the 89.6% win rate is read against an already-stringent automated gate, not against crowd consensus alone. The 98% annotator agreement is a strong signal but is reported on only 100 samples, and length-compliance filtering (≤280 chars) is applied before the GPT-4.1 gates, so the absolute numbers depend on this preprocessing.
- **Significance**: Beyond the numbers, the paper articulates a design principle for misinformation governance — "every flagged post should become experience before the next similar one recurs" — that is portable beyond Community Notes. The 13-hour-to-2-minute latency collapse is operationally meaningful for time-sensitive health misinformation where early spread makes correction delay especially costly (Kouzy et al., 2020; Van Der Linden, 2022).
- **Red flags / open concerns**: (1) The memory bank grows unbounded (4,277 items on 1.2K episodes in the reported run); without decay / dedup, retrieval cost and memory staleness become first-order issues at scale. (2) The benchmark is English-only and X-only, so cross-platform and multilingual generalization is unproven. (3) The automated judge relies on GPT-4.1, creating a self-referential loop when an LLM judges another LLM's output on a task domain it has been trained on. (4) Captioning IMAGE / VIDEO to text is shown to be necessary because the multimodal MedGemma-27B backbone fails to complete trajectories, but this also means EVONOTE inherits any caption-induced information loss — the 0.95 / 0.96 accuracies on 100 samples are encouraging but small. (5) The motivating taxonomy of correction-relevant claim patterns is built by two human experts on 200 sampled notes; inter-annotator agreement on the taxonomy itself is not reported.

## Strengths

- **End-to-end self-evolution**: A clean closed loop τ_k → u_k → ΔM_k → τ_{k+1} that improves notes without updating model parameters, making the improvement interpretable, lightweight, and reversible.
- **Phase-directed memory**: Items are tagged with PHASE ∈ {CLAIM ANALYSIS, EVIDENCE ACQUISITION, NOTE WRITING}, so memory retrieval is filtered by the agent's current stage. This is more targeted than retrieving whole prior trajectories (ExpRAG) or letting the actor infer lessons (ReMem), and the ablation confirms it.
- **Multimodal benchmark with real human references**: 1.2K posts, 400 each in TEXT / IMAGE / VIDEO, paired with human-written Community Notes and crowd-derived helpfulness labels — not synthetic.
- **Strong empirical results against humans**: 89.6% pairwise win rate over human-written Community Notes, 82.0% helpful on unresolved "Needs More Ratings" cases, 77.0% rescue rate on judge-rated Not Helpful human notes.
- **Stronger evidence profile**: Higher source quality, multi-source rate, cited-domain diversity, and semantic diversity than CrowdNotes+, DeepResearch, ExpRAG, and ReMem — i.e., the gain is not just stylistic, it shows up in evidence acquisition.
- **Quality–efficiency trade-off**: Higher win rate than DeepResearch and ReMem with fewer turns and less total time; median 13-hour-to-2-minute latency collapse vs. the human pipeline.
- **Interpretable memory bank**: 4,277 items on 1.2K episodes, each tied to a phase and a reusable strategy; dominant memories are inspectable and classifiable into a human-built taxonomy.
- **Caption-based multimodal handling**: Human-validated captions (0.95 / 0.96 majority-vote accuracy on 100 samples per modality) preserve correction-relevant content while letting medical-capable LLMs handle tool use.
- **Layered safety considerations**: Human oversight, memory safety, dual-use, and data privacy are addressed in a dedicated Ethical Considerations section.

## Limitations

- **English-only, X-only**: EVONOTE focuses on English health misinformation on X; political, financial, sociocultural claims and multilingual deployment remain open.
- **Memory bank grows unbounded**: 4,277 items on 1.2K episodes in the reported run; without decay / dedup / revision mechanisms, retrieval cost and staleness are first-order concerns at scale. The authors flag this explicitly.
- **Backbone and workflow fixed**: Self-evolution is implemented only at the memory level; broader forms — prompt revision, workflow adaptation, tool-policy learning — are left for future work.
- **Multimodal backbone fails on IMAGE**: MedGemma-27B cannot complete valid note-generation trajectories in nearly all IMAGE cases (63 cases remain invalid even after repair heuristics), forcing reliance on captions and inheriting any caption-induced information loss.
- **Self-referential judgment loop**: The hierarchical and pairwise judges both rely on GPT-4.1; the 98% annotator agreement is on 100 samples, and the absolute helpfulness numbers depend on the length-compliance preprocessing step.
- **No upstream detection**: EVONOTE starts from flagged posts and does not model detection, prioritization, or real-time routing; practical deployment requires integration with systems that decide when human review is needed.
- **Taxonomy subjectivity**: The motivating taxonomy of correction-relevant claim patterns is built by two human experts on 200 sampled notes; inter-annotator agreement on the taxonomy itself is not reported.
- **Noisy crowd labels**: The pipeline uses crowd-derived helpfulness as a benchmark anchor; crowd signals are known to be noisy and affected by voting dynamics, which the authors acknowledge.
- **Memory can propagate flawed lessons**: The authors explicitly flag that storing incorrect trajectories can propagate errors, and call for future auditing, revision, decay, and deletion mechanisms.
- **Risk of fluent-but-incorrect notes**: Generated notes may appear authoritative even when evidence is incomplete or misrepresented — a particular concern in health contexts.

## Relevance

与用户当前研究方向高度相关：本文把"LLM 智能体在长任务流中的经验复用"与"健康谣言治理"这两个交叉议题结合起来，提出一种阶段化的、可解释的记忆机制，比单纯 RAG 或全程反射更具落地性，且在多模态真实帖文上验证。中文方面，可作为"用大模型辅助健康社区治理 / 证据驱动辟谣自动化"主题的方法参考。

## Questions for Further Exploration

- How should the memory bank be bounded — via embedding-based clustering / dedup, recency-weighted decay, or human-audited eviction — so that retrieval cost stays tractable as M_k grows past tens of thousands of items?
- Does EVONOTE transfer to other modalities and platforms (e.g., TikTok video misinformation, Telegram health channels, multilingual posts) without retraining, and which utility dimensions need re-grounding for non-English evidence norms?
- Can the memory-update step be made safer by jointly learning from agent trajectories and high-quality human corrections, given that crowd labels are noisy and the current pipeline ignores the human-note bank at training time?
- Could the same (TRIGGER, ACTION STRATEGY, PHASE) memory schema be lifted to other correction / moderation tasks — political claim fact-checking, financial rumor verification, scientific paper retraction notices — by swapping the four utility dimensions for domain-specific ones?
- The multimodal MedGemma-27B backbone fails on IMAGE; would stronger agentic VLMs (e.g., GUI-trained agents, fine-grained VLM planners) close the gap, or is the caption-then-LLM split a structural choice driven by tool-use reliability rather than a model-capability ceiling?
- The GPT-4.1 judge is self-referential; would an independent open-weight judge, or a panel of heterogeneous judges with disagreement-aware aggregation, change the 89.6% win rate materially?
- How sensitive are the gains to the binary UNSATISFACTORY / SATISFACTORY / EXCELLENT routing — would a continuous utility score, or a fine-grained failure taxonomy, give the Memory Evolver more informative gradients?
- For the four health-grounded qualities (understandability / meaningfulness / usability / trustworthiness), which are the most predictive of human-helpfulness judgments, and can a smaller subset suffice for online judgment?
- Can EVONOTE be combined with an upstream detector that flags emerging misinformation, so the self-evolving loop also informs prioritization and routing decisions in addition to note quality?
