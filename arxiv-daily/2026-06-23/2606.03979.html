LLM Sleep — periodic Knowledge Seeding consolidation plus RL Dreaming — beats ICL, EWC, SFT, GRPO, and SEAL across six task families.

Language Models Need Sleep: Learning to Self-Modify and Consolidate Memories ↗ Ali Behrouz · Farnoosh Hashemi · Vahab Mirrokni continual-learning memory-consolidation self-improvement long-context knowledge-distillation
Key Metric · Few-shot ARC

80%

Sleep (Llama-3.2-1B) on held-out ARC tasks.
vs. SEAL 72.5% · TTT 10% · ICL 0%.

Why This Matters

Targets two of LLMs' most cited limits — catastrophic forgetting and the inability to transfer in-context knowledge into durable parameters — with a clean engineering loop.

Critical Edge

Wall-clock cost is never benchmarked: each sleep cycle does teacher sampling + GKD + m LoRA SFTs + m evaluations — likely far pricier than one SFT pass.

Methodology

方法 · 唤醒 / 睡眠两阶段循环
  • Continuum Memory System (CMS): MLP blocks parameterized by update frequency f1 ≥ … ≥ fc; sleep triggers at multiples of chunk size C(ℓ).
  • Memory Consolidation: deactivate a high-frequency block, grow a low-rank expert (pre-allocated, masked) in a slower block, then run Knowledge Seeding — on-policy distillation from the pre-expansion teacher into the post-expansion student.
  • Learning to Imitate (LTI): student continues teacher prefixes; reward = γ·rsem (frozen 0/1 judge) + (1−γ)·Levenshtein-based rabs; combined with KL as 𝓛KS.
  • Dreaming (REM): sample m continuations with each MoE router forced to pick one random expert; rank dreams by SFT-gradient norm (top-k + b random); LoRA-SFT each, reward 1 if it improves the task, optimize with ReSTEM.
  • Synaptic pruning: after consolidation, the previously-added low-rank parameters are reset to free capacity for the next cycle.

Results & Highlights

实验 · 6 类任务 · 2 个模型家族
  • Class-incremental (CLINC, Banking, DBpedia): Hope + Sleep beats ICL, EWC, InCA, plain Hope.
  • Long-context (MK-NIAH, LongHealth, QASPER): more sleep stages → better ICL and long-context; very persistent memories should update rarely.
  • BABILong up to 10M tokens: Hope nearly perfect, beating RMT, ARMT, Titans, GPT-4o-mini, Llama-8B + RAG; only GPT-4 competitive.
  • Continual Translation (Kalamang + Manchu): Hope-3 retains most single-language ChRF; ICL collapses, Cartridges/SFT forget.
  • Math reasoning (Qwen3-8B): AIME-24 73.8 → 79.2, beats SFT (75.5), GRPO (76.4), OPSD (76.6).
  • SQuAD knowledge incorporation: Sleep 48.9 vs. SEAL 46.7 (n=1); 46.2 vs. 43.2 (n=200 passages).
  • ARC few-shot: 80% vs. SEAL 72.5%, TTT 10%, ICL 0%.

Strengths & Limitations

优势 · 局限 · 双栏对照

Strengths

  • Coherent wake/sleep framing cleanly separates how to consolidate (KS) from what to consolidate (dreams).
  • Practical mechanisms for catastrophic forgetting (frozen old params + KL) and self-improvement (gradient-scored dreams + ReSTEM).
  • Implementation avoids tensor-shape churn via pre-allocated, masked low-rank experts.
  • Empirically broad: 6 task families, 2 model families, full ablation on every named component.

Limitations

  • Wall-clock cost unanalyzed — no GPU-hours per sleep cycle reported.
  • rsem unspecified — frozen binary reward model with no name, citation, or training recipe.
  • No comparison to retrieval-augmented reasoning, GRPO+RAG, or latest continual-RL methods.
  • Hyperparameter sensitivity (chunk schedule, m, LoRA rank, α, λ, γ) not swept.
  • Code not released; architecture validated only on Llama-3 / Qwen3 — MoE, SSM, multimodal open.

Questions for Further Exploration

延伸问题 · 后续可验证
  • Energy budget: GPU-hours per Sleep cycle vs. SFT, GRPO, and RAG on the same data — does sample efficiency survive a 10× cost?
  • Spec of rsem: which frozen judge on SQuAD / ARC / CTNL, and does swapping it move the gap to SEAL?
  • Scaling beyond 10M tokens: at what working-memory size does continued parameter growth in the slow block saturate?
  • Hyperparameter robustness: does a single default (sleep-step schedule, m, LoRA rank) work across task families?
  • Retrieval integration: can RAG-conditioned dreams close the remaining gap to an oracle retriever on SQuAD?
  • Continual RL safety: reward hacking, mode collapse, distributional drift after many cycles vs. SEAL's documented failures.
  • Modalities: does the wake/sleep abstraction transfer to vision-language, audio, or tool-using agents?
