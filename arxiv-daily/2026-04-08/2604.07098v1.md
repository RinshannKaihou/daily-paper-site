# Selective Neuron Amplification for Training-Free Task Enhancement

> **一句话总结：** 提出 Selective Neuron Amplification (SNA) 方法，通过差分激活分析识别任务相关神经元并在推理时放大其输出（不改参数），发现模型置信度与 SNA 效果呈单调负相关（Spearman rho = -0.762），在低置信度区域平均提升 27.85%，最高达 70.3%。

> **TL;DR:** Introduces SNA, which identifies task-relevant neurons via differential activation analysis and scales their outputs at inference time without parameter changes, finding a monotonic negative relationship between baseline confidence and SNA improvement (rho = -0.762), with 27.85% average improvement in low-confidence regimes.

## 核心贡献

- 提出 Three-Zone Saturation Model：Zone 1（baseline < 0.07）平均提升 27.85%，最高 70.3%；Zone 2（0.07-0.10）不稳定；Zone 3（> 0.10）增益不超过 7%
- 发现基线置信度是 SNA 效果的最强预测因子，解释约 58% 的方差，而任务类型贡献最小
- 揭示了层级功能特化：Layer 8 专门响应算术任务，Layer 21-22 更多参与语义和句法处理
- 发现跨任务干扰的不对称性：放大算术相关神经元会抑制诗歌性能，但反向影响较弱

## Key Contributions

- Three-Zone Saturation Model: Zone 1 (baseline < 0.07) yields 27.85% mean improvement up to 70.3%; Zone 2 (0.07-0.10) is unpredictable; Zone 3 (> 0.10) rarely exceeds 7% gain
- Baseline capability accounts for ~58% of variance in outcomes; circuit localization adds a smaller share; task type contributes least
- Layer-wise functional specialization: Layer 8 recurs in arithmetic, Layers 21-22 in semantic/syntactic processing (GPT-2 Medium)
- Asymmetric cross-task interference: amplifying arithmetic circuits suppresses poetry but not vice versa
- SST-2 classification: 57.3 +/- 7.4 pp improvement for uncertain predictions; no degradation for confident ones

## Methodology

SNA works in three steps: (1) Differential activation analysis identifies task-relevant neurons by running the model on task-specific inputs versus neutral reference text and computing the difference in mean activation per neuron; (2) Activation hooks are attached at a chosen layer using TransformerLens to access intermediate activations; (3) During inference, selected neurons' outputs are scaled by a factor (typically 1.5-3.0) before passing through the rest of the network. The entire process requires no parameter updates and is fully reversible. Experiments span 24,192 configurations across 12 tasks on GPT-2 Small (117M) and GPT-2 Medium (345M).

## Results & Highlights

- 24,192 experimental configurations across 12 tasks and 2 model sizes
- Spearman rho = -0.762 (p = 0.004) between baseline probability and SNA improvement
- Zone 1 mean improvement: 27.85%, max: 70.3%
- SST-2 uncertain predictions: +57.3 +/- 7.4 pp
- Pythia-160M validation: 178.98% mean improvement on arithmetic tasks
- Zero degradation for already-confident predictions across 60 configurations

## Critical Assessment

The paper makes a valuable empirical observation -- that model failures often stem from weak activation rather than missing knowledge, and that baseline confidence predictably determines intervention effectiveness. The Three-Zone model is a useful heuristic. However, several concerns limit the contribution: (1) the method is essentially a form of activation steering applied at the neuron level, which has been explored in prior work (CAA, activation engineering), though the differential activation analysis and confidence-based framing are new; (2) experiments are limited to models under 345M parameters, and the claim of generalization to larger models is unsupported; (3) the cross-task interference finding is interesting but not deeply analyzed -- the asymmetry could simply reflect that arithmetic neurons are more specialized than poetry neurons; (4) the paper lacks a formal connection to mechanistic interpretability theory.

## Strengths

- Extremely thorough experimental coverage (24,192 configurations) with transparent reporting
- The Three-Zone Saturation Model provides a practical heuristic for when activation interventions are worthwhile
- Cross-task interference analysis is a genuine and important finding
- Clean separation between "missing knowledge" and "weak activation" failure modes

## Limitations

- Limited to small models (max 345M parameters); unclear if patterns generalize to frontier models
- No theoretical grounding for why baseline confidence should predict intervention effectiveness
- The differential activation analysis is simplistic (mean difference) compared to more sophisticated probing methods
- No comparison with other activation steering methods beyond brief mentions

## Relevance

直接关联隐藏状态与可解释性研究：SNA 的核心发现——模型失败源于弱激活而非知识缺失——为理解 LLM 推理失败提供了重要的机制性洞见。层级功能特化和跨任务干扰的发现也对理解模型内部计算结构有直接贡献，对推理评估和输出一致性分析具有参考价值。

## Questions for Further Exploration

- Does the Three-Zone model hold for models with 7B+ parameters where representation structure is qualitatively different?
- How does SNA interact with chain-of-thought reasoning and multi-step inference?
- Can the cross-task interference pattern be predicted from the overlap of task-relevant neuron sets?
- Is the asymmetric interference pattern a general property of transformer architectures or specific to GPT-2?
