Energy-Regularized Spatial Masking: A Novel Approach to Enhancing Robustness and Interpretability in Vision Models

一句话总结： 提出ERSM框架，将特征选择重新定义为可微能量最小化问题，通过轻量级能量掩码层实现自适应空间稀疏性，同时提升视觉模型的鲁棒性和可解释性。
TL;DR: ERSM reformulates feature selection as a differentiable energy minimization problem, producing emergent sparsity and interpretable spatial masks that improve robustness in vision models.

核心贡献

  • 将能量模型原理从全局图像生成适配到内部特征选择，提出基于能量最小化的空间掩码框架ERSM
  • 设计包含一元重要性代价和成对空间一致性惩罚的能量函数，实现无需预定义稀疏度的自适应特征选择
  • 提出摊销推理机制，将全局能量约束蒸馏为局部一元势能，实现高效的推理时决策
  • 在分类精度保持的同时，产生连续、语义对齐的空间掩码，提升鲁棒性和可解释性

Key Contributions

  • Adapts energy-based modeling from global image generation to internal feature selection with an intrinsic information-preservation principle
  • Introduces a lightweight Energy-Mask Layer with unary (redundancy template) and pairwise (spatial coherence) energy terms
  • Develops amortized inference that distills global pairwise constraints into a fast local unary projection at inference time
  • Demonstrates emergent sparsity, improved robustness to structured occlusion, and highly interpretable spatial masks without segmentation supervision

Methodology

ERSM embeds a differentiable energy system within standard convolutional backbones. Feature maps are partitioned into patches, each assigned a scalar energy composed of a unary cost (alignment with a learned noise template W_mask) and a pairwise spatial coherence penalty (cosine similarity with Moore neighbors). The training objective combines task loss with an energy regularization term: L = L_task + L_reg, where L_reg is the expected energy of retained tokens. During training, the full energy (including pairwise interactions) guides learning; at inference, only the local unary projection is used via amortized inference. The gating policy uses sigmoid(-E_unary) as the retention probability, naturally suppressing high-energy (redundant) tokens.

Results & Highlights

  • ERSM-Unary achieves 69.76% on Food-101, comparable to L0-Gating (69.86%), with lower variance across seeds
  • Average keep probability ~0.65, indicating consistent ~35% feature suppression
  • Energy-guided deletion outperforms random deletion in robustness tests; accuracy initially increases when removing top-20-25% patches
  • Learned energy masks are contiguous, semantically aligned, and emerge without explicit supervision
  • ERSM significantly outperforms magnitude-based pruning in deletion-based robustness tests

Authors & Credibility

The authors are from multiple French institutions: DAVID Lab at Paris-Saclay University, LIPN at Sorbonne Paris Nord University, and LISN at Paris-Saclay University. The work spans computer vision and machine learning with a physics-inspired perspective.

Critical Assessment

The paper offers a creative adaptation of energy-based modeling principles to feature selection. The key insight -- treating the forward pass as an energy minimization trajectory and using learned noise templates to identify redundancy -- is novel. The amortized inference strategy that distills pairwise constraints into unary projections is practical and elegant. However, the evaluation is primarily on a controlled setting (Food-101 with a compact CNN), and the gains over simpler baselines like L0-Gating are modest. The physics-inspired framing, while motivating, does not establish a formal connection to energy-based probabilistic models, which the authors themselves acknowledge.

Strengths

  • Novel reformulation of feature selection as energy minimization with clear physical motivation
  • Emergent sparsity without predefined sparsity budgets -- the network discovers its own optimal density
  • Amortized inference provides efficient deployment without sacrificing the benefits of global constraints
  • Spatial masks are contiguous and semantically meaningful without segmentation supervision

Limitations

  • Evaluation primarily on Food-101 with compact CNNs; scalability to larger architectures and datasets unclear
  • Modest accuracy improvements over simpler baselines (L0-Gating, DropBlock)
  • The energy formulation is a training regularizer rather than a true energy-based probabilistic model
  • The pairwise term's contribution appears marginal compared to the unary term alone

Relevance

本文提出了一种基于能量最小化的特征选择方法，通过学习到的空间掩码实现可解释性。虽然主要针对视觉模型，但其将物理启发的能量模型与表征学习结合的思路，对理解模型内部特征重要性具有参考价值。

Questions for Further Exploration

  • How do the learned noise templates (W_mask) relate to background/foreground statistical properties?
  • Can ERSM be combined with attention mechanisms, or do they serve redundant purposes?
  • Would this approach be effective in transformer architectures where spatial tokens are already used?
