# ConceptTracer: Interactive Analysis of Concept Saliency and Selectivity in Neural Representations

> **一句话总结：** 提出 ConceptTracer 交互式工具，通过两个信息论度量（concept saliency 和 selectivity）量化神经元与人类可解释概念之间的关联，在 TabPFN 的表示中发现不到 1% 的神经元对特定概念具有显著的 saliency 和 selectivity。

> **TL;DR:** Introduces ConceptTracer, an interactive tool using two information-theoretic measures (saliency and selectivity via normalized mutual information) to quantify neuron-concept associations in neural representations, discovering interpretable neurons in TabPFN with less than 1% of neurons showing significant concept-specific responses.

## 核心贡献

- 定义了两个信息论度量：saliency（基于归一化互信息，衡量神经元与概念的关联强度）和 selectivity（衡量神经元对特定概念的特化程度）
- 开发了 ConceptTracer 交互式仪表板，支持网络级、层级、神经元级和概念级四个视角的分析
- 在 TabPFN 表示中发现可解释神经元，识别出与恶性肿癌、肥胖、循环系统疾病等临床概念关联的特定神经元
- 揭示了两种互补的神经响应模式：复合输入特征内的概念分化和跨相关输入特征的概念整合

## Key Contributions

- Two information-theoretic metrics: saliency (normalized mutual information between neuron activations and concept labels) and selectivity (fraction of total saliency attributable to a single concept)
- Interactive dashboard with four complementary views: network, layer, neuron, and concept perspectives
- Discovery of interpretable neurons in TabPFN on clinical triage tasks (MIMIC-IV-ED dataset, AUC 0.79-0.85)
- Identification of two complementary neural response patterns: concept differentiation within composite features and concept integration across related features

## Methodology

ConceptTracer operates on neural activations A (M samples x N neurons) and concept labels B (M samples x C concepts). Saliency is defined as the normalized mutual information between a neuron's activations and a concept's binary labels. Selectivity is the fraction of a neuron's total saliency attributable to a given concept. Non-parametric permutation testing provides significance thresholds. The dashboard computes these metrics for all neuron-concept pairs and displays them through an interactive interface with Pareto fronts. Experiments use TabPFN 6.3.2 on the MIMIC-IV-ED dataset with ICD diagnostic codes as concepts at three hierarchical levels (354 low-level, 282 mid-level, 24 high-level concepts).

## Results & Highlights

- TabPFN achieves AUC 0.79-0.85 across four ED triage tasks
- Less than 1% of neurons are significantly salient and selective for specific concepts
- Pareto front neurons span initial, middle, and final layers (no preferential layer representation)
- Sparse probes (SHAP) and optimal probing only partially recover the global Pareto front
- Identified clinically meaningful neuron-concept pairs: e.g., neuron 162 in layer 0 selective for ICD E87 (fluid/electrolyte disorders)

## Critical Assessment

ConceptTracer is a solid tools paper that addresses a real gap in the mechanistic interpretability toolkit -- systematic concept-level analysis of neural representations. The information-theoretic metrics are well-defined and the permutation testing provides statistical rigor. However, several limitations are apparent: (1) the tool is demonstrated only on TabPFN, a tabular foundation model, and it is unclear how well it scales to the much larger representation spaces of LLMs; (2) the finding that less than 1% of neurons are interpretable could be a limitation of the method (binary concept labels may be too coarse) rather than a genuine property of the model; (3) no comparison with Sparse Autoencoder-based approaches which are the current standard for feature discovery; (4) the paper is more of a demonstration than a research contribution -- the metrics themselves are straightforward applications of mutual information.

## Strengths

- Well-designed interactive tool with clear four-view analysis framework
- Rigorous statistical methodology with permutation testing and Pareto front analysis
- Timely application to TabPFN, an under-studied model in interpretability research
- Clinical domain relevance adds practical impact

## Limitations

- Demonstrated only on tabular data with TabPFN; scalability to LLMs untested
- Binary concept labels may be too coarse to capture nuanced neuron behavior
- No comparison with SAE-based feature discovery methods
- The paper is primarily a tools demonstration with limited novel analytical findings

## Relevance

关联隐藏状态与可解释性研究：ConceptTracer 提供了一种系统化的方法来分析神经表示中哪些神经元对特定人类可理解概念敏感，这对理解模型内部编码方式具有直接价值。saliency 和 selectivity 的信息论定义为量化可解释性提供了严格的数学框架，对 LLM 的表示分析和安全性评估有参考意义。

## Questions for Further Exploration

- How does ConceptTracer scale to LLM hidden states with thousands of dimensions and continuous concept labels?
- Can the saliency/selectivity framework be extended to discover polysemantic neurons that respond to concept combinations?
- How do the discovered interpretable neurons in TabPFN compare with attention-based explanations for the same predictions?
- What is the relationship between concept-selective neurons and sparse autoencoder features?
