# The Illusion of Stochasticity in LLMs

> **一句话总结：** 系统性揭示LLM作为智能体时无法可靠地从特定分布采样——即使模型能正确推断最优策略，其内部概率估计到随机输出的映射存在根本缺陷，前沿模型虽可通过随机种子转换分布，但直接采样能力严重不足。

> **TL;DR:** LLMs fundamentally fail to map their internal probability estimates to stochastic outputs when acting as agents, with rigorous empirical analysis across multiple model families showing that while frontier models can convert random seeds to target distributions, their ability to sample directly from specific distributions is fundamentally flawed.

## 核心贡献

- 揭示LLM在智能体场景中的根本性缺陷：无法可靠地将内部概率估计映射为随机输出
- 系统性实验覆盖多个模型家族、规模、提示风格和分布类型，量化了采样失败的严重程度
- 发现前沿模型（Gemini-2.5-Pro/3.0-Pro）可通过随机种子转换分布，但直接从分布采样仍存在根本缺陷
- 提出"知道-做到"差距的新解释：即使模型知道正确策略，无法按照该策略随机行动是独立且根本的失败模式

## Key Contributions

- Identifies reliable stochastic sampling as a fundamental yet unfulfilled requirement for LLM-based agents
- Rigorous empirical analysis across multiple model families (Gemini, others), sizes, prompting styles, and distributions
- Demonstrates that frontier LLMs can convert provided random seeds to target distributions (a deterministic process) but fail at direct sampling from specific distributions
- Proposes that the "knowing-doing gap" in agentic settings partly stems from this fundamental stochasticity failure

## Methodology

The paper first motivates why stochastic behavior is crucial for agentic LLMs (exploration in RL, mixed-strategy equilibria, randomized algorithms). It then systematically tests LLMs' ability to sample from distributions of varying complexity: uniform distributions (e.g., MCQ answer selection), discrete distributions over small action spaces, and continuous distributions. The key experimental manipulation compares: (1) direct sampling (ask the LLM to generate a random sample), (2) seed-based sampling (provide a random seed and code to convert), and (3) distribution conversion (transform one known distribution to another). This decomposition isolates whether the failure is in generating randomness or in applying distribution transforms.

## Results & Highlights

- LLMs heavily biased toward certain outputs (e.g., "C" in MCQ) rather than sampling uniformly
- Frontier models (Gemini-2.5-Pro, Gemini-3.0-Pro) can simulate PRNGs and convert between distributions using CoT
- Direct sampling from specific distributions is fundamentally flawed across all tested models
- The failure is not in understanding the distribution but in generating random numbers
- PRNG simulation via CoT is computationally prohibitive for repeated sampling and stateful (ill-suited for stateless inference)
- Distribution conversion also fails when transformation complexity increases

## Critical Assessment

This paper identifies a genuinely fundamental and underappreciated limitation of LLMs as agents. The decomposition into "generating randomness" vs. "understanding distributions" vs. "converting between distributions" is elegant and revealing. The finding that LLMs can convert seeds but not generate randomness directly points to a clear architectural limitation. However, the paper could benefit from more formal analysis of why autoregressive models struggle with randomness generation. The practical implications are significant -- any deployment of LLMs as RL agents or in game-theoretic settings needs external randomness injection. The work could also explore whether fine-tuning on random number generation tasks can mitigate the issue.

## Strengths

- Identifies a fundamental, underappreciated limitation with significant practical implications
- Elegant experimental decomposition isolating the specific failure mode
- High-authority team from Google DeepMind
- Clear motivation connecting stochasticity failure to the broader "knowing-doing gap"
- Demonstrates that the problem is not in distribution comprehension but in randomness generation

## Limitations

- Lacks formal theoretical analysis of why autoregressive models fail at randomness generation
- Does not explore whether fine-tuning or architectural changes can mitigate the issue
- Limited to Google's Gemini models for the main experiments (other frontier models could be tested)
- The PRNG simulation approach is interesting but not a practical solution

## Relevance

对理解LLM作为智能体的可靠性具有重要价值。随机采样能力缺失是一个基础性问题，影响所有需要LLM执行随机策略的应用场景。这一发现对LLM推理可靠性和输出一致性的研究有重要启示，提示需要在系统层面引入外部随机性机制。
