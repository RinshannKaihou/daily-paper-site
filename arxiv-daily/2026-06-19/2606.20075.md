> **一句话总结：** 本文从信息论视角剖析潜在思维链（Latent CoT）的监督失效，将其诊断为"双重坍缩"——沿优化路径的梯度衰减与潜在空间的流形漂移；将过程监督分解为轨迹监督（逐步注入因果信息）与空间监督（语义锚定），并通过统一潜在探针（ULP）度量潜在状态与显式推理步骤的互信息，在 GPT-2 + GSM8k-Aug 上验证了"信息-性能绑定"——推理准确率严格受限于潜在链中保留的互信息，且生成式重构远优于几何压缩。

> **TL;DR:** This ICML 2026 paper gives an information-theoretic account of why outcome-only supervision fails for Latent Chain-of-Thought reasoning, diagnosing the failure as a "dual collapse" (gradient attenuation + manifold drift), decomposing process supervision into Trajectory Supervision (sequential information injection) and Space Supervision (semantic anchoring), introducing the variational Unified Latent Probe (ULP) to measure I(latent state; explicit step), and empirically demonstrating on GPT-2/GSM8k-Aug that reasoning accuracy is strictly upper-bounded by the mutual information preserved in the latent chain — with generative reconstruction beating geometric compression.

## 核心贡献

- **双重坍缩的诊断机理**：从优化与表征两个层面，将纯结果监督（OS）失效归因为 (i) **梯度衰减**——答案级信号在沿潜在链传播时逐位衰减，后期潜在位置几乎收不到梯度，退化为"结构性捷径"（类比梯度饥饿 gradient starvation）；(ii) **流形漂移**——中间潜在状态失去语义锚定，PCA 轨迹显示其发散到无结构区域。给出了机理而非经验性的解释。
- **过程监督的双维分解框架**：将过程监督显式拆为 **轨迹监督**（最大化局部逐步互信息 I(L≤k; S_{k+1})，注入因果信息降低条件熵）与 **空间监督**（在符号空间或潜在空间约束单个潜在状态），为设计监督信号提供清晰坐标系。
- **几何压缩 vs 生成式重构的对比结论（含理论论证）**：几何压缩（GC，对每步平均 embedding 做 MSE）既受信息上界限制（将推理路径压成固定几何点本身就有损），又在优化上失效（高维下欧氏距离无法约束方向对齐）；生成式重构（GR，辅助解码器恢复文本步骤）最小化条件熵 H(S_t|L_t)，是 I(L_t;S_t) 的变分下界，作为"语义系绳"保留内在维度。
- **统一潜在探针 ULP**：一个变分重构式诊断框架——冻结各基线 checkpoint，训练单个共享条件解码器 q_φ(S_t|L_t) 在统一探测数据集上恢复逐步推理步骤；其收敛重构损失 L_Info 是 H(S_t|L_t) 的上界，从而给出 I(L_t;S_t) 的下界，量化"潜在状态里还保留多少可恢复推理信息"。
- **信息-性能绑定（Information-Performance Binding）**：实验显示推理准确率与 ULP 损失呈严格反相关，被信息保真度严格上界约束；PS-GR 达到最优前沿（39.1%），OS 变体重构损失高、退化为不可解释的捷径特征。
- **额外机理发现**：(i) 插入新潜在步骤会带来梯度尖峰（主动适应而非衰减）；(ii) **优化器重置（optimizer reset）** 对诱导有效训练信号至关重要，可避免"陈旧动量"压制梯度（PS-Latent w/o Reset 比 w/ Reset 低 6.5 个百分点）；(iii) **粒度 g** 越大性能越好但有效率-容量权衡；(iv) 自回归推理中存在普适的**时空信息衰减**，GR 充当"周期性校准"重置语义漂移。
- **可复现性**：代码开源 https://github.com/EIT-NLP/Supervision-in-Latent-CoT ；发表于 ICML 2026（PMLR 306）。

## Key Contributions

- **Mechanistic dual-collapse diagnosis**: Outcome supervision (OS) fails for two coupled, formally characterized reasons — (i) *gradient attenuation*: answer-level signal decays position-wise along the latent chain (G(t)=‖∂L_OS/∂L_t‖ concentrated on L_1, near-zero on L_2..6), producing an early-improve-then-degrade pattern and a structural shortcut reminiscent of gradient starvation; (ii) *manifold drift*: PCA trajectories show intermediate latent states diverge into unstructured regions, losing semantic tethering to the explicit-CoT embedding space.
- **Two-dimensional decomposition of process supervision**: Trajectory Supervision maximizes local stepwise mutual information I(L_≤k; S_{k+1}) (sequential information injection that lowers conditional entropy), while Space Supervision constrains individual latent states either via Geometric Compression (GC: MSE to averaged step embeddings) or Generative Reconstruction (GR: auxiliary decoder recovering the textual step).
- **Analytical verdict: GR > GC, with formal grounding**: GC is doubly flawed — an oracle experiment shows even "ground-truth" compressed embeddings cap at 41.23% (below Explicit CoT), proving information loss from compressing a reasoning path to a fixed point, and high-dimensional MSE cannot constrain directional alignment. GR minimizes H(S_t|L_t) and thus maximizes a variational lower bound on I(L_t;S_t), preserving intrinsic dimensionality.
- **Unified Latent Probe (ULP)**: A variational reconstruction-based diagnostic that freezes baseline checkpoints and trains one shared conditional decoder q_φ(S_t|L_t) over a unified probing dataset; the converged reconstruction loss L_Info upper-bounds H(S_t|L_t), yielding a quantitative lower bound on mutual information and operationalizing "how much structured reasoning is recoverable."
- **Information-Performance Binding**: A striking inverse correlation — reasoning accuracy is strictly upper-bounded by retained information fidelity. PS-GR sits on the optimal frontier (39.1% peak acc), while OS variants exhibit high reconstruction loss and degenerate into uninterpretable shortcut features.
- **Additional mechanistic findings**: (i) Inserting new latent steps causes gradient spikes (active adaptation); (ii) *optimizer reset* is essential to induce "exploration shock" and avoid stale momentum (PS-Latent w/o Reset loses 6.5 pts); (iii) higher granularity g improves accuracy (efficiency vs capacity trade-off); (iv) a universal spatiotemporal information decay along the autoregressive chain, against which GR acts as a recurrent calibration that periodically resets semantic drift.
- **Reproducibility & venue**: Code released at https://github.com/EIT-NLP/Supervision-in-Latent-CoT ; published at ICML 2026 (PMLR 306).

## Methodology

**Problem formalization.** Reasoning is cast as learning p(y|x) with latent intermediate states L=(L_1,…,L_T), each L_t∈R^{g×d} (g = latent granularity). The paper introduces a *Representational Interface Gap* argument: explicit CoT is bandwidth-limited by I(R;C|x) ≤ H(C|x) ≤ N·log_2|V|, whereas latent states satisfy I(R;L̃|x) ≤ T·g·d·B_eff, and since d·B_eff can substantially exceed log_2|V|, latent reasoning can in principle encode richer non-linguistic computation — but high-dimensional states may also encode noise, spurious correlations, or drift from the pretrained semantic structure.

**Diagnosis of the optimization barrier (Section 3).** Testing the hypothesis that latent dynamics should emerge from outcome supervision alone, the authors train GPT-2 on GSM8k-Aug with NLL over the final answer. All OS variants (OS-No-CoT, OS-Latent, OS-Latent-proj) fail to beat the trivial no-CoT baseline (≈18.7%) and fall far short of Explicit CoT (43.1%). They trace this to (a) position-wise gradient attenuation (Fig 2b) and (b) manifold drift visualized via PCA (Fig 2c).

**Process supervision framework (Section 4).** A progressive stage-wise training scheme: at stage k the model generates the first k reasoning steps as continuous latent blocks L_≤k followed by discrete steps; the objective (Eq. 10) maximizes local stepwise MI I(L_≤k; S_{k+1}). Space supervision is added via a joint loss L = L_stage-k + λ·Σ L_Align(L_t,S_t), with L_Align ∈ {L_GC (MSE to averaged embeddings), L_GR (cross-entropy through a jointly-trained decoder D_ψ)}. Optimizer reset between stages is shown necessary to avoid stale momentum (Fig 3).

**Information-theoretic view & ULP (Section 5).** Because direct MI estimation is intractable, they use a step-wise variational proxy: I(L_t;S_t)=H(S_t)−H(S_t|L_t), and since H(S_t) is dataset-constant, maximizing MI ⇔ minimizing H(S_t|L_t). A parametric probe q_φ(S_t|L_t) gives L_Info(L_t,S_t)=E[−log q_φ(S_t|L_t)] ≥ H(S_t|L_t). They freeze best checkpoints of all baselines and train a *single* ULP architecture over all generated latent states (shared conditional decoder + per-position heads), so reconstruction loss is directly comparable across models.

**Experimental setup.** Backbone GPT-2; dataset GSM8k-Aug (Deng et al. 2023); baselines span OS variants and PS variants (PS-Latent, PS-GC, PS-GR); ablations on granularity g∈{1,2,3,~1-per-token}; oracle/upper-bound experiments for GC targets and maximal granularity.

## Results & Highlights

- **Optimization barrier confirmed**: OS-Latent/OS-Latent-proj ≈ 18.7%, no better than OS-No-CoT, far below Explicit CoT 43.1% (Table 1). Gradient norm concentrated on L_1, near-zero downstream.
- **Trajectory supervision works, but needs optimizer reset**: PS-Latent w/ Reset reaches 31.2% (S6) vs 24.7% without reset — a 6.5 pt gap (Table 3).
- **GC is actively harmful; GR is optimal**: PS-GC degrades to 23.2% (worse than PS-Latent), while PS-GR reaches 39.1% — an 8.0 pt gain over PS-Latent and 7.9 pt over PS-GC (Table 3). GC oracle ceiling (41.23%) sits below Explicit CoT, confirming information loss from compression.
- **Granularity matters**: accuracy rises with g (g=1→30.4%, g=2→35.1%, g=3→39.4%, ~1-per-token converges toward but does not exceed Explicit CoT), showing structural imitation is bounded by explicit-supervision quality (Table 2).
- **Information-Performance Binding (Fig 4)**: strict inverse correlation between ULP loss and accuracy across {OS-GC, OS-GR, OS-Latent, PS-GC, PS-Latent, PS-GR}; PS-GR sits on the optimal frontier with lowest probe loss.
- **Spatiotemporal information decay (Fig 5)**: ULP loss accumulates along L_1→L_6 for all models (universal autoregressive drift); unaligned/GC baselines collapse catastrophically while GR periodically resets the drift, keeping the whole trajectory a faithful reasoning carrier.

## Critical Assessment

- **Novelty**: The dual-collapse diagnosis and the Trajectory-vs-Space decomposition are a clean, teachable re-vocabularying of known failure modes (weak outcome signal, latent drift), and the real new artifacts are ULP and the Information-Performance Binding. The GR-vs-GC contrast is conceptually anticipated by JEPA-vs-MAE debates the authors themselves cite, but applying it rigorously to *latent CoT supervision* with a variational MI bound is a genuine contribution. Mid-to-high novelty.
- **Rigor**: Stronger than typical. The gradient-attenuation and manifold-drift claims are backed by measured gradient norms and PCA trajectories (not just narrative). ULP is given a proper variational interpretation (lower bound on MI via upper bound on conditional entropy), and the single-shared-probe protocol makes cross-model comparison fair. The GC oracle experiment is a thoughtful control.
- **Reproducibility**: Good — code released, venue-grade, standard GPT-2 + GSM8k-Aug setup. Caveat: ULP probe capacity and probing-dataset construction details live in appendices; fidelity of MI estimates depends on probe quality.
- **Significance**: If the Information-Performance Binding generalizes beyond GPT-2 scale, it is a genuinely prescriptive result — it tells practitioners to *measure and maximize* MI with reasoning steps, not just add outcome loss. The optimizer-reset and granularity findings are immediately actionable.
- **Red flags**: (a) **Scale**: all experiments on GPT-2 with GSM8k-Aug — the authors themselves flag this as the main limitation; whether the binding holds at 7B+/harder reasoning is unproven. (b) **Circularity risk**: ULP is a reconstruction-of-explicit-steps objective; since explicit steps are themselves predictive of correctness, "low ULP loss → high accuracy" could be partly tautological. The paper mitigates this (OS-GR has a reconstruction objective yet still fails without trajectory supervision), but the binding's causal strength is not isolated. (c) **Dependence on process annotations**: PS requires ground-truth rationales, limiting scalability to annotation-free regimes. (d) "Shift to mutual information maximization" is partly slogan until instantiated as a general training objective beyond the staged PS-GR recipe.

## Strengths

- Mechanistic, measurement-backed diagnosis (gradient norms, PCA, oracle ceilings) rather than hand-waving.
- Clean two-axis framework (Trajectory/Space) that maps directly onto design decisions.
- Variational MI grounding for ULP gives it theoretical legitimacy, not just an empirical probe.
- Strong, surprising negative result: GC is actively harmful, with a principled explanation (info upper-bound + directional misalignment in high-D).
- Actionable practical knobs documented (optimizer reset, granularity trade-off).
- ICML 2026 publication + open code.

## Limitations

- Single backbone (GPT-2) and single task family (GSM8k-Aug arithmetic reasoning); generalization to larger models / broader reasoning domains unverified — explicitly acknowledged by authors.
- Requires ground-truth process annotations (rationale steps), limiting applicability where step labels are unavailable.
- ULP MI estimate is bounded by the variational probe's capacity — likely a conservative (loose) lower bound on true information content; absolute MI values should not be over-interpreted.
- Partial circularity between ULP (reconstruct explicit steps) and downstream accuracy (steps predict correctness); causal isolation of the binding is incomplete.
- Compute/scalability cost of dense Trajectory Supervision + generative reconstruction decoder is not characterized.
- PS-GR converges toward but does not exceed Explicit CoT at max granularity, so the "transcend discrete CoT" promise remains aspirational.

## Questions for Further Exploration

- Does the Information-Performance Binding hold at 7B+ scale and on non-arithmetic reasoning (code, multi-hop QA, planning), or is it a small-model/structured-task phenomenon?
- Is the binding causal? Can one intervene to *raise* ULP (e.g., auxiliary MI objective at train time) and reliably raise accuracy without changing other factors?
- How sensitive are ULP's absolute MI estimates to probe capacity, probing-dataset size, and the choice of variational family — i.e., how tight is the bound in practice?
- Can trajectory supervision be made annotation-free (e.g., self-generated or model-inferred step labels) while preserving the scaffolding effect?
- How does the GC-vs-GR conclusion interact with modern latent-structure methods (SAEs / dictionary learning / JEPA-style targets) — could SAE features serve as a richer "space supervision" target than either GC or GR?
- The optimizer-reset finding suggests a curriculum/stage-transition signal; can this be automated or made continuous rather than discrete-stage?
- Does the universal autoregressive information decay (Fig 5) imply a principled limit on latent CoT depth, and can GR-style calibration be applied at *inference* time (not just training)?
