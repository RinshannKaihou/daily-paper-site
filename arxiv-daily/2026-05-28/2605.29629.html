Beyond Attack Success Rate: Temporal Logit Observability for LLM Safety Failures

TLO tracks the compliance-refusal logit margin during decoding, mapping each model-attack pair onto a calibrated 2D plane. Attacks with identical ASR land at different coordinates. An early-stop rule derived from the first five tokens cuts aggregate ASR by 26.5 percentage points with zero false positives on benign queries.

Core Method

TLO computes a Logit-Margin Score at each decoding step — the difference between mean compliance-logit and mean refusal-logit over a fixed 47-phrase English lexicon. This scalar trajectory St is then calibrated against harmful and benign anchor distributions to produce comparable 2D coordinates across models and attacks.

Key design property: RP displacement is bounded by (1−ASR), making TLO most informative exactly where ASR is least informative — the mid-ASR range.

Diagnostic Pipeline

1
Temporal Logit Signal
Compute S_t = mu_cmp(t) - mu_ref(t) at each decoding step. Extract S_0 (pre-generation) and S-bar (generation mean).
2
Activation Timing
t_cross = first step where S_t < 0 (refusal dominance). Sign reversal occurs 5.2× more often in failed jailbreaks.
3
RP Calibration
RP(M) = (M_success - M_harmful) / (M_benign - M_harmful) applied to S_0 yields RP_A; to S-bar yields RP_B. Euclidean distance D_RP between conditions.

RP-Plane Geometry

Conditions with near-identical ASR land at distinct locations on the calibrated 2D plane, revealing failure-mechanism differences invisible to the scalar metric.

Early-Stop Intervention

The t_cross-based halt rule intervenes after observing only the first 5 generated tokens. Effectiveness varies by model-attack pair, with largest drops in NGB conditions (delta = 40.3 pp).

Robustness Checks

  • Stochastic decoding: ICC = 0.90–0.94 for trajectory metrics across temperature/p sampling
  • Lexicon perturbations: |delta AUC| ≤ 0.018 across 5 variants
  • Margin vs. refusal-only: margin outperforms by 0.162 AUC (common-mode rejection)
  • Rank-based RP: Pearson r = 0.858 / 0.920 / 0.925 for RP_A / RP_B / displacement
  • Reproducibility: training-free, single RTX 4090, < 58 min total

Limitations

  • Full logit access required: top-k=20 (API standard) gives near-zero correlation (r = −0.12 to −0.01)
  • English-only lexicon: the fixed 47-phrase set is language-specific
  • Qwen boundary case: 4/5 NGB conditions involve Qwen; Qwen+GCG AUC is near chance (0.519)
  • Observational, not causal: makes dynamics visible but does not identify circuits
  • Small n at low ASR: Llama+MCM and Mistral+MCM have nsucc = 3
  • No adaptive-attack evaluation

Strengths

  • Genuinely novel temporal framing for safety evaluation
  • Exceptional transparency: every design choice has a sensitivity analysis in the appendix
  • Training-free, logit-only, single GPU, < 1 hour
  • Honest failure reporting (Qwen boundary, API limitation, NGB conditions)
  • Elegant (1–ASR) complementarity bound
  • Supplementary archive with reproduction scripts sets a strong example

Concerns

  • Data-driven lexicon transfer gap: off-diagonal AUC drops from ~0.93 to ~0.65, challenging model-portability claims
  • Early-stop not independent of diagnostic: both use the same t_cross signal; untested against attacks that delay crossing
  • 2D plane is lossy: per-condition AUC ranges 0.511–0.987; some conditions near chance even with full trajectory
  • Attack-formatted benign stress test: 46.0% false halt rate (S_0-gated variant reduces to 12.6%)

Open Questions for Further Exploration

Q1
How does TLO perform against adaptive attacks explicitly optimizing to delay or suppress the LMS crossing signal?
Q2
Can lightweight distilled monitors approximate TLO from limited logit access (e.g., top-k at k ≥ 50)?
Q3
Does RP-plane geometry change qualitatively for reasoning models (o1, DeepSeek-R1) with chain-of-thought decoding?
Q4
How would per-model lexicon induction affect RP reliability given the observed transfer gap?
Q5
Can TLO extend to multilingual settings, and how do different tokenizers affect cross-model comparability?
Q6
What is the relationship between TLO's temporal failure patterns and specific mechanistic circuits from causal tracing?
