To See the Unseen: Embedding Collapse in Transformers

Nevena Lazić · Liam Fowl · András György · Csaba Szepesvári  (Google DeepMind)

在带 weight decay 和 LayerNorm 的标准训练下，未见过 token 的 (un)embedding 向量必然坍缩（cosine 相似度 0.23–0.78），在 Gemma 3（1B–27B）上实证验证，坍缩 token 微调收敛慢 10 倍。三要素修复：copy attention + 高符号多样性 + 冻结/重置 embedding。 A rigorous DeepMind paper proving unseen-token embedding collapse is mathematically inevitable under weight decay + LayerNorm SGD — independent of architecture or dataset size. Empirically confirmed in Gemma 3 where collapsed embeddings cause 10× slower fine-tuning. Fix: copy attention + frozen embeddings + symbolic diversity.

Yongcan Yu et al.

精准诊断 TTRL 中 GRPO 归一化对低共识组虚假奖励的放大，BCS+DAE+COR 三组件框架在 Qwen2.5-Math-1.5B 上相对 ETMR 基线 +6.7%，COR 离策精调仅需 3–5 分钟。 First rigorous characterization of why GRPO-based TTRL is unstable. Fixed-advantage DAE eliminates normalization amplification. +6.7% on Qwen2.5-Math-1.5B vs. ETMR baseline; 3–5 min off-policy COR stage.

Clock Skew Silently Breaks Causal Observability in Distributed AI Inference

Ankur Sharma · Deep Shah · David Lariviere · Hesham ElBakoury  (OCP)

仅 3–5ms 时钟偏差静默破坏因果可观测性（负时间跨度、乱序事件），吞吐量完全不受影响。causality_health 指标在 Kafka 和 ZeroMQ 均可检测，可直接集成进 SLO 框架。 A production-grounded paper: clock skew ≥ 3–5 ms breaks causal observability while leaving throughput/latency metrics entirely green. The causality_health binary metric is transport-agnostic and immediately integrable into SLO dashboards.
