Process Supervision via Verbal Critique Improves Reasoning in Large Language Models

Hao-Yuan (Mark) Chen

arXiv: 2604.21611v1

Date: 2026-04-23

https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.21611

一句话总结： 提出"语言过程监督"（VPS）框架，通过强监督模型对弱演员每一推理步骤提供自然语言批评替代标量奖励，在 GPQA Diamond 上以纯语言反馈达到 94.9% SOTA，在 AIME 2025 上将弱演员从 11.7% 提升至 90.0%。
TL;DR: VPS identifies critique granularity as a fourth, independent axis of inference-time scaling. A stronger supervisor LLM provides step-indexed natural-language critique to guide an actor through an iterative generate–critique–refine loop, outperforming both outcome-level verbal critique (Reflexion) by +8.5 to +12.1 pp and matched-compute sample aggregation (SC@5) across GPQA Diamond, AIME 2025, and LiveCodeBench V6.

核心贡献

  • 发现第四条推理扩展轴：在链深度、样本广度、学习评分器之外，提出"外部语言监督粒度"作为独立的推理时间扩展轴，既无需梯度更新也无需人工标注。
  • VPS 框架：形式化步骤级语言过程监督，监督者对每个推理步骤标记"正确/部分正确/错误"并给出针对性修正，演员保留正确步骤、仅重新生成错误步骤。
  • 弱演员救援机制：在 AIME 2025 上，将 11.7% 的弱演员提升至 63.3%–90.0%，绝对提升 +51.6 至 +63.3 pp，揭示"监督者余量"（r = +0.90）是 VPS 收益的强预测变量。
  • 有原则的领域边界：代码合成任务中，语言监督对所有开放权重模型失效（-28 至 -64 pp），揭示语言批评的可表达边界，并提出混合语言-可执行监督作为自然扩展。

Key Contributions

  • Fourth scaling axis formalized: Critique granularity — distinct from chain depth, sample breadth, and learned scorers — is proposed and empirically validated as an orthogonal axis requiring no training cost, no human labels, and no additional sampling.
  • VPS algorithm: A generate–critique–refine loop where the supervisor issues step-indexed critiques, the actor regenerates conditioned on the full trajectory and critique, and iteration terminates on convergence or budget R. Reduces the effective refinement search space from the full trajectory to a single failing step.
  • Weak-actor rescue regime: On AIME 2025, actors at 11.7% standalone accuracy are lifted to 63.3–90.0% with strong supervisors, yielding absolute gains of +51.6 to +63.3 pp — the most statistically robust result in the paper.
  • Supervisor headroom predictor: Pearson r = +0.90 between (supervisor accuracy − actor accuracy) and VPS gain, providing a deployment criterion before running VPS.
  • Principled domain boundary: Verbal supervision fails on code synthesis regardless of headroom, motivating hybrid verbal–executable supervision as a natural extension.

Methodology

VPS frames inference-time policy improvement as conditional regeneration in language space. The actor $\pi_\theta$ generates a trajectory $\tau = (s_1, \ldots, s_T)$; the supervisor $\mathcal{C}$ produces a structured natural-language critique $c = \mathcal{C}(\tau, x)$ labelling each step as correct, partially correct (with a targeted fix), or incorrect. The actor then regenerates $\tau_{r+1} \sim \pi_\theta(\cdot \mid x, \tau_r, c_r)$. The update operator $\mathcal{F}(\tau_r, \mathcal{C}(\tau_r, x))$ is iterated for at most $R$ rounds or until a CONVERGED signal is emitted.

Three benchmarks are used: GPQA Diamond (n=198, graduate-level multiple-choice science), AIME 2025 (n=30, competition math), and LiveCodeBench V6 (competitive programming, pass@1). Four supervisor–actor pairs are evaluated: GPT-5.4 (High|Low), GLM-5.1|Nemotron-3-Super, Gemma 4 (31B)|GPT-OSS (20B), and GPT-OSS (120B|20B). Baseline comparisons are held at matched inference compute: Self-Consistency@5 (5× actor tokens, no supervisor) and Reflexion (same supervisor as VPS, outcome-level critique only — isolating granularity as the operative variable).

Results & Highlights

  • GPQA Diamond: GPT-5.4 (High|Low) reaches 94.9% at R=4, exceeding the 94.1% reported SOTA and +2.1 pp over actor standalone. VPS beats Reflexion by +8.5 pp and SC@5 by +5.0 pp.
  • AIME 2025: GPT-5.4 Nano (High|Low) reaches 90.0% at R=3, a +63.3 pp absolute gain over the 26.7% actor. Weak-actor pairs generally lift to 63–90%.
  • LiveCodeBench V6: GPT-5.4 Mini (High|Low) reaches 50.0% at R=4 (no actor standalone reported), beating Reflexion by +12.1 pp and SC@5 by +8.3 pp. Open-weight pairs all degrade below actor standalone.
  • Round-count non-monotonicity: 9 of 11 configurations show interior peaks; optimal R varies by pair, arguing against a fixed round count and motivating adaptive stopping.
  • Degradation regime: When actor ≥ supervisor (GLM-5.1|Nemotron on AIME, actor 90.2% vs. supervisor 92.7%), VPS degrades by >10 pp below actor standalone.

Authors & Credibility

Single author: Hao-Yuan (Mark) Chen, affiliated with Mindify AI Research and University of London. The paper is a preprint with no listed peer review. The Mindify AI affiliation is a small/independent lab with limited public track record. All main-body numbers are single-run point estimates with no seed averaging; the author explicitly flags this and promises multi-seed results in a camera-ready version. The statistical appendix is unusually careful and transparent about variance.

Critical Assessment

Novelty: The step-level verbal critique framing is incremental over Reflexion (Shinn et al., 2023); the key empirical contribution is the controlled ablation isolating granularity as the operative variable. The "fourth axis" framing is a useful conceptual contribution but is primarily marketing for an ablation study.

Rigor: Single-run point estimates are a significant limitation acknowledged by the author. The GPQA SOTA margin (+2.1 pp, ±3.6 pp at 95% CI on n=198) is within variance and requires multi-seed confirmation. AIME weak-actor rescue (+51–63 pp on n=30) is statistically robust. The matched-compute comparison with Reflexion is well-designed.

Reproducibility: The paper does not release code or supervisor prompts in full (Appendix G gives the prompt design but not the exact system prompts). The Reflexion baseline prompt design is explicitly specified and verified, which is commendable.

Significance: Practically, VPS requires a stronger supervisor than the actor — a constraint that limits deployment to settings where such a supervisor exists. The code domain boundary is a meaningful negative result. The headroom predictor (r=0.90) is the most actionable finding.

Red flags: Single-author preprint from a low-profile affiliation. No camera-ready version yet. One LiveCodeBench row is explicitly incomplete. The claim of "state of the art of 94.1%" on GPQA is within noise for a single run.

Strengths

  • Cleanly designed ablation isolating critique granularity as the sole variable against Reflexion
  • Transparent statistical appendix with honest variance characterization
  • Headroom predictor provides a practical deployment criterion
  • Principled domain boundary analysis (code synthesis) is an intellectually honest negative result
  • No training, no gradient, no human labels — very low deployment cost

Limitations

  • All main-body numbers are single-run point estimates; GPQA SOTA claim requires multi-seed validation
  • Fails on all open-weight code pairs (domain boundary limits practical scope)
  • VPS benefit requires the supervisor to substantially outperform the actor — not always available
  • Single-author preprint; methodology has not been independently peer-reviewed
  • English-only evaluation; no analysis of non-English reasoning tasks
  • Adaptive stopping (based on headroom prediction) is motivated but not implemented

Relevance

本文提出的 VPS 框架直接涉及 LLM 推理评估与推理时间扩展：以受控消融隔离语言批评粒度的影响，并揭示监督者余量作为预测性部署准则，对研究推理能力边界与评估方法设计有参考价值。
