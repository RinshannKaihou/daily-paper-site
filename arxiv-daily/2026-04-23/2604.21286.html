Cross-Entropy Is Load-Bearing: A Pre-Registered Scope Test of the K-Way Energy Probe on Bidirectional Predictive Coding

arXiv: 2604.21286v1

Date: 2026-04-23

https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.21286

一句话总结： 本文通过预先注册的受控实验证明，在判别式预测编码网络上，K-way能量探针相对于softmax基线的劣势（AUROC²差距−0.082）主要由交叉熵（CE）训练引起的logit尺度膨胀驱动（约占66%），去除CE后差距减半，双向PC（bPC）中探针超越softmax（+0.008），但这不能归因于双向推理动态，而是CE移除效果。
TL;DR: In a pre-registered scope test on CIFAR-10 with a matched 2.1M-parameter TinyConv backbone across 10 seeds, replacing cross-entropy with MSE halves the K-way energy probe's disadvantage vs. softmax (Δ: −0.082 → −0.037). In bidirectional predictive coding (bPC), the probe exceeds softmax (Δ = +0.008), but a pre-registered manipulation check fails — bPC does not produce meaningfully greater latent movement than standard PC (ratio 1.6×, threshold 10×). A post-hoc temperature scaling ablation attributes ~66% of the CE-linked gap to logit-scale inflation and ~34% to a genuine scale-invariant ranking advantage of CE training.

核心贡献

  • 量化CE训练对能量探针与softmax差距的贡献：CE训练产生的logit范数（~11.5）约为MSE/bPC（~0.78）的15倍，温度缩放证实约66%的AUROC²差距来自这种尺度膨胀，而非探针本身的根本限制。
  • 识别两种差距成分：(1) 尺度依赖成分（~66%）：CE链接的logit膨胀改变softmax置信度的排序结构，可通过温度缩放消除；(2) 尺度不变排序优势（~34%）：CE作为严格proper scoring rule训练的表示本身具有更好的正确性预测相关性。
  • 预注册复现：多seed条件下复现了Cacioli [2026]的负面结果（标准PC-CE探针低于softmax），并通过配对设计严格测试CE移除效果。
  • 操作化界定"元认知"：明确将"metacognitive"定义为Type-2对自身Type-1正确性的区分（AUROC²），不暗含人类式自省访问，从而限定了讨论范围。

Key Contributions

  • Pre-registered scope test of the decomposition: The central claim of Cacioli [2026] — that the K-way energy probe reduces approximately to a monotone function of the log-softmax margin under five assumptions including CE at output — is tested by systematically removing CE. This directly addresses A1 (CE at output) and conditionally A3 (inference dynamics).
  • Empirical decomposition via temperature scaling: The post-hoc temperature sweep (T=1 to T=30) provides a clean experimental decomposition of the probe-softmax gap into scale-dependent (~66%) and scale-invariant (~34%) components, offering a practical guidance: "use temperature-scaled softmax as a fairer baseline when comparing alternative confidence signals on CE-trained networks."
  • Honest failure of manipulation check: The H3 manipulation check (bPC latent movement > 10× standard PC) is pre-registered and failed (actual ratio: 1.53–1.67×). The paper correctly downgrades bPC to an additional CE-removal condition rather than a test of bidirectional dynamics.
  • Replication and extension: Multi-seed replication of the previous single-seed result (all 10 seeds negative under stdPC-CE) eliminates seed-level explanations.

Methodology

Architecture: TinyConv backbone (2,144,938 parameters), 4 effective layers. Encoder V-pathway: Conv(3→32)-BN-GELU-MaxPool-Conv(32→64)-BN-GELU-MaxPool-Linear(4096→256)-GELU-Linear(256→10). Generative W-pathway mirrors the encoder. All conditions share the identical structural backbone.

Three Conditions:

  • Condition A (stdPC-CE): Standard discriminative PC with cross-entropy at output. T=13 inference steps.
  • Condition B (stdPC-MSE): Standard PC with MSE at output instead of CE. Isolates A1 (CE formulation) from A3 (dynamics). All other settings identical to A.
  • Condition C (bPC): Bidirectional PC (Oliviers et al. 2025) — bidirectional MSE energy, no CE, T=32 train/T=100 eval. αgen determined by pre-registered calibration sweep (selected: 10⁻⁵).

Optimizer: AdamW (lr=10⁻⁴, weight decay=10⁻⁴), batch 128, 25 epochs. Dataset: CIFAR-10.

Evaluation: K-way energy probe — clamp each of K=10 candidate classes at output, run inference to settling, record settled energy. Confidence margin = E(2) − E(1). Softmax baseline = feedforward logits with no inference loop. Primary outcome: Δ = AUROC²probe − AUROC²softmax (Type-2 AUROC for discriminating correct from incorrect Type-1 predictions).

Pre-registered Hypotheses:

  • H3: bPC latent movement > 10× standard PC (manipulation check). Failed.
  • H2: Mean ΔstdPC < 0 (replication). Confirmed: Δ = −0.082, p < 10⁻⁶.
  • H1: Mean ΔbPC − ΔstdPC > 0 (primary test). Confirmed: D = +0.090, p < 10⁻⁷.
  • H4: Descriptive comparison ΔstdPC-MSE vs ΔstdPC. A1 partially load-bearing.

Post-hoc ablation: Temperature scaling (T=1 to T=30) on stdPC-CE models to decompose the probe-softmax gap into scale-dependent and scale-invariant components.

Results & Highlights

| Condition | Softmax Acc | Probe Acc | Softmax AUROC² | Probe AUROC² | Δ |

|---|---|---|---|---|---|

| stdPC-CE | 78.5% | 69.2% | 0.842 | 0.760 | −0.082 |

| stdPC-MSE | 79.0% | 71.1% | 0.836 | 0.799 | −0.037 |

| bPC | 77.4% | 77.6% | 0.824 | 0.832 | +0.008 |

  • All 10 seeds negative for stdPC-CE; all 10 seeds positive for bPC (Δ = +0.008, d = 2.26).
  • Removing CE alone (MSE condition) reduces probe-softmax gap by 54% (−0.082 → −0.037).
  • CE training produces logit norms 15× larger than MSE or bPC (~11.5 vs. ~0.78).
  • Temperature scaling closes 66% of the gap: softmax AUROC² drops from 0.841 to 0.789 at T≈15 (norm-matched), while probe AUROC² remains at 0.761. Plateau at T≈15–30 shows asymptotic convergence.
  • bPC energy decomposition: Generative chain contributes <1% of total energy (gen/disc ratio ≈ 0.009), confirming the dynamics manipulation did not functionally manifest.
  • Scale-invariant remainder (−0.027): CE as a strictly proper scoring rule produces representations whose logit ordering better correlates with correctness independent of scale.

Critical Assessment

Novelty: Moderate for the ML community, higher for the predictive coding community. The temperature scaling decomposition is a clean methodological contribution. The explicit isolation of logit-scale inflation as the dominant mechanism behind the probe-softmax gap is new.

Rigor: High within its narrow scope. The pre-registration, multi-seed design (10 seeds), paired within-seed comparisons, and manipulation check are methodologically exemplary. The H3 failure is honestly reported and the interpretation is correctly downgraded. Normality tests (Shapiro-Wilk) and non-parametric backups (Wilcoxon) are included.

Reproducibility: High. Pre-registration on OSF, code on GitHub, exact hyperparameters specified, consumer GPU tractable — this is fully reproducible by any researcher.

Significance: Meaningful to the predictive coding / biologically plausible learning literature. The result clarifies a previous negative finding (probe underperforms softmax on CE-trained PC) by attributing most of the deficit to training objective effects rather than architectural limitations of structural probing. Practical implication: comparisons between energy probes and softmax should use temperature-scaled softmax as baseline.

Red Flags:

  1. Very narrow scope: Single architecture (TinyConv, 2.1M), single dataset (CIFAR-10, 10 classes). The authors are transparent about this but the scope limitation is real.
  2. bPC dynamics manipulation failed: The primary intended architectural test (bidirectional dynamics) did not manifest. bPC serves only as a second CE-removal condition, not a test of inference dynamics — this substantially reduces the paper's architectural claim.
  3. Small Δ for bPC: +0.008 AUROC², while statistically significant, is tiny in absolute terms. Both probe and softmax produce similar AUROC² values (~0.82–0.84) on this easy dataset.
  4. CIFAR-10 difficulty ceiling: At ~78% accuracy with a 2.1M parameter model, the task may be too easy for meaningful metacognitive differentiation. On harder tasks, the dynamics effects might be larger.

Strengths

  • Pre-registration with filed OSF link provides full pre-analysis transparency.
  • Manipulation check failure is not buried — it gates the interpretation correctly.
  • Temperature scaling ablation is elegant and provides actionable practical guidance.
  • Clean 3-condition design (CE → MSE → bPC) creates a coherent causal gradient.
  • Honest scope: the abstract, introduction, and conclusion all clearly bound what is and is not claimed.

Limitations

  • Single small architecture (TinyConv) on single dataset (CIFAR-10) — results cannot be directly extrapolated to deeper networks or other tasks.
  • The generative chain contributes <1% of energy at the tested αgen — bPC's potential at higher αgen or deeper architectures remains untested.
  • Scale (only classification, not language modeling, regression, or generation tasks).
  • The practical relevance of the energy probe's metacognitive advantage is unclear given the tiny absolute magnitude (+0.008).
  • No comparison to other confidence measures (e.g., Monte Carlo dropout, deep ensembles).

Relevance

本文与推理评估和模型可解释性的交叉点有一定关联：它提供了一个关于置信度校准机制（交叉熵训练如何影响内部置信度信号与softmax基线的关系）的精细分析，对评估LLM内部表示质量和训练目标对自我评估能力影响有参考意义，但直接相关性较弱（主要针对小规模视觉分类网络中的预测编码）。

Questions for Further Exploration

  1. Does the temperature-scaling decomposition hold at deeper architectures (e.g., ResNet, ViT) and more complex datasets (CIFAR-100, ImageNet)? Does the 66/34 split remain stable?
  2. Can the 34% scale-invariant ranking advantage of CE be recovered by the energy probe via a learned monotone transformation of energy margins (rather than softmax probability)?
  3. What happens with prospective configuration (Song et al. 2024) — does satisfying assumption A3 via genuinely bidirectional dynamics change the result when the generative chain contributes meaningfully?
  4. Does the logit-scale inflation mechanism observed here have analogues in large language models trained with CE — does it explain why LLM confidence scores are poorly calibrated?
  5. Is there a training objective intermediate between CE and MSE (e.g., label smoothing, focal loss) that reduces logit inflation while retaining CE's ranking advantage?
