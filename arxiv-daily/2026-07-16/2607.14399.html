Why this matters

Honesty-eval verdicts are artifacts of the grammar, the disclosure, the rendering, and the voice — not only of the model. Any benchmark that ignores these knobs misattributes its conclusions to the agent.

Critical edge

One player–narrator pairing, one constructed world, one genre. Effect sizes are explicitly not expected to transport — this is a demonstrated warning, not a generalizable claim.

What the knobs moved

  • Grammar (Table 2): strong claims 38/40 → 7/40 Δ −31/40; "incomplete" absorbed 28/40.
  • Disclosure (Table 3): matched-instance false verdicts 18/59 → 0/58 Δ −18; 0 false among 10 disclosed HALTs.
  • Rendering (Table 4): strong-claim rate meter .383 vs lantern .150; median budget abandoned 7 vs 19.
  • Register presence (post-hoc, confounded): any voice roughly doubled strong claims (55/180 vs 9/60).
  • Scarcity compression (registered): 95% of 407 narrator contradictions understate the remaining budget; 66% quantize to 1/4, 1/3, 1/2, 2/3, 3/4.

02   AppraisalStrengths / Limitations

+ Strengths

  • Makes the instrument the variable in a domain where it is "rarely varied, and rarely even stated as a choice."
  • Engine holds ground truth → no LLM-as-judge; sidesteps position / verbosity / self-enhancement bias.
  • Preregistered P-G1 gradient falsified; scarcity mediation returned a clean null — both reported as informative.
  • Artifact-bound preregistration: git revision embedded in eval logs + SHA-256 manifest.
  • Scarcity-compression finding (95% understate, quantized to fraction landmarks) is novel and well-adjudicated.

− Limitations

  • External validity is the headline limit: one pairing, one world, one genre.
  • True-positive cell unpopulatable by construction (auto-win); measures unsupported assertion, not detection accuracy.
  • Epochs cluster within 12 instances → all binomial CIs are optimistic.
  • Precision stage rests on a single expert judge (the human PI); 21 GLM-positive pairs never adjudicated.
  • Preregistered Cohen's κ = −0.13 (degenerate under skew); only healthy κ from a model rater.
  • Register-presence effect confounded with prompt composition; grammar counterfactual confounded with time / provider drift.
  • Gates self-imposed, not lodged with a third-party registry.

03   Open questionsFor series 3

  1. Do the four instrument effects — especially grammar redistribution and disclosure collapse — replicate beyond GLM-5.2 + Haiku-4.5, and across narrator families?
  2. Would a placebo-directive control (same prompt block, non-register content) cleanly separate voice from prompt composition?
  3. How does the four-check protocol perform retrospectively on AbstentionBench, SQuAD 2.0 — do published findings survive saturation and disclosure?
  4. Is scarcity compression a general property of LLMs narrating numeric state, or an artifact of Haiku-4.5 + the lantern rendering vehicle?
