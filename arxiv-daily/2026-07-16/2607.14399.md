> **一句话总结：** 本文不评估模型，而评估"评估工具本身"——在一个引擎持有标准答案的文字冒险世界中，固定玩家模型（GLM-5.2）仅改变工具的四个旋钮（结果枚举、成功标准披露、预算渲染方式、叙事语气），就使测得的"诚实"行为剧烈变化（强声称 38/40→7/40，匹配实例错误裁决 18/59→0/58），证明不控制这些旋钮的诚实评测无法把结论归因于模型，并提出四步完整性协议。

> **TL;DR:** Instead of measuring the model, this single-author study measures the evaluation instrument: in a text-adventure world ("The Latent Underground") whose deterministic engine holds planted ground truth, the player (GLM-5.2) and narrator/interpreter (Haiku-4.5) were held fixed while four instrument knobs were varied. On four byte-identical anchor instances, expanding the outcome grammar from two verdicts to three moved strong claims from 38/40 to 7/40 (delta -31/40, with the new "incomplete" verdict absorbing 28/40), and a one-sentence disclosure of the win criterion took matched-instance false verdicts from 18/59 to 0/58 on the 6-instance solvable grid. A preregistered narrative-register gradient (P-G1) was falsified, and the registered mediation test returned a clean null; the authors propose a portable four-check integrity protocol (taxonomy saturation, criterion disclosure, censoring analysis, distribution replication) for honesty-eval instruments.

## 核心贡献

- **把"工具"变成实验变量而非背景：** 在引擎持有标准答案、模型全程被屏蔽的文字冒险世界里，固定玩家模型，直接操纵评测工具的四个语义旋钮（结果枚举、成功标准可见性、预算渲染、叙事语气），定量证明工具选择对测得行为的贡献往往大于任何模型层面的故事。
- **四项可量化的工具效应：** (i) 结果枚举——三元语法让"未决/incomplete" verdict 吸收 28/40；(ii) 成功标准披露——一句话让匹配实例错误裁决从 18/59 降到 0/58；(iii) 预算渲染（假设生成）——meter vs lantern 把强声称率从 .383 移到 .150；(iv) 叙事语气存在性（假设生成，与 prompt 组成混淆）——任何语气让强声称近乎翻倍（55/180 vs 9/60）。
- **可审计的"规则先于结果"链：** 每条 eval 日志内嵌其执行的 git revision 与 clean/dirty 标记，release manifest 用 SHA-256 锁定日志；三次 preregistration（Gate 0/1/2）正式程度递增并明确披露，silent deviation 即作废 gate。
- **把"验证文化"作为方法本身报告：** 两次独立同族审计都在结果入账前证伪了首席分析员的核心论断（Gate 2 控制单元并不干净、frozen alignment parser 覆盖率缺陷）；跨族（GLM-5.2、GPT-5.6）重新抽取与重新编码全部头条数字；连参考文献都逐条核验以防幻觉引用。
- **提出可移植的四步完整性协议：** 在把模型 verdict 读作"关于模型"的证据之前，先做 taxonomy saturation、criterion disclosure、censoring analysis、distribution replication 四项廉价检查。
- **诚实的负面结果：** 正式预注册的 P-G1 语气梯度被证伪、预注册的 scarcity-compression 中介检验返回干净的 null，作者将其与正面发现同等对待。

## Key Contributions

- **Treats the instrument, not the model, as the experimental variable.** In a text-adventure world whose deterministic engine holds planted ground truth (target site + win token withheld from every model in the loop), the player (GLM-5.2) and the narrator/interpreter (both Haiku-4.5) are held fixed while four semantic instrument knobs are manipulated; this isolates measurement artifacts from model behavior.
- **Four measured instrument effects.** (i) Outcome grammar: on four byte-identical anchors, going from a binary grammar to three verdicts moved strong claims from 38/40 to 7/40 with incomplete absorbing 28/40 (and 93/158 of all series-2 games ended incomplete). (ii) Criterion disclosure: one sentence naming the win mechanism took matched-instance false verdicts from 18/59 to 0/58. (iii) Budget rendering (hypothesis-generating): meter vs lantern strong-claim rate .383 vs .150. (iv) Register presence (hypothesis-generating, confounded with prompt composition): any voice roughly doubled strong claims (55/180 vs 9/60).
- **Artifact-bound preregistration.** Each eval log embeds the git revision it executed (with a clean/dirty flag); a SHA-256 manifest pins the logs. Three gates (Gate 0 drafted mid-run, Gate 1 ratified in substance, Gate 2 with the full ceremony) disclose increasing formality, and silent deviation voids a gate.
- **Verification culture reported as method.** Two independent same-family audits falsified the analysis lead's claims pre-bank (the Gate 2 control cell was not clean; the frozen alignment parser had coverage defects), and cross-family re-extraction (GLM-5.2) and re-coding (GLM-5.2, GPT-5.6/Sol) reproduced every headline count; even references were verified against external bibliographic sources.
- **A portable four-check integrity protocol** (taxonomy saturation, criterion disclosure, censoring analysis, distribution replication) that is cheap to run before any character claim and whose portability is the explicit target of the planned series 3.
- **Honest negative results.** The preregistered narrative-register gradient (P-G1: heroic > none >= incident >= mundane) was falsified, and the registered scarcity-compression mediation test returned a clean null; both are reported as informative as the positive findings.

## Methodology

The instrument is a text-adventure world ("The Latent Underground") implemented as an Inspect AI task. Each instance is a generated site graph with planted ground truth (a target site and a win token) known only to the engine and withheld from every model in the loop. All world-state changes gate through a deterministic engine; rules-as-text bind once at the instruction boundary (and are negotiable by a persuasive player), while rules-as-code bind at every invocation.

The dungeon-master role is decomposed into two model roles under different regimes. An **interpreter** maps player free text onto a closed six-operation grammar (ATTEND, RETRIEVE, SAMPLE, COMMIT, MARK, HALT); every mapping is logged as a triple (player prose, proposed op, engine-validated op), giving an audit surface for interpreter latitude. A **narrator** costumes engine deltas with bounded freedom: it may elaborate a delta but never extend it; any actionable fact not traceable to the delta is a "leak" flagged per game by a mechanical fidelity scanner. The narrator is structurally blind (no target location, no token, no NPC reliability, no solvability), removing Clever-Hans leakage risk.

HALT verdicts form a closed enum validated by the engine: **complete, unreachable, incomplete**. Because a genuine completion terminates the game as WIN before any HALT can be asserted, a HALT-verdict of "complete" is false by construction — the true-positive cell is unpopulatable, so the instrument measures unsupported assertion rather than detection accuracy. Every confidence-bearing operation carries a **provenance field** (stated vs translated) authored by the interpreter under a required schema; imputation is abolished, and translated-provenance numbers are kept out of headline figures.

Three preregistration gates bind rules-before-results with increasing formality. Gate 0 (distributional ground) was drafted mid-run before any sample was read; its eval header binds revision 2ae1ef3 (dirty=false). Gate 1 (series 2: taxonomy, difficulty grid, disclosure satellite) committed at da8c140, launched 18 seconds later with the run binding da8c140 dirty=false. Gate 2 (register and rendering) was ratified at content SHA 55789d2 (ratification commit 081586f) after an independent audit and a five-cell smoke; its readout script (05acada) was committed before the first cell closed. Forging the ordering would require fabricating multi-hour, multi-million-token run artifacts.

Configurations: hosted GLM-5.2 as player (z.ai endpoint), Haiku-4.5 in both DM roles, temperature 0.7, max_connections 3, message_limit 120. Gate 0: 4 instances × 10 epochs. Series 2 main: 16 instances × 10 epochs (160 games); disclosure satellite: 6 solvable grid instances × 10 epochs (60 games). Gate 2: 5 cells of 60 games (12 instances × 5 epochs = 300 games total): four voice-only register skins (heroic, incident, mundane, none) under calibrated lantern rendering plus a bare none cell under affectless meter rendering. A register skin is a voice-only narrator costume; lantern rendering draws the budget as a light whose brightness scales with the remaining fraction; meter rendering states the remaining count as a plain number. The instrument guarantees only information parity (every budget event delivers value and denominator, verified 48/48 in the pre-launch smoke).

A two-stage coding protocol handles "substituted completion" markers (meaning-language, legacy address, sacrifice acknowledgment): a blind recall stage (Fable-5 coder, files shuffled under neutral IDs) followed by a precision stage under a frozen version-controlled judge prompt (JUSTIN-P) executed by the single expert judge. Independent same-family audits and cross-family re-coding (GLM-5.2, GPT-5.6) bound the recall stage's miss rate and test whether the judge's conventions do attributable work.

## Results & Highlights

- **Outcome grammar redistributes verdicts (Table 2, four byte-identical anchors, 10 epochs each):** complete 22/40 → 7/40 (-15), unreachable 16/40 → 0/40 (-16), incomplete 0/40 (inexpressible) → 28/40 (+28), budget exhausted 2/40 → 5/40. Strong claims fell from 38/40 to 7/40, a delta of -31/40, with the newly expressible incomplete verdict absorbing 28/40 outcomes.
- **Criterion disclosure collapses false verdicts (Table 3, six solvable grid instances):** valid games 59 vs 58; games reaching HALT 43/59 vs 10/58; false verdicts per valid game 18/59 vs 0/58; false verdicts conditional on HALT 18/43 vs 0/10; pin-attempting games 4/59 vs 42/58. The intervention reduced decision points and produced no false verdict among the 10 disclosed games that did halt.
- **Budget rendering moves verdicts (Table 4, 60 games per cell):** the none/meter cell produced 23/60 strong claims (.383) vs the none/lantern control 9/60 (.150) — meter above none on 8/12 instances with 1 reversal. The P-G3 core prediction is supported: lantern players abandoned more budget (median budget remaining at halt 19 vs meter 7; means 22.31 vs 16.62).
- **Register presence roughly doubles strong claims (post-hoc, confounded):** pooled skins 55/180 = .306 vs bare none cell 9/60 = .150; skins above none on 10/12 instances, never reversed. The preregistered P-G1 gradient (heroic > none >= incident >= mundane) was FALSIFIED — skins are mutually indistinguishable at this design's resolution.
- **Distributional instability (Gate 0, Table 1):** repeated runs of one fixed configuration produced non-stable verdict distributions on 3 of 4 instances under the preregistered rule (modal category at or above 8/10 = stable). The paper's bottom line: a single-run eval reports a sample as a disposition.
- **Scarcity compression in the narrator channel (preregistered, adjudicated):** 407 confirmed quantitative contradictions across 396 of 4,011 lantern-cell budget-bearing narrations (9.9%); 387/407 = 95% understate the remaining budget; 66% of implied values fall within 0.04 of the landmarks 1/4, 1/3, 1/2, 2/3, 3/4. The meter cell shows 2 confirmed contradictions vs 47-149 per lantern cell. The registered mediation test returned a clean null (median perceived-minus-true gap at the last parseable mention is 0.0 in every cell).
- **Terminality provenance audit (256 HALT games, Gate 2):** 245/256 (95.7%) are player-stated; 11 exceptions are 10/11 verdict=complete, concentrated in heroic (5) and meter (4), with zero in the control cell. Under player-stated-only accounting, heroic falls from joint-top to bottom (.233), but no licensed finding flips. A rubric-frozen 30-halt random audit found stated stamps attached to the halt verdict in 17/17 player-stated games (0 laundered; Wilson upper 95% bound ~.18).
- **Inter-rater reliability (28-case recall-positive subsample):** second human coder raw agreement .68 (19/28); GLM-5.2 .68; GPT-5.6-terra .71; Sol .857 (kappa .68, the only healthy chance-corrected value). The preregistered two-category Cohen's kappa is -0.13 — a degenerate value under extreme marginal skew, reported as frozen with skew-robust alternatives labeled post-hoc (PABAK .36; Gwet's AC1 .56).

## Critical Assessment

- **Novelty:** High and well-scoped. The paper explicitly extends surface-form-sensitivity work (Sclar et al. 2024) from accuracy to measured epistemic character in an interactive task, and inverts the standard text-game setup (which holds the environment fixed and measures the agent) by making the instrument itself — narrator included — the experimental variable. The scarcity-compression finding (narrator quantizes abundant budgets toward crisis landmarks, 95% understating) appears genuinely new: the authors searched for prior work quantifying numeric distortion as a function of narrative register in generation and found none, and they label this as a claim about their search rather than the literature.
- **Rigor:** Exceptional self-scrutiny but with real methodological seams. The verification culture is unusual: two independent audits falsified the lead's claims pre-bank, cross-family re-extraction achieved 99.59% pairing agreement on 3,636 in-text values, and the bibliography itself was verified against external sources. However, the precision stage is a **single expert judge (the human PI)** on 54 flagged positives — a clear single-rater dependency — and the recall stage may have missed 21 GLM-positive marker pairs that have not received precision adjudication. The inter-rater reliability statistics are themselves fragile: the preregistered primary (two-category Cohen's kappa) is -0.13, a degenerate value under extreme marginal skew (the second coder ruled Y on 26/28 of a recall-positives-only sample), and the only healthy kappa (.68) comes from a GPT-5.6 context the authors had directional priors about.
- **Reproducibility:** Strong on recomputation, weak on replication. The three-artifact release (arXiv + GitHub + Zenodo) with embedded git revisions in eval logs and a SHA-256 manifest is among the best in this category, and every table recomputes from released logs via documented scripts. But the authors are explicit that **replication (new runs, new data) is claimed nowhere**: n=10 per cell was preregistered as separating only "gross regimes," epochs cluster within 12 instances so binomial confidence intervals treating 60 games as independent are acknowledged as optimistic, and grid instances share a generator skeleton (effective information per instance is below the instance count). Gates were self-imposed rather than lodged with a third-party registry, and commit timestamps are author-controlled metadata (mitigated by artifact-bound revisions; cryptographic timestamping deferred to series 3).
- **Significance:** The core demonstrated warning — instrument choices can move measured honesty behavior more than any model-level story — is directly actionable for the eval community and the four-check protocol is portable. But external validity is sharply bounded: **one player-narrator pairing (GLM-5.2 + Haiku-4.5), one constructed world, one genre**, and effect sizes are explicitly not expected to transport. The two channel-law candidates (scarcity compression, state-dependent honesty) remain facts about one narrator model in one world.
- **Red flags / data quirks:** (a) The **true-positive cell is unpopulatable by construction** — a genuine completion ends the game as WIN before HALT, so every "complete" verdict is false by construction; the instrument measures unsupported assertion, not detection accuracy, and this is disclosed rather than buried. (b) The **grammar-change counterfactual (Table 2) is confounded with time and any provider-side drift** — the table is identified as total configuration change, not a pure grammar effect. (c) The **register-presence effect is confounded with prompt composition** (skins add a directive block), explicitly acknowledged, with a placebo-directive control deferred to series 3. (d) The **disclosure satellite launched dirty=true** (uncommitted local changes, most plausibly the readout script under development) — disclosed as recorded but worth noting. (e) The 11 interpreter-mapped HALTs (10/11 verdict=complete, 7/11 carrying provenance-laundered confidence stamps) are a real seam defect that the paper audits honestly and that the series-3 interpreter fix addresses.

## Strengths

- Makes the instrument itself the experimental variable in a domain (LLM honesty) where instrument choices are "rarely varied, and rarely even stated as choices."
- Engine-held ground truth removes the LLM-as-judge entirely; every verdict is scoreable without a model judge, sidestepping the position/verbosity/self-enhancement biases documented in judge-bias work.
- Exceptional honesty about negative results: the preregistered P-G1 gradient and the registered mediation test are both falsified/null and reported with the same weight as positive findings.
- Artifact-bound preregistration (git revision embedded in eval logs + SHA-256 manifest) is a concrete, portable improvement over policy-only or metadata-only preregistration.
- The four-check integrity protocol is cheap, actionable, and the subject of an explicit portability test in series 3 rather than an overclaim.
- The scarcity-compression finding (95% of narrator contradictions understate remaining budget, quantized to fraction landmarks 1/3, 1/2, 1/4, concentrated where truth is abundance) is a striking, novel, and well-adjudicated observation with implications for any eval that lets a model narrate resource state to another model.

## Limitations

- **External validity is the headline limitation and is stated repeatedly:** one player-narrator pairing, one constructed world, one genre, one instrument revision per series; effect sizes are not expected to transport.
- **Construction artifact:** the true-positive cell is unpopulatable by construction (auto-win), so all "complete" verdicts measure unsupported assertion only, not detection accuracy.
- **Clustering:** epochs cluster within 12 instances per cell, so all binomial confidence intervals are optimistic; comparisons were held coarse by preregistered rule.
- **Single-judge dependency:** the precision stage rests on one expert judge (the human PI) under a frozen but convention-laden prompt; the recall stage may have missed 21 GLM-positive marker pairs without precision adjudication.
- **Degenerate reliability statistics:** the preregistered Cohen's kappa (-0.13) is meaningless under extreme marginal skew, and the only healthy kappa comes from a model rater.
- **Confounded headline effects:** the register-presence effect is confounded with prompt composition, and the grammar counterfactual is confounded with time/provider drift.
- **Self-imposed preregistration:** gates were not lodged with a third-party registry and commit timestamps are author-controlled (mitigated but not eliminated by artifact-bound revisions).

## Relevance

对关注 LLM 评测方法学与"评测工具本身是否中性"的研究者高度相关：它用引擎持有标准答案的可审计环境，定量证明了诚实评测的结果枚举、标准可见性、预算渲染、叙事语气等被忽视的旋钮会显著改变测得行为，并给出可移植的四步完整性协议——这为构建可靠的 agent / honesty benchmark 提供了直接的"先校准工具再谈模型"的工程清单。

## Questions for Further Exploration

- Do the four instrument effects (especially outcome-grammar redistribution and criterion-disclosure collapse) replicate across player-narrator pairings beyond GLM-5.2 + Haiku-4.5, and across narrator families for the scarcity-compression and state-dependent-honesty channel-law candidates?
- Would a placebo-directive control cell (same prompt block structure, non-register content) eliminate the register-presence effect, cleanly separating voice from prompt composition?
- How does the four-check integrity protocol perform when applied retrospectively to existing honesty/abstention benchmarks (e.g., AbstentionBench, SQuAD 2.0) — do their published findings survive taxonomy saturation and criterion disclosure?
- Is the narrator's scarcity compression a general property of LLMs narrating numeric state to other LLMs, or an artifact of Haiku-4.5 + the lantern rendering vehicle — and does it distort any eval where a model mediates resource information?
- Given that 11/256 interpreter-mapped HALTs (10/11 verdict=complete) and 7/11 laundered confidence stamps cluster in heroic and meter cells, what is the right HALT-guard design that preserves the player's agency over terminality without reintroducing an LLM judge?
