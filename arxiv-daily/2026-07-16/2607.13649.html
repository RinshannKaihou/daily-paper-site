02Why this matters

A concrete architectural blueprint for breaking the LLM inference memory wall — fusing in-memory and in-interconnect compute with FeFET NVM and runtime precision scaling.

03Critical edge

The marquee 25×/10× gains rest on an H100 baseline cited without precision, batch size, or measurement method — not apples-to-apples, and the point most likely to be challenged in review.

§ 01

Methodology

CIMERA is organized as chiplets, each a Compute Tile (CT) hosting a 32×32 2D-mesh Inter-PE Computational Network (IPCN) connecting 1024 PEs (128×128 macro). PEs are OS-FeFET crossbar arrays performing SMAC (static-weight MAC for projections & FFN). IPCN routers perform DMAC (dynamic-data MAC for attention Q·Kᵀ), partial-sum reduction, and activations in-network — directly cutting data movement.

Precision & device

FeFET cells store up to 4-bit (QLC); spatial bit-slicing extends wider. A SAR ADC (16 converters, strided-shared across 128 columns) reconfigures resolution by truncating its search loop. Weights are programmed once (non-volatile). BEOL-compatible monolithic-3D stacks FeFET above CMOS peripherals, so ADC area is vertically absorbed — decoupled from array scaling.

Dataflow & evaluation

W_Q/K/V/O kept stationary across crossbars; KV cache co-located with its weights. Intra-layer: broadcast / reduction / unicast. Inter-layer: serpentine across adjacent CTs. DRAM touched only at prefill & decode. Verified RTL→Synopsys DC→Cadence Innovus, CACTI for SRAM, plus a fabricated & measured FeFET compact model in a cycle-accurate ISA simulator. 1 GHz, 7 nm, 186.4 mm²/CT.

§ 03

Strengths & Limitations

Strengths

  • Real silicon, not just sim: fabricated & measured FeFET array folded into the model.
  • SMAC / DMAC split maps cleanly onto Transformer structure and trims redundant movement.
  • M3D hides ADC area — a clean, transferable design pattern decoupling peripheral from array scaling.
  • Full co-verification: RTL → synthesis → PnR → CACTI → cycle-accurate ISA sim.
  • Precision + power gating give monotonic gains across 1B / 7B / 13B.

Limitations

  • No accuracy results — the "LLMs tolerate low precision" premise is assumed, never measured; FeFET non-idealities not propagated to task quality.
  • H100 baseline opaque — no precision / framework / batch / measurement method stated.
  • Only 3 models, 2 context lengths — no long-context, no MoE, no 70B+ scale.
  • No ablation isolating SMAC vs DMAC vs M3D vs precision reconfiguration.
  • No PIM head-to-head — only vs Nvidia H100; own LEAP/JADE cited but not benchmarked.
  • ADCs = 92.2% power, unmitigated — advantage may erode at higher precision.
§ 04

Open questions

  1. What downstream accuracy (perplexity, MMLU) does CIMERA hit at 4-bit once variability, drift, and ADC noise are in the loop?
  2. Under a matched-precision comparison (CIMERA 4-bit vs H100 INT4/FP8), does the 25× survive?
  3. How does the 92.2% ADC power share evolve at 8/16-bit — would bit-line-level or current-mode S/H shift the optimum?
  4. What is the marginal contribution of in-interconnect DMAC vs pure in-memory SMAC, isolated by ablation?
  5. Does the serpentine dataflow still pipeline for MoE or long-context (≥8k) where KV-cache may overflow router scratchpads?
  6. Can the ISA express progressive precision scaling at runtime, and at what reconfiguration latency?
