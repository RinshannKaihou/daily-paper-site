# 

> **一句话总结：** 本文揭示了多模态MoE模型中"看得见却想不对"的现象--模型能准确感知图像内容但在后续推理中失败，根本原因是视觉输入导致中间层路由偏离，未能充分激活领域推理专家。

> **TL;DR:** ---

## 核心贡献

- **现象发现**: 系统性地定义并验证了"Seeing but Not Thinking"现象，即在MATH500等数据集上，68.2%-73.1%的视觉推理失败源于推理错误而非感知错误。
- **机制分析**: 通过跨模态语义干预实验排除了语义对齐失败作为唯一解释，揭示了视觉专家与领域专家在层间的分离分布，以及视觉输入在中间层引发的显著路由偏离。
- **Routing Distraction假设**: 提出视觉输入使路由机制无法充分激活任务相关的推理专家，导致计算资源分配次优。
- **干预方法**: 设计了路由引导干预方法（Soft/Hard），通过增强领域专家的路由权重来缓解该问题，在三个模型六个基准上取得一致改进（最高+3.17%）。

## Methodology

The authors conduct a three-stage mechanistic analysis on multimodal MoE models (primarily Qwen3-VL-30B-A3B):

**Stage 1 -- Cross-Modal Semantic Sharing Verification.** Using a digit-based arithmetic completion task, the authors perform concept intervention: replacing hidden-state representations of image tokens with textual semantic vectors. The intervention success rate follows an inverted U-shape across layers, peaking above 90% in middle layers (8-42). This confirms that MoE architectures achieve cross-modal semantic sharing in middle layers, ruling out alignment failure as the sole cause of reasoning degradation.

**Stage 2 -- Layer-wise Expert Specialization.** The authors identify two types of experts via activation frequency differences between domain-specific data (GSM8K) and general data (Alpaca): (a) **domain experts** (math-reasoning), concentrated in middle layers; (b) **visual experts**, concentrated in early and terminal layers. These exhibit minimal overlap -- a critical structural separation.

**Stage 3 -- Routing Divergence Analysis.** Using Jensen-Shannon Divergence (JSD) between expert activation distributions for text vs. image versions of the same MATH500 problems, the authors show that routing divergence concentrates in middle layers where domain experts reside. Greater visual complexity leads to higher JSD and lower reasoning accuracy (v1: 89.0%, v2: 88.2%, v3: 87.4% vs. 92.8% text baseline).

**Intervention Method.** Domain experts are identified by comparing Top-K activation frequencies between domain and general data (requiring only ~20 samples). During inference, Soft intervention adds $\lambda \cdot \sigma(r_l)$ to domain expert logits; Hard intervention forces domain expert logits to the layer maximum. A Random baseline selects the same number of experts at random to control for routing perturbation effects.

## Critical Assessment

**Strengths:**

1. **Clean experimental design.** The use of semantically equivalent text/image pairs (rendering LaTeX problems as images) elegantly isolates reasoning from perception, a methodological contribution that enables controlled causal-like analysis.
2. **Thorough mechanistic investigation.** The three-stage analysis (semantic sharing -> expert distribution -> routing divergence) is well-structured and progressively narrows down the failure mode. Each stage builds on the previous one's conclusions.
3. **Multi-model validation.** Testing across three models with different scales (16B-109B) and routing mechanisms (Top-K vs. Top-1) strengthens the generality of the Routing Distraction hypothesis.
4. **Honest limitations section.** The authors acknowledge that the method cannot address perceptual errors, requires task-specific configuration, and that cross-modal alignment was only verified for simple numerical concepts.

**Weaknesses:**

1. **Marginal gains on strong models.** On Qwen3-VL-30B-A3B, the average improvement is +1.48% (60.72% vs. 59.24%). On Llama4-Scout-109B-A17B, only +1.17%. While statistically meaningful, these are modest absolute improvements that raise practical significance questions -- especially when requiring per-task hyperparameter tuning (layers, $\lambda$, $\tau$).
2. **Incomplete causal evidence.** The authors establish correlation between routing divergence and reasoning degradation, and the intervention results are suggestive. However, the paper does not provide direct causal evidence that routing distraction is the *primary* mechanism. Alternative explanations (e.g., attention distribution shifts induced by visual tokens, capacity dilution from longer visual sequences) are not ruled out.
3. **Limited scope of semantic sharing verification.** The concept intervention experiment only tests simple digit substitution. Whether complex mathematical or spatial concepts achieve comparable cross-modal alignment remains unverified, which the authors acknowledge but do not address.
4. **Practical deployment concerns.** The method requires: (a) knowing the task domain in advance, (b) constructing appropriate text references, (c) tuning $\lambda$ and intervention layers per model-task pair. This limits applicability in general-purpose multimodal assistants where the task domain is unknown at inference time.
5. **GSM8K-V results are weak.** On the benchmark that most resembles real-world visual reasoning (extracting information from scene images), improvements are marginal (+0.83% to +1.06%), suggesting limited utility when perception remains a bottleneck.
6. **Evaluation relies on proprietary model for error analysis.** Error classification uses gpt-5.2-1211-global, introducing an opaque dependency that may bias the perception-vs-reasoning breakdown.

## Relevance

本文对多模态MoE模型的机理性理解做出了有价值的贡献。从研究角度看，"Routing Distraction"假说为理解MoE架构中视觉-推理解耦提供了新视角，实验设计（语义等价对照）值得借鉴。从应用角度看，路由引导干预作为一种推理时优化手段，在特定场景（已知任务域的数学推理）中有实用潜力，但其对通用多模态助手的价值有限。对于关注多模态推理、MoE可解释性、推理时干预技术的研究者而言，本文值得一读。相关工作包括R2-T2（test-time rerouting for MoE）、SteerMoE（expert de/activation steering）和SCMoE（self-contrast for MoE），本文在分析深度和实验控制上优于这些前作。
