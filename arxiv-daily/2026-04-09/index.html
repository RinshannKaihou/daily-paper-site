Training Stability · Distillation
Demystifying OPD: Length Inflation and Stabilization Strategies for Large Language Models
Feng Luo, Yu-Neng Chuang, Guanchu Wang et al. — Johns Hopkins & Rice
揭示on-policy蒸馏中重复token获得4-9倍过大优势信号、导致训练崩溃的机制；Stable-OPD通过KL正则+混合蒸馏修复，六个数学推理集平均+7.2%准确率。 Identifies repetition-saturation as the core failure mode of on-policy distillation — repetitive tokens receive 4-9x larger reverse-KL advantages, triggering a feedback collapse. Stable-OPD (KL regularization + mixture distillation) fixes it: +7.2% avg accuracy on six math benchmarks.
