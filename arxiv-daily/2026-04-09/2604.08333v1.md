# Lost in the Hype: Revealing and Dissecting the Performance Degradation of Medical Multimodal Large Language Models in Image Classification

> **一句话总结：** 概述

本文首次对医疗多模态大语言模型（MLLM）在图像分类任务上持续逊色于传统深度学习方法的根本原因进行了系统性、逐层逐模块的剖析。通过对14个开源医疗MLLM在三个医疗影像数据集（乳腺超声、CT新冠诊断、胸部X光）上进行大规模特征探测实验，作者追踪了分类相关视觉信息在MLLM流水线中的退化轨迹：从视觉编码器、到连接器、到LLM、再到生成输出。研究提炼出四种失效模式，提出了用于跨模型定量比较的特征健康分数，并揭示了阻碍当前医疗MLLM实现临床潜力的三个关键障碍。

## Critical Assessment

**Strengths**: The paper provides a genuinely systematic and methodologically rigorous analysis. The layer-wise, module-level probing approach goes well beyond prior phenomenological observations to pinpoint exactly where information loss occurs. The FHS metric is a creative and useful contribution for standardizing cross-model evaluation. The three barrier discussions are thoughtfully designed and the vision tower SFT comparison (Table 3) starkly demonstrates the suboptimality gap.

**Limitations**: The study focuses exclusively on classification, so the conclusions may not generalize to other medical tasks like detection or segmentation. The three datasets are relatively small and span only 2D imaging modalities -- whether the findings hold for 3D volumetric data or pathology whole-slide images remains untested. The probing methodology, while informative, trains classifiers on frozen features and thus captures only what information is potentially recoverable, not necessarily what the model actively uses. The paper is primarily diagnostic rather than prescriptive: it catalogs failure modes but does not propose concrete architectural fixes.
