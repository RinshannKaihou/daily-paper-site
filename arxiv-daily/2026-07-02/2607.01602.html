The Decision Stack

runtime config-selection over a pre-synthesized variant library
1

Workload Criticality Score

How fault-sensitive is layer Wi? Entropy-based data sensitivity + control criticality + single-bit error-propagation.

WCS = α·Sdata + β·Scontrol + γ·Perror
2

Fault Propagation Factor

Couples WCS to utilization and downstream fanout across the K partitions.

FPF = WCS · λk · Fanout
3

Reliability Risk Score

Time-varying, normalized to [0,1] from per-region fault probability × FPF × severity.

RRS = (1/Z)·Σ pkfault·FPF·ρk
4

Composite Cost & Policy

Receding-horizon greedy-with-PR-penalty controller (note: Figure 2 labels it "Bi-level MDP" — mismatch).

J = Ctotal + 𝟙[≠curr]·ΔPR

Composite Objective

Three normalized objectives collapse into one scalar. PR switching overhead is an explicit term — the genuine improvement over threshold-based reactive schemes, preventing over-triggering.

Ctotal = ηT·T̃ + ηE·Ẽ + ηR·RRS    (ηT,ηE,ηR) = (0.4, 0.3, 0.3)

Strengths & Limitations

read side-by-side

Strengths PROS

genuine engineering contribution
  • Clear positioning between two extremes: always-on TMR vs. reactive recovery.
  • Multi-objective cost with an explicit PR-overhead term — the real methodological advance over threshold schemes.
  • Workload criticality is principled (entropy + control + error-propagation), not a hand-tuned heuristic.
  • Real ZCU104 hardware with measured PR timing — not simulation.
  • Three architecturally diverse CNNs: heavy ResNet-18, depthwise MobileNetV2, compact EfficientNet-Lite.
  • Time-varying sinusoidal + random SEU model is more realistic than constant-rate injection.
  • Thorough ablation + sensitivity over weights and estimation error.

Limitations CONS

integrity & generalization risks
  • Reference list is entirely unrelated to the topic — the most consequential weakness.
  • "ROMA-LPD" named in the title but never defined in the body.
  • Figure 2 calls the controller a "Bi-level MDP Policy"; the MDP is never formulated or solved.
  • Software SEU injection can't capture all physical mechanisms; radiation/thermal testing flagged as future work.
  • Composite cost compresses three objectives into one scalar — Pareto behavior summarized, not plotted.
  • CNN-only scope; transformer-attention applicability unaddressed.
  • No code / bitstream release; PR-flow reproducibility uncertain.
  • pfault(t) assumed available at runtime — source unspecified.
