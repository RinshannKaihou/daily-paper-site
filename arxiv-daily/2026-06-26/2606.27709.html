Low-Agreeableness Persona Conditioning for Safe LLM Fine-Tuning

Austin MY Cheung & Yi Yang · Hong Kong University of Science and Technology
LLM Safety Fine-Tuning / LoRA Personality · Big-Five Mechanistic Interpretability Data-Centric Empathetic Dialogue
Key finding

Pairing low-Agreeableness user turns with warm, de-escalating assistant turns preserves conversational warmth while cutting jailbreak rates — without any safety labels.

01

Methodology

Data-only recipe · no labels, no objective change

Safety as an emergent side-effect of personality-conditioned discourse — not of explicit refusal data. The pipeline rewrites conversational structure so warmth and compliance stop co-occurring.

PILOT · layer + trait selection

Find the safety lever

Scale each residual-stream layer 1.3× on Llama-3.1-8B → layer 10 most safety-disruptive. Across 10 Big-Five trait subsets, low Agreeableness yields lowest jailbreak.

STEP A · user-turn rewrite

Inject low-A style

User turns rewritten toward a skeptical, direct register resistant to social pressure — breaking the warmth↔compliance co-occurrence at the source.

STEP B · assistant-turn rewrite

Re-attach warmth

Assistant turns rewritten warm + de-escalating, conditioned on the rewritten user turn — recovering per-token warmth without re-coupling compliance.

Three experiments vary only dataset composition under a shared LoRA config: Exp.1 compares empathy corpora (PersonaFuse trait subsets vs. EmpatheticDialogues / Lahnala); Exp.2 isolates each rewriting component on a matched ShareGPT Vicuna Unfiltered source; Exp.3 transfers to mental-health support (MentalChat-16K). Safety is measured by the Qi et al. (2024) jailbreak benchmark (300 prompts / 10 categories) and Revilla Llaca et al. (2023) red-teaming suite (265 prompts / 6 subsets), evaluated via generation rather than multiple choice. Crucially, no harmful prompts and no explicit refusals are injected into the source data — gains emerge purely from communication-pattern design.

Mechanism

Warmth ↔ compliance decoupling

Why does personality rewriting help?

Under linear-representation theory, warmth and compliance occupy nearby directions in the residual stream — so warmth fine-tuning incidentally amplifies compliance (and thus susceptibility). Layer-wise direction-cosine probing shows the low-A conditioning reduces the geometric alignment between the two directions, decoupling them. Consistent on 3 of 4 models.

exploratory · not causal
04

Strengths & Limitations

Side-by-side

✓Strengths

  • Data-centric & low-friction. Sidesteps harm labels or reward models — easy to adopt.
  • Clean ablation. Attributes the dual benefit to the two conditioning components: user-side breaks warmth↔compliance; assistant-side re-establishes warmth.
  • Strong mixed evidence — category-level jailbreak breakdowns plus warm-refusal vs. warm-compliance examples.
  • Honest about limits — discloses pilot circularity and null results (Qwen).
  • Open pipeline with dual-domain (general + mental-health) validation.

△Limitations

  • Single-turn focus. Multi-turn jailbreak eval is underpowered (50 samples) and deferred.
  • Model sensitivity. Gains shrink / lose significance on well-aligned models (Qwen) — limited headroom.
  • Correlational mechanism. Cosine analysis is exploratory, not causal.
  • GPT-4o dependency. Rewrite noise could attenuate signal at scale; spot-check covers only 100 samples.
  • Accuracy cost. Modest MMLU degradation (−1.5 to −3.9 pp).
  • Not a deployment guarantee — mental-health transfer needs clinical evaluation.
05

Open Questions

For further exploration
  • Q1How does conditioning behave under systematic multi-turn suites (e.g. SafeMTAttack_600), where warm de-escalation may still create disclosure momentum?
  • Q2Is the warmth–compliance decoupling a causal mechanism (testable via targeted steering/ablation) rather than a correlation?
  • Q3Can it combine with post-hoc filters (LARF) for additive safety — and eliminate the residual harmful-response tail?
  • Q4Does low-agreeableness conditioning hurt factuality/utility beyond MMLU (reasoning, instruction-following)?
  • Q5Do gains scale to larger / more strongly aligned models where geometric headroom is limited?
  • Q6Could other trait combos (e.g. low-A + high conscientiousness) yield further safety without warmth loss?
06

Rigor & Credibility

Novelty · reproducibility

A genuinely fresh angle: safety as an emergent property of personality-conditioned discourse, orthogonal to filtering (LARF), Constitutional AI, and RLAIF — none of which need safety infrastructure. Controls are careful: matched-size and matched-source comparisons, a clean user-vs-assistant ablation, Bonferroni-corrected two-proportion z-tests, and transparent disclosure of pilot-selection circularity (mitigated via held-out Qwen / Mistral / SmolLM3). Code, data, and a shared LoRA config are public.
