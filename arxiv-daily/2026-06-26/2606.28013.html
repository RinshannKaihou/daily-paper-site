Critical Edge

Semantic ground truth rests on Claude Opus 4.7, not expert Lean validators — a single misjudged cluster could distort SO/TO counts, and only one of six (model, dataset) cells carries full Opus dual-judging.

01

Methodology

A diagnostic that converts one scalar into a mechanistic cell decomposition.

Statement-level autoformalization maps a natural-language theorem to a Lean 4 signature ending in := by sorry. The authors factor evaluation through a 2×2 matrix that two strata, separately, must agree on.

The type stratum — the Lean elaborator — labels pass / fail: complete on whether the term type-checks, but silent on whether the statement is semantically right. The semantic stratum — a cross-family judge — labels equiv / not-equiv: covers meaning but forgives surface type differences. Each of n outputs falls into exactly one cell.

TC% = (TS + SO) / n     SF% = TS / n     ⟂ not equal

A 4×4 per-problem transition matrix then tracks movement across methods, letting any TC% gain be decomposed rather than reported as a single number. Methods are compared at a fixed ~4-LLM-call budget across ProofNet# (186 type-complex textbook problems) and MiniF2F-test (244 type-simple olympiad problems), bracketing type-system complexity.

Judge Divergence
Opus vs GTED disagree by +26 to +37pp on feedback outputs (vs +7pp on Vanilla) — concentrated in the Structural category, measuring elaborator-driven rewriting.
Saturation & Floor
Refinement saturates at K=1 (+21.5pp); K=2,3 add only +3.3pp. Only 1/186 problems are durably SO — the plateau approximates ProofNet#'s gold-error floor.

Strengths & Limitations

A workshop paper with main-conference statistical discipline.
Strengths
  • Diagnostic, not just descriptive. Converts a scalar (TC%) into a mechanistic cell decomposition — a "which cell moved" credit assignment.
  • Exceptional statistical transparency. Every rate carries a Wilson/bootstrap CI; negative and self-consistency caveats stated openly.
  • Causal probe. The deterministic-IR SAF cleanly isolates surface-form rewriting as mechanism, not artifact.
  • Benchmark hygiene. Discovers and documents gold-formalization bugs in ProofNet# (Ireland-Rosen 1^30, Dummit-Foote 7.2.2) with corrections.
  • Cross-validated. Six (model, dataset) cells support the generality of the R²=0.96 linearity claim.
Limitations
  • Small absolute n. 186 ProofNet# problems (14 BF) yield wide CIs on smaller strata.
  • LLM-as-ground-truth. Semantic labels rest on Opus 4.7, not expert Lean validators — a small audit is flagged as future work.
  • Partial dual-judging. Only one cell carries full Opus; the cross-model panel rests on GTED-only judging.
  • Workshop scope. Full MiniF2F dual-judge analysis across all four methods is deferred.
  • One unpatched failure. SAF's Qwen3.5-Plus IR-extraction failure (−12.9pp) is reported unpatched, slightly muddying the robustness claim.
