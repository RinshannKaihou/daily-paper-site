From Geometric Recovery
to Causal Validation.

A reproducible audit of SAE features — Mohamed Abdessalem Bal, independent researcher (Algiers).

Even at cosine similarity 0.9998, a "recovered" SAE feature can be entirely causally inert — geometric alignment with ground truth is not causal efficacy.

Key metric — causal inertness
77%
of cosine≥0.90 "recovered" features are causally inert in the degraded TopK k=13 SAE (17/22).
Falls to 9% (2/22) in a well-trained TopK k=4 SAE — and to 17% under the deterministic reference run.
Why this matters

SAE features that pass every recovery test can still be invisible to ablation yet highly steerable — so raw activations are unsafe to gate any automated interpretability decision on.

Critical edge

The headline 77% comes from a non-deterministic, multi-threaded configuration the author himself calls a "noise amplifier"; the deterministic reference environment reports 17%.

Methodology — the causal battery

A toy ReLU autoencoder (32→8 at sparsity s=0.95) supplies known ground truth. Every correlational match is then forced through the model's actual output stage x̂=ReLU(ĥW+b) and tested interventional end-to-end.

STEP 1

Reproduce

From-scratch Elhage 2022 superposition phase diagram + Gao 2024 TopK-vs-L1 Pareto, multi-seed.

STEP 2

Match

Greedy per-feature argmax on decoder direction; collisions surface as findings, not errors.

STEP 3

Screen — fired_frac

Fraction of feature-present samples where the atom fires. Alone catches all 17 originally-inert cases.

STEP 4

Read test

Ablation specificity: targeted output drop ÷ mean off-target movement. ∞ = perfectly surgical.

STEP 5

Write test

Sign-correct steering specificity: force the atom on feature-off inputs, measure targeted rise.

Packaged as sae-causal-audit — protocol-typed (any object exposing W_dec, encode, decode, including sae_lens.SAE), 37 tests, SHA-256 hash-verified CI gate.

Results & highlights

+22.5%magnitude shift
Magnitude refinement closes 91.2% of L1's reconstruction gap while shifting active magnitudes +22.5%; the same refinement on TopK moves magnitudes only −0.05% — direct evidence L1 systematically biases activation strengths low.
0.9998inert cosine
Geometrically perfect matches that never fire. Bad-SAE inert cosines: 0.986, 0.994, 0.9995. Good-SAE inert cosines: 0.9997, 0.9998 — fired_frac=0 across 500 samples.
r=0.859 → 0.471cosine reliability
Correlation of cosine with log ablation specificity is 0.859 on the good SAE but collapses to 0.471 on the bad SAE. The proxy degrades precisely where an audit is most needed.
310write specificity
Read/write dissociation. Five antipodal pairs have ablation specificity exactly 0 (read-inert) yet steering specificities of 143, 203, 261, 310 — the same atom is unmonitorable yet highly steerable.
1.40‖W_i‖₂ diffuse
A "diffuse sharing" regime at ratio 8, s=0.99: every feature reports D_i≈0.08 yet norm 1.40 (above a clean orthogonal feature's 1.0), recon loss 0.136 — a third geometry the dedicated/antipodal/dropped trichotomy misses.
8 / 83atom collisions
On gpt2-small-res-jb blocks.8: atom 14149 is the nearest match for 8 semantically disjoint concepts (astronomy, cryptography, law, …), replicated across three independent concept batches.

Strengths & limitations

Strengths

  • Causal validation runs end-to-end through the model's actual output stage — the strongest interventional test available in this setting.
  • Two-axis taxonomy (structural vs competitive; read vs write) is mechanistically grounded and gives actionable diagnoses.
  • Interface-level bug prevention: the sign-convention failure is made unrepresentable, not merely patched.
  • Exceptional honesty — four bugs reported in the paper itself; the cross-platform reproducibility ceiling is documented, not hidden.
  • Open, tested, deterministic tooling that runs on sae_lens.SAE with zero adapter code.

Limitations

  • All headline numbers come from small synthetic toy models (≤32 features, ≤8 hidden dims) — calibration points, not production benchmarks.
  • Real-model census is a single SAE, single hook layer, 83 concepts at a lowered cosine bar of 0.5; only 7 features cleared recovery, 1 inert.
  • The read/write decomposition rests on 5 antipodal pairs in one seed; cross-seed rates are not reported.
  • The original 77% headline is from non-deterministic multi-threaded runs; the deterministic reference reports 17%.
  • The "diffuse sharing" regime is observed but left mechanistically unresolved.
