> **一句话总结：** 本文以合成实体-编码任务结合 LoRA 定点放置为因果探针，把 Transformer 的记忆仲裁与幻觉统一为"吸引子盆地几何"——PM 是 MLP 雕刻的持久盆地，WM 是注意力驱动的瞬时牵引；冲突是盆地竞争、幻觉是盆地缺失，而冻结的 LM 头无法区分二者，导致对尺度越大、越自信的幻觉越失明；作者证明预 LM 头隐藏态到最近盆地的"margin"可在零误拒下分离正确回忆与幻觉（LoRA AUROC 0.993、预训练知识 AUROC 1.000，远超 entropy 0.968/0.622），并给出 C=exp(-c/Δ̄) 的尺度律解释为何"自信幻觉"随模型变大反而更多。

> **TL;DR:** Using a synthetic entity→5-digit-code task on Qwen2.5-3B-Instruct with rank-8 LoRA restricted to QK / VO / MLP / Full components as a causal probe, the authors give a unified attractor-geometry account of two LM failure modes: conflict (basin competition between parametric memory and working memory) and hallucination (basin absence). On the synthetic task the hidden-state distance to the nearest memorized basin ("margin") achieves AUROC 0.993 vs entropy's 0.968, and to catch 100% of hallucinations margin preserves 100% of correct recalls while entropy must refuse 99.5%. On 196 natural-language factual queries with no adapter, margin AUROC is 1.000 vs entropy 0.622, and on TruthfulQA margin reaches 0.858 vs entropy 0.656. Across 17 models (0.36B–72B) on TriviaQA the hallucination rate follows H ∝ N^−0.27 while the confident-hallucination fraction rises with scale according to a parameter-free law C = exp(−c/Δ̄) (r² = 0.88 across 21 points), so undetectable errors U = H·C grow from <0.5% at sub-1B to ~18% at 14B.

## 核心贡献

- 将两类 transformer 失败模式（PM-WM 冲突与幻觉）统一为同一套"吸引子盆地几何"语言：MLP 权重形成持久盆地、注意力形成瞬时牵引、冻结 LM 头只读 logit 间距而抹除盆地区位信息。
- 用 LoRA 的组件定向放置（QK / VO / MLP / Full）作为因果探针，配合 Jacobian 对称性分解（S 收缩、A 旋转）证实 QK=路由、VO=读出、MLP=盆地雕刻这三种角色在预训练模型中就已存在（MLP 的 ‖S‖²_F 比 QK/VO 大 25×，VO 对称性 φ≈0.59 在第 15 层达峰 φ=0.72），不是微调产物。
- 引入"geometric margin"（隐藏态到最近盆地的距离）作为权威认知状态信号：在零误拒下分离正确回忆与幻觉，并在随模型尺度变大、entropy 监控退化时反而变好。
- 发现一个跨 4 个家族、12 个模型、2 个基准通用的"自信幻觉"尺度律 C = exp(−c/Δ̄)（r²=0.88，参数自由），量化"模型越大、自信错误越多"的现象，并给出 softmax 饱和与 logit gap ∝ N^(1/3) 的机制推导。
- 给出一个明确的负结果与架构启示：端到端训练的认知监控头会扰动盆地几何（margin AUROC 从 0.994 跌到 0.728），即使几何蒸馏也只能部分恢复（AUROC 0.932 vs oracle 0.993）；并指出盆地几何在第 24 层才成熟、产生"单遍前向无法自指"的时间悖论。

## Key Contributions

- A unified attractor-geometry account of two previously separate LM failure modes: PM–WM conflict is basin competition (a memorized basin exists but WM steering interferes), and hallucination is basin absence (the hidden state wanders outside all basins). Both share one architectural consequence: the frozen LM head cannot distinguish basin occupancy from absence and fires confidently either way.
- Causal dissociation of transformer components via targeted rank-8 LoRA placement on Qwen2.5-3B-Instruct (QK / VO / MLP / Full). A Jacobian S/A decomposition confirms these roles are intrinsic to the pretrained model — MLP dominates ‖S‖²_F by 25× (basin shaping), VO has φ≈0.59 peaking at layer 15 with φ=0.72 (Hopfield-like readout), QK is unstructured (φ≈0, routing) — not fine-tuning artifacts.
- A representation-space diagnostic, geometric margin δ(x)=min_i‖h(x)−m_i‖₂, that separates correct recall from hallucination with zero false refusals: on the synthetic task margin AUROC = 0.993 vs entropy 0.968, and catching 100% of hallucinations preserves 100% of correct recalls vs 0.5% preserved by entropy.
- A scaling-law C = exp(−c/Δ̄), parameter-free and fitting 21 (model, benchmark) points across 4 model families with r² = 0.88, showing the confident-hallucination fraction rises with scale even as overall hallucination rate H ∝ N^(−0.27) falls, so undetectable errors U = H·C grow from <0.5% at sub-1B to ~18% at 14B.
- Negative-result methodology on metacognitive heads: end-to-end training of a correctness-predictor head disrupts the very basin geometry it depends on (margin AUROC collapses 0.994 → 0.728), and a layer-wise analysis shows basin geometry only emerges at layer ~24, creating a temporal paradox that motivates multi-pass architectures over single-pass self-monitoring.

## Methodology

**Controlled synthetic task.** Because natural-language recall confounds semantic knowledge with retrieval strategy, the authors install exact ground truth in Qwen2.5-3B-Instruct (d=2048, 36 layers): N=1,600 entities (E000000–E001599) each map to a unique 5-digit code (1,586 of 90,000 strings). Parametric memory (PM) is installed via rank-8 LoRA (α=16, lr=5×10⁻⁵, 10 epochs); working memory (WM) is a code placed in the prompt context.

**Three degrees of freedom.** (1) *What is memorized*: the entity→code task. (2) *How strongly*: "brittle PM" uses one template (format-gated), "robust PM" uses 53 templates with 25% synonym paraphrasing and 10% distractor prefixes (format-invariant). (3) *Where*: LoRA target modules — QK={q_proj,k_proj}, VO={v_proj}, MLP={gate,up,down_proj}, Full=all four. Identical hyperparameters, only target modules differ, so placement acts as a causal probe.

**Attractor-geometry formalism.** A transformer with fixed weights induces a discrete-time dynamical system h_{t+1}=F(h_t; x, y_{≤t}) composing attention, MLP, residual, and norm. PM is persistent attractor basins carved by MLP updates; WM is transient attention-mediated steering. For a query with hidden state h(x) at the first-digit position, the geometric signal is the margin δ(x)=min_i‖h(x)−m_i‖₂, where m_i is the basin center of entity i averaged over canonical templates; gap(x)=δ₂(x)−δ(x) measures basin separation.

**Component role verification via Jacobian decomposition.** J=S+A with S=½(J+J⊤) capturing contraction and A=½(J−J⊤) capturing rotation; the symmetry correlation φ=corr(J_ij,J_ji) and ‖S‖²_F are computed from exact 2048×2048 Jacobians at multiple layers of the pretrained model.

**Evaluation scenarios.** Five scenarios over 200 held-out entities each with greedy decoding: PM-seen (trained entity, training format), WM baseline (adapter off), WM recall (adapter on), WM–PM conflict (context code ≠ memorized code), PM-unseen (hallucination). Metrics: exact/substring accuracy, per-digit error, correct-token rank, digit entropy H=−Σ_d p_d log₂ p_d.

**Scaling experiments.** 12 instruction-tuned models (0.36B–14B, 4 families) on 2,000 TriviaQA items, plus a 2-layer MLP teacher–student ablation sweeping width m∈{16,…,256} to isolate width from data/architecture. A three-phase geometric-distillation training protocol tests whether a small MLP head can learn margin parametrically.

## Results & Highlights

- **Margin vs entropy on the LoRA task (N=450, 5 conditions):** logistic-regression AUROC for margin alone is 0.993 vs 0.968 for entropy; per-condition margins separate cleanly — PM-seen δ≈22, format-generalization δ≈34.9, conflict δ≈102.2, hallucination δ≈133.1, WM-unseen δ≈149.8.
- **Zero-false-refusal intervention (PM-seen vs hallucination, N=300):** to catch 100% of hallucinations by thresholding a single scalar, entropy must refuse 99.5% of correct recalls while margin preserves 100%; ~13% of hallucinations produce near-zero entropy indistinguishable from correct recall, which margin separates (PM-seen δ<32, hallucination δ>104).
- **Pretrained-knowledge transfer (196 factual queries, no LoRA adapter, Qwen2.5-3B):** margin AUROC = 1.000 vs entropy 0.622; correct-recall margin 44.7 vs hallucination 87.5; at the all-incorrect-catch threshold, margin preserves 149/149 correct while entropy preserves only 16/149 (10.7%).
- **TruthfulQA stress test (817 questions, 59 correct):** within-domain oracle margin AUROC = 0.858 vs entropy 0.656 vs full hidden-state probe 0.689; margin approaches 1.000 on factual subcategories (Misconceptions 0.999, Law/Health/Sociology/Economics 1.000) but cross-domain transfer from capital facts fails (0.476), bounding the framework to factual (basin-absence) errors.
- **Circuit dissociation under robust PM (Qwen2.5-3B, rank 8):** MLP-only and Full adapters achieve 100% format-invariant PM recall but collapse WM to 0% ("context deafness"); QK-only preserves WM at 95% but fails robust PM at 12%; VO-only sits in the middle (53%/53%). Under WM–PM conflict with robust PM, PM accuracy decays from ≈77% at digit 1 to near-chance while output entropy stays flat.
- **Hallucination scaling across 17 models on TriviaQA/NQ-Open:** hallucination rate follows H ∝ N^(−0.27) (r²=0.90, p<0.001) across six families; the confident-hallucination fraction C (entropy <0.1) rises from 0.3% at 0.5B to 7.8% at 7B, fitting C=exp(−c/Δ̄) with r²=0.88 across 21 (model, benchmark) points using no free parameters; absolute undetectable-error rate U=H·C grows from <0.5% at sub-1B to ~18% at 14B.
- **Teacher–student width ablation:** as width grows 16× (m=16→256), margin separation δ_u/δ_s improves from 1.2× to 153× while C rises from 52% to 99% — geometry sharpens exactly as output entropy saturates.
- **Learning efficiency (Table 3):** MLP-only and Full reach training loss <0.05 in ~3–4× fewer gradient steps than QK-only (e.g., at N=1600: MLP 8,260 vs QK 30,620 steps), confirming MLP as the efficient substrate for associative storage.
- **Geometric distillation of a metacognitive head:** end-to-end training collapses margin AUROC from 0.994 → 0.728 (representation collapse), but three-phase geometric distillation recovers predicted-margin AUROC 0.898 with intervention (correct recalls preserved at 100%-hallucination-catch threshold) of 58.5%, vs 0.5% for entropy and 100% for oracle margin.
- **Layer-wise emergence:** margin AUROC exceeds 0.95 only from layer 24 onward, peaks at layers 29–30 (AUROC 1.000, separation ≈10–11 units), and degrades at the final layer (36: AUROC 0.725), establishing that basin geometry is a late-layer phenomenon.

## Critical Assessment

- **Novelty:** Genuinely high. The paper does not just claim "hidden states carry epistemic information" (that is established by Orgad/Belinkov, Slobodkin et al.); instead it gives a *mechanistic* attractor-basin decomposition and a causal probe (LoRA placement) that ties each failure mode to a specific circuit (QK routing vs MLP basin shaping). The C=exp(−c/Δ̄) scaling law and its derivation from softmax saturation + Liu et al.'s τ^(1/3) logit-gap growth is a real micro-derivation with an empirical parameter-free fit (r²=0.88), not a slogan.
- **Rigor:** Strong experimental hygiene. The synthetic task is deliberately designed so that chance code overlap is only 1.76% (1,586/90,000), making "hallucination" detection unambiguous. The component roles are doubly verified — both behaviorally (per-digit WM error signatures: QK near-zero, VO gradual from digit 3, MLP/Full steep collapse at digits 4–5) and via Jacobian decomposition on the *pretrained* model, ruling out fine-tuning artifact. The teacher–student ablation cleanly isolates model width from data and architecture.
- **Reproducibility:** Good but not perfect. LoRA hyperparameters (rank 8, α=16, lr=5×10⁻⁵, 10 epochs, AdamW, bf16), all 53 robust-PM templates, and per-model TriviaQA numbers are tabulated. However, the basin-center computation requires exhaustively averaging hidden states over canonical templates for every memorized fact — the authors themselves flag that "scalable computation of basin centers for deployment beyond controlled evaluation" remains open, which limits immediate production use.
- **Significance:** The unification of conflict and hallucination under one geometric framework, plus the demonstration that geometric margin degrades *less* with scale while entropy degrades *more*, is a meaningful advance for hallucination monitoring, RAG reliability, and mechanistic interpretability.
- **Red flags and honest caveats:**
  - **Brittle training is a strawman baseline for the conflict story.** "Brittle PM" uses a *single* template, so the "format gating collapses to 0% on any format variation" result (Figure 8) is almost definitional; the more interesting robust-PM case is the one carrying the scientific weight, and even there robust training is conflated with 53 templates + 25% paraphrasing + 10% distractors, so individual contributions are not ablated.
  - **Margin AUROC is near-ceiling on a 5-digit-code task with only 1,600 entities.** Table 27 shows that under *brittle* training, margin AUROC collapses to chance (0.310–0.536) at N=1600 for Full/MLP and degrades sharply with rank; only *robust* multi-template training recovers AUROC=0.993. The headline margin numbers therefore depend on a specific (robust) training regime — a scope condition underplayed in the abstract.
  - **The pretrained-knowledge AUROC=1.000 is on only 196 hand-chosen factual queries (capitals/languages/continents).** The TruthfulQA stress test (817 questions) drops margin AUROC to 0.858, and cross-domain transfer from capitals fails (0.476). The paper honestly reports this, but the abstract's claim that "the separation holds on natural-language factual queries" is much stronger than the narrow, curated 196-query evaluation supports.
  - **Causal-vs-correlational slippage.** Margin is described as the "authoritative epistemic signal," but the end-to-end metacognitive-head experiment (margin AUROC collapses to 0.728 when the head is co-trained) actually shows the geometry is fragile to representation drift — the authors frame this as motivating multi-pass architectures, but it also means the oracle margin depends on a frozen backbone that real deployment does not have.
  - **Missing ablations:** the role of LoRA rank in the *robust* regime is not isolated (rank sweep Table 4 is for Full/brittle only); no comparison to Semantic Entropy Probes (Kossen et al. 2024) or other hidden-state probes at matched compute; no test of whether margin still works under RLHF/instruction-tuning drift, which the authors acknowledge as open.
  - **Overclaiming language:** "zero false refusals" holds *at the all-hallucinations-caught threshold on this task*, not in general — on TruthfulQA, by construction, margin must refuse some correct adversarial answers, and the cross-domain failure (0.476) shows the property does not transfer freely.

## Strengths

- A genuinely unifying framework that explains *both* conflict and hallucination as geometric configurations in one attractor landscape, rather than treating them as separate phenomena.
- Causal, interventionist methodology: LoRA component placement and Jacobian decomposition isolate *which* circuit does *what*, going beyond correlational probing.
- The C=exp(−c/Δ̄) scaling law is derived, parameter-free, and empirically validated across 21 (model, benchmark) points spanning 0.36B–14B and four families — a non-trivial universality claim.
- Intellectual honesty about scope: explicit TruthfulQA stress test, explicit cross-domain-transfer failure, explicit negative result on end-to-end metacognitive heads, and a layer-wise analysis revealing the "temporal paradox" for single-pass self-monitoring.
- Practical payoff: a single scalar (margin) with zero false refusals at the 100%-catch threshold, generalizing from synthetic LoRA knowledge to pretrained factual knowledge.

## Limitations

- Basin-center computation does not scale: it requires averaging hidden states over canonical templates for every memorized fact, which the authors flag as an open deployment problem; the geometric-distillation head only partially closes this gap (AUROC 0.932 vs oracle 0.993; intervention 58.5% vs 100%).
- The headline margin numbers depend on robust multi-template training; under brittle training, margin AUROC collapses to chance at N≥400 (Table 27), so the result is not unconditional.
- The natural-language validation is narrow: 196 curated factual queries (capitals, languages, continents) on a single model family. TruthfulQA drops AUROC to 0.858 and cross-domain transfer from capitals fails (0.476), bounding the framework to factual (basin-absence) errors.
- Conflict is explicitly not solvable by static margin (AUROC < 0.75); trajectory-based methods are left as future work.
- No evaluation under RLHF drift, no comparison to Semantic Entropy / hidden-state probes at matched compute, and no isolated rank sweep in the robust regime.
- The paper studies only greedy decoding; stochastic sampling and its effect on basin trajectories are not addressed.
- The synthetic 5-digit-code task is deliberately sterile; transfer to open-ended generation, where "ground truth" is fuzzy, remains unproven.

## Relevance

这篇论文对"机理可解释性 + 幻觉检测 + 规模化评估"三条线都有直接贡献：其 LoRA 定向放置作为因果探针、Jacobian 对称性分解、以及跨 17 个模型的 C=exp(−c/Δ̄) 尺度律，为研究大模型记忆结构与"自信幻觉"提供了可复用的几何工具和量化规律，契合可解释性、不确定性量化与可信 LLM 部署的研究兴趣。

## Questions for Further Exploration

- Can margin be estimated *without* exhaustive basin-center computation, e.g., via a lightweight parametric head trained once on a generic domain and transferred — the cross-relation result (margin head trained on capitals reaches AUROC 0.887 on held-out relations) suggests yes, but is it robust to RLHF drift?
- Does the C=exp(−c/Δ̄) law survive under temperature sampling, nucleus decoding, or RLHF-tuned output distributions, where softmax saturation may behave differently?
- The conflict condition resists static margin (AUROC < 0.75); can *trajectory* margin — measured over autoregressive steps as in the layer-wise heatmaps of Appendix D.5 — recover diagnostic power, and at what compute cost?
- Margin collapses under brittle training at N≥400 (Table 27) yet recovers under robust training; is there a *continuous* predictor (e.g., a basin-depth / basin-volume statistic) that interpolates between these regimes without re-training?
- The framework predicts multi-pass architectures should enable genuine self-referential metacognition (since basin geometry only emerges at layer ~24, too late for single-pass routing); can a two-pass "margin-then-route" model be built and shown to reduce confident hallucinations on TruthfulQA, where single-pass margin currently tops out at AUROC 0.858?
- How does attractor geometry interact with retrieval-augmented generation, where WM is not a 5-digit code but a variable-length passage — does the basin-competition account still predict when the model will ignore retrieved evidence?
