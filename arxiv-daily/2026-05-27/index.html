Training Stability

Can Entry-Wise Clipping Give Spectral Control of Stochastic Gradients?

Zitao Song, Cedar Site Bai, Zhe Zhang, Brian Bullins, David F. Gleich — Purdue University

Heavy-tailed gradient noise in LLM training exhibits a "localization" property where just a few extreme entries dominate spectral perturbations. Exploiting this, the authors derive a Bayes-optimal smooth shrinkage operator φ(y) = y · e^{−|y|/τ} that replaces expensive SVD-based spectral clipping with two lines of PyTorch — saving ~7% training tokens on NanoGPT's Adam and ~2% more on Muon.

随机梯度噪声的重尾污染具有"局部化"特性，据此推导出贝叶斯最优逐元素平滑收缩算子，无需SVD即实现谱控制，NanoGPT预训练节省约7%训练token。

Key finding: Smooth shrinkage achieves O(ε⁻⁴) convergence under Cauchy-contaminated noise with constant (not iteration-dependent) thresholds — a practical advantage over prior clipped-SGD methods.

Agent RL

Cyclical Entropy Eruption in Agent Reinforcement Learning (SEAL)

Wendi Li, Shawn Im, Sharon Li — UW–Madison

Agent RL training undergoes recurring entropy eruptions unlike the monotonic collapse in single-turn RL: policy entropy spikes, degeneracy builds, then subsides — only to cycle again. SEAL, a lightweight auxiliary BCE loss, separates correct from incorrect trajectory representations at the token level. On WebShop, Llama3.2-1B recovers from 0% → 79.69% success rate.

首次发现Agent RL训练中独特的"周期性熵爆发"现象，提出SEAL辅助损失将Llama3.2-1B在WebShop上从0%恢复至79.69%成功率，跨4种模型稳定提升。

Key finding: High representation similarity between correct/incorrect trajectories causes gradient interference (Lemmas 3.2–3.3) — the root cause of eruptions, not reward distribution or architecture.

Computation Reliability

Expressive Power of Floating-Point Neural Networks with Arbitrary Reduction Orders

Yeachan Park, Geonho Hwang, Wonyeol Lee, Sejun Park — Sejong / GIST / POSTECH / Korea University

A new "distinguishability" framework proves that floating-point NNs with arbitrary reduction orders and bounded ulp errors remain universal representators for Sigmoid, tanh, ReLU, GELU, Swish, Mish, and sin (four layers suffice) — while cos, cosh, and e^{−x²/2} provably fail. Foundational for reliability under float16/bfloat16/float8.

提出可区分性框架，证明浮点神经网络在任意归约顺序和有界ulp误差下仍为通用表示器，同时揭示cos/cosh等函数无法实现通用表示——对量化与混合精度训练具有基础性意义。
