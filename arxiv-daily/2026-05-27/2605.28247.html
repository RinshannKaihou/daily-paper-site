IRDS: Interpretable RLVR Data Selection via Verifier-Coupled Sparse Autoencoder Coverage

arXiv: 2605.28247

Date: 2026-05-27

https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.28247

一句话总结： 提出 IRDS 方法，通过稀疏自编码器（SAE）聚类实现可解释的 RLVR 训练数据选择，结合验证器信号和贪心对数行列式最大化，在数学推理任务上超越现有最强基线 +3.9/+4.0 pp，且计算成本低一个数量级。
TL;DR: IRDS selects RLVR training data via sparse autoencoder (SAE) clusters for auditability, using a verifier-coupled coverage objective solved by greedy log-determinant maximization, achieving +3.9/+4.0 pp over the strongest baseline on Qwen models at an order of magnitude lower cost.

核心贡献

  • 提出 IRDS（Interpretable RLVR Data Selection），在 SAE 聚类基础上选择 RLVR 训练实例，使选择过程本身可在可识别的问题模式上进行审计。
  • 引入 验证器耦合覆盖目标（verifier-coupled coverage objective），在 SAE 基上同时考虑模型失败和可学习的实例。
  • 采用 贪心对数行列式最大化（greedy log-determinant maximization） 求解该目标。
  • 在三个指令微调模型和六个数学推理基准上的实验表明，IRDS 在两个 Qwen 模型上超越最强基线 +3.9/+4.0 pp，在 Llama-3.1-8B 上超越 +0.5 pp。
  • 运行成本比基于轨迹的基线 低一个数量级。

Key Contributions

  • Proposes IRDS, which selects RLVR training instances on a sparse autoencoder (SAE) cluster basis, making the selection auditable on recognizable problem motifs.
  • Introduces a verifier-coupled coverage objective on the SAE basis to select instances the model both fails on and can still learn from.
  • Solves the objective via greedy log-determinant maximization.
  • Achieves the highest overall accuracy across three instruction-tuned models and six math benchmarks: +3.9/+4.0 pp over the strongest baseline on two Qwen models and +0.5 pp on Llama-3.1-8B.
  • Runs an order of magnitude cheaper than the trajectory-based baseline.
