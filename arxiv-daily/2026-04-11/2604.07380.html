The Lifecycle of the Spectral Edge: From Gradient Learning to Weight-Decay Compression

一句话总结： 在grokking过程中分解谱边缘（spectral edge）为梯度和权重衰减分量，发现两阶段生命周期：grokking前边缘由梯度驱动且功能活跃，grokking后变为压缩轴，且信息被重编码（非线性R²=0.99 vs 线性R²=0.86）而非丢失。
TL;DR: Decomposes the spectral edge into gradient and weight-decay components during grokking, revealing a two-phase lifecycle: pre-grokking gradient-driven edge becomes a compression axis post-grokking that is perturbation-flat yet ablation-critical (>4000x more impactful than random directions), with information re-encoded rather than lost.

核心贡献

  • 发现谱边缘在grokking过程中经历两阶段生命周期：从梯度驱动到权重衰减驱动的压缩轴
  • 压缩轴对扰动平坦但对消融极为敏感（比随机方向影响大4000倍以上）
  • 证明信息在压缩过程中被重编码（非线性R²=0.99 vs 线性R²=0.86）而非丢失

Key Contributions

  • Identifies two-phase lifecycle of spectral edge: gradient-driven (pre-grokking) → compression axis (post-grokking)
  • Shows compression axis is perturbation-flat yet ablation-critical (>4000x more impactful than random directions)
  • Demonstrates information is re-encoded, not lost (nonlinear R²=0.99 vs linear R²=0.86)
  • Identifies three universality classes (functional, mixed, compression) predicted by gap flow equation

Methodology

The author decomposes the spectral edge (dominant direction of Gram matrix of parameter updates) during grokking on two sequence tasks (Dyck-1 and SCAN), separating gradient and weight-decay components, and analyzing via linear and nonlinear probes.

Results & Highlights

  • Two-phase lifecycle: gradient-driven → compression
  • Compression axis: >4000x more ablation-sensitive than random directions
  • Information preserved: MLP R²=0.99 where linear R²=0.86
  • Removing weight decay post-grokking reverses compression while preserving the algorithm

Authors & Credibility

Single author (Xu, Y) — institutional affiliation unclear. The analysis is mathematically sophisticated but single-author status warrants careful review.

Critical Assessment

Novelty: High — the spectral edge lifecycle framework provides a new lens on grokking. The finding that compression is reversible while preserving the algorithm is particularly interesting. Rigor: Good experimental design with two tasks and multiple probing methods. The >4000x ablation sensitivity is striking. Red flags: Limited to two simple sequence tasks (Dyck-1, SCAN). Unclear if the two-phase lifecycle generalizes to more complex models or tasks. Single author.

Strengths

  • Elegant decomposition of spectral edge dynamics
  • Striking ablation sensitivity finding (>4000x)
  • Reversibility of compression is a practically important insight

Limitations

  • Only tested on two simple sequence tasks
  • Single author
  • Unclear scaling to larger models and complex tasks

Relevance

直接分析训练动力学的谱特性，研究grokking过程中梯度学习和权重衰减压缩的相互作用，与用户关注的训练稳定性和数学基础高度相关。
