What Drives Representation Steering? A Mechanistic Case Study on Steering Refusal

一句话总结： 通过多token激活修补框架，发现引导向量主要通过OV电路与注意力机制交互（而非QK电路），可稀疏化90-99%而保持大部分性能，且不同引导方法在关键维度上达成一致。
TL;DR: Discovers that steering vectors primarily interact with the OV circuit (not QK circuit — freezing attention scores drops performance only 8.75%), can be sparsified 90-99% while retaining most performance, and different steering methods agree on important dimensions.

核心贡献

  • 提出多token激活修补（multi-token activation patching）框架，揭示引导向量通过OV电路而非QK电路影响模型输出
  • 证明冻结所有注意力分数仅导致8.75%的性能下降，说明引导向量主要通过值矩阵而非查询-键匹配发挥作用
  • 发现引导向量可稀疏化90-99%而保持大部分性能，不同引导方法在关键维度上达成一致

Key Contributions

  • Proposes multi-token activation patching framework for analyzing steering vector mechanisms
  • Shows steering vectors primarily act through OV circuit; freezing QK attention scores drops performance only 8.75%
  • Demonstrates 90-99% sparsification of steering vectors with minimal performance loss
  • Reveals semantically interpretable concepts in mathematical decomposition of steered OV circuit

Methodology

The authors conduct a case study on refusal steering, using multi-token activation patching to trace causal mechanisms. They decompose the steered OV circuit mathematically and test the effects of freezing attention scores during steering. Sparsification analysis identifies the most important dimensions.

Results & Highlights

  • Freezing QK attention scores: only 8.75% performance drop across two model families
  • Steering vectors can be sparsified 90-99% while retaining most performance
  • Different steering methodologies leverage functionally interchangeable circuits at the same layer
  • Mathematical decomposition reveals semantically interpretable concepts

Authors & Credibility

Wiegreffe is known for interpretability work; Manocha is an established professor at UMD. The team has prior publications at top ML venues.

Critical Assessment

Novelty: High — first mechanistic analysis of WHY steering vectors work, going beyond demonstrating that they work. The OV vs QK finding is genuinely insightful. Rigor: Comprehensive analysis across two model families with mathematical decomposition. Red flags: The refusal case study is somewhat narrow — unclear if findings generalize to all steering tasks. The 8.75% drop from freezing QK is interesting but could be task-dependent.

Strengths

  • First mechanistic explanation of how steering vectors work internally
  • Clean experimental design with mathematical decomposition
  • Practical finding: 90-99% sparsification enables efficient deployment

Limitations

  • Single task (refusal) case study; generalizability unclear
  • Analysis limited to two model families
  • The interchangeable circuits finding may not hold for all steering tasks

Relevance

直接研究表示引导的机械原理，通过OV电路和注意力机制分析揭示引导向量如何影响LLM内部电路，与用户关注的机械可解释性和表示分析高度相关。

Questions for Further Exploration

  • Do OV-circuit dominance findings generalize to other steering tasks (e.g., sentiment, topic)?
  • Can the sparsified steering vectors be used for efficient real-time model control?
