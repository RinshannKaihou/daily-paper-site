# Optimal Decay Spectra for Linear Recurrences

> **一句话总结：** 本文提出 Position-Adaptive Spectral Tapering (PoST) 框架——通过 Spectral Reparameterization 用累积 softplus 强制几何间隔的衰减谱（被证明是对幂律核近似的最小最大化最优结构），再用唯一的 Position-Adaptive Scaling 把谱随位置 t 动态拉伸，把近似误差从 O(exp(−cN/log T)) 收紧到 O(exp(−cN/log t))；在 Mamba-2/RWKV-7/Gated DeltaNet/GLA/RetNet 上以零开销嵌入，180M–440M 预训练显示零样本 LM 一致小幅提升，并显著改善 Mamba-2 的 MQAR 与 NIAH 长上下文检索。

> **TL;DR:** The author introduces PoST, a two-component architecture-agnostic framework for the decay spectrum of any diagonal linear recurrence (SSMs, RWKV-7, GLA, RetNet, Gated DeltaNet). (1) Spectral Reparameterization structurally enforces geometrically spaced log-decay rates via a cumulative-softplus map, proven minimax-optimal for power-law kernel approximation at rate O(exp(−cN/log T)) (Theorem 4.7, Gonchar-Rakhmanov theory). (2) Position-Adaptive Scaling is derived as the unique continuous mechanism (Theorem 4.11) that stretches the spectrum to the actual dependency range [1,t], sharpening the rate to O(exp(−cN/log t)) and yielding a scale-free impulse response. Empirically, on FineWeb-Edu at 180M/440M, PoST improves Mamba-2's average MQAR at state=16K from 54.2% to 61.0%, lifts Mamba-2 180M NIAH average from 4.9 to 11.1, and gives consistent (if modest) zero-shot LM gains (e.g., Mamba-2 440M avg 42.2 → 42.5). The drop-in module is zero-overhead (Proposition 4.17) and preserves expressiveness (surjectivity onto ordered spectra, Proposition 4.18). Code at github.com/SiLifen/PoST.

## 核心贡献

- 信息论蓝图：在序列数据的对数信息等分配（logarithmic information equipartition）、尺度不变相关（1/f^β 幂律）与单 token 分辨率边界三个建模条件下，唯一导出几何间隔的时间尺度分配。
- 诊断两种失败模式：随机初始化使最小谱间隙坍缩到 O(N⁻²)（Lemma 4.1，导致次指数误差 exp(−Ω(N/log N))）；线性间隔虽避免坍缩但在长上下文退化为 exp(−O(N/√T))，实际是代数速率（Lemma 4.3/B.5）。
- Spectral Reparameterization：用累积 softplus 递推 p_k = p_{k-1} + Softplus(δ_{k-1}) 结构性地强制严格有序且几何间隔的 log-decay，并证明非简并性（Proposition 4.5）。
- Theorem 4.7（最小最大化最优）：用 Gonchar–Rakhmanov 有理逼近理论证明几何间隔对幂律核 K(t)=t^{−β} 在 [1,T] 上达到最优指数速率 exp(−π²N/(log T + C₆))，且渐近必要。
- Position-Adaptive Scaling（Theorem 4.11 唯一性）：在几何保持与全覆盖两个约束下，唯一的连续解是 τ*_k(t) = t^{α_k}，α_k=(N−k)/(N−1)，把误差指数从 log T 收紧到 log t，并诱导尺度不变的脉冲响应（Corollary 4.13）。
- 不变性：计算复杂度与状态维度不变（Proposition 4.17），且 Spectral Reparameterization 是到严格有序谱空间 R^N_< 的满射，不损失表达能力（Proposition 4.18 / Corollary 4.19）。
- 在五种主流线性递推架构（Mamba-2、RWKV-7、Gated DeltaNet、GLA、RetNet）上实例化，提供架构无关的 drop-in 模块（Algorithm 1）。

## Key Contributions

- An information-theoretic blueprint: under three modeling conditions (scale-invariant 1/f^β correlations, single-token resolution boundary, and logarithmic information equipartition across octaves), the optimal timescale allocation is uniquely geometric spacing.
- Diagnosis of two failure modes: random initialization collapses the minimum spectral gap to O(N⁻²) (Lemma 4.1) yielding sub-exponential error exp(−Ω(N/log N)); linear spacing avoids collapse but degrades to exp(−O(N/√T)), practically algebraic, over long contexts (Lemma 4.3 / B.5).
- Spectral Reparameterization: a cumulative-softplus recurrence p_k = p_{k-1} + Softplus(δ_{k-1}) structurally enforces strictly ordered, geometrically spaced log-decay rates throughout training, with a non-degeneracy bound (Proposition 4.5).
- Theorem 4.7 (minimax optimality): using Gonchar–Rakhmanov rational approximation theory, geometric spacing achieves the optimal exponential rate exp(−π²N/(log T + C₆)) for power-law kernel approximation, and is asymptotically necessary.
- Position-Adaptive Scaling (Theorem 4.11, uniqueness): under geometric-preservation and full-coverage constraints, the unique continuous solution is τ*_k(t) = t^{α_k} with α_k = (N−k)/(N−1), sharpening the exponent from log T to log t and inducing a scale-free impulse response (Corollary 4.13).
- Invariance properties: zero computational/state overhead (Proposition 4.17) and expressiveness preservation via surjectivity onto strictly ordered spectra (Proposition 4.18 / Corollary 4.19).
- Instantiation across five architectures (Mamba-2, RWKV-7, Gated DeltaNet, GLA, RetNet) with an architecture-agnostic drop-in module (Algorithm 1).

## Methodology

**Diagonal linear recurrence primitive (Definition 2.1).** S_t = diag(w_t)·S_{t−1} + F(x_t, S_{t−1}), y_t = G(S_t, x_t), where w_t ∈ (0,1)^N are per-channel decay gates. Log-decay parameterization p_k := −log w_k > 0; timescale τ_k := 1/p_k. Spectral coherence µ_ij := |⟨h_i,h_j⟩|/(‖h_i‖₂‖h_j‖₂) = sech(|ln p_i − ln p_j|/2) measures channel redundancy (h_k(s) = e^{−p_k s}).

**PoST module (Algorithm 1).** Two steps replacing the base-decay parameterization: (1) Spectral Reparameterization — g_j ← Softplus(δ_j); p_k ← θ + Σ_{j<k} g_j; d_{base,k} ← −exp(p_k), enforcing strict ordering; (2) Position-Adaptive Scaling — compute mean gap Ḡ, taper exponents α_k ← clamp((N−k)/(N−1) + ((p_k−p_1)−(k−1)Ḡ)/log T_train, 0, 1) (Proposition 4.16, spectrum-adaptive correction), then d_{eff,k} ← d_{base,k}/t^{α_k}; w_{t,k} ← exp(d_{eff,k}).

**Theory.** Modeling conditions 3.2 (hierarchical stationarity: R_b(s) = b^{−β}R(s)), 3.3 (resolution irreducibility σ_min=1), 3.5 (logarithmic information equipartition, slack ϵ). Lemma 3.6 derives R(s) = C·s^{−β}. The minimax bound (Theorem 4.7) follows from reducing t^{−β} exponential-sum approximation to rational approximation of s^{β−1} via the Laplace transform and applying Gonchar–Rakhmanov condenser capacity theory. Robustness: Corollary 4.15 bounds taper deviation under approximate equipartition by (2ϵ/(1−ϵ))·(N−k)/(N−1).

**Architecture instantiations.** Mamba-2 PoST (Algorithm 2): replaces A parameterization with cumulative-softplus and scales A by s_{l,k} = t_l^{−α_k} inside the SSD kernel (Ā_{k,t} = exp(A_k·s_{t,k}·Δ_{k,t})), O(L·N) overhead (<1% wall-clock). RWKV-7 PoST (Algorithm 3): additive logit-space scaling ℓ_{eff,t,k} = w_{0,k} + [LoRA(x_w)]_k − α_k·log t inside the sigmoid gate, with macro-micro decomposition and zigzag LoRA bias. Gated DeltaNet, GLA, RetNet share analogous reparameterizations (GLA and RetNet converge to the same PoST model).

**Experiments.** MQAR (Zoology framework): 2-layer models, T_train=512, four-stage curriculum K∈{16,32,64,128}, BF16, AdamW (wd 0.1, grad clip 1.0), tested at T∈{512,1024,2048,4096} (1×–8×), state sizes 64K/32K/16K (d∈{512,256}, h∈{4,8}). Zero-shot LM: pretrained on FineWeb-Edu at T_train=2048, 180M and 440M params, evaluated on 7 benchmarks (HellaSwag, PIQA, ARC-Easy, ARC-Challenge, WinoGrande, OpenBookQA, LAMBADA) via the LM Evaluation Harness. NIAH: single-needle (Single-1/2/3) and multi-needle (MultiKey/Query/Value) at T∈{1K,2K,4K}. Baseline and PoST share identical hyperparameters, data, and compute; only decay parameterization differs.

## Results & Highlights

- **MQAR length extrapolation (Table 2, state=16K, Mamba-2):** PoST lifts average accuracy across T∈{512,1K,2K,4K} from 54.2% → 61.0%, with the largest gains at the hardest 8× extrapolation: T=4096 jumps from 5.7% → 12.7% and T=2048 from 31.2% → 44.1%. RWKV-7 at state=16K goes 63.0 → 72.2 avg (T=2048: 46.0 → 70.9). RetNet at state=64K improves dramatically (37.3 → 69.7 avg). The sole regression: GLA at state=64K (71.5 → 69.7 avg), though GLA-PoST wins at 32K and 16K.
- **Zero-shot LM (Table 3):** consistent modest average improvements across all architectures at 180M — Mamba-2 38.7 → 38.8, RWKV-7 39.8 → 40.6, Gated DeltaNet 39.1 → 39.8 — and at 440M for Mamba-2 (42.2 → 42.5). The biggest single jump is Mamba-2 440M HellaSwag acc 24.1 → 28.0 and LAMBADA ppl 77.3 → 62.6 (lower is better).
- **NIAH long-context retrieval (Table 4):** Mamba-2 180M average improves from 4.9 → 11.1 (Single-3 at 2K: 0.8 → 8.4; MultiKey at 1K: 15.4 → 71.0; MultiValue at 2K: 5.5 → 12.6). Mamba-2 440M average improves from 24.4 → 28.0. Gated DeltaNet 180M improves modestly 28.9 → 29.8. RWKV-7 stays roughly flat (25.5 → 25.1) because its baseline sigmoid/power-law init already maintains a well-distributed spectrum.
- **Theoretical minimax bound (Theorem 4.7 / B.6):** geometric spacing achieves E_N(K) ≤ C₅·exp(−π²N/(log T + C₆)) for K(t)=t^{−β}, β∈(0,1), with constants depending only on β; PoST's position-adaptive scaling sharpens this to exp(−cN/log t) at each position t (Proposition 4.9). Random spacing is lower-bounded by exp(−C₂N/log N) (Lemma B.4); linear spacing by exp(−C₄N/√T) (Lemma B.5).
- **Empirical spectrum validation (Figures 1–2):** in pretrained Mamba-2 (180M and 440M), the baseline head timescale distribution collapses into narrow fast-timescale spikes (severe minimum gap collapse), whereas PoST produces a perfect straight line on the log-scale across all layers/heads, confirming structural geometric spacing (Proposition 4.5). Figure 3 shows learned α_k closely tracks the theoretical linear (N−k)/(N−1) blueprint.

## Critical Assessment

The empirical scale is the most prominent weakness and the author concedes it: LM and NIAH evaluations use only 180M and 440M-parameter models trained on 4–9B tokens, and the author states they are "actively scaling PoST to 1.5B parameters trained on 30B tokens" — so the headline LM gains are tiny in absolute terms (Mamba-2 440M avg 42.2 → 42.5; RWKV-7 180M 39.8 → 40.6), within plausible seed variance, and the paper reports no error bars or multi-seed confidence intervals despite the differences being <1 point on several benchmarks. The zero-shot improvements are explicitly described as "consistent, though modest," and the strongest empirical case rests entirely on MQAR (a synthetic 2-layer task) and NIAH retrieval for Mamba-2 specifically. The theoretical results, while rigorous, depend on three strong modeling assumptions (Conditions 3.2, 3.3, 3.5) — scale-free 1/f^β correlations, single-token resolution, and logarithmic information equipartition with slack ϵ — whose empirical validity is asserted via citations rather than measured on the actual pretraining data, and the framework is derived only for diagonal (scalar-per-channel) recurrences, excluding matrix-valued states like Mamba-2's full SSD form except as an approximation. The RWKV-7 NIAH result (25.5 → 25.1, a slight regression) is explained post hoc as "baseline already well-distributed," which is plausible but underscores that PoST's benefit is conditional on a poorly-conditioned baseline rather than universally positive.

## Strengths

- Clean, rigorous theory: the minimax bound (Theorem 4.7/B.6) genuinely connects SSM decay design to classical Gonchar–Rakhmanov rational approximation, and the uniqueness result (Theorem 4.11) makes the linear taper α_k=(N−k)/(N−1) a principled rather than heuristic choice.
- Architecture-agnostic and truly drop-in: proven zero-overhead (Proposition 4.17) and expressiveness-preserving (Proposition 4.18 surjectivity), with concrete algorithms for five major model families.
- Honest about scale and about the one regression (GLA at state=64K, RWKV-7 NIAH); does not overclaim universality and explicitly frames PoST as "spectral hygiene" most beneficial when the baseline spectrum is poorly conditioned.
- Empirical spectrum visualizations (Figures 1–3) directly confirm the theory's predicted geometric spacing and the minimum-gap-collapse pathology of standard Mamba-2 init.

## Limitations

- Small empirical scale (180M/440M, 4–9B tokens); the headline LM gains are <1 average point and reported without error bars or multi-seed CIs, leaving statistical significance unverified.
- Theory rests on three modeling assumptions (scale-free correlations, single-token resolution, log information equipartition) asserted by citation rather than measured on the pretraining corpus.
- Restricted to diagonal (scalar-per-channel) recurrences; matrix-valued SSD states are handled only approximately.
- Largest gains are on Mamba-2 specifically; RWKV-7 and GLA show flat or mixed results because their native spectra are already well-distributed, so the benefit is architecture-dependent.
- The Position-Adaptive Scaling assumes t is known and meaningful at inference; behavior under cached/padded/generation settings and under training-inference length mismatch (beyond the T_train anchor) is only partially analyzed.
- Single-author, self-funded compute; reproducibility rests on the released code rather than independent replication.

## Relevance

直接映射到 **LLM Hidden States & Interpretability** 与 **ML Reliability & Mathematical Foundations**：本文从逼近论与信息论出发，刻画线性递推模型（SSM/RWKV/linear attention 这类高效 LLM backbone）隐藏状态的"衰减谱"应如何设计才能可靠保留长程记忆，并给出结构性的、可证明最优的参数化。它同时触及 **LLM Inference Evaluation**——通过 MQAR、NIAH、零样本基准量化长上下文检索与长度外推性能，是训练-评估闭环的工作。

## Questions for Further Exploration

- 在 1.5B+ 规模与 30B+ tokens 上，PoST 的零样本 LM 增益是否会从"modest"放大到显著，还是会被更强的默认初始化吸收？
- 衰减谱的最优几何结构在因果/自回归生成（而非并行训练）下是否仍然成立？位置 t 在 KV-cache 增量推理中如何定义？
- Position-Adaptive Scaling 与 Transformer 的 RoPE/YaRN/NTK-aware scaling 是否可在同一理论框架下统一——两者都是在做"频率/时间尺度基的尺度缩放"？
- 三个建模条件（1/f^β、单 token 分辨率、对数信息等分配）能否在真实预训练语料上直接测量并拟合 β、ϵ，从而数据驱动地选择 taper？
- 矩阵值状态（Mamba-2 完整 SSD、Gated DeltaNet）能否扩展该理论，还是 diagonal 假设是根本性的？
