Babel: Jailbreaking Safety Attention via Obfuscation Distribution Optimized Sampling

arXiv: 2605.17971

Date: 2026-05-18

一句话总结： 本文发现LLM的安全对齐仅依赖稀疏分布的注意力头，基于此提出Babel黑盒越狱框架，通过系统性混淆采样和迭代分布优化，在GPT-4o上将攻击成功率从41.33%提升至82.67%。

  • 在前沿商业模型上取得了最先进的攻击成功率和查询效率（GPT-4o: 82.67%, Claude-3.5-haiku: 78.33%）。

Key Contributions

  • Identify an intrinsic vulnerability in LLM safety mechanisms: safety alignment relies on a small set of sparsely distributed attention heads, leaving much of the representational space weakly monitored.
  • Formalize a mathematical jailbreaking model that characterizes the boundary of effective text obfuscation and analytically explains observed jailbreak behaviors.
  • Propose Babel, an efficient black-box attack framework using systematic obfuscation sampling with iterative, feedback-driven distribution refinement.
  • Demonstrate state-of-the-art attack success rates on frontier commercial models, doubling the success rate of prior methods on GPT-4o and Claude-3.5-haiku.

Methodology

Babel operates as a black-box attack framework grounded in the observation that safety alignment in LLMs is concentrated in sparse attention heads. The approach consists of:

  1. Mathematical Modeling: A formal jailbreaking model is constructed to characterize the delicate boundary of effective text obfuscation, explaining why certain obfuscation strategies bypass safety filters.
  2. Obfuscation Sampling: Systematic generation of obfuscated text variants that exploit the weakly monitored regions of the representational space.
  3. Iterative Distribution Refinement: A feedback-driven loop that refines the obfuscation distribution based on attack outcomes, progressively improving success rates without access to model internals.
  4. Query-Efficient Black-Box Design: The entire framework operates without access to model gradients, logits, or internal representations, relying only on output-based feedback.

  • Average query budget: ~40 queries per successful attack, demonstrating strong query efficiency.
  • Comprehensive evaluation across multiple frontier commercial models confirms robustness of the approach.
