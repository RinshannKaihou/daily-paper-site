> **一句话总结：** 本文提出"表征-读出分解"框架（f = W·ϕ_θ），用临界维数 N_crit、GLUE 几何量、线性探针与最后一层 NTK 对齐四类任务无关诊断量，证明 grokking 并非"懒到富"的突变，而是缓慢持续的表征学习 + 偏训练集的读出校准两条速度不同的过程，并用此框架将一个 MNIST grokking 与一个 double-descent 例子诊断为"伪泛化"。

> **TL;DR:** A task-agnostic "representation-readout decomposition" framework that splits any net into encoder ϕ_θ and linear readout W, tracked by 4 diagnostics: critical dimension N_crit, GLUE geometric measures (D, R, ρ_c, ρ_a), linear-probe accuracy, and last-layer NTK-label alignment. Applied to canonical grokking tasks (modular addition p=113, permutation composition S_5, sparse parity n=40,k=3) on MLPs and Transformers, the paper shows representation learning is *continuous and gradual* (20–80% of the N_crit drop on train, 5–50% on test happens before grokking onset), refuting the strict lazy→rich account, while the readout is systematically *train-biased* (NTK train−test alignment gap peaks at train-accuracy saturation then decays to 0). It then diagnoses a previously-reported MNIST grokking (Liu et al. 2022b, ~80% final acc vs. ~98% SVM) and a label-noise epoch-wise double descent as *spurious* — both disappear under standard training, confirmed by the diagnostics vanishing.

## 核心贡献

- 提出"表征-读出分解" f(x)=W·ϕ_θ(x)：把网络在最后一层线性层处切开，分别追踪编码器表征学习与读出校准两条动态。
- 设计四类任务无关的诊断量：临界维数 N_crit、GLUE 几何量（D, R, ρ_c, ρ_a）、线性探针精度、最后一层 NTK-标签对齐，并给出每项的诊断含义。
- 修正"懒到富"理论：在 grokking 之前表征学习已缓慢持续进行（N_crit 持续下降），并非在 grokking 起点突变；读出在 grokking 前对训练集系统性偏置（NTK 对齐差为正）。
- 给出区分"真伪泛化"的四条诊断特征（读出过拟合、表征退化、次优读出、伪对齐），并用其将一个 MNIST grokking 例子与一个 double-descent 例子重新诊断为非标准训练配方导致的伪现象。

## Key Contributions

- **Representation-readout decomposition (Sec 2.1):** Write f = W·ϕ_θ(x) with ϕ_θ the encoder (all params up to penultimate layer / pooled last hidden state) and W the final linear readout; deliberately chosen because the linear readout is shared across MLPs, CNNs, and Transformers, enabling task-agnostic diagnostics.
- **Four task-agnostic diagnostics (Sec 2.2):** (1) Critical dimension N_crit — median projection dimension at which label-conditioned manifolds {M_c} remain linearly separable with prob ≥0.5 (smaller = more untangled = more generalizable); (2) GLUE geometric measures D (effective manifold dim), R (radius), ρ_c, ρ_a (center/axis alignment) analytically linked to N_crit; (3) linear-probe accuracy gap (best regularized linear readout vs. the model's actual readout) — diagnoses readout over/under-fit; (4) last-layer NTK-label alignment (train vs test) — diagnoses whether readout gradients move toward label-discriminative directions.
- **Two speeds of learning in grokking (Sec 3):** Across modular addition (p=113), permutation composition (S_5, C=120), sparse parity (n=40, k=3), on both MLPs and Transformers — representation learning (N_crit drop) is continuous throughout training, with 20–80% (train) / 5–50% (test) of the drop occurring *before* grokking onset; the readout is systematically train-biased (NTK train−test alignment gap positive, peaking at train-acc saturation, decaying to 0 as grokking completes); the gap disappears in non-grokking controls.
- **Architecture-discriminating geometric pathways (Sec 3.3):** MLPs and Transformers reach the same surface grokking behavior via *opposite* geometric routes — MLPs increase ρ_c (hurts N_crit) and decrease ρ_a (helps), Transformers do the opposite — showing the framework can distinguish algorithms.
- **Diagnosing spurious learning (Sec 4):** Four diagnostic signatures (readout overfit, representation degradation, sub-optimal readout, spurious alignment); applied to Liu et al. 2022b's MNIST grokking (~80% final acc vs ~98% SVM) → readout overfit + representation degradation; applied to Nakkiran et al. 2021 label-noise double descent → sub-optimal readout + representation degradation + spurious alignment. In both cases the signatures *vanish* under standard training, proving the phenomena are recipe-induced.

## Methodology

**Decomposition (Sec 2.1).** f(x)=W·ϕ_θ(x); for an L-layer MLP, ϕ_θ(x)=h_{L−1}, W=W_L; for a Transformer, ϕ_θ(x)=Pool(h_L) (e.g., last-token), W=unembedding matrix U. Bias absorbed into W via a constant feature; post-readout softmax omitted.

**Diagnostic 1 — Critical dimension N_crit (Appendix A.1.1, after Cohen et al. 2020 / Chou et al. 2025a).** The smallest projection dim N_proj at which randomly projected label-conditioned manifolds {M_c} are linearly separable with probability ≥0.5; small N_crit ⇒ robustly untangled ⇒ more generalizable.

**Diagnostic 2 — GLUE geometric measures (Appendix A.3, Chou et al. 2025a).** Effective manifold dimension D, radius R, center alignment ρ_c and axis alignment ρ_a; analytically linked to N_crit via manifold capacity theory (Chung et al. 2018). Decreasing ρ_c and increasing ρ_a both improve separability.

**Diagnostic 3 — Linear probe (Alain & Bengio 2016).** Fit a regularized linear probe on ϕ_θ(x) using the model's train/test split; gap between probe and model accuracy isolates encoder quality from the actual readout.

**Diagnostic 4 — Last-layer NTK-label alignment (Appendix A.5).** Alignment between the last-layer NTK and the label kernel; high alignment ⇒ readout gradient flow makes fast label-discriminative progress. Computed on both train and test splits; train−test gap diagnoses readout overfitting.

**Tasks (Sec 2.3, details in Appendix B):**
- *Modular addition:* predict (a+b) mod p, p=113, C=113 classes.
- *Permutation composition:* σ∘τ in symmetric group S_5, C=120 classes.
- *Sparse parity:* parity of a fixed unknown k=3-subset of x∈{−1,+1}^40, n=40, C=2.
- *MNIST grokking (Liu et al. 2022b):* initial weights scaled >4×, trained on 1k of 60k samples.
- *Epoch-wise double descent (Nakkiran et al. 2021):* induced via label noise.
Each grokking task trained as both MLP and Transformer where applicable; results averaged over 3 seeds.

**Four spurious-learning signatures (Sec 4 intro):** (1) readout overfit (model's generalization gap ≫ LP's); (2) representation degradation (LP accuracy ↓ while N_crit ↑); (3) sub-optimal readout (LP acc ≫ model acc); (4) spurious alignment (NTK alignment of a spurious signal ≫ that of the true task signal).

## Results & Highlights

- **Representation learning is gradual, not silent, before grokking (Fig 3c):** across all (task, model) pairs, the fraction of total N_crit drop occurring *before grokking onset* is **20–80% on train and 5–50% on test** (averaged over 3 seeds) — directly contradicting the strict lazy→rich prediction that N_crit should stay flat until onset.
- **Readout is train-biased before grokking (Fig 4):** the NTK train−test alignment gap is **positive and largest in the train100→onset (blue) phase**, decaying to ~0 as grokking completes; the gap is **substantially smaller in non-grokking controls** (Fig 4b red dashed lines), confirming it is specific to the grokking regime.
- **MLPs vs Transformers use opposite geometric routes (Fig 5):** across ModAdd and PermComp (and SparseParity in App D.1), MLPs **increase ρ_c and decrease ρ_a**, Transformers do the **opposite** — both improve separability but via qualitatively different mechanisms; radius R and dimension D consistently decrease in both.
- **Spurious MNIST grokking diagnosed (Fig 6, Sec 4.1):** the Liu et al. 2022b example reaches only **~80% final test accuracy vs ~98% for an SVM** on the same task; diagnostics show (i) model generalization gap ≫ LP gap during pre-grokking epochs (readout overfit), and (ii) LP accuracy **decreases** while N_crit **increases** in pre-grokking phases (active representation degradation). Removing the >4× initial weight scaling and the 1k-sample restriction **eliminates both the grokking pattern and the diagnostic signatures**.
- **Spurious double descent diagnosed (Fig 7, Sec 4.2):** under Nakkiran et al. 2021's label-noise recipe, LP accuracy on clean labels exceeds model accuracy (sub-optimal readout); during the test-accuracy descent phase N_crit rises and NTK alignment drops (representation deterioration); in the recovery phase the noisy-label NTK alignment surpasses clean-label train/test (spurious signal domination). Removing label noise eliminates both double descent and the signatures.

## Critical Assessment

- **Diagnostics are correlational, not causal:** the four measures track *what* changes during grokking/double descent, but no intervention (e.g., freezing the readout, or forcing a particular NTK alignment) is shown to *cause* or *prevent* the phenomena — so "two speeds of learning" is a descriptive refinement, not a proven mechanism.
- **The "spurious" relabeling rests on a strong normative claim:** the MNIST example is called spurious partly because an SVM reaches ~98% vs the model's ~80% — but grokking research deliberately uses small-data / high-weight-norm regimes to *isolate* dynamics, so dismissing them as "non-standard" arguably throws out the phenomenon under study.
- **Decomposition is at the final linear layer only:** the authors themselves flag intermediate-layer analysis as future work, so the framework cannot currently localize where in the encoder the representation degradation actually occurs.
- **Small synthetic tasks dominate:** modular addition, permutation composition, sparse parity, and a 1k-sample MNIST — the framework's behavior on realistic large-scale training (ImageNet/LLM pretraining) is untested, and the paper acknowledges this.
- **NTK alignment as "readout learning direction"** assumes the NTK regime is at least approximately valid late in training, which is exactly where grokking occurs and where NTK assumptions are known to break down.

## Strengths

- Genuinely task-agnostic: the same four measures apply unchanged across MLPs, Transformers, modular arithmetic, permutations, parity, MNIST, and label-noise double descent.
- Provides a *negative-result* tool (the four spurious signatures) that lets researchers flag recipe-induced artifacts — a real methodological contribution.
- The architecture-discriminating geometric finding (MLPs and Transformers use opposite ρ_c/ρ_a routes) is a non-trivial, falsifiable empirical claim.
- Refines a prominent existing theory (Kumar et al. 2023 lazy→rich) with quantitative phase-fraction measurements rather than anecdote.

## Limitations

- Decomposition limited to the final linear layer.
- Diagnostics are observational, not interventional.
- Tested only on small synthetic grokking tasks + one small MNIST setup.
- NTK-alignment interpretation is least valid exactly when grokking happens.

## Relevance

直接对应 **"LLM Hidden States & Interpretability"**（表征几何、线性探针、NTK 对齐都是分析 LLM 内部表征与学习动态的核心工具）与 **"LLM Training Stability & Observability"**（提供可贯穿训练全程、跨 train/test 一致的诊断量，可作为训练监控与"伪泛化"预警）。对研究 grokking、双下降与大规模模型训练动态的研究者具有方法论价值。

## Questions for Further Exploration

- The four diagnostics are observational — would *intervening* (e.g., freezing the readout, or projecting gradients onto the test-NTK-aligned subspace) prevent or delay grokking, providing causal evidence for the "two speeds" account?
- The decomposition is final-layer only; the authors flag intermediate-layer analysis as future work — does the representation-degradation signature in the spurious MNIST case originate in a specific early layer, and could that localize the failure?
- The NTK-alignment measure assumes an approximately NTK regime, which is least valid late in training when grokking occurs — was the last-layer NTK actually checked for stability across the grokking transition?
- The paper refines Kumar et al. (2023)'s lazy→rich account — could the continuous N_crit descent shown here be predicted by Kumar et al.'s laziness parameter, reconciling the two frameworks quantitatively?
- All tasks are small/synthetic; would the four spurious-learning signatures fire on realistic LLM pretraining or instruction-tuning where delayed generalization is sometimes anecdotally reported?
