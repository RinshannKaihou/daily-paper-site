Methodology

The model leverages Gemini's multimodal architecture as its backbone. Training follows a multi-stage, multi-task contrastive learning paradigm. It handles arbitrary interleaved combinations of modalities (image+text, video+audio, etc.) and produces a single embedding vector per input. The staged curriculum progressively moves from simpler unimodal tasks to complex cross-modal and multimodal retrieval tasks.
