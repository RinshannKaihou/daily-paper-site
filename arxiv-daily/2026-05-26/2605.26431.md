> **一句话总结：** 通过设计UD距离不变的wh-movement实验刺激，发现13个LLM在4个模型家族中一致表现出阶段结构（phase structure）的表征证据——包括跨从句的阶段计数梯度和从句内部的符号不对称性，且激活修补实验证实这些表征具有因果作用。

> **TL;DR:** This paper designs wh-movement stimuli where Universal Dependencies distances are invariant across conditions, demonstrating that 13 LLMs from four families encode Minimalist Program phase structure — including a phase-count gradient and a within-clause sign asymmetry — that UD-based probing cannot detect by construction, with activation patching confirming causal involvement in 12/13 models.

## 核心贡献

- **设计了UD距离不变的实验刺激**：三个条件（bare small clause、infinitival、finite）的wh-movement刺激中，被探测词对之间的UD树距离完全相同，确保任何非零效应都反映了UD之外的结构信息。
- **发现跨从句的阶段计数梯度**：在wh-esubj探测对上，12/13个模型在规范层报告下显示出 β_fin > β_inf > 0 的梯度，与阶段边界数量递增一致。
- **发现从句内部的符号不对称性**：在esubj-evb探测对上，所有13个模型均显示 β_fin < 0 且 β_inf > 0，这一模式由阶段内聚性（phase-internal cohesion）预测，而UD、线性距离或单调复杂性均无法解释。
- **引入规范层报告方法**：将所有对比锚定在最大化最可靠对比的层上，消除了逐对比峰值报告中的自由度。
- **激活修补实验证实因果作用**：12/13个模型中嵌入主语的表征因果编码了从句类型信息。

## Key Contributions

- **UD-invariant stimulus design**: Wh-movement stimuli across three conditions (bare small clause, infinitival, finite) where UD-tree distances between probed word pairs are identical by construction, ensuring non-zero effects reflect structure beyond UD annotation.
- **Cross-clause phase-count gradient**: On the wh-esubj probe pair, 12/13 models show β_fin > β_inf > 0 under canonical-layer reporting, consistent with increasing phase boundaries crossed during wh-movement.
- **Within-clause sign asymmetry**: On the esubj-evb probe pair, all 13/13 models show β_fin < 0 and β_inf > 0 — predicted by phase-internal cohesion (an MP abstraction invisible to UD) and contradicted by UD-distance, linear-distance, and monotone-complexity accounts.
- **Canonical-layer reporting**: Anchoring all contrasts to the layer maximizing the most reliable contrast (L*) removes a per-contrast degree of freedom, yielding strictly stronger claims than per-contrast peak reporting.
- **Causal corroboration via activation patching**: Replacing the embedded subject's residual-stream representation with a counterfactual source shifts probe distances in the predicted direction in 12/13 models.

## Methodology

**Stimuli**: Triples of wh-questions sharing the wh-phrase, embedded subject lemma, and embedded verb lemma, varying by matrix verb class and complement type: bare (perception/causative verbs), infinitival (ECM/object-control verbs), finite (bridge verbs). 1,000 items yield 3,000 stimuli.

**Probing**: Structural probes (Hewitt & Manning 2019) — linear projections from hidden states to Euclidean space — trained on UD-EWT tree distances, then evaluated on the wh-movement stimuli without re-fitting. Probe distances measured for two word pairs: wh-esubj (cross-clause) and esubj-evb (within-clause).

**Effect-size estimation**: Treatment-coded OLS regression per (model, layer, pair) with cluster-robust standard errors at item level. Benjamini-Hochberg FDR correction applied across all tests within each model.

**Models**: 13 decoder-only base models from Gemma-3, Llama-3, Mistral, and Qwen families (1B–27B parameters).

**Causal experiment**: Activation patching replaces the embedded subject's residual-stream representation at L* with a counterfactual (infinitival → bare), measuring Δβ on probe distances.

## Results & Highlights

- **Phase-count gradient**: Median β_fin/β_inf ratio ≈ 5.1 under canonical-layer reporting; β_fin ranges from +0.55 (Gemma-3-1B) to +0.99 (Gemma-3-27B). 94% median FDR-significant positive layers for β_fin across the panel.
- **Sign asymmetry**: 13/13 models show β_fin^peak < 0 and β_inf^peak > 0 on esubj-evb with magnitudes typically 0.3–0.6. No alternative account (UD, linear distance, monotone complexity) predicts this pattern.
- **Causal patching**: 12/13 models show positive Δβ on wh-esubj with 95% CI excluding zero (range +0.08–+0.40). Negative control (patching at wh-position) yields |Δβ| ≤ 0.05 in 12/13 models.
- **Qwen-3-4B is the sole failure**: The only model failing canonical-layer reporting on both the phase-count gradient and the causal experiment.
- **Scaling is heterogeneous**: Gemma scales monotonically with size on β_fin; Llama-3.2-1B (+0.77) exceeds Llama-3.1-8B (+0.72).

## Critical Assessment

**Novelty**: High. The UD-invariant stimulus design is a genuine methodological advance — it closes the "UD ceiling" loophole that has limited structural probing conclusions. The within-clause sign asymmetry is a striking result with no obvious non-phase-theoretic explanation.

**Rigor**: Strong. Canonical-layer reporting is a meaningful methodological contribution that addresses a real (if underappreciated) p-hacking vector in prior work. The activation-patching experiment directly engages with probing-scepticism concerns. However, the study is English-only, and the specific stimulus design exploits English verb-class properties.

**Reproducibility**: Good. Code and stimuli are provided; the panel uses publicly available base models; probe training takes <2 hours on a single GPU.

**Significance**: The central claim — that UD probes provide a lower bound, not an upper bound, on syntactic encoding — is important for the interpretability field. However, the connection from observed effects to phase-internal cohesion specifically (rather than some other within-clause tightening mechanism) relies on theoretical inference rather than direct mechanistic evidence.

**Red flags**: The phase-cohesion explanation is post-hoc and theory-laden; the paper acknowledges it is "one possible account among others." The Qwen-3-4B failure is unexplained. No models above 27B or instruction-tuned variants are tested.

## Strengths

- Elegant experimental design that controls for UD confounds by construction rather than by statistical adjustment.
- 13/13 replication of the sign asymmetry across four architecture families is exceptionally robust for a probing result.
- Canonical-layer reporting sets a new standard for multi-contrast structural probing studies.
- The causal patching experiment with a negative control addresses the probing-scepticism literature directly.

## Limitations

- English-only stimuli exploiting English-specific verb-class and case properties; cross-linguistic generalization is uncertain.
- The panel excludes instruction-tuned models, MoE architectures, and models above 27B parameters.
- The phase-count gradient prediction is directional only; no prediction about magnitude ratios.
- Phase-internal cohesion is inferred rather than directly measured; alternative formal accounts are not ruled out mechanistically.
- Qwen-3-4B's failure remains unexplained.

## 相关性

这项研究对理解LLM内部语法表征具有重要意义。它通过巧妙的实验设计证明，仅依赖UD标注的探测方法可能低估了模型的语法编码能力，这对可解释性研究和形式语言学与神经网络之间的桥梁构建都有启发。

## Questions for Further Exploration

1. Would the phase-count gradient and sign asymmetry replicate in languages with overt phase-structure diagnostics (e.g., V2 languages, ergative languages)?
2. Do instruction-tuned or larger models (>27B) show stronger or qualitatively different phase-structure effects?
3. Can mechanistic interpretability methods (e.g., circuit analysis) identify specific attention heads or MLP subnetworks responsible for phase-sensitive representations?
4. Is the within-clause sign asymmetry driven by phase-internal cohesion specifically, or by some other within-clause representational tightening that correlates with phase structure?
5. Does the Qwen-3-4B failure reflect a genuine architectural difference or a training-data artifact?
