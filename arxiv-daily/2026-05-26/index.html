Just 0.1% of an LLM's Computational Graph Is Enough to Predict Its Next Token

Corentin Kervadec, Iuliia Lysova, Iuri Macocco, Marco Baroni, Gemma Boleda

Optimizers & Training Dynamics 9 papers

MuCon: Clipped Muon Updates for LLM Training Albert Yi Replaces Muon's full orthogonalization with singular-value clipping for controlled spectral norm updates. When Muon Optimizer Meets Adversarial Training Jun Yan et al. Muon's orthogonalized updates impose a spectral-norm stability ceiling, competitive with SGD in adversarial training. Convergence of Spectral Descent for Non-smooth Optimization Yixuan Yang et al. Proves global linear convergence for Spectral Descent (a simplified Muon variant) in non-smooth convex settings. The Stability of Singular Distribution: A Spectral Perspective Hongtao Zhang et al. Singular value spectra stabilize early in pre-training, bounding loss descent via ε∝η/||W||. Two Speeds of Learning: Grokking & Double Descent Chi-Ning Chou et al. Representation-readout decomposition unifies grokking and double descent through different encoder-classifier learning speeds. SNR & Sample Size Govern Representational Alignment Ali Hussaini Umar et al. Representational alignment is monotonically governed by SNR and non-monotonically by sample size; stronger alignment ≠ better generalization. Negligible in Size, Significant in Effect: Scale Vectors in LLMs Mingze Wang et al. Normalization scale vectors are tiny in parameter count but critical for pretraining via self-amplifying preconditioning. Agile Online Model Selection via Safeguarded Large LR Kei Takemura et al. Pilot-Commit: Rollout Allocation for RL Post-Training Woojeong Kim et al. Two-phase rollout allocation concentrates budget on high-variance prompts, reaching target accuracy 4× faster than DAPO. Tournament-GRPO for Open-Ended Long-Form Generation Zixuan Yang et al. Tournament-style pairwise comparisons replace absolute scoring for RL in open-ended generation, +4.52 points over strongest baseline. RLVR Data Allocation: Reasoning Depth & Complexity Yihua Zhu et al. Joint depth-complexity coverage outperforms single-axis RLVR recipes; abductive reasoning fails to generalize beyond training coverage. RLVR Datasets and Where to Find Them (ATLAS / DAPO++) Hsiu-Yuan Huang et al. ATLAS traces RLVR data lineage to 20 atomic sources, reveals massive overlap, and builds decontaminated DAPO++ dataset. SAERL: SAE-Guided Post-Training Data Engineering Yi Jing et al. Sparse Autoencoder internals guide RL data engineering for diversity, difficulty, and quality, improving accuracy +3% and reducing steps 20%. Calibrated Interactive RL for Multi-Turn Dialogue Xiaohua Wang et al. Proves context distribution shift compounds quadratically over dialogue turns; calibrated simulator + interactive GRPO achieves SOTA. GraphGPO: Graph-Based Credit Assignment for Agentic RL Xin Cheng et al. Graph-based step-level credit assignment replaces coarse trajectory-level attribution, SOTA on ALFWorld, WebShop, and Sokoban.

Dense2MoE: Unified Pruning & MoE Upcycling Fengfa Li et al. Unifies layer pruning with MoE upcycling for on-device LLMs, pushing Pareto frontier with only 225B tokens of continual pretraining. Cassandra: Self-Speculative Decoding at Edge Soongyu Choi et al. Training-free self-speculative decoding via fine-grained pruning achieves 2.41× lossless speedup for edge LLM inference. ReMoE: Expert Reuse via Router Fine-Tuning Xiongwei Zhu et al. Fine-tuning the MoE router for temporal expert reuse achieves 43–50% latency reduction with negligible quality loss. RT-Lynx: Activation Sparsity for Diffusion Transformers Xing Cong et al. Shifts N:M sparsity from weights to activations in Diffusion Transformers, achieving up to 1.55× linear-layer speedup. JetViT: Post-Training Attention Search for ViT Dongyun Zou et al. Converts full-attention ViTs to hybrid attention models via post-training search, achieving 1.79× speedup without accuracy loss. Mixture of Activations: Token-Adaptive FFN Mingze Wang et al. Token-adaptive mixing of FFN activation functions via lightweight gates outperforms SwiGLU with only ~1.1× wall-clock overhead.

Interpretability & Mechanistic Analysis 8 papers

Must Read s-Trace: Tracing Computation Density in LLMs Corentin Kervadec et al. Greedy subgraph search on 10 LLMs reveals ~0.1% of the graph suffices for greedy decoding, exposing a two-phase computation structure. CoT Works via Local Co-Occurrence at Probe Time Xiang Wang et al. CoT gains come from local token co-occurrence (n=2–3), not sentence-level logical derivation—even shuffled rationales work. CoT Disrupts Simple Steering of Refusal Kia-Jüng Yang et al. Refusal is jointly encoded in activations and CoT; single-direction steering achieves only 39–43% compliance. Composition Collapse: Stable Facts ≠ Compositional Reasoning Zhe Yu et al. Training recipes with indistinguishable atomic knowledge can diverge 40+ points in compositional reasoning. Layer Swap: Rethinking the Multilingual Reasoning Gap Maxence Lasbordes et al. Training-free Layer Swap transfers mid-layers across languages, closing the multilingual reasoning gap to just 1.9–3.5%. Probing Minimalist Phase Structure in LLMs Yuanhao Chen et al. 13 LLMs encode Minimalist Program phase structure invisible to UD-based probing, confirmed by causal activation patching. EpiMer: Model Merging on Loss Landscape Juanwu Lu et al. Model merging as Fréchet mean on Riemannian manifold with Hessian curvature, outperforming all baselines on CLIP-ViT. Extra-Merge: Rank-1 Subspace Model Merging Wenjie Zhou et al. Merged checkpoints collapse onto rank-1 subspace; extrapolating along it yields training-free merging across GPT-2 and LLaMA.

Jailbreak Susceptibility via Behavioral Geometry Hayden Helm et al. Predicts jailbreak vulnerability with 0.94 AUPRC using only ~2% probe cost via behavioral geometry across model populations. Fine-Tuning Defenses Susceptible to Simple Attacks Kevin Kuo et al. Open-weight safety defenses bypassed by gradient-free attacks (abliteration, prefilling), raising ASR from <10% to 16–96%. Vectors Are Not Neutral: Privacy in LLM Summarization Weixin Liu et al. LLM representation vectors leak sensitive attributes like patient race; SurfaceLoRA reduces recovery to near chance. ContextGuard: Structured Self-Auditing for ICL Hongbo Jin et al. Separates model outputs into confirmed and uncertain regions for targeted revision, improving ICL reliability by +4.21 pp.
