# Representation over Routing: Overcoming Surrogate Hacking in Multi-Timescale PPO

> **一句话总结：** 本文系统揭示了多时间尺度PPO中动态路由机制的两种算法病态——代理目标黑客（Surrogate Objective Hacking）和时间不确定性悖论（Paradox of Temporal Uncertainty），并提出Target Decoupling架构：Critic端保留多时间尺度预测作为辅助表征学习，Actor端严格隔离短期信号仅依赖长期优势（gamma=0.999）更新策略，在LunarLander-v2上以统计显著的5-seed实验超越200分阈值并消除策略崩溃。

> **TL;DR:** Identifies two critical optimization pathologies in multi-timescale PPO -- surrogate objective hacking (gradient hijacks attention routing) and the Paradox of Temporal Uncertainty (variance-based routing locks onto myopic heads) -- and proposes a Target Decoupling architecture that uses multi-timescale signals solely for auxiliary representation learning on the Critic while updating the Actor with only the longest-horizon advantage, achieving statistically significant improvements over single-timescale baselines on LunarLander-v2.

## 核心贡献
- **首次定义并实证揭示代理目标黑客现象**：在多时间尺度Actor-Critic架构中，当路由权重暴露于策略梯度时，优化器会绕过物理动作学习，转而操纵注意力权重选择数值最大的短期优势，导致策略在近视与远视之间震荡直至崩溃。
- **揭示时间不确定性悖论**：采用无梯度方差路由（如逆方差加权）时，短期预测的TD误差天然趋近于零，导致Softmax路由永久锁定在gamma=0.5的近视神经元上，使Actor不可逆地退化为无意义的空中悬停行为。
- **提出Target Decoupling架构与"表征优于路由"范式**：完全放弃Actor端的动态路由聚合，Critic端通过多时间尺度预测强制学习鲁棒的物理特征表征（辅助表征学习），Actor端严格仅使用gamma=0.999的长期优势更新，从根本上消除两种病态。
- **在LunarLander-v2上实现统计显著的性能突破**：5个独立随机种子实验表明，该方法在约1500回合突破200分"环境已解决"阈值，峰值达约240分，方差极小，完全消除策略崩溃，无需任何超参数调优。

## Key Contributions
- **Formal identification of Surrogate Objective Hacking**: Demonstrates that exposing dynamic attention routing weights to policy gradients in multi-timescale PPO causes the optimizer to abandon physical action learning and instead manipulate routing weights to minimize the surrogate loss -- a degenerate shortcut that severs the connection between routing and the underlying MDP.
- **Discovery of the Paradox of Temporal Uncertainty**: Shows that gradient-free uncertainty-based routing (e.g., inverse-variance weighting via TD errors) permanently locks attention onto the shortest-timescale head (gamma=0.5) because its TD errors are inherently minimal, causing irreversible myopic degeneration where the agent aimlessly hovers rather than pursuing long-term goals.
- **Target Decoupling architecture ("Representation over Routing" paradigm)**: Retains multi-timescale predictions only on the Critic side as auxiliary representation learning to enrich feature extraction, while strictly isolating the Actor from all but the longest-horizon advantage (gamma=0.999), fundamentally eliminating both routing pathologies.
- **Statistically rigorous empirical validation**: Across 5 independent random seeds on LunarLander-v2, the architecture consistently surpasses the 200-point "Environment Solved" threshold at ~1500 episodes, reaches ~240 points at 2500 episodes, and exhibits minimal variance -- all without hyperparameter hacking.

## Methodology

The paper introduces a three-part methodological framework:

1. **Multi-Timescale Value Representation**: The Critic network outputs a vector of value predictions across a set of discount factors Gamma = {0.5, 0.9, 0.99, 0.999}, trained with a mean squared loss across all timescales. This forces the shared feature extractor to simultaneously learn immediate physical feedback (e.g., gravity, momentum from gamma=0.5) and long-term environmental signals.

2. **Diagnosis of Dynamic Routing Pathologies**: Two failure modes are formally analyzed:
   - *Surrogate Objective Hacking*: When an Actor-parameterized attention network generates routing weights, the PPO optimizer discovers that manipulating these weights (rather than action probabilities) is a shortcut to minimizing the surrogate loss, causing policy collapse.
   - *Paradox of Temporal Uncertainty*: When gradient-free TD-error-based Softmax routing is used, the routing permanently locks onto the shortest timescale because short-horizon TD errors are inherently near-zero, causing the agent to degenerate into aimless hovering.

3. **Target Decoupling Architecture**: The Critic retains multi-timescale optimization for auxiliary representation learning. The Actor is completely decoupled from multi-scale mixing and receives only the advantage estimate from the longest timescale (gamma_target = 0.999). The standard PPO-CLIP objective is applied to this single, pure advantage signal. This design exploits the fact that forcing the Critic to fit short-horizon signals produces highly robust feature representations that benefit the long-horizon advantage estimates.

## Results & Highlights

- **Surrogate Hacking ablation**: The Actor-driven attention routing variant (pink curve) suffers catastrophic failure -- episodic return collapses below 0 despite the surrogate loss decreasing, confirming that gradient hijacking of routing weights is a degenerate optimization shortcut.
- **Temporal Uncertainty Paradox ablation**: The TD-error-based Softmax routing variant (green curve) achieves an unprecedentedly low value loss but extremely poor episodic return. Episode length analysis reveals prolonged, unstable wandering behavior -- the agent hovers aimlessly to avoid immediate collision penalties rather than attempting to land.
- **Target Decoupling vs. Baseline**: Over 3000 episodes across 5 seeds, the single-timescale baseline (gamma=0.99) plateaus around 150 points with high variance (stuck in the "hovering for survival" local optimum). Target Decoupling breaks through 200 points at ~1500 episodes, peaks at ~240 points, and maintains a tightly converged variance band.
- **Auxiliary representation effect**: Despite eliminating multi-timescale mixing on the Actor, the Target Decoupling Critic's value loss remains significantly lower than the baseline throughout mid-to-late training, directly confirming that multi-timescale auxiliary learning enriches the underlying feature representations.
- **No hyperparameter hacking**: All results use default PPO settings (clip ratio epsilon=0.2, constant entropy coefficient) without learning rate annealing or environment-specific heuristics.

## Critical Assessment

The paper makes a valuable conceptual contribution by clearly identifying and naming two failure modes in multi-timescale RL architectures. The "Representation over Routing" paradigm is elegant -- rather than trying to fix dynamic routing (which is inherently exploitable), it repurposes multi-timescale signals as auxiliary supervision for representation learning. The ablation design is particularly strong: each pathology is demonstrated through a controlled experiment with clear diagnostic evidence (loss curves, episodic returns, and episode lengths).

However, there are notable concerns: (1) The evaluation is restricted to LunarLander-v2, a relatively simple environment, making it unclear whether the findings generalize to high-dimensional or visual domains. (2) The paper does not compare against other multi-timescale or multi-head Critic baselines from prior literature. (3) The choice of gamma=0.999 as the sole Actor target is presented without sensitivity analysis -- what happens with gamma=0.99 or gamma=0.95? (4) The claims of statistical significance are made qualitatively from learning curves without reporting confidence intervals or formal statistical tests. (5) The connection to neurobiology, while motivating, is not directly validated -- the architecture does not actually implement distributional dopamine coding.

## Strengths

- **Clear problem identification**: The paper precisely defines two distinct failure modes with intuitive explanations and formal mathematical characterization, making the problems accessible and actionable for the community.
- **Elegant architectural solution**: Target Decoupling is conceptually simple (remove routing from Actor, keep multi-scale on Critic) yet effective, embodying the principle that sometimes the best fix is removal rather than addition.
- **Strong ablation design**: Each pathology is isolated and demonstrated with its own controlled experiment, supported by multiple diagnostic metrics (loss, return, episode length).
- **Practical significance**: The findings are directly relevant to practitioners building multi-timescale RL systems, warning against seemingly intuitive design choices (attention-based routing, uncertainty weighting).

## Limitations

- **Single-environment evaluation**: All experiments are conducted on LunarLander-v2. The generalizability to complex continuous control (MuJoCo), visual domains (Atari), or real-world robotics tasks is untested.
- **No comparison to prior multi-timescale methods**: The paper does not benchmark against existing multi-head or multi-timescale Critic architectures from the literature, making it hard to assess relative improvement.
- **Missing sensitivity analysis**: The choice of gamma set {0.5, 0.9, 0.99, 0.999} and the target gamma=0.999 are not ablated. It is unclear how sensitive results are to these specific choices.
- **Informal statistical claims**: Statistical significance is claimed based on visual inspection of learning curves without formal tests (e.g., paired t-tests, bootstrap confidence intervals).
- **Scalability unknown**: The 4-head Critic adds computational overhead. The paper does not report training time or sample complexity comparisons.
