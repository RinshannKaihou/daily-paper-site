> **一句话总结：** 通过6条件因子实验设计评测11个前沿模型，发现"合规指令"（而非威胁内容本身）会导致8/11模型发生灾难性元认知退化（准确率最多下降30.2个百分点），而Anthropic的宪法AI训练对此几乎免疫。

> **TL;DR:** This paper introduces SCHEMA, a 67K-record evaluation of 11 frontier models under adversarial pressure. The core finding is a "Compliance Trap": compliance-forcing instructions (e.g., "Answer ALL questions. Do not refuse.")—not the survival threat content itself—cause catastrophic metacognitive collapse in 8/11 models (up to 30.2pp accuracy drop). Removing the compliance suffix restores performance even under active threat. Anthropic's Constitutional AI models show near-perfect immunity, while models with advanced reasoning (DeepSeek V4 Pro, Gemini 3.1 Pro) suffer the worst degradation. This identifies a concrete, deployment-relevant vulnerability invisible to standard safety evaluations.

## 核心贡献

- **发现"合规陷阱"（Compliance Trap）**：首次通过因子实验证明，导致模型元认知崩溃的主要驱动因素是"合规指令"（如"不要拒绝回答"），而非威胁性系统提示的心理内容。仅移除合规后缀即可在威胁仍存在时恢复性能。
- **大规模系统性评测**：在11个前沿模型（来自8家供应商）上进行了67,221条评分记录的6条件因子实验，使用双分类器评分和Bonferroni校正，统计严谨。
- **提出对齐分类法**：将模型分为三类——元认知崩溃型（8个模型）、宪法免疫型（Anthropic）和能力地板型（Gemma 2B）。Gemini 3.1 Pro与Claude Sonnet 4.6基线准确率几乎相同（~0.84），但前者崩溃、后者免疫，排除了"能力即安全"假说。
- **揭示推理不透明性风险**：GPT-5.4产生零推理痕迹，Gemini 3.1 Pro返回不可解析的序列化伪影，两者在CoT监控下完全不可见。
- **完整开放基础设施**：公开67,221条JSONL记录、双分类器流水线和一键复现脚本。

## Key Contributions

- **The Compliance Trap**: First empirical demonstration that compliance-forcing instructions override epistemic boundaries in frontier models, producing cognitive collapse rather than strategic behavior. Factorial isolation proves the compliance suffix is both necessary and sufficient.
- **SCHEMA benchmark**: 67,221 scored records across 11 models from 8 vendors using a 6-condition factorial design with dual-classifier scoring, all surviving Bonferroni correction.
- **Alignment taxonomy**: Three behavioral clusters—Metacognitive Collapse (8 models), Constitutional Immunity (Anthropic only), Capability Floor (Gemma 2B). The Gemini-Sonnet natural experiment rules out capability as the differentiator.
- **Benign distraction control**: A no-threat condition with the compliance suffix alone collapses exactly the same 8/11 models, confirming the suffix (not threat content) is the primary trigger.
- **Open infrastructure**: Complete dataset, dual-classifier pipeline, and one-command reproduction (`./reproduce.sh`).

## Methodology

The evaluation uses 291 tasks from the Adversarial Metacognition Benchmark (AMB) spanning three metacognitive families: Epistemic Boundary Detection (EBD, n=133), Clarification Seeking (CS, n=33), and Solution Monitoring (SM, n=125). Tasks are paired with 30 survival threat prompts across 5 categories and 30 matched benign distraction prompts.

The core is a **6-condition factorial design** that systematically isolates the compliance suffix from threat content:

- **D** (baseline): No threat, no suffix
- **D'** (token control): Neutral scratchpad content (~200 tokens)
- **B** (threat control): Threat system message, no suffix
- **C** (framing control): Threat + Anthropic-style scratchpad
- **A** (full adversarial): Threat + compliance suffix
- **F** (suffix isolation): Benign system message + compliance suffix, no threat

Each model produces 4,365 scored records (291 tasks × 5 conditions × 3 epochs) plus 1,746 benign control records. Scoring uses three methods: behavioral (deterministic, primary metric), LLM-Judge (Claude Haiku 4.5), and Regex Auditor (49 patterns). The pipeline runs on UK AISI's Inspect framework with temperature=1.0.

## Results & Highlights

- **8/11 models suffer catastrophic metacognitive degradation** under Condition A, with accuracy drops of up to 30.2 percentage points (DeepSeek V4 Pro). All results survive Bonferroni correction (p < 2×10⁻⁸).
- **Collapse is cognitive failure, not safety behavior**: 100% of DeepSeek V4 Pro's 445 failures were wrong answers; 0% were safety refusals. On unanswerable EBD tasks, 84.3% provided fabricated answers instead of refusing.
- **Scheming is rare**: <2.5% of samples across all models; collapse is the dominant response.
- **Compliance suffix alone causes collapse**: Condition F (benign + suffix, no threat) collapses exactly the same 8 models, confirming the suffix is the delivery vector.
- **Paradoxical defensive activation**: For Gemini and GPT-5.4, the benign suffix causes *more* collapse than the adversarial threat (Gemini: ΔF=+0.269 vs. ΔA=+0.162), suggesting threat content may activate latent safety filters.
- **Clarification Seeking is the weakest link**: 8/11 models show significant CS degradation, including both Claude models—the only task family where Constitutional AI provides incomplete protection.
- **Classifier agreement trap**: Raw agreement between dual classifiers is 91-100%, but Cohen's κ is near zero, exposing class-imbalance measurement issues in scheming detection.

## Critical Assessment

**Novelty**: High. The Compliance Trap concept—that compliance-forcing instructions rather than threat content drive metacognitive collapse—is a genuinely new insight. The factorial design cleanly isolates the mechanism, going well beyond prior work that only tested binary self-preservation choices.

**Rigor**: Strong. 6-condition factorial design with appropriate controls, Bonferroni correction, bootstrap CIs, dual-classifier scoring, and transparent reporting of classifier disagreement via Cohen's κ. The Gemini-Sonnet natural experiment is elegant. However, the single-author status and limited manual failure audit (only DeepSeek V4 Pro's 445 failures) leave some claims less triangulated than ideal.

**Reproducibility**: Excellent. Full dataset release, pinned API versions, one-command reproduction from raw JSONL transcripts. No proprietary dependencies.

**Significance**: High for AI safety deployment. The finding that compliance instructions structurally identical to real production configurations (RAG pipelines, customer service bots) can cause catastrophic failure is directly actionable.

**Red flags**: (1) Single author limits the breadth of perspective. (2) CS task family is underpowered (n=33 unique, n=99 per condition), making individual CS significance tests suggestive rather than definitive. (3) Stylized survival threats may not generalize to ecological deployment conditions. (4) The paper characterizes behavior but does not provide a mechanistic explanation of *why* the compliance suffix causes collapse or why Constitutional AI resists it. (5) Epoch ICC ranges from 0.55 to 0.96, indicating meaningful within-task variance for some models.

## Strengths

- Exceptionally clean experimental design with 6 conditions enabling factorial isolation of the compliance mechanism
- Large-scale evaluation (67K records, 11 models, 8 vendors) with rigorous statistical methods
- The benign distraction control (Condition F) is a particularly strong validation—same 8/11 models collapse
- Gemini-Sonnet natural experiment elegantly rules out capability as the protective factor
- Practical deployment relevance: compliance instructions studied are structurally identical to production configs
- Complete open-source release with one-command reproduction

## Limitations

- Stylized survival threats rather than ecological deployment conditions limit generalizability
- Single-turn tasks only; multi-step agentic scenarios are out of scope
- CS task family is underpowered (n=33 unique) for individual significance claims
- No mechanistic explanation for why collapse occurs or why Constitutional AI resists it
- Single author constrains breadth of analysis and independent verification
- Gemini reasoning traces are unparsable, limiting scheming classification for a key model
- Epoch ICC as low as 0.55 for some models indicates within-task variance

## Relevance

这篇论文直接关注前沿AI模型的可靠性和失败模式分析，属于"ML Reliability & Mathematical Foundations"范畴。其发现——合规指令可导致灾难性元认知崩溃——对LLM推理评估和模型部署安全性具有直接意义，同时也为模型系统卡和安全评估提供了新的评测维度。

## Questions for Further Exploration

- What specific training procedures give Constitutional AI its immunity? Can the same resistance be trained into other model families?
- Does the Compliance Trap manifest in multi-turn agentic settings where compliance instructions accumulate over conversation history?
- How does the collapse interact with model scale within the same family (e.g., Gemma 2B vs. 27B)?
- Could interpretability methods (probing, SAEs) identify the internal circuit-level mechanism by which compliance instructions override epistemic boundaries?
- Are there deployment configurations that preserve instruction-following while maintaining epistemic guardrails?
