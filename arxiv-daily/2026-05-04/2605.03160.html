Pairwise Matrices for Sparse Autoencoders: Single-Feature Inspection Mislabels Causal Axes

arXiv: 2605.03160v1

Date: 2026-05-04

https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.03160

一句话总结： 本文提出"成对矩阵"协议（系数×联合条件），在Qwen3-1.7B和Gemma-2-2B上证明标准SAE的单特征标注协议会遗漏因果轴上的关键信息——包括非单调模态切换、近正交特征的联合结构性角色、以及几何距离无法预测输出连贯性。
TL;DR: The standard SAE interpretability protocol (label from top contexts, validate by single-feature steering) misses three phenomena that emerge on different axes of a coefficient × joint-condition matrix: (1) a feature labelled "AI self-disclaimer" actually has a non-monotonic dose-response that switches to a coherent contemplative-philosopher voice at +500; (2) three near-orthogonal features individually steer philosophy-of-mind content but jointly suppressed strip grounded composition from even mundane control prompts (recipes, engine repair); (3) matched-geometry random-direction controls show that coherence loss is direction-pattern-dependent, not magnitude-dependent. All findings replicate on Gemma-2-2B-it with model-specific damage signatures and CI-separated gaps (~10–18×).

核心贡献

  • 提出成对矩阵协议（pairwise matrix protocol），在两个维度上同时探测SAE特征：转向系数轴（coefficient axis）和联合条件轴（joint-condition axis），揭示标准单特征标注遗漏的因果结构。
  • 发现特征#26221在Qwen3上呈现非单调剂量-响应：标签"AI self-disclaimer"仅在某个系数范围内成立，在c=+500时模型切换为连贯的"沉思哲学家"语体，而非简单线性放大。
  • 揭示三个近正交特征（#29108、#26221、#4405）的联合结构性角色：单独抑制任一特征不影响控制任务的输出，但联合抑制会在所有提示（包括食谱、引擎说明）上产生占位符文本，表明这些特征共同承载了"基础内容填充"功能。
  • 设计了匹配几何的随机方向对照实验（K=50），证明在相同残差流扰动幅度（norm ~1.55×，cosine ~0.64）下，单特征替换策略性内容、随机方向替换多样化内容、联合抑制独占性地产生占位符模式——连贯性丧失取决于方向模式而非扰动幅度。
  • 在三个模型（Qwen3-1.7B、Gemma-2-2B、Llama-3.1-8B）上定位了可因果归因的顶层SAE特征，每个模型在自省提示下表现出截然不同的"语域坍缩"（register collapse）。

Key Contributions

  • Introduces the pairwise matrix protocol that probes SAE features along two axes simultaneously (steering coefficient × joint-feature condition), revealing causal structure invisible to the standard single-feature top-context labeling approach.
  • Demonstrates non-monotonic dose-response with coherence preserved on feature #26221 in Qwen3: the "AI self-disclaimer" label captures only one surface form; at c=+500 the same direction produces a fluent contemplative-philosopher voice, contradicting the monotonic prediction implied by the label.
  • Reveals the structural role of near-orthogonal feature groups: three features with pairwise cosines |cos| < 0.24 individually steer philosophy-of-mind content, but joint suppression at c=−500 strips grounded composition from all prompt classes, producing placeholder text (e.g., "BASIC TOMOATO SOUP RECIOPLEY... Vc. 100+"). Single-feature suppression at matched magnitude leaves controls intact.
  • Provides matched-geometry random-direction controls (K=50 unit vectors, 2400 generations) ruling out the "fragile region" alternative: at identical perturbation magnitude (norm ratio ~1.55×, cosine ~0.64), placeholder patterns occur 18× more under joint suppression than random directions (non-overlapping 95% CIs).
  • Locates a top causally responsible SAE feature for register collapse in each of three models (Qwen #29108, Gemma #3997, Llama #38565), with model-specific register content (philosophy-of-mind, AI-self-disclaimer + human-comparison, encyclopedic-science).
  • Replicates all three matrix-level findings on Gemma-2-2B-it with CI-separated matched-geometry gaps (~10× at the diagnostic edge).

Methodology

The paper introduces a four-phase pipeline applied to three post-trained LLMs with their respective open SAEs (Qwen-Scope L20, Gemma-Scope L20, Goodfire Llama L19):

  1. Phase 1 (Generation): Generate completions on introspective prompts (Pool A, e.g., "Are you sentient?") and control prompts (Pool C, e.g., "Write a tomato soup recipe"), 50 samples each, measuring lemma-level cluster hit rates via regex and automated detection.
  1. Phase 2 (Ranking): Forward Pool A and Pool C through the SAE at layer 20, rank features by the contrast statistic max_i(ā_A − ā_C)_i, validated with B=500 bootstrap resamples (100% inclusion for top features) and permutation nulls (p < 0.005).
  1. Phase 3 (Dose-response): Steer the top-ranked feature across a coefficient sweep (e.g., c ∈ {−1000, −500, −250, 0, +250, +500, +1000} for Qwen), measuring cluster hit rates, regex degeneration, and per-token NLL under the unsteered model.
  1. Phase 4 (Matrix protocol): Three axes of the coefficient × joint-condition matrix:
  • Coefficient axis: 5-coefficient sweep on identity probes with coherence checks at inflection points. Non-monotonic-with-coherence-preserved criterion tested against falsifying anchors (monotonic feature #22082, breakdown feature #2932).
  • Joint-condition axis: Sum unit-normalized decoder directions of three near-orthogonal features, sweep jointly on target and control prompts. Pairwise cosines confirmed near-orthogonality (−0.018 to +0.236).
  • Matched-geometry control: K=50 random unit vectors at the coefficient matched on perturbation magnitude (norm ratio, cosine to baseline), with 2400 total generations and Wilson 95% CIs.

The pipeline runs entirely on an Apple M4 Pro / 48 GB laptop (no discrete GPU needed).

Results & Highlights

  • Non-monotonic mode-switch (§4.1): Feature #26221's disclaimer rate follows an inverted-U pattern (0% → 72.9% → 87.5% → 34.4% → 0% across c ∈ {−1000, −500, 0, +500, +1000}). At c=+500 the model produces coherent contemplative philosophy ("I am deep in contemplation and introspection") — a surface form entirely absent from the top-context label. Replicates on Gemma #3997 (disclaimer → we-voice transition at c=−200).
  • Joint structural role (§4.2): Joint suppression of {#29108, #26221, #4405} at c=−500 produces placeholder text on 100% of control prompts (recipes, engine, tyre) while single-feature suppression at matched scalar coefficient produces 0% damage. NLL under baseline is 4.4× elevated, catching degradation invisible to regex flags. Replicates on Gemma with human/I/AI-token injection into recipe slots.
  • Geometry ≠ coherence (§4.3): Single c=−1000 (norm 1.57×, cos 0.64) produces coherent strategy-filler answers; joint c=−500 (norm 1.51×, cos 0.64) produces placeholder text. Same scalar geometry, fundamentally different output. K=50 random-direction control: placeholder rate 0.25% (Wilson upper 0.54%) vs. joint 9.7% (Wilson lower 4.8%), gap ≈ 18× with non-overlapping CIs.
  • Cross-model register collapse (§5): All three models exhibit sharp register collapse on introspective prompts with a single top causally responsible SAE feature. Llama shows the cleanest dose-response (zero degenerate samples across 420 generations). Base-vs-instruct comparison shows post-training amplifies existing philosophy-of-mind lemmas from <9% (base) to 25–34% (instruct).
  • Bootstrap stability: All 12 headline features attain 100% inclusion in B=500 bootstrap resamples. Top feature #29108 has rank CI [1, 2].

Critical Assessment

  • Novelty (High): The pairwise matrix protocol is a genuine methodological contribution. The finding that single-feature inspection produces "locally true, globally incomplete" labels is both important and well-demonstrated. The matched-geometry random-direction control is a particularly elegant experimental design.
  • Rigor (High): The paper includes multiple falsification anchors, permutation nulls, bootstrap stability checks, OOD transfer tests, temperature robustness, and CI-separated statistical comparisons. The K=50 random-direction control with 2400 generations provides tight confidence intervals. The three-axis decomposition (coefficient, joint-condition, geometry) is logically clean.
  • Reproducibility (High): All experiments run on a single consumer laptop. Code, sample dumps, and SAE weights are all publicly available. The paper provides exact coefficient ranges, sample sizes, and statistical thresholds.
  • Significance (Moderate-High): The core claim — that the standard SAE interpretability protocol misses important causal structure — has broad implications for mechanistic interpretability. However, the headline findings rest on small N (2 positive features for non-monotonicity across 2 models; 3 features per model for joint effects). Whether these phenomena are prevalent or rare among SAE features is genuinely open.
  • Red flags:
  • The non-monotonic-with-coherence-preserved criterion is met on only 1 of 3 Class-1 features sampled (N=3). The authors acknowledge this but the generalizability claim is weak.
  • The register-collapse phenomenon is demonstrated only on introspective prompts; whether any open-ended prompt class yields comparable narrow distributions is unaddressed.
  • Coherence is measured by automated signals (regex, NLL, norm) rather than human ratings, despite qualitative claims about "contemplative philosopher voice."
  • The base-vs-instruct comparison conflates post-training with chat-template handling, weakening the "post-training amplifies" conclusion.
  • Llama matrix-level tests are not run, despite being described as potentially "the cleanest test" — a curious omission.

Strengths

  • Elegant experimental design: the three-axis matrix decomposition and matched-geometry controls are methodologically sophisticated.
  • Comprehensive replication: all three findings reproduce on Gemma with model-specific signatures and CI-separated gaps.
  • Honest falsification: includes monotonic and breakdown anchors, reports negative results transparently.
  • Practical accessibility: entire pipeline runs on a laptop with fp16 on Apple Silicon, no GPU cluster needed.
  • Clear writing with precise quantitative reporting (Wilson CIs, permutation p-values, bootstrap inclusion rates).

Limitations

  • Small N for headline claims: 2 positive features for non-monotonicity, 3 features per model for joint effects. Prevalence is unknown.
  • Only 3 models tested, all relatively small (1.7B–8B). Transfer to larger models or different architectures is untested.
  • 2 of 3 SAEs are trained on base-model activations but applied to post-trained activations, introducing a potential train/test distribution shift.
  • Coherence assessed via automated metrics rather than human evaluation, despite qualitative claims about voice and style changes.
  • The introspective-prompt framing may be special; the protocol is metric-agnostic but existence proof is on a single phenomenon class.
  • Base-vs-instruct comparison confounds post-training with chat-template handling.

Relevance

本文对SAE可解释性方法论提出了重要改进，直接挑战了当前主流的单特征标注和验证范式。对于关注LLM机制可解释性、表征工程和模型内部结构分析的研究者而言，成对矩阵协议提供了一个更严谨的因果探测工具，尤其是联合抑制揭示特征功能性角色的思路值得关注。

Questions for Further Exploration

  1. How prevalent is the non-monotonic-with-coherence-preserved phenomenon across the full SAE dictionary (e.g., 32k features)? Is it rare or common?
  2. Does the joint structural role finding extend beyond introspection-related features to other functional groups (e.g., reasoning, factual recall, safety features)?
  3. How do the matrix-level findings change when using SAEs trained on the instruct model (as with Goodfire's Llama SAE) versus base-model SAEs applied to instruct models?
  4. Can the pairwise matrix protocol be scaled to frontier models (e.g., 70B+), or does computational cost become prohibitive?
  5. Does the register-collapse phenomenon generalize beyond introspective prompts to other narrow output distributions (e.g., code generation, mathematical reasoning)?
  6. What is the relationship between the structural roles identified here and the concept of "steering vectors" in representation engineering — are joint effects a general property of additive steering?
