> **一句话总结：** 本文提出 AHOIS，一个将"苏格拉底式产婆术"嵌入闭环实验的多智能体 AI 科学家系统——由专门的物理批判智能体（Duzhi）通过澄清、约束检查、因果追问与反例挑战来质询候选假说，并在真实多模光纤光学平台上自主提出了"随机干涉编码"假说、发现自适应稀疏采样策略、诊断多种失败模式并将已发表论文转化为可执行实验流程，迈向真正"认知自主"的科学发现。

> **TL;DR:** AHOIS is a multi-agent AI scientist that embeds Socratic midwifery into closed-loop experimentation on a real multimode-fibre optical platform. A dedicated physics-critic agent interrogates hypotheses through clarification, constraint checking, causal probing and counterexample generation, turning brittle reasoning into testable, revisable mechanistic models; the system autonomously discovered a backscattered random-interference encoding, adaptive sparse scanning, modality-specific failure diagnosis and a paper-to-platform reproduction, advancing from procedural to epistemic autonomy.

## 核心贡献

- **提出"苏格拉底产婆术"作为认知自主的计算原则**：区别于多数现有系统只在人类预先定义的目标/动作空间内做流程自动化，AHOIS 引入了一个被严格约束为"只质询、不直接给答案"的物理批判智能体（Duzhi），通过澄清概念、物理约束检查、因果链追问和反例挑战，把隐含且脆弱的推理转化为显式、可证伪、可修正的机制模型。
- **五智能体分工 + 共享科学状态架构**：Boxue（策略/假说生成）、Gewu（硬件抽象与执行）、Mingde（系统完整性监测）、Qiushi（建模与定量分析）、Duzhi（苏格拉底批判）。核心不是分工本身，而是假说生成智能体与苏格拉底批判智能体之间的"认知闭环"。
- **在真实高维物理平台上完成自主科学发现**：在真实多模光纤光学系统上（而非纯仿真），系统在未给定编码方案、分类器或散斑模型的前提下，自主提出并实验验证了"随机干涉编码"假说（16×16 测量，有效秩 56.9，MNIST 76.97%、Fashion-MNIST 83.17% 分类准确率）。
- **自主发现任务自适应稀疏采样、失败模式诊断与论文复现**：系统提出 coarse-to-fine 自适应稀疏扫描（在保持视频级帧率下完成任务识别）、区分编码不稳定/荧光污染/探测器噪声三种失败模式并选择模态特异性处理工具（SCNet / MFNet）、并将一篇已发表的多模光纤成像协议迁移到非原始配置完成 pilot 级复现。
- **受控消融证明苏格拉底式提问的价值**：四类提问（澄清 0.42、约束检查 0.58、因果追问 0.83、反例挑战 0.74 的平均增益）显著提升了物理一致性、假说完备性、不确定度校准与下游实验方案有效性，且其贡献来自提问的"形式"而非"数量"。

## Key Contributions

- **Introduces Socratic midwifery as an operational mechanism for epistemic autonomy.** Whereas most agentic science systems operate within a human-supplied scientific frame (fixed objectives, action spaces, success criteria), AHOIS uses a constrained physics-critic agent (Duzhi) that only interrogates — never supplies conclusions — forcing the hypothesis-generating agent to make its assumptions, causal links and failure conditions explicit and falsifiable.
- **A five-agent architecture coupled through a shared scientific state.** Boxue (strategy/hypothesis), Gewu (hardware abstraction), Mingde (integrity monitoring), Qiushi (modelling/analysis) and Duzhi (Socratic critic) exchange hypotheses, executable plans, integrity checks and analytical results rather than running as a fixed pipeline.
- **Closed-loop discovery on real high-dimensional hardware.** On a physical multimode-fibre platform with no pre-supplied encoding scheme, classifier or speckle model, the system autonomously proposed and validated a random-interference backscattered encoding (16×16 measurements, effective rank 56.9, 76.97% MNIST / 83.17% Fashion-MNIST).
- **Multiple emergent capabilities.** Task-adaptive coarse-to-fine sparse scanning (video-rate recognition vs. >2-orders-of-magnitude slower full scanning), modality-specific failure diagnosis and tool selection (SCNet for speckle, MFNet for fluorescence noise), and translation of a published MMF imaging protocol onto a non-original configuration with agent-designed reconstruction models.
- **Ablations isolate the contribution of Socratic structure.** Across Boxue-alone, generic reviewer, non-Socratic critic and full Socratic critic, the Socratic configuration yields the best normalized physics consistency, plan validity, planning efficiency and downstream task performance, with causal probing and counterexample challenges delivering the largest gains.

## Methodology

**Architecture.** AHOIS separates five roles cooperating through a shared scientific state that records, at each step, the current hypothesis, physical assumptions, proposed action, expected observation, relevant uncertainty and evidence returned by the instrument. The epistemic loop is between the hypothesis generator (Boxue) and the Socratic critic (Duzhi). Candidate explanations are iteratively converted into structured scientific states (mechanism, assumptions, competing interpretations, predicted outcomes, discriminating experiments, uncertainty estimates, rejection conditions), translated by Gewu into executable hardware operations (DMD holograms, camera triggering, metadata), audited by Mingde (saturation, dark-frame validity, frame completeness, speckle repeatability), and analysed by Qiushi (loaders, reconstruction, training, quantitative metrics).

**Socratic interrogation (four levels).** (1) Clarification — define physical meaning of terms, distinguish measured vs inferred vs assumed quantities. (2) Constraint checking — compatibility with optical principles and hardware limits (energy transfer, coherence, multimode propagation, detector response). (3) Causal probing — connect the full chain from optical modulation → propagation → object interaction → collection → measurement. (4) Counterexample — test whether the explanation holds under changed object, modality, sampling or stability conditions.

**Physical testbed.** A real multimode-fibre platform: a laser modulated by a DMD (1024×768, active 624×624) via binary Lee holograms, a 100 µm / 0.22 NA step-index illumination MMF calibrated via phase-stepped interferometry (~7,500 input patterns; ~173,000 output positions), an adjacent collection MMF, and both far-field coherent and fluorescence modalities sharing hardware but governed by different physics. A human scientist supervises only at predefined safety/quality decision points (veto unsafe actions, correct hardware-incompatible plans, annotate uncertain interpretations); all states and outcomes are logged.

**Experiments.** (a) Backscattered random-interference encoding — a four-focus pattern raster-scanned across the object, each position yielding one encoded intensity, assembled into a 16×16 matrix; validated by correlation analysis, SVD/effective-rank and intensity distribution; a compact classifier trained directly on the matrices (no reconstructed image). (b) Adaptive sparse scanning — coarse-to-fine, evidence-directed measurement allocation compared across five density settings on far-field, fluorescence and endoscopy scenes. (c) Failure diagnosis — modality-specific degradation mapped to SCNet (speckle) vs MFNet (fluorescence noise). (d) Paper-to-platform reproduction — a published holographic/polarization MMF imaging protocol adapted to the local setup at pilot scale, with Qiushi implementing a U-Net baseline then a residual-attention improvement (compound loss with SSIM; evaluated by SSIM, PSNR, pixel correlation). (e) Socratic ablations — four configurations compared on physics consistency, hypothesis completeness, uncertainty calibration, plan validity, invalid-plan rate and steps-to-valid-plan; questioning utility quantified per intervention class.

## Results & Highlights

- **Random-interference encoding works.** The 16×16 encoder matrices were not mutually redundant (correlation analysis), spanned a multidimensional space (effective rank 56.9), used a healthy dynamic range, and supported direct classification of **76.97% (MNIST)** and **83.17% (Fashion-MNIST)** — reframing backscattered speckle from a nuisance into a computationally accessible physical encoding.
- **Adaptive sparse scanning recovers video-rate recognition.** At background 1/12 + ROI 1/8 density, both ROI and content recognition succeeded while retaining **48.50 fps**, versus **0.47 fps** for full scanning (>100× slower) — an evidence-directed allocation rather than fixed subsampling.
- **Modality-specific diagnosis is non-generic.** Speckle-dominated far-field images triggered SCNet; noise-dominated fluorescence images triggered MFNet — chosen from measured characteristics and inferred physical origin, not prescribed.
- **Paper-to-platform reproduction succeeded at pilot scale.** A 38-step trajectory (paper parsing → hardware abstraction → pilot acquisition → baseline model → improved residual-attention model → tuning → reporting) shows responsibility shifting across agents; Qiushi autonomously designed and improved the reconstruction network. The authors deliberately drew the line at reasoning-intensive steps, leaving repetitive large-scale acquisition to researchers using the agent-established procedure.
- **Socratic structure measurably helps.** Over seven questioning rounds, physics consistency rose from ~2 to >4.5 on a 0–5 scale; per-class mean utility was causal probing 0.83 > counterexample 0.74 > constraint check 0.58 > clarification 0.42. The full Socratic configuration beat Boxue-alone, generic-reviewer and non-Socratic-critic on normalized physics consistency, plan validity, planning efficiency and task performance, with the lowest invalid-plan rate and fewest steps to a valid plan.
- **Token/accountability audit provided.** Input context dominated token load; the trajectory is fully logged and replayable, supporting the modular-inspectability claim.

## Critical Assessment

**Novelty — High.** The conceptual move from *procedural* autonomy (automating a human-fixed workflow) to *epistemic* autonomy (constructing/challenging/reviving physical explanations) via a constrained Socratic critic is a genuinely distinct framing. The four-level interrogation protocol and the strict "critic questions, never answers" constraint (shown to differ from generic review and non-Socratic correction) is the most defensible novelty. The multi-agent decomposition and the fibre-optic discoveries are individually less novel (random-speckle encoding and adaptive sampling exist in the optics literature), but their *autonomous, unsupervised discovery by an agent* is the differentiator.

**Rigor — Moderate-to-High.** Real-hardware experiments, controlled ablations across four critic configurations, multiple complementary statistics (correlation, effective rank, intensity distribution), and a fixed train/val/test partition with five-seed means for classification are solid. However: (1) the headline reasoning-quality metrics (physics consistency, hypothesis completeness, uncertainty calibration) are scored on a 0–5 rubric that is described but not shown to be inter-annotator reliable or model-agnostic — a risk of circular/self-flattering evaluation; (2) the paper-to-platform reproduction was only at *pilot* scale, with the bulk of acquisition done by researchers using the agent's procedure — a legitimate boundary but one that weakens the "fully autonomous" claim; (3) comparison baselines for the discovered encoding/scanning are largely the system's own alternatives rather than published specialist methods.

**Reproducibility — Moderate.** Hardware specifics (fibre specs, DMD, calibration counts, TM procedure) are given, and the authors promise LM config, tool permissions, prompts and trajectories in Supplementary Information (not in the main text). Reproduction requires the exact optical bench and TM calibration, which is a high barrier; software-only aspects would be easier. The self-defined rubrics and LLM-stochasticity mean reasoning-quality numbers may not reproduce exactly.

**Significance — High (conceptually) / Moderate (empirically).** If the Socratic-midwifery principle generalizes, it is a meaningful architectural contribution to AI-for-science and trustworthy agentic systems. Empirically it is demonstrated on a single (admittedly demanding) platform and a bounded conceptual domain; it does not claim novel physical laws.

**Red flags / honest concerns.**
- *Evaluation self-referentiality:* the same LLM family that generates hypotheses also underpins the critic and arguably the scoring; risk that Socratic gains reflect stylistic conformity rather than deeper physical correctness.
- *Cherry-picking risk:* representative dialogues and scenes (Lego Mario, "UCAS" text, bronchial bifurcation) are illustrative, not exhaustive; no systematic error analysis of *when the agent was wrong*.
- *Human-in-the-loop understated:* the supervisor plus researcher-run acquisition materially shape outcomes, yet the framing leans on "autonomous."
- *Generalization unproven:* claims of transferability to microscopes, photonic processors, synthesis platforms are aspirational, not tested.
- *Statistical hygiene:* sample sizes chosen for "representative coverage," not power; confidence intervals deferred to the SI.

## Strengths

- Strong and clearly articulated conceptual thesis (epistemic vs procedural autonomy; Socratic midwifery as a computational principle).
- Executed on **real** high-dimensional hardware with genuine ambiguity (encoding vs drift vs noise), not a simulator.
- The critic-only constraint is principled and the ablation cleanly separates *form* of criticism from *amount* of criticism.
- Modular, logged, replayable architecture enables inspection — valuable for trustworthy AI-for-science.
- Diverse emergent behaviors (encoding discovery, adaptive sampling, diagnosis, protocol transfer) from a single open-ended prompt.
- Honest about limitations (natural-language questioning, human supervision, no new physical laws).

## Limitations

- Socratic interrogation is implemented in natural language, introducing ambiguity in assumptions/constraints/falsification criteria; no formal/causal-graph grounding yet.
- Human supervision and researcher-performed large-scale acquisition remain integral — not yet fully autonomous end-to-end.
- Does not invent new physical laws; restricted to mechanistic explanation within a known conceptual domain.
- Evaluation of reasoning quality relies on rubric scores whose annotator reliability and model-independence are not established.
- Single-platform demonstration; transferability is argued, not demonstrated.
- Computational cost (token accounting) is reported but the economics of repeated closed-loop interrogation are not analyzed.

## Relevance

该论文对"自主科学发现/可信 AI for Science/多智能体闭环实验"方向高度相关：它给出了一个把哲学上的"苏格拉底式诘问"工程化为可计算批判机制的清晰范式，对构建在真实硬件上能自我纠错、显式陈述假设与证伪条件的科研智能体具有方法论参考价值，也呼应了多模光纤/复杂介质光学中"测量意义不可直接解读"这一类难题的自动化需求。

## Questions for Further Exploration

- Can the Socratic critic be grounded in formal representations (causal graphs, symbolic optical models, machine-verifiable protocols) instead of natural language, and would that change the measured gains?
- How robust are the reasoning-quality rubric scores to the underlying LLM, the scorer, and inter-annotator agreement — i.e., are the Socratic improvements evidence of deeper physical correctness or of stylistic conformity?
- How does AHOIS behave when its initial physical model is *wrong* at a deep level — does Socratic interrogation recover from a fundamentally incorrect paradigm, or only refine within one?
- What is the marginal cost/benefit of each additional questioning round beyond seven, and where does the system plateau or over-regularize?
- Can the framework's transferability claims be tested on a second, genuinely different platform (e.g., scattering imaging, photonic processor, autonomous synthesis) to validate the architectural principle?
- How should the boundary between "autonomous reasoning" and "human/exhaustive robotic labour" be quantified and benchmarked systematically?
- Could the discovered backscattered-interference encoding be benchmarked against published specialist speckle-encoding methods on identical data, rather than only against the system's own ablations?
