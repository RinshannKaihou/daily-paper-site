LLM Efficiency & Quantization

Section B · 9 reports

From ternary weights to FP4 sparsity and KV-cache compression — making frontier models fit the edge and the budget.

Must Read · Ternary PTQ

CAT-Q: Ternary Quantization Beating QAT BitNet

Shigeng Wang, Chao Li, Yangyuxuan Kang, Jiawei Fan, Anbang Yao
512 samples (~1M tokens) quantize 1.7B–235B LLMs to 1.58-bit ternary, beating BitNet trained on 100B tokens (~100,000× fewer) — 235B done in 60 GPU-hours.
FP4 × Sparsity

SharQ: Bridging Activation Sparsity and FP4

Haoqian Meng, Yilun Luo, Yafei Zhao, Wenyuan Liu, Huaqing Zheng, Xindian Ma, Peng Zhang
Training-free sparse-dense decomposition fuses N:M sparsity with FP4: 2.2-2.4× latency cut on RTX 5090, recovering 43-63% of the NVFP4 accuracy gap.
Diffusion LM

Nemotron-TwoTower Diffusion Language Modeling

Fitsum Reda, John Kamalu, Roger Waleffe, Mostofa Patwary, Mohammad Shoeybi, Bryan Catanzaro
NVIDIA's frozen-AR-context tower plus a trainable diffusion denoiser retains 98.7% quality at 2.42× throughput on a 30B MoE.
KV Compression

InfoKV: Information-Aware KV Cache Compression

Jushi Kai, Zhuiri Xiao, Alexandra Birch, Zhouhan Lin
A forward-looking, entropy-based view of KV compression: high-entropy tokens dominate long-range future impact in long reasoning.
Linear Attention

Erase-then-Delta Attention

Xiao Li, Chengruidong Zhang, et al.
Decoupling erase vs write addresses in delta-rule linear attention tops dense 2.5B and MoE 25B baselines.
Architecture

CascadeFormer & Gradient Fan-in Asymmetry

Huzama Ahmad, Cao Viet Hai Nam, Se-Young Yun
Gradient fan-in asymmetry explains deep-layer diminishing returns; a width-tapered design trims 8.6% latency, adds 9.4% throughput.
Speculative Decoding

HyperDFlash: MHC-Aligned Speculative Decoding

Luxi Lin, Shuang Peng, et al.
Three MHC-aligned designs boost block speculative decoding for DeepSeek-V4: draft acceptance 2.93→3.69, speedup 2.25×→2.80×.

Pearse Jim, Steven Kolawole, Opegbemi M. Busoye, Glory Bagai, Virginia Smith
Aggressive quantization breaks global reasoning in recursive reasoners (local survives, global collapses); fixed via per-channel INT4.

First PRISMA survey dedicated to FL quantization, with an FL-centric taxonomy and six system-level dimensions.
