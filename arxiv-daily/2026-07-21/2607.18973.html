Why this matters

Solves how to do validation-gated skill evolution on open dialogue — where no stable offline verification signal exists — yielding inspectable, reproducible LLM agent skills.

Critical edge

Empirically thin: one proprietary dataset, no learned baselines (only random/constant), no precision/recall/F1 or CIs, and no downstream answer-skill A/B test proving better answers.

Posture Formulation + industrial case study Offline stage only Reproducibility: poor Builds on SkillOpt [Yang et al., 2026, arXiv:2605.23904]; relates to USS, SPUR, See & Manning (2021).

Methodology / five layers on SkillOpt

Recover a verifiable objective by holding the logged answer fixed and predicting its observed outcome — not scoring a counterfactual rewrite.

  1. Moving-target formulation. A log x=(C,H,Q1,A,Q2) cannot unbiasedly score arbitrary answer revisions, because p(Y|X,A) ≠ p(Y|X,A′) — editing A invalidates the logged reaction Q2.
  2. Verifiable surrogate. Optimize a feedback skill S_F that predicts the observed label Y; held-out score J(S_F′) is measurable without changing the interaction — reproducible and rejectable.
  3. Validation-gated evolution. The optimizer receives high-value failures (especially confident false positives), proposes a bounded text edit; the candidate is accepted only if it beats held-out accuracy, else rejected. A lexicographic variant trades accuracy for asymmetric business utility.
  4. Data construction. Feedback signals map to binary resolved/unresolved; silent/ambiguous turns are dropped (noise ↔ selection bias). Quality filters + ~1:1 balanced split prevent majority-class masking.
  5. Feedback–generation duality. Learned prediction rules (tool-success ≠ adoption; weak ack ≠ acceptance; need an actionable anchor) double as diagnostics for the answer skill S_A.

Strengths +

  • Moving-target formulation is precise — a clean statement of why p(Y|X,A) ≠ p(Y|X,A′) defeats naive offline validation.
  • Holding the logged answer fixed and predicting its outcome is a genuinely clean way to recover a verifiable offline objective in open dialogue.
  • Unusual intellectual honesty — repeatedly separates observational accuracy from counterfactual validity; offline stage, not a replacement for human/online eval.
  • Operationally sensible learned rules (tool success ≠ adoption; actionable next step required) useful to practitioners.
  • Feedback–generation duality is a productive framing for automating rationale-to-guidance compilation.

Limitations –

  • Counterfactual distribution shift — optimizing S_A may generate OOD responses that exploit S_F; it's a validated observational model, not satisfaction proof for arbitrary A′.
  • Silent users excluded — labels capture explicit/behavioral resolution, not total satisfaction (selection bias).
  • Proprietary data + cleaning — external reproduction impossible; no public benchmark (e.g., USS) evaluated.
  • Binary feedback collapses graded satisfaction; users can accept while partly dissatisfied or reject for unrelated reasons.
  • No downstream answer-skill experiment — no human eval or online A/B test; the central practical claim (better answers) is unproven.
  • No learned baselines, no per-class metrics — only random/constant compared; precision, recall, F1, and CIs absent.

Open questions for further exploration

How much of the >75% comes from skill evolution versus data cleaning + 1:1 balancing? An ablation on the same curated split would isolate the method's contribution. ablation · attribution
How does the evolved skill compare to learned baselines — LLM-as-judge zero-shot, SPUR-style rubrics, or a USS-trained classifier — on the same held-out data? baselines · head-to-head
Does distilling the feedback rules into answer-skill edits actually improve downstream answers in a human study or online A/B test? The paper leaves this open. end-to-end · causal
How robust is the predictor under counterfactual distribution shift when S_A emits OOD responses? Can the held-out gate detect or prevent exploitation? robustness · adversarial
How would the method fare on a public dataset (e.g., USS) with graded satisfaction labels and the silent/ambiguous-turn population included? reproducibility · generalization
