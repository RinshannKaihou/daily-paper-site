Relevance
This work sits at the intersection of mechanistic interpretability and multimodal emotion understanding. It is relevant to researchers working on: - Mechanistic interpretability of vision-language models: extends circuit-level analysis from object perception to abstract emotional processing - Cross-modal information flow: provides a concret
Methodology
Stage I -- Emotional Direction Extraction: - Constructs paired emotional (+) and neutral (-) image datasets using Google Nano Banana for neutralization, paired with neutral event descriptions from Gemini-3.0-Pro - Computes emotional directions as the difference in hidden states: s_{i,l} = h_{i,l}^+ - h_{i,l}^- - Filters pairs to retain only those where the model successfully exhibits the target emotion (threshold-based quality control) Stage II -- Hierarchical Circuit Discovery: 1. Critical Layer Identification: Injects emotional directions into each layer during inference and measures behavioral impact via hit rate change ratio C 2. Attention Head Localization: Uses backward activation patching with the Latent Restoration Metric R to identify upstream heads whose output

Critical Assessment

Rigorous mechanistic analysis pipeline
from counterfactual construction through hierarchical circuit discovery to targeted intervention, with each step grounded in causal reasoning
Point 2
The functional decoupling finding (sentiment-specific aggregation -> emotion-general execution) is a genuine structural insight with implications beyond this specific task
Point 3
The Latent Restoration Metric is a thoughtful methodological contribution that addresses the fundamental mismatch between discrete metrics and diffuse emotional expression
Point 4
VEEENA's design is directly motivated by the discovered mechanism rather than being a generic intervention, and achieves strong results with minimal parameter overhead (~3%)
Comprehensive evaluation
9 emotion datasets + 3 general ability benchmarks + ablation studies + comparison with prior methods
Point 6
The paper validates circuit specificity by showing that random intervention fails to reproduce improvements and that general capabilities are largely preserved
