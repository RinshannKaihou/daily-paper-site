Methodology
### Theoretical Foundation: HT-SR Theory The method is grounded in Heavy-Tailed Self-Regularization (HT-SR) theory, which analyzes the Empirical Spectral Density (ESD) of weight correlation matrices. The ESD characterizes how "well-trained" a layer is: layers with stronger heavy-tailed ESD (measured by PL_Alpha_Hill) are more mature/converged, while weaker heavy-tailedness indicates under-trained layers. ### Core Algorithm (Algorithm 1: LLR) The algorithm operates in four steps per update interval: 1.
