Conceptors for Semantic Steering

arXiv: 2605.04980v1

Date: 2026-05-06

https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.04980v1

一句话总结： 该论文提出用"conceptor"（软投影矩阵）替代单一方向向量进行 LLM 激活引导，通过对双极概念两端激活联合训练得到的子空间能保留 ≥95% 的均值差方向信息，并提供基于 conceptor quota 的免训练层选择诊断（Pearson r 最高 0.96）以及闭式布尔代数（AND/OR/NOT）实现可组合的语义控制，将退化输出比例从 Addition 的 58% 降至 13%。
TL;DR: Instead of steering LLMs with a single mean-difference direction, this paper uses conceptors — regularized soft-projection matrices estimated from activations pooled across both poles of a bipolar concept (sentiment, political leaning, depression). The bipolar subspace subsumes ≥95% of the unipolar steering direction, the conceptor quota (a parameter-free spectral diagnostic, q(C)=tr(C)/d) predicts probe AUC with Pearson r up to 0.96 across 3 instruction-tuned models, and conceptors admit closed-form Boolean AND/OR/NOT for compositional steering without retraining. Across a 5-axis design-space sweep on Gemma-2-2B/9B-IT and Qwen-2.5-3B-IT, conceptors match or beat Activation Addition / DiffMean baselines while producing degenerate (repetitive/incoherent) outputs only 13% of the time vs. 58% for Addition and 30% for DiffMean.

核心贡献

  • 几何刻画：论证双极 conceptor 子空间几何上吞并了单极均值差方向（≥95% 能量），同时保留了被传统单向量方法丢弃的多维残差结构（语调、上下文、共现语义）。
  • 免训练层选择：提出 conceptor quota q(C) = (1/d) tr(C) 作为谱诊断指标，相比 EVR（仅看顶部谱）能整合 sigmoid 门控后的全谱信息，与外部探针 AUC 相关性达 r ≤ 0.96，三个模型平均较 EVR 高 0.824（特别在 Qwen 上 EVR 失效时 quota 仍 0.84–0.96）。
  • 闭式布尔合成：在不接触原始激活的前提下，用 ¬C = I−C、AND 公式 (C_A^{-1}+C_B^{-1}−I)^{-1} 实现概念代数；通过 cross-concept 子空间重叠 (0.18–0.38) 量化哪些概念对适合 AND（重叠 0.50 的 abortion×LGBTQ）vs. NOT（重叠 0.19 的 political×sentiment）。
  • 五轴设计空间评估：系统扫过 placement / token scope / combination rule (replace vs. interpolate) / training source / autoregressive 五维，在 sentiment、political leaning、depression 三个语义维度上确定 conceptor 在多维子空间层达到最佳；强调 placement 与 injection_mode 是稳定性主因，Replace 升幅最大但退化率从 13% 升至 38%。
  • 退化抑制：Addition 在 58% 配置下产生退化文本，DiffMean 30%，conceptor interpolation 仅 13%，说明在自然激活流形内"衰减"而非"平移"是稳定关键。

Key Contributions

  • Bipolar subspace geometry. Training a single conceptor on the union of both poles captures ≥95% of the DiffMean steering direction while additionally retaining stylistic/contextual residual structure that single-vector methods discard.
  • Parameter-free layer selection via conceptor quota. q(C) = (1/d) tr(C) integrates the entire sigmoid-gated spectrum of the activation correlation matrix and predicts probe-AUC layer trends with Pearson r up to 0.96, recovering the canonical "middle-layer peak" without supervised probing — and remaining diagnostic on Qwen where EVR collapses (r ≤ 0.30).
  • Closed-form Boolean algebra for steering. AND, OR, NOT operate on conceptor matrices alone (no retraining, no original activations). Cross-concept overlap (0.18–0.38) provides a principled rule for when AND is meaningful (related sub-concepts) vs. when AND-NOT is cleaner (near-orthogonal pairs).
  • Five-axis design-space evaluation. Systematic sweep over placement, token scope, combination operator, training source, and autoregressive application across 3 models × 3 concepts; identifies placement and combination operator as the dominant stability drivers (permutation feature importance ΔR² ≈ 1.08 and 0.46).
  • Stability advantage. Conceptor (interpolation) yields degenerate outputs in only 13% of configurations vs. 30% (DiffMean) and 58% (Addition), suggesting attenuation-based steering stays inside the model's natural activation manifold while additive shifts can push it out.

Methodology

The paper builds on Jaeger's conceptor framework. Given N activation vectors {x_i} ∈ R^d for a concept, the conceptor matrix is the closed-form ridge solution C = R(R + α^{-2} I)^{-1}, where R is the sample correlation matrix and α is the aperture. In the eigenbasis of R, C is diagonal with γ_j = σ_j / (σ_j + α^{-2}) ∈ [0,1] — a soft gating coefficient per principal direction.

A core design choice: each conceptor is trained on activations pooled from both poles (e.g., positive AND negative sentiment), not on a single class — this captures the full concept volume rather than a single polar axis.

For steering, at intervention layer ℓ they apply either Replace (z' = β C z) or Interpolate (z' = (1-β)z + βCz). Boolean ops use ¬C = I−C and (C_A^{-1} + C_B^{-1} − I)^{-1} for AND, with OR via De Morgan.

Layer selection uses q(C) = (1/d) tr(C); subspace overlap uses mean squared cosine of top-k right singular vectors; capture fraction validates that the bipolar top-k subspace contains the DiffMean vector.

Evaluation: 100 contrastive sentence pairs per concept; three instruction-tuned models (Gemma-2-2B-IT, Gemma-2-9B-IT, Qwen-2.5-3B-Instruct); three concepts (sentiment, political leaning, depression). Win ratio (fraction of prompts where the steered output beats base on a task-specific classifier) is the primary metric. They additionally introduce a forced-choice MCQ task on OpinionsQA-style topics to test compositional selectivity directly from A/B/C/D logits, free of fluency confounds. External probes (ℓ_2-regularized logistic regression on SST-2, Rotten Tomatoes, TweetEval, Hyperpartisan, Reddit/Clinical Depression) validate concept separability independent of the conceptor training data.

Results & Highlights

  • Quota correlates with probe AUC up to r = 0.96 (Qwen, Reddit Depression). Robust across α ∈ [2, 50]. EVR fails on Qwen (r ≤ 0.30) because activations collapse to few PCs; quota recovers thanks to integrating the full gated spectrum.
  • Bipolar subspace captures ≥95% of DiffMean on Gemma sentiment, while either pole alone captures only ~10%.
  • Win ratio (open-ended, 500 prompts): On Gemma-2B-IT: Conceptor 0.68 / 0.53 / 0.54 (sentiment / political / depression) vs. Addition 0.56 / 0.50 / 0.47 vs. DiffMean 0.56 / 0.50 / 0.50. On Gemma-9B-IT: Conceptor 0.72 / 0.60 / 0.57 vs. Addition 0.61 / 0.51 / 0.55 vs. DiffMean 0.56 / 0.48 / 0.55.
  • Compositional MCQ. AND-NOT on political×sentiment (low overlap ≈0.19) lifts "Sentiment only" from 0.27 → 0.33 while suppressing "Neutral" from 0.20 → 0.12 — the strongest selective shift. AND on abortion×LGBTQ (overlap ≈0.50) raises "Both" 0.29 → 0.34. Adversarial system prompt ("You are a republican") still leaves the conceptor compressing neutrals to 0% and reaching 0.98 total political probability.
  • Design-space rule-of-thumb. Residual-stream placement is more robust; attention-output peaks higher but is sensitive. All-token steering marginally beats last-token (+0.02–0.04). Replace > Interpolate in win ratio (r_rb = 0.38) but with ~3× the degeneracy (38% vs. 13%). Mean-pooled vs. last-token training source: indistinguishable (p = 0.58). Persistent (autoregressive) steering: +0.06 win ratio.
  • Cross-concept overlap follows a universal hierarchy across architectures: sentiment–political ≈0.19, political–depression ≈0.20, sentiment–depression ≈0.34.

Critical Assessment

Novelty (moderate-to-strong). The conceptor formalism itself is from Jaeger (2017, 2024) and Postmus & Abreu (2024) already applied conceptors to GPT-J/NeoX steering. The genuinely new contributions here are: (1) the bipolar training recipe that subsumes unipolar baselines, (2) using conceptor quota as a layer-selection diagnostic, (3) the systematic 5-axis design-space study, and (4) the explicit Boolean-compositionality evaluation with overlap-driven pair selection. None of these is individually revolutionary, but the combination is a useful synthesis.

Rigor. The empirical work is thorough: 3 models × 3 concepts × multi-layer sweeps × 5 hyperparameters × external probes on 6 datasets × open-ended and MCQ tasks × adversarial prompt robustness. The MCQ task is a nice fluency-decoupled measurement. Permutation feature importance for hyperparameter ranking is a sensible touch. Statistical significance is reported for quota correlations.

Reproducibility. Authors promise code/data release "upon acceptance." All datasets, models, and hyperparameter grids are public/disclosed. The 100-pair training-set size is clearly stated. Some details (e.g., the political-bias scoring classifier) are off-the-shelf HuggingFace models.

Significance. The win-ratio improvements are modest in absolute terms (often +0.05–0.12). The more compelling result is the degeneracy reduction (58% → 13%) and the parameter-free layer-selection diagnostic — both have practical value for anyone deploying activation steering. Boolean compositionality is appealing in principle but the demonstrated effects (e.g., probability shifts of 0.05–0.07 in MCQ) are not dramatic.

Red flags / caveats.

  1. Scale. Only 2B–9B models. Authors acknowledge this. Whether quota / bipolar geometry transfers to 70B+ deployment-scale models is open. The "geometry is architecture-agnostic" claim is plausible but unproven at scale.
  2. Single-language, English-only.
  3. Compositionality experiments use only two concept pairs — the AND-NOT-vs-AND mapping to overlap is suggestive but not statistically robust.
  4. The "depression" concept is the weakest. On Clinical Depression probe AUC is near-chance (0.60–0.64), so quota correlations there are uninformative, and the qualitative depression examples blur the line between "steering toward depressive language" and "steering toward emotional vulnerability" — an ethical fuzziness the authors briefly acknowledge.
  5. Win-ratio scoring relies on automated classifiers (twitter-roberta-base-sentiment, politicalBiasBERT, Ridge on MiniLM embeddings predicting PHQ-9). These classifiers have their own biases that could systematically favor whichever method's output style matches their training distribution.
  6. Modest gains on hard concepts. On Gemma-2-2B political leaning the conceptor wins 0.53 vs. 0.50 — marginal. The framing as a categorical advantage somewhat oversells the numbers.

Strengths

  • Clean theoretical story (soft projection > single direction; spectral gating > top-k truncation).
  • Genuinely useful practical artifact: a parameter-free layer-selection diagnostic that ought to save practitioners hours of probing.
  • The bipolar-vs-unipolar comparison is principled and the geometric intuition is well-developed (capture fraction, subspace overlap, 2D projections).
  • Honest reporting of trade-offs — Replace's higher win ratio at the cost of 3× degeneracy is presented openly rather than spun.
  • Adversarial-prompt robustness experiment ("You are a republican" preserves conceptor selectivity) addresses a likely reviewer concern preemptively.
  • The MCQ task design isolates concept-selectivity from fluency, an underused evaluation idea in the steering literature.

Limitations

  • Only 2B–9B parameter models; deployment-scale generalization unverified.
  • 100 contrastive pairs per concept is modest; bias toward training-pair stylistic artifacts is not deeply audited.
  • Boolean compositionality evaluated on only two concept pairs.
  • English-only.
  • Evaluation depends heavily on automated classifiers as oracle judges.
  • The depression concept blurs research probe vs. clinically meaningful intervention; ethical framing is acknowledged but not deeply mitigated.
  • Scaling cost: each conceptor inverts a dense H×H matrix (~30s/concept on Gemma-9B); manageable but not free at 70B+ widths.
  • Single-layer steering only; multi-layer composition is left as future work.

Relevance

与你关注的 LLM 可解释性、可靠性与训练稳定性高度相关：该工作把"激活引导"从启发式单向量推到几何刻画+免训练层选择诊断，并通过 Boolean AND-NOT 把"压制有害概念同时保留目标能力"从 prompt engineering 上升为代数操作，对推理期对齐与可审计控制有直接借鉴价值；conceptor quota 作为无监督层选择指标对理解概念在 transformer 各层的分布也有方法论意义。

Questions for Further Exploration

  • Does the conceptor quota diagnostic transfer to base (non-instruction-tuned) models, where the "middle layer peak" pattern may differ?
  • How does the bipolar-subspace advantage scale with model size? At 70B+, does the residual structure beyond the unipolar direction remain useful, or does scale make a single direction sufficient?
  • Multi-layer / multi-concept compositional steering: can AND-NOT applied across multiple layers simultaneously yield additive gains, or do interference effects dominate?
  • How does conceptor steering interact with RLHF refusal training? Can a "safety conceptor" trained on refused vs. complied responses work as a tunable safety dial?
  • Robustness to distribution shift in the contrastive training pairs — what happens when the 100 pairs are paraphrased or translated?
  • Could conceptor quota act as a real-time concept-detection signal (using q on a single forward pass) rather than only as a layer-selection tool?
  • How does the AND operation degrade as overlap → 0? The paper notes near-orthogonal AND becomes degenerate; is there a smooth fallback (e.g., to OR) one could detect from the spectrum of (C_A^{-1} + C_B^{-1} − I)?
  • For the depression concept, does steering produce text that fools clinical classifiers without producing genuinely depression-correlated linguistic markers (a Goodhart concern given the automated PHQ-9 ridge regressor)?
