Distillation · Interpretability

Rethinking On-Policy Distillation of Large Language Models: Phenomenology, Mechanism, and Recipe

Yaxuan Li, Yuxin Zuo, Bingxiang He et al. — Tsinghua University (THUNLP)

两大核心条件决定蒸馏成败：教师与学生的思维模式需兼容、教师须拥有学生未见过的新知识。成功的OPD本质是学生在生成状态上与教师高概率重叠token的渐进对齐（72% → 91%）。 Two conditions govern on-policy distillation success—thinking-pattern compatibility and genuinely novel teacher knowledge. Effective OPD is driven by progressive alignment on high-probability overlap tokens (72%→91%) at student-visited states, not raw benchmark scores.
